There are various comfy positions for breastfeeding-
1 Cradle 
2 Cross cradle 
3 Back lying 
4 football
5 Australian hold
6 Inverted side lying 
7 Side lying cradle 
8 Side lying 
Alsooo There's no right or wrong way to hold and feed your baby, and each mum and baby will find their own preferred position to feed in. What's important is that you both feel comfortable. Knowing a few different breastfeeding positions and techniques can be helpful.❤️❤️❤️
