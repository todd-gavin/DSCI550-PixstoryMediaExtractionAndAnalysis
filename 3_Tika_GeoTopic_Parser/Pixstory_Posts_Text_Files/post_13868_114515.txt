 Himachal Pradesh Shilaroo Hockey stadium is the world s highest hockey stadium The stadium is located in Netaji Subhash High Altitude Training Centre The stadium is one of the most modern facilities like astro turf The speciality of this stadium is its multi purpose usage for training of national players in sports such as boxing judo wrestling hockey and gymnastics Shilaroo was selected by SAI due to climatic conditions which help players in building stamina endurance and quick recovery from hectic training schedules
