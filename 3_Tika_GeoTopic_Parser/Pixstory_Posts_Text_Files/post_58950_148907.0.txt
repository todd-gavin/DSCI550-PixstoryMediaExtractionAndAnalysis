Gehlot himself indicated just hours before the crucial Congress Legislature Party meeting in Jaipur on Sunday evening that a new generation should be given a chance to lead. "In terms of the position of Congress President, I have been in politics for 50 years and held various positions for 40 years; what more can a person expect? Now is the time to give the next generation a chance so that we can all lead the country together "He stated.
Meanwhile, Sachin Pilot supporters are optimistic that the young leader will soon be in charge of the state. In Jodhpur, posters with quotes like 'Naye Yug ki Taiyari' (preparing for a new era) in support of Pilot have appeared.
