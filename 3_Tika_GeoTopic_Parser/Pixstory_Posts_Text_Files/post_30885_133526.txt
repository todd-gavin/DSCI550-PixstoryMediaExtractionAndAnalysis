Coach Gianmarco Pozzecco made the last choices by drawing up the list of players who will be part of the adventure of the Italian national team at the next European basketball championships which will start in September in Milan with the challenge against Estonia The last cut involved center Amedeo Tessitori The roster will be therefore formed by Marco Spissu Nico Mannion Paul Biligha Stefano Tonut Danilo Gallinari Niccol Melli Simone Fontecchio Giampoalo Ricci Tommaso Baldasso Achille Polonara Alessandro Pajola and captain Luigi Datome
