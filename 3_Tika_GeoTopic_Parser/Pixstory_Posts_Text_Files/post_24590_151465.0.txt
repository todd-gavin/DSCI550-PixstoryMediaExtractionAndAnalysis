The men’s football event at the National Games 2022 commenced on Sunday, October 2. Kerala started their campaign with a 2-1 victory over Odisha, while another game between Manipur and Services Sports Control Board ended in a 1-1 draw. Meanwhile, in the Women’s event, Odisha and Assam registered victories in their opening encounter of the season. 
36th National Games 2022: Football Results - 2nd October 
Women’s 
Odisha beat Maharashtra 2-1 
Assam beat Gujarat 4-2
Men’s
Kerala beat Odisha 2-1
Manipur drew with Services Sports Control Board 1-1
