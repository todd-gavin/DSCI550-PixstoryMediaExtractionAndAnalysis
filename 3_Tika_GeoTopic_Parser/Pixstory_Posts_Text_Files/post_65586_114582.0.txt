The birthdate of India's most beloved captain, MS Dhoni, makes July 7 a significant day for all cricket fans in that country. In an Instagram message, the former Indian captain Virat Kohli wished him well and thanked him for his contributions to Indian cricket. Under MS Dhoni's direction, Indian cricket reached previously unimaginable heights. The Indian team won every major ICC trophy, including the 2007 T20 World Cup and the 2011 ODI World Cup. He retired from international cricket in 2020 and is regarded as one of the best finishers in the history of the sport. Virat Kohli, a former Indian captain and current top-order batsman, wished MS Dhoni a happy 41st birthday with an unique Instagram message.
