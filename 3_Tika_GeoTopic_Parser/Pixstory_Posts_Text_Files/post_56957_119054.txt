A little good news amid all the gloom Technology is paving the way to a more sustainable future for fashion Here are a few ways to get started Use D printing to test samples for fit design and quality before manufacturing physical pieces to cut down on waste Use alternative sustainable materials that are less harmful to the environment Make use of analytics and production intelligence to forecast demand and avoid overproduction Increase transparency in your supply chain by using production software such as PLM and ERP to increase accountability Encourage customers to share or rent clothing instead of buying and discarding it after one or two uses Collect data about your supply chain to determine where there s room for improvement and growth Fortunately many innovative new technologies can help solve some of these issues around transparency and factory safety while reducing the environmental impact of fashion manufacturing
