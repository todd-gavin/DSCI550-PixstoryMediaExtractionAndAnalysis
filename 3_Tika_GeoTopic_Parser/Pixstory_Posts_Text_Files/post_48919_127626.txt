Harpy Eagles are rapidly disappearing due to habitat loss and shooting Harpy eagles are a beautiful species of eagle that can be found mainly in parts of Central and South America They re known for their powerful wings and sharp claws which make them excellent hunters Unfortunately their habitat is rapidly disappearing due to deforestation and hunting which has made it difficult for them to survive in the wild The harpy eagle is currently listed as a vulnerable species by the BirdLife International meaning that its population has decreased by over over the past two decades If this trend continues it s likely that these birds will become extinct within the next few years We need to do everything we can to protect their habitats and conserve their numbers so that they can continue to inspire us with their incredible natural beauty
