After the National Trust of Queensland designated Lamington as an Australian icon it has been dubbed the National Cake of Australia It was named after Lord Lamington the previous Governor of Queensland who was known for his sweet taste The lamington is a simple square shaped sponge covered in desiccated coconut and dipped in chocolate As per skyscanner other varieties include two layers with a cream or jam filling which many people consider to be essential They re available in all fine bakeries and cafes and pair great with a lovely cup of bush tea or one of Melbourne s world famous coffees Although it is believed that the cake was initially served in Toowoomba its national success has seen it spread across Australia Flour and Stone bakery in Sydney s Woolloomooloo is touted as one of the best places to sample this Australian food
