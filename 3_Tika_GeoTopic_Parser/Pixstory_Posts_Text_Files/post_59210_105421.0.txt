On June 7, the United Nations observes World Food Safety Day raise awareness about food and water pollution around the world. WHO continues to pursue efforts to minimize food-borne diseases around the world.

World Food Safety Day strives to reform food systems to provide better health and prevent foodborne infections in the long run. This day also presents an opportunity to strengthen efforts to ensure the safety of the food we eat and reduce the global burden of foodborne illnesses.

This year the theme for #WorldFoodSafetyDay highlights safe food is vital not only to better health and food security but also for our livelihoods and economic development.
