A loner with antisocial tendencies finds his life turning around for the better when a lively pup named Charlie enters his life. 
This naughty and energetic pup gives a lonely and reclusive man a new perspective towards his life.
Penned and directed by Kiranraj, 777 Charlie stars Rakshit Shetty in the lead role.Watch the beautiful bond between Dharma & his pup Charlie in the superhit movie!
