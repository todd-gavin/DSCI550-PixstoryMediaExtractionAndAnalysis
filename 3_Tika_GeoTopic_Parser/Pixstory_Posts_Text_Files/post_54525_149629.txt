One is fighting between sections of the civilian population caused by cattle raiding among other things Another is armed confrontation between government forces and insurgents like the South Sudan People s Movement Army This is a group of disgruntled soldiers who were recently discharged from the military and are agitating for new government roles Broadly the crisis speaks volumes of the fragility of South Sudan as a state It is the consequence of weak political institutions in the country not just Unity State It is my view that the national government of South Sudan and Unity State
