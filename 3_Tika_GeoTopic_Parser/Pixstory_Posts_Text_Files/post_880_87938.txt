This is an article from BBC Bolzano Victory Monument a striking arch made of white marble with columns sculpted to resemble fasces the bundle of sticks that symbolised the fascist movement It has an ethereal almost ghostly presence rising like a mirage out of the grey apartment buildings and green trees that surround it Along its frieze an inscription in Latin reads Here at the border of the fatherland set down the banner From this point on we educated the others with language law and culture
