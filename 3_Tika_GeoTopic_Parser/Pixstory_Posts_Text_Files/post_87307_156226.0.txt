Spain, on the other hand, have played 2 games this season in FIFA U17 Women’s World Cup 2022 and they have managed to win one game and have lost one game so far. In this process, they have scored two goals and have conceded 2 goals. Spain have beaten Colombia and lost to Mexico in their opening games of the U17 World Cup.
