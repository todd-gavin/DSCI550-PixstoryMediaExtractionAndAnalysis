Congress leader Rahul Gandhi is being questioned by the Enforcement Directorate officers in connection with National Herald money-laundering investigation. 

Before reaching the ED headquarters in central Delhi, Gandhi went to the Congress office on Akbar Road accompanied by party leaders including Priyanka Gandhi Vadra, Rajasthan Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot and Chhattisgarh CM Bhupesh Baghel. Gaurav Gogoi tweeted about the detention of Congress leaders who wanted to walk along with Gandhi in protest.
