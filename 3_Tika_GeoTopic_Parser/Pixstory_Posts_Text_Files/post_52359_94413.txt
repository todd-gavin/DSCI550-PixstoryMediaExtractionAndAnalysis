The ANZAC biscuit is one of Australia s most historic desserts These cookies made of sustaining oats and coconut were created as a long life bread substitute for the Australian and New Zealand Army Corps ANZACs as they fought in World Wars ANZAC biscuits were originally tough solid bricks of admittedly tasty sustenance The modern version is softer and chewier but the key ingredients remain the same oats flour coconut butter sugar and golden syrup
