Anoop Misra Ashok kumar Das Abstract Background Current guidelines state that screening for diabetes should be done at years of age in India Methods Investigators from multiple sites in India were involved in providing data regarding patients with type diabetes T D aged years or less Other relevant studies were also reviewed Results Overview of published and unpublished data show increasing prevalence of T D in individuals years and less About th of them had overweight obesity Conclusion Screening for diabetes in India should start at years in non pregnant adults instead of years as currently stipulated Sources
