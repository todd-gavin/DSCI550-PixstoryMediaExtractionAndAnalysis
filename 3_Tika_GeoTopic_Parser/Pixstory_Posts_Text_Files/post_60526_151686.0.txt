"One Dollar Lawyer" against "Golden Spoon".
"One Dollar Lawyer' won against 'The Golden Spoon' in the competition of Friday-Saturday dramas of the same broadcasting time. The 3rd episode of 'One Dollar Lawyer, which aired on September 30th, achieved a nationwide viewer rating of 12.9%. This is a remarkable increase from 8.5% recorded in episode 2, which was aired last week. On the other hand, episode 3 of "The Golden Spoon' recorded 5.5% in nationwide viewership. This is sadly a significant drop from the record of 7.4% set by last week's broadcast (episode 2).
