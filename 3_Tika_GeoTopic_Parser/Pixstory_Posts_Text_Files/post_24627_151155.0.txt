The women’s football event at the National Games 2022 commenced on Saturday, October 1. 4 teams from Group A met each other on the opening day of the sport. Goa and Manipur started their campaign with a win and will look to extend their winning lead in the competition. Meanwhile, Mizoram and Tamil Nadu lost their season openers and will look to come back stranger during their upcoming matches.
