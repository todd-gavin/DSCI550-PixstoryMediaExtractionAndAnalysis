Are you even a Aussie if you haven t heard of Vegemite It s the pride of Australia through and through The most popular dish that foreigners discover and almost always detest is vegemite But we know that we love it As NW says regardless of how often people tell you it s horrible you should at least give it a shot It must be an acquired taste I suppose Then what is Vegemite A dark brown paste known as Vegemite is created by combining various vegetables with yeast extract and spice additions It s comparable to marmite in the UK which had the well known love it or hate it commercial since the company recognised that not everyone liked it It is most frequently consumed with butter on toast or bread It can also be eaten with avocado melted cheese or tomato in Australia So vegemite over nuts any day
