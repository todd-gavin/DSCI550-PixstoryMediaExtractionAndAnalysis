Paris Saint Germain are pleased to announce the appointment of Christophe Galtier as first team coach The French coach has signed a two-year contract until June With this press release, PSG formalize the new coach who will therefore take the place of the outgoing Pochettino Here are the first statements from the technician I am moved and proud I arrived at the Parco dei Principi a few minutes ago and saw the phrase Ici c est Paris a phrase that embodies so many expectations I am prepared If I have accepted this responsibility it means that I am capable of it Alone it is very difficult together we are stronger The presence of Luis Campos was a determining factor
