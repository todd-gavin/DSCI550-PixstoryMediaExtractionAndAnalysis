Ahead of the season Ajax announced an all black shirt with Yellow Green and Red trim on the shoulders and the sleeves it was sold in record numbers on the release But Ajax weren t able to wear the jersey on the pitch So why did UEFA ban this shirt The original design for this shirt included three birds sitting below the base of the collar The birds like the kit were a tribute to Bob Marley But these birds were not allowed according to UEFA rules no advertising or non official marks of identification are permitted on that area of the shirt Meaning that Ajax had to wear the shirts without these three birds UEFA also said that The three birds being a tribute to Bob Marley s song do not represent a team identification of Ajax as set out in the Article of the Equipment Regulations Therefore the display of the birds together with the crosses was not approved for UEFA competitions
