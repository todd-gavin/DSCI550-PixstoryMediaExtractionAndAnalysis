Thursday February hundreds of workers in the factories of Asni res Hauts de Seine Saint Donat Dr me Sarras Ard che Cond Indre and Issoudun Indre have decided to fold their arms The employees are demanding better wages and protest against a reorganization of working hours wanted by the management of the maison by Lvmh and Bernard Arnault which according to the unions provides for an annualisation of working hours with consequent limits on the use of weekly overtime in periods of the year in which production peaks are concentrated and on the choice of holidays
