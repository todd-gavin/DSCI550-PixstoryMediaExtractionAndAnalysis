Bhagwani Deswal aka Bhagwani Devi from India won the gold medal in 100 metres with a timing of 24.74 seconds at Tampere Finland in the World Masters Athletics Championship 2022.

94-year-old Bhagwani Devi had earlier won three gold medals at the Delhi State Athletics Championship in the 100-metre race, shot put, and javelin throw.

Bhagwani Devi proved that age has no boundaries and you can do anything if you are willing to do so.
