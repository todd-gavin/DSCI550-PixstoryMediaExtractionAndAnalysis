The midfielder becomes the second foreign recruit for the Highlanders.
Two time Indian Super League (ISL) semi-finalists NorthEast United FC are set to complete the signing of Spanish midfielder Jon Gaztanaga Arrospide, Khel Now can confirm. The 31-year-old will become second foreign recruit for the Highlanders.

“NorthEast United are set to complete the signing of Jon Gaztanaga on a one-year deal. The Spaniard is expected to land in Guwahati to team up with the rest of the squad very soon,” a source close to developments told Khel Now.
