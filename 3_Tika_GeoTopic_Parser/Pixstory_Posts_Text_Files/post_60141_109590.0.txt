Online food delivery service Zomato announced that it will swap shares with Grofers India to acquire Blink Commerce for a total purchase amount of Rs 4,447.48 crore.
In a meeting conducted , the company's board of directors approved buying up to 33,018 equity shares of Blink Commerce from its shareholders for a total purchase price of Rs 4,447.48 crore, or Rs 13.45 lakh per equity share, Zomato stated in a regulatory filing.


This transaction would be carried out through the issuing and allotment of up to 62.85 crore fully paid-up equity shares of Zomato, each with a face value of Re 1, for a price of Rs 70.76 

Currently, the company has 3,248 preference shares and 1 equity share in BCPL
