In New York Anybag has become an object of desire, the shopper that recycles plastic bags, a sustainable fashion project born two years ago in the midst of the pandemic from the creativity of Alex Dabagh, an entrepreneur of Lebanese origin who has always produced luxury bags and refined leather goods the Park Avenue International brand The idea was to create an elegant system for recycling plastic bags and turning them into useful bags The Anybag was liked right from the start Miranda Kerr was among the first to buy it then big brands such as Adidas and Ralph were added Lauren Last year, Anybag collected around 1,000 plastic bags to clean and disinfect them before turning them into a shopping bag Two years after its birth Anybag represents the Park Avenue International turnover
