NAAC stands for National Assessment and Accreditation Council that basically inspects the college campuses and rates them respectively. Last month kristu jayanti had a NAAC  inspection . 
 There are various criteria such as greenery , cleanliness, faculty responsiveness on basis of what NAAC grades the colleges. 
NAAC prepares their final assessment in the for of CGPA that ranges between 2.73 to 4.00 being the highest(A++) .
As per the NAAC criteria Kristu jayanti college , autonomous has been ranked A++ being qualified and accredited to be a deemed university. 
NAAC inspections are beneficial to colleges as they helps the colleges to know their weaknesses and strengths and what areas needs to be improved.
