Tradition and innovation distinguish the new format of the 17th edition of the Trento Festival of Economics Tradition represented by the academic and university strands and will see alternating meetings and dialogues with Nobel Prize winners and international economists Innovation represented by two strands of in-depth analysis on the one hand industrial economy and, on the other, the local economy which represents the backbone of the Italian system A vast program which, starting from the theme chosen for the edition, will stimulate reflections and debates among the brightest minds in the scientific world, opinion leaders of national and international experts from the most diverse disciplines to analyze how the world has changed after the pandemic and the war in Ukraine Here is the program
