 WM Entertainment released an official statement addressing the alleged similarities and explaining their concept behind the album art for “HUSH RUSH.”
After WM entertainment released Lee Chaeyon's album teaser photo. Netizens notice a similarity between Lee Chaeyeon's "HURSH HOUR"  and the shinee Key solo album "Gasoline". Netizens point out the specific elements of Lee Chaeyeon's teasers for "HURSH HOUR" including the imagery in a concept trailer, the font style, color combination of purple and green, also noticed the layout of teaser posters was similar to Key's album art for "Gasoline".
