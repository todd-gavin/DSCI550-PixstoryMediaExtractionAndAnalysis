Divock Origi is a new Milan player The Belgian striker will be released in less than three months on a free transfer from Liverpool and is preparing to wear the Rossoneri shirt in the next season. In the last few hours, Milan has accelerated and closed the deal quickly to bring rossonero Divock Origi The agreement in principle with the player whose contract expires at the end of June has already been reached A three-year contract for one million euros is ready for him
