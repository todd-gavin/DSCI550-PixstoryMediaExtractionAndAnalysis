North Lakhimpur:  Two suspected drug peddlers were shot by police in Lakhimpur’s Banderdewa area while they allegedly “tried to escape” from custody.

The incident took place in the Parbatipur area of Banderdewa on the Assam-Arunachal border at 1 am on Friday after their arrests.
