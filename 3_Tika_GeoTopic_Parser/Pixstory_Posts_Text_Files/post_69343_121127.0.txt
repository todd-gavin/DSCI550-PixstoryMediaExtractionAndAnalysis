Gangabal Lake, which is tucked away in Mount Harmukh's foothills, gives a picture-perfect view of the gorgeous lake set against the imposing mountain. A pleasant journey through the meadows of Naranag, which are intriguing for nature lovers and photography aficionados, is promised by the Gangabal Lake trek. 

The route is tough due to its lovely gorges and mountains, but the ever-changing scenery and nice weather will charm you the entire time. If you enjoy the outdoors, take this walk because it is one of Jammu and Kashmir's most captivating hikes.

Location: Naranag-Trunkhol-Butsheru-Gangabal-Naranag.

Altitude: 3600m/11811.02ft.

Duration: 5 days.

Difficulty level: Moderate.
