Microaggressions are defined as the everyday subtle intentional and oftentimes unintentional interactions or behaviors that communicate some sort of bias toward historically marginalized groups Sometimes it s an insult other times it s an errant comment or gesture The micro in microaggression doesn t mean that these acts can t have big life changing impacts They can which is all the more reason to address them when you see them The difference between microaggressions and overt discrimination or macroaggressions is that people who commit microagressions might not even be aware of them It is important to recognise such behaviour patterns and try to understand the lived experiences of historically marginalized groups and try to think outside of your own perspective
