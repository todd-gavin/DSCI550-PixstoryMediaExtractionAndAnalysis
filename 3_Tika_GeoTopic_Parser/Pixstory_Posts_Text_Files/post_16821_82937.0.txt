It is pouring weddings in Bollywood! After Farhan Akhtar-Shibani Dandekar and Vikrant Massey-Sheetal Thakur's star-studded wedding, filmmaker Luv Ranjan also tied the knot. The director got married to longtime girlfriend, Alisha Vaid in Agra, yesterday February 20.
From Ranbir Kapoor to Kartik Aaryan, many celebs were spotted at Luv Ranjan's Agra wedding. While Ranbir twinned with Shraddha Kapoor in white, lovebirds Rakul Preet Singh and Jackky Bhagnani were seen holding hands.
Reportedly, Luv and Alisha are college mates and their common interest in arts brought the couple closer.
