Pandora announces the launch of a new line of jewelry with diamonds created in the laboratory in the United States and Canada The operation explains the company aims to reduce CO emissions and protect the planet by discontinuing the use of diamonds extracted in mines The future of luxury Pandora CEO Alexander Lacik explains here today Lab-created diamonds are as beautiful as mined ones but accessible to more people and with fewer carbon emissions We are proud to expand the diamond market and offer innovative jewelry that defines a new standard for reducing the impact of the sector on the planet
