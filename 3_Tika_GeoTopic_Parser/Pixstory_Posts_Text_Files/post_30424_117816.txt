Mourning in the world in football Luciano Nizzola passed away years ago former president of the Federcalcio and Lega Serie A Born in Saluzzo by profession a civil lawyer he was managing director of Turin over the years while in he assumed the position of president of the Lega Serie A which at the time was called Lega National Professionals From to then held the role of number one in Italian football Gabriele Gravina, current president of the FIGC, recalled Nizzola as follows The world of football salutes a gentleman manager his spirit of service and his availability marked an era In the various roles he held he also distinguished himself for the decisive impulse that was given to our system in those years
