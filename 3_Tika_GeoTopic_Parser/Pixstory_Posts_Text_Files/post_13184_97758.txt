Simultaneously with the joyous airstrikes in Kabul and a number of provinces local sources reported a fire on Fourth Taimani Street located in District of Kabul city The fire was caused by a joyous firing by Taliban forces Khalid Zadran a Taliban police spokesman in Kabul had previously tweeted accusing a number of compatriots of firing Khalid Zadran said that Kabul police commanders have instructed all security personnel to arrest the perpetrators of these shootings and enforce the law on them Pictures posted on social media show flames of fire and a column of smoke rising from a building in the fourth district of Kabul Local media reported that a market had caught fire as a result of the fire Statistics on the possible casualties of this fire are not yet known The Taliban have not yet commented
