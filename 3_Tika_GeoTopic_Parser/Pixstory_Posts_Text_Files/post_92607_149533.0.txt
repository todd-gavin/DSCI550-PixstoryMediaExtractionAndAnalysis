CT/GD Dev Kumar was a member of ITBP contingent deployed in Afghanistan for providing security to Border Road Organization (BRO) personnel engaged in road construction project Delaram-Zaranj. 
On 05.06.2008 at around 1715 hrs, an ITBP convoy was returning to the base camp after completion of duties, when suddenly a Corolla Vehicle laden with explosive came from the opposite direction and hit 
ITBP leading convoy vehicle.
In the massive explosion that followed, CT/GD Dev Kumar,thus made supreme sacrifice in the service of the Nation, upholding the highest tradition of the Indo-Tibetan Border Police Force.
