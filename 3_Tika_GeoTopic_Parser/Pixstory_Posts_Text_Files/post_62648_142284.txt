Acne is a common skin condition that can be difficult to treat It s caused by an overproduction of sebum which is a type of oil that s produced by the skin There are many things you can do to control sebum production and improve acne prone skin Use an anti acne face wash every day This will help cleanse the skin and reduce the amount of sebum produced Use a moisturizer every day to keep your skin hydrated This will help reduce the appearance of pores and stop the formation of acne lesions Avoid using products that contain coal tar as they can increase sebum production and cause more breakouts Use sun protection everyday to protect your skin from the harmful effects of the sun This will help reduce the risk of developing acne in the first place
