Ramandeep Kaur and Juned Khan rewrite km race walking National Records Ranchi Juned Khan Haryana and Ramandeep Kaur Punjab rewrote the men and women s km race walking National Records respectively when winning top honours in ninth Indian Open Race Walking Competition here on Sunday The year old Ramandeep Kaur clipped more than minutes from Priyanka Goswami s National Record set in the World Athletics Race Walking Championships in Muscat last month Her Punjab team mate Manju was abreast till the km mark but while Ramandeep Kaur soon opened a comfortable lead Manju came home inside Priyanka Goswami s mark In the men s race year old Juned Khan pulled away from Ram Baboo and Chandan Singh after the km mark to lower the National Record by five minutes Eknath Sambhaji Turambekar who held the National Record was among the four who were bunched in the lead till the km mark but dropped out after being second with km to go Uttarakhand s Sachin Bohra edged out Gujarat s Rohit Kumar Yadav by a mere second to win the men s U km crown Aditya Negi Uttarakhand and Rajasthan s Arvind Singh were the only other athletes who reached the halfway mark inside minutes but the former kept pace to claim bronze while the latter dropped to seventh spot Deepika Sharma Rajasthan won the women s U km race with seconds to spare while Haryana s Bharti Bhadana and Indu claimed the other medals Mansi Negi Uttarakhand opened up an second lead by the halfway mark but was unable to sustain the pace eventually finishing fifth behind Tamil Nadu s Mokavi Muthurathinam The results Men km Juned Khan Haryana New National Record Old Eknath Sambhaji Turambekar Muscat March Ram Baboo Uttar Pradesh Chandan Singh Uttarakhand Women km Ramandeep Kaur Punjab New National Record Old Priyanka Goswami Muscat March Manju Punjab Payal Uttarakhand Men U km Sachin Bohra Uttarakhand Rohit Kumar Yadav Gujarat Aditya Negi Uttarakhand Women U km Deepika Sharma Rajasthan Bharti Bhadana Haryana Indu Haryana AFI Media
