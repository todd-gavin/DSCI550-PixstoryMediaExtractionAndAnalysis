For Matteo Berrettini the satisfactions do not end After the semifinal against Nadal in the last Australian Open and the achievement of the best position of his career in the sixth ATP ranking, the Roman tennis player is preparing to take the stage of the Sanremo festival as a sports guest of the opening evening It is not known still what will Berrettini do if he emulates Novak Djokovic who performed in a mini match with Fiorello and sang a few pieces of Terra Promessa by Eros Ramazzotti or if he does anything else Of course participation in Sanremo represents a sort of consecration for the only blue tennis player to have played a final at Wimbledon
