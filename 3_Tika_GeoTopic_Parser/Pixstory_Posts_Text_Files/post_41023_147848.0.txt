Earth-shattering blockbuster RRR has made NTR and Ramcharan famous around the world. Despite not being chosen by the Indian jury, Rajamouli's film still has a slim possibility of being nominated for an Academy Award. News reports say that the most recent information is that renowned director Rajamouli has joined Hollywood's top Creative Artists Agency (CAA). To put it simply, CAA is a Los Angeles-based talent agency that represents hundreds of actors and directors and handles branding, marketing, and endorsements for motion pictures. Given how well-known Rajamouli has now become globally, this agreement has become a heated subject. The Baahubali filmmaker will next collaborate with Mahesh Babu, and it will begin in the following Spring.It will surely be a global adventure!
