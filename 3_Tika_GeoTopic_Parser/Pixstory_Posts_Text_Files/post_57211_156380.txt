There are two kinds of symptoms associated with acrophobia psychological and physical Psychological symptoms are Feeling intense fear and anxiety when thinking about looking at or being in high places Fearing that something negative will happen in a high place such as falling or being trapped in a high place Feeling a strong desire to escape if you re in a high place Physical symptoms and signs are Experiencing a rapid heartbeat when thinking of or looking at heights Feeling dizzy and lightheaded when thinking of or looking at heights Feeling queasy Trembling Having shortness of breath
