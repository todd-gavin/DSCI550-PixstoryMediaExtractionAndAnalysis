Relative to lean individuals overweight individuals tend to experience food cravings that are more intense and difficult to resist Foods high in fat calories and sugar bind receptors in the brain that induce feelings of pleasure and delight Whether or not food cravings precede or follow from obesity they play a key role in the maintenance of overweight status Thus food cravings may be an important point of intervention in the prevention and treatment of obesity and overweight Understanding variability in food craving experiences is a necessary step in developing evidence based treatments
