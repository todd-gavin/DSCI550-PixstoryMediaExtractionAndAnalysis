During the National Games Gujarat 2022, Shankar Mahadevan, a well-known Bollywood singer, also graced the audience's ears with his excitement and electrifying performance.
By singing traditional Gujarati music and booming the stage, he welcomed the crowd.
With his combination of Carnatic, Hindustani, and Jazz, Shankar Mahadevan quickly became a cultural icon in the indipop genre.
In 1998, his non-film album Breathless reached the top of the Indian music charts.
