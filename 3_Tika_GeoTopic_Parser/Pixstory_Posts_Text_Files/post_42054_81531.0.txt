National Council of Allowed Economics Research conducted a study which indicates that pandemic caused significant job losses in this sector. 14.5 million jobs were lost during the first quarter, followed by 5.2 million jobs during the second quarter and another 1.8 million jobs during the third quarter of 20-21, after the lockdown was imposed. Also,Foreign Exchange Earnings between January and December 2020 fell by 76.3% in comparison to corresponding period in 2019.
