Wide Sargasso Sea novel by Jean Rhys published in A well received work of fiction it takes its theme and main character from the novel Jane Eyre by Charlotte Bront The book details the life of Antoinette Mason known in Jane Eyre as Bertha a West Indian who marries an unnamed man in Jamaica and returns with him to his home in England Locked in a loveless marriage and settled in an inhospitable climate Antoinette goes mad and is frequently violent Her husband confines her to the attic of his house at Thornfield Only he and Grace Poole the attendant he has hired to care for her know of Antoinette s existence The reader gradually learns that Antoinette s unnamed husband is Mr Rochester later to become the beloved of Jane Eyre
