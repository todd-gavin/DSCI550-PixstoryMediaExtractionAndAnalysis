Since February, the Mudec Museo delle Culture in Lugano has been hosting the major exhibition dedicated to the Jeffrey Montgomery Collection For over fifty years at home in Lugano one of the largest and best-known collections of Japanese art outside of Japan Set up on the two floors of the exhibition space of Villa Malpensata, seat of the MUSEC, the JAPAN ARTS AND LIFE exhibition presents to the public works dating back to the period between the 12th and 20th centuries, including fabrics, furnishings, paintings, masks, objects of worship and everyday life, carefully selected from over a thousand objects collected during a life from Jeffrey Montgomery Renowned throughout the world, the Montgomery Collection displays an extraordinary richness and a very singular oriental substance and at the same time expresses a popular culture reinterpreted in extremely high aesthetic terms by the elegant and refined choice of the collector who has dedicated his life to it. open until January
