Jaunpur's Judicial Magistrate Ashutosh Singh has filed a complaint against five, including the producer of the film Adipurush Om Raut and actors Prabhas and Saif Ali Khan, on the complaint of advocate Himanshu Srivastava. The court has fixed October 27 for the statement of the complainant. Srivastava alleged indecent portrayal of Lord Rama, Sita, Hanuman and Ravana in the film's teaser that has hurt religious sentiments.
