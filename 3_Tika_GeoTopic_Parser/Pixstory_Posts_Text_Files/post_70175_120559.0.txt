Mohith from Kangra, Himachal Pradesh and Dakshayani Murgod from Mapusa
won the 21km, half marathon at the 8th Goa Tourism-BNI Rotary Rain Run 2022 held in
Bambolim on Sunday. While Mohith clocked one hour, 31 minutes and 53 seconds,
Daskshayani clocked two hours, 17 minutes, 43 seconds for the distance.
In the 10kms open category, Pradyumna Naik of Porvorim clocked 37:04 minutes, while
Amrita George, a grade IX school girl from Sirsi in Karnataka shocked her more experienced
competitors winning the race in 52:57 minutes.
In the 21kms veterans category ( above age 45), Sube Singh Yadav of the Indian Navy
clocked 1:42:33 hours, while Kranti Salvi, celebrated Mumbai based marathoner clocked
2:00:18 to win their respective races. Similarly, in the 10kms veterans category, Naresh
Kumar of Ponda clocked 55:18 minutes and Leena Kamat of Mapusa clocked 1:00:56 hours
to win their respective races.
School girl Amrita George from Sirsi, winner of the 10kms women’s race said, “I am thrilled
to win, as this is the first time,”
While, Kranti Salvi of Mumbai, winner of the 21kms women’s veteran race said, “Winning
always feels amazing, but the experience of running this beautiful route was even more
joyful.”
