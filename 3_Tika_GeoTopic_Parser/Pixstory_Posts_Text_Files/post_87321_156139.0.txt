The United States and Morocco will be up against each other on Matchday 03 of the FIFA Under 17 Women’s World Cup 2022. While United States are on the top spot in Group A of the FIFA Under 17 Women’s World Cup points table, Morocco sit at the third spot in the points table.
The United States have played 2 games this season in FIFA U17 Women’s World Cup 2022. They have managed to win one game and have drawn one game so far. In the process, they have scored 9 goals and conceded 01 goal. The United States have beaten India and drawn Brazil in their first two games of the Under 17 World Cup.
Morocco, on the other hand, have played 2 games this season in FIFA U17 Women’s World Cup 2022 and they have managed to win one game and have lost a game so far. In this process, they have scored 3 goals and have conceded 1 goal. Morocco, on the other hand, have beaten India and have lost to Brazil in their opening games of the U17 World Cup.
