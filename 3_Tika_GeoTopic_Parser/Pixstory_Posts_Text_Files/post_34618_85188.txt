Aged Valentino Rossi became dad This morning his partner Francesca Sofia Novello gave birth to Giulietta Rossi and the nine-time world champion of moto GP made the announcement on social media Mom and dad are over the moon, he wrote by posting a photo of a little foot in black and white Many comments and messages reached Valentino Rossi and his partner including those of TV personalities or the world of sport who are also great friends like Cesare Cremonini and Fabio Quartararo The pregnancy has been known since last August when, always on social networks, Rossi had staged with Francesca Sofia Novello a visit complete with lab coat and stethoscope to give the happy announcement to the fans Now the wait is over
