Chief Minister N Biren Singh is ahead of rival Congress candidate Pangeijam Saratchandra Singh by over 15,500 votes in Heingang Constituency.

Nemcha Kipgen of the BJP is leading by 3,146 votes from Congress’ Ngamkhohen Kipgen in in the Kangpokpi Constituency, while Y. Antas Khan (BJP) is leading by 423 votes from JD(U)’s Mohd Abdul Nasir in the Lilong constituency.
