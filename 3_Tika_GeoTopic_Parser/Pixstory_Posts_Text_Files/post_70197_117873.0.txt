Pope John XXIII HS, Quepem, defeated Immaculate Conception HS, Avedem, Paroda, by a solitary goal to emerge champions in the girls U-17 Subroto Mukherjee Inter-School Quepem taluka football tournament at Pontemol ground, Curchorem. The teams were tied goalless at half time and the all-important goal was scored in the second session through the boots of Rivency Dias.  She collected a loose ball from the opposition and unleashed a powerful shot which hit the crossbar and went in. Pope John XXIII will now represent Quepem at the district level.
