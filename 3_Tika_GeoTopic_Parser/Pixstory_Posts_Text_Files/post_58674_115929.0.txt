A view of Ganges and its riverfront in Varanasi,  is wonderful , calm and quiet consisting a chain of 84 ghats from Assi to Rajghat, as well as  heritage of our centuries old civilization. These ghats are chained in a long area of 7 kilo meter. Devotees or tourists, without riding on boat, steemer and cruise, can have a view of the heritage ghats from any corner of the riverfront. Most of ghats were built during 11th to 18th century. It is calm and quiet. spiritual beauty of riverfront  has been attracting to Indian and foreigners tourists for centuries to visit to Kashi.
