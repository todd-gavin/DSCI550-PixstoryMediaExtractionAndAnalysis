Balmain&#39;s armored suits for Autumn Winter have nothing to do with the war the inspiration comes from motorcycling from haters and from the explosion that injured the designer Olivier Rousteing In October the creative director Rousteing woke up with his face and part of his body burned afterwards to the explosion of the fireplace of his Parisian apartment The black and white armor that paraded on the catwalk are a metaphor for the online attacks of the haters as well as a reminiscence of the bandages that the designer wore after a serious burn Among futuristic minidresses neon details corsets and reinforced biker jackets one thing for sure Balmain has captured the sense of restlessness of our times and translated it into clothes
