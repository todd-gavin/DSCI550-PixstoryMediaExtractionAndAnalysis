Diligence in work is required to ensure that public sector entities do well and an example of this is Mohammed Shams Chand. A ticket checker with Central Railways he earned Rs.1 crore this financial year (from April 2021 to February 2022) as fines levied on ticketless travellers becoming the member of the elite one-crore club.

This amount has come in just 11 months and despite Covid-19 pandemic restriction. In this period Chand apprehended 13,472 ticketless travellers and collected Rs 1,06,41,105 as fine from them. This includes those passengers who travelled in class different from the one mentioned in their ticket.
