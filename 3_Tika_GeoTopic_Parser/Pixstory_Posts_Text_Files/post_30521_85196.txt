Change of plans for the Turkish Airlines Euroleague Final Four the decisive matches for the award of the title will no longer be played in Berlin but in Belgrade The dates of the event have also been changed, which will be held from Thursday to Sunday The decision was made due to the epidemic situation linked to Covid in Germany and the health measures consequently adopted in the capital in Germany if prolonged they would not allow a large number of fans to attend the Final Four and could even cause the non-participation of some athletes
