Spicy Korean Tofu - quick and easy Korean braised tofu with chili powder, garlic, soy sauce, sugar and sesame oil. This Korean side dish (banchan) is healthy and delicious.

How to Make Spicy Korean Tofu

First, cut the tofu into rectangle shape and prepare the sauce by mixing soy sauce, sugar, Korean chili peppers and other sauce ingredients together. Next, pan fry the tofu until they turn brown on both sides. Add the sauce and braise the tofu. This recipe is so easy and healthy!

The end result is amazing, and tastes just likes my favorite Korean restaurant.
