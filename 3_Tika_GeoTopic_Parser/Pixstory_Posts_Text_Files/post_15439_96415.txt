The UK Department of Health reports in a circular on cases of acute hepatitis of unknown aetiology that no link has been identified with the COVID vaccine A questionnaire administered to cases about food and personal habits has not identified any common exposures As of April the UK United Kingdom reported cases in pediatric age of which underwent transplant Although laboratory investigations have excluded in all cases a viral aetiology of the ABCD and E type, the ministry underlined, the United Kingdom health authorities consider the infectious hypothesis the most probable given the epidemiological picture and the clinical characteristics of the cases However, toxicological investigations have also been started and are still in progress
