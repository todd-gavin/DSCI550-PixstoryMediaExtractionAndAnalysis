Bappi Lahiri, Bollywood’s disco legend, died at a Mumbai hospital on Tuesday night after suffering from a sleep-related breathing disorder. He was 69. The legendary music composer-singer was admitted to Juhu’s CritiCare Hospital.
Several celebrities including Kajol, Shaan, Alka Yagnik, Rakesh Roshan, and Poonam Dhillon arrived at the late composer’s residence to pay their last respects. Bappi Lahiri’s funeral will take place tomorrow, Thursday, after his son, Bappa Lahiri’s arrival from the USA.
