The telenovela Michael Masi officially ended The controversial race director who had decided the frantic finale of season F amidst controversy through choices that, following the conclusion of the internal investigation, the FIA itself had branded as human errors he is no longer a member of the International Automobile Federation The Australian from Sydney had reached the top of the control room ad interim in the Melbourne GP marred just over the weekend by the untimely death of his predecessor Charlie Whiting to then be definitively confirmed in the role until the events in Abu Dhabi The FIA confirms that Michael Masi he decided to leave the organization and to re-establish himself in Australia to be closer to his family and take up new professional challenges, reads the brief official note issued by the Instagram Photo Federation fia official
