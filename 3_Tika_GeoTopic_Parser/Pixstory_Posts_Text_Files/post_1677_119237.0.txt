Dr. Sushovan Banerjee passed away. His death is a great loss to the poor. For 60 years he had been treating patients in droves. His last fee was only one rupee. He was fondly called "Ek takar daktar".

In the year 2020, Dr. Banerjee was awarded Padma Shri. The same year his name was recorded in the Guinness World Records for treating the maximum number of patients.
