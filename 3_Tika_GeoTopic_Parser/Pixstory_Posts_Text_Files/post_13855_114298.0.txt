The history of Ice skating club Shimla goes back to the 1920.The saga of skating started in the open air, when a English gentlemen,Mr Blessington,  one cold morning found that his water taps had flooded  his tennis court with water .The water tap which was flooded in his tennis court was frozen.He jumped with joy and began the conversion of tennis court to a ice skating club.The ice skating club in Shimla (Himachal Pradesh) is the oldest skating club in India and South East Asia.The elegance of this rink is its natural freezing , without any use of Mechanical and artificial devices to freeze water.The only thing that makes Shimla ice skating rink special is that it is the only natural ice skating rink in India.
