Uber Technologies Inc said it will list New York City s yellow taxis on its app a move that could help the ride hailing giant overcome a driver shortage in its biggest U S market The city has for years been a battleground between Uber and yellow taxi drivers who have for long protested against the company s technology on grounds that it disrupted the industry and limited their earnings The deal would see Uber work with taxi software companies Creative Mobile Technologies and Curb whose apps together account for all the yellow cabs in New York city It is likely to bring cab drivers more business at a time when the spike in gas prices has added to the woes of an industry buffeted by the COVID pandemic Passengers will pay roughly the same fare for taxi rides as for Uber X rides according to the Wall Street Journal
