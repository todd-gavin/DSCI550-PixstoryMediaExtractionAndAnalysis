Sri Lanka Cricket announced the squad for the upcoming three-match T20 International against India National Cricket Team, which will be part of the Sri Lanka tour of India 2022.

The Sri Lanka tour of India 2022 consists of a three-match T20 International series and a two-match Test series.
The first match of the T20I series will be played on February 24 at the Bharat Ratna Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee Ekana Cricket Stadium in Lucknow while the second and third T20 International will be played on February 26 and 27 at the Himachal Pradesh Cricket Association Stadium in Dharamsala.

Squad For The 3-match T20I Series vs India:
Dasun Shanaka – Captain, Pathum Nissanka Kusal Mendis, Charith Asalanka –Vice-Captain, Dinesh Chandimal, Danushka Gunathilaka, Kamil Mishara, Janith Liyanage, Wanindu Hasaranga, Chamika Karunaratne, Dushmantha Chameera, Lahiru Kumara, Binura Fernando, Shiran Fernando, Maheesh Theekshana, Jeffrey Vandersay, Praveen Jayawickrama Ashian Daniel – Subjected to Ministerial approval.

Heading into the series, both teams are polar opposites on the ICC T20I rankings.
India are topping the table after whitewashing the West Indies at home. Sri Lanka on the other hand, have dropped to ninth after their 4-1 defeat against Australia. 

Besides, upcoming India vs Sri Lanka T20I series will be crucial for both teams to carry forward the momentum. It will be a dress rehearsal for both teams for this year’s T20 World Cup to be played in Australia in October-November.

Both teams have injury concerns heading into the series. For India, Deepak Chahar and Suryakumar Yadav are ruled out with injuries. Sri Lanka have Avishka Fernando and Bhanuka Rajapaksa missing from the squad due to injuries. 

Both teams have played 22 T20I matches till date with India winning 14 games and Sri Lanka seven. India lost the series 2-1 against the Lankans the last time both these teams played against each other.
