World-renowned Italian tenor Andrea Bocelli internationally renowned Chinese pianist Lang Lang and renowned Chinese soprano Lei Jia teamed up to perform the new song Forever You and Me a musical gift for the Beijing Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games The song was published in collaboration with the Organizing Committee of the Beijing Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games and the Federation of Literary and Artistic Circles of Beijing and distributed worldwide by Universal Music Group A cultural gift presented by distinguished musicians from China and Italy Milan and Cortina d Ampezzo will host the Winter Olympic Games in Forever You and Me sung in Chinese and English by Andrea Bocelli and Lei Jia with Lang Lang on the piano The song composed by Shu Nan with lyrics by Zhang Heping
