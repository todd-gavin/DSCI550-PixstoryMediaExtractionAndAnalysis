Those who are shunned by the society because of their physical condition and ailment need to be brought into the mainstream and gainfully employed. Doing precisely that is Asia’s first café located in Kolkata’s Jodhpur Road, which is run exclusively by HIV positive staff.

The twin objectives of the eatery are to spread awareness and generate employment for HIV positive people to make them confident and give them a chance to lead a normal life and become part of the society.

At present there are seven teenagers who are working in this café.
