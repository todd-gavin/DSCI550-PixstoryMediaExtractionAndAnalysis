Afghanistan national cricket team defeated Sri Lanka in the first match of Asia Cricket Cup 2022. The national teams of the two countries met at the Emirates International Stadium on Saturday night and Afghanistan won against Sri Lanka by 8 wickets. Sri Lanka, who started the game first, set 105 runs to Afghanistan.
Afghanistan achieved the set goal by losing one player and won the first match of the Asian Nations Cup against Sri Lanka.
Afghanistan national team is going to play Bangladesh in their second game on Tuesday, 29th August.
