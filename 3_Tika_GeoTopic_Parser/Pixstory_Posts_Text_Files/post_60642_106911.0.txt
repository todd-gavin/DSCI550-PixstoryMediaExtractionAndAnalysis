Netflix has announced the second season of its much-loved Korean thriller Squid Games. Director Hwang Dong-hyuk also hinted at Gong Yoo's return to the show.
Netflix announced the news on social media. The first season of the show was released last year and was critically acclaimed and popular among fans.

The streaming giant posted a teaser of the show on Twitter and announced the second season. They wrote, “Red light… GREENLIGHT! Squid Game is officially coming back for Season 2."
