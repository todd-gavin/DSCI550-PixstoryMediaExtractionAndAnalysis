Our Lady of Perpetual Succour HS, Cortalim, defeated St Thomas HS, Cansaulim, 7-0 to emerge champions of the boys U-14 Mormugao taluka Subroto Mukherjee football tournament at DSYA ground, Velsao, on Wednesday.

Declan, Nigel and Sanchay scored a brace each while Fraden scored one goal for Perpetual Succour.
