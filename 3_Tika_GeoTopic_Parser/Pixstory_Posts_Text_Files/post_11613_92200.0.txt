How about some peanut butter cookies that's sugar free which also doesn't make you go out of your diet. 

Check out the recipe for some peanut butter cookies 

• 1 cup flour

• 2 tablespoons flour

• 1⁄2 cup Splenda granular (or any sugar alternatives)

• 1 1⁄2 teaspoons baking powder

• 2⁄3 cup creamy natural-style peanut butter

• 1⁄4 cup vegetable oil or 1/4 cup canola oil

• 1 egg

• 2 tablespoons water

• 1 teaspoon vanilla



Steps to follow for some yummy cookies 

So the first step is to set your oven at 375 degrees and then grease the cookie sheet. 

Now pick a bowl and put in flour, the sugar alternative you are going for and some baking powder, now combine them all together.

Next add in peanut butter, oil, egg, water and vanilla and now mix them all together gently. 

Now roll them and shape them into 1 inch balls and keep them on the cookie sheet. 

Flatten down each cookie with a fork. 

The time required for baking is approximately 10 to 12 minutes or until it turns brown. 

After cooling down, enjoy your cookie treats with some cold milk or a drink which you like.
