Andrea Cambiaso has completed a permanent move from Genoa The year old has signed a contract that keeps him at Juventus until June Genoan by birth Andrea emerges from a positive season above all from a personal point of view In his debut Serie A campaign the year old made appearances in all competitions and stood out especially because of his ability to adapt to top flight football and to the demands of the different tactics adopted throughout the campaign by Genoa from wing back in a formation to full back on either side of a four player defence
