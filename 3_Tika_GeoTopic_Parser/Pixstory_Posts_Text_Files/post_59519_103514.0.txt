Gujarat Titans won the Indian Premier League for the first time on Sunday, with to all-rounder Hardik Pandya's seven-wicket triumph over Rajasthan Royals in the final. 
This trophy will be significant because we talked about leaving a legacy, and future generations will talk about it," said Pandya, who won four IPL titles with Mumbai Indians before switching franchises and being named Gujarat captain this year. 
"This is the perfect example for any team in the world because if you can play together, you can achieve great things."
