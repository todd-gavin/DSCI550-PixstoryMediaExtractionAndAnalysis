The national games of India comprise various games from which sportsmen of different states compete. 
The 2022 National Games is the 36th edition, as the 2020 Goa edition was postponed due to Covid-19. 
Players from all states and UTs will be participating as well as a team from the defence Forces. 
The 2022 National Games will take place in Gujrat from 29th September to 12th October.
