This winter makes your body warm and healthy with jaggery halwa. 
Jaggery is India's ancient superfood it is especially helpful during the winter season to keep your body warm and healthy. Jaggery is not only full of antioxidants comma Minerals and vitamins but also an excellent replacement for refined sugar. 
This jaggery halwa not only packs the richness of jaggery but also desi ghee, wholewheat flour  , and nuts.
Ingredients:
100 gm (1/2 cup) ghee
105 gm (3/4 cups) wholewheat flour or atta
375 ml (1 1/2 cups) water
220 gm (3/4 cup) unbleached jaggery
4 cardamom pods, crushed to a powder
