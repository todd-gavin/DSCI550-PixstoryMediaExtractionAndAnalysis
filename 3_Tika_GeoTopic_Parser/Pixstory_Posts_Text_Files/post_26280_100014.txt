Miu Miu The nickname of Miuccia Prada has become the name of the more ironic and young brand compared to her sister Prada, it seems perfect for the May cover of Harper s Bazaar Germany The protagonists of the cover are in fact two twins Maria and Alexandra The Greek sisters wear two looks in shades of blue from the latest Miu Miu collection Photographed by Stefan Armbruster and styled by Kai Margrander who is also fashion director of the publication
