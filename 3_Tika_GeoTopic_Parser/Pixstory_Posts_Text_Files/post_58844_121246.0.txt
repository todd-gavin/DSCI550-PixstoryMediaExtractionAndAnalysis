On Sunday, searches were carried out by the National Investigation Agency (NIA) across Madhya Pradesh, Gujarat, Bihar, Karnataka, Maharashtra, and Uttar Pradesh. Raids were conducted on 13 locations belonging to "suspects" in the six states "in connection with operations connected to ISIS."
The organisation carried out searches in the following districts: Bhopal and Raisen in Madhya Pradesh; Bharuch, Surat, Navsari, and Ahmedabad in Gujarat; Arariya in Bihar; Bhatkal and Tumkur City in Karnataka; Kolhapur and Nanded in Maharashtra; and Deoband in Uttar Pradesh.
The raids on Sunday allegedly netted incriminating papers.
Sections 153A and 153B of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) and sections 18, 18B, 38, 39, and 40 of the Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Act were used by the agency to suo-motu record a case in this subject on June 25. (UAPA). Additional research is being done.
