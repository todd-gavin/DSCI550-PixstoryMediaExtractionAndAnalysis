The fine arts department held an exhibition and here is a brief description of the original products displayed as a part of the show. 
As in past the craft is still practiced at homes and in workshops or karkhanas where the products are sold itself at homes women take part in making of the objects. Usually polishing the end products, pasting the layer of fabric inside the end product and packaging. The papier mache craft has gone through great developments over the years in the use of raw materials. With increasing popularity and the nature of craft,papier mache has reached beyond the borders of the valley. 
Production got spread across other states and popular centers namely Ujjain in MP, Gwalior, Bhopal etc. As Europe has increased the demands for these objects the decorative character of the craft and the exquisite skills shown by the Naqash (painter) got international appreciation.
