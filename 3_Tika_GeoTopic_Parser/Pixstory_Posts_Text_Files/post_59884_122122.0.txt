The Congress will protest today over rising costs, unemployment, and an increase in the GST rate. The party leaders will march to Rashtrapati Bhawan in Delhi.
Ahead of the nationwide protest, Congress officials have assembled at the party headquarters in Delhi.

Senior leaders and members of the Congress Working Committee (CWC) will participate in the "PM House gherao," while MPs from the Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha will hold a "Chalo Rashtrapati Bhavan" from Parliament.

In advance of the Congress march, the administration issued prohibitory orders that forbid mass assemblies in some areas of Delhi.

Top Congress officials got together on Tuesday to discuss the party's plan for today's massive protest.
