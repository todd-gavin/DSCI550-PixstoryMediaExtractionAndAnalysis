Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson who is set to appear as the anti-hero Black Adam in the movie of the same name has announced that the trailer will have its world premiere on 8th June 2022 and the movie is set to release on 21st October 2022.

The preview of the movie reads the following, 
"Nearly 5,000 years after he was bestowed with the almighty powers of the Egyptian gods -- and imprisoned just as quickly -- Black Adam is freed from his earthly tomb, ready to unleash his unique form of justice on the modern world."
