The versatile singer turned 49 today with a career of almost 2 decades. His voice has given some of the most soothing and melodious music of our times.

The best of them are :- 

1. Allah ke Bande

This was the song which instantly turned the singer into a star. It was released in 2006 as part of the 'Kailasa Album' and was featured in the movie Waisa Bhi Hota Hai.

Listen to it here :- https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=dUe1IsoJfrE

2. Teri Deewani - Kailasa ( album)

Another Gem from Kher is Teri Deewani released it 2007. It has become an all time love and has been remixed several times on platforms such as MTV unplugged.

Teri deewani :- https://youtu.be/zZasH6qkn8M

3. Yu hi Chala Chal Rahi - Swades

A travelling song featuring superstar Shah Rukh Khan from the movie swades. In the movie it is sung by Khan's character while traveling on the road to his village.

https://youtu.be/eEeX2QMlSlo

4. Mere Nishaan - Oh My God

A soulful & melodious chartbusters from the movie Oh My God featuring mega star Akshay Kumar.

https://youtu.be/8c6GRDxa6HQ

5. Chaand Sifarish - Fanaa

A romantic song from the movie Fanaa featuring perfectionist Aamir Khan and stunning superstar Kajol. A duet between Kher & Shaan, it's a treat for the mind & soul.

https://youtu.be/zWEOx7TSM6I

6. Jay Jay Kaara - Baahubali 2

The biggest blockbuster of Indian Cinema's history Baahubali 2 features a song from Kailash Kher. Jay Jay Kaara, song about the might of Baahubali, Kher's deep voice was the perfect choice for this powerful song.
