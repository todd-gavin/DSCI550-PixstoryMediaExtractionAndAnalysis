Various low lying localities in trans Varuna area have been submerged due to rise of water level  in Ganges , touching the mark of 69.77 meter , below the danger mark of 71.66 meter. The Varuna river connected with Ganga , has submerged old licalities, forcing a population of 10000 people to move to safer places in camps, after being rescued by the administration. The water level had stopped rising
