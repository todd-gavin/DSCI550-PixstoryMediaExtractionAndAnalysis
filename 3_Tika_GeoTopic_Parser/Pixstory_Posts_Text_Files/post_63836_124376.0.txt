Paramount Studios has announced that Sonic The Hedgehog will is coming to theatres on December 20, 2024. The upcoming sequel sees the return of the beloved Sega character, with both Tails and Knuckles the Echidna teased alongside him.Earlier this year, Sonic the Hedgehog 2 opened to a huge $72.1 million in a major boost for the domestic box office recovery, and particularly for family films, managing to gross over than $402 million at the worldwide box office.“Running faster, flying higher, and punching harder,” said an official announcement via Twitter. “#SonicMovie3 hits theatres on December 20, 2024.”Sonic 3 is however set to face stiff competition from Avatar 3, which is presently set to open over the year-end holidays in 2024 . It will also be debuting in the same month as another high-profile project in the form of the film adaptation of the Broadway musical Wicked. Not much else is known about the plot for this forthcoming follow-up, other than this vague synopsis, though Sonic the Hedgehog 2 has given us overall a pretty good idea of what we can expect from the movie.
