Research from supports the idea that earlier exposure to peanuts for example may lower the risk of developing a peanut allergy later on This is what parents can do Try first rubbing a very small amount of an allergenic food on your child s lips to observe any signs of reaction If all looks good add around a quarter teaspoon of the allergenic food to your baby s usual puree and mix well Over time increase the amount of the allergenic food by another quarter teaspoon if you don t observe an allergic reaction Don t rub foods into your baby s skin It doesn t help with identifying allergies and it might actually increase the risk of your baby developing an allergy to that particular food Sources healthline com
