Jyoti Kumari, an 18-year-old footballer, was found dead in a hostel in Kolkata on April 4. The defender, who played for India in several age groups, hailed from Uttar Pradesh's Varanasi and had been living in Odisha for several years. Jyoti's family have demanded CBI investigation into the matter, claiming that she hasn't died by suicide.
