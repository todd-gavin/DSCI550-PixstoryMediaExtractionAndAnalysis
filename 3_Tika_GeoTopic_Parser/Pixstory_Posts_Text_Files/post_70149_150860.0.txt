Goa's top seed and young rising player Aarush Pawaskar wins Silver at the ICSE School Nationals. The talented boy was part of Team Maharashtra and Goa Zone for the Inter ICSE school Nationals held at Padukone Dravid Centre of Excellence at Bangalore from September 22-25.
This team won their first round against Kerela, second round against UAE, and
quarters against MP and CG zone, followed by a win over UP and North East Zone in the semi finals. 
In a highly exciting and entertaining Finals, Team Maharashtra and Goa Zone lost against Karnataka in a nail biting match with a 2-1 scoreline.
