Exports of engineering goods surged 54 per cent to $81.8 billion during the April-December 2021 period as compared with $52.9 billion recorded during the same period of the previous year, the government data showed on Monday.

The engineering goods sector constituted the largest, more than 27 per cent, share in India's total exports basket during the period. In the month of December 2021, exports of engineering goods touched $9.79 billion, registering a growth of 38.41 per cent over $7.07 billion logged in December 2020, according to a statement released by the Ministry of Commerce and Industry.
