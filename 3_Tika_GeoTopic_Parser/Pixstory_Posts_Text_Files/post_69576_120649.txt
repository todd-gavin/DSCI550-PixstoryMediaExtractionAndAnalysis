Dastgeer Sahib is a year old shrine situated in Khanyar Sheikh Syed Abdul Qadir Jelani never stayed there It has the old Quran written by Ali Ibn Abi Talib A S and the relic which is locally called Mouia Pak a hair strand of Abdul Qadir Gilani It was constructed in and expanded in by Khwaja Sanaullah Shawl It is the center of the activity hub of the Khanyar and is surrounded by a large market On the Urs or birth date of the Abdul Qadir Gilani R A thousands of people from all over Kashmir go there to pray to Allah and watch the ziyarat
