The fast fashion group has confirmed its position on the Russian-Ukrainian conflict and has communicated through an official note that it has started a process of liquidating its assets in the country. The decision comes after the Swedish giant had already temporarily closed all its outlets on the territory since March and dictated by the fact that it is currently impracticable to continue the business due to the operational challenges that have arisen and the unpredictable future After careful consideration we believe it is impossible given the current situation to continue our business in Russia said the CEO of HM Helena Helmersson The company that has been operating in Russia since estimates an extraordinary cost of billions of crowns equal to millions of euros at today&#39;s exchange rate as a result of this operation The goal is now to ensure responsible closure and support all employees who they will inevitably be affected
