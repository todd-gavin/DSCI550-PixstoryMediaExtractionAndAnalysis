During our run for office or school we can munch on some mid day bars like these which will boost our energy and will also keep us going for the day. 

Ingredients 

For the Strawberry Bars: 

• 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats gluten free if needed 

• 3/4 cup white whole wheat flour or substitute all-purpose flour or 1:1 baking flour to make gluten free 

• 1/3 cup light brown sugar 

• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger 

• 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt 

• 6 tablespoons unsalted butter melted (or substitute melted coconut oil to make vegan/dairy free) 

• 2 cups small-diced strawberries about 10 ounces, divided 

• 1 teaspoon cornstarch 

• 1 tablespoon  freshly squeezed lemon juice from about 1/2 small lemon 

• 1 tablespoon granulated sugar divided 

Instructions 

• Keep the rack on the center of the oven and preheat the 375 degrees F. Line them up on an 8×8 - inch baking pan with parchment paper so that the papers hang up on the sides like handles. 

• In a bowl combine all the ingredients like oats, flour, brown sugar, ginger and salt. Now pur the butter that's melted and stir them till it forms clumps and the dry ingredients gets combined. Keep the crumble mixture aside for a while and press the rest of the mixture as an even layer at the bottom of the pan. 

• Spread out half of the strawberries over the crust. And sprinkle cornstarch over the top and sprinkle the lemon juice and 1/2 tablespoon of the granulated sugar. Scatter over the remaining berries and the remaining sugar. Sprinkle the reserved crumbs over the top that shows through some fruits as well. 

• Now bake the bars for 35 to 40 minutes till the fruit is bubbly and the crumbs smells like toasty. Keep the pan on awire rack and let the vars completely cool down. 

• As the bars cool down let's prepare the glaze for it. In a bowl work together powdered sugar, vanilla and milk till its smooth. You can add more milk if you prefer thinner consistency. Drizzle with the glaze and slice the bars and enjoy them.
