Here are some facts about Bollywood movies that are sure to leave you intrigued. 
1. 1. Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara was enlisted as a caste study in marketing management courses in the universities of Spain. Since the tourism of the country saw a rise by 65% after the film’s release. Impressive right? 
2. The pregnancy scene in 3 idiots was originally written for Munna Bhai M.B.B.S. 
3. Ranbir Kapoor got his ears pierced for the film Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani. 
4. Milkha Singh challenged Farhan Akhtar for a race when they met for the first time before they began shooting for Bhaag Milkha Bhaag. 
5. The crashed plane that was seen in the song ‘Gerua’ from Dilwale is actually real. 
6. Dabangg 2 was Salman Khan’s first sequel in 23 years. 
7. Prem Ratan Dhan Payo holds the record for the longest song and climax scene in a film.
8. Drishyam holds the record for 10 national award winners working on a single film. 
9. Tabu and Ranbir Kapoor were the original choice to play Anil Kapoor’s wife and son in the film Dil Dhadakne Do. 
10. Aamir Khan never blinks in the film PK.
