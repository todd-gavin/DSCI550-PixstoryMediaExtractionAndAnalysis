UNICEF welcomes announcement that warring parties in Yemen have accepted UN proposal to renew current truce for another two months Since beginning in April, the truce has led to a significant reduction in the intensity of the conflict and the number of victims However, since the truce was announced in April, children have been killed or maimed according to numbers verified by the United Nations Actual figures are likely to be much higher more needs to be done to protect children in Yemen UNICEF calls on all parties to the conflict to respect terms of the truce fully and to continue efforts for a sustainable peace in Yemen All parties to the conflict must protect civilians everywhere and spare no effort to remove landmines and unexploded ordnance
