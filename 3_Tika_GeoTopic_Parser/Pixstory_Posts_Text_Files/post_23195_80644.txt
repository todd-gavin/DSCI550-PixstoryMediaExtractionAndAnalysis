A journalist was shot dead yesterday in Mexico and the fifth reporter killed in similar circumstances in the country since the beginning of this year according to a count by AFP based on data from Reporters Sans Frontieres Heber Lopez Vazquez head of the news site Noticias Web in the southern state of Oaxaca he was shot while he was in his car, judicial sources told Milenio TV channel Two suspects were arrested while trying to flee and their weapons were also found It is not yet clear who ordered the crime
