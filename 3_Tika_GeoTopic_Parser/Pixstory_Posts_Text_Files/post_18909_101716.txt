Good news from the Alessandria hospital where Stefano Tacconi has been hospitalized since last April, former goalkeeper of Juventus and the national team. He was transferred from the Intensive Care unit to the Neurosurgery department thanks to improvements. He acquired respiratory autonomy and a good state of vigilance. This allows us to be the more optimistic it will be necessary to manage some problems in the Neurosurgery department but we can hypothesize the transfer to a rehabilitation facility in a couple of weeks Words from the director of Neurosurgery of the Alessandria hospital Andrea Barbanera A few days ago the transfer of the department had been anticipated by the son of Stefano Tacconi Andrea After defeating another fever, he will probably leave the intensive care unit during the day and be moved to a new ward
