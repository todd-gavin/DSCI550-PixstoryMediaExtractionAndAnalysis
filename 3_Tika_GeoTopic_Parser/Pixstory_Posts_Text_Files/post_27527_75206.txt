It had been an epic clash and other times between the two great rulers of the World Rally Championship in the 21st century And even today on the last day of the WRC in Monte Carlo the challenge proved to be unforgettable Sebastien Ogier against Sebastien Loeb protagonists of a crazy duel that annihilates more than anything else all the young opponents called upon to take up their legacy precisely in the season in which the two legends will be employed part-time by Ford and Toyota In the end, it was Loeb who prevailed, back from second place in the Dakar and therefore triumphant in the first round of the World Championship with hybrid cars All this not without surprises with the puncture that caused Ogier to lose the lead in any case second ahead of Craig Breen in the last special stages The WRC returns in just over a month with Rally Finland scheduled from to February
