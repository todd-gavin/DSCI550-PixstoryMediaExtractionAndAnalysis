Jigar Makwana is a PhD Research scholar, Lecturer, Blogger and Poet. He hails from Ahmadabad, Gujarat, India. He is featured in “World’s First Book on Haiku Poetry” and many anthologies. He is the editor and compiler of “The Rising Channel’s Anthology”. His writing is based on real events and sometimes he expresses supernatural elements in his writings. You can visit his blog at
