A special afternoon today at Loyola Marymount University The Bianconeri on the pitch for the afternoon training session received a visit from the youngsters from Street Soccer USA a Juventus partner in the United States in the Gioca Con Me project The participants about twenty in all played together on the Juve training ground in anticipation of the team s arrival Today s get together strengthened a collaboration with Street Soccer USA a non profit organisation set up in which aims to help children and young people through football allowing them to cultivate positive relationships and develop abilities and fundamental skills in life The collaboration began in in New York at the Street Soccer Times Square Cup an event that will resume next September after a two year hiatus
