The Taliban are preparing to hold a celebration on the anniversary of the departure of the last American soldier from Afghanistan
The Taliban are preparing to hold a celebration on Wednesday, on the occasion of the first anniversary of the departure of the last American soldier fr Afghanistan. In the photos, it can be seen that the Taliban have illuminated parts of the city.
The United States of America ended its 20-year mission in Afghanistan last year on the 9th of Sabla, corresponding to August 31, with the departure of its last soldier from Kabul Airport.
