Sydney, the capital of New South Wales and one of Australia's largest cities, is best known for the Sydney Opera House, which has a distinctive sail-like design and is located on the harbourfront. The massive Darling Harbour and the smaller Circular Quay port are waterside hotspots, with the arched Harbour Bridge and renowned Royal Botanic Garden nearby. The Skywalk on Sydney Tower's outdoor platform provides 360-degree views of the city and suburbs. In a global survey of 450 cities, Sydney was named the best city in Australia for quality of life and the tenth best in the world. When people think of Australia's cities, Sydney is at the top of their list. A well-known location is filled with sun, sea, and skyscrapers.
