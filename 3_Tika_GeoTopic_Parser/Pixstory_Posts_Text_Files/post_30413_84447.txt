The first series of sanctions decided by FIFA against Russia had been judged too mild and today the highest body of world football ratified in a joint statement with UEFA that it had suspended the Russian national team from the play-offs for the World Cup in Qatar as well as the Russian clubs from all European cups The presidents of Fifa and UEFA hope that the situation in Ukraine will improve significantly and quickly so that football can again be a vector of unity and peace among peoples, the statement reads The Russian Football Union reacted by labeling the decision as discriminatory while UEFA added that it had terminated all sponsorship contracts with the Gazprom company A similar decision was taken by the basketball Euroleague Russian clubs were suspended from the top continental basketball competition and also from the Eurocup
