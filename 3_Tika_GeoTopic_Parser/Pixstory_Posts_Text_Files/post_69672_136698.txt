RM of BTS and ENHYPEN are headed to Japan together for an upcoming reality show On September the years old BTS idol along with seven members of boy group ENHYPEN departed for Japan from the Gimpo Airport As reported by HYBE LABELS JAPAN both of them will be attending AUDITION The Howling FINAL ROUND the idol survival program run by the company to debut their next boy band RM will be there as the representative of the group while members of ENHYPEN will return to cheer the trainees of boy band who will debut as a new boy group later in the day The reality show will be aired at PM JST broadcasting live from Tokyo on September Previously BTS had appeared on the program with a video message and a BTS challenge for the trainees of the show
