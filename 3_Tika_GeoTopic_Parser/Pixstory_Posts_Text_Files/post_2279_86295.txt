Initial trends of counting of votes in Punjab suggest that the state is slipping out of the Congress party s hands and going into the lap of the Aam Aadmi Party AAP In the counting of postal ballots the AAP has taken a lead over the Congress and if the trend continues the exit poll projections might come true All exit polls had predicted a clear AAP victory in Punjab If that happens Punjab will be the second state where the AAP has uprooted the Congress after Delhi AAP leaders had claimed right through the campaigning that their party is a credible alternative to the Congress in states and nationally
