Max Verstappen won the first edition of the Miami GP The Dutchman started third and overtook Carlos Sainz at the start and Charles Leclerc after laps showing a pace superior to the Ferraris The Safety Car that entered the lap after the accident between Pierre Gasly and Lando Norris reunited the group but Leclerc failed to carry out an attack on Verstappen finishing second ahead of Sainz who resisted the attacks of Sergio Perez The Monegasque still maintains the leadership of the championship with a points advantage Fifth and sixth place for the Mercedes of George Russell and Lewis Hamilton Next race in two weeks in Spain
