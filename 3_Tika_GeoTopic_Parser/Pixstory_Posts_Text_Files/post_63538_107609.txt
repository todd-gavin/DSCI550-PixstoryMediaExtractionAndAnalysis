The Delhi Police accompanied a team of prominent Congress leaders to meet with the Lieutenant Governor after the party staged a protest outside his residence on Thursday On Wednesday the party demanded action against police highhandedness demonstrated at its offices On Thursday afternoon chaos broke out as Congressmen scaled barricades and police used water cannons to scatter the protesters This occurs a day after the party claimed that Delhi Police officers barged into the headquarters and assaulted leaders who were protesting the purported money laundering investigation of Rahul Gandhi by the Enforcement Directorate ED
