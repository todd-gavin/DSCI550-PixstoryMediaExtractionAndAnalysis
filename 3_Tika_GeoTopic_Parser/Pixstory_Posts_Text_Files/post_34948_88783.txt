Lautaro Martinez was found positive for Covid and missed the call with the Argentina national team The positive swab was performed a few hours after departure and the Nerazzurri company communicated it with a press release FC Internazionale Milano announces that Lautaro Martinez tested positive for Covid later to the test carried out on Sunday before leaving for Argentina where the commitments with his national team are scheduled The forward is already in isolation and if the swab tests negative in time he could return to training in view of the championship match against Juventus With the national team Lautaro Martinez should have played two matches against Venezuela and Ecuador valid for the qualifications for the next World Cup in Qatar
