Singer Jessi’s new track “ZOOM” music video has surpassed 10 million views on YouTube. The video first recorded 2 million views in the first 12 hours and 4 million views in two days. It has also maintained in the TOP 10 on YouTube’s Rising Popularity Music.

“ZOOM” is a trap hip-hope genre track that expresses the people of today living in the moment to take pictures. The addictive hook and melody has also been well received by domestic and international listeners.
