Words Of Soul Community completed their 1st year anniversary by organising one special event in which 4 esteemed speakers where invited which included Celebrity Best Selling Author Abhishek Kapoor,Gobal Star Ka’Ron Gaines,Celebrity illustrator Danna Love and Celebrity Entrepreneur Durlav Sarkar was invited as the Special Chief Guest of the event.This event was for the launch of 1st Edition Of Words Of Soul’s official magazine which was based on the topic ‘LGBTQ+’ and the cover star of it was Zoya Lobo(India’s First Transgender Photo Journalist) who was also present in this special event organised by Words Of Soul on the occasion of the 1st Year Anniversary of the community and it was hosted by the Co-Founder,Lucky Pandey & Maria Shaikh.
