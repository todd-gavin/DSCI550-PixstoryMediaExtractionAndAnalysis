MBC’s new drama ‘Big Mouth’ has marked its highest ratings yet! The new Lee Jong Suk and Girls’ Generation’s YoonA starrer has been successfully recording an increase in ratings with every new episode so far. The noir drama continued this streak by marking an average nationwide rating of 8.6 percent with its fourth episode, which aired on August 6. This is an increase from its previous personal best of 7.6 percent, earned with its third episode. In the metropolitan area, ‘Big Mouth’ recorded 8.7 percent.
