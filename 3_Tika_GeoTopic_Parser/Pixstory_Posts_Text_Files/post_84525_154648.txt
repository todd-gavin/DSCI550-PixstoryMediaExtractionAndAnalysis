Floating post office L is pictured at world famous Dal Lake in Srinagar on October This two centuries old floating post office claimed to be world s only floating post office was started in the British era and still continues to deliver letters and couriers for years to the people living on the Dal lake The floating post office was inaugurated in by Omar Abdullah the then Chief Minister of the state of Jammu amp Kashmir along with Sachin Pilot Minister of State for Communications and IT The post office is a major attraction for tourists for sending letters and post cards at large and the services offered in the post office include internet facility and international phone calls The postman Mohammad Ismail working in the floating post office for years has been delivering letters in the shikara boat everyday to people living on the lake
