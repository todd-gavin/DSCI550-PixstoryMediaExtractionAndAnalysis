Gabriel Martinelli has been a key player for Mikel Arteta’s Arsenal this season. Labelling him the best left winger in the 2022/23 Premier League campaign would not be an unfair suggestion.Yesterday proved once again how good the Brazilian is - and why so many people rate him highly.At just 21 years of age, the sky really is the limit for Arsenal’s number 11 - and many top clubs will undoubtedly have their eyes on such an explosive young talent.However, Arsenal have been handed a boost in their efforts to extend Martinelli’s contract, with the player confirming his interest in staying in North London after yesterday’s win against Liverpool…🗣️ “Of course, I want to stay [at Arsenal]. We are talking. Let’s see what is going to happen, but I want to stay, of course.”
