The United States defeated winless Austria 7-0 on Saturday to improve to 3-0 in group play at the world junior championship. Wyatt Kaiser and Carter Mazur each scored twice. 

US News reported that for the reigning champion Americans, Matt Coronato, Red Savage, and Sasha Pastujov also scored goals. Remington Keopple made four saves in the third session after Andrew Oke had stopped 11 shots in the opening two frames. 

U.S. coach Nate Leaman stated, "We knew we had to get off to a good start today. "Today's timetable was different, thus it was crucial that we rapidly adapt. I'm pleased with how the team began and continued the first period. 

The United States' last Group B match comes on Sunday versus Sweden.
