Ingredients cup unsweetened cocoa powder cups unsweetened almond milk teaspoon vanilla extract tablespoon maple syrup or agave nectar pinch of salt optional tablespoons vegan marshmallows or chocolates chopped optional Instructions Preheat your oven to degrees Fahrenheit Line an x inch baking dish with parchment paper In a large bowl mix together the cocoa powder almond milk vanilla extract maple syrup or agave nectar and salt if using Stir in the vegan marshmallows or chocolates if desired Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish Bake for minutes until the hot cocoa is thick and bubbly Let cool slightly before serving
