On Sunday, Bulgaria will host Georgia in their second UEFA Nations League group stage match
at the Stadium Ludogorets Arena.

Bulgaria began their League C campaign with a 1-1 draw against North Macedonia on Thursday, while Georgia thrashed Gibraltar 4-0.

The host squad will attempt to bounce back from the draw with two more games left this month.

Meanwhile, Georgia will go to Razgrad hoping to build on their recent victory.
