Cia Agricoltori Milano in collaboration with MM Spa presents Balconiamo an initiative aimed at increasing the presence of greenery on Milanese balconies to help improve the well-being of citizens educate them in the care and recognition of cultivated plants create biodiversity and propose a new aesthetic of the city The goal ofbalconiamo is to create and spread the greenery present in Milan in a more homogeneous way and improve knowledge of the city&#39;s immense plant heritage by actively participating in the vitality of the ecosystem and reaping the benefits that derive from it of which Cia has already carried out two important initiatives Arms lent to agriculture in collaboration with the Liceo Classico Parini dedicated to young people and the Guide to the trees of Milan which aims to make known the rich Milanese arboreal heritage
