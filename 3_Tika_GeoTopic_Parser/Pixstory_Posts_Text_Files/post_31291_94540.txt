The Dyson brand has been synonymous with ingenious technological innovation for decades. Yet its success began with well-built prototypes and discarded by the founder in just four years before arriving at a satisfactory solution for his famous cyclonic vacuum cleaner Today for the first time James Dyson talks about himself in his autobiography Innovation My story by revealing the source of inspiration behind his famous inventions, the secrets to starting a successful business and many anecdotes in a book projected towards an extremely original vision of the future
