India recently lost the music legend Bappi Lahiri who brought disco beats in focus during the period of the 80s and 90s.Heavy gold jewellery was his permanent outfit and he often wore it in hands and neck. Fans across the world, often identified with his look that consist of gold.Fans are often curious to know about his obsession with jewellery, therefore, once on being asked about the matter, the singer revealed the reason.

Once in an interview, the music maestro unveiled that his obsession with gold comes from legendary American singer Elvis Presley. As he saw and loved watching the singer wearing gold chains during his initial period of life.After watching Elvis at that look Bappi Da got fully inspired and always wished to be like him, once he earned a lot of name and fame.
