Nigeen Lake also known as Nageen Lake is a slightly eutrophic lake that can be found in Srinagar Jammu and Kashmir India It is connected to the Dal lake by a strait and is occasionally seen as a part of it It also has a waterway known as Nallah Amir Khan that connects to the Khushal Sar and Gil Sar lakes Willow and poplar trees are abundantly planted all around the Nigeen lake As a result it has earned the nickname nageena which means the diamond in the ring A regional spelling of the same word is nigeen To the west of the Dal lake close to the Hari Parbat hilltop is where the lake is situated
