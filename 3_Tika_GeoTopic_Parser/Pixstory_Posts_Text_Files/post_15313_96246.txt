A year-old woman died after an injection made at home in the Modena area by an alleged beautician at the moment on the run who would have given her the treatment with the aim of lifting her breasts The alarm because too often real interventions and medical treatments are confused with small aesthetic remedies is raised by Confestetica which clarifies that in this case injections to lift the breasts are precisely a medical treatment that cannot be practiced abusively by a beautician It remains to be clarified whether the thirty-five year old had an anaphylactic shock or what else caused his death and for this reason an autopsy was ordered
