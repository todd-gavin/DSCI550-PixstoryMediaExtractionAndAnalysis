By Shinjinee Majumder An image that shows people offering namaz outside the Bandra Railway Station in Mumbai has gone viral amid the ongoing Hanuman Chalisa row and on the occasion of Ramadan The image has been shared by several users on Twitter Link Link Alt News also received several fact check requests on WhatsApp about the image Fact check Through a reverse image search of the image in circulation Alt News found it on Getty Images and it is dated August It was taken during the month of Ramadan in outside the Bandra Railway Station An article dated August published by The Atlantic had also used the image It must be noted that in the Chief Minister of Maharashtra was Congress leader Prithviraj Chavan The image is thus being falsely linked to the ongoing Hanuman Chalisa row in Shiv Sena led Maharashtra Hanuman Chalisa Row Politician Raj Thackeray s public ultimatum to Maharashtra CM Uddhav Thackeray to remove loudspeakers from mosques sparked a controversy in the state Raj Thackeray warned the government to remove the loudspeakers by May or his party would play Hanuman Chalisa outside mosques This was supported by MP MLA couple Navneet Kaur Rana and Ravi Rana who then publicly announced that they would chant Hanuman Chalisa outside Uddhav Thackeray s residence Matoshree This was met with a lot of protests by Shiv Sena workers who even flocked outside the couple s residence to prevent them from going out The couple later withdrew their plan but an FIR was filed against them and they were arrested on April under multiple IPC sections including sedition and charges of creating enmity between groups
