After a long and patient wait, Brahmastra movie track, Rasiya is finally out now. Although the film is better know for its famous ‘Kesariya’ track, the audience went crazy when they heard ‘Rasiya’ in the theatre. Fans have been waiting ever since for the release of the full track on Youtube and today, finally the full version of the romantic song is out, specially on public demand. The love track was composed by Pritam and sang by the melodious Shreya Ghoshal and initially it was supposed to be just a sample track and the love it received from the audience was totally unexpected for the makers of the film.
