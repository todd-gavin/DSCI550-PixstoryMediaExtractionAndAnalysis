36th National Games: Hockey Results - 9th October
Haryana registered a convincing victory against Jharkhand in the 1st semi-final match of the Women’s Hockey event. Meanwhile, Punjab defeated Madhya Pradesh to storm into the summit clash of the competition. 
Haryana and Punjab will lock horns in the final of the Women’s Hockey event on Tuesday, October 11. Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand will play for the bronze medal on the same day.
Women’s
Semi-Final 1 - Haryana beat Jharkhand 5-2
Semi-Final 2 - Punjab beat Madhya Pradesh 2-1
