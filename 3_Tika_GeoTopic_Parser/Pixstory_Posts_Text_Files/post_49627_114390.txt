The Colorado s democratic Governor has mandated that his state will not cooperate with any investigations into abortions led by other states Jared Polis ratified an executive order on Wednesday vowing that the western US state will not help other states in criminal or civil investigations used to deter people from accessing abortions The executive order adds safety for individuals and organizations that provide abortions as well as for individuals achieving an abortion including people who have traveled from other states We are taking needed action to protect and defend individual freedom and protect the privacy of Coloradans said Polis in a statement obtained by the Colorado Sun Nearly two weeks after the supreme court overturned Roe v Wade dissolving a nearly year precedent of federal abortion protections states have taken steps to protect reproductive rights for that occupancy in the state or those traveling for reproductive healthcare services Source The Guardian
