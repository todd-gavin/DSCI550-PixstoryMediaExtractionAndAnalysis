If you're hosting events, you need feedback from your audience. More thoughts from our last blog post. Audience members may be reluctant to give you their real thoughts on your events for various reasons. So, you need to create an atmosphere of confidence and trust with your audience.

Just as you put effort into your event, you need to put effort into how you go about getting feedback. With feedback, you need to see what works better for your audience. You will need to adjust your ways of getting feedback that suit your audience. The more you can improve the quality of the feedback you get, the better your events can get....
