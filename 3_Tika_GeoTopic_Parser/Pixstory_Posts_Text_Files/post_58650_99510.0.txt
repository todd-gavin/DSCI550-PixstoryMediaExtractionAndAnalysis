These are the latest pictures of the newly renovated and expanded campus of the ancient Kashi Vishwanath temple in Varanasi, the Lok Sabha constituency of Prime Minister Narendra Modi. 

Vishwanath Dham is the dream project of Prime Minister Modi. 

Kashi Vishwanath Temple is one of the most famous temple in Varanasi, also known as the Golden temple dedicated to the Lord Shiva. It was constructed in the year 1780 by the Maratha monarch, Maharani Ahilyabai Holkar of the Indore.
