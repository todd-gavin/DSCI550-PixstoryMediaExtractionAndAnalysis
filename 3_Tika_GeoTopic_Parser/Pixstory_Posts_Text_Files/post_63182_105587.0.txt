According to the state health department, Maharashtra recorded 1,881 new coronavirus cases today, a significant increase from the 1,036 infections reported the day before. 

In Mumbai alone, 1,242 new cases were registered, up from 676 on Monday. 
During the day, one case of B.A.5 variant was also discovered, according to the official release. 
Maharashtra had 1,635 new cases as of February 19. Following that, the numbers began to fall. 
For the previous few days, Covid instances have been on the rise across the state. 

The state administration also published a public recommendation last week encouraging people to wear masks in all public locations.
