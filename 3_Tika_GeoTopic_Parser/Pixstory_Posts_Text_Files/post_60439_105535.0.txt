1. My Mister (2018)
A man in his 40’s withstands the weight of life. A woman in her 20’s goes through different experiences, but also withstands the weight of her life. The man and woman get together to help each other.

2. Black Dog: Being A Teacher (2019)
After failing her teaching certification again and again, Ko Ha-neul finally passes an interview for a temporary role at Daechi High. As a new teacher, Ha-neul struggles with the intricacies of teaching as well as departmental strife.

3. The Light In Your Eyes (2019)
A tragic accident causes a woman to lose all her time before she can use it up; a man gives up all of his radiant moments and lives a lethargic life.

4. 18 Again (2020)
A 37-year-old Dae-young is on the verge of being divorced with Da-jung finds himself inside his 18-year-old body.

5. Itaewon Class (2020)
An ex-con and his friends fight to make their ambitious dreams for their street bar a reality.

6. Move To Heaven (2021)
A young man with Asperger syndrome and his uncle work as trauma cleaners, clearing out the possessions of dead people, uncovering the stories they left behind and telling them to loved ones.

7. Navillera (2021)
A 70-year-old man with a dream and a 23-year-old man with a gift lift each other out of harsh realities and rise to the challenges of becoming ballet dancers.

8. Tomorrow (2022)
The series depicts the gri reapers who guided the dead to save the people who want to die.

9. My Liberation Notes (2022)
It tells the story of three siblings and a secretive stranger who want to escape from their dead-end life.

10. Our Blues (2022)
The series revolves around the sweet and bitter life of people standing at the end, climax, or beginning of life, and depicts their stories in an omnibus format against the backdrop of Jeju Island.
