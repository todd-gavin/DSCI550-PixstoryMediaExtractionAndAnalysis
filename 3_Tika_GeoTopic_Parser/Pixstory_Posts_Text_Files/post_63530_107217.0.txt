According to the latest report from Kasturba Hospital Laboratory, Mumbai has reported the first cases of three patients with the BA.4 variant and one patient with the BA.5 variant. These patients range in age from 14 to 24 May, with two 11-year-old girls and two males in the 40 to 60-year-old age group. All of these patients were treated in their own homes. A total of 1,885 Covid-19 instances were recorded in Maharashtra on Monday, with 1,118 of them coming from Mumbai.
