The final 10 teams vying for this year's championship will compete in the second round of the fifth iteration of the Kashmir Premier League (KPL), which began today with a solemn opening ceremony at the Delhi Public School Srinagar. 
After more than 200 teams participated in Phase-I with more than 150 league and playoff matches, the top 10 teams from Kashmir's 10 districts are now competing for the KPL 2022 title. All-league and knockout games will be played in the 10 districts that the clubs represent before the final, which is scheduled for August 20, 2022.
