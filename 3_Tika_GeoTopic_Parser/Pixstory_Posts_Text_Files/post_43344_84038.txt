The number of tigers has decreased substantially over the last few years The major causes of its population reduction are poaching habitat loss and human wildlife conflicts Tiger conservation benefits not just tigers but also other co habiting animals As the top predator at the top of the food chain tigers play a critical role in the health and variety of an ecosystem Tigers keep the number of wild animals in control ensuring a healthy balance between prey herbivores and the plants on which they graze and if the tiger went extinct the entire system would collapse
