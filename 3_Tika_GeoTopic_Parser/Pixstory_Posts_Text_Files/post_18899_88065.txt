Roger Federer will donate a thousand euros through his foundation to help Ukrainian children continue their studies at this very difficult time. He wrote it in a message on social media in which he said he was heartbroken thinking about the people who are suffering right now. he and his wife are on the side of peace For our part we will do everything possible to assist those in need of help for those millions of children who are currently unable to go to school and who cannot continue studying We will help them deal with this traumatic experience Through the Federer Foundation we will support War Child Holland with a donation of 1,000 euros to give Ukrainian children the opportunity to continue studying
