2gether❣️
2gether is Thai BL series aired in 2020. Bright and Win really had a very romantic chemistry between them, that even the audience can feel it👯‍♂️. The Characters of Sarawat and Tine really stole my heart 💖. Unlike other BL series 2gether goes in a slow pace. And I recommend this one for first timers who wanna experience BL series. 👍🏽
