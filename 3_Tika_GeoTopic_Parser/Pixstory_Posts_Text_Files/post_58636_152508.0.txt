Amid crowd of more than 50000 devotees, the effigies of Rawan, Meghnath and Kumbhkaran were burnt on the vast open ground in BLW in Varanasi this evening. As the effigies caught fire by the firing arrow, released by Lord Rama, effigies started catching flame. The entire venue sounded, Jai Shri Ram
Soon, effigies were completely burnt.These effigies had been made by a muslim family, engaged for last three decades. Due to corona pendemic, religious event were not organized for two years, so this time, there was much enthusiasm among devotees to witness it. Not only railway staff but devotees from different parts of city came here to witness the religious event.
