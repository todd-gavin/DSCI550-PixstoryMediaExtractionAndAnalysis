Khilan Marg is an offbeat destination that is kilometres from Gulmarg It is the right blend of natural beauty energizing serenity and thrilling sports activities The aromatic flowers blooming during spring offer a warm welcome to the visitors Winters are all about snow sports like skiing Khilan Marg is a secluded destination of all the tourist places near Srinagar as to reach here one must walk or take a pony ride from Gulmarg
