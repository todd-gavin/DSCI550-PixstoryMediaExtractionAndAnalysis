Slovenia, capital of world cycling Two champions from the small Baltic country win the two classic Italian and French stage races on the same day Pogacar enjoys the last stage of the Corsa dei due mari won by the German Bauhaus on the finish line in San Benedetto del Tronto and repeats the success last year In the standings he closes with a margin over the Danish Vingegaard and with the Spanish Landa In the French race instead Primoz Roglic has to thank his teammate Van Aert who in the final of the last stage brings his group back on the fugitive the British Simon Yates and gives him the triumph In the end Roglic will finish with Yates and the Colombian Daniel Martinez in the standings
