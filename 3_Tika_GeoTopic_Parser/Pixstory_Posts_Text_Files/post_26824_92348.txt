Yesterday afternoon in Milan it was quite difficult to get an appointment for a manicure or a hairdresser. Stylists, web stars, singers and fashion journalists were all intent on dressing up for the charity event of the season. The dinner organized by the Monzino Cardiology Center Foundation and the European Institute of Oncology created in collaboration with Camera della Moda an event to celebrate the extraordinary work of doctors and researchers in the treatment and research to prevent heart diseases and cancer that are increasingly curable From Umberta Gnutti Beretta patron and collector of contemporary art to Michela d Angelo influencer passing for the stylist Massimo Giorgetti and the journalist Jacopo Bedussi All in their best clothes but above all all aware of the importance of the evening and of scientific research
