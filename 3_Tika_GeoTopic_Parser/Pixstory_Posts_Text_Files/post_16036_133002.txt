Abstract Background Alexithymia is common in patients with type diabetes mellitus T DM Although the estimated prevalence of alexithymia in patients with T DM is widely reported these results have not been synthesized Aim To systematically assess the prevalence and characteristics of alexithymia in patients with T DM Methods We searched for relevant publications in PubMed Embase Web of Science China Knowledge Resource Integrated Database Wanfang Database Chinese Biomedical Database and Weipu Database The prevalence of alexithymia the mean scores and standard deviations of the total scale of the item Toronto Alexithymia Scale TAS were pooled using random effects meta analysis in Stata with studies stratified by study location in this meta analysis Results This meta analysis included thirteen articles Pooled prevalence of alexithymia TAS total scores were CI and the prevalence of alexithymia was higher in China CI compared with non China CI The pooled mean score for the TAS total scale was CI Leave one out analysis showed that none of the studies significantly impacted the overall pooled results Conclusions This meta analysis indicated a high prevalence of alexithymia in patients with T DM Thus clinicians need to be aware of and assess appropriately for alexithymia in patients with T DM Sources
