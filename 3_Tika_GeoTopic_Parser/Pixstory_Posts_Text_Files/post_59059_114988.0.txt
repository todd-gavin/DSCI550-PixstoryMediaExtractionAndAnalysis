After the Release of Prithviraj, the next movie by Akshay Kumar is already ready to release on 11 August 2022. 

Raksha Bandhan is an upcoming Indian Hindi-language comedy-drama film directed by Aanand L. Rai and written by Himanshu Sharma and Kanika Dhillon. It is produced by Zee Studios, Colour Yellow Productions and Cape of Good Films. The film stars Akshay Kumar and Bhumi Pednekar. The music is composed by Himesh Reshammiya.
