Charlotte Casiraghi face of Chanel and excellent horsewoman walked the catwalk on horseback with an elegant black jacket, boots and the classic riding hat Thus opens the Haute Couture show of the French brand A simple, elegant and perhaps too clean collection for fashionistas yet accustomed to the follies of Karl Lagerfeld But Virginie Viard once historical collaborator of the Kaiser of fashion and now creative director has never exaggerated and this is why Chanel customers love her collections I am sure that every look that I have labeled as banal will be ordered by some wife of a bored Russian oligarch with a passion for throwing stellar parties
