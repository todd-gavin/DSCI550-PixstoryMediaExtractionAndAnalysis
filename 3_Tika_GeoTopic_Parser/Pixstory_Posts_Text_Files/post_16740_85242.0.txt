Ananya Panday has been one of the leading divas in Bollywood who has managed to leave a mark in the industry. She recently stated that she looks up to Kareena Kapoor Khan and Alia Bhatt as an actress.Ananya was all praises for Kareena and Alia and their impressive line of work. “They don’t shy away from any kind of cinema; they do a little bit of everything. And they’re so good at what they do. I don’t think that there are any roles that they’ve done that I feel like I could have done justice to; because they’ve been phenomenal in everything,” she added. Ananya also emphasised that she wishes to play a character similar to Geet from Kareena’s Jab We Met given the subtle arc in the character, the subtle nuances which left her impressed.
