Sonam Kapoor and Anand Ahuja welcomed their first child on August 20. On Tuesday, the actor celebrated her baby’s first month anniversary with a special cake. Sonam took to Instagram Stories and shared a photo of her son’s Boss Baby themed cake, and thanked a Mumbai bakery for the creation.The white cake for Sonam and Anand’s baby was personalised with a message in blue that read ‘Boss Baby Kapoor Ahuja’. The cake was decorated with white angel wings and cut-outs of the famous cartoon character from the Boss Baby franchise. There was also a giant cut-out of the number one in blue. The cake came with the message, “30 days of love. Happy 1 month.” Sharing the picture on Instagram Stories, Sonam Kapoor wrote, “@cocoatease thank you for my baby's one month birthday cake.”
