Ghulam Nabi Azad resigns It is a huge Shoker for Congress as the veteran leader Ghulam Nabi Azad resigned from all roles including primary membership of the Congress Party Senior Congress leader Ghulam Nabi Azad on Friday resigned from all party positions including its primary membership delivering another blow to the embattled party that has seen a series of leaders leave it In a five page letter to Congress president Sonia Gandhi he told he does so with a heavy heart The Congress he said has lost both the will and the ability under the tutelage of the coterie that runs the AICC to fight for what is right for India Azad part of the G group seeking change in the party said Before starting a Bharat Jodo yatra the leadership should have undertaken a Congress Jodo yatra the veteran leader said in his letter
