What is food freedom?

Indeed Freedom is a state of mind, but also a choice made by the body to eat the kind of food it needs and craves.

To eat with a conscious and mindful palate is all that is required for a fitter you. Here are some actions that will liberate you to food freedom. 

1. LIVING A BALANCE LIFE WITH NO RESTRICTION
2. EATING WHAT YOUR BODY CRAVES
3. MENTAL WELL BEING
4. NEVER WORRYING ABOUT FOOD
5. NEVER SAYING NO TO SOCIAL EVENTS
6. KNOWING YOU ARE DOING YOUR BEST
