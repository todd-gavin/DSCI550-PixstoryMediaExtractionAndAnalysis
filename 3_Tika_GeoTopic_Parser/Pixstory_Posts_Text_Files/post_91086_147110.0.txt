Raju Srivastava: Actor by heart, comedian by careerThe legendary comic wasn’t just good at making us all laugh at his jokes but he had also tried his hands at actingIn fact he had shifted to Mumbai in 1980’s in quest of building an acting career. He got his first Bollywood break in a Rajashri Production film ‘Meine Pyar Kiya’ and since then he had nailed a lot of side roles in films like Baazigar, Aadmi Atthani Kharcha Ruppaiya, Meri Padosan. It was not until 2005, that he thought of trying his luck at stand up comedy. He took part in The Great Indian Laughter Challenge Championship and even won the position of second runner up. The rest is history.
