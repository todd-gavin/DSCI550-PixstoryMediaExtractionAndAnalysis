Massimo Giorgetti takes us into Space in search of a new dimension Inspired by the sky and one of the latest discoveries made in the sky a celestial body whose existence was predicted by physical models but which had never been observed before Emits radio signals every minute and located just a thousand light years away from us inside the Milky Way. The creative director of MSGM was struck by its beauty, its spherical shape surrounded by elliptical luminescence. where the Human becomes small and its certainties only hypotheses The collection entirely focused on these themes the star is at the center and winds its way through each garment for both day and evening
