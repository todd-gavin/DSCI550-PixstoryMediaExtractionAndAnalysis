A pancake or hot cake or flapjack is a flat cake often thin and round prepared from a starch based batter that may contain eggs milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan often frying with oil or butter It is a type of batter bread There are many different ways to make pancakes from sweet to savory there s something for everyone They are a great breakfast food because they re filling and provide energy for the day ahead Pancakes can be made with all sorts of ingredients including fruits vegetables nuts and seeds Pancakes toppings are endless you can go sweet with syrup or chocolate sauce or savory with bacon or sausage You can even turn pancakes into dessert by adding whipped cream and berries
