Noise pollution is an invisible danger It cannot be seen but it is always present both on land and under the sea It is an unwanted sound that affects the health and well being of humans and other organisms It creates disturbance and hinders our everyday life Sound is always measured in decibels The environment is filled with different sounds such as the rustling leaves to decibels a thunderclap decibels a siren to decibels The sound that is decibels or higher can harm an individual s ears Such sounds that exceed this level include lawn mowers subway trains and other such sources that are all around us Thus noise pollution impacts people on a daily basis and causes high blood pressure stress sleep disturbances and noise induced hearing loss
