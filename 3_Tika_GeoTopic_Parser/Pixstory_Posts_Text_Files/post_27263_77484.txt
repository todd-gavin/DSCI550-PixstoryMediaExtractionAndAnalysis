The new building designed by Stefano Boeri Architetti China is called Easyhome Huanggang Vertical Forest City Complex and represents a new type of Vertical Forest that combines open and closed balconies that break the regularity of the building and create a continuous and changing movement accentuated by the presence of trees and shrubs that thanks to this specific structure can grow freely in height following the design of the facade Located in the city of Huanggang in the province of Hubei has now welcomed the first lucky residents
