Ayushmaan Khurana and Ananya pandey starrer Dream Girl 2 teaser released. 
Both actors took their Instagram to share the teaser of their upcoming film Dream Girl 2. The movie star cast also includes Annu Kapoor, Paresh Rawal , Manoj Joshi, Vijay Raaz, Seema Pahwa and Rajpal Yadav. Ananya Pandey captioned the post, " Aapki Dream Girl Phir se aa rhi hai, millye Pooja se 29th June 2023 ki Eid par."Dream Girl was released on 2019 hit and starred Nushratt Bharuchha with Ayushmaan .
