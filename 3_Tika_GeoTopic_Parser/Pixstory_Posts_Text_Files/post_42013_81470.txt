The environment has a significant impact on a child s personal health and many of them are unaware of how much it impacts them Environmental factors can either cause or aggravate a variety of health problems Air pollution is a huge health threat that can lead to asthma cancer and other severe respiratory illnesses and many of these children are unaware of how to protect themselves from these diseases Students in grades four through six should be informed about the health risks posed by air pollution as well as learn at school how to reduce air pollution for their own health and that of future generations Biking and walking to school are examples of applications Thus we must protect it to protect ourselves
