When India's women's lawn bowls team earned their first-ever medal at the Commonwealth Games 2022 on Monday, history was being written. The Indian Women's Fours team in Lawn Bowls brought honour to the entire country by winning the competition's first-ever medal. They defeated New Zealand 16-13 in the semifinals to advance to the final. For the finals, India scored 17 to 10 to win the gold medal (south africa).
