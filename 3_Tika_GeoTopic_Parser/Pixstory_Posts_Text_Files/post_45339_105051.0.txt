Serbia and Slovenia both will coming off loses when they face each other in Group A UEFA Nations League in Rajko Mitić Stadium

Serbia and Slovenia are part of a cracking group, put along with Nordic countries Sweden and Normay in Group 4 for League B.

Serbia won only one of six Nations League matches in the 2020 campaign, something that they will be hoping to rectify this time around.

Slovenia have fared worse, missing out on qualification for the World Cup by finishing fourth in their group. They will be looking to perform better in the UEFA Nations League this time around.

Serbia vs Slovenia: Probable Playing XI

Serbia: Vanja Milinkovic-Savic (GK), Nikola Milenkovic, Milos Veljkovic, Strahinja Pavlovic, Nemanja Radonjic, Nemanja Maksimovic, Sergej Milinkovic-Savic, Filip Kostic, Dusan Tadic, Andrija Zivkovic, Aleksandar Mitrovic

Slovenia: Jan Oblak (GK), Zan Karnicnik, Jaka Bijol, Miha Mevlja, Petar Stojanovic, Jan Gorenc Stankovic, Adam Gnezda Cerin, Gregor Sikosek, Domen Crnigoj, Andraz Sporar, Benjamin Sesko

Serbia vs Slovenia: Predictions

Serbia and Slovenia have met twice in the past, both enjoying a win towards themselves. Serbia are generally good at home, and their squad has enough top leagues quality to see off Slovenia. 

Prediction: Serbia 2 - 0 Slovenia
