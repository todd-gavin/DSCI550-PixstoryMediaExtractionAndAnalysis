Queen Elizabeth II on postal stamps Jamaica chocolate and violet The Jamaica chocolate and violet was a planned but unissued Jamaican postage stamp The stamp s design was identical to the King George VI stamp issued in which depicted a scene of workers rolling cigars by hand but with the vignette image of King George VI replaced with that of Queen Elizabeth II Although the stamp was abandoned after printing and not issued there are seven copies in existence It was replaced by a stamp depicting Jamaica s coat of arms
