Shakhtar Donetsk coach Roberto De Zerbi and his staff composed of assistant coach Davide Possanzini goalkeeping coach Giorgio Bianchi trainers Vincenzo Teresa Agostino Tibaudi and Marcattilio Marcattili and assistants Michele Cavalli and Paolo Bianco are currently still in Ukraine and are in a hotel in Kiev The staff had in fact returned on Sunday after the retreat held in Belek in Turkey with the team to prepare for the start of the championship which for them was scheduled for tomorrow According to reports from the Agi agency, the group followed the advice of the Italian embassy of avoid unnecessary travel and stock up on water and food
