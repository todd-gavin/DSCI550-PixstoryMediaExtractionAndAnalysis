Day 1 - IPL Auction 2022 - Ishan Kishan at ₹ 15.25 crore became the most expensive player  

Former Indian U-19 World Cup captain Ishan Kishan, 23, became the most expensive player in the IPL 2022 auction (Day 1) after Mumbai Indians got him under their fold for Rs 15.25 crore.

The wicket-keeper batsman, Kishan, made his debut in One Day International last year against Sri Lanka.
