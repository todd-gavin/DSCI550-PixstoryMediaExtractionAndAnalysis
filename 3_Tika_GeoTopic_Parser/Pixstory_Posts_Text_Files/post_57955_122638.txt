Korean society gives a lot of importance to age how you interact with people depends on your place in society Irrespective of whether you know the person or not it is customary to respect an elder person Calling someone whose elder than you by their first name is seen as disrespect Koreans do not call people who are older or have a higher position in work than them by name They use their title or ssi or nim after the name or title Older males females are always called oppa hyung unnie noona depending on what you gender is Oppa boys unnie girls for girls Noona girls hyung boys for boys Even if you are same age full name is used to call people you are not close to Only when a person is your family or is a close friend is first or a nick name used The way you address people can indicate your degree of closeness
