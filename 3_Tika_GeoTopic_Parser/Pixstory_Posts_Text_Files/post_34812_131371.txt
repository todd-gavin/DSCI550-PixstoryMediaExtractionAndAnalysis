With the war still going on, Ukraine is trying to start again And it is also doing it with football where possible Yesterday the first match of the top division was played since last February when the Russian attacks forced everything to be suspended Shakthar Donetsk and Metalis took the field in one stadium empty for security reasons The match finished with the result of In the next few days other matches will be played in the western part of the country which is safer but not entirely from Russian attacks All the stadiums will be closed and a system of sirens and air-raid shelters will be installed as well as protective weapons supplied by the Ukrainian army
