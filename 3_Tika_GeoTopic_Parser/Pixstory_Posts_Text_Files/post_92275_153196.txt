RBI to soon launch e rupee on pilot basis for limited use As the extent and scope of such pilot launches expand RBI will continue to communicate about the specific features and benefits of e rupee from time to time the central bank said in a concept note on Central Bank Digital Currency CBDC The concept note also discusses key considerations such as technology and design choices possible uses of the digital rupee and issuance mechanisms among others It examines the implications of the introduction of CBDC on the banking system monetary policy and financial stability and analyses privacy issues
