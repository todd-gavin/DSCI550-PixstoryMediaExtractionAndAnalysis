Fershta Hosseini is an Afghan film and theater actor in Iran. Like thousands of other Afghan boys and girls born in Tehran, she has succeeded in shining in cinema, theater and home theater, consolidating her position among her competitors.
Fershta Hosseini, an Afghan actor in Iranian cinema, won an honorary diploma at the country's 40th Fajr Film Festival.

Ms. Hosseini received the award for her supporting role in a film called "Girls' Band."

He was born to Afghan immigrant parents in Tehran in 1997 and has been in the cinema since 2009.
