The definition of Consumer right is the right to have information about the quality potency quantity purity price and standard of goods or services as it may be the case but the consumer is to be protected against any unfair practices of trade It is very essential for the consumers to know these rights Consumers rights under section of the consumer protection act are as follows Right to Safety Right to be Informed Right to Representation Right to Choose Right to be Heard Right to Seek Redressal Right to Consumer Education Right to Basic Needs Right to Healthy Environment
