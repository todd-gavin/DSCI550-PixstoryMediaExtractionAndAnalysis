Song Ha Yoon and Jo Byung Kyu are in talks to work in upcoming webtoon based drama History of Losers Plot A story about love and relationships that could turn anyone into a loser It will be portrayed in the form of a romantic comedy that shows the inherent pathetic sides of people and diverse thrilling moments that come with various forms of love The hyperrealistic depiction of humans at their lowest will be accompanied by laughter and empathy that goes beyond discomfort Seo Min Ki is a freshman in college who is majoring in Korean literature He is a hesitant person with a lot on his mind who sometimes makes bold actions Being a year old the result of his actions are always a series of clumsiness and mistakes However because he s someone who thinks a lot and has an ideological personality he always self reflects He is a simple outsider whose only wish is to drink with his friends and play games at internet cafes
