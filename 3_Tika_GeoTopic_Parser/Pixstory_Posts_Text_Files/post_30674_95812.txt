On the eve of the long-awaited Imola weekend, Ferrari announced that it had extended the contract expiring at the end of Carlos Sainz for another two years. The Spanish driver commented on the news. I am very happy to have renewed the contract with Scuderia Ferrari. that in Formula there is no better team to race for and after more than a year with the team I can say that this experience has gone even beyond my expectations Wearing the red uniform and representing the Scuderia is a great honor for me and I am proud to continue this adventure together Team principal Mattia Binotto added I have said several times that I am convinced that I have the best pair of drivers in Formula so reconfirming our trust in Carlos by extending his contract in the name of stability and continuity seemed to us an increasingly natural step race after race
