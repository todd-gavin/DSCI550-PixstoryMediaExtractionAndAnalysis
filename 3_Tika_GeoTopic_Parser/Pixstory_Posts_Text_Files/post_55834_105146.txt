Marchetta has a unique understanding of the mind of an adolescent Italian Australian Looking for Alibrandi was her first novel and it portrayed the story of an Italian Australian schoolgirl attending a snooty all girls school and her battle to fit in with both of her cultural identities As per CT Marchetta published three more young adult novels about the same psychology before releasing her first crime thriller set in the United Kingdom in to rave reviews In the novel Josephine Alibrandi the Australian Italian daughter of Italian immigrants is the protagonist of the tale Josie lives in Sydney and attends a Catholic high school where she is disillusioned by her snooty classmates cliques and social politics Her usually sophisticated sassy demeanour is challenged when she is overcome with the pressures of her senior year of high school
