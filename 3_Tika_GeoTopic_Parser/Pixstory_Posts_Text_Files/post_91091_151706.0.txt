Urmila Matondkar sets OTT debut with crime thriller series ‘Tiwari’.  The actress who entered the world of politics in the year 2020, was last seen on screen in a 2014 Marathi film, Ajoba.Tiwari’s director Saurabh Varma said, “The kind of varied graph Tiwari aka Urmila's character has in the show, we really couldn't think of anyone else but Urmila”Apart from the series being an OTT debut for Matondkar, it is also a comeback in the line after a gap of whole 8 years after rejecting over 50 scripts in the past few years.
