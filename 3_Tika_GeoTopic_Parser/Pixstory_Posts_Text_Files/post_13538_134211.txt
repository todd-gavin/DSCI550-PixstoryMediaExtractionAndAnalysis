Local Taliban officials have announced that the roof of the historic mosque in the old city of Ghazni is on the verge of collapse The recent rains have destroyed the roof of the Grand Mosque in the old city of Ghazni the first Muslim mosque in this province The Grand Mosque of the Old City of Ghazni with an area of about square meters and two floors is located in the west of the Old City of Ghazni According to historians this building is the first Muslim mosque in Ghazni city
