Why was BGMI removed from the Indian Google Play and Apple App Stores?

Following a government order, the popular combat mobile game Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI), which is comparable to PlayerUnknown's Battleground (PUBG), has been removed from the Google Play and Apple App Stores in India. PUBG was outlawed in India two years ago.
