Lazio and Spezia will be up against each other on Matchday 08 of the Serie A 2022-23 season. While Lazio are on the 4th spot of the Serie A points table, Speiza sit at the 12th position in the points table.
Lazio have been in a tremendous run ever since the inception of the Serie A 2022/23 season. With 4 wins, 2 draws, and just one loss this season, Lazio have been one of the dark horses in Serie A title contention.
Speiza, on the other hand, haven’t had a very good run but they have done well as per their standards. Out of the 7 games played this season, Speiza have clinched a couple of wins, have drawn a couple of games but have ended on the losing side thrice.
