While Services continue to advance, Pragnya Mohan triumphs in the triathlon with style.
By putting on a strong performance at the IIT Gandhinagar on Sunday, Pragnya Mohan of Gujarat defied all odds and won the 36th National Games Women's Individual Triathlon gold medal.
She easily outperformed the competition, clocking the third-fastest swimming time as well as the quickest timings in the cycling and running legs, leaving Mansi Mohite of Maharashtra trailing behind her more than 5 minutes. The bronze medal went to S Aarthi of Tamil Nadu.
Gujarat now boasts 12 gold medals overall, an excellent number after Pragnya's victory, who placed eighth in the previous Games.
