Phone Bhoot song Kaali Teri Gut out tomorrow,Katrina Kaif shares teaser.
Actress Katrina Kaif took her Instagram account and shared a teaser video of her song Kaali Teri Gut still from her upcoming movie Phone Bhoot. She announced that the song will be out on Tuesday. The video featured Siddhant Chaturvedi, Ishaan Khatter along with Katrina. The film is going to be released on November 4.
