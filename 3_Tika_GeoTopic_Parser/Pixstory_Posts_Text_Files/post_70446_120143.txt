The Goa Football Development Council GFDC Centre at Siolim celebrated the th anniversary of it s formation by having a brief celebration One can recall that on March the dream of football loving Goans was finally turned to reality when the then chief minister Manohar Parrikar declared football as the official State sport and announced the formation of the GFDC On July GFDC was formed and since then this day has been commemorated across all their centres in Goa
