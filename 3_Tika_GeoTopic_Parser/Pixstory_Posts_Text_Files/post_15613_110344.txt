With votes in favor against and abstentions, the City Council of Turin approved today an agenda that urges Parliament to approve a bill on the legalization of the production and consumption of cannabis and its derivatives. The document also commits the presidency to send the text approved by the presidents of the Chamber and Senate In presenting the document, the first signatory Silvio Viale, head of the Civic List for Turin, recalled that our country has already legalized the use of cannabinoids for medical purposes, while in an agreement between ministries a pilot project at the Military Pharmaceutical Chemical Plant in Florence for the production of inflorescences successfully completed in billion euros of which approximately attributable to the consumption of cannabis derivatives with the consequence that over a third of the prison population today consists of people detained for crimes related to the production, trafficking or consumption of narcotic substances
