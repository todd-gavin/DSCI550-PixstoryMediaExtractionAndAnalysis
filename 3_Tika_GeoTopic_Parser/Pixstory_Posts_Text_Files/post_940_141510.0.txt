Brian Lara names his daughter Sydney for a cricketing reason. 
Seen in the picture is Brian Lara with daughter Sydney. Lara, one of the greats of the game was so proud of his 277 for the West Indies at the SCG in 1993 that he named his first daughter after the city.
(Michael Holding later quipped cheekily that it was fortunate the double ton was not scored in Lahore).
