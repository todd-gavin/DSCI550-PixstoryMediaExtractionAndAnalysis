Aston Martin have signed newly crowned FIA Formula champion Felipe Drugovich as a Formula development driver for onwards Brazilian Drugovich clinched the F title at Monza on Saturday in the sprint race after nearest rival Theo Pourchaire failed to claim the necessary result to keep the title race alive The MP Motorsport driver has five wins this season including in the feature race at Zandvoort which all but secured him the title However the EuroFormula Open champion was not previously on the radar of any F teams despite the ongoing silly season about F seats Drugovich who signed his contract on Saturday after clinching the F title will take over Lance Stroll s car in FP at the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix as well as enjoy an extensive testing programme in the car from next season Becoming a member of the AMF Driver Development Programme is a fantastic opportunity for me and only adds to what has been an extremely enjoyable and successful season he said in a statement Winning in Formula has long been regarded as the best possible launchpad into a career in Formula One and I see my role at AMF as giving me all the tools to take that crucial next step For me will be a learning curve I will be working with the F team but my primary goal is to learn and develop as a driver I hope that will give me an opportunity to race in Formula One in the future
