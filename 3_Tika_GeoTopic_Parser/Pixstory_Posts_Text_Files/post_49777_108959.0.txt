‌Even though he is not a traditional activist his influence has become an influence that strives for hope among the generations of the LGBTQ+  community and people who believe in equality. He is not only the first openly gay politician to undertake a viable presidential campaign, the former South Bend, Indiana mayor did so without compromising his status as a proud gay man. 
‌
‌He is also the first openly gay Cabinet secretary in U.S. history. Nominated at age 38, he is also the youngest Cabinet member in the Biden administration and the youngest person ever to serve as Secretary of Transportation.
‌
‌He has been very vocal about his opinions and he favors amending civil rights legislation, including the Federal Equality Act so that LGBT Americans receive federal non-discrimination protections.
