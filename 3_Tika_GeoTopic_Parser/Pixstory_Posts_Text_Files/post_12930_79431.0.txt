Actor Ileana D'Cruz posted an unedited photo on her social media where she can be seen promoting body positivity and self love. 

She also informed that she have deleted all the apps that make one look slimmer and more tone. She captioned the photo as “So easy to get sucked into apps that get you to alter your body so effortlessly to make you look "slimmer', 'more toned', etc etc etc… Proud of the fact that I've deleted all those apps and chosen this instead. This is me and I'm embracing every inch, every curve, all of me," with a hashtag #Youarebeautiful
