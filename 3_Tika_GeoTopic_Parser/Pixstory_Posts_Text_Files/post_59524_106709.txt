The Indian Air Force IAF intends to develop roughly modern fighter jets in India and has began discussions with international aircraft manufacturers Top government sources told India Today that the Aatmanirbhar project will use Indian money for the first time to pay for about of the project s cost The goal is to manufacture planes in India with of them being paid for partially in Indian money and partially in foreign cash All payments for the remaining planes would be done in Indian rupee alone they stated The IAF intends to purchase planes to strengthen its combat capabilities and replace its outgoing fleet of MiG series planes
