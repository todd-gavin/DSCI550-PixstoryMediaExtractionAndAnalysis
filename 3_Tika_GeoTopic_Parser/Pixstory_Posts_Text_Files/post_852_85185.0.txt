The cricket world is reacting with shock and sadness at the tragic news Australian cricket legend Shane Warne has died aged 52.

Warne’s management released a statement in the early hours of Saturday morning (AEST) announcing he had passed away in Thailand of a suspected heart attack.

Within minutes some of the biggest names in cricket, including many of Warne’s former international rivals, took to social media to pay tribute.
