Three artists were called upon to create the new Prada advertising campaign for the Symbole bag starring the actress, model and activist Hunter Schafer Three artists three different sides of Prada and above all three different gazes Thomas Ruff legendary German photographer and artist proponent of glitch art Catherine Opie American photographer who is a lover of queer bodies and has always been a supporter of their representation And finally Carrie Mae Weems whose shots refer to the concepts of identity, representation and projection that reflect ideas of transformation and courage The creative direction as always curated by Ferdinando Verdieri
