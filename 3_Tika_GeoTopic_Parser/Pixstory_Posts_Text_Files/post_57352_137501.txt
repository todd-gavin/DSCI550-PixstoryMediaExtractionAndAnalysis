During the acupuncture sessions many techniques are used some of these are Moxibustion involves using heated sticks from dried herbs held near the acupuncture needles to warm and stimulate the acupuncture points Electroacupuncture An electrical device is connected to several needles providing a weak electrical current that stimulates the acupuncture needles during the treatment Cupping Glass or silicone cups are applied to the skin to have a suction effect which aims to help stimulate blood flow and balance your energy Source
