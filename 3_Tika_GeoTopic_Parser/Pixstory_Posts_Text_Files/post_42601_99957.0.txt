Dharma Productions finally announced the release date of their new film “JugJugg Jeeyo” and also released four new posters of the movie. 

JugJugg Jeeyo will release on 24th June 2022 and the star cast of the movie includes Anil Kapoor, Neetu Kapoor, Varun Dhawan, Kiara Advani, Manish Paul, and Prajakta Kohli.
