#7 Valentino Mazzola: Italy
Valentino was a member of the excellent Tornio team that was killed in a plane crash. He was regarded as the greatest Italian player of all time and an excellent all-around performer. He was unlucky not to be able to lead his country to the World Cup because his time coincided with World War II, which thwarted his World Cup dreams.
