The dried fish also known as Hokhe Gad or Hoggard in local dialect is a delicious winter dish from Kashmir that is fried in mustard oil and prepared with a number of spices A hot meal of Hokhe Gad according to elderly grandmothers from the Valley can heal even the severe colds and flu In order to protect the fish from predators and to dry them perfectly under sanitary conditions they are initially wrapped in a thin cotton towel before being exposed to the sun s heat The fish are stocked in preparation for the icy winter months when a lot of snow seals off the valley from the outside world
