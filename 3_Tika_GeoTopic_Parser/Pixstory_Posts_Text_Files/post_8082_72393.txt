The Afghanistan Cricket Board ACB says the Under national team has been training ahead of their first match Tuesday in the U World Cup According to the cricket board the team spent three days in quarantine after arriving in the West Indies and are scheduled to play their first game against Papua New Guinea on Tuesday Afghanistan U s were meant to take on Zimbabwe in their opening game on January in Trinidad but the match has been rescheduled to January The fixture against Pakistan U s is unchanged and will be played as scheduled on January ACB said Afghanistan is placed in Group C of the competition alongside three opponents The top two teams from the group qualify for the quarter finals while the bottom two from each group will compete in a plate championship
