WARM UP - 
Warming Up before doing any workout is necessary as it prevents injuries. The most common shoulder injury is the Rotator-Cuff muscles injury. 
These are Supraspinatus, Infraspinatus, Teres minor and Subscapularis. 
Performing Internal, external, anterior and posterior rotations before the workout in warmup hits the Rotator cuff muscles which prevents injuries.
