Juan Martin Del Potro announces his retirement from professional tennis The thirty-three-year-old tennis player who has been absent for almost three years due to injuries had announced his return to the field in the ATP of Buenos Aires Despite this, he surprisingly announced at the press conference that the Argentine tournament and that of Rio de Janeiro will be his last tournaments These are the words of the tennis player I wanted to say goodbye on the court It is very difficult for me to continue playing because of the pain that haunts me
