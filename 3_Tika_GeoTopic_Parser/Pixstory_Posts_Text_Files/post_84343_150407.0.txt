Maharashtra women and Gujarat men emerged victorious on the opening day of the Kabaddi competition at the EKA TransStadia indoor stadium 
A thrilling performance from Maharashtra stunned the national champions Himachal Pradesh 32-31 with their controlled ploy in the first half. 
On the other hand Maharashtra defeated Tamil Nadu 49-25. Services defeated Chandigarh 66-32 and Gujarat defeated Goa 56-27
