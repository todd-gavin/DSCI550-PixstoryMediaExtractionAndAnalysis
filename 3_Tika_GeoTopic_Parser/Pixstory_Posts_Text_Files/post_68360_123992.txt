Weightlifter Jeremy Lalrinnunga has made India proud by winning gold medal in the nd Commonwealth Games in Birmingham Jeremy was born on October in Aizawl Mizoram Lalrinnunga s mother is Lalmuanpuii His father is Lalneihtluanga Ralte who was a boxing prodigy of Mizoram s circuit in early s Jeremy is also very close to his younger brother Jerry Jeremy comes from a lowly background because his family circumstances prevented him for a very long time from pursuing his aspirations He began working as a labourer on contract with the Neighborhood Power Works department in an effort to provide for his family Jeremy unlike his father began boxing before beginning to lift weights In he won silver medals in youth world championship and Asian youth championship respectively He made history by becoming the first Indian to win a Gold medal at the Youth Olympics in the men s kg category in Before competing in an international competition Jeremy was a member of the Army s Boys Sports Company where he earned multiple medals In Kolkata in he also triumphed in the national championships At the world weightlifting championships Lalrinnunga finished at st rank at men s kg event He won gold in the commonwealth games in the kg category with a game record lift of kg in snatch and kg in clean and jerk Weightlifter Jeremy Lalrinnunga was accorded a hero s welcome as he returned home in Aizawl after winning a gold medal for India at the Birmingham Commonwealth Games He was received by Mizoram Sports minister and Mizoram Olympic association president Robert Romawia Royte ruling Mizo National Front MLA Zothantluanga senior officials and family members at the Lengpui airport on Monday The weightlifter said he will prepare for the Paris Olympics to be held in Where he will participate in the kg category He urged the Mizo youths to stick to their goals with a strong determination and abstain from drugs and alcohol
