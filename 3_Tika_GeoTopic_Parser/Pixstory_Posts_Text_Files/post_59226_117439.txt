When you hear the word Electric Vehicle the first thing that pops into your head is eco friendly Right But do you know in a recently published study in Nature magazine found that in some cases electric vehicles EV could be more polluting than vehicles powered by an internal combustion IC engine Researchers involved in the study analyzed vehicles in India and discovered the higher emissions of EVs It should be noted that the higher emission EVs depend heavily on where and how the EVs are charged The study shows that in the short to medium term India could benefit from hybrid vehicles to realize quick emissions reductions
