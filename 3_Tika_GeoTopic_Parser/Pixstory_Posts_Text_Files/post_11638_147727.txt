Start up your dinner with this sweet salad and make it a sweet full one Here is a sweet cabbage salad for you Ingredients you will need cups shredded cabbage about head cup shredded carrot about medium cup chopped green bell pepper about medium cup chopped red onion cup sugar cup white wine vinegar tablespoons olive oil teaspoon dry mustard teaspoon celery seeds teaspoon salt Directions to follow Mix the first four ingredients well in a large bowl and toss them well Combine sugar along with other ingredients in another small bowl and stir it with a whisk Pour in the vinegar mixture over the cabbage mixture and toss them gently to combine Cover it and chill it for an hour Enjoy your cold and sweet salad Sources My Recipes
