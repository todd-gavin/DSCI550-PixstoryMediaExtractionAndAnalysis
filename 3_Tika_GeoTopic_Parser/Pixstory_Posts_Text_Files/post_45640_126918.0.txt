Well here is your next watch! Never Have I Ever!

This coming-of-age sitcom' third season just debuted on Netflix. It is among the most popular teen comedy-drama programmes on the network. After her father passed away suddenly, the drama centres on the life of teenaged Devi Vishwakumar, an Indian-American Tamil girl. 

It mostly highlights her romantic relationships, her relationship with her mother Nalini, and other aspects of what happens in a teen's life. Leading roles on the programme are played by Maitreyi Ramakrishnan, Poorna Jagannathan, Richa Moorjani, Darren Barnet, Jaren Lewison, John McEnroe, Lee Rodriguez, and Ramona Young. On July 27, 2022, Netflix released the third season's trailer.
