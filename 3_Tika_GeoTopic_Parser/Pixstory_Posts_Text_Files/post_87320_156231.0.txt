Tanzania have played 2 games this season in FIFA U17 Women’s World Cup 2022. They have managed to win one game and have lost one game so far. In the process, they have scored 2 goals and conceded 05 goals. Tanzania have beaten France and lost to Japan in their first two games of the Under 17 World Cup.
Canada, on the other hand, have played 2 games this season in FIFA U17 Women’s World Cup 2022 and they have managed to draw a game and have lost a game. In this process, they have scored one goal and have conceded five goals. Canada have drawn against France and have lost to Japan in their opening games of the U17 World Cup.
