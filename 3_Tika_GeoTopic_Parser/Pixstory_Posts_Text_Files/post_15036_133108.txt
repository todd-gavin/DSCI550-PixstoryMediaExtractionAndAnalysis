After the great success of the last edition, the new edition of the MEI Meeting of Independent Labels will be held on September and October in the central squares of Faenza RA, the most important review of Italian independent music conceived and coordinated by Giordano Sangiorgi Three days of concerts forum conferences fairs and exhibitions in the main squares, theaters and palaces and stages in Faenza by adding Extra MEI content online, live streaming and exclusive content on the website and on the official social pages of the event which is celebrating its first years This year&#39;s edition is dedicated to Ivan Graziani who passed away years ago This year the SPECIAL MEI PLATE goes to GIANLUCA GRIGNANI on the occasion of the anniversary of the publication of the album La Fabbrica di Plastica This is the reason for the Special MEI Plaque The MEI assigns the Special MEI Plaque to Gianluca Grignani for the years of La Fabbrica di Plastica one of the best examples of alternative Italian rock outside the traditional schemes of Italian pop of the time Gianluca Grignani an Italian singer-songwriter guitarist and record producer Among the main songwriters and musicians of his generation in over years of career he has received numerous awards and recognitions Furthermore La Fabbrica Di Plastica was decreed by the magazine Rolling Stone the best Italian rock song Artist not aligned with the mainstream has sold a total of about million records and thanks to his works described by critics as outside the box who were looking for alternative paths already in the early years of his career he is considered one of the pillars of rock music Italian
