Do you love gender bender stories ? 
Gender Bender refers to the act up of covering up or concealing your real gender . In these types of stories one of the main characters hide their gender for some purpose and cross dress to avoid suspicion. 

Here are some recommendations for you guys 

Dragon and Phoenix Flip 
Youxi and Mingzu hide their real genders and cross dress for different purposes. Mingzhu after not being able to debut wants a job and Youxi is investigating his sister's disappearance. Somehow they both end up together , and help out each other. 
Available on Bilibili comics

Caught by the villain
After her brother runs away ,selena decides to enlist in the army for the sake of her sick father . She tries her best to not get noticed and draw attention but somehow ends up being the Prince's bodyguard. 
Available on Tappytoon 

Its not like I want to wear women's clothing
People go to great lengths to get a job , even going as far as to cross dress . A cute workplace romance that will tug at your heart strings.
