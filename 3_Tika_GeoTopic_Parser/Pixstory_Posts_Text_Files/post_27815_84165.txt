The pre-season tests in Barcelona were certainly not the best week for Alfa Romeo. The Swiss team sponsored by the Alfa Romeo company suffered from several technical problems, a symptom of the youth of the C project which will be entrusted on the track to Valtteri Bottas and the Chinese rookie Guanyu Zhou In the morning, the Hinwil-based team meanwhile unveiled the official livery of the single-seater that had taken to the track in tests at the Circuit de Catalunya with a camouflage color aimed at hiding its shapes. The car then immediately took to the track in Barcelona for the km filming day useful for recording advertising video material The new livery is very different from those used by the team in recent years still characterized by Alfa red and white with a design studied directly by the Alfa Romeo style center
