An impressive star cast consisting of A list actors does indeed create hype for the movie but ultimately if the story is not upto the mark they cannot do much for it Indian masses these days gravitate towards a good script they will watch the film even if there are no big names included in the star cast It is something Bollywood is failing to understand Films like Stree Bareilly ki barfi andhadhun show that in the end script and writing determines how the film will fare at the box office
