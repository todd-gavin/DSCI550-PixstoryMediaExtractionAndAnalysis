Gary Oldman who has worked with Christopher Nolan on the Dark Knight Trilogy, has confirmed that he'll be a part of Oppenheimer. The movie will chronicle the life story of J. Robert Oppenheimer, a theoretical physicist who contributed towards the invention of the atomic bomb, played by Cillian Murphy, with Emily Blunt, Matt Damon, Robert Downey Jr, Florence Pugh and Rami Malek among the all-star cast.
