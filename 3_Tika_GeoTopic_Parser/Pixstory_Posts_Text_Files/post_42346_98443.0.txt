After all the drama, action, and emotions in the semifinals of the UEFA Champions League, football fans got the two finalists which will face each other to win the tournament.

Liverpool will face Real Madrid in the final of the Champions League and the winner of that match will be crowned as European Champions.

The final will be held in Stade De France, Paris on 29th May 2022.

In 2018, Liverpool and Real Madrid faced each other in the UEFA Champions League final where Real Madrid beat Liverpool by 3-1 and lift the prestigious trophy.

Now, Liverpool will be looking forward to taking the revenge on Real Madrid and Madrid will be looking forward to winning their 14th Champions League trophy.
