Bollywood actor Akshay Kumar, on Wednesday announced the trailer release date of his next action thriller film ‘Ram Setu’.The trailer of the much-awaited film will be out on October 11, 2022.Helmed by Abhishek Sharma, the film also stars Jacqueline Fernandez, Nushrratt Bharuccha and Satya Dev in the lead roles.The film is all set to hit the theatres on October 25, 2022.
