Anandibai Gopalrao Joshi March February was the first Indian female doctor of western medicine She was the first woman from the erstwhile Bombay presidency of India to study and graduate with a two year degree in western medicine in the United States She was also referred to as Anandibai Joshi and Anandi Gopal Joshi where Gopal came from Gopalrao her husband s first name She was a great honor of our country
