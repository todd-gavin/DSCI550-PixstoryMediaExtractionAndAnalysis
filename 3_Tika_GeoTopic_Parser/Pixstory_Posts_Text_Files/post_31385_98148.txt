According to Istat, in March the number of employed people once again surpasses the million mark and the employment rate stands at a record since the beginning of the historical series Excellent news An undoubtedly very positive figure says Massimiliano Dona, president of the National Consumer Union a time lag between the trend of production and that of employment The impact of the economic cycle on employment levels is deferred over time In short, the March employment figure has not yet discounted the cyclical drop in GDP in the first quarter concludes Dona
