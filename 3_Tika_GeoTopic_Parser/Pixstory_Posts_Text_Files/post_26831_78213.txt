Camille Miceli ready for her debut at Emilio Pucci Called last September to revive the Italian maison, the designer signed her first collection for the brand which will be presented in Capri on April with a format yet to be defined. These are her words to following the announcement I thank Sidney Toledano for his trust I am very happy to become Artistic Director of the Maison Pucci a Florentine brand that has been celebrating the Italian joy and art of living for more than years It is an honor for me to contribute to the magnificent history of this Italian Maison has commented Camille Miceli
