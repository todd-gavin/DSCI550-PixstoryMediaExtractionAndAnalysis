A number of petitions from private tour operators seeking an exemption from the Goods and Services Tax (GST) for the Hajj and Umrah excursions they provide were dismissed by the Supreme Court. 

According to the petitions for exemption, Saudi Arabia-bound pilgrims were subjected to discrimination. 

The applications were denied on the grounds of exemption and discrimination by a panel of Justices A M Khanwilkar, A S Oka, and C T Ravikumar.
