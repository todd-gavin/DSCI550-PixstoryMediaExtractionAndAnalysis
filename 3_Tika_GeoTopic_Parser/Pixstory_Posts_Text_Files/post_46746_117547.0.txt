Prime Minister Narendra Modi, lauded all vaccinators for achieving the 200 crore COVID-19 vaccine doses landmark.

The PM congratulated all vaccinators by sending appreciation letters to them personally. The letters can be downloaded from their CoWIN login IDs, the government said.

"Saving lives is crucial, especially during a once-in-a-century global pandemic. Our vaccinators, healthcare workers and support staff, and frontline workers have played a critical role in protecting Indians," PM Modi wrote in the letter.

According to the Union Health Ministry, 90 percent of the population has been vaccinated with atleast one dose of covid 19 vaccine.
