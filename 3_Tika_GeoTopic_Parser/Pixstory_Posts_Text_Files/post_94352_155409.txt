Currently the fourth edition of the Street Child World Cup in Doha Qatar is taking place with young teams from countries across the continents having come together to give street connected children a voice for change The Street Child World Cup is an day event that features boys teams and girls teams with of the teams represented by refugees and displaced children The UN estimates there to be over million children living on the streets worldwide Many of them lack formal identification and have no legal rights access to education or healthcare The event aims to empower street children who are passionate about playing sports The charity s flagship initiative is the Street Child World Cup an international football tournament and congress for street connected children which kicks off ahead of each FIFA World Cup
