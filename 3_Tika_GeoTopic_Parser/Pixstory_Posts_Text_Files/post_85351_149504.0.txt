Delhi Court grants interim bail to Jacqueline Fernandez. 
Bollywood actress Jacqueline Fernandez appeared before Delhi Patiala House court in connection with a Rs 200 crores extortion case linked to alleged conman Sukesh Chandrashekhar. The actress was granted a bail today on bond of Rs 50,000 in the court. She has appeared at the court as an accused. The next date of hearing is October 22, 2022.
