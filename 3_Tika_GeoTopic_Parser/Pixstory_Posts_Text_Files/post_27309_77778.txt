Camera Nazionale della Moda Italiana becomes a member of the Milano Fashion Institute. Institute a non-profit institute which sees the collaboration of excellences of the Italian and international academic scene and which has the ambition of training new managers of the international fashion industry thanks to a training proposal that combines economic, scientific and humanistic skills CNMI also enters the Scientific Council of the Milanese institute alongside the Polytechnic of Milan and the Cattolica University thus also bringing its know-how and experience gained through constant comparison with all the brands and companies with which it interfaces on a daily basis One more opportunity for the construction industry fashion to make use of trained managers able to face the new challenges of a constantly evolving business And above all another step to confirm the leadership of Italian fashion in markets around the world
