Robyn Rihanna Fenty is a Barbadian singer, actress, fashion designer, businesswoman and a “Billionaire”😀

The 34-year-old is now the youngest self-made female billionaire in the United States after amassing a net worth of $1.4 billion, according to Forbes' 2022 list. Her remarkable milestone comes more than three years after the outlet bestowed the much-coveted title to Kylie Jenner, who is estimated to have a current net worth of $600 million.
