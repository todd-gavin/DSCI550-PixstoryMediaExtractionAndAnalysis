BLACKPINK broke records with BORN PINK
• BLACKPINK is the first K-pop girl group to become a "Double Million Seller". They have sold 2,141,281 copies for 2nd Album 'BORN PINK'.
• BLACKPINK's 'Pink Venom' extends its record as the longest-running 2022 song by a K-Pop act in the top 10 on the global Spotify chart (34 days)
• BLACKPINK's "SHUT DOWN" remains at #1 on the global Spotify chart with 5 million streams. It extends its record as the longest running #1 by a Korean act (6 days).
