Rumours have started to surface that Dušan Vlahović wants to join Juventus either in this transfer window or in the next. The Serbian forward has been a keen interest for Juventus and many other clubs. As of now  per reports Arsenal are in prime position to sign the forward. The English club has made a huge contract proposal to the player. According to La Gazzetta Dello Sport, Vlahović isn't considering going to Arsenal and highly favours a move to Juventus. If Juve want to sign him, they'll have to pay Fiorentina, €70m.
