On the occasion of International Women&#39;s Day, the artist Andi Kacziba inaugurates in Milan Take my heart a paraphrase of Sappho&#39;s poem Take my heart and take me far A unique opportunity to give women a voice and take stock through a series of sculptures and performances on gender-based violence and the gender gap of which women have also been victims during the pandemic For the artist, March is not a celebration but a moment of reflection on the past and the present It allows us to measure how far the finish line is and elaborate our experience to give a better future to the next generations
