Indian Railways has shared some breathtaking images of the Chenab bridge which is part of the Udhampur-Srinagar-Baramulla rail link project built over the Chenab river in Jammu and Kashmir.
About Chenab bridge:
The Chenab Bridge holds the reputation of being the highest railway bridge in the world at 359 metres above the river bed level. For reference, it is 35 metres higher than the Eiffel Tower in France.
