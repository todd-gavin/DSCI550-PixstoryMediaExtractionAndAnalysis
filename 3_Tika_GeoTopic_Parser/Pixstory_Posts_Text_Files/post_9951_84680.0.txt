07: Lviv Historic Centre Ensemble

Founded in the year 1254, Lviv is another beautiful Ukrainian city that is home to some beautiful ancient sites. The entire ancient part of the city is enlisted as a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Some of the prominent monuments here include the Town Hall, Black House, Rynok Square, Kornyakt Palace, Royal Arsenal and Powder Tower among others. Winters are magical in this quarter!

08: Chersonese

It is an ancient and Byzantine city that was founded in 422-421 BC by Greek. The city is set on the shore of the Black Sea on the outskirts of Sevastopol. It used to be an important political and trade hub in the bygone era. UNESCO listed Chersonesus as a World Heritage Site in 2013.
