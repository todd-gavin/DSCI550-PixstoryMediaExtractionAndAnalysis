Fencing was one of the first Olympic sports to be introduced The modern sport evolved towards the end of the nineteenth century based on ancient swordsmanship skills with the Italian school modifying the historical European martial art of classical fencing and the French school later perfecting the Italian method The mechanics of modern fencing started in an Italian Renaissance school of fencing in the th century and were developed by the French school of fencing under their influence The Spanish school of fencing died out and the Italian and French schools took its place
