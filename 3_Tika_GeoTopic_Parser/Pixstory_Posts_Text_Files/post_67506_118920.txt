Pictured above homemade mozzarella and fresh tomato slices on a slice of homemade focaccia topped with a balsamic glaze and fresh basil Mozzarella is such a versatile cheese The delightfully stringy cheese blankets our pizzas or maybe you prefer it coated with breadcrumbs and fried with a tangy sauce Watching the melted cheese stretch as you pull it is a sight that everyone enjoys Mozzarella can be used in salads sandwiches pasta and even desserts But did you know this cheese can be made at home That s right You can have your favorite cheese right at home And you don t even have to use rennet All you need are ingredients milk and vinegar To make fresh homemade mozzarella Heat up litres of non homogenized milk to C Remove from the heat and add a cup of white vinegar Stir for seconds then cover Let the milk sit for minutes undisturbed Using a large slotted spoon or a strainer gently remove the formed milk curds into a microwave safe bowl Press gently to remove as much whey as possible Don t throw the whey away You can use it in your baking or in curries for a protein boost Microwave the milk curds for about seconds or until it reaches about C It gets hot here so be careful Using tongs or heat safe gloves stretch and fold the cheese gently Repeat the heating and stretching process more times until the cheese is smooth Form the cheese into a ball and place a bowl with the whey There should be enough whey to cover the mozzarella Refrigerate for at least hour before cutting And you re done You can now use your fresh mozzarella any way you like Have you tried this recipe Comment below or Support to share your creations
