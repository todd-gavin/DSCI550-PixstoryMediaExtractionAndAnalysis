In recent years, performance of English clubs in European competitions has been better. England were also runners up in Euro cup 2020. 

Champions League

Season 2021-22

Liverpool have already reached the final beating Villarreal for an aggregate score of 5-2. Manchester City are already leading 4-3 in the first leg against Real Madrid, hoping to reach the final for the second time ever in history (first was in 2021). 


2020-21 

Chelsea were winners in the league, lifting the trophy for the second time beating Manchester City in an all England affair in the Final at Porto. 

2019-20 

In the last 5 years, it was the only season without any English clubs in the semi finals. 

2018-19 

Season saw 2 thrilling semi finals, in which Tottenham Hotspur beat unrelenting Ajax, in away goals, after an aggregate score of 3-3. Who can forget that second leg match of 3-2. In the final, Spurs were beaten by Liverpool 0-2.


2017-18 

Final was marked by horrible mistakes from Liverpool goalkeeper Loris Karius, gifting 2 goals for Real Madrid and winning their 13th title. 



Europa league

2021-22

In the current season, West ham had a dream run, reaching the semi final. They lost in the first leg to Eintracht Frankfurt 1-2. 

2020-21 

Two English teams reached the semi-finals of the Europa league, Manchester United and Arsenal. Final went into a penalty shootout against Villarreal, after a 1-1 till the end of extra time, which went on to take 11 kicks by each team. Match ended 11-10, when De Gea missed the decisive kick. 

2019-20

Manchester United reached the semi final of the season and lost to Sevilla, 1-2. Sevilla became Champions beating Inter Milan in the final. 

2018-19 

Season saw an all England final in Champions league and Europa league, where Chelsea met Arsenal and Chelsea winning (4-1) it for the second time in the history. 

2017-18

Arsenal reached the semi final of the Europa league, losing to Atletico Madrid(1-2). 



Europa Conference league, is the third tier of European leagues. 2021-22 is the first season of the league and Leicester are in the semi final, drawing the first leg with Roma 1-1.
