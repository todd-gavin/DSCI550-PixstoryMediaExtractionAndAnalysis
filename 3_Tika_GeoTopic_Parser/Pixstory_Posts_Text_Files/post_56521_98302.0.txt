Entrepreneur Natasha Poonawalla checked into fashion's biggest night - Met Gala 2022, in a golden Sabyasachi saree. The Met Gala, which is being held at Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York, saw some striking red carpet appearances. Natasha Poonawalla marked desi representation at the event, dressed in a gold Sabyasachi saree with a sheer trail - in keeping with this year's theme - 'In America: An Anthology of Fashion, or "Gilded Glamour."
