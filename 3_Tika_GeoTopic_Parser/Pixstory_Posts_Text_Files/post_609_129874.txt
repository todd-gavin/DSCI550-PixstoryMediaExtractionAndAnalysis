The traditional and famous kehwa can sometimes lit up your morning Here is the recipe Kehwa for Two Ingredients Water cups Sugar to tsp tsp churned Kashmiri green tea leaves Cinnamon powder or inch cinnamon stick Green Cardamom whole Almonds crushed Saffron Few strands Method Heat water in a pan Pound cardamom and cinnamon with the help of mortar pestle Once water starts boiling put sugar and pounded mixture and let it boil for a while Churn the tea leaves with the palm of your hands and throw in the pot At this stage you may add few strands of saffron too Stir and let it brew for a while Crush the almonds and divide between two cups Pour Kehwa into cups Straining tea is optional I like to see boiled crushed cardamom floating Your Kehwa is ready
