The rib pain that conditioned Rafael Nadal in the final of the Indian Wells tournament lost by sets to the American Fritz turned out to be a rather serious physical problem. As Nadal himself confirmed on Twitter, the subsequent analyzes carried out by his medical staff upon his return in Spain they showed a stress fracture in the rib The Spaniard who had started the year with a streak of consecutive successes will have to sit out from to weeks thus missing the start of the season on the beloved red clay Nadal will certainly miss the Monte Carlo and Barcelona tournaments scheduled for April while his presence at the internationals in Rome scheduled for early May remains in doubt
