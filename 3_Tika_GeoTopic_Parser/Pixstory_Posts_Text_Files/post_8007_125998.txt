On the eve of the one year anniversary of the Taliban coming to power in Afghanistan a number of active and protesting women once again went to the street of Kabul But they were faced with aerial fire from the Taliban forces These women who protested today in the center of Kabul city chanted the slogans work bread and education and justice justice of voice we are tired of ignorance Women protestors who following their previous protests have protested this time against injustice crimes and the captivity of women by the Taliban The Taliban suppressed their demonstrations with aerial shots and broke the mobile phones of some women This is despite the fact that during the past year when the Taliban came to power in Afghanistan women have repeatedly protested against the actions and restrictions imposed by this group In many cases women s protests have been suppressed by the Taliban and even a number of protesting women have been arrested and imprisoned
