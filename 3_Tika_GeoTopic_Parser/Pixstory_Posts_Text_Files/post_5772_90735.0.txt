Tina Dabi, the 2015 batch topper IAS officer gets engaged to 2013 batch IAS officer Pradeep Gawande. 

Dabi announced her engagement to Gawande by sharing a picture with her fiance on Instagram with the caption " I'm wearing the smile you gave me #fiance".

This is Dabi's second marriage as she was earlier married to batchmate IAS officer Athar Aamir Khan but the couple ended their marriage through a mutual divorce in august last year.
