Rahul Bajaj was born on June 10, 1938, in Calcutta. Bajaj graduated from St Stephen's College in Delhi University with an Honors Degree in Economics in 1958. Aside from this, Bajaj additionally has a degree in Law from Bombay University and a MBA from Harvard Business School.

He assumed control over a Chief Executive Officer of Bajaj Auto in 1968 and was selected as Managing Director in 1972. He additionally filled in as President of the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) from 1979 to 1980 and leader of Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers (SIAM). He was selected as Chairman of the recent Indian Airlines from 1986-89 and became CII President briefly time during 1999-2000.

From 2006 to 2010, he additionally filled in as a Member of Parliament in the Rajya Sabha. In April 2021, he ventured down as Chairman of Bajaj Auto. Be that as it may, he was selected as company's Chairman Emeritus for a considerable length of time.

TOI reported that Rahul Bajaj had pneumonia and also a heart problem. He was admitted to the Ruby Hall Clinic a month ago and breathed his last on Saturday at 2.30 pm.

"It is with deep sorrow that I inform you about the passing away of Shri Rahul Bajaj, husband of the late Rupa Bajaj and father of Rajiv/Deepa, Sanjiv/Shefali and Sunaina/Manish. He passed away on the afternoon of 12th February, 2022 in the presence of his closest family members," a statement from Bajaj Group read.
