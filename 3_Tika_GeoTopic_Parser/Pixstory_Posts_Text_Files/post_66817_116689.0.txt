Jupiter is the 5th planet, being the largest one with the  biggest mass and density. 

Up until this point we didn't have any idea on how the planet looked save for some pictures by Hubble. 

But for the first time, we have a clear picture of Jupiter. 

In the latest pictures released by JWST, we can clearly see Jupiter, it's rings, and many of his moons. 

We have made a huge leap in Space exploration and we are just getting started
