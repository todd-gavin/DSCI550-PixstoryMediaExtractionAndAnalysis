The Spanish League will officially change its name from next year The partnership with Banco Santander which gave its name to the league for the last seven seasons has ended A five-year contract has been signed with EA Sport the giant of sports video games Thirty million euros that the Spanish league will collect for the name of the first division championship The name that EA Sport will give to La Liga has not yet been revealed but it is certain that it will not stop here. In fact, the agreement also provides for a change in all the visual elements such as logos, graphics and fonts and will be based on five digital technology pillars core sports content and branding
