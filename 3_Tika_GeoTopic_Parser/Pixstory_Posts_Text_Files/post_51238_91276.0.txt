Akshay Kumar and Kriti Sanon-starrer Bachchhan Paandey is not doing great at the box office. The film is facing stiff competition from RRR and The Kashmir Files. Bachchhan Paandey follows an aspiring filmmaker (Kriti) trying to make a film on a gangster (Akshay).

BACHCHHAN PAANDEY BOX OFFICE COLLECTION

Bachchhan Paandey opened to mixed reviews. The film managed to collect only Rs 50 crore during the first week. On the 12th day, the collections were similar to the previous day and the film earned Rs 1 crore. The film's collection has not been impressive since the first day.

ABOUT BACHCHHAN PAANDEY
