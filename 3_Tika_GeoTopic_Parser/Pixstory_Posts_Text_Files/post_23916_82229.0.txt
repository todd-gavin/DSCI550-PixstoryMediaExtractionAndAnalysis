A young man's Startup from Indore turned into a million-dollar company by finding bugs in the applications made by Google, Samsung, and Apple.

Aman Pandey started his company "Bugsmirror" after he was paid Rs 70,000 by Google for finding a bug in one of their applications in 2019, while he was still studying.
