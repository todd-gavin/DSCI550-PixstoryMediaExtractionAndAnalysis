Tess Holliday is a renowned model who recently took to social media  to share a story about being body shamed by a woman who told her, "You are so pretty. You have a beautiful face and you have beautiful hair Why don't you just lose a little bit of weight?' 

The model opened up about receiving this kind of comments often. To the body shaming comment  Tess replied "Ma'am, I am one of the most famous plus-size models in the world; I think I'm doing okay."
