While the winds of war continue to blow on the border between Ukraine and Russia Oleksandr Abramenko and Ilia Burov give the world an image that has gone viral The Ukrainian and the Russian finished in second and third place in the freestyle skiing aerials specialty behind the Chinese Qi Guangpu and for the second consecutive Olympics they found themselves together on the podium The two rivals but also friends embraced on the podium wrapped in their respective national flags in spite of the tensions that are being experienced between Ukraine and Russia Scenes already seen in PyeongChang when Abramenko won the gold and Burov still the bronze
