Acupuncture can treat many health conditions, even headaches or sinus pressure. There are claims that acupuncture can be used to treat
-allergies 
-Anxiety and depression
-osteoarthritis
-chronic pain often in the neck, back, knees, and head
-hypertension
-insomnia
-menstrual cramps and PMS
-migraines
-morning sickness
-sprains
-strokes
