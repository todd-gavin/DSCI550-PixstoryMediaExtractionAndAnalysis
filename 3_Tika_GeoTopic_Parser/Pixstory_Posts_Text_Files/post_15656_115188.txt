GIOVENT MUSICALE D ITALIA returns to the magical courtyard of Palazzo Reale in Milan with the eighth edition of the ETHNO MUSIC FESTIVAL with an unmissable calendar of concerts linked together by passion and enthusiasm every Thursday in July at artistic direction by Saul Beretta who promote popular music in support of one of the fundamental aspects of the cultural identity of every country and community where musical language becomes an instrument of dialogue for comparison and cultural and social integration ETHNO will see four female artists on stage protagonists between music ancient bossa nova and Latin American folklore four different journeys to discover the southern hemisphere of fascinating medieval creatures of a renewed Renaissance folk with the flavor of a land and a lost time All the concerts will begin at and precede the screening of the film scheduled in the calendar of the ARIANTHEUS review
