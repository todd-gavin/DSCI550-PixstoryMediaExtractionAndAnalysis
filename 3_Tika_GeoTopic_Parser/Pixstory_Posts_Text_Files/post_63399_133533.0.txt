Love a rose-flavored drink? There is
nothing better than a summer afternoon and a glass of Pink Lemonade.
You can drink rose anytime! Pink Lemonade or Rose Lemonade, it's a refreshing drink with just a few ingredients to beat the heat.

Ingredients
4-5 tbsp rose syrup
3 tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp sumac powder
2 cups chilled water or soda

Pour the rose syrup and lemon juice into a serving pitcher.
Add 2 cups of cold water and taste. Add more water if needed. Refrigerate 30 to 40 minutes, serve with lots of ice cubes and fresh mint leaves, sprinkle some sumac on top, and you're done!
