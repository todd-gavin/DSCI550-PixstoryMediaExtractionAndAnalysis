It goes without saying that the towering sandstone cliffs of Kings Canyon will humble you despite being overshadowed by more famous sights like Uluru. 

Did you know that the Kings Canyon, which can be seen in the Watarrka National Park near the western end of the George Gill Range, was created by erosion over the course of millions of years?

You can explore the strange domes within The Lost City, ascend to the top of the old canyon to catch the sunrise, and descend to the unique Garden of Eden. Sounds like an adventure, right? 

As per CT, Kings Canyon, which is located 323 kilometres southwest of Alice Springs, offers a range of lodging options, including lodge rooms, camp sites, and luxurious, resort-style rooms.

So, plan your next vacation here!
