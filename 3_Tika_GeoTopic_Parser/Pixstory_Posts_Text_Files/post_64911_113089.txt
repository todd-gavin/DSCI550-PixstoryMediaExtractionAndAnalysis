Amid tight security the sixth batch of over pilgrims left from Jammu on Monday for the twin base camps of metre high cave shrine of Amarnath in south Kashmir Himalayas officials said They said a total of pilgrims left the Bhagwati Nagar Yatri Niwas here in a convoy of vehicles amid heavy security of CRPF the officials said Of these are males females children Sadhus and Sadhvis they said The annual day long yatra commenced on June from the twin base camps traditional km Nunwan Pahalgam in south Kashmir s Anantnag and km shorter Baltal in central Kashmir s Ganderbal Till today over pilgrims had offered their prayers at the cave shrine housing the naturally formed ice Shivlingam the officials said The Yatra is scheduled to end on August on the occasion of Raksha Bandhan
