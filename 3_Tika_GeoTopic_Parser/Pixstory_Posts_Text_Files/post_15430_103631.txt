To ensure that young children do not find themselves living in prison following imprisoned mothers This is the aim of the bill approved in the first reading by the Montecitorio Chamber with votes in favor and against The measure signed by the deputy dem Paolo Siani rapporteur Walter Verini aims to promote the model of family homes and to exclude that mothers with cohabiting children under the age of 18 end up in prison At the same time, in the presence of exceptionally important precautionary requirements, recourse to institutions with attenuated custody for inmate mothers is foreseen Icam
