As we all know monsoon is here and it is soothing relief from the unbearable heat. Also, we all know that the monsoon also invites a lot of health issues with it. As the rainy season affects the immune system of our body, it is very important to be extra careful about what we eat and drink during this season. 
Here are some tips for healthy diet in monsoon :
1. Eat seasonal fruit- Fruits like apple, litchi, jamuns, peaches, papaya, pomegranate, etc 
2. Curd over milk
3. Boil water
4. Avoid salt
5. Avoid watery food
6. Avoid junk food
7. Bitter is better- vegetables like methi , neem and turmeric as addition to diet can help in preventing infection 
8. Limit meat 
 Stay healthy eat healthy!
