AMI and the Italian hat manufacturer Borsalino have combined their know-how to create an exclusive range of felt hats in the first ever collaboration of the Parisian house around an iconic accessory. Creating the perfect hat has always been the dream of Alexandre Mattiussi founder and director creative by AMI A hat that represents the accessory par excellence that emphasizes an outfit and defines a personality In this exclusive collaboration Alexandre Mattiussi and Borsalino have imagined a range of superior quality floppy hats
