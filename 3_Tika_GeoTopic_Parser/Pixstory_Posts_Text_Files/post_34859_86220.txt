Fifa today issued a press release officially postponing the match between Ukraine and Scotland valid for the World Cup qualifying playoffs in Qatar The match was scheduled for the end of March like all the others including those of Italy Roberto Mancini but due to the ongoing conflict the highest body of world football deemed it inappropriate to let the Ukrainians play as well as exclude them from the competition In March the Football Federation of Ukraine wrote to FIFA asking that its qualifying matches be rescheduled due to the impossibility of organize both travel and training for a team under the current circumstances, the statement reads. After consulting UEFA and the four member associations in Path A of the European qualifying play-offs, it was unanimously agreed in a spirit of solidarity to accept this request. Scotland and Ukraine initially scheduled for March will be postponed to the June window The match between the two national teams is in a semi-final in June
