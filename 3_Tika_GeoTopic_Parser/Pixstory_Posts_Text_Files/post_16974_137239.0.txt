Happy Birthday Rishi Kapoor

His breakthrough leading performance as a grownup was in the teenage romance “Bobby”, starring alongside Dimple Kapadia, for which he was nominated for the Filmfare Award for Best Actor. He died on April 2020, and this year is his birthday anniversary. He was born on September 4, 1953, so Happy Birthday Rishi Kapoor.❤️
