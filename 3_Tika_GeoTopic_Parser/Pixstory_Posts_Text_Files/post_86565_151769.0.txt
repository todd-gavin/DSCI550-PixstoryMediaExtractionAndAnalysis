H.E. Prime Minister and Minister of Interior Sheikh Khalid bin Khalifa bin Abdulaziz Al Thani inaugurated the Security Force's official uniform for the FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 during a ceremony held on 2 October 2022 at the Ministry of Interior (MOI) building.
His Excellency witnessed a visual presentation on the uniforms of the World Cup security force, the locations, protection missions, and the security roles of each unit. Additionally, His Excellency witnessed the inauguration of the mechanisms, vehicles, and motorcycles used in the tournament.
Their Excellencies members of the Security Committee and a number of senior officials and security leaders attended the inauguration ceremony.
