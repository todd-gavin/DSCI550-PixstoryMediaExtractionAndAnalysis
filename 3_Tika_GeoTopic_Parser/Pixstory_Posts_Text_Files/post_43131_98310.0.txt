India’s Best Dancer season 2 was judged by Terence Lewis, Malaika Arora, and Geeta Kapoor. But, as Malaika was tested positive for Covid-19, Nora Fatehi had replaced her for a few weeks. Nora’s chemistry with Terence had become the talk of the town, and there were speculations that the two are in a relationship. Recently, Terence opened up about the rumours of his relationship with Nora and stated that they are very good friends. He also praised Nora and called her hard-working.
