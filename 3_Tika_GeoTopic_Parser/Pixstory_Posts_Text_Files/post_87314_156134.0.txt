New Zealand and Germany will be up against each other on Matchday 03 of the FIFA Under 17 Women’s World Cup 2022. While New Zealand are on the bottom spot in Group B of the FIFA Under 17 Women’s World Cup points table, Germany sit at the top spot in the points table.
New Zealand have played 2 games this season in FIFA U17 Women’s World Cup 2022. They have managed to win no game. They have lost both the games so far, In the process, they have scored 1 goal and conceded 07 goals. New Zealand lost to Chile and Nigeria in their first two games of the Under 17 World Cup.
Germany, on the other hand, have played 2 games this season in FIFA U17 Women’s World Cup 2022 and they have won both these games. In this process, they have scored 8 goals and have conceded 01 goal. Germany have beaten Chile and Nigeria in their opening games of the U17 World Cup.
