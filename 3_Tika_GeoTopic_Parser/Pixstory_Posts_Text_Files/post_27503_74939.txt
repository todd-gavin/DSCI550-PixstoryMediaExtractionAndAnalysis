On the second day of the legendary Rallye Monte Carlo, the first round of the WRC World Rally Championship, another challenge between the two French motoring champions. On one side, the legend Sebastien Loeb, back from second place in the Dakar on the brand new Ford Puma WRC, on the other, Sebastien Ogier, the world champion in office at the wheel of the small but very fast Toyota Yaris WRC After a very balanced Friday in the special stages on Saturday, it was the reigning world champion who took the chair, closing the last special stage with a second advantage over his compatriot.
