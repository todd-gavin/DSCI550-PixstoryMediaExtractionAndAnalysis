Some observers are hoping that after occupying eastern Ukraine, Russia will proclaim victory and end the hostilities. Western leaders would keep pleading with Ukraine to accept the lost land, move forward with reconstruction of the remainder of Ukraine, and accept massive Western help.

Western commentators have expressed doubts about this assumption, citing recent Russian missile attacks on the Ukrainian capital Kyiv, the south of the country, and a mall. When and where will Russia stop after destroying so much of Ukraine?
