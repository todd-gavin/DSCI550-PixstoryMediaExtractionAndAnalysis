Sri Guru Hargobind Sahib Ji was th amongst Sikh Gurus He succeeded Guru Arjan Sahib in to the spiritual throne of Guru Nanak Sahib Ji at the age of eleven years Guru Arjan Dev was martyred at Lahore on the th May A D after great torture on the order of king Jahangir After that at the time of the ceremony of assuming leadership of Sikhs Guru Hargobind addressed the congregation saying I have to raise an army that will fight the tyranny being carried out in the country Come join that army and get the blessings of the Guru In August A D at the time of entrusting the responsibility to his grandson Guru Har Rai Guru Hargobind said Always keep twenty two hundred horse men in fighting fit condition with you Acting upon the command of the Guru he and his successor Guru Har Rai continued the program of weapon training and teaching of scriptures as before The teaching of Guru Nanak had awakened the people of India in such a way that they re were always ready to fight tyranny The barbarity of the rulers could not waver them from their faith They attained martyrdom smiling
