Since the outbreak of war in February many families have spent days weeks and even months taking refuge underground or traveling long distances where the internet is not always available To help those having difficulty connecting the NUMO application is available for offline use once installed on a mobile device and completing a short registration form The application will help children independently learn to use their free time effectively engage in cognitive activities develop key skills and distract themselves from war The application was created by UNICEF Ukraine together with UNICEF Montenegro in collaboration with the Ministry of Education of Montenegro and the company SoftServe
