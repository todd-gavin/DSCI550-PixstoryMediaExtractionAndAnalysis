Two Soup recipes to have a headstart Vegetable Soup Ingredients medium finely chopped carrot gm finely chopped cabbage small finely chopped green chilli teaspoon powdered black pepper finely chopped green beans bunch finely chopped coriander leaves teaspoon cornflour pinch salt Instructions Step Take a bowl and mix the cornflour with cup of water and keep aside Heat a heavy bottomed pan over medium flame Pour cups of water and bring to a boil Step Add the finely chopped carrot beans cabbage coriander leaves and green chillies Cook till tender Add the cornflour and bring to a boil To accentuate the taste of this dish you can top it with some grated cheese If you want to make it a bit spicy you can also add spices as per your taste preference Remove and transfer to a serving bowl Sprinkle pepper powder and salt serve hot Tip You can avoid corn flour while preparing the soup Cabbage Soup Ingredients sliced onion cloves finely chopped garlic teaspoon ground coriander seeds cups veg stock diced tomato pinch ground salt tablespoon butter teaspoon jalapenos tablespoon mustard seeds tablespoon lemon juice cup sliced cabbage pinch ground black pepper Instructions Step Heat ghee or butter in a saucepan over moderate flame Add sliced onions in the saucepan and saut them over medium flame till they are golden in colour Once done add the garlic and jalapeno Continue to saut for another two minutes Add coriander and dry mustard Stir well Step Now add the vegetable stock and tomatoes Make sure that that the saucepan is uncovered and simmer the ingredients for about minutes Then add cabbage and cook for another five minutes Season with salt pepper and lemon juice Serve the warm soup with garlic bread
