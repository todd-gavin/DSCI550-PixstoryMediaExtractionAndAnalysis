Starbucks is one of the most famous outlets known for having a peaceful ambient atmosphere and a delicious cup of coffee But did you know the story behind it s logo The beautiful green coloured logo was inspired by the Twin Tailed Siren from Greek mythology According to Greek mythology the twin tailed sirens were known for their beauty and melodious music They used this to their advantage by luring sea men to their death The company s logo thus stands for obsession with a habit that is drinking coffee
