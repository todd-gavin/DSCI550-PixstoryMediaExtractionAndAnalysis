The Islamic State IS has claimed responsibility for the attack in Afghanistan on Thursday that killed at least people IS has claimed responsibility for the four explosions across Afghanistan on Thursday April in which at least people were killed and wounded One of the blasts was carried out at a mosque in Mazar e Sharif At least people were killed and dozens injured in the blast According to ANI the Taliban has condemned the blasts which were carried out in Kabul Balkh and Kunduz The first blast occurred in a public square in Kabul s Police District in which many including children were injured The second blast occurred at Mazar e Sharif the capital of Balkh province while the third explosion was reported from Kunduz
