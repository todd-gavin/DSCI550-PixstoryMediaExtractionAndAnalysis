
After his family is killed in a terrible accident Hyun a reclusive loner who is suicidal finds himself alone in a world surrounded by monsters . Now he must he do what he wants to avoid the most take initiative and interact with others . After being caught up in multiple life and death situations Hyun finds the will to live  again.
 Together with other survivors he feels a sense of solidarity and security. As he discovers his fears he realises that maybe the thing he was afraid of  after  all is what  haunts everyone the most REGRET.
