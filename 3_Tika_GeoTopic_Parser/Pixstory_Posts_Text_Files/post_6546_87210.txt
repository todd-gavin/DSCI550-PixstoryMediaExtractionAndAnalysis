By Aritraa Dey A collage of three men hanging from the ceiling is being widely shared on social media platforms As per the post three students who had been preparing for competitive exams died by suicide after the Samajwadi Party SP did not come to power in Prayagraj Uttar Pradesh Viral text in Hindi This photo is massively viral on Facebook Fact check Alt News found that at least two out of the three images are old Furthermore Prayagraj police has denied these incidents took place after poll results were announced Image The details of this image were found on a news site named Sanjeevani Today On November a man going through a financial crisis died by suicide in Ramgarh The man is identified as Randhir Kumar from Bokaro son of Tara Mahato Image We did a reverse image search on the second image and found a link to an article that identified the person as Nayab Najir from Begumgang Lucknow The incident took place on February The article is no longer accessible but can be read on Google cache and the image comes up on Google reverse search Dainik Bhaskar had also reported on the suicide case three years ago Image We could not find any details of the third image But we found a tweet by Prayagraj police on March that stated no such cases of suicides have been reported in the past hours The results of UP elections were announced on March Thus old images were shared with the false claim that three people died by suicide after SP lost UP polls
