The Napoli striker Andrea Petagna seriously risks being away from the playing fields for at least a month The centre-forward who came out limping from the Bentegodi match against Hellas Verona has suffered a second-degree strain of the semimembranosus muscle of his right thigh There are therefore fears of at least a month stop for him with a possible return on April in the important league match at the Diego Armando Maradona stadium against Roma
