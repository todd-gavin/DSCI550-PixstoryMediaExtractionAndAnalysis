One of the most significant and auspicious days of Navratri is Durga Ashtami. On Ashtami Tithi of Shukla Paksha in the month of Ashwina, Durga Ashtami is observed. Maha Ashtami is another name for Durga Ashtami. The third day of Durga Puja or the eighth day of Navratri is Maha Ashtami.
Happy Ashtami everyone!
