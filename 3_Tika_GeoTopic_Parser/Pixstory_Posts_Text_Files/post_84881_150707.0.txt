Strasbourg and Rennes will be up against each other on Matchday 09 of the Ligue 1 2022-23 season. While Strasbourg are on the 18th spot of the Ligue 1 points table, Rennes sit at the 8th position in the points table.
Strasbourg are in the relegation zone currently and have had one of the forgetful Ligue 1 seasons so far. In the 8 matches played this season, Strasbourg have managed to win none of the games, being on the losing end thrice.
Rennes, on the other hand, have had a decent opening in Ligue 1 so far but are very far away from the top 4 bunch of teams. In the 8 games played this season, Rennes have managed to win 3 games, draw 3 games and have lost on a couple of occasions.
