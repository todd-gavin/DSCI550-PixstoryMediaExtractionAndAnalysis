Chetna Keer's new book "GIDDHA ON MY GULMOHAR", is a racy literary and romantic fiction set in the literary landscapes and bookscapes of the Pandemic, spanning the cultural hub of Dilli Haat to litfests tucked in lakesides and hillsides of City Beautiful to Himachal Pradesh.

It's a multilayered narrative about Pandemic's people like you and me -- Lollita to Lambaji, Gupta ji to Ganguly ji -- whose middleclass dreams are caught on the cusp of the Corona-scape. 

While chasing her literary dream, the protagonist, budding writer Lollita, finds herself being wooed by a younger guy. 

What befalls this summer-spring romance and the everyday dreams of Quarantine's quirky cast of characters ?
