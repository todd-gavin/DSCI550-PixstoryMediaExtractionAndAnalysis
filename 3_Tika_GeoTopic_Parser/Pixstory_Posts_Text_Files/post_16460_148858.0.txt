Bollywood actress Alia Bhatt recently shared her first look at her Hollywood debut action thriller Heart of Stone. She will be screen space with Gal Gadot and Jamie Dornan. Alia shared the video teaser and wrote, “The first look of Heart Of Stone and Keya coming to Netflix in 2023 #Tudum”.
