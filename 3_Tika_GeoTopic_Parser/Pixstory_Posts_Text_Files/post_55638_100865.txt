Rake is a quirky show but with a very different protagonist and environment Cleaver Greene Richard Roxburgh is a Sydney based barrister who is recognised for defending the guilty and winning cases using unconventional methods As per collider his extracurricular activities are as colourful as his courtroom antics he owes bookies money has a long term relationship with a prostitute with whom he has built an emotional bond and has continuous tax troubles with a foe whom he outwits at every turn Rake is a five season wild ride all the way to the High Court of Australia and it looks at how Cleaver takes on a wide range of ridiculous seemingly unwinnable legal matters
