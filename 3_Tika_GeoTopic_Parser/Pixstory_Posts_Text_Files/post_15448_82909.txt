Colombia&#39;s President Ivan Duque announced the approval of a regulation for the use of medical cannabis in food, beverages and fabrics and the authorization for the export of dried cannabis flowers The government issued the resolution governing the decree approved last year in relation to quota licenses and authorizations for safe and informed access to the use of cannabis and the plant cannabis, its derivatives and products This resolution allows defines and establishes all mechanisms and procedures for the industrial use of cannabis in sectors such as food drinks and also textile uses, obviously defining that these uses have to do with the non-psychoactive component, explained the president in a statement to the Casa de Narino
