Fatima Akbari is an Afghan entrepreneur and women s advocate who is founder of the Gulistan Sadaqat Company and non governmental organization the Women Affairs Council In she received the Women Entrepreneurial Achievement Award Fatema Akbari was driven into carpentry by necessity as a means of supporting her children following the death of her husband in originally working on building sites in Iran where her family fled when the Taliban took control of Afghanistan In she returned to homeland and started furniture manufacturing business by establishing Gulistan Sadaqat Company in Kabul with a carpentry school She attempted to provide a workforce base as a means of earning to wives of men killed or disabled during conflict in Afghanistan In she enrolled in the Goldman Sachs sponsored Women program at the American University of Afghanistan a program aimed at training women from developing countries in business and management
