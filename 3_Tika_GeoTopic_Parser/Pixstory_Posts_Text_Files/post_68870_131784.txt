Eighteen year old Gyamar Nikum from Arunachal Pradesh helped Rajasthan United FC RUFC produce the biggest upset of the st Durand Cup so far beating local favourites ATK Mohun Bagan ATKMB in a group B fixture at the Vivekananda Yuba Bharati Krirangan VYBK in Kolkata on August It was a grand debut for RUFC in the tournament as Gyamar Nikum got the all important winner in the fifth minute of stipulated time
