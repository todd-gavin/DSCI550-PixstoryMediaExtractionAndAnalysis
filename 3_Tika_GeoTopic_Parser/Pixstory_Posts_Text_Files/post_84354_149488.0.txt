The UEFA Euro 2012 finals took place between Spain and Italy, with Spain humiliating the Italians 4-0 in front of 63,000 fans. It was then the infamous 4-6-0 formation was implemented, but what exactly is this? 
With 4 defenders and 6 midfielders, this unconventional tactic draws out the defenders of the opposing team as they increase their attacking line against such a defensive formation. 
Yet with no strikers this formation hopes to counter with quick conversions by the 6 midfielders against a team whose members are all pushed forward.
