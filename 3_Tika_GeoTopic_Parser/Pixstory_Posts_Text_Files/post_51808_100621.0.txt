Since BLACKPINK‘s Rosé has always kept her hair long ever since the group’s trainee days, fans were completely surprised when the idol recently appeared with short hair.

For the first time ever, Rosé showed off a shoulder-length haircut that left fans blown away by her beauty.

The haircut was immediately a hit among fans who were amazed to see a different side of the idol and looking forward to seeing more of it.

While gracing multiple covers for Elle Korea magazine, Rosé looked as stunning as she usually does with her lovely blonde hair.

Whether her hair is short or long, Rosé’s visuals are always absolutely stunning.
