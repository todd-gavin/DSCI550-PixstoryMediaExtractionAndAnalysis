It was magical watching Ranbir Kapoor- Alia Bhatt starrer Bhramhastra which embodies the mix of fiction, fantasy, and reality on a huge screen.
Directed by Ayan Mukerji, the movie addresses the love story of a young man which embedded with supernatural powers and envisions.
Eventhough, the movie only attained mixed reviews from the critics, the film has managed to crowdpull the audience with it magic - realism. 
The lead actors has also done a fine job.The movie was worth watching for the people who enjoy myth and fiction.
