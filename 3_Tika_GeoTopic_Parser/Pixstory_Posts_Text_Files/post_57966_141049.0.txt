Tae - ung receives a happy sum of money to act as a bodyguard for a girl from rich family . It is a win -win situation for him , but things get complicated when he finds out Gamin ,the girl he is supposed to protect is getting bullied. But Gamin doest let the other students have it their way , she fights back ! She retaliates and this escalates the situation even further. 
A webtoon that touches upon deep topics of school bullying, mental health etc . It is a thriller you are bound to enjoy !
