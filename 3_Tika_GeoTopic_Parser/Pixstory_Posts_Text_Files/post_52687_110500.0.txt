Chandigarh Press Club in association with Department of Public Relations, Centre For Social Work, Panjab University, Chandigarh, is hosting a three-day photography exhibition 'CANVAS OF LIFE: A MEDIA KALEIDOSCOPE' at Students Centre, PU. 

The photo exhibition was inaugurated Patrick Hebert, Consul General of Canada in Chandigarh, on Tuesday. 

The work of 51 photo journalists, consisting 101 frames, covering sports, different facets of life, including life during COVID pandemic, nature, weather, wildlife, political protests, etc... are on display.
