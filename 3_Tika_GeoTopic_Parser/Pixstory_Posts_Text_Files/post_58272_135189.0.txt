Real idols in a world of magical idops 
TXT ' star seekers is a webtoon based on the real life members of kpop group TXT . 
Set in a fictional world where where magical idols who are gifted with powers are considered tier 1 while magicless idols are considered ti be tier 2 . The audience prefers tier 1 to tier 2 who use their magic to entertain people. As the abilities manifest before adulthood the kpop group StarOne failed to awaken it's powers and is to be disbanded by the agency . However the their leader suddenly develops magic powers during a terrorist attack and saves everyone. While his bandmates too start developing powers one by one . A of a sudden they rise to fame .
