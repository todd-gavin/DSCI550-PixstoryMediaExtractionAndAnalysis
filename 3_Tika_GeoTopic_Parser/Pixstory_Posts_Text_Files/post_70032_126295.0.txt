Students of Royale School, Taleigao on Friday flagged off a 'tiranga' cycle rally to create awareness about the 'Har Ghar Tiranga' programme to mark India's 75 years of Independence. 

The rally was flagged off by dance choreographer, Akshay Vishwakarma in the presence of the Headmistress Maria Da C Afonso as well as other teaching and non teaching staff.

The vision of 'Har Ghar Tiranga' campaign is to unify the nation and remember the country's freedom struggle by instilling patriotism in the hearts of citizens.

Primary and High School students participated enthusiastically in the 5.7 km cycle rally.
