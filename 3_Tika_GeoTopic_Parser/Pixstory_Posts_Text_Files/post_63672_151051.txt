While not fully a console game the Valkyrie Profile is in many ways synonymous with Playstation as the acclaimed first two games of the series Valkyrie Profile and Valkyrie Profile Silmeria appeared on the PS and PS respectively Now after a year absence from consoles the series is back with Valkyrie Elysium The new action RPG features an evocative world inspired by Norse mythology thrillingly fast paced combat great visuals bombastic audio and a dark thought provoking narrative Developed by Soliel and Square Enix Valkyrie Elysium is an action RPG where players assume the role of a Valkyrie tasked with finding and purifying lost souls Played from a third person perspective players explore environments and battle enemy opponents including monsters fellow Valkyries and boss characters using a plethora of attacks ranging from melee combat e g swords spears to elemental attacks e g blasts of fire Released on September Valkyrie Elysium is available as a physical and digital Standard Edition on PlayStation and PlayStation with a digital only Standard Edition available on PC via STEAM which will release on November
