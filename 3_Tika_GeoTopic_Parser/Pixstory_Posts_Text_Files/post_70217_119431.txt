Goa Kurash Association conducted selection trials for Cadets and the Junior Kurash team at Peddem Sports complex Mapusa recently The team will be participating in the upcoming Cadets and Junior Kurash National Championship at Ganganagar Rajasthan to be held between July th To th The following players have been selected for Kurash Nationals Boys Darshan Naik Sushant Palyekar Girish Pednekar Girls Payal Naidu Riyansha Naik Neha Naik Pallavi Dande Eshwar Naik and Pradosh Parmekar will accompany the team as its Coach and Manager respectively The team will be leaving on July to represent the state at the national championship
