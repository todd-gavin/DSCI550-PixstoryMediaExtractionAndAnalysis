The Italy of curling never stops Today Stefania Constantini and Amos Mosaner first overcame Australia and then the very strong Great Britain confirming themselves as the only undefeated team in the entire Olympic mixed curling tournament in Beijing So when there are still only three challenges left to go of the round robin phase against Sweden, Canada and China, the Azzurri are already mathematically certain of qualifying for the semifinals thanks to the victories achieved so far.
