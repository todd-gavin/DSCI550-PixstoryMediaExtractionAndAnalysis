Sikyong Penpa Tsering to embark on official visit to JapanDharamshala Central Tibetan Administration s Sikyong Penpa Tsering is embarking on a week long official visit to Japan from to September Upon arrival Sikyong is scheduled to attend a meeting with Parliamentary supporters for Tibet and meet a group of Tibet supporters and sponsors His week long schedule includes addressing university students on the Tibetan cause and meeting with the local Tibetan community
