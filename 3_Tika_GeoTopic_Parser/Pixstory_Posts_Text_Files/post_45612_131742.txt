Sky a major pay TV provider owned by Comcast saw record viewership for the premiere of House of the Dragon in the United Kingdom According to data obtained by The Hollywood Reporter million viewers turned in to the premiere of HBO s high profile prequel to Game of Thrones which is set years before the original series Nearly million either stayed up to watch at a m local time when it first appeared on the platform or downloaded and watched later that day with attributed to the p m prime time linear broadcast With those figures House of the Dragon surpasses Game of Thrones as the most popular drama to debut on Sky s premium Sky Atlantic channel and becomes the most popular American drama to debut on Sky overall
