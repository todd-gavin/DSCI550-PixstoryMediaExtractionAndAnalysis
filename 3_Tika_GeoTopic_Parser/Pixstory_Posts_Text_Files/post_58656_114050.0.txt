The riverfront of Ganges in holy city , Varanasi, is glittering on eve of PM Natendra Modi arrival here tomorrow noon. The PM would be giving the gift of different developmental schemes and projects worth Rs 1800 crores  to the masses of his Lok Sabha constituency. The security under supervision of SPG and local administration has been beefed up. The District Magistrate of Varanasi, Koushal Raj Sharma has directed to the school management to run classes upto 11 am only and let students go home ..
