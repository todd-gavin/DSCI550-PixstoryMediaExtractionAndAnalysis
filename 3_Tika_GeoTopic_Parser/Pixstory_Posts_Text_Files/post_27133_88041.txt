According to The Hollywood Reporter The Cannes Film Festival an austere French film festival which once banned selfies on the red carpet has now officially partnered with TikTok Cannes is reportedly looking to tap into TikTok s billion strong user base by supplying exclusive backstage content as well as talent interviews and red carpet glamour Timed to the Cannes deal TikTok officially announced the launch of TikTokShortFilm a global in app competition of vertical short films between seconds and three minutes in length A jury will judge the entries and three awards will be handed out in Cannes Commenting on the partnership Cannes director Thierry Fr maux said the TikTok deal was part of efforts to diversify the festival and would allow Cannes to share the magic of the event with a wider much more global and just as much of a cinephile audience than ever before
