India defeated Australia by 6 wickets in the third and final T20I. On the last ball of the game, India successfully chased down a 187-run goal thanks to 63 runs from Virat Kohli and 69 from Suryakumar Yadav. Early in the innings, India lost the wickets of KL Rahul and Rohit Sharma, but the Kohli-Suryakumar partnership contributed 104 runs for the third wicket to reclaim the lead.
Rahul was beat by Daniel Sams, while Pat Cummins outperformed Rohit. Earlier, Australia scored 186 for 7 in 20 overs with batting performances from Tim David and Cameron Green, who combined for 54 runs. Once again, Axar Patel shone with the bat, taking 3 for 33 in his four overs. India chose to field in the series finale after winning the toss.
