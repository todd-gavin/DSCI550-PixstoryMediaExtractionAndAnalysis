Video clip of Actor Kartik Aryan travelling in Economy Class recently went viral on the internet. In the video he was seen interacting with his fan in his flight from Jodhpur to Mumbai. It’s not the first time, that the Internet sensation is seen travelling economy class and when asked why he doesn’t travel via business class he jokingly replied that business class is ‘too expensive’.No matter how quick the actor received fame and popularity, he is the most down to earth and humble actor in town.
