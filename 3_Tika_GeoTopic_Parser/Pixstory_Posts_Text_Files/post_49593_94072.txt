Today we celebrate Jackie Robinson s th anniversary of shattering USA Major League Baseball s color barrier He is a role model to countless especially to an year old immigrant who also happens to be my mom She sailed from Shanghai to America and settled in Brooklyn NY Not knowing a lick of English it wasn t easy to make friends Then a miracle baseball happens It is and Jackie Robinson star of the Brooklyn Dodgers is everyone s hero Jackie is proving that a Black the grandson of a slave can make a difference in America And for my mom America became the land of opportunity Of all the books my mom authored the one recounting that first year in America In The Year Of The Boar and Jackie Robinson is still my favorite To this day I still get emails from adults who read the book years ago letters from th graders inviting me to speak to their class and packages with dog eared copies of the book with a note asking for my mom s autograph Thank you Jackie Robinson for smashing the color barrier and reminding us that America is a land that welcomes all humans regardless of race or origin
