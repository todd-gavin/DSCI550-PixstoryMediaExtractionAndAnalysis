Shortly after 3 p.m. ET, Hurricane Ian officially just made landfall as a Category 4 storm in southwest Florida, according to the National Hurricane Center:
Around 305 PM EDT, according to NOAA Doppler radar imagery, the eye of Ian made landfall near Cayo Costa on Florida's southwest coast (1905 UTC). Ian's maximum sustained winds, according to data from a reconnaissance aircraft operated by the Air Force Reserve, were thought to reach close to 150 mph (240 km/h).
