Timon and Pumbaa are the most loved disney duo. This adorable and lovable duo from The Lion King goes beyond the goal of best friends. Timon and Pumbaa are the epitome of friendship goals in all their antics. Timon and Pumbaa support each other through everything from Pumbaa's flatulence to their decision to adopt a dangerous lion cub together. In both animated classics and new live-action films, Timon and Pumbaa are easily the highlight of the film. #FriendshipDay22
