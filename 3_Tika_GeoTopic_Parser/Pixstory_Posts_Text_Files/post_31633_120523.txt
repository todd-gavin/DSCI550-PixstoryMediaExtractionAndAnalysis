Medium difficulty Ingredients Large quantity of onions Olive oil to taste Celery to taste Swiss chard Vegetable broth Stale bread Procedure Cut a large quantity of onions into slices and fry them in the olive oil Then add chopped celery and brown Mix the two ingredients well and combine coarsely chopped chard and vegetable broth. When cooked, pour piping hot on slices of stale bread arranged in layers in a bowl and leave to rest. It can be eaten hot or cold
