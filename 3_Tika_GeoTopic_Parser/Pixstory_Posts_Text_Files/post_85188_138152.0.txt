Two of the dominant teams across Europe, Sevilla and Man City will be taking on each other in the Group G match of the UEFA Champions league 2022-23 season at the Ramón Sánchez Pizjuán on September 07.
Man City, Sevilla, Dortmund, and Copenhagen are the 4 teams in Group G of the UCL 2022-23 campaign which will be fighting for the coveted European title. Eyes will be on Erling Haaland,as it will be his first European outing for City. Haaland has been in phenomenal form in thePremier League having announced his arrival in style.
City and Sevilla will be meeting for the 2nd time in UCL history. Prior to this, they met in the2015-16 season in the Group Stage where City won both the Home and Away legs.
