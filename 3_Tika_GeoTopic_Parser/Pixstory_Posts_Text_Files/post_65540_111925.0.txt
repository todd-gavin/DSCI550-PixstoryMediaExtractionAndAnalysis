Golden Temple is a historical place. It situated in amritsar. It's also known as harmandir sahib. 
It's a holy place for the sikhs. And all the people related to different religion visit there. significant shrine in Sikhism. It became a centre of the Singh Sabha Movement between 1883 and 1920s, and the Punjabi Suba movement between 1947 and 1966. In the early 1980s, the Gurdwara became a centre of conflict between the Indian government and a radical movement led by Jarnail Singh Bhindranwale.
