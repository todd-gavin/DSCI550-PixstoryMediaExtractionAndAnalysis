Human life is full of ups and downs. There is no such person who hasn’t witnessed hard times in his or her life. Without ups and downs, there is no existence in our life. It’s the law of nature. So, there is a possibility that your past was pretty harsh to you.

Well, we can understand that coming out of those situations is a pretty big deal. 
But you have to acknowledge the fact that your past is not going to come back to you. So, the best thing that you can do is to live your present. It is the most significant time of your life. Also, what you are doing now is going to decide your future.

So, if you want to witness a successful future, you have to come out from the darkness of your past. In that case, the best thing that you can do is to focus on your present. So, try to work hard and achieve the goal that you have always dreamt of.
