90% of DNA is shared by humans cats. As a result, they are remarkably similar to us in many aspects, which enables us to "read" them and understand their actions and movements. On four paws, they resemble cute miniature humans. Their eyes are in front of their heads, unlike those of most other animals, giving them a visage that appears more human-like than those of many other creatures. Additionally, they have lovely, expressive eyes that help us interact with them nonverbally. They have adapted well to us, utilising meows to "speak human," showing that they are familiar with us and our traits. If you've ever noticed that mature cats meow just at humans in an effort to communicate with us.
