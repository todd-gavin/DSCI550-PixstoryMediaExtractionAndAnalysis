
Ethan Vaz has maintained his winning momentum by stunning the second seed FM Movahed Sina (ELO 2345) of Iran on the top board in the third round of the World Cadets Open U-12 Chess championship being played at Batumi, Georgia, on Sunday.
The 11-year-old Goan won his first two matches against Turkey's Utlu Mustafa Ali (1386) and Canada's Zhu Charis (1350).
