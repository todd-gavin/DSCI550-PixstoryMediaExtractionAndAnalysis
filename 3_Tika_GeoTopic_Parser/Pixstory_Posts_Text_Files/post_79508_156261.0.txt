MP Police register case against neighbour couple in Vaishali Thakkar's suicide. 
A day after television actress Vaishali Thakkar committed suicide at her house in Indore. MP Home Minister Narottam Mishra said state police have registered a case of abetment of neighbourhood. 
A case under Indian Penal Code Section 306 was registered against her neighbour Rahul Navlani and his wife Disha in the matter and further investigation is underway. A five pages note was found at the spot in which Vaishali named Rahul for harnessing her.
