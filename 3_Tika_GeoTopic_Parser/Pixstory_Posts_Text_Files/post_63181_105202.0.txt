In the midst of a scandal over former BJP spokespersons Naveen Jindal and Nupur Sharma's remarks against Prophet Mohammed and his wife, Congress leader Rahul Gandhi declared on Sunday night that it is time to unite India. Four countries have issued official statements in response to the remarks, with Saudi Arabia being the most recent to join Iran, Qatar, and Kuwait. "Hatred simply spawns more hate. India can only prosper if it follows the path of love and brotherhood. It is past time for India (sic) to unite "Gandhi, 51, used the hashtag #BharatJodo in his tweet (unite India).
