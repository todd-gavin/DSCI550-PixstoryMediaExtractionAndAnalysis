Two US tourists, a year-old woman and her partner caused thousands of dollars of damage to the Spanish Steps in Rome by throwing their scooters down the historic steps. It was around June at night when the two were stopped by the police The video of their scooter bouncing from step to step has gone round the Net Now the two fined with a euro fine cannot approach the staircase for six months, one of the main Roman tourist attractions which was built in and connects the Piazza of Spain in Piazza Trinit dei Monti
