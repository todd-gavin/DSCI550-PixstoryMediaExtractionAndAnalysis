In order to develop the culture of reading in the province a number of Badakhshan University students held a street book exibition entitled Let s Read for the Development of the Book Society The fair was organized in coordination with the Taliban Information and Culture Department in Badakhshan and a bookstore in Faizabad the capital of Badakhshan province Following the recent political upheaval the culture of reading among Afghanistan s youth has waned The purpose of holding this exhibition along with institutionalizing the culture of reading and reading was to show that the people of Afghanistan especially the people of Badakhshan have chosen the path of reading and reading And launching such exhibitions encourages young people to buy books and read Earlier some bookstores across Afghanistan including in Badakhshan province were closed due to lack of markets and bookstore owners have changed professions
