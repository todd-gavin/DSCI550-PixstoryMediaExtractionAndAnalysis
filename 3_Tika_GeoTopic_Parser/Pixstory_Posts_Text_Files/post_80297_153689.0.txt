New National Record Set by Shivangi Sarma in National Aquatic Championship
In the just completed 75th Senior National Aquatic Championship 2022, held in Guwahati on Saturday, Assam swimmer Shivangi Sarma won the gold medal.
Shivangi, who is from Sivasagar, won the medal in the competition held at the Dr. Zakir Hussain Aquatic Complex in Sarusajai, Guwahati, after swimming the 100 metres freestyle in 57.85 seconds, breaking the previous record.
