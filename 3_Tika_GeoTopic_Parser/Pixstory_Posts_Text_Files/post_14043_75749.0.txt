Claudio Ranieri has been sacked by Watford as their Manager, he has been incharge of the team for just three months. The former Premier League winner joined the club after Xisco Munoz was sacked in October 2021, at that time the team was at 14th place. Since then Watford have only managed to pick up 7 points and are at the 19th place and will get relegated if things remain the same.
Claudio Ranieri has been sacked after the team lost 3 - 0 to Norwich City on Friday.
