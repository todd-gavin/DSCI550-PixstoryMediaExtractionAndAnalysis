Playback singer Krishnakumar Kunnath, well known as KK, died at 53 shortly after performing at a concert in Kolkata's Nazrul Mancha. He collapsed after the show and was rushed to a hospital in the city where he was declared dead.

He recorded songs in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Bengali, Assamese, and Gujarati languages.

A great loss to the music industry. Sir, you will be missed.
