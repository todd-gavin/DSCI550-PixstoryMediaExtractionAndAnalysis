Finland will be looking to get their first victory as they face Montenegro for the current edition of the
UEFA Nations League at the Helsinki stadium.

Visitors Montenegro have enjoyed a better start to the tournament, picking up three points against
Romania 2-0. Hosts Finland however, were able to scrap a 1-1 against Bosnia and Herzegovina.

With both teams targeting promotion, this fixture becomes incredibly important with Montenegro
getting the opportunity to put clear daylight between themselves and other teams in the group.

The two sides have never met before, in what is shaping to be a very fascinating fixture.

Finland vs Montenegro: Predictions

Finland have found themselves in a rich vein of form at home, winning 5 out of their last 7 matches.

Visitors Montenegro have struggled away.
However, the two teams find themselves evenly matched with
the match likely to end in a draw.

Prediction: Finland 1 - 1 Montenegro
