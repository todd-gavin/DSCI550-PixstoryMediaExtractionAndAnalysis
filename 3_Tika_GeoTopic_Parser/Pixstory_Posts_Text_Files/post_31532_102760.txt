Friend of the Sea is a project of the World Sustainability Organization WSO for the certification of products and services that respect the marine habitat and aquatic species. Fish products and Omega supplements from sustainable fisheries and aquaculture can be certified if they pass compliance audits meet stringent sustainability requirements Audits are performed by qualified auditors of independent, nationally accredited certification bodies Other products and services may apply for Friend of the Sea certification such as sustainable salt restaurants and chefs UV creams ornamental fish public aquariums seaweed operators of shipping and whale and dolphin watching operators Over a thousand companies have products certified to WSO standards in over countries Friend of the Sea also implements its own conservation projects and campaigns to protect endangered species and habitats
