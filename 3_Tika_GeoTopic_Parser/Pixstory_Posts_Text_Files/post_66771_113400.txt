Written by Brazilian author Paulo Coelho in It was initially written and published in Portuguese The story is about a Shepherd boy from Spain whose name is Santiago He keeps getting the same dream about treasures that are lying in the Pyramids of Egypt He embarks on a journey to follow his dream after meeting an old king who offers him magic stones and advice Santiago crosses the Mediterranean and Sahara to find his treasures in Egypt and also accomplish his personal legend which is his purpose in life The book details his journey and the various encounters that he experiences when following his dream Throughout the journey Santiago meets many new people and a lot of difficulty which ultimately helps him to learn and grow the entire way The Alchemist is a fantastic book and the storytelling is beautiful The choice of words are impeccable full of wisdom and philosophy I totally loved it Ny favorite quote from the book is One is loved because one is loved No reason is needed for loving
