The Chamber has decided that the use of animals to pull the casks will no longer be permitted in the carriages that offer tours in the centers of Italian cities. of civility Lega and Fratelli d Italia vote against The agenda is part of the Infrastructure and Transport decree The text states that the government undertakes to prohibit the use of animals to drive vehicles to the square service and for non-scheduled public services aimed at the transport of people for tourist and recreational purposes throughout the national territory The executive also promises to provide for administrative sanctions and confiscation of the vehicle and of the animal in the event of unauthorized transport It is also provided that abused animals are entrusted to those who can take care of them and that the licenses for the conduct of the casks are converted into licenses for driving electric carriages or taxis
