Delhi College of Arts and Commerce is accepting applications for UG admission through Common University Entrance Test (CUET). The last date to register for CUET UG 2022 and corrections in the application form has been extended to June 24, 2022. Aspiring candidates can apply online on the official website - cuet.samarth.ac.in. CUET UG 2022 exam is scheduled to be held from July 15 to August 10, 2022. Delhi College of Arts and Commerce has also started the admission process for PG courses through DUET. The last date to register for DUET is June 30, 2022.

Delhi College of Arts and Commerce is a co-educational institute affiliated with Delhi University. The college is located in Netaji Nagar in the South campus of Delhi University. DCAC is accredited by NAAC with a grade of ‘B+’ and is ranked 31st in the Arts Department by India Today 2021. It is also ranked 26th in the Commerce category by India Today 2021. Similarly, Outlook 2021 has also ranked Delhi College of Arts and Commerce 21st in the Commerce academy.
