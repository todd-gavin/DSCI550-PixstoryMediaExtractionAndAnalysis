Roy Krishna, a striker for the Fijian national team, has signed a two-year contract with Bengaluru FC, giving their frontline further firepower. Krishna, 34, played in the Indian Super League for ATK FC and ATK Mohun Bagan before becoming Fiji's captain and scoring the most goals and making the most appearances for his nation. Following the conclusion of the legalities on his signing, Krishna said, "I had chats with the coach (Simon Grayson) when I was in the early stages of negotiations and he truly convinced me that I would have an influence at the club.
