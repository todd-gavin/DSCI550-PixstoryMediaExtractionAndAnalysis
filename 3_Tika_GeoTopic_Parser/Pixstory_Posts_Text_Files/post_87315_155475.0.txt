Japan and Canada will be up against each other on Matchday 02 of the FIFA Under 17 Women’s World Cup 2022. While Japan are on the top spot in Group D of the FIFA Under 17 Women’s World Cup points table, Canada sit at the second spot in the points table.
Japan has played 1 game this season in FIFA U17 Women’s World Cup 2022. They have managed to win the game. In the process, they have scored 4 goals and conceded no goal. Japan beat Tanzania in their first group match of the Under 17 World Cup.
Canada, on the other hand, have played 1 game this season in FIFA U17 Women’s World Cup 2022 and they haven’t won this game. In this process, they have scored 1 goal and have conceded 01 goal. Canada drew against France in their first game of the U17 World Cup.
