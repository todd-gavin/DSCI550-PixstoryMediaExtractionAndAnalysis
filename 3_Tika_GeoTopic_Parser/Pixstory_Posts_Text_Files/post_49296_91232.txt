Howlin Wolf Chester Arthur Burnett spent the majority of his childhood in Mississippi and Arkansas as a farmer However growing up in the Delta at the time provided him with unrivalled access to the region s blues masters Charley Patton taught him guitar methods and showmanship Sonny Boy Williamson II taught him the ins and outs of the harmonica and was married to Wolf s step sister Maggie and he tried to imitate Jimmie Rodgers yodelling Robert Johnson Johnny Shines Honeyboy Edwards Son House and Willie Brown were among his collaborators For a short time Burnett was married to Brown s sister He also took up additional jobs to assist pay the bills One of these was selling advertising on West Memphis Arkansas s KWEM radio station On the station he would also play his music during a minute planter s show which led to a recording contract
