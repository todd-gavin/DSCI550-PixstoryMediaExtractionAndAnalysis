Fotu La Top is a high mountain pass on the Srinagar Leh Highway in Zanskar Range that attracts photo enthusiasts It provides wonderful opportunities for taking pictures as you get an amazing view of Himalayan peaks and other places near Srinagar The scenic beauty lunar like landscape and breezy weather make the drive to Fotu La Top entertaining with its naked hills and snow crowned mountains greet you with a warm welcome to Ladakh The pass separates the city of Leh from Kargil A drive further from Fotu La takes you to Lamayuru a picturesque town at Leh
