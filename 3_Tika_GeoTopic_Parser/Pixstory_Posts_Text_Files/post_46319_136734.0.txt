- Milk - 2 litres

- Crushed coriander seeds - 1 tsp

- Chilli flakes - 1 tsp
- Fresh coriander - 1 tsp
- Fresh mint - 1 tsp

Steps to follow ; ğŸ‘©â€ğŸ³
1 Boil the milk , once it once to a good boil , add coriander seeds , pepper and chilli flakes and mix well .
2 Add 2 tbsp vinegar and mix .
3 Milk will start to curdle .
4 mix well and add another two tbsp of vinegar and let it boil on low to med heat till the water separates .
5 Strain through a Muslin cloth and squeeze the paneer to remove the water .
6 Add cold water and squeeze paneer again .
7 Add salt and repeat the process .
8 Then tie the paneer tightly in the cloth and place a heavy object on it for 30 mins .
9 It will set well by then , you can slice and use ! 
You can alternate vinegar with lemon or curd as well .
