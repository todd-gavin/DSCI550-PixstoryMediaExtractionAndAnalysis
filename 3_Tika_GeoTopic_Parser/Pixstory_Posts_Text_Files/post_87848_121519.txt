TIPS TO INCREASE YOUR WATER INTAKE While everyone knows that it s important to stay hydrated doing so can be difficult at times SET A REMINDER ALARM Try setting a reminder to take a few sips of water every minutes or set a reminder to finish drinking your current glass of water and refill it every hour DRINK A GLASS OF WATER WITH EACH MAIN MEAL If you eat main meals per day this adds an extra cups mL to your daily water intake FLAVOUR YOUR WATER If you dislike the flavor of water or just need a bit of flavor to help you drink more you have many choices such as Diet Cordial or adding slices of fresh orange or lemon EAT FOODS HIGH IN WATER Fruits and vegetables that are particularly high in water include lettuce celery zucchini watermelon rockmelon cabbage and honeydew melon KEEP A REUSABLE DRINK BOTTLE WITH YOU When you have a reusable water bottle you can easily drink water in any setting whether you re running errands traveling or at home work or school
