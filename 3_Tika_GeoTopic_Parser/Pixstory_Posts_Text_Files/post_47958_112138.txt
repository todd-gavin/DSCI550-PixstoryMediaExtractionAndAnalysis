By NewsDrum Desk New Delhi Delhi Police on Saturday produced Alt News co founder Mohammed Zubair before a court here in a case related to an objectionable tweet he had posted in against a Hindu deity and sought his day judicial custody Zubair was produced before the court on expiry of his five day custodial interrogation The police told Chief Metropolitan Magistrate Snigdha Sarvaria that he was not required anymore for custodial interrogation After the police application Zubair moved a bail plea before the court
