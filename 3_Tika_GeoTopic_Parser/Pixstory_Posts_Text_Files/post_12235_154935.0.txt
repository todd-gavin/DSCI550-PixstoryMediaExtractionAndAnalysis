What is Cloud Computing ?
Everyone might have used Google drive, once in their lifetime ... what is drive ? It is basically cloud storage ... it saves space of your computer and adds your data in cloud storage.
Cloud Computing is  storing and accessing of data and computing services over the internet. It doesn’t take of space of data on your personal computer.
The main purpose of cloud computing is to give access to data centers to many users. Users can also access data from a remote server.
