In 1967, His Holiness the 14th Dalai Lama made his first visits abroad since coming into exile, visiting Japan and Thailand. 

In 1973, His Holiness made his first visit to the West, visiting 12 European countries in a record 75 days! His first visit to the Americas was to the United States and Canada in 1979.
