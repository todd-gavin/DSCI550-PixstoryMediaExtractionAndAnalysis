Udinese Calcio announces that Gabriele Cioffi will not renew his contract expiring in June The club is already working on planning for next season and setting up a new technical staff capable of maintaining and improving the results achieved with the same ambition and passion This is the press release official appeared on the Udinese company website The bianconeri therefore have no intention of continuing the relationship with the coach Cioffi who already seems projected towards a new adventure in Serie A with Verona in pole position to grab him
