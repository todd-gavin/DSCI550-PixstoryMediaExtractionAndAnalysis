While the McLaren driver Daniel Ricciardo has already recovered from the covid and this weekend he will be able to participate in the Bahrain GP, the inaugural race of the Formula world championship, Sebastian Vettel will not be there instead. The German driver who may have been infected by the Australian himself during the tests when Ricciardo showed up for the anti-war photo in Ukraine before being confined to the hotel, tested positive for the swab and will be replaced by his compatriot Nico Hulkenberg
