UMANG Umang is one of the top college fests in Asia The amazing part of this fest is that it is organised all by the students themselves from organising to bringing sponsors to managing the security to food stalls and much more is handled by the students Umang is a great opportunity for the students of the college to have experience in managing events and at the same time enjoy their college lives It is a confidence booster for all the shy and introverts out there
