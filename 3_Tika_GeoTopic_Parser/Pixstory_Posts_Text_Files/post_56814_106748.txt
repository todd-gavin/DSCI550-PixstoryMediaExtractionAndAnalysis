Vegan Leather is leather made without the use of animal skin Vegan leather is made from a range of different materials It can be made from vegetan pineapple leaves PVC glazed cotton cork apple skin polyurethane PU and even cactus Vegan leather comes in several forms and qualities so some are more leather like than others Generally speaking and focusing on good quality vegan leather there isn t much difference to natural leather However as vegan leather is synthetic it doesn t form a patina like genuine leather does when ageing On the other hand it is much less breathable as the pores printed onto the surface of the pleather are artificial These are some brands one can choose to buy for their love of leather and guilt free fashion Stella McCartney Will s Vegan Shoes Ethique Angela Roi Beyond Skin Urban Expressions Wilby Brave GentleMan
