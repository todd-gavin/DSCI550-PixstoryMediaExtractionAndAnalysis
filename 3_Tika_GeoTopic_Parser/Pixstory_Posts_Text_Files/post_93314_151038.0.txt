NAVRASA- THE 9 EMOTIONS OF LIFE 
The word NAVRASA has its origin in sanskrit and is a combination of two words. "NAV" meaning "nine" and "RASA" meaning "emotions". Put together, it translates to nine emotions that define the whole essence of our lives ! Isn't it beautiful ?ðŸ¦‹
The ancient Sanskrit scholars defined the 9 emotions as- 
Shringara (love/beauty); 
Hasya (laughter); 
Karuna (sorrow); 
Raudra (anger); 
Veera (courage); 
Bhayanak (fear); 
Bibhatsa (disgust); 
Adbutha (surprise) and 
Shantha (peace)
