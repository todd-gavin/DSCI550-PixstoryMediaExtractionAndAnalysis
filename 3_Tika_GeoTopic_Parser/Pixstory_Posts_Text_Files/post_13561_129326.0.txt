In the latest ranking report of 146 countries, Afghanistan, along with Lebanon and Zimbabwe, was once again at the bottom of the list of the happiest countries in the world.

After the domination of the Taliban, Afghanistan's position in this global report has not changed.

In this report, the ranking of happiness and prosperity of countries is determined based on the evaluation of factors such as gross domestic product, healthy life expectancy, financial and administrative corruption, individual freedoms and social services.

In the report of the happiest countries in the world in 2022, the Scandinavian countries of Finland, Denmark and Iceland are at the top of the list. But in terms of quality of life, Switzerland, followed by Denmark and the Netherlands, is mentioned as the top country in this report.

Unhappy countries in this list are Afghanistan, Lebanon, Zimbabwe and Rwanda.
