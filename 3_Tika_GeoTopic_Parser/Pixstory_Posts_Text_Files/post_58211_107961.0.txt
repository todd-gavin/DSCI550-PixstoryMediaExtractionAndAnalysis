Hometown cha cha cha is sweet heart touching drama ,which decribes the life of city dentist who moves to countryside and meets a chief hong the person who does all sorts of odd jobs in the village. It a heart warming drama without any major conflicts which rouch your soul.

But most importantly it has helped increase tourism in the city  of Pohang where the drama was shot. Tourists have been flocking to popular filmong sites like Oh Yoon’s Live Cafe & Pub, Yoon’s Dental Clinic, Hardware Store, Bora’s Supermarket, Gongjin Market, the boat on Hilltop, and Red Lighthouse.

Yoon Hye-jin’s dental clinic has also been converted into a restaurant that serves warm meals.
