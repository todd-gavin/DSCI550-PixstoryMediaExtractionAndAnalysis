After Mizoram, cases of African Swine Fever (ASF) have been detected in Tripura's government-run breeding farm located at Devipur in Sepahijala district, top sources in the department told ANI requesting anonymity.

A team of experts from Agartala's disease investigation Centre reached the farm and formed rapid response teams to handle the situation.
