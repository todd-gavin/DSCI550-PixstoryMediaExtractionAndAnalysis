Soorarai Pottru won five awards at the 68th National Film Awards, including best feature film, best actor award to Suriya, best actress award to Aparrna Balamurali, best original screenplay, and GV Prakash was awarded the best background music.
Directed by Sudha Kongara, Soorarai Pottru is based on the life of Simplify Deccan founder Captain GR Gopinath, and featured Suriya along with Paresh Rawal and Aparna Balamurali in the lead roles. The film was released on Amazon Prime Video on November 12, 2020. And finaly,
