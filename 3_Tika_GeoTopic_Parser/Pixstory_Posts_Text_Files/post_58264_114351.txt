Delicacies Destiny a Chinese drama has been accused of plagiarizing an acclaimed Korean drama titled Jewel in the Palace also known by its Korean name Dae Jang Geum The drams which streamed on disney worldwide in all countries except south korea suspicions of plagiarism arose on July after an online community titled Right Now on Disney shared a series of stills from Delicacies Destiny On seeing the pictures many netizens commented that the Chinese drama seemed strikingly similar to that of the renowned Korean drama Jewel in the Palace and pointed out that the characters in the former are dressed in apparels that look like Hanbok the traditional Korean attire The drama revolves around a woman who because of her culinary skills is asked cook for royalty where she finds herself in love with a prince
