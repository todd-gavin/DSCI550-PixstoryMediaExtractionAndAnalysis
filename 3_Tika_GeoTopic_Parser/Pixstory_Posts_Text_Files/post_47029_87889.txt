Wall sit and chair pose The wall sit and chair pose are quite similar and involve replicating sitting in a chair The chair pose is a standard yoga asana whereas in case of the wall sit you do the same thing but with back support back flat against the wall In both these static holds you need to keep your spine straight and knees bent at an angle of degrees Both these holds are excellent workout for your lower body especially the quadriceps hamstrings calves and ankles Wall sit and chair pose The wall sit and chair pose are quite similar and involve replicating sitting in a chair The chair pose is a standard yoga asana whereas in case of the wall sit you do the same thing but with back support back flat against the wall In both these static holds you need to keep your spine straight and knees bent at an angle of degrees Both these holds are excellent workout for your lower body especially the quadriceps hamstrings calves and ankles
