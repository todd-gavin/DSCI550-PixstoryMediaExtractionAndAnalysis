The Delhi High Court is hearing various petitions challenging the validity of an exception to section 375 of the Indian Penal Code, which exempts forcible intercourse between a man and his wife if the wife is over 15 years of age.A number of committees have proposed criminalising marital rape. The J.S. Verma Committee had suggested that the law should state that a relationship between a man and woman is not a valid defence for rape.The Pam Rajput committee in 2015 also suggested making marital rape an offence. Around 50 countries have already
made it an offence.
