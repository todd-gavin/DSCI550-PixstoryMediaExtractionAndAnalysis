Cosmetics is a mixture of chemical compound. People use cosmetics in their daily life for various purposes :- 
1 :- it is used to cleanse the body or skin.
2 :- it can also be designed to add fragrance to the body.
3 :-  it can be used to enhance one's natural features and change the appearance of the face. 
   If we are using cosmetic products than we  should also do proper skin care to protect skin from chemical compound.
