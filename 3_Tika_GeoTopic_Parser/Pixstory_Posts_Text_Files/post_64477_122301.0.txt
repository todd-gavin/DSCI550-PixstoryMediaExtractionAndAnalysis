Striker Gurkirat Singh scored 4 goals including a hat-trick while Himanshu Jangra scored the other was India U20 routed Bangladesh U20 5-2 in the final to emerge champions in the SAFF U20 Championship at the Kalinga Stadium in Bhubaneswar on Friday (August 5, 2022).
 
Both teams were locked 1-1 at the interval, and 2-2 after completion of regulation time. Gurkirat scored in the 1st, 60thth, 94th and the 99th minutes respectively, while Himanshu scored in the 92nd minute. For Bangladesh, Rajon Howladar and Shahin Mia scored in the 44th and 48th minutes.
