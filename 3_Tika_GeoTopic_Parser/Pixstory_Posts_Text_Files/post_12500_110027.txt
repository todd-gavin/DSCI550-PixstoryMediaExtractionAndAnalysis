One may even contribute directly to another Those suffering from depression have an increased likelihood of substance abuse while those with a substance use disorder are at an increased risk for depression It s a vicious cycle The majority of people with a depressive disorder who abuse substances do so to self medicate By abusing drugs or alcohol they may find a boost in their moods or a reduction of negative feelings At least at first However long term use of these substances can trigger or escalate sadness lethargy and other negative consequences Moreover as individuals become dependent on substances as a means of escape it may prevent them from seeking treatment In this guide we ll explore the relationship between depression and substance abuse along with steps you can take to recognize the warning signs and how to seek help
