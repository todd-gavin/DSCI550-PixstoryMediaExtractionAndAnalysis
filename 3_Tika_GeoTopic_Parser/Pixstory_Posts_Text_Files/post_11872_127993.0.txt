Do you have irregular / missed periods ?? ☹️

Have you heard of seed cycle ?? 
Seed cycle is one of the easiest ways you can support your hormones in a gentle , yet effective way ! More and more women are struggling with hormone imbalance ! ✅

Seed cycle is a practice of eating specific seeds during the two main phases of menstrual cycle to help promote the healthy balance of estrogen and progesterone level . ✅👍

How to do it ?? 🤔

1 - 14th day of periods -
1 tbsp pumpkin seeds powder and flaxseed power each . 

15 - 28th day of periods - 
1 tbsp sesame seeds powder and sunflower seed powder each . 

Do this for 3 months and more to regularise your periods . ✅
