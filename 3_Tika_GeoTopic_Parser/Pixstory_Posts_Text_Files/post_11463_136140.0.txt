Your dinner soup is right here 😋 🍲 
Want to do something about the leftover veggies in your fridge, here is the right recipe for you. Gather all the leftover veggies and let's get started with this simple yet healthy soup filled with leftover veggies. 
Ingredients 
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil 
• 1 large onion, finely chopped 
• 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped 
• 2 large carrots, peeled and chopped into bite-sized chunks 
• 2 celery ribs, chopped into bite-sized chunks 
• 3 cups vegetables, chopped into bite-sized chunks 
• 2 1/2 cups stock, vegetable, beef or chicken 
• 1 bay leaf 
• 1 teaspoon five-spice powder, optional 
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin 
• Salt, to taste 
• Freshly ground black pepper, to taste 
Directions to follow 
• Gather all the ingredients and take a large stockpot and heat the vegetable oil. 
• Add in all the chopped vegetable and stir them. 
• Add in garlic, carrots and celery and cook them for 5 minutes without burning the garlic. 
• Add the onions and cook them till they become softened. 
• Add in the stock, bay leaf and any other spices that you would like. 
• Cook them till all the veggies become soft and cooked well for about 20 minutes. 
• Now blend the soup and make it as creamy or chunky as you wish. 
• Cook the soup for additional 5 minutes and slightly reduce the amount of it. 
• Season it up with salt and pepper to taste and serve it hot.
