Pro Kabaddi League (PKL) is the highest form of competition for men's kabaddi in India, it started in 2014 and till date has completed 8 seasons. 
These are the winning teams across the seasons:
S1 - Jaipur Pink Panthers
S2 - U Mumba
S3 - Patna Pirates
S4 - Patna Pirates
S5 - Patna Pirates
S6 -  Bengaluru Bulls
S7 -  Bengal Warriors
