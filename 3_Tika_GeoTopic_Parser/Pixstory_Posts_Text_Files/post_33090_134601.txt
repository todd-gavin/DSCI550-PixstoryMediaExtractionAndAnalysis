Silvana Stanco earns the first blue pass for Paris Rain of gold medals at the European clay pigeon shooting The European clay pigeon shooting of Fossa and Skeet are underway in Larnaca, offering the first Olympic passes and Italy immediately hit the target with Silvana Stanco who graduated as European champion in women&#39;s trap by overcoming the British Lucy Hall in an error-free final Jessica Rossi, Olympic gold medalist in London, won bronze in the individual competition. La Stanco was the first Italian athlete to win a pass in any discipline for Paris. In the event in pairs then came the gold for Jessica Rossi and Daniele Resca while in the team event the golds were two with Stanco Grassia and Rossi among women and De Filippis Ferrari and Resca among men
