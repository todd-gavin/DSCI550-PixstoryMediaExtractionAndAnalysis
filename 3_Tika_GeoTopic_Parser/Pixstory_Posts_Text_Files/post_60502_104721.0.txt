"I PROMISE YOU THE MOON", "SEMANTIC ERROR", AND "MY BEAUTIFUL MAN" ARE THE MINI BL SERIES THAT ARE SUBMITTED TO SEOUL INTERNATIONAL DRAMA AWARDS 2022.

Seoul International Drama Awards (Korean: S 200), simply known as SDA, is an annual award ceremony based in Secul, South Korea which honors excellence in television drama productions worldwide.

This year, 3 popular mini BL series are submitted to SDA

for nomination which are Thailand's I PROMISE YOU THE

MOON, Korea's SEMANTIC ERROR, and Japan's MY BEAUTIFUL MAN. Last year, BKPP bagged the Most Popular Foreign Drama

of the Year and PP also won the Asian Star Prize.
