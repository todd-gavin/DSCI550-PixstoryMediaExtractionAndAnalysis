We see a lot of dysphoria around discussions like this, but it's high time we started paying attention to it. 

SPD is not your common cold, that will just be a minor inconvenience for you as you go on about your work. It changes people and how they see the world. 

The people suffering from the disorder already have a lot to deal with on their plate, so we need to focus if we are not creating any more problems for them, and should make sure to do everything in our power to make them feel safe and comfortable around us. 

That is what the basic decency expects us to do
