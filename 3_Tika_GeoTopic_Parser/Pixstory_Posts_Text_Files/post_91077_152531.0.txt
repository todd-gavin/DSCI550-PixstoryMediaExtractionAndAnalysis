Aamir Khan and Kareena Kapoor Khan’s Laal Singh Chaddha finally gets its OTT release on Thursday.Laal Singh Chaddha initially released on 11 August and the makers of the film were waiting patiently for the “two month waiting  period” to get over so that the film could be released online. While earlier it was discussed that the film will get its OTT release on 20th of October, Netflix streamed it live on 6th October right in the midnight!According to reports, Aamir Khan demanded Rs 150 crore deal with Netflix for signing the contract of the OTT release but late negotiated and the amount came down to nearly 80 crores.
