Beyonce has dropped the first single from her much-awaited seventh solo studio album Renaissance. Titled Break My Soul, the song brings together the team of Single Ladies, including Tricky Stewart and The Dream, and is already being declared a hit. The lyrics have also got everyone talking as Beyonce sings, “Release your anger, release your mind, release your job, release the time, release your trade, release the stress, release the love, forget the rest.”

The writing credits include the singer’s rapper husband Jay-Z, along with Freddie Ross and Adam Pigott. Beyonce had previously announced on Thursday that her long-expected album will be arriving on July 29. While the wait for the complete album remains, following its midnight release, netizens have been hooked to the new track.
