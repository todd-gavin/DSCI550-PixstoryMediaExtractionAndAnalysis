According to the latest exit polls, there might be a hung assembly in Goa as no party getting a full majority of 21 seats to form the government. 

Out of the 40 seats , both major players BJP and congress might be getting 16 seats each while the remaining share going to the regional parties.

Now, any of the two parties will be getting a complete majority or the exit polls will be proven true, we will get to know after today's count.
