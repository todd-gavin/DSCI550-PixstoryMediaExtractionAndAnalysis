The PDP leader and former Chief Minister of Jammu and Kashmir, Mehbooba Mufti Sayeed has alleged to be house arrested by the government in Kashmir

Mufti is the president of the PDP and was a member of the Indian parliament, representing Anantnag in the 16th Lok Sabha; before she was sworn in as the Chief Minister of J&K. 1st women CM of Jammu And Kashmir. She also represented Anantnag in the 14th Lok Sabha (2004–09).
She was born on   May 22, 1959 .
In her tweet, she has shared the lock on gate of her house and deployment of CRPF . Mufti said, she was not allowed to go the house of Sunil Bhatt, who was slain by terrorists on August 16.
