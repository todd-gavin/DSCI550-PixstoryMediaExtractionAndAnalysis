What was supposed to be the 2020 U-17 Women's World Cup will now be held in October 2022.

India is poised to host the FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup in 2022.

After the tournament's 2020 edition was canceled, India was named as the host country for the 2022 U-17 World Cup finals, making it the country's second FIFA event.

England won the men's U-17 World Cup in 2017, which India hosted successfully.

 

When and where will the FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup be held in the year 2022?

The FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup will be held in India in 2022, marking the first time the country has hosted a FIFA women's competition.

This will be the tournament's seventh edition, which will begin on October 11, 2022, and conclude on October 30, 2022, with the final.

 

When and where will the FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup draw take place in 2022?

According to a statement made by FIFA on Wednesday, the 2022 FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup draw will take place on June 24, 2022, in Zurich.

So far, six countries—Brazil, Chile, China PR, Colombia, Japan, and New Zealand—have confirmed their participation in the competition and will be included in the draw.

 

The remaining nine teams competing for gold in the event will be determined by their results in their respective continental competitions.

The final competition will be divided into four groups of four teams, with the champion and runner-up from each group advancing to the quarter-finals.

 

Cities to host the FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup in 2022

The FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup was intended to be held in five different cities in 2022. The number of sites has been cut to three following a recent assessment by FIFA Director of Tournaments Jaime Yarza. As a result, the following cities will host the tournament:

 

Bhubaneswar's Kalinga Stadium

Navi Mumbai's DY Patil Stadium

Goa: Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium, Goa
