The popular korean drama series Extraordinary Attorney woo is getting a webtoon adaptation. The series has shattered all records and is appreciated not only among korean but international fans as well. It follows the story of a autistic lawyer and troubles she faces to be accepted in society. Naver has announced a webtoon adaptation,It was   released on 27 July. Currently it is available only in Korean language , but it will soon be translated in english , Japanese and Chinese as well .
