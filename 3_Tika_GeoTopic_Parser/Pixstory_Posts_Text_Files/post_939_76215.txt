Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella and Google s Sundar Pichai are among the Padma Bhushan awardees this year Padma Bhushan is the third highest civilian award in India after Bharat Ratna and Padma Vibhushan The Padma honours are given for exceptional service in different fields art social work public affairs science and engineering trade and industry medicine literature and education sports and civil service Indian American business executives Mr Nadella and Mr Pichai have been honoured for their contribution in the trade and industry category
