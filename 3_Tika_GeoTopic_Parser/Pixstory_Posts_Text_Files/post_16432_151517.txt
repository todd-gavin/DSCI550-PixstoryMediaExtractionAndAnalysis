Saif Ali Khan who over the years has established himself as one of the most versatile actors in the Hindi film industry is seen sharing screen space with Hrithik Roshan in filmmaker duo Pushkar Gayathri s recent movie Vikram Vedha While the Tamil version starring R Madhavan and Vijay Sethupathi was a huge hit the Hindi remake made with a massive budget of Rs crore has failed to recreate the magic in the domestic circuit From Parampara his debut movie to Bunty Aur Babli Saif has come a long way in his career and won several awards for his performances The year old actor was also one of the first A listers to appear in an OTT show like Sacred Games and proved his mettle as an actor But Saif Ali Khan s journey hasn t been smooth sailing as he faced a few roadblocks in the form of failed projects and also turned down films that went on to become huge hits Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge Devdas Kuch Kuch Hota Hai States Race Talaash The Answer Lies Within
