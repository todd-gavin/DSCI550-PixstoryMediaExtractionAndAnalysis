After ten days of waiting, Suzuki finally issues an official press release in which it announces its intention to leave MotoGP. At this point the fight opens to grab the two slots vacated by the Japanese manufacturer that could be occupied by Aprilia which already last year tried to have a satellite team and a new team, it is rumored that Leopard Racing is currently engaged in motorcycles. A lot of work from do also for the two riders who will now have to find a new team Joan Mir seems to be in the orbit of Honda while for Alex Rins the possibilities are currently all and none
