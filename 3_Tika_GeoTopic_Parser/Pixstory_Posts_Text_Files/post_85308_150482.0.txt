Empoli and Milan will be up against each other on Matchday 08 of the Serie A 2022-23 season. While Empoli are on the 14th spot of the Serie A points table, Milan sit at the 5th position in the points table.
Empoli are having a nightmare start to their Serie A campaign with 1 win coming in the 7 games that they have played so far this season. Their last 3 matches have ended in a draw whereas, in the last 5 games, they have secured the only win that has come in their favor this season..
Milan too, have been suffering after an explosive start to their campaign in Serie A. With 4 wins, 2 draws, and a loss so far in the 7 games, Milan will have to step up to stay in contention for the Serie A title this season.
