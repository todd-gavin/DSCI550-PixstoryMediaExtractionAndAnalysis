UN Secretary General The suspension of girls education is a violation of equal rights that harm Afghanistan The UN Secretary General once again deeply regretted the non reopening of girls schools in Afghanistan saying that the suspension of girls education was an unjustifiable violation of equal rights that would harm the whole country Antonio Guterres emphasizes support for Afghan women s rights saves children from starvation and society from poverty Earlier in the day the UN secretary general called on everyone in Afghanistan to step up pressure on the Taliban to reopen girls schools Emphasizing the right of girls to education Guterres says the humanitarian situation in Afghanistan has also deteriorated alarmingly in recent months with about million people at risk of starvation
