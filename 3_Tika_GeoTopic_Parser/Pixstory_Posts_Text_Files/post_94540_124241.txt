The New York designer collaborated with AllModern, the brand specializing in modern furnishings, to create a homeware collection that includes plaid rugs and cushions. For this limited edition capsule, the designer was inspired by a timeless modern style that takes into account modern and Scandinavian influences contemporary Designed to adapt to different furnishing schemes and domestic environments the pieces integrate geometric shapes abstract motifs stripes and tonal textures and also draw inspiration from the works of Josef Albers and Mark Rothko I thought of something sophisticated and textural that can adapt to different types of case explained the designer himself
