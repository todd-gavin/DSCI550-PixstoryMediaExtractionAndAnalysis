A few dozen women defied the Taliban regime in Afghanistan by staging a protest in Kabul to demand bread, work and freedom and demanding the right to female education This was confirmed by local journalistic sources according to the Ansa news agency Education is my right Reopen the schools they announced the demonstrators many with their faces covered as imposed by the recent provisions of the fundamentalists who returned to power last August The demonstrators marched for a few hundred meters before dispersing Taliban militiamen in civilian clothes confiscated their mobile phones to prevent them from resuming the protest
