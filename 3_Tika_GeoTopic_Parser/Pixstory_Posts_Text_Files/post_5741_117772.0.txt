Today, India will get it's 15th President either in Droupadi Murmu or Yashwant Sinha as the vote count in underway. The presidential election took place on 18th july as MLAs and MPs all over the country voted. 

It looks like Murmu's hometown of Rairangpur, Odisha is pretty confident about her victory as photos of preparations to celebrate have surfaced. A huge amount of ladoos can be seen in the photos and much more will be ready by the time results are in.
