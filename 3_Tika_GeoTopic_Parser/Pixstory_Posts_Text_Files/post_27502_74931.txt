Bad mishap for Kevin Bonifazi The Bologna defender was busy on the pitch at the Bentegodi stadium against Verona last night, a match that Hellas won when the alarm went off in his apartment in the historic center around 12:00, requiring the immediate intervention of the Carabinieri According to the first reconstructions of the investigators the thieves would have reached the roof using the condominium spaces and then descended onto the balcony of the young Rossobl footballer
