Kim Kardashian was recently fined million for her failure to follow the Security Exchange Commission s security touting laws which require that advertisers of securities in this case a cryptocurrency disclose the fact that they were paid the nature of the payment and the amount that they were paid In Kim Kardashian s case she was paid to advertise EtherMax a cryptocurrency which lost of its value since Kim s advertisements This is the first case filed against a cryptocurrency advertiser by the SEC Over the past year many new scam cryptocurrencies with no real purpose were created and advertised by big name celebrities and influencers often without disclosure Many people lost money as a result yet very few cases have been brought Experts believe that this case may actually be a good sign for the cryptocurrency industry in a way as it may deter big names from advertising primarily because the advertisement would be useless if they disclosed how they were paid and how much they were paid in the advertisement
