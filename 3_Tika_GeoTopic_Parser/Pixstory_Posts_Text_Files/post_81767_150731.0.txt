Richa Chadha and Ali Fazal shares pics from Sangeet Ceremony. 
Richa Chadha and Ali Fazal is all set to get married on October 6. On Friday she took her Instagram handle and shared pictures with Ali Fazal from their mehendi ceremony. She wore a pink floral lehenga with necklace around her neck. Ali wore white kurta pyjama with white stole. She used the caption, "Mohabbat Mubarak."
