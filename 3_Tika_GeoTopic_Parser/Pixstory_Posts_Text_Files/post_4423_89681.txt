 Plump and juicy cherry tomatoes make for a good silky sauce that comes together in minutes This takes about minutes to put together with minimal pantry staples and I m sure you ll love it Ingredients Cherry tomatoes g Pasta Linguine spaghetti Garlic cloves finely chopped Black pepper tsp Dried basil tbsp Olive oil tbsp Salt Directions Drop your pasta in a boiling pot of water and cook till al dente Reserve a cup of pasta water In a pan add olive oil and the add the cherry tomatoes Add garlic pepper and salt Mash the tomatoes down to help them burst Now add the basil Add pasta water and continue stirring Finally add the pasta grate parmesan and give it a final toss Enjoy You can definitely go ahead and use fresh basil if you have it in hand
