The Indian political map shows all the states and union territories of India along with their capital city. Our country India is also known as the republic of India. It has 28 states and eight union territories. The second most populated country in the world is India. India is divided into major zones black East India, West India, North India, South India, North East India and Central India. 
East India includes the state like Bihar,  Jharkhand, West Bengal, etc. 
The West India includes Gujarat, Maharashtra, etc. 
The South India covers Tamilnadu, Karnataka, Kerala, Andhra Pradesh, etc. 
The North India includes Himachal Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, etc. 
The North East includes the seven sisters of Arunachal Pradesh,  Assam, Meghalaya, Tripura, Mizoram, Manipur, and Nagaland.
And the last Central zone of India includes Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh.
