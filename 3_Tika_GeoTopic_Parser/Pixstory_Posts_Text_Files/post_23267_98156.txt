Yesterday the Spanish government revealed that the mobile phones of Prime Minister Pedro Sanchez and Defense Minister Margarita Robles were hacked between May and June 2019 with powerful software produced by the Israeli company Nso Group Pegasus The revelation reopened the case of political espionage after an organization that monitors cybersecurity last month discovered that Pegasus had been installed on the phones of dozens of pro-independence politicians in Catalonia Pere Aragones leader of that region denounced the hack as mass espionage Pegasus developed by Israeli company NSO Group allows the user to check every detail of the spied phone video calls messages and photos A powerful espionage tool used by many countries around the world to keep an eye on terrorist criminals and drug traffickers but also dissidents and activists
