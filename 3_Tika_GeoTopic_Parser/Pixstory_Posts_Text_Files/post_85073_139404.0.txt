Fenerbahce and Dynamo Kyiv will be up against each other in their opening encounter of the Europa League 2022-23. This will be a Group B match which will be played at the Şükrü Saracoğlu.
Dynamo Kyiv, Rennes, Fnerbahce, and AEK Larnaca are the 4 clubs that have been drawn in Group B of the Europa League campaign this season. Fenerbahce and Dynamo Kyiv have come across each other six time so far and the Turkish side has failed to register even a single win.  
Since 2018, Fenerbahce have failed to win any home fixture in the Group stage of the UEFA Europa League and don’t boast a good record against visiting sides at home in European competitions.
