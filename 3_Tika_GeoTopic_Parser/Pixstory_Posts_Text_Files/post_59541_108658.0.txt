As army and other agencies conducted rescue operations, the number of flood-affected individuals taking refuge in relief shelters increased to 2.31 lakh. On Sunday night in Kampur, Nagaon district, two Assam police officers perished while attempting to rescue marooned civilians. 

In Kaziranga, eight animals were slaughtered. 

At least eight wild animals died in the flood and after being hit by automobiles on NH-37 in Assam, while ten others were saved. According to a notice released by the national park's management, flood water submerged around 18% of the park's territory. 

Four hog deer were among the eight wild creatures. Vehicles kill animals as they run to the Karbi Anglong highlands on the other side of the river.
