The Sky Mile Tower is a mini city project under Japan s Next Tokyo plan and is expected to be finished by the year It will be five times taller than the Paris Eiffel Tower and double the height of the Bhuj Khalifa It will not be just a building but a tiny city intended to fight climate change It will have floors in total which will be able to house people To achieve the best wind resistance the structure would be hexagonal in shape Water will be immediately filtered from the atmosphere and kept outside of the structure The tower will be a hub for everything Including commercial residential and agricultural activities Elevator without cables would be used to move both vertically and horizontally throughout the tower
