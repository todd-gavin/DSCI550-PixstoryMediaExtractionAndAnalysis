" Suji cheela " a perfect healthy , easy and tasty breakfast recipe , can be prepared within minutes . Do you know ? This recipe is inherited from the popular besan ka cheela recipe and is prepared very similarly . suji ka cheela is served for early morning breakfast with choice of chutney . ❤️

Let me tell you how to make Suji Cheela ;

Ingredients 👩🏼‍🍳
1 Suji
2 Yoghurt 
3 Chopped onion
4 Chopped tomato
5 Chopped capsicum
6 Salt to taste 
7 Black pepper
8 Red Chilli powder
9 Vegetable oil

Steps To Follow 👩🏼‍🍳
1 Mix Yoghurt and Suji together in large bowl . Whisk properly to attain a thick mixture . You can also add 3-4 tbsp water if required .

2 Now add chopped onion , tomato and capsicum to the mixture . Add red chilli powder , black pepper and salt according to taste . Mix it well .

3 Heat 1tsp oil on a non - stick tawa . Now pour some batter on the tawa and spread it . Let the cheela cook from both sides until golden - brown . 

4 Serve them hot with mint chutney , tomato sauce .
