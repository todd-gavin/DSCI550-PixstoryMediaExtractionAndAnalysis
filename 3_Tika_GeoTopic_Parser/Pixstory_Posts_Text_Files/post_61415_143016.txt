onion soup A typical base for the soup is meat broth and caramelized onions It is served au gratin with croutons or a large piece of bread with cheese floating on top The dish which is centuries old experienced a resurgence in popularity in the United States over the years as a result of increased interest in French cuisine French onion soup can be eaten as an entree or as a complete meal. can last days in the fridge Gruyere and Parmesan are the classic cheeses used for French Onion Soup Ingredients include onions butter sugar salt beef or chicken broth brandy pepper black virgin oil bay leaves and parmesan cheese
