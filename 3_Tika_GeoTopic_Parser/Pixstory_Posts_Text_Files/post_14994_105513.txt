The Trenitalia press release The Rome-Naples high-speed line will be reactivated today with the circulation of high-speed trains gradually recovering from this afternoon This will also allow for the improvement of the circulation of commuter trains on conventional lines Tomorrow morning Wednesday normal train scheduling will be able to resume both between Rome and Naples and in the railway junctions of the two cities Rete Ferroviaria Italiana Gruppo FS Italiane worked to renovate about one kilometer and three hundred meters of track, replace meters of technological cables and restore meters of electric power line for trains. The restoration activities, completed in less than hours, were able to take place in two steps, the first started on Sunday afternoon following the release from seizure of part of the affected area, the second since yesterday evening when the Judicial Authority allowed the removal of the misdirected locomotive, thus also freeing up the tunnel
