Four Olympion Balbirs ( L to R, Balbir Punjab , Senior Balbir, Balbir services, Balbir Railways). 

Late Balbir Singh senior was part of Indian men's hockey team that won gold in three successive Olympic games (1948, 52, 56).

Balbir services also known as Col Balbir Singh was part of 1968 Olympic team. He is an Olympic bronze medalist. 

Late Balbir Punjab was an Olympic gold medalist. He was part of gold-medal winning 1964 Olympic team and 1968 Olympics, where India won bronze. 

Balbir Railways he is an Olympic bronze medalist. He was part of 1968 Olympic team.
