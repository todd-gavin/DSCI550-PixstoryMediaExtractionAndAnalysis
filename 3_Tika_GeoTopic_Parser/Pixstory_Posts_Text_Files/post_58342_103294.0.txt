Ways to take care of your mental health that are completely free:-

1) Write your anxious thoughts out to externalize them vs keeping them in mind.

2)consistency check-in with your needs.

3) engage more in spaces that are safe + you can be authentic and less in unsafe spaces .

4) go outside to get some sunshine and take deep breath of fresh air.

5) communicate with others assertively vs passively or aggressively.

6) Reduce your caffeine intake.

7) Set boundaries with social media.

8) Prioritize getting 7-8 hours of restful sleep.

9) make sure that you eat when you're hungry (cause hunger leads to increased feelings of anxiety, anger, or tiredness).

10) de-clutter your external environment.

11)try new experience + change your scenery.
