Let s make quick healthy yet tasty Mint and Almond Pasta Ingredients lb penne pasta c parsley tightly packed c mint leaves tightly packed c roasted almonds jalape o deseeded and cut in pieces garlic clove tbsp lime juice c extra virgin olive oil plus more for serving Kosher salt and pepper Directions to make Cook pasta per package directions Drain pasta and transfer to a large bowl Meanwhile make the pesto In a food processor pulse parsley mint almonds jalape o garlic and lime juice and tsp salt until finely chopped stopping and occasionally scraping down sides With machine running slowly add oil processing until well incorporated and scraping down sides as necessary Toss pasta with pesto until coated Serve drizzled with additional oil salt and freshly ground black pepper if desired Tip Save cup of pasta water and add in slowly if you feel the pasta is dry Start adding cup mix taste and add more if necessary The pasta water will make the pasta creamier Try this tastiest recipe at home
