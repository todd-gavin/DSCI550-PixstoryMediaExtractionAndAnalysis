At the 36th National Games, Kerala's Sajan Prakash breaks the men's 200m butterfly record with a time of 1.59.56 seconds.
In the women's 50m breaststroke, Chahat Arora of Punjab establishes a new National Games mark (33.31s).
In the women's 200m butterfly, Karnataka's Hashika Ramachandra establishes a new National Games record (2:19.12s).
