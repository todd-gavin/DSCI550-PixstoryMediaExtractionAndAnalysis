Google celebrates women with a Doodle dedicated to the years since the birth of Donna Francesca Sanna Sulis known as the lady of the mulberry trees Sardinian courageous and nonconformist eighteenth-century stylist Born in in a small village in southern Sardinia, she grew up on the family farm and learned to manage an enterprise She married years ago and together with her husband moved to the family business dedicated to the cultivation of mulberries Soon Francesca found herself supervising the entire silk production chain from making sure that the silkworms had enough mulberry leaves to feed on silk threads from cocoons When she took over the business she invested in modern looms and transformed the estate into a state of the art silk production site for its time Little did she know her business would revolutionize the entire fashion industry as women who did not wear dresses traditional people risked being marginalized
