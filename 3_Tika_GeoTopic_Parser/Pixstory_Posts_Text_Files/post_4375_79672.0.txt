Want to serve your taste buds but fear of binge eating or cheating on your diet? Broccoli bites will do the trick here! 

Ingredients
2 cups blanched Broccoli
1 tsp chopped ginger
1 tsp chopped garlic
1 green chili (optional)
1/4 cup Coriander leaves
Salt as per taste
1/4 tsp Red chilli powder
1/8 tsp black pepper powder
1/2 tsp Coriander powder
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1/2 amchur/mango powder
1/4 tsp Garam masala
2 tbsp roasted gram flour
1 tbsp roasted sesame seeds

Procedure: 

-In a blender add all the above mentioned ingredients except gram flour and sesame seeds. 

-Transfer the mixture in a bowl and add gram flour and sesame seeds to it and mix it all up. 

-Roll about small bite size tikkis out it 

-Greece the pan and cook the bites from both the sides. 

-Enjoy it with mint chutney!
