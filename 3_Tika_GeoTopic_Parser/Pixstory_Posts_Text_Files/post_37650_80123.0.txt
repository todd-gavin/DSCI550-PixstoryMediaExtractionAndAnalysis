With the upcoming mega auction here are a few big names that weren't retained by their former sides and will go under the hammer:

As per the auction modalities, the ten-player marquee set, which includes Shikhar Dhawan, Mohammed Shami, Faf du Plessis, David Warner, Pat Cummins, Shreyas Iyer, Ravichandran Ashwin, Quinton de Kock, Kagiso Rabada and Trent Boult, will kickstart the proceedings. The marquee players will be followed by capped players in order of batters, all-rounders, wicketkeepers, pacers and spinners before the uncapped players.
Meanwhile, the finalised auction list, which has been trimmed down from 1214 to 590, has 48 players in INR 2 crore base price bracket, 20 in INR 1.5 crore and 34 in INR 1 crore.
