Today, Aamir Khan released the first song of his upcoming movie, Laal Singh Chaddha. The name of the song is ‘Kahani.’

Laal Singh Chaddha will be released on 11th August 2022 and you can watch it near your theatres.

The cast of Laal Singh Chaddha also includes Kareena Kapoor Khan and Naga Chaitanya.
