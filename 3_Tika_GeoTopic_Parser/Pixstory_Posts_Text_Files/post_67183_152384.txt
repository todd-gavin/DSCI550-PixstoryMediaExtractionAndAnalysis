Vishnu is among the most important deities of Hinduism and is regarded as the preserver and protector Throughout the four yugs Satyug Tretayug Dwaparyug and Kalyug Lord Vishnu is said to have been incarnated ten times in ten different avatars SATYUG Matsya The Fish Kurma The Tortoise Varaha The Giant Boar TRETAYUG Narsimha The Half man Half Lion Vamana The Dwarf Parshuram The Warrior Shri Ram The Avatar of Morality and Rules DWAPARYUG Shri Krishna Buddha in some cases Buddha is also considered to be an avatar of Lord Vishnu KALYUG Kalki His appearance will mark the end of Kalyug
