On the auspicious occasion of Ganesh Chaturthi today August 31, 2022 let's have a look at some of the most iconic scenes related to this elephant-headed God in Hindi cinema which made us go 'Ganpati Bappa Morya' all the way.❤️‍🔥❤️‍🔥

1- Agneepath🔥❤️

This popular scene from Agneepath begins with Hrithik Roshan's character Vijay performing Ganpati puja to Ajay-Atul's 'Deva Shree Ganesha' before he sets out to bump off Surya who plans to assassinate Police Commissioner Gaitonde during the Ganpati procession.

2-Satya❤️‍🔥❤️‍🔥

Just like how Lord Ganesha is known to be 'Vighnaharta' or the removal of all obstacles, in the pre-climax scene in Ram Gopal Varma's Satya, the main protagonist kills Bhau during Ganesh Chaturthi celebrations on the beach to avenge the death of Bhiku Mhatre.

3-Atithi Tum Kab Jaoge?🔥❤️‍🔥

Punit and his wife Munmun have a change of heart and hit a realization when their uninvited guest Chachaji goes missing during Ganpati immersion celebrations on the beach in Atithi Tum Kab Jaoge? All's well that ends well!

4-ABCD😍❤️

The cold-hearted Jehangir is reminded of his old friendship with Vishnu when he watches the latter's dance group DDR's heartfelt tribute to Lord Ganesha in the form of 'Sadda Dil Vi Tu.'

5-Banjo🤯🔥🔥

Lord Ganesha is also believed to be a marker of new beginnings and opportunities. Riteish Deshmukh's character Taraat's life takes a big turn when Mickey , who is in search of an instrumentalist capable of playing the banjo comes across him at a local concert for Ganesh festival.
