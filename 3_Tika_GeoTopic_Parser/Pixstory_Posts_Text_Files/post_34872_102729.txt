Romanian tennis player Irina Begu risked disqualification in the second round of Roland Garros played against the Russian Alexandrova because, returning to her bench and annoyed by the last ball played, she threw her racket which, unfortunately for her, bounced to the ground, ending up among the spectators and hitting a child The game was not resumed and the supervisor arrived on the field who, unlike what happened to Djokovic in a similar episode at the US Open, Begu was not disqualified but only received a warning In the end it was the Romanian who won the match with the result of Applause from the rostrum for the winner who returned to the audience to make sure once again of the condition of the affected child
