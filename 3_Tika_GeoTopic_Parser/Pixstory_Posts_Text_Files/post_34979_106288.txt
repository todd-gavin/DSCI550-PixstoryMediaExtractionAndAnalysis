Juventus and Di Maria are getting closer and according to La Gazzetta dello Sport they are close to finding an agreement that will satisfy both parties The Argentinian player is asking for a one-year contract and a salary of millions of euros net The Juventus club offered him a salary of millions and a half plus bonuses but for two years in order to take advantage of the Growth Decree and save on taxes There seemed to be no way to fill the gap but instead a way would have been found To take advantage of the Decree it is enough for Di Maria to stay until August Juventus therefore could propose a contract until the end of December and give him the net millions of euros requested for this period If everything materializes in an offer, there could be the long-awaited signing
