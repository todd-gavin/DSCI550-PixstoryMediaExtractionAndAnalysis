Since the beginning of the week, the global cryptocurrency market cap has dropped by nearly $100 billion. According to data on CoinMarketCap at 8.37 a.m. India time, the global crypto market cap was $893 billion at the time of writing, down 7% from the previous day. 

On Thursday (June 16th), the crypto markets showed signs of improvement, with several leading coins seeing increases of more than 10%. Cryptos, on the other hand, are experiencing a downturn as a result of weak global cues and sinking traditional financial markets.
