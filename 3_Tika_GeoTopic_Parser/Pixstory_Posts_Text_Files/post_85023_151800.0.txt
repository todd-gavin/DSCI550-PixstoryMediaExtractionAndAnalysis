Real Madrid and Shakhtar Donetsk will be up against each other on Matchday 03 of the UEFA Champions League. Real Madrid are on the top spot in the points table in Group F of the UEFA Champions League 2022-23 while Shakhtar Donetsk sit at the 2nd spot in the points table.
Real Madrid have been breathing fire this year in La Liga and in the European competition. So far, in the Champions League, Madrid have played a couple of games against Celtic and RB Leipzig and have won both these games.
Shakhtar have been impressive too in the UCL campaign in 2022/23. Out of the 2 games that they have played in the UEFA Champions League, they have won a game and lost a match so far.
