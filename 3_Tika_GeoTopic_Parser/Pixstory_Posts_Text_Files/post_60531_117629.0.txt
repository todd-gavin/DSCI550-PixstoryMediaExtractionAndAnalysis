On the 19th, Tving unveiled the first to fifth places among the “Tving Popular Rankings in the First Half of 2022.” The aggregation standard is based on the net viewer (UV) among all VODs of TVing.
tvN’s Monday-Tuesday drama “The Military Prosecutor Doberman” ranked fifth in the most viewed content. The fourth place was tvN’s Wednesday entertainment show “You Quiz on the Block,” and the third place was tvN’s Thursday entertainment show “Somehow President 2” and the second place was tvN’s Monday-Tuesday drama “Twenty-five Twenty-one.“
In the first place, Tving’s original entertainment show ‘Seoul Check-in‘ was named. ‘Seoul Check-in’ was watched by the most people among all VODs from January to June 2022.
