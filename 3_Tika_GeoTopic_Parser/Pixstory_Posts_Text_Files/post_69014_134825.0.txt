Rohit Sharma, Virat Kohli meet specially-abled Sri Lankan fan Gayan Senanayake after win over Pakistan.

After India's memorable win over Pakistan in their opening match of the continental showpiece event, Virat Kohli and Rohit Sharma met Sri Lankan fan Gayan Senanayake in Dubai on Monday. Senanyake had earlier attended Virat Kohli and his wife Anushka Sharma's wedding reception in Mumbai a few years ago.
