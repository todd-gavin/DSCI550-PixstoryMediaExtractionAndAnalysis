In Europe on Saturday May, on the occasion of the European mobilization against the EU green taxonomy proposal, various ecological realities and associations will take to the streets to demonstrate against the inclusion of nuclear power and gas as sustainable investments, equating them to renewable energies. It is a senseless and unfair choice which moves away from the just ecological transition towards renewable sources In addition to France Belgium Germany Austria Italy will also be at the forefront on Saturday May with two flash mobs scheduled in Rome and Milan at 1.00 pm at the headquarters of the European institutions and which will see over twenty acronyms in the square between associations, committees and environmental movements In Milan the appointment will be at Palazzo delle Stelline in Corso Magenta n where a flash mob will be held which will have as its theme the lethal lie of the green taxonomy
