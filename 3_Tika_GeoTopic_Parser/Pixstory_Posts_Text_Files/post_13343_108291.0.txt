Kobra Khademi, an Afghan artist, engraved the names of 11 protesting Afghan women on placards and hung them on the streets of Ruba, in northern France, in an art composition called "Heroes of Today."

Letters from women on the streets protesting the group's policies after the Taliban entered Kabul.

Tamna Zaryab, Tamna Rezaei, Farideh Akbari, Nayreh Kouhestani, Medina Darvazi, Morsal Ayar, Vahideh Amiri, Zahra Mohammadi, Rakhshaneh Rezaei, Alieh Azizi and Hoda Khamoush are some of the women whose letters have been installed on the streets of this northern French city.
