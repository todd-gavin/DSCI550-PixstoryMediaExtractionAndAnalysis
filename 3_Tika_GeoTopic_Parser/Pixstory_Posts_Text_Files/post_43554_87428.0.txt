BTS will be taking the stage at the 2022 Grammy Awards!
On March 15 local time, the Recording Academy officially announced its first lineup of performers for the upcoming 64th annual Grammy Awards. BTS will be one of the artists performing at this year's show, joining a star-studded lineup that includes Billie Eilish, Olivia Rodrigo, Lil Nas X with Jack Harlow, Brandi Carlile, and Brothers Osborne. Just like all the other performers announced thus far, BTS is in the running for one of this year's Grammy Awards: the group's hit song "Butter" is nominated for Best Pop Duo/Group Performance, marking BTS's second nomination in the category.
