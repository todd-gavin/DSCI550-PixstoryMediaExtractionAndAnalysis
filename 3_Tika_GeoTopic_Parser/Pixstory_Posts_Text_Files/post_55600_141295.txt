September will be a holiday to coincide with a memorial service for the Queen Prime Minister Anthony Albanese told the ABC ABC reported that it will be a one time national holiday to allow people to honour the memory of Queen Elizabeth according to Mr Albanese The memorial service will be place when the Prime Minister and Governor General return from the Queen s funeral in London According to Mr Albanese he wrote to inform state and territory officials of the vacation According to the PM the government will also make up the four sitting days that were missed when the Queen s death caused a suspension of the legislative process It would be difficult to envisage the sort of adversarial activity that occurs in our parliament so I think it was appropriate and protocols require the automatic cancellation he said He added that one of the days would be committed to condolences from the parliament
