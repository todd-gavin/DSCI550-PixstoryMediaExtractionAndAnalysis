Football Writers’ Association just announced that Mohamed Salah is the winner of Football Writers’ Association Footballer of the Year.

Mo Salah received 48 percent of the vote and he beat Kevin De Bruyne and Declan Rice who clinched second and third spots respectively.

Till now, Mo Salah scored 30 goals and made 14 assists in 44 matches this season, and his team, Liverpool is in contention to win quadruple.
