The best in show biz showed up and showed out in Los Angeles on Saturday night for the 2022 Academy Museum Gala, which collected over $10 million in donations for the Academy Museum of Motion Pictures. The event honored Miky Lee, Steve McQueen and Tilda Swinton for their impactful work in cinema, plus Julia Roberts — who was presented with the Icon Award in recognition of her expansive career.
Jonathan Majors, Emma Stone, Kerry Washington, Mia Goth, Alexa Demie, George Clooney, Taylor Russell — accompanied by Luca Guadagnino — and many others graced the carpet and mingled at the reception before a special musical performance by Diana Ross.
