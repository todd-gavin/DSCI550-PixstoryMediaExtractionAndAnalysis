If you believe the Western narrative, Ukraine is close to humbling Russia.

Commentators cite massive casualties, stiff resistance and slow progress to claim that Putin is in deep trouble.

Francis Fukuyama has gone so far to predict that Russian army's collapse could be sudden and catastrophic, rather than slowly through a war of attrition:

https://www.americanpurpose.com/articles/preparing-for-defeat/ 

Eliot Cohen says the Russian army has committed more than half its combat forces to the fight. And behind those forces stands very little:

https://www.theatlantic.com/ideas/archive/2022/03/ukraine-is-winning-war-russia/627121/ 

Is it misplaced optimism or are we really going to see a Russian defeat?
