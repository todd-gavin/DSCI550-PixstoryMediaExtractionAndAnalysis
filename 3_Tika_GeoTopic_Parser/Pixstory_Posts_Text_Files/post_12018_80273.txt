The government briefed to Rajya Sabha about lakh vaccine doses that have been administered to pregnant women till nd February out of which lakh of them are completely vaccinated with both the doses of the vaccine On July the health ministry had announced inclusion of pregnant women in the ongoing vaccination drive against Covid The government also said as on st January adverse events have been reported in pregnant women of which were minor events which were self limiting in nature and were serious and severe adverse events junior health minister Bharati Pravin Pawar said A communication strategy is in place which is implemented across all states and UTs with a focus to sustain vaccine confidence the minister said Sources
