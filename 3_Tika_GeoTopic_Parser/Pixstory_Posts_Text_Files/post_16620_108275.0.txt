The first poster of epic period film 'Shamshera', which will see Ranbir Kapoor  recently got leaked after a fan activation event.
Shamshera's trailer would be released in less than a week, according to a source speaking to Bollywood Hungama.

“Shamshera’s trailer will be unveiled on Friday, June 24. The film will release in theatres on July 22. Hence, it’ll be a nice 29-day promotional window that the producers, Yash Raj Films (YRF), are looking at,” the source said.
Directed by Karan Malhotra of Agneepath (2012) and Brothers (2015) fame, Shamshera is reported to be a period drama under the banner of Yash Raj Films.
