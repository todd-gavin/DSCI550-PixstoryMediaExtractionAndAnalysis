Earth Day was commemorated at Kendriya Vidyalaya No.2 Raipur on #EarthDay2022 with various activities such as Drawing and Slogan Writing Competitions to spread the message of Invest In Our Planet. Elocution Competition stressed the importance and need to protect our Earth and students pledged. Clean Vidyalaya and plastic-free Vidyalaya campaigns were organised to make students aware of the drastic effects of plastic pollution on Earth. 
Earth Day Network-India requests schools across India to do a special Morning Assembly on the #EarthDay2022 theme #InvestInOurPlanet and send us details on https://bit.ly/EarthDayMorningAssemblyContest2022 
The last date for schools to submit photos and details online on the link is July 31.
