ActionAid is an independent international organization active in Italy and in other countries. It collaborates with more than thousand partners, NGO alliances and social movements to fight poverty and social injustice. For over years, ActionAid has been fighting alongside the poorest and most marginalized individuals and communities, choosing to side with them. part because it is aware that a collective effort of solidarity and justice is necessary to bring about real social change A fair and just world for all this is the vision from which ActionAid draws inspiration and vital strength To make this vision of the world a concrete reality ActionAid sets itself a mission specific to be pursued in the next few years work to promote and animate spaces for democratic participation and to involve people and communities in the protection of their rights collaborate at the local, national and international level to bring about change and to grow social equity by improving the quality of democracy and thus supporting those who live in situations of poverty and marginality
