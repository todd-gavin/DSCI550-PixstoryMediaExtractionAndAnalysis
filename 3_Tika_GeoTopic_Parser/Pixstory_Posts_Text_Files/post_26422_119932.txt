The first international summit dedicated to the sustainable transition of the Fashion sector will be held in Venice on and next October at the Cini Foundation on the Island of San Giorgio. The Venice Sustainable Fashion Forum will be divided into two days on October entitled Just Fashion Transition created by Confindustria Venezia Metropolitan Area of Venice and Rovigo and The European House Ambrosetti with the patronage of Assocalzaturifici and the one in October entitled The Values of Fashion created by the Camera Nazionale della Moda Italiana and Sistema Moda Italia Smi
