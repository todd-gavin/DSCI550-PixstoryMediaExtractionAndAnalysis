Now-a - days,  a pair of  Chukar Partridge with their newly borne babies moving around in lawn of the Constitution Club of India , situated at Rafi Marg in South Delhi is a prime attraction of visitors , coming here from different parts of country and abroad to attend international and national conferences and workshops 
The Chukar Partridge (Alectoris Chukar) or generally Chukar or Chakor Palearctic upland gamebird in the Pheasant family
Phasianidae
This bird has well marked black and white bars on the flanks and a black band running from the forehead across the eye and running down the head to form a necklace that encloses a white throat
