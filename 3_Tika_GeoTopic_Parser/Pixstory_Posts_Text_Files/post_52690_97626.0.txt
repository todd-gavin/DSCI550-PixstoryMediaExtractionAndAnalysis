Chandigarh Press Club in association with UT health department organised a Covid vaccination camp at Chandigarh Press Club, Sector 27, on Saturday. 

In the camp, 50 children in the 12-14 age group were administered Covid vaccine ‘Corbevax’. 

UT health secretary Yashpal Garg and Director Public Relations (DPR) Rajiv Tewari, also visited the camp. Garg interacted with children and health department staff. 

On the request of Chandigarh Press Club, UT health secretary Yashpal Garg assured that in the coming days more such camps will be organised in Chandigarh press club for other age categories as well.
