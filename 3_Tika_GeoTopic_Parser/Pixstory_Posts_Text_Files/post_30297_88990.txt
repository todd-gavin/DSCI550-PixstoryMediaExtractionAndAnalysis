The physical ordeal for Marc Marquez is not over yet The multiple MotoGP champion underwent a medical examination in Barcelona after complaining of problems after returning from the trip to Indonesia The results of the tests showed a return of diplopia the eye problem that had already stopped the Spaniard last year The positive aspect is that, as Marquez himself underlined on his social profiles, diplopia manifested itself more lightly than in the past Another good news concerns the exams carried out this morning in Madrid which did not show any physical consequences from the terrible crash that saw Marquez protagonist in the warm up of the Indonesian GP and that had forced him to miss the Asian race It is currently impossible to make a prediction on the times of recovery given that diplopia requires prolonged rest and the need for surgery has not even been ruled out.
