Uttar Pradesh and Karnataka will compete for the men's hockey gold title.
On Tuesday at the Maj. Dhyan Chand Stadium in this city, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh will play in the men's hockey championship match.
Today's quarterfinal matchup against Haryana was won easily by them 3-1 thanks to goals in the first and fourth quarters. Prior to it, Uttar Pradesh shocked highly favoured Maharashtra 6-5 in a tie-breaker after the teams were tied 3-3 at the end of regular.
Karnataka scored from their first penalty corner in the fourth minute thanks to Abharan Sudev, but Haryana responded in the seventeenth minute with a goal from Kohinoor Preet Singh. Karnataka scored two goals in the 47th and 51st minutes of the third quarter to secure their berth in the top match.
