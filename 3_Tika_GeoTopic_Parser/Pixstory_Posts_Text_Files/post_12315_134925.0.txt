The white sauce pasta 
For pasta
Add 1 bowl pasta in a vessel, add 2 cups water and bit of oil and salt, so that it doesn't stick.
After 15 mins, strain it 
For white sauce  
Simply add butter, bit of wheat flour and some milk .... stirr it till its thick. Add some cheese and pasta mix. 
Now add some vegetables in white sauce, stir it well till everything gets cooked. Now add pasta in it tooo, keep it in low flame for 10 mins
