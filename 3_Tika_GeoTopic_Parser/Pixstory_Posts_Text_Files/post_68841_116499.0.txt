Gateway of Northeast Guwahati is included as one of the main venues for the 131th edition of the prestigious Durand Cup 2022. This time the tournament will be held in three different States in India like Assam,Manipur and West Bengal. According to Nibedan Das Patowary, director, Directorate Sports and Youth Welfare, govt.of Assam,10 league matches will be held in Sarusajai  Stadium Guwahati from 17th August 2022.This time Durand cup will have 20 teams - 11 from ISL, 5 from I-League and four from the organisers.It may be mentioned that the tournament is jointly organised by Indian Armed forces and concerned government.
