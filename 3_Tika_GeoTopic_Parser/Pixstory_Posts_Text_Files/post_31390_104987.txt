NATURALLY PIANO organized by the PratoVeteri cultural association of Pratovecchio Stia with the contribution of the Municipality of the Casentinesi Forest Park Authority and numerous companies in the valley and in the province of Arezzo, a biennial piano festival which sees the Artistic Direction of Enzo Gentile Always characterized by its rich and diversified musical proposal ranging from classical to jazz without forgetting experimental music and pop and singer-songwriter proposals the festival aims to develop a popular music project between concerts workshop improvisations by great professionals and non-professionals The Casentino valley reconfirms itself as a place perfect for hosting and bringing together people from all over Italy, a crossroads for artists who are passionate about music and for those looking for an authentic experience immersed in the unspoiled nature of the forests, castles and sacred places
