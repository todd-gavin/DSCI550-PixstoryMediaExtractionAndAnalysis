Ozone depletion Ozone layer depletion is the gradual thinning of the earth s ozone layer in the stratosphere caused due to the release of chemicals containing gaseous bromine or chlorine from industries and other human activities The causes of ozone layer depletion are mainly chlorofluorocarbons and hydrochlorofluorocarbons that are released by refrigerators solvents spray aerosols and air conditioners Unregulated rocket launches and various nitrogenous compounds can also lead to the ozone hole Natural causes such as volcanic eruptions play a role in the depletion of the ozone layer as well
