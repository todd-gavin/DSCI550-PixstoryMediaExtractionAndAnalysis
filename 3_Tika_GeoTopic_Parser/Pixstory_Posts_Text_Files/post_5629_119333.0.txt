APJ Abdul Kalam was the former president of India. He was a renowned aerospace scientist and one of the most beloved and celebrated president. 

He was involved in India's missile development efforts. Thus, he came to known as 'missile man of India'. He played a major role in strengthening nation's defense capabilities.
He also worked on SLV-III, the first satellite launch vehicle that put the Rohini satellite into orbit around the Earth.
