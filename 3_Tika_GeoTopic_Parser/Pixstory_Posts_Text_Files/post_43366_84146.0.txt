Going by legend, it is said that a Mughal queen during the Mughal Era, once visited army barracks and thought that the soldiers were under-nourished. Therefore, she asked the chef to prepare a special dish, which provided a balanced norishment. After a few rejections, she finally settled on Biryani, considering it the ‘complete meal’, rich in carbohydrates, protiens and minerals which could be eaten as a single serving. Biryani has emerged as one of the most popular dishes in India, evident from the fact that it was the highest ordered food on swiggy,zomato in 2021.
