On Wednesday, June 8, a major fire broke out at an e-rickshaw parking station near Delhi's Jamia Nagar metro station. 
The parking lot is engulfed in flames, according to photos shared on social media from the scene. 
The Delhi Fire Service said at least eleven fire tenders were on the scene after a report was received at 5:02 a.m. The fire was quickly brought under control. 
The fire burnt at least 10 cars, 30 new e-rickshaws, 50 old e-rickshaws, one motorcycle, and two scooters.
