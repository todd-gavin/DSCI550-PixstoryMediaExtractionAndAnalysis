Former "produce 101 seasons 2" contestant Jeong Jong Ji die at the age of 30
On September 20 Former"produce 101 seasons 2" contestant Jeong Jong Ji's mother took to Twitter to share the tragic news of his passing.
According to Jeong Jong Ji's mother her son die on September 9. She claims that her son took his own life. 
Jeong Jong Ji was first seen in 2017 as a contestant on Mnet's "produce 101 seasons 2". The singer was 30 years old at the time he died.
