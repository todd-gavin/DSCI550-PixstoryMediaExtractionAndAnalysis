 Bright Candles is a novel about the Danish resistance to German occupation during World War II During the German occupation of Denmark a sixteen year old child matures into a man as his annoying little pranks develop into extensive resistance actions It tells the story of a group of people who are determined to fight back against the settlers even if it means risking their lives The book is based on true events and is packed with action and suspense from beginning to end It is an excellent way to learn more about the history of World War II and the Danish resistance movement and it will leave you feeling inspired by the strength of human spirit and will help you understand the Danish struggles better If you re looking for an exciting read that will keep you hooked all the way until the finish line then Bright Candles is definitely worth your time
