Every year from August to August countries around the world observe World Breastfeeding Week The goal of the international campaign is to increase public awareness about breastfeeding and its advantages There was a decline in the number of moms who desired to breastfeed after an increase in infant mortality The initiative became crucial and the WHO advised that breastfeeding should continue until a kid is two years old Despite the fact that everyone is entitled to their own opinions breastfeeding can significantly improve the health of both mother and child Let s work together to support breastfeeding throughout this year s World Breastfeeding Week while educating ourselves and others on the importance of breastfeeding
