UNICEF Eight children were killed in recent floods and many are missing The spokesperson of the United Nations Children s Fund in Afghanistan said on Tuesday that during this week s floods in central and eastern Afghanistan at least children lost their lives and a large number of them are missing According to Samantha Moret the aid teams of this organization have been sent to eastern Afghanistan He told Reuters that there is a possibility that the number of children killed in the floods will increase Taliban officials announced that people died in this week s floods but they have not yet provided the media with new information about casualties wounded and missing people in this incident Floods have destroyed many houses in rural areas in provinces such as Logar and Nangarhar and have caused a lot of damage to people s livestock and agricultural products which can fuel the hunger crisis in these areas UN aid workers said they were concerned about the spread of infectious diseases such as cholera due to contaminated water
