The European Commission tightens measures against fake news and announces a new Code of Conduct on disinformation the original of for which it announces that it has already obtained the signature of subjects including also big techs such as Google Facebook Meta Microsoft or TikTok Among the commitments foreseen in the revised code, among other things, the cut in financial incentives for disinformation interventions to stop manipulative behaviors such as fake bot accounts or the dissemination of disinformation greater fact checking and the creation of a Center and a task force for the supervision and implementation of the code The reports the ANSA news agency
