A major part of the revolt of 1857 through which the British Raj pervaded led to the siege of Delhi and also to the establishment of colonial houses in the Civil Lines area of Nortn Delhi.
The Nehru Ridge featured a watch house in the shape of the Queen's crown. Most of the houses in this region have been uprooted given the urbanisation. Some that remain are testament to the horrors of the era but continue to remind us of the resilience that India had showcased to get freedom.
