Le Harve Le Havre is a port city in the Normandy region of the northern department of Seine Maritime France It is located in the south west of the Pays de Caux on the right bank of the Seine estuary on the English Channel King Fran ois I established the city and port in The center was completely rebuilt by Belgian architect Auguste Perret mentor to Le Corbusier after being nearly destroyed in September by Allied bombings that killed civilians Miraculously the bright and harsh modernist vision Auguste Perret is still largely intact A museum filled with fascinating impressionist works of art a high modernist church with a magnificent stained glass tower hilltop gardens with views of the city and a medieval church that has risen from the ashes are just some of the attractions
