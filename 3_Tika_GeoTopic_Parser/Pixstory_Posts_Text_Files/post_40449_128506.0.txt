in Mexico City The National Basketball Association (NBA) and Zignia today announced that the three-time NBA champion Miami Heat and the five-time NBA champion San Antonio Spurs will play a regular-season game at the Arena CDMX in Mexico City on Saturday, Dec. 17. This will be the league's 31st game in Mexico overall, the most of any nation other than the United States and Canada. Over the following few years, the NBA and Zignia will continue to work together on new NBA projects in Mexico.
