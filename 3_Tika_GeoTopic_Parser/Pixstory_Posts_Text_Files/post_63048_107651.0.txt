IPL's media rights for the next five years (2023 to 2027) were sold for 48,390 crore, while Pakistan Super League's (PSL) two-year media rights for 2022 and 2023 were sold for around 166 crore in 2021. IPL's per match value as per the domestic media rights stands at approximately 115.4 crore, while PSL's per match value stands at 2.76 crore.
