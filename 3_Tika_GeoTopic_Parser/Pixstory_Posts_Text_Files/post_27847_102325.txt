The show was not missed in the th stage of this edition of the Giro d Italia The Czech Jan Hirt of Intermarch won alone at the finish line in Aprica after the fearsome climbs of Mortirolo and Valico di Santa Cristina, the fifth rider in the history of the Czech Republic to win in the corsa rosa Great battle also for the leadership of the standings with Jai Hindley good at outwitting Richard Carapaz in the sprint for third place by taking bonus seconds that bring him just behind the pink jersey in the general.
