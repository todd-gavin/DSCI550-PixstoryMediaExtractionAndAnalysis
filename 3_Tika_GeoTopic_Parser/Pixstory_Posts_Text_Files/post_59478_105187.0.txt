According to data published on Sunday by the Union Health Ministry, India registered 4,518 new coronavirus infections, bringing the total number of COVID-19 cases to 4,31,81335, with a daily positivity rate of more than 1% after 34 days. 
The number of active cases increased to 25,782 and the death toll increased to 5,24,701 with 9 new fatalities, according to figures updated at 8 a.m.
