AC Milan and Sorare have announced a new exclusive multi-year partnership that will see the company that creates crypto fantasy football based on collectible digital cards purchasable in the form of non-fungible NFT tokens become Official NFT Fantasy Football Game Partner and new Premium Partner of AC Milan Con this new deal AC Milan officially becomes part of the Sorare fantasy football based on NFT It allows users to buy sell exchange and manage their team formed by official digital cards that provide a global and immersive gaming experience Since the season the over million users registered on the Sorare platform will also be able to buy, exchange and manage AC Milan trading cards, further strengthening the bond between Rossoneri fans around the world and the Club in the digital world
