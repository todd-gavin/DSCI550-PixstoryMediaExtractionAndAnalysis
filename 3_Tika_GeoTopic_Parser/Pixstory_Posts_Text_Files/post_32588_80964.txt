The Italian snowboarding couples fly under the snowfall of Zhangjiakou A medal was expected from the snowboardcross team event and so it was Michela Moioli and Omar Visintin took the silver only beaten by the American champions Baumgartner and Jacobellis Bronze for Canada while Lorenzo Sommariva and Caterina Carpano another blue couple finished in fourth place The debut of this new format in the five circles program is therefore also tinged with the Italian flag with Moioli good at redeeming herself after the disappointing individual round and Visintin excellent in adding the team silver to the individual bronze of two days ago
