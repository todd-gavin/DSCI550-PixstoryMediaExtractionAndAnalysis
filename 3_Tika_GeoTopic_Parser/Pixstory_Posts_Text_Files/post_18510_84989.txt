Filippo Ganna will race Tirreno Adriatico instead of Paris Nice next week A change of plans not foreseen for the time trial world champion but decided by his team the British Ineos Grenadiers because Geraint Thomas did not appear too fit to race in Italy The latter was the designated for the Tirreno Adriatico but in the end the decision and the change arrived Thomas will not race for France but will have intense training sessions to reach the best condition Participating in the race close to home could be a great advantage for Ganna who will aim for the third victory of the season also helped by the initial time trial in Lido di Camaiore
