Hertha and Hoffenheim will be up against each other on Matchday 08 of the Bundesliga 2022-23. Hertha are at the 13th position in the Bundesliga points table while Hoffenheim sit at the 5th position in the points table.
Hertha have been a disappointment to their fans so far in the Bundesliga 2022-23 season. In the 7 games that they have played until now, Hertha have just managed to win 1 games and have drawn 3 matches and ended up being on the losing side thrice.
Hoffenheim, on the other hand, have done reasonably well in the Bundesliga this season. In the 7 games that they have played this season, Hoffenheim have won 4 games, have drawn a game and have lost on a couple of occasions.
