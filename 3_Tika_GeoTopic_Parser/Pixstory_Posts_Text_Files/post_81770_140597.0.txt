Brahmastra day 1 collection. 
Ranbir Kapoor and Alia Bhatt's Brahmastra has taken a record start at the box office. According to early estimates the movie has collected in the range Rs 36.50 to 38.50 crores on its opening day on non- holiday opening of all time. Brahmastra has defeated the opening day collection like Sanju, Tiger Zinda Hai, Dhoom 3 pre- pandemic release. 
It's a non - holiday record and this bumper opening has set a positive vibe to Hindi Film Industry. Brahmastra is the biggest pandemic opener for the Hindi Film Industry by surpassing the collection of Sooryavanshi. The opening day has set a platform for the movie to hit a century in 3 days. A strong weekend is on cards for the movie.
