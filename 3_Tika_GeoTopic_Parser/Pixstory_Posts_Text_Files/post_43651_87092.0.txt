TWICE has once again reached a music video milestone!

The girl group's music video for "KNOCK KNOCK" surpassed 300 million views on March 14 at approximately 1:45 p.m. KST. This is about five years, 22 days, and 14 hours since its release on February 20, 2017 at 12 a.m. KST. "KNOCK KNOCK" is TWICE's 13th MV to hit

300 million views following"TT," "Likey" "Cheer Up," "Like OOH-AHH," "What Is Love?", "Heart Shaker," "FANCY" "Feel Special," "YES or YES," "I CAN'T STOP ME," "Dance The Night Away," and "MORE & MORE."
