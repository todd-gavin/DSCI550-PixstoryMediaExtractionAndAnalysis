We are all aware about the love the City of Pune has for 'Misal', the covid times were a tough time for everyone, especially the poor.

So the Hotel Management Department of our College took this iconic initiative and prepared the misal in Campus itself with help of World- renowned Chef  Vishnu Manohar!

How was The world record achieved?
* 7000 kgs of misal cooked
*In a time period of only 7 hours 
*Distributed among 30,000 people in need
*A total of 30 students and facilities contributed to cooking the misal.

Special mention to various NGOs,students and facilities who helped in distribution of the misal.Kudus to everyone who was a part of this great cause.
