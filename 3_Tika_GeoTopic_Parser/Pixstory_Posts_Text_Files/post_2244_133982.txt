Congress President s election on October The Congress Working Committee met on Sunday with party chief Sonia Gandhi in the chair It considered and approved the following final schedule of dates suggested by the Central Election Authority of the AICC All India Congress Committee for election to the post of Congress President Date of notification nbsp September Dates of filing of nomination September to September Date of scrutiny October Last date of withdrawal October Date of election if needed October Date of counting and declaration of result if needed October The CWC reiterated its resolve to make the Mehangai Par Halla Bol rally in New Delhi on September and the launch of the Bharat Jodo Yatra from Kanyakumari on September and its continuation thereafter a resounding success The CWC extended its best wishes to the Congress President for a full and speedy recovery
