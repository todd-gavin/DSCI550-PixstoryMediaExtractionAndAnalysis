The newly elected Vice President , Jagdeep Dhankhar took oath as the 14th Vice President of the country on Thursday afternoon. President Draupadi Murmu administered the oath to Dhankhar. Several cabinet ministers including former President Ram Nath Kovind, outgoing Vice President M. Venkaiah Naidu, Prime Minister Narendra Modi, Home Minister Amit Shah were present in the swearing-in ceremony of the elected Vice President.
