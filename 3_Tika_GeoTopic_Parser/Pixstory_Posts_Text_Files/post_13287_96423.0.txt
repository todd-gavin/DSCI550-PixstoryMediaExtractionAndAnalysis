انا الله و انا الیه راجعون
All of us offer our condolences to all the dear families of the martyrs of the school of Abdul Rahim Shahid and Mazar-e-Sharif, and we hope that you will bear this loss with the help of the Almighty. We ask God for forgiveness for your deceased and for you, dear mourners, reward and patience. And we always hope that the soul of that dear one is drowned in mercy.

Candlelight vigil for the martyrs who lost their lives in a tragic explosion in Afghanistan a few days ago، The Afghan Refugee Cohesion Committee in India extends its condolences to the esteemed families of all Afghans
