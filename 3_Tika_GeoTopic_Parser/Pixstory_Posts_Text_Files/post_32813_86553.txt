The first uphill finish of the Corsa dei due Mari goes to the obligatory favorite the Slovenian Tadej Pogacar who arrives alone at the Bellante finish line, sprinting meters from the finish after responding to the attacks of his rivals Behind him Vingegaard and Lafay then Evenepoel and Ciccone fifth and first of the Italians In the general standings Pogacar now commands with an advantage over Evenepoel and Filippo Ganna who loses the leadership as predicted
