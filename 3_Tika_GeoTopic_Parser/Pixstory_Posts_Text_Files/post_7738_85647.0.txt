The Batman has earned a staggering $128.5M on the opening weekend domestically. It has become the second-biggest pandemic movie debut. This has been the highest opening so far of 2022 and its the 5th biggest opening of movies adapted from the DC comics. This is also the 3rd highest opening of a solo Batman movie. This is the first movie of Warner Brothers that has crossed $100M on its opening weekend since 2017. Worldwide the movie has earned a whopping $248.5M
All the signs point to a sequel being announced very soon.
