Around 24 hours to go for the FAWSL Academy League Cup final!

Manchester United Women’s U-21s will face Birmingham City Women U-21s in the League Cup final tomorrow - March 09, 2022 at St. George’s Park from 18:00 GMT. The fixture was originally scheduled for April 24, 2022 at the Loughborough University Stadium from 15:30 BST. 

Charlotte Healy’s United side defeated Aston Villa in the semi-final. Safia Middleton Patel started between the sticks and kept a clean sheet in that game. Poppy Lawson led the young Red Devils and was a part of the Back 4 alongside Niamh Murphy, Emma Taylor and Jess Simpson. The midfield 3 comprised of Rebecca May, Polly Watson and Ella Kinzett. The consistent attacking trio of Karna Solskjaer, Alyssa Aherne and Maria Edwards scored a goal each. Second-half substitute Eleanor Ashton scored a brace after coming on in the 70th minute as United scored 5 past Villa. 

Birmingham won 2-0 vs Ipswich Town in the semi-final. 

United faced Birmingham in the league on February 25th. Karna Solskjaer and Carrie Jones both found their names on the scoresheet; Maria Edwards scored a brace. The game ended in a 5-0 victory for the United devs after an own goal from Birmingham City. 

In Birmingham City Women’s senior team’s last game, academy star Delphi Cole was a part of the matchday squad.
