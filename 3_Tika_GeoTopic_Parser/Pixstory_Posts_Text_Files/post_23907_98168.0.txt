German Chancellor Olaf Scholz on Monday invited Prime Minister Narendra Modi as a special guest for the forthcoming G-7 summit in Germany next month.

"I invited you as our guest to the G7 Summit at the end of June and we look forward to welcoming you back to Germany," Scholz said while addressing a  joint press conference with PM Modi after the 6th India-Germany Inter-Governmental Consultations (IGC).

“Such consultations help India and Germany deepen their relationship,” Chancellor Scholz observed.
