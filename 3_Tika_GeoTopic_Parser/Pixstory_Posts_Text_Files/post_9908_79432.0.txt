Kabul was not the sad capital of the world; Kabul was a city of fresh and new love with imagination.
It was freshness, the city was full of flowers, the lush garden of thousands of hopes had blossomed. 

City of Dreams
City of Wishes
City of Memories
The city of untold stories
The city was reaching peace!
Kabul, a city full of languages, smells of love and affection.  Now, it has become a city of ruined dreams.  Everything is dead in it, not even kisses and hugs, if it is, it is a crime.  Kabul is the graveyard of young dreams;  Because we grew old in our youth!
We have heard the history of this sad city from our mothers, How bitter and sweet it was, But these days, its sufferings have increased. 

Migration;  It means being a stranger to everything, to everyone ...
