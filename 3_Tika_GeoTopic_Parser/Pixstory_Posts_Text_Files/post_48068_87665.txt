The Humayun s tomb is the tomb of the Mughal Emperor Humayun in Delhi India The tomb was commissioned by Humayun s chief consort Empress Bega Begum also known as Haji Begum in and designed by Mirak Mirza Ghiyas and his son Sayyid Muhammad Persian architects chosen by her It was the first garden tomb on the Indian subcontinent and is located in Nizamuddin East Delhi India close to the Dina panah Citadel also known as Purana Qila Old Fort that Humayun found in
