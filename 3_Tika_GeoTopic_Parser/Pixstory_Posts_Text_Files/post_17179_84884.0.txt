Actor Sanah Kapur got married in Mahabaleshwar on Wednesday and the pictures from her wedding are all things pretty. Sanah's brother Shahid Kapoor shared a picture with the bride. The brother-sister duo co-starred in Shandaar. He wrote: "How time flies and little Bitto is now a bride. All grown up all too soon my baby sister ... An emotional beginning to a wonderful new chapter. Dearest Sanah Kapur, wishing you and Mayank sunshine and good vibes always." Meanwhile, Sanah's sister-in-law Mira Rajput shared a set of pictures from the ceremonies and she wrote: "In a garden of love, there was magic. Congratulations darling Sanah and Mayank. Wishing you both the intensity love you brought alive today, every day."
