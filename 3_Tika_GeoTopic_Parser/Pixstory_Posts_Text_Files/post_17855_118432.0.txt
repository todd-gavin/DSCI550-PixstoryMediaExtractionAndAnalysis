On Friday, July 22, Selena Gomez officially turned 30 years old!

The “triple threat” was born on July 22, 1992, in Grand Prairie, Texas. Selena started acting in the TV series -Barney & Friends- and quickly rose to fame as a child star. Fans know her for her successful singing career, with hits like -Love You Like a Love Song- , -Lose You to Love Me- , -Wolves- , and -Ice Cream- .

But fans are celebrating all of her massive accomplishments on her day, including her 30th trip around the sun!
