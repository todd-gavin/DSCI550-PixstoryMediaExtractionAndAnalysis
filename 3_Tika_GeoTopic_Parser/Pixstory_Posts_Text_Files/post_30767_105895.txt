Parma have hired Fabio Pecchia as their new coach The former Juventus and Napoli footballer who is now a specialist in Serie B and currently boasts the promotions centered with Verona in and with Cremonese in the season that has just ended At the presentation that took place at the Tardini stadium Pecchia said Above all, positive energy will be needed The team and the club must restore enthusiasm to their people It will be essential to speed up the times because our opponents will not wait for our running-in period In recent years the level of the cadetry has risen further it will certainly be an exciting championship President Kyle Krause added Parma is a club that deserves Serie A This is why they must return to the top Italian league as soon as possible For this reason we have focused on a figure who has shown that he is capable of obtaining results through a convincing and entertaining game
