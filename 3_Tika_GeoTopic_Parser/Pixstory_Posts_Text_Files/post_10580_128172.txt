The th edition of ALL INDIA J P ATRAY MEMORIAL CRICKET TOURNAMENT TO GET UNDERWAY FROM nd SEPTEMBER The th Edition of All India J P Atray Memorial Cricket Tourament will be played from nd September to rd October at Maharaja Yadavindra Singh International Cricket Stadium Mullanpur I S Bindra Cricket Stadium Mohali Sector Cricket Stadium Chandigarh Tau Devi Lal Cricket Stadium Sector Panchkula and GMSS Sector Chandigarh According to Capt Sushil Kapoor Organising Secretary of the Tournament HPCA the winners of the last year alongside PCC PCA Colts MPCA Baroda UPCA UTCA RBI Mumbai CAG Delhi Goa Cricket Association JKCA Delhi Capitals and others are likely to participate in the tournament teams will be divided into four pools and the tournament will be played on league cum knock out basis The matches will be of overs each side as per BCCI One Day Ltd Over Tournament rules All the matches will be played with SG Test White Balls and coloured clothing After the league stage the top two teams of each group will play the quarter finals All the matches will start at a m PCA Panel scorers and umpires will be officiating in the tournament The Tournament will be played under the auspices of Punjab Cricket Association The winners will get a cash prize of Rs lacs and winners trophy while the runners up team will bag a cash prize of Rs and a runners up trophy Apart from this there will be man of the match award for each match as also the best bowler best batsman and the man of the tournament award Mr Vivek Atray the Convener of the tournament hoped that the th Edition of this prestigious tournament under the aegis of Punjab Cricket Association will be a great preparation for the cricketers for the upcoming domestic season
