Lokmanthan 2022, is organized to seek the cultural and traditional treasures hidden in the various corners of our country and is all set to be held in Guwahati from 21st September to 24th September at Srimanta Sankardeva Kalakshetra. Many popular artists of the Northeast will perform at the four-day festival.Popular singer Kalpana Patowary perform at Lokmanthan on the 23rd of September.An upcoming singer of Assam, the very talented Mayuri Dutta will be also performing on the 23rd of September.
