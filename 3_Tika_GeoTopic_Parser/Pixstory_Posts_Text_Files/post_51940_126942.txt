The first prime minister of a newly independent nation Jawaharlal Nehru was the longest serving prime minister of India He delivered speeches from the Red Fort during Independence Day from to Image Courtesy Wikimedia Commons The Red Fort Complex was built as the palace fort of Shahjahanabad the new capital of the fifth Mughal Emperor of India Shah Jahan Named for its massive enclosing walls of red sandstone it is adjacent to an older fort the Salimgarh built by Islam Shah Suri in with which it forms the Red Fort Complex The private apartments consist of a row of pavilions connected by a continuous water channel known as the Nahr i Behisht Stream of Paradise The Red Fort is considered to represent the zenith of Mughal creativity which under the Shah Jahan was brought to a new level of refinement The planning of the palace is based on Islamic prototypes but each pavilion reveals architectural elements typical of Mughal building reflecting a fusion of Persian Timurid and Hindu traditions
