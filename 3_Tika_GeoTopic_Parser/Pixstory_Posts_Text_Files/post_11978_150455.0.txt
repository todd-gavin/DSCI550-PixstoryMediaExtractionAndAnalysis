Delayed periods are one of the most common problems faced by young women . With the changing lifestyle & food habits , the issue of delayed periods is on the rise ! If it occurs once in a while , do not worry about it . But if it’s persistent , recurrent or if you’re skipping months together , better get it checked to diagnose the root cause of it .
Let's discuss the reasons for delayed periods ; 
1 Stress
2 Obesity
3 Thyroid
4 PCOS
5 Being too thin 
6 Pre - Menopause
Do check if you’re facing too much stress in the recent times or if you’re gaining a lot of weight or losing weight suddenly , or if you’re nearing menopause esp if you’re above 40 yrs . For proper diagnosis and treatment do consult doctor . ❤️
