Earthlings may now hear what a black hole sounds like for the first time in history a low pitched groaning as if a very creaky heavy door were being opened and closed again NASA released a second audio recording of the sound earlier this month based on electromagnetic data from the Perseus Galaxy Cluster which is located million light years away NPR reported that the data has been laying around since the Chandra X Ray Observatory collected it over years ago It was only recently decided to turn it into sound as part of NASA s two year attempt to convert its breathtaking space photos into something that might be appreciated by the ear I started out the first years of my career really paying attention to only the visual and just realized that I had done a complete disservice to people who were either not visual learners or for people who are blind or low vision NASA visual scientist Kimberly Arcand told NPR in an interview with Weekend Edition
