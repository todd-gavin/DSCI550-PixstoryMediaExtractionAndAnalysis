Noori Chammb which is named after the Mughal Queen Noor Jahan is renowned for its natural beauty and water fall It is located kilometres from Poonch town in Surankote Tehsil close to Behram Galla As the stream falls thick clouds of water vapour form covering the environment and spreading everywhere It was given the name Noori Chammb by Emperor Jahangir in honour of his adored queen Noor Jahan since he had grown so fond of and fancy of this fall The Mughal queen liked to come here to relax After taking a bath she used to do her makeup in front of the mirror that had been attached to the mountain wall next to the fall Image source
