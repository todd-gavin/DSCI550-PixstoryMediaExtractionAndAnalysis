To purify your soul and get rid of your original sin every Christian should be baptized Every human has inherited the sin of Adam and Eve the first humans who ate from the tree of knowledge in the Garden of Eden Most babies are baptized after a brief time in Christianity however it is also possible to be baptised later in life The Priest will dip your head in holy water and guide you through the four steps of baptism which are repentance faith in the Lord Jesus Christ and obedience Baptize yourself Let the Holy Spirit to fill you with grace
