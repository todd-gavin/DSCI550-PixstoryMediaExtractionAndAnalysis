BTS Jungkook's "Stay Alive" (prod. by SUGA) now holds the first three places on the list of biggest weekly streams on Spotify of OSTS by Korean soloists in history. Since it was released on Feb. 15, "Stay Alive" from BTS' "7Fates: CHAKHO" has been ranking on Spotify's Global Weekly Chart and recording the highest streams among Korean solo OSTS.
On the Spotify Global Chart for the week of Feb. 27 to March 5, the webtoon OST marked its third consecutive week at No. 1. It ranked first after accumulating a total of 8.1 million streams, the third-biggest weekly streams for an OST by a Korean soloist of all time.
