The third round of the season ended on the French circuit of Magny Cours according to the double-round Sprint format of the GT World Challenge Europe. The race run under a scorching sun was won by the Italian Raffaele Marciello, author of an impeccable race on his shared Mercedes AMG GT with his Russian partner Timur Boguslavskiy Behind the winning pair with more than a second margin was the other Mercedes of Olivier Pla and Maximilian Goetz while Valentino Rossi finished paired with his partner Frederic Vervish only eleventh after starting from tenth place aboard the Audi R of the WRT team Instagram photo gtworldcheu
