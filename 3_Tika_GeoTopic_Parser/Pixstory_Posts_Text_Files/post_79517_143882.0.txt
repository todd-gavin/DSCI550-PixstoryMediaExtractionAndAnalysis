Ranveer Singh claims that one of his photo is from the nude photoshoot is morphed. 
Ranveer Singh recently came under news after he showcased his bold side by doing a nude photoshoot for a magazine. While he received a lot of support from industry and encouragement from fan. But some took offense to his photos and even approached  the Mumbai police registered by FIR against him. Followed by which he was summoned recently to record his statement. During which  Ranveer said that one of his photo from the photoshoot that was posted online was actually morphed but further details are not revealed.
