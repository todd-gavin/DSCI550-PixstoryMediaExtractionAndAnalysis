A statue of a Captain James Cook with an arm raised in a position that some liken to a Nazi salute has been removed from a main street in Cairns The Black Lives Matter movement led to the dismantling of statues glorifying colonial figures all around the world in prompting calls to remove the statue The statue is a smack in the face to all Indigenous people according to First Nations artist Emma Hollingsworth s petition which has received signatures Since the James Cook statue on Sheridan Street has stood as a symbol of colonialism and genocide she wrote For us it represents dispossession forced removal slavery genocide stolen land and loss of culture among many other things Well I say good riddance
