Vamana (Sanskrit: वामन, romanized: Vamana, lit. 'Dwarf'), 
also known as Trivikrama (Three Steps),
Urukrama (One Of Large Step), 
Upendra (Younger Brother Of Indra),
Dadhivamana (Hindi: दधिवामना, romanized: Dadhivaamana, 'Milk-Dwarf'),
and Balibandhana ((He) Who Bound The Asura Bali)
was a Brahmin avatar of the Hindu God Vishnu.
He was the fifth avatar of Vishnu and the first Dashavatara in the Treta Yuga, after Narasimha.
