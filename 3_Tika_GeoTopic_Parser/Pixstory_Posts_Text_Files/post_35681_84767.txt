After Schalke which in recent days had canceled all agreements with the main Russian shirt sponsor Gazprom, now Everton has also cut ties with all its Russian commercial partners. In an official note, the London club wanted to show closeness to the Ukrainian people by illustrating the decision to terminate the agreements with Russian partners with immediate effect Here is the press release from the Toffies All commercial sponsorship agreements with the Russian companies USM Megafon and Yota uk have been suspended with immediate effect
