While reading korean webtoons and watching dramas we get to know new korean words , sometimes which may not have a translation in English here or are not used in the same context . 

Oppa - used by females to call an older male ,may be brother or person of close relation.

Hyung - older brother , used by males to call older males 

Noona - used by males to call an older woman , may or may not be related it indicates closeness

Unnie -older sister ,  used by females to call older females may or may not be related
