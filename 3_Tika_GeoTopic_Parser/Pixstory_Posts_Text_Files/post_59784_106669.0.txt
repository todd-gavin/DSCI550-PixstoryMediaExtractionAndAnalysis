Famous singer and congress leader Sidhu Moosewala was shot dead by unidentified killers on his way by 24 bullets on 29th may , 2022 . His Antim Yarta and Bhog was performed on 8th may ,2022 and people from different parts of the country took a part in the ceremony. 
During the ceremony, his mother asked each of his followers, friends, and family to plant at least one plant or tree in his honour, and thousands of plants were planted on Sidhu's birthday by his fans, friends, and family. The internet was flooded with heartfelt birthday greetings, videos, and photos of his admirers planting trees and paying tribute to their favourite musician.
