Macarons A macron is a confectionery of French origin with egg white icing sugar caster sugar almond flour and food coloring They are mouth-sized with bright colors and tempting texture the Renaissance the cook Italian by Queen Catherine of Medici is said to be credited with its creation in France There are many flavors of macarons available ranging from the conventional chocolate raspberry to the exotic matcha foie gras The candy has a flat base with a smooth square top and a ruffled circumference known as a crown It has a slight moisture content and melts quickly in your mouth They last seven days at room temperature and seven weeks in the refrigerator For about fifty years the chain of bakeries Ladur e Paris is famous for its macaroons Macaroons have become a very popular delicacy all over the world
