After so much pressure, UEFA would have decided to do as some sports clubs have already done and put an end to the sponsorship with Gazprom, the Russian giant which gives one of the main sponsors of the Champions League. The move by UEFA was announced by the English media The Times and The Telegraph according to which the Executive Committee has already given a mandate to the lawyers to immediately terminate the contract that binds the two parties despite a recent renewal From what we learn, Gazprom has guaranteed UEFA millions of euros a year to date
