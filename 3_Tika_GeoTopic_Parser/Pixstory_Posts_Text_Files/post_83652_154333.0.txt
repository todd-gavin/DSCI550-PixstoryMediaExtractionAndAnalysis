Mere Abbu kehte hai! "Beta yaad rakhna yeh Mushkilen tumhara hausla Aazmayegi, Zindagi ki uljhano se ru-baru bhi karayengi, gir-gir ke tumhe khud hi sambhalna hoga, Her raste pe be-khuaf chalna hoga, Meri bete yeh takleefen tumhe mazeed bahadur banayengi, Aur yeh thokrey hi toh tumhe chalna sikhayengi!"
