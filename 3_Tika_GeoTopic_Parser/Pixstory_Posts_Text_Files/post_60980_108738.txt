The topic for the International Day Of Yoga in is Yoga for Humanity The day which falls on June emphasizes the relevance of yoga in our daily lives To commemorate this day many events are being held all across the world This subject highlights how yoga is truly universal as well as how yoga has aided individuals throughout the COVID pandemic Yoga has also been a significant lifeline for hundreds of millions of individuals worldwide during the COVID pandemic giving an important means of coping with the issues of social isolation and stress
