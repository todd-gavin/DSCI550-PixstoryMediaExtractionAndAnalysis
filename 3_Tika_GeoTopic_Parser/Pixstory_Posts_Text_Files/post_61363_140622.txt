Vincent Van Gogh Vincent Van Gogh was a Dutch Post-Impressionist painter He is one of the most famous painters in history His masterpieces include Starry Night Sunflowers Bedroom Arles The Potato Eaters etc. He created a prolific number of works of art mainly still lifes landscapes and self-portraits His works are characterized by bright colors and expressive brushstrokes He was born into an upper middle class family in the Netherlands He settled in Paris where he met members of the avant garde and developed a new approach to still life and landscape French President Marie Fran ois Sadi Carnot was very impressed by his works Suffering from severe depression and poverty the great artist committed suicide
