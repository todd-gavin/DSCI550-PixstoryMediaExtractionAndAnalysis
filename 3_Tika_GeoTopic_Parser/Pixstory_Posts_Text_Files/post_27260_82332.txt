A tribute to a fascinating animal A day dedicated to all lovers of the magnificent feline world For this edition, La Citta dei Gatti returns to Milan, the event dedicated to feline culture with exhibitions, concerts, themed meetings and events. The absolute protagonist of this edition is the Cat with the Boots that will be celebrated with an exhibition set up at WOW Comic Space Comic Museum with original drawings by masters such as Dino Battaglia Guido Crepax Fabio Visintin and Luca Salvagno who have lent their imagination and their pencils to this extraordinary character
