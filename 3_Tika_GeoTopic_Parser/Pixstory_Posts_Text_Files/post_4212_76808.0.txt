‌Punjab : Fielding of new faces by major political parties in Mansa has spiced up the contest in for the Punjab assembly polls.

While the ruling Congress has fielded popular Punjabi singer Shubhdeep Singh Sidhu, better known as 'Sidhu Moosewala', the opposition Aam Aadmi Party's candidate is Vijay Singla and Shiromani Akali Dal has fielded Prem Arora in Mansa.

‌Rahul Gandhi will be visiting Punjab today, during which he will visit religious places along with 117 candidates in an obvious show of strength ahead of Assembly elections that are due next month.

‌ Goa : Former Goa CM late Manohar Parrikar's son Utpal Parrikar offers blessings at Mahalaxmi Temple in Panaji. He will file his nomination papers from the Panaji Assembly constituency today as an Independent candidate.

‌Uttarakhand : Former Uttarakhand chief minister and Congress leader Harish Rawat has changed his seat. He will now contest the February 14 assembly elections from Lalkuan seat instead of Ramnagar, which was announced earlier. 

‌Yet another setback to the Congress ahead of the assembly elections as the former chief of the Uttarakhand Pradesh Congress Committee Kishore Upadhyay joined the Bharatiya Janata Party.

‌UP : BJP's Amit Shah offered prayers at Bankey Behari Temple in Vridavan, Mathura district, Uttar Pradesh.  Sri Krishna janambhoomi has been raked as issue by BJP leaders in past including deputy CM Keshav Prasad Maurya. 

‌Samajwadi Party chief Akhilesh Yadav banged the Uttar Pradesh government over a spectrum of issues, terming the current phase as "half income, double inflation". In an open letter addressed to the public, the SP chief pledged to fulfill all poll promises including 300 units of free electricity per household, free irrigation, MSP for all crops, and guaranteed payment to sugarcane farmers within 15 days, among others.
