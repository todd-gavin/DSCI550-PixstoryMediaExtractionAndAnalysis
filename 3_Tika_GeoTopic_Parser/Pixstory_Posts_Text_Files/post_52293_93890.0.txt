On Wednesday, March 23, at 11:30 a.m. PDT, the Hollywood Chamber of Commerce proudly honoured entertainer Morris Chestnut with the 2,716th star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. The star was dedicated at 6353 Hollywood Boulevard in the category of Motion Pictures.

"Morris Chestnut is a man of many talents and one of Hollywood's favourite leading men, having appeared in some of the entertainment world's most popular television shows and films," Ana Martinez, Producer of the Hollywood Walk of Fame, said. We are honoured to welcome him to our illustrious Walk of Fame, where all of his fans can visit his star." Martinez added.

The Hollywood Chamber of Commerce manages the popular star ceremonies and encourages those who are unable to attend as well as fans around the world to watch the event exclusively on www.walkoffame.com.
Morris Chestnut, a Los Angeles native, has had tremendous critical and commercial success as a film and television star for more than two decades. He is best known for his memorable roles in films such as Boyz in the Hood, The Brothers, The Perfect Holiday, Think Like a Man, and the commercially and critically acclaimed The Best Man and its successful sequel The Best Man Holiday.
