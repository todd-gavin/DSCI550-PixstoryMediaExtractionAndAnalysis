Kartik Aaryan is one of the most popular actors in today's world. He is now enjoying the success of his recently released film Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2, in which he co-stars alongside Kiara Advani. Bhulaiyaa 2 has not only won the hearts of the public, but it is also raging at the box office like no other film since the pandemic, ultimately crossing the $175 million milestone. The Cry Foundation NGO would be celebrating the big  of 175 crore collections with little children.

Kartik Aaryan has organised a special screening for roughly 100-120 children, who have prepared a surprise for the heartthrob. After delivering the year's greatest opening weekend, and the only Hindi movie since the pandemic that is still playing strong in theatres, the young sensation has obviously risen to the top league of actors with Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2. 

Meanwhile, according to a recent media source, with the success of Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2, Kartik has increased his fee to 35-40 crore each film. The actor responded to the story by denying the reports of a pay raise. Taking to Twitter, he wrote, "Promotion hua hai life mein. Increment nahi". 

 As his film passed the 100 crore mark, the actor expressed his delight to his fans. He shared a photograph of himself beaming with excitement on his social media account. "100 crore wali smile (folded hands & heart emojis) #BhoolBhulaiyaa2," he tweeted.
