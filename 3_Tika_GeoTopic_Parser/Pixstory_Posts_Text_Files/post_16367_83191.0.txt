Shah Rukh Khan’s son Aryan Khan is all set to mark his debut in B-Town, but not in front of the camera. In 2020, Aryan graduated from the University of Southern California. He has a Bachelor's degree in Fine Arts, Cinematic Arts, Film and Television Production. 
Aryan is reportedly in talks with an OTT platform for a web series and is also working on a feature film, which will be produced by SRK’s Red Chillies Entertainment.
