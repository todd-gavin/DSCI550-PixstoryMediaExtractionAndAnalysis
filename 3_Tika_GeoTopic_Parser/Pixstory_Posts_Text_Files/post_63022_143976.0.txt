Scotland is home to some of the best food in Europe. The seafood is especially renowned, but don't forget about traditional Scottish dishes like haggis (a sheep stomach stuffed with oats and herbs) or Cullen skink (a delicacy made from fresh scampi). 
Scots are passionate about their history and heritage. There are many historical sites scattered throughout the country that offer visitors an insight into Scottish history dating back centuries. Glasgow is one of the most vibrant cities in all of Europe - it's full of interesting museums, lively nightlife spots, and attractions galore! And finally, Edinburgh is widely considered to be one much prettier city than London - so if you're looking for a charming place to stay while visiting Scotland, Edinburgh would be your top pick!
