The Juventus infirmary continues to fill up at a worrying rate After the injury suffered by Weston McKennie in the Champions League, the young Kaio Jorge was injured today Involved with the Under team in the match valid for the Serie C championship against Pro Patria, the Brazilian remained at ground after a game clash For him there are fears of an injury to the patellar tendon of his right knee with a consequent long stop Alex Sandro will also be unavailable for at least three games after the muscle strain felt yesterday at the end of the first half The two Brazilians thus join a long list of players stopped in the pits which also includes Chiellini Dybala Bernardeschi Rugani and Chiesa
