On Thursday evening, three men—two of whom were bikers—died after falling from a flyover in Ghaziabad. The three guys fell from the flyover when the sweeper was struck by a speeding bike, which also caused the disaster. 
On Thursday night, a motorcycle collided with a sweeper on a service road in Ghaziabad, killing three individuals, two of whom were bikers. The three men can be seen falling from the flyover in footage from the disaster that was captured by a CCTV camera. 

Anmol, a municipal sweeper working on the flyover, has been named as one of the victims, and Rajat and Vishal, the other two people killed who were riding the bike, have also been named.
