Sources of information are instead documents that contain the legal text of the sources of production or in any case favor knowledge of them. subsequent to their publication art co it is said that once the period of vacatio legis has elapsed which represents the period of time between the publication and the entry into force of the normative act, one has a presumption of knowing the law ignorantia legis non excusat and the obligation of the judge to apply it without the need for the parties to prove its existence jura novit uria
