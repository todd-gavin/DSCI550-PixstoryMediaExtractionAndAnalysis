The Telugu superstar Vijay Devarakonda after wrapping up the shooting of his highly anticipated sports action film Liger directed by Puri Jaggannadh last month will again be collaborating with the ace filmmaker in the new project titled JGM.

The project is announced at the movie launch event that took place in Mumbai, Vijay on March 28, had informed the fans about the same through his social media platforms.

After the announcement at the launching event, the actor took to Twitter and posted a thrilling poster of his new film as per the poster and the title the film seems a patriotic film.
