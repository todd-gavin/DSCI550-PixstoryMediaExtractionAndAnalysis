The Peruvian Football Federation has decided to contact FIFA directly for what happened in the final match against Uruguay. In fact, the goalkeeper of the Albiceleste Rochet is guilty of having blocked a cross from the red and white team after it had crossed the goal line. The game then ended with the victory of Suarez and his companions and the consequent qualification for the World Cup in spite of Per For this reason the Football Federation has decided to denounce the work of the referee Daronco to FIFA communicating it through an official note Photo Federcalcio Per
