The number of national teams already qualified for the World Cup scheduled in Qatar at the end of the year has risen The South American group has issued the final verdicts with the qualification of Uruguay and Ecuador The biancocelesti beat Per with a goal by De Arrascaeta in the match that counted as a clash direct for fourth place The result also sanctioned the qualification of Ecuador despite the defeat on the field of Paraguay With a margin of points over Peru and only one game to play, the two national teams thus closed the qualification discussion
