Burberry has chosen a different way to attend the Coachella music festival Usually fashion houses dress a celeb and then repost the photos of the outfits but Burberry and its creative director messed things up Riccardo Tisci in fact went himself in the company of his best friends and muses of the maison the models Lea T Irina Shayk and Stella Maxwell The quartet of friends attended all the concerts of the first weekend of the festival wearing the latest Burberry collections having fun and sharing the best moments on their Instagram
