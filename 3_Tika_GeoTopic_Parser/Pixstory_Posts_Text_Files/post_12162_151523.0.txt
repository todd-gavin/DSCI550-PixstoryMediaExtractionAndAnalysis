The list of breaking records continues  ...
Rosy Meena Paulraj of Tamil Nadu sets a new National Record of 4.20m in the Women’s Pole Vault.  
At 157cm, she is much shorter compared to other pole vault athletes and Paulraj. She has started her career at 23. Just imagine the passion and dedication she has for the her sports.
Rosy fell in love with pole vault while studying at a college in Madurai but had to delay the pursuit of the sport to finish her studies. In 2019, she traveled to Chennai for further trainin. later she added, she won't quit this sport, until she gets an Olympic medal or represents India in Olympics.
