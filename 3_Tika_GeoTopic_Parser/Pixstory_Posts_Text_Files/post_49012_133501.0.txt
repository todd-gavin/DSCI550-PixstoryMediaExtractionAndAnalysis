Ingredients:
-1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
-2 cups unsweetened almond milk
-1 teaspoon vanilla extract
-1 tablespoon maple syrup or agave nectar
-pinch of salt (optional)
-2 tablespoons vegan marshmallows or chocolates, chopped (optional)
Instructions: 
1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Line an 8x8 inch baking dish with parchment paper. 
2. In a large bowl, mix together the cocoa powder, almond milk, vanilla extract, maple syrup or agave nectar, and salt (if using). Stir in the vegan marshmallows or chocolates, if desired. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish. 
3. Bake for 15 minutes, until the hot cocoa is thick and bubbly. Let cool slightly before serving.
