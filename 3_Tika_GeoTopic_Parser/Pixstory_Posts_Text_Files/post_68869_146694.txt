Lokmanthan is organized to seek the cultural and traditional treasures hidden in the various corners of our country and is all set to be held in Guwahati from st September to th September at Srimanta Sankardeva Kalakshetra Many popular artists of the Northeast will perform at the four day festival Popular singer Kalpana Patowary perform at Lokmanthan on the rd of September An upcoming singer of Assam the very talented Mayuri Dutta will be also performing on the rd of September
