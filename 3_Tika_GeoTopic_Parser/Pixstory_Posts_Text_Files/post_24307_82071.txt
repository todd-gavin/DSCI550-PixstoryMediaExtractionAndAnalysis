Puneet Singh Chandhok President of the Indian World Forum on Wednesday wrote a letter to UN Secretary General Antonio Guterres seeking immediate intervention for the safety of minorities living in Pakistan as they are repeatedly facing atrocities and are victims of heinous crimes in the country In a letter Chandhok said I am writing to you today for seeking your immediate intervention for the safety of minorities living in Pakistan They are repeatedly facing atrocities and are victims of heinous crimes there Minorities in particular belonging to Hindu and Sikh religions which have close bonds with India are being persecuted Chandhok in the letter highlighted the persecution of Sikhs and Hindus living in Pakistan Read more
