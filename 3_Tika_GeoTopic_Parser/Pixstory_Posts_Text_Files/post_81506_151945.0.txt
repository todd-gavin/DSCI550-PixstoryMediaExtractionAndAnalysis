This morning saw our men's first-team line up for their official 2022/23 squad photo at London Colney.The traditional shot was captured once again by long-serving club photographer Stuart McFarlane, with Mikel Arteta and our players putting on their best faces for the camera as they lined up for the shot.Taking their places in the line-up for the first time in our colours were summer recruits Gabriel Jesus, Oleksandr Zinchenko, Matt Turner, Fabio Vieira and Marquinhos.
