Bino George s Kerala clinched a thriller to emerge victorious in front of their home crowd Kerala beat West Bengal in the penalty shootout to win the Santosh Trophy at the Manjeri Stadium in Malappuram Kerala Dilip Orawn had handed the visitors the lead in the th minute but super sub Bibin Ajayan equalised in the th minute of the match to bring his side back in the game During the penalties Kerala converted all their spot kicks while West Bengal s Sajal Bag failed to convert his kick This was Kerala s seventh Santosh Trophy title The last time they bagged the trophy was during the season when they defeated the same opponents in the penalty shootout at the Salt Lake Stadium in Kolkata
