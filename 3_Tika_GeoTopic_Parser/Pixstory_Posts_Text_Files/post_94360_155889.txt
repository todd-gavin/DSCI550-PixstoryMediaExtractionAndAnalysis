More than visually impaired persons from across Odisha gathered at Orissa Association for the Blind office Bhubaneswar to celebrate International White Cane Day on th October An assemblage meeting was followed by a foot rally which was taken out from the office of the Orissa Association for the Blind to Sriya Cinema Hall During the rally the participants displayed placards with messages on the importance of white cane in the lives of the visually impaired The event was organised by Orissa Association for the Blind and VIEWS a non profit organization in collaboration with German non profit DKBW
