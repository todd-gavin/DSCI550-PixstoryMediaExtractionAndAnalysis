On Friday night the Copa do Nordeste match was played between Bahia and Sampaio Correa Before the match for while the home team was heading towards their stadium the Fonte Nova Arena a bomb exploded inside the bus Several players were injured of the club the one in the most serious condition is goalkeeper Danilo Fernandes hit in the face by a shrapnel The goalkeeper has suffered multiple injuries to the face, neck and lower limbs as well as a cut near the eye He is currently being observed for further tests An episode of violence that stains Brazilian football Despite the incident, Bahia still decided to take the field, giving their fans a win for three points.
