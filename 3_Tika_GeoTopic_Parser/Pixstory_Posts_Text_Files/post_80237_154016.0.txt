Medals in the National Games are confirmed for Lovlina, Jaismine, and Hussamuddin.
After winning their quarterfinal matches at the Mahatma Mandir in this city on Sunday, Olympic bronze medalist Lovlina Borgohain and the Commonwealth Games medal-winning team of Mohammed Hussamuddin and Jaismine Lamboria guaranteed themselves a medal each at the 36th National Games Boxing competition.
Gujarat's Asifali Asgharli Syed and Ruchita Rajput lined up for a podium finish as the favoured names advanced, giving the home audience much to shout about. Ruchita's matchup with Sanika Sasane of Maharastra was interrupted by the referee because Sasane had broken her ankle in the first round.
