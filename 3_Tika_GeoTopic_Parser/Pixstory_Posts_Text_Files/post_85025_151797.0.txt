Benfica and PSG will be up against each other on Matchday 03 of the UEFA Champions League. Benfica are on the second spot in the points table in Group H of the UEFA Champions League 2022-23 while PSG sit at the top spot in the points table.
Benfica has witnessed a Golden run in the UEFA Champions League this year. The only aspect that separates them with PSG at the top of the Points Table is the Goal Difference. They have won both the games played so far in the UCL.
PSG, on the other hand, have been daunting teams in Ligue 1 and in the Champions League this year. The MNM trio has been destroying teams this year and in the UCL too, they have won both the games that they have played this year in the UCL.
