Here's the recipe

INGREDIENTS:

➖1 tbsp instant coffee
➖1/4 cup water
➖1/2 tbsp maple syrup
➖1 tbsp peanut butter
➖Ice Cubes
➖1/4 cup Home Oat Milk

METHOD:

➖Add coffee and water. Mix it well
➖To the mixture add peanut butter and maple syrup. Now froth everything well with a coffee frother. 
➖Add ice cubes and oats milk to the mixture. 

Your super delicious iced coffee is readyyy🕺🏻
