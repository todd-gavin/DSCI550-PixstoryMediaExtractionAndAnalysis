Dr NK Arora Chairman of the COVID working group NTAGI said today that India will likely begin inoculating children in the age groups against Covid in March as the population will be mostly fully vaccinated by then Over crore youngsters in the age group have already received their first dosage of Covaxin with their second dose expected in days according to him Adolescents in this age group have been actively participating in the inoculation process and based on this rate of vaccination the remaining beneficiaries in the age group are likely to be covered with the first dose by the end of January and their second dose is expected to be administered by the end of February
