Actor Arun Bali dies at 79 in Mumbai. 
Veteran actor Arun Bali, known for his role as kunwar Singh in Swabhimaan, died at the age of 79 on Friday. As per the news agency ANI , the actor died in Mumbai. His last film Goodbye released today in theaters. Earlier this the actor was diagnosed with a rare neuromuscular disease and hospitalised. He worked in several films and television series.
