Sawan kumar tak passes away
Filmmaker Sawan kumar tak who gave debut to salman khan with the movie sanam bewafa has passed away due to heart attack . The filmmaker was admitted to the hospital due to lung related issue , however died due to cardiac arrest in the evening . His family and friends are completely shattered by the death of the filmmaker . Salman khan is also shaken by his death and penned down a emotional note for the maker on his Instagram. 
He wrote, "may you rest in peace, my dear sawaan ji . Have always loved n respected you "
The maker death was confirmed by his nephew he said he died at around 4:15pm. He died due to multiple organ failure . The funeral will be held today evening. Sawan kumar tak was a part of indian cinema for more than five decade .
