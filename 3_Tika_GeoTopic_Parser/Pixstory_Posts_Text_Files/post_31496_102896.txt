Early Eighties From Milan to Marsala Charles H Traub led by Luigi Ghirri composes the ironic and spontaneous portrait of an Italy that today we cannot say if it really existed. Published for the first time in Dolce Via Nova, it expands the original sequence with a wide selection of edited by Giulia Zorzi and Francesco Ceccarelli and transforms the book with a new design by Bunker The work is completed by a dialogue with the poet Luigi Ballerini and an interview by Gus Powell
