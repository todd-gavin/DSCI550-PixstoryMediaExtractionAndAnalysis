The demise of Britain s longest reigning monarch Queen Elizabeth I has to reawaken social media chatter demanding the return of the Kohinoor diamond to India With her son Prince Charles succeeding to the throne the carat diamond which is steeped in history will go to his wife Duchess of Cornwall Camilla who has now become Queen consort Kohinoor which means Mountain of Light is a large colourless diamond that was found in southern India in the early th century The precious gem which moved into British hands during the colonial era is the subject of a historic ownership dispute and is claimed by at least four countries including India Some Twitter users were serious in their demand forthe return of the Kohinoor diamond while others had a humorous take on the issue
