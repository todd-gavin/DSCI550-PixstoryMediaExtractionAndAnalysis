ICC has released the ranking for the top 10 test playing nations. 
Australia regained the top spot at the world rankings again after there they thumped England during their home season of ashes 4-0. Australia sits at the top of the table with a rating of 119, two points ahead of The World Test championship winners New Zealand. 

Australian dominance over the test rankings does not stop here with Pat Cummins being the Number 1 Test bowler and Marnus Labuschagne being the Number 1 test Batsman. 

India sits on the third spot rated 116, one less than New Zealand, India’s rating suffered a blow after their recent loss in the South African series. While New Zealand’s defeat over Bangladesh led to their drop in ranking.
