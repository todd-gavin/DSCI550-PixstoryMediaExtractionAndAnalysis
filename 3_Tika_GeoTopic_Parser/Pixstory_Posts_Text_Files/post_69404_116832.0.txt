In the world of skyscrapers, modernisation and technology an entrepreneur decided to revive the centuries old mud-building heritage. These mud houses, which are blend of ancient architecture and modern amenities, have been built on the banks of the picturesque Sindh River at Kangan. From traditional hay roofs to old doors and windows, the mud houses are also equipped with every modern facility. This beautiful venture has been named “Kulube Mud houses”.  These houses are well liked by the tourists as these give them a experience of Kashmiri culture and heritage. Nowadays only nomads stay in such houses built from mud, dung, stones, husk, and leaves. Apart from providing traditional stay experience traditional dishes such as Wazwaan is also served to the guests and a unique chance to experience rural village life in its entire colorful splendor.
