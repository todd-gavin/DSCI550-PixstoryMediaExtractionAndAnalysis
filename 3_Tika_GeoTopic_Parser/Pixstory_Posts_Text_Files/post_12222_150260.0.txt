The famous Bandhani Saree of Gujarat ....
The trending tie-dye outfits, are in every girls wardrobe. But do you'll know this concept of tie-dye was first originated in gujrat !!! 
Bandhani saree and dupatta are most in-demand and become famous handicraft all over the world.  
Bandhani is considered as a symbol of love, girlhood and marital happiness and has been an auspicious and important part of wedding ceremony from ages.
