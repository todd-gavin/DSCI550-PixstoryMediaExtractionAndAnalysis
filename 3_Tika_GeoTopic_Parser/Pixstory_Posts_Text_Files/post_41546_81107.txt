One hundred men and women will take part in medal events from August September at the Royal Artillery Barracks in Woolwich London Events The Paralympic Shooting programme includes both rifle and pistol events three men s events three women s events and six mixed events Positions and distances Athletes shoot from a variety of positions standing sitting or prone at distances of m m and m The rules for each event depend on the firearm the distance shooting position number of shots and the time limit but each competition consists of a qualification and a final round The score in the final round is added to the athlete s score in the qualification round The winner is the competitor who gathers the most points by the end of the competition
