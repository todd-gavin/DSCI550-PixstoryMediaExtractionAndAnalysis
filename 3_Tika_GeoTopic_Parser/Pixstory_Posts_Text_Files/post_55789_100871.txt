This famous dish developed in honour of Russian ballerina Anna Pavlova s visit to Australasia in the s is claimed by both Australia and New Zealand The pavlova s crispy meringue crust light fruit filling and whipped cream topping make it a family favourite after dinner dessert The origins of this dish in Australia date back to the s at the Esplanade Hotel in Perth As per sky scanner even though the hotel is no longer standing the city still has a strong claim to the dessert Although the finest slice is still up for dispute try the alternatives at Whisk Creamery C Restaurant in the Sky or The Treasury Lounge Bar to give your voice to the ongoing argument
