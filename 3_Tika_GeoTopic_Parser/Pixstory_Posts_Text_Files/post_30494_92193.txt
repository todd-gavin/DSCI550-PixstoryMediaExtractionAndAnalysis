The diplopia that had prevented Marc Marquez from competing in the Argentine GP seems to give the Spaniard a break after the conservative treatments he underwent and so the Honda rider received the green light from the doctors to compete in Austin over the weekend The news was given by Repsol Honda team and Marquez himself who posted a photo while waiting to embark at the airport to Texas Yesterday the Spaniard had a private test with a Honda CBR RR from which he had evidently positive feedback Austin a magical track for Marquez who always won except when he fell while leading
