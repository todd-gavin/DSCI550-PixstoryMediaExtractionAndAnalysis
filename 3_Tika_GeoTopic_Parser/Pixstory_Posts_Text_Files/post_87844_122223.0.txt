The country that holds the #1 currency, where temperature have broken the ceiling and beyond, where spotting a Rolls Royce or a Lamborghini in usual traffic is no biggie! 

From it’s mouth-watering machboos and mutabbak samak and almost 24/7 open cafe places. You will never have to miss out on your favorite international groceries ever. The expats relish the kebabs and hummus to their  heart’s content. 

Kuwait City; hosts majority of the skyscrapers, it’s a delight to look at these gorgeous towers in the daytime with the skies reflecting on their outer mirror surfaces. 

Most claim that life in Kuwait is painfully peaceful and quiet. Time takes it’s own sweet time here. 

While many have come and left, there’s something in store for each one here. What you get depends on how you take it.
