As a part of World Environment Day celebrations twenty four school pupils in Queanbeyan Palerang New South Wales are reimagining their local town and incorporating sustainable living components to reflect the idea of living in harmony with nature using the popular video game Minecraft As per UNEP at a Queanbeyan Palerang Regional Council public screening ceremony the young people aged shared their creations with commentary from their makers detailing how they reflected the World Environment Day subject Expanded green spaces with abundance of trees and flowers communal gardens for fresh produce and buildings with solar panels were among them Over one million trees have been planted as a result of the UNEP facilitated Playing for the Planet Alliance which has also engaged million gamers on environmental issues The game studios that are members and associates of the alliance have committed to including environmental themes in games and embracing a collective shift to green energy among other goals
