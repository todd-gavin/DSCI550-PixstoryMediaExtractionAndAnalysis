Everyone has a story to tell Every individual has a unique and very personal story of their journey to recovery Many successful survivors stories concern a single defining moment often while at their lowest point that became the turning point that drove their recovery Like many survivors my never ending road to recovery initially suffered many setbacks and had far more lows than highs I learned that our recovery could only be helped by people who shared the same experience of addiction and beaten it You are not alone Do not be ashamed to ask for help Do not be ashamed to share your story Your story may inspire someone at their lowest point
