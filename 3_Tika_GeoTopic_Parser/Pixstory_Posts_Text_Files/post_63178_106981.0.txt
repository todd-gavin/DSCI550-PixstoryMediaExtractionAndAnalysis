For their alleged involvement in the violence, at least 92 persons have been arrested in Prayagraj, 80 in Saharanpur, 51 in Hathras, 41 in Ambedkar Nagar, and 35 in Moradabad. According to UP's ADG Law & Order Prashant Kumar, at least 16 persons were apprehended in Firozabad, six in Aligarh, and four in Jalaun.
