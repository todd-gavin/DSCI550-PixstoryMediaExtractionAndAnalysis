An important technique to assess the proportion of the image you are clicking with your phone camera use your phone camera s gridline feature and place the object at the Gridlines or at the intersection of horizontal and vertical lines This will help make your images more balanced This is also known as rule of thirds The rule of thirds helps proportion your image help you place the target strategically and make it more dynamic Rule of thirds is not a mandatory technique it is merely a suggestion to make it easier for you to help in your photography
