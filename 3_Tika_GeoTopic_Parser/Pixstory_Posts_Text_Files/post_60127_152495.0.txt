In the women's hockey competition for the National Games at the Dhyanchand hockey stadium in this city on Wednesday, Haryana and Punjab competed for their positions in pools A and B, respectively, with an all-win record.
In pool B, Haryana defeated Uttar Pradesh 5-1 for its third straight victory. In another pool A match, Odisha destroyed Gujarat 23-0. Young Jharkhand ladies put up a strong fight, but Punjab's captain Gurjit Kaur scored a hat-trick as Punjab prevailed 3-2 in Pool B.
