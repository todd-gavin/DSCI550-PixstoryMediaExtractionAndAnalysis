While the first free practice sessions of the Saudi Arabian GP weekend were taking place, the F paddock experienced moments of apprehension A large column of black smoke in fact rose about twenty kilometers from the Jeddah Corniche Circuit due to the fire that broke out in one of the plants of the Saudi oil company Aramco main sponsor of the grand prix The flames were caused by a missile launched by the Houthi rebels who have been part of the civil war in neighboring Yemen for years and against whom Riyadh officially took the field by supporting the government in office L attack has been formally claimed even if at the moment the local authorities do not seem to question the regular continuation of the event
