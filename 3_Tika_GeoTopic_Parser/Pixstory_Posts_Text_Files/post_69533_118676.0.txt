The number of COVID-19 cases in J&K increasing daily as 557 new cases and two fatalities were recorded on Saturday. The number of active positive cases increased by about 400 over the past 24 hours, bringing the total to 2801. 
Out of the total cases, 275 cases and 282 cases, respectively, from the Kashmir and Jammu divisions were reported.
According to official statistics, 2943 cases or so were reported in J&K during the course of the previous eight days, which is concerning.
Source- Greater Kashmir.
