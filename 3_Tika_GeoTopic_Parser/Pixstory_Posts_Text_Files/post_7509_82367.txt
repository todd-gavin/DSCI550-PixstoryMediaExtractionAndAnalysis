An FDNY firefighter died after collapsing in his Queens firehouse a day after battling a house fire officials said Thursday Firefighter Jesse Gerhard was on duty at his firehouse Ladder Co in Far Rockaway when he had a medical episode Wednesday Acting Fire Commissioner Laura Kavanagh said Fellow firefighters rushed to his aid and then took him to St John s Episcopal Hospital where he died A day earlier Gerhard responded to a fire that broke out at a three story home on Beach Channel Drive near Beach th St about p m Tuesday Four people suffered minor injuries and the fire was brought under control at p m At the time Gerhard appeared to be fine sources said The cause of his death has not yet been determined Kavanagh called him an extraordinary young man who always answered the call and never wavered in his sworn duty to save others
