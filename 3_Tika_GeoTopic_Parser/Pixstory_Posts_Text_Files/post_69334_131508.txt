The dried flower buds that form on clove plants are known as cloves They are a very well liked spice that is used all over the world Cloves are a staple food item in Indian families because of their distinctive flavour and therapeutic benefits For a delicious and entrancing aroma add whole cloves to rice based meals like pulao and biryanis They can also be used whole in recipes with gravy Use roasted cloves and other spices to create a fantastic spice blend that will improve the flavour of your food To make your tea healthier tastier and more beneficial add a few crushed cloves to it For improved digestion consume a clove and fennel seeds after meals
