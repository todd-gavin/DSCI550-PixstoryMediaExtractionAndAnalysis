Why Avtar is good show and why you should watch it

1. It is not a kid's show . 
It has complex characters , dealing with serious issues such as mental health and War PTSD.

2. It is well balanced
The show balances comedy , tragedy and philosphical scenes .It gives its message without being preachy.

3. It discuss philosophy and moral values
Uncle Iroh and his tea time talk will give your much needed daily dose of philosophy.

4.Villains 
Yes villains . It has great villans, they are sympathetic with  tragic back stories ,that you genuinely feel bad for them .But dont worry they have all the grace , elegance,style and nuace and vibes that you expect from villains.

5. It is a guide on how to write a redemption arc 
Zuko has the one of the best or might I say the greatest redemption arc in the fiction genre . It really connects you to him .

So what are you waiting for ,go watch it now
