AIZAWL: Mizoram’s main opposition party Zoram People’s Movement (ZPM) on Wednesday filed a complaint to state Lokayukta, asking the anti-corruption ombudsman to probe into the alleged shareholding and availing of profit from a government contract by state assembly speaker Lalrinliana Sailo.
