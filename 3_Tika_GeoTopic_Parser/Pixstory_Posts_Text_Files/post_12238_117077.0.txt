Coding is always considered as sports in the Computer world. And participating in such sports is essential, when we are speaking about the competitions for top notch jobs in tech giants. 

CPL was conducted in 3 rounds, first two rounds were conducted online on the hackerank platform, top 10 were the finalist who went to compete in round 3. The final round was offline, in our college lab. The final round lasted for 6 hours, with three problem statement given to the participants. Every participant was under the supervision of the volunteers as there is no cheating. 

Top 10 finalist were awarded with certifications, and top 3 were applauded with trophies and cash prizes.
