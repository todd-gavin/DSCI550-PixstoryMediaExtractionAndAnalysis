Teacher s day We celebrate this day every year on th September This day is the birthday of our former President Dr Sarvapalli Radhakrishnan This day is celebrated to pay respect to all the teachers who are doing a great job and hardwork for our upcoming generation Dr S Radhakhishnan was a great teacher He was born on th September He loved teaching so much so his birthday is celebrated all over India as Teacher s day Teachers are called our nd parents Like our parents our teachers also teach us so many things As a student it must be our responsibility to thank them It is a great occasion for us HAPPY TEACHER S DAY
