While walking through the beautiful village of Jibhi you ll find a waterfall hidden amidst the lap of nature in this little village this waterfall can t be witnessed from the roads One has to trek a little A separate gate for entry is made for the waterfall Once you have entered the gate it feels like you re in a different world of it s own something similar to Alice in the wonderland From the gate to the waterfall there is natural setup of flora and fauna along with wooden bridges and steams of water flowing making you feel all magical Jibhi waterfall is surrounded by deep forests with breathtaking views with a pool at the bottom of the falls with refreshingly clear and drinkable water
