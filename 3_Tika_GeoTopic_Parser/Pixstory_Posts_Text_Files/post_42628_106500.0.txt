Real Madrid CF just announced their second signing for the 2022-2023 season and the name of the player is, Aurelien Tchouameni.

Aurelien Tchouameni is a French footballer who plays for AS Monaco in the French league called Ligue 1 and he plays as a defensive midfielder.

Real Madrid also released a statement regarding this signing which is, 

Real Madrid C. F. and AS Monaco have agreed to transfer the player Aurélien Tchouameni, who will be linked to the club for the next six seasons.

Next Tuesday, June 14, at noon in
the City of Real Madrid, the presentation ceremony of Aurélien Tchouaméni as a new Real Madrid player will take place after the
corresponding medical examination.

Aurélien Tchouaméni will then appear
before the media.
