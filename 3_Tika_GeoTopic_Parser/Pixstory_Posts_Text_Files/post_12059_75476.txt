At least positive Covid cases have been registered in China among personnel related to the Winter Olympics in Beijing and all of them are non athletes according to a statement by the Beijing Organizing Committee for the Olympics on Sunday The statement said positive cases had been confirmed following airport PCR tests among Olympic related arrivals who entered China at the airport from Jan to Jan In the closed loop system during the same time confirmed positive cases had been detected from personnel related to the Winter Olympics in Beijing who were not athletes or team officials the statement said Chinese authorities have implemented a closed loop system a bubble completely cut off from the rest of the city for the Winter Olympics to limit the spread of infection All Olympic related personnel who entered China and Games staffs implement closed loop management They are completely separated from the outside society the statement said As of Jan a total of athletes and team officials have arrived in Beijing for the Winter Olympics and none have tested positive for Covid so far In addition no athlete inside the Olympics closed loop system has tested positive since Jan as well Source CNN
