William Ruto the former vice president of Kenya was declared the winner of the presidential elections yesterday The result comes after a long electoral counting and a painful head-to-head The last three elections in Kenya have been marked by controversial results that have led to prosecutions and street violence For this an electoral commission was appointed to ensure fair electoral process in the nearly thousand polling stations Raila Odinga rejected the result even before it was announced His top aides said the elections were mismanaged and called for those responsible to be arrested According to the undressed Ruto received 100% of the vote against 100% Odinga, a difference of only votes but enough to avoid a runoff
