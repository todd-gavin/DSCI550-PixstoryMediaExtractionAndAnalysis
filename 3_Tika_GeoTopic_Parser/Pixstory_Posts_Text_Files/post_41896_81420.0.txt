Bamboo steamed fish is a Naga style Steamed Fish, which is an authentic dish prevalently found in all Naga families. This dish is simple to get ready. The main ingredients are red dried chilies, fresh fish, garlic, bamboo shoot squeeze, and salt. This dish isnâ€™t steamed in ordinary utensils like the traditional method of steaming. It is, instead, cooked with the steam inside a bamboo steamer. Thus, the dish is full of rich and authentic unpretentious bamboo flavor. It is served hot with plain steaming rice. It is best to make this dish with fermented bamboo shoots which is traditional.
