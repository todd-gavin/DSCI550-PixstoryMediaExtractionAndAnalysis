Most of the Hindus in Japan are from India and Nepal. Hinduism is a minority religion in Japan,  followed by nearly 25,597 people.
                    The Hindu God Ganesha is displayed more than Buddha in a temple in Futako-Tamagawa, Tokyo.
     Some of the Hindu Temples in Japan : 
           -  Shiva Shakti Temple and Ashram,  Tokyo
           -  Shirdi Sai Baba Tokyo Temple
           -  Iskcon New Gaya
           -  Ganesha Temple, Asakusa
                   People have written books on the worship of the Hindu Gods and Goddesses in Japan, which encourages a deeper study of Hindu Gods.
