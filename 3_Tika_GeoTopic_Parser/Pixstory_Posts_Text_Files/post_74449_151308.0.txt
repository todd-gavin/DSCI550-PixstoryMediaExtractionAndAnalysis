Ponniyin Selvan 1 aka PS1 is unstoppable at the box office. Directed by Mani Ratnam, Ponniyin Selvan released on September 30. The film, which stars Chiyaan Vikram, Jayam Ravi, Karthi, Trisha and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan in lead roles, is doing exceptionally well at the box office. 😍As per trade analyst Ramesh Bala, the film has already touched Rs 150 crore on the worldwide box office. His tweet reads, "In 2 days, #PS1 has grossed more than 150 Crs at the WW Box office (sic)." 😱😱🔥🔥Trade experts reveal that PS1 has already surpassed Rs 150 crore mark at the worldwide box office.✅He also revealed that the film is doing impressive business in the US, has collected $3 million already. 💯Bala tweeted. “#PS1 has hit a hat-trick of $1 Million gross per day for 3 days in USA," he said, adding that it is the first Tamil movie to do so. According to Variety, Ponniyin Selvan 1 is likely to become among the top five films this weekend in the US. ✅The report stated that the Mani Ratnam film is likely to end its weekend run with a $4.1 million collection in the US. The collections are impressive for a Tamil film in the States however, it is still lesser than RRR’s impressive $9.5 million opening weekend collection and Brahmastra Part 1: Shiva’s $4.5 million collection.🔥🔥😱😱😱
