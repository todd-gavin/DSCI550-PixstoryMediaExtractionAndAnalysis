Many of us have this penchant for reading books as there are different ways how books heal you. However, not everyone is a reading fan but still likes stories and books.
For these people, audiobooks would be the best way not to miss their favourite books.
Listening to audiobooks is yet again a therapy.

These are some 2022 recommendations for audiobooks.

'Sabrina & Corina' by Kali Fajardo-Anstine

'The Hate U Give by Angie Thomas

'Red, White & Royal Blue by Casey McQuiston

'One by One by Ruth Ware

'The Vanishing Half' by Brit Bennett

'Sherlock Holmes' by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle

'Know My Name: A Memoir' by Chanel Miller

'Lafayette in the Somewhat United States by Sarah Vowell

Talking to Strangers' by Malcolm Gladwell

'At Home: A Short History of Private Life' by Bill Bryson
