Religious fervor and gaiety was witnessed all over the ancient city in Varanasi on first Monday on holy month of Shrawan, dedicated to Lord Shiva. Lakhs of devotees thronged the holy Ganges, took dip and later performed jalabhishek of Lord Shiva in Vishwanath Dham and other Shiva temples in city. Members of Yadav community took out a religious peocession after taking Ganga Jal in their utensils from Kedarghat  and proceded in procession to Vishwanath dham as per centuries old tradition. Religious ambience gripped the city today.
