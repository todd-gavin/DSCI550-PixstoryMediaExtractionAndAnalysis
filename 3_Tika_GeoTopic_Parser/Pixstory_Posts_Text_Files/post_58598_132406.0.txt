The Ganges, after recording decrease for a few days, has again started rising  3 cm per hour till 6 pm today. According to Central Water Commission, the current wayer level is 70.44 meter while the warning level is 70.266 meter and danger mark 71.266 meter. Several low lying areas along the river have been submerged and nearly 400 people have been shifted to relief camp at Saraiyah in the city. The District administration is on high alert, monitoring the situation to meet any challenge.
