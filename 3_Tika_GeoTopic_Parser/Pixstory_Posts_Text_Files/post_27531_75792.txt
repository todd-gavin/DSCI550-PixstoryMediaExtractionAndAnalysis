She was certainly the most fit Italian athlete in view of the upcoming Beijing Winter Olympics. Bad luck was fierce against Sofia Goggia. The strong Italian skier was in fact injured after falling yesterday in the Super-G downhill in Cortina. The tests confirmed partial injury of the cruciate ligament of the left knee small fracture of the fibula and tendon muscle pain Now the flash recovery is being tested but it is certain that the twenty-nine year old from Bergamo will not be the blue flag bearer at the opening ceremony of the Games next February The goal is to be in good physical condition for the February match In good luck Sophia
