 Journalists are the present day freedom fighters as they bring the truth in front of society in a fearless manner Journalists had performed a major role in the freedom struggle of the country by bringing the atrocious truth of the British inhumanity They have to play the same role today Vice Chancellor Kurushetra University Prof Som Nath said this while delivering the presidential address in one day National Seminar Indian Journalism organized in the Senate Hall of Shrimad Bhagwat Gita Sadan on Tuesday The seminar was organised by KU under the joint aegis of Institute of Mass Communication and Media Technology Kurukshetra University Kurukshetra and Haryana Granth Akademi Panchkula
