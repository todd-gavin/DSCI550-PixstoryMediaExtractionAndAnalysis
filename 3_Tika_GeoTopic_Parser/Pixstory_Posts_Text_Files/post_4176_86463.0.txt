BJP steadied to form its government in Goa again after arising as the single largest party, the 48-year-old party leader, Pramod Sawant, is all set to continue as the Chief Minister. He won from his Sanquelim constituency by a slim margin.

The Election Commission lifted its earlier prohibition imposed on victory parades during the counting of votes and declaration of results in the five states where elections were held.
