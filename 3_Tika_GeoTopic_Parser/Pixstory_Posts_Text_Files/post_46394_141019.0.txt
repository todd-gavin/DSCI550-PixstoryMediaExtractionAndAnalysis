Ingredients :- 👩‍🍳
1 Papad readymade
200 gms Sprouted moth dal
1 Onion
1 Tomato
1 Cucumber
1/2 Capsicum
Dressing :- 👩‍🍳
Half green chilly
2 tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp chaat masala
Salt to taste
2 tbsp olive oil
Put everything in a bowl , mix well and it's ready ! 😋🥙
Enjoy!!!🥗
