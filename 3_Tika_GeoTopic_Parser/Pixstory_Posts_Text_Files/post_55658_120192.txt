This two part series which will have its world premiere at the Revelation film festival in and debut on ABC iView in March documents the year old Laura Henkel s journey to death As The Guardian says Laura decided to end her life on her own terms and asked her daughter and granddaughter Cathy Henkel and Sam Lara to make a documentary about it The end result is a production that the filmmakers have infused with reflective qualities such as voiceover narration in the form of letters to their beloved matriarch This tenderness could only come from a family connection The ability of presenting significant social concerns through a personal perspective is demonstrated in Laura s Choice which has a warmth and a quietly questioning approach that make people think of Sarah Polley s Stories We Tell Despite being depressed in it s an ultimately uplifting story a celebration of a woman who did it her way as they say and left behind a deeply meaningful legacy
