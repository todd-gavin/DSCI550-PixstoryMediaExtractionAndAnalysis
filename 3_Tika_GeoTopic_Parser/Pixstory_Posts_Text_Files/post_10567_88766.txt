Devendra Jhajharia becomes the first para athlete to get Padma Bhushan award Jhajharia is India s most decorated para athlete He won gold in javelin throw event in Athens Games followed by another gold in Rio and silver in Tokyo Paralympic Games The year old Jhajharia received the prestigious award from President Ram Nath Kovind at the Rashtrapati Bhavan on Monday He hails from Rajasthan
