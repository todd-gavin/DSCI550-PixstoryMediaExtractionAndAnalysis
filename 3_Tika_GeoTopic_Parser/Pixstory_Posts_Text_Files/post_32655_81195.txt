In Rome, England mistreats Italy in the first home outing of the Six Nations At the Olympic stadium, the XV della Rosa grabs a success that is never in question, the result of total domination, also thanks to the Azzurri who unfortunately take decisive steps backwards compared to the decent performance of their debut in France For coach Kieran Crowley the confirmation of what unfortunately many others before him had tried to correct men can change you can launch promising young players you can find reliable equals but the attitude the mentality the ability to read the phases of the match and individual technique represent gaps at this level unpresentable It ends without any extenuating circumstances
