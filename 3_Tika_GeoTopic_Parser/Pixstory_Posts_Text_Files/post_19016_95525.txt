Sinisa Mihajlovic has been hospitalized since March to prevent a possible return of the leukemia disease that had struck him since and which he had managed to defeat thanks to a bone marrow transplant. He spoke about his temporary physical withdrawal from football at a press conference a few days before returning in the hospital About three weeks after that day, his wife Arianna Rapaccioni published a photo in which she portrayed her together with Mihajlovic in a very sweet post You have to be tough without ever losing tenderness You are a soft-hearted Lion Sinisa Mihajlovic in the photo appears smiling and without the cap that has never left him in the last three years. His wife, on the other hand, as per regulations, has a surgical mask and a retina on her head
