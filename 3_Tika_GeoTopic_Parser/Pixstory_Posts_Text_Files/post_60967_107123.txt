World Blood Donor Day is observed every year on June by the World Health Organization to raise awareness about the need for safe blood and blood products for transfusion WHO The day also honors the crucial role that unpaid and volunteer blood donors play in saving lives in healthcare systems It s a selfless life saving deed Countless lives have been saved thanks to the untold number of people who have donated blood for a good cause and a good deed A person who has donated blood must understand how they have positively impacted the lives of one or many individuals Every year on June World Blood Donor Day is honored worldwide to raise awareness and honor individuals who conduct this act of love and charity Support for blood transfusion services blood donor groups and other non governmental organizations active in blood donation campaigns is demonstrated on this day
