If you plan to organize a trip to Italy you cannot forget a stop in Puglia The Caribbean sea is just one of the positive aspects Puglia means art history magic Among the easiest museums to visit and also suitable for children is that of the ceramics of Grottaglie which is housed in the Castello Episcopio is the most important Apulian museum on the theme of the art of ceramics and represents the union between this ancient art and the Ionian town Currently in the Museum you can visit very different sections divided as follows Archeology of Maiolica Traditional ceramics for contemporary use Cribs I have already been there three times always after years and I must say that I never get tired The whole country is full of shops specializing in ceramic creations and especially in the summer you can visit the workshops and admire the masters at work while they create sculptures of all kinds
