The English Premier League is finally back after the two week long international break. In the first game of the English Premier League after the international break, Arsenal are all set to host Tottenham Hotspur at Emirates Stadium. 
Arsenal are on top of the Premier League table after seven gameweeks. They have six wins and a loss in the English top-flight in the 2022/23 season. They are finding their way back to the top. In their previous game, Arsenal registered a 3-0 win over Brentford. William Saliba, Gabriel Jesus and Fabio Vieira were on the goal sheet for the Gunners. 
On the other hand, Tottenham Hotspur remain unbeaten in the league with five wins and two draws. They brutally thrashed Leicester City in their previous game. Heung Min Son scored a hattrick against the Foxes.
