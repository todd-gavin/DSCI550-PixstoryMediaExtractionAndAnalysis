Debuting in the original game Scorpion has appeared in every main installment except Mortal Kombat The character s backstory establishes him as Hanzo Hasashi a warrior from the fictional Shirai Ryu clan who was killed by Bi Han of the rival Lin Kuei clan Subsequent games reveal that his family and clan were murdered by the sorcerer Quan Chi who becomes Scorpion s primary target for vengeance
