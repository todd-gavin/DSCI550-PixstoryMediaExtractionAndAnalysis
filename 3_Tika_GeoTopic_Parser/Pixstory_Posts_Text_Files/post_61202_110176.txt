A bowl of freshly cooked oats is the healthiest breakfast Tasty and healthy that s all we need for our breakfast Personally I m not a big fan of oats But the health benefits of oats cannot be ignored and hence making it an ideal meal to start your day This oats upma is one of the healthiest can be prepared in less than minutes Steps to make it Dry roast cup oats until crisp on a medium flame They do not turn as crisp as poha but a bit Transfer these aside to a plate Heat tablespoon oil in a pan Add teaspoon mustard seeds and teaspoon cumin seeds optional When the mustard begins to pop add half teaspoon chana dal and half teaspoon urad dal Then add tablespoons peanuts or to split cashews Fry until the dals turn golden Then add half teaspoon grated or chopped ginger and sprig curry leaves Fry for a minute until the ginger smells good Add finely chopped onion to chopped green chilies Saute until the onions turn transparent This is optional If using mix vegetables add to cup Half small carrot to small beans and tbsps green peas Fry for mins Sprinkle tbsps water and cook covered until slightly soft for to mins Then add teaspoon turmeric and half teaspoon salt Pour water and bring it to a boil If using rolled oats pour cup water If using instant quick cook oats then pour half cup water If you like mushy oats then pour more water Mix well The water will be absorbed with in a minute or Then cover and cook on a low flame for mins or until no excess moisture is left
