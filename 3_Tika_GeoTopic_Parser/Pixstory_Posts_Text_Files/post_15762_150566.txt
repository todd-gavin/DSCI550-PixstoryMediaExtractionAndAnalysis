Situated off the northwest tip of Bird s Head Peninsula on Papua the most eastern island of the Indonesian Archipelago Raja Ampat or literally meaning The Four Kings is an archipelago comprising over small islands cays and shoals surrounding the four main islands of Waigeo Batanta Salawati and Misool The name Raja Ampat itself is believed to derive from a legend where a woman found seven eggs with four of them hatch and become the kings of the four main islands while the other three became a woman a ghost and a stone Raja Ampat is often dubbed as the last paradise on Earth for its rich marine biodiversity
