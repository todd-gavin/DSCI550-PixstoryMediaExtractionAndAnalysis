
One can eat earlier in the day and it could actually be good for one if they have a goal of having control over their weight, the researchers at Brigham and Women's Hospital says this. 
The study finds that people those who ate later in the day were hungrier throughout the day and they had low levels of serum leptin which is the hormone which regulates the body fat. 
This habit have also burned fewer calories and had a lower core of body temperature. 
As per the study, late eating changes the gene expression in adipose tissue which is in favor of the increased fat storage. 
When these alterations happens, it could predispose people to become obese.
The US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), obesity is one if the common conditions in the US with 41.9% of adults being classified as being obese as per 2017. 
Statistics also shows that there is a rapid rise concerning with the prevalence of obesity which is increasing from 30.5% to 41.9% between the years 1999 and 2020. 
CDC also adds on that obesity is linked to preventable causes of premature death like cancer, type 2 diabetes, stroke and heart disease.
