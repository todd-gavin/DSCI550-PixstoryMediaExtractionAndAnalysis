EXO's D. O. is known for his beautiful voice, his charismatic looks and his amazing acting skills. 

On July 26th, 2021, D. O. released his first solo album, 'Empathy' which has now become the fastest SM solo album to reach 100 Million streams on Spotify in just 221 days. 

'Empathy' was released without any promotions and even so it became really successful and was a complete breakthrough for our D. O. Furthermore, 'Empathy' also holds the record of becoming the fastest SM solo album to reach 10 to 90 Million streams on Spotify.
