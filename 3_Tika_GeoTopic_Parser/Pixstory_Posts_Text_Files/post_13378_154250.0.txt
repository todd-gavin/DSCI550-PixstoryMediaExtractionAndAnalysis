Asian Forum for Human Rights and Development: Hazara are at serious risk of "genocide".In response to the attack on the kaaj  educational centrel, the Asian Forum for Human Rights and Development has expressed its concern over the serious danger of the "genocide of Hazara" in Afghanistan.This organization has said in a statement that for more than a century, the Hazaras in Afghanistan have faced systematic discrimination, hatred and persecution.The Asian Forum for Human Rights and Development added that the main number of casualties in the attack on the Kaaj educational centre is higher than what the Taliban have provided.The statement also stated that following this attack, a number of women in Herat and Bamyan provinces protested against the killing of Hazaras and the imposition of restrictions on women, but the Taliban responded with violence and repression.
