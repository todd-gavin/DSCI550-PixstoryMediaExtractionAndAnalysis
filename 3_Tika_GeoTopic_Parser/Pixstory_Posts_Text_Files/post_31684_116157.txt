It was the spring of when the Medusa Trio called Elio e le Storie Tese to dream together a great concert to end bad luck An indeclinable request actually sounded more like blackmail for the little band who, despite having dedicated themselves to golf and modeling, put on the supergroup mask and relaunched the proposal by adding the will to immediately help the world of music thus born together with the humanitarian organization CESVI a solidarity auction to raise funds for all those independent and deserving bands hard hit by the pandemic while waiting to be able to celebrate a big party together to be organized in Bergamo, one of the symbolic cities of the Coronavirus emergency and also the headquarters of CESVI which will be present at the event with its own corner to tell about the projects underway in Ukraine and how to support them Promoberg, the company that manages the Bergamo Fair, has also been transformed hard in the field hospital puts his mind into it and decides to offer his spaces for free to organize the most beautiful party ever
