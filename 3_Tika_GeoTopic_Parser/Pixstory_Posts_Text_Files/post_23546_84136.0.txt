On February 27th, SEVENTEEN's agency, Pledis Entertainment, announced that SEVENTEEN's memeber DK will be releasing an OST "Go!" for tvN drama 'Twenty-Five, Twenty-One'. 

This will be DK's first solo OST after in four years since his OST "Missed Connections" for MBC drama 'Tempted'. 

"Go!" was released on February 27th, 6PM KST. Listen on Spotify now!
