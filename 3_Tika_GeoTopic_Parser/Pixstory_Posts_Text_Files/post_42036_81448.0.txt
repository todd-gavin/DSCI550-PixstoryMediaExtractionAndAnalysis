Yellowstone National Park is the oldest and one of the largest national park in the United States. It is in Northwestern Wyoming, partly in southern Montana and eastern Idaho 

 There are more than 10,000 hydrothermal features in the Yellowstone. The four types of thermal features present here are geysers, hot springs, mud pots, and fumaro. It is world renowned for its bison herd and has abundant wildlife. It has 300 species of birds, 16 types of fish and 67 species of mammals.
