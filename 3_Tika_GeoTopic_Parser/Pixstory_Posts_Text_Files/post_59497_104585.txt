After authorities warned of impending starvation Sri Lanka is seeking urgent UN assistance to construct a stockpile of critical supplies according to the prime minister s office In the island nation s unparalleled economic crisis acute shortages of food fuel and other critical items as well as record inflation and rolling blackouts have caused widespread misery Farmers have struggled to cultivate crops due to a lack of fuel diesel and fertiliser and the agriculture sector is still hurting from a failed organic policy that cut harvests last year According to Prime Minister Ranil Wickremesinghe s office the UN Food and Agriculture Organization is currently drafting a food crisis response plan to shore up reserves and will also offer more cash for urban agriculture
