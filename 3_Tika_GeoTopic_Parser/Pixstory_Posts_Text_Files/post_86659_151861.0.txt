Are you guys deleting the rows manually in Excel?🤯
It requires a lot of time if you have large data.
Your dose of Excel hack is here😋🍕
So, simply save your time like this⏩
1) Select the whole data.
2) Press F5.
3) A Go To tab will be displayed, then click on special option to the left.
4) In Go To special tab select blanks.
5) Now press Ctrl➕-.
6) This will open a delete tab, select Entire rows.
