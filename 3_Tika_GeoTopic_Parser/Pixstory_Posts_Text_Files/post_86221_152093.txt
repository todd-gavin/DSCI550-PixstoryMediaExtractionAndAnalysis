Stairway to public speaking I had always been someone who had the inclination more towards writing than speaking However I wanted to come out of my comfort zone These are some of the simple lessons I learnt regarding public speaking Be confident If you are confident the listeners will trust you automatically Personalize the content Use of examples questions real life stories and humor can help connect with the listeners Know the content well It is better to choose a topic you are passionate about and know well Additional research also plays a vital role Practice Rehearse the speech as frequently as you can Use body language It is proven that a major part of the communication happens non verbally Avoid information overload Too much information may leave the audience disinterested
