Ponniyin Selvan crossed the 181 crores mark in mere span of two weeks. No movie ever in the history of Tamil Nadu had grossed such an amount, not even Bahubali or Vikram.Overall PS 1 has accumulated Rs. 276 crores approx in India and Rs. 424 crores worldwide till the 13th.Ponnyin Selvan was at 179 cr since some time and two crores short from beating Vikram but by evening the film filled the shortfall and became the highest grossing film in Tamil Nadu.
