-A Dutch company has created a new way to dine
-They made a submarine that lets you dine 200 metres under water
This concept will soon be a reality!

 All sorts of new dining experiences have been curated in the recent past, for instance, dining while suspended in the sky is quite popular these days. 

However, can you imagine dining approximately 200 metres underwater in a submarine? 

A Dutch company called U-Boat Worx is going to make this soon!
