 Pm modi on G New Delhi India September ANI Ushering in a new era in technology Prime Minister Narendra Modi is scheduled to launch the G telecom services on Saturday that seeks to provide seamless coverage high data rate low latency and highly reliable communications system The Prime Minister is scheduled to launch the G services at am on Saturday at an event organised at Pragati Maidan in the national capital On the occasion the three major telecom operators of the country will demonstrate one use case each in front of the Prime Minister to show the potential of G technology in India Reliance Jio will connect a teacher from a school in Mumbai with students in three different locations in Maharashtra Gujarat and Odisha This will demonstrate how G will facilitate education by bringing teachers closer to students obliviating the physical distance between them It will demonstrate the power of Augmented Reality AR on screen and how that is being used to teach children across the country remotely without the need of an AR device
