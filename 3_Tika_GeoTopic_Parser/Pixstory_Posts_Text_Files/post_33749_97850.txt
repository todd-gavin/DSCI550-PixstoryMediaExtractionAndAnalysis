Serie A is the first football league to broadcast a match in the metaverse in the Middle East and North Africa in fact they watched the match between Milan and Fiorentina in the Lega Serie A room present in the metaverse of The Nemesis Users were therefore able to interact with various features present in the room reserved for the match An opportunity seized by the Lega Serie A made possible thanks to the technological innovations that are revolutionizing the methods of transmission The strategic choice of the geographical area should be read in this sense, as explained by the administrator of the Lega Luigi De Siervo Middle East and North Africa represent an important area for us given the dominant presence of generation Z who are particularly receptive to news We will be the first to broadcast a football match in the metaverse because we believe it is the frontier of existential technological innovation for a modern league like ours
