Prime Minister Modi has arrived in Berlin today. He took to his Twitter handle to announce his advent. This is first of his three nation europe visit, germany being the first of them.

"Landed in Berlin. Today, I will be holding talks with Chancellor OlafScholz, interacting with business leaders and addressing a community programme. I am confident this visit will boost the friendship between India and Germany". PM Modi
