 FridayReads BookRecommendation DalaiLama How to see yourself as you really are by His Holiness the th Dalai Lama translated and edited by Jeffrey Hopkins Full of insights and very practical this book by His Holiness the Dalai Lama shows that self knowledge is the key to personal development and creating positive relationships The book is enlivened throughout with warm personal anecdotes and intimate accounts of His Holiness experiences as a student a meditator a political leader and an international figure working with other Nobel Peace Laureates to address crises around the world
