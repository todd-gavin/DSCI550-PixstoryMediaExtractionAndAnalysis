Tilak Nagar s well known Afghan bakery Which people come here to buy cookies These cookies are light and flaky with a little bit of a crunch from the ground pistachios Best of all they are gluten free In Afghanistan these cookies are also called Kulch e Nowrozee which translates to new year cookies and are made at the end of March when Afghans celebrate Nowroz New Year The recipe is very much the same except that bright green food coloring is used to make a plus shape design in the center of the cookies for a festive holiday look
