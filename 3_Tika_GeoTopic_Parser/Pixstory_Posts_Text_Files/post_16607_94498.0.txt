Actor Sanjay Dutt has recalled his reaction when he was diagnosed with cancer a couple years ago, during the Covid-19 lockdown. In a new interview, Sanjay said that his sister Priya Dutt broke the news to him. Sanjay revealed crying for hours as he was thinking about his family and life. The actor also revealed how he fought the disease to regain his strength back. He recalled his doctor warning him of several side effects of chemotherapy but he assured her that nothing will happen to him
