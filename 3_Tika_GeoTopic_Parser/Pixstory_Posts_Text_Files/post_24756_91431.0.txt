Brazil has spent the most time as top ranked in history. They are back on top after a long time pushing Belgium to second spot. Belgium were ranked 1st from 2018.

Eight teams (Argentina, Belgium, Brazil, France, Germany, Italy, the Netherlands and Spain) have held the top position ever since the introduction. 

CONCACAF Gold cup winners USA are at 15. African Cup of nations winner Senegal are at 20. Iran is the top ranked team from Asia (21). India at 106. Recent sensation Canada is at 38 th position. 4 time world cup winners Germany are at 12 in the latest list. 

5 time World cup champions Brazil have been top from 1994 to 2001, 2002-2007 and small spans in 2009, 2010 and 2017. Their lowest being 22 rank in 2013. From 2008 to 2014, Spain dominated the top spot. Argentina came to first rank only twice, 2007 and 2016. 


AWARDS AS PER RANKS

FIFA gives 2 awards as per the ranking.

1. Team of the Year
2. Best Mover of the Year

Team of the Year is awarded to the team that finishes top of the FIFA World Ranking. The Best Mover of the Year was awarded to the team who made the best progress up the rankings over the course of the year. Since 2006, awards have not been given but the list is published. As per the latest list, Canada was the Best Mover in 2021. 

Since 16 August 2018, the current version of the ranking system has adopted the Elo rating system used in chess and Go. As Coca-Cola are sponsoring the list, it is known as FIFA Coca-Cola World Ranking.
