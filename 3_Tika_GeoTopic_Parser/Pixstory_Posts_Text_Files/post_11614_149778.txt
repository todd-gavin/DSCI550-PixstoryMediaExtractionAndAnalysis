Ingredients Required cup pumpkin seeds tablespoon olive oil teaspoon salt teaspoon chili powder teaspoon garlic granules Recipe Remove as much pulp from the seeds as you can Toss the seeds in the olive oil salt and spices Spread in a single layer in a large cast iron skillet or glass baking dish Bake at F for minutes Stir Continue baking for another to minutes or until the seeds have turned golden and crunchy Test one for crunchiness If it s chewy put it back in the oven for a couple more minutes Eat immediately or store the roasted pumpkin seeds in a glass jar after they have cooled Sources zerowastechef com Recipe By Anne Marie Bonneau
