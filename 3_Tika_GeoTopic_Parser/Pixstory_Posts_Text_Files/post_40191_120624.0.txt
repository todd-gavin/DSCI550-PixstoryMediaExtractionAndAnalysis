Carter is an upcoming South Korean Netflix Original action thriller movie directed by Jung Byung Gil.

As per WON, despite the clear and obvious popularity of Korean content on Netflix, movies have lagged behind in terms of quantity as Carter will be only the fourth South Korean movie to land on Netflix in 2022.

They also said that Carter would be coming to Netflix on August 5th, 2022, and thanks to the release of the trailer we can confirm that date is correct. Carter will be available to stream on Netflix worldwide from August 5th, 2022.
