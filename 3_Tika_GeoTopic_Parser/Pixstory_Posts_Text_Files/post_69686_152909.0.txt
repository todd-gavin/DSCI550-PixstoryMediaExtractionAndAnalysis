Blackpink breaks all the records
BLACKPINK's "BORN PINK" Becomes 1st Album By Female K-Pop Act To Spend 2 Weeks In Top 4 Of Billboard 200. "BORN PINK" had remained on the chart at No. 4 for the week ending on October 8-making it the first album by any female K-pop artist in history to spend two weeks in the top five of the Billboard 200.
