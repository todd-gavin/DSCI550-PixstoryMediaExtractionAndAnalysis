The best lead actress and best supporting actress awards were announced to be permanently discontinued on August by the Independent Spirit Awards the most recent organisation in Hollywood to do so Hollywood Reporter said that instead on March there will only be best lead performance and best supporting performance awards given to a pool of nominees in the film and scripted series categories who identify as women To make up for the two eliminated categories a new category honoring a breakthrough performance in film was added for a total of seven total acting awards presented down from eight previously It s something we ve been discussing for a while says Josh Welsh president of Film Independent which mounts the Independent Spirit Awards It s come up over the last two years as something for us to consider both at the staff level and the board level
