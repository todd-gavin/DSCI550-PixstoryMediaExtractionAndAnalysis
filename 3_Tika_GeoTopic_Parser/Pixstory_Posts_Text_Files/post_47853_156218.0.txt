By Shailesh Khanduri
New Delhi: Aam Aadmi Party supremo and Delhi chief minister Arvind Kejriwal Monday said that CBI has arrested his deputy Manish Sisodia.
Kejriwal said this during his public rally in Gujarat.
https://twitter.com/ArvindKejriwal/status/1581945257566306304?t=YqgQ6qUdW26x0wR3lJfCsw&s=19
Addressing the public gathering, Kejriwal said that Sisodia was to come to Gujarat but he was arrested today.
"They will keep him in jail for a month till the elections in Gujarat are over and then he will be released," Kejriwal added.
