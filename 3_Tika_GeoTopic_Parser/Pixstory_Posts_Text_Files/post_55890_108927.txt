The Treasury has confirmed the news of the nomination of Mohegan Indian Tribe Lifetime Chief Marilynn Malerba as U S treasurer marking the first time in history for a Native American s signature will appear on the currency Malerba is nominated by Joe Biden After Jovita Carranza left to become Small Business Administrator in the Trump administration the treasurer position has remained vacant Treasurer being a Native American spur the hope of boosting their economy and supporting developments of tribal economies The treasury secretary Janet Yellen has said With this announcement we are making an even deeper commitment to Indian Country Malerba was a nurse for a long period and has also been the chief of the Connecticut based Mohegan tribe since and previously chaired its tribal council and served as its executive director of Health and Human Services President Biden is yet again consciously putting efforts to represent people of diversity in all the fields of governance which is to be appreciated Source the guardian
