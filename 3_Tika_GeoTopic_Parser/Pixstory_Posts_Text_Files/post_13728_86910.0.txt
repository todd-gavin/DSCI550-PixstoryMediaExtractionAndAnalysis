Portuguese football superstar Cristiano Ronaldo became "all-time leading scorer in history" with his thrilling hat-trick against Manchester United against Tottenham.

The English Premier League match ended on Saturday, 21st, with a result of 3 - 2 in favor of Manchester United.

Ronaldo's total goals with this hat-trick reached 807 goals.

The official history of FIFA shows that the Portuguese star was able to surpass the record of 805 goals by Josef Bican and register his name as the best scorer in history.
