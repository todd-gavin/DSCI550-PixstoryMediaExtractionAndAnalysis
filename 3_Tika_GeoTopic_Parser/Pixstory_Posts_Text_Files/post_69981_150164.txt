Eating well is a way of self care Self care looks different for everyone But one thing that we can all agree on is that self care is about nourishment mentally physically and emotionally Nutrition is the foundation of good health Sadly our diet culture has programmed us to think of food as the enemy but in reality it s the ultimate form of Nourishment for the mind and body You can follow some smart eating habits that ll help you lead a healthier and happier life Load your plate with fruits and vegetables Use herbs and spices Eat regularly Increase intake of healthy fats Say hello to nuts and seeds Limit added sugars Slow carb instead of no carb Eat foods that will help you sleep better Pay attention to your plate Proper Nutrition plays a vital role in your ability to thrive and prioritising it is making an investment in yourself
