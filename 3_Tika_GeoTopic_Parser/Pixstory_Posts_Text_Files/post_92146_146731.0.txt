                     WORLD'S PEACE DAY
   It is celebrated on 22 September all over the world.This day is devoted to strengthing the ideals of peace,  observing 24 hours non violence and justice.But achieving true peace means  building a society where all 
members feel that they can flourish.It involves creating a world in which  people are treated equally, regardless of their caste.
We all have a role to play in fostering peace andtackling racism is a crucial way to contribute.
We can work to finish the structures that entrish racism in our midst. We can support  movements for equality and human rights everywhere.We can speak out against hate  speech--both offline and online.We can promote anti- racism through education  and justice.
The theme 2022 for the international Day of Peace is"End racism.Build peace"
