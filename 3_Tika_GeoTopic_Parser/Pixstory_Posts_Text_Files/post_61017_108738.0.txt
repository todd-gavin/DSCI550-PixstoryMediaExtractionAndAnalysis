The topic for the International Day Of Yoga in 2022 is "Yoga for Humanity." The day, which falls on June 21, emphasizes the relevance of yoga in our daily lives. To commemorate this day, many events are being held all across the world.
This subject highlights "how yoga is truly universal," as well as how yoga has aided individuals throughout the COVID-19 pandemic. Yoga has also been a significant lifeline for hundreds of millions of individuals worldwide during the COVID-19 pandemic, giving an important means of coping with the issues of social isolation and stress.
