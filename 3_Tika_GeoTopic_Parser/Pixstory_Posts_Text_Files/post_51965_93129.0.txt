Few Do's and Don'ts that I keep in mind while introducing myself:-
                           *Do's*
1. Stay optimistic
2. Tell them about your study/work experiences 
3. Tell them what kind of person you are.( your personality type etc.)
4. Your strengths 
5. Stick to the Context
6. Stay truthful 
7. The most important thing:- STAY CONFIDENT 
                        *Don'ts*
1.Don't get Informal 
2. Don't exaggerate anything 
3. Don't lie about anything 
4. Don't think pessimistically 
5. Don't say yes to everything
