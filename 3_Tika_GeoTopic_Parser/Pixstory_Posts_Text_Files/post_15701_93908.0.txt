Gorriti Island is a small island near the shores of Punta del Este, Uruguay.

Along with Isla de Lobos, the island has been declared to be a National Heritage, attracting tourists on beaches annually.Surrounding waters are rich in wildlife both for permanently and seasonally, including southern right whales, southern elephant seals and orcas chasing tunas or South American sea lions and South American fur seals breed on Isla de Lobo.
