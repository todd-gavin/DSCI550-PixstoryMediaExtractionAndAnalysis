The 36th edition of the National Games is underway in Gujarat. On Saturday, October 1, the Badminton Mixed Team Event began with four matches. Telangana received a bye in Round1 of the competition. Meanwhile, Maharashtra, Gujarat and Kerala registered a convincing victories against their respective opponents and advanced to the semifinals.
Telangana will play Maharashtra in the 1st semi-final, while Gujarat will take on Kerala in the2nd semi-final match of the competition. Both semi-final matches and the final will take placeon Sunday, October 2.
