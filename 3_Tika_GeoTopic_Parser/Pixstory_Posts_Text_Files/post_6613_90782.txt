By Shinjinee Majumder A video of a woman dancing to Rajasthani folk music amidst a crowd is viral on social media with the claim that she is IAS officer Rukmani Riar who is currently the District Collector of Sriganganagar in Rajasthan Twitter user beingvikas shared this video and garnered close to views and more than retweets Link Several other users shared this video on Twitter Link Link This video is also viral on Facebook with the same claim Rukmani Riar first came into the limelight in when she cracked the Union Public Service Commission UPSC exams with an All India Rank She then became the second Punjab born District Collector in Sriganganagar in January Fact check We reached out to Rukamni Riyar who denied these claims and called them completely fake Upon reverse image searching frames from the video with relevant keywords the earliest instance of the same video that we could find was on YouTube dated December Link The video is captioned New Rajasthani Marriage Dance New Marwadi Dj Song and does not claim that the woman in the video is IAS officer Rukmani Riar We also found a post on Facebook dated June It is captioned which translates to The Jatani of Shekhawati region of Rajasthan danced so well balancing the glass and pot kept on top of her head Link We compared stills from the two videos and found they are the same
