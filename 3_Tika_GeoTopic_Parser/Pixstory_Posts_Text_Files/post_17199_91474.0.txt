Today, Falguni Shane Peacock and Rohini Iyer will be co-hosting an impromptu get-together in honour of Mohammed Al Turki, Chairman – Red Sea International Film Festival, the biggest film festival in Saudi Arabia and a dear friend of Falguni Shane Peacock. The co-hosts celebrated Middle East’s most influential man at the dinner. With award-winning films like Arbitrage, 99 homes, Adult World and At Any Price to his name, he is a hotshot Hollywood producer, entrepreneur and philanthropist. Mohammed Al Turki, fondly called ‘Mo’, has been the catalyst in changing the face of the Saudi Arabia’s film industry and putting it on the list of cinema hotspots across the world.
