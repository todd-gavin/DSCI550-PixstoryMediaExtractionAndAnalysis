On the occasion of Hema Malini s th birthday ETimes connected with Aruna Raje Patil who directed the Dream Girl of the Hindi cinema in the film Rihaee Talking about working with the veteran beauty Aruna said It was great working with Hemaji Everyone except my dialogue writer thought she would never do the film But after my narration she said yes immediately Raje also shared that Hema didn t shy away from doing the film even after knowing that the character talks about women s sexual needs and the double standards of society when her own image was a very pure and pristine one She got the women s perspective and agreed to do it The Rihaee director added that Hema Malini is a strong person and tackled situations with dignity
