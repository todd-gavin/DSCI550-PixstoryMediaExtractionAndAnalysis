The Afghan men's and women's national snowboard teams won two gold and one bronze medal in the Pakistan tournament.

The Afghan men's national team won the gold medal in the individual event, according to the Afghan Shafaqna news agency.

On the other hand, the women's national team also won a bronze medal in the individual section of the competition.

The tournament was held from February 5 to 12 in Pakistan-controlled Kashmir.
