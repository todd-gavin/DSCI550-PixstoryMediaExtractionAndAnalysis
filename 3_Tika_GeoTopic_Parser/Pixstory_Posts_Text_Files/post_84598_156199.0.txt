Abdul Salam Kumar, a Kashmiri Muslim potter arranges earthen lamps at his home workshop ahead of the Diwali festival, the major festival of the Hindus, on the outskirts of Srinagar on October  17,2022. Diwali, the festival of lights symbolizes the victory of good over evil, and commemorates Lord Ram's return to his kingdom Ayodhya after completing a 14-year exile. Diwali will be celebrated on 24 November.
