DELHI METRO An ideal means of transportation is for people living in Delhi is Delhi metro It connects each and every corner of the Capital city It saves both time as well as money It is run by electricity It moves from one place to another very fast It is very smooth also runs under ground Plastic tokens are given as a ticket for travelling People need to buy this tokens to travel in any corner of Delhi The routes of Delhi Metro is divided into different colour lines As Delhi is a populated city metro helps to control traffic It reduces traffic It is very safe and very clean
