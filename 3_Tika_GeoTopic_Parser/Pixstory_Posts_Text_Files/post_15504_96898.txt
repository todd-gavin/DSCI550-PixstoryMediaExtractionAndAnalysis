Umami Taste Experience has decided to combine its offer with respect for the marine environment For this reason Umami Seregno joined the Friend of the Sea Sustainable Restaurants project The initiative was launched by Friend of the Sea the international certification standard for sustainable fishing and aquaculture products to reward those restaurants that are concretely committed to the over-exploitation of the planet&#39;s resources and that make the protection of marine habitats a fundamental point of their culinary offer. A challenge accepted with enthusiasm by Umami Seregno who had already started a rethinking of its activities so that they were more attentive to environmental issues From the goal by of of eliminating all superfluous plastic objects from its premises to collaborations with other organizations such as One Tree Planted which plants trees to counteract CO emissions and deforestation to the use of take-away trays made of recycled and recyclable materials
