NO CT GD Desha Singh was part of the Quick Reaction Team QRT deployed for providing security to the Border Roads Organization BRO personnel in Afghanistan On rd January at hours the Quick Reaction Team received information that a bomb kept in a motorcycle has been detonated before the convoy of BRO returning from the Quarry operations in a Taliban infested town The Quick Reaction Team immediately reached the blast site Without wasting time and cordination with QRT commander and after ensuring that the last vehicle of the convoy moved away from blast site safely he joined his team for returning to Headquarters when a suicide bomber blew up himself close to him He sustained severe injuries and later succumbed to his injuries Thus Shri Desha Singh displayed raw courage determination and sacrificed his life on the line of duty
