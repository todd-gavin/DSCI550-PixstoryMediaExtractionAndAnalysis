Italian basketball mourns Franco Casalini, historical assistant coach of Dan Peterson at Olimpia Milano before inheriting the Red Shoes bench from the American coach and winning the Champions Cup of the season. Casalini was found dead in his home according to the first rumors the the cause could be a heart attack After ending his coaching career while still young, Casalini had become a highly appreciated television commentator on the microphones of Tele Sky and Eurosport
