Seville's Ramón Sánchez-Pizjuán Stadium will stage the final of the 2021/22 UEFA Europa League on 18 May. Host club Sevilla hold a special place in the history of the UEFA Cup and UEFA Europa League, having won the competition a record six times; twice as the UEFA Cup and four more times in the UEFA Europa League era, most recently in 2019/20.

The Europa League final will be contested by Eintracht Frankfurt and Rangers, the two leading scorers in the competition with 20 and 22 goals respectively. The Scottish side have already eliminated two German clubs in the knockout stages: Dortmund in the knockout round play-offs, and Leipzig in the last four.
