The famous perfume "Chanel" celebrated its 100th anniversary. The most famous perfume of this company, cape number 5 with the main element of jasmine flower fragrance was produced in France in the twenties. Cultivated jasmine flowers are used to produce this perfume. Gabriel Coco Chanel created this perfume inspired by modern women, and its purpose was to reflect the intricacies of modern women. Perfume No. 5 is the first perfume produced by a woman for women.
