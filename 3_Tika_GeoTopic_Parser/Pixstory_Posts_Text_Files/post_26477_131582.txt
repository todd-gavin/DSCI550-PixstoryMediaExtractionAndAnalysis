Anna Fendi on September in Venice during the ma Mostra del Cinema will receive the Women in Cinema Award the recognition born from an idea by Angela Prudenzi Claudia Conte and Cristina Scognamillo The aim of the award is to enhance and pay tribute to the talent of women mainly within the film industry and the world of the arts but also to celebrate all those artisans who make cinema and the arts in general great. The prize-winners are chosen from among the excellences of the cinema and the arts close to it, witnesses able to represent the different cultures and the different ways of tell stories and characters The designer with her four sisters Paola Franca Carla and Alda has dedicated a life to fashion largely demonstrating how fashion dresses cinema
