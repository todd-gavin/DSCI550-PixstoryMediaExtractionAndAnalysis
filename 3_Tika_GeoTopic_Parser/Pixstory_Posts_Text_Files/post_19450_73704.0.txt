Meghalaya chief minister Conrad Sangma has placed himself under isolation after experiencing COVID symptoms. 

Meghalaya CM Conrad Sangma will take a COVID-19 test, according to reports. 

Due to absence of Meghalaya CM, the statehood day function in Shillong was inaugurated by deputy CM Prestone Tynsong.
