Pavel is a NonBinary Femme Person A queer feminist by practice ze currently associated with Nazariya Foundation A Queer Feminist Resource Group as program manager A writer storyteller and Co founder of The Chinky Homo Project through their work ze try to understand themselves and the community they belong to better Pavel will be participating in the Fireside Chat on Locating healthcare in daily trans lives in the upcoming TransCare Med Ed National conference on May th About the Conference In recent years there s been a growing push to make medical education trans affirmative in the backdrop of Madras and Kerala High Court judgements But transforming India s healthcare system comes with the need for a roadmap towards achieving this goal Through a systematic participatory and inclusive effort TransCare MedEd has developed a set of draft trans affirmative competencies on trans affirmative healthcare in India The upcoming TransCare Med Ed National conference on May th and th is aimed at disseminating and discussing the draft competencies highlighting best practices and building consensus on the way forward Watch this space for narratives by transgender people navigating healthcare settings and for thoughts and pathways on the way towards creating access to trans affirmative healthcare
