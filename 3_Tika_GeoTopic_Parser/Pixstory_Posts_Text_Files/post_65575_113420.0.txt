Live updates on the Mumbai rains Chief Minister Eknath Shinde has ordered officials to remain vigilant and monitor the situation because areas of Mumbai are experiencing waterlogging as a result of the city's recent heavy rains. Over the past 24 hours ending at 8.30 am on Tuesday, the observatories in Colaba and Santacruz both recorded rainfall totals in the triple digits, 117 mm and 124 mm, respectively. Tuesday will also see a lot of rain across the whole Konkan region. According to the Chief Minister's Office, NDRF teams have been dispatched and the district administrations of Thane, Palghar, Raigad, Ratnagiri, Sindhudurg, and Kolhapur have been placed on high alert.

Balasaheb Thackeray had already stated that the Shiv Sena would win the floor test in the Maharashtra Assembly on Monday, according to Shinde.
