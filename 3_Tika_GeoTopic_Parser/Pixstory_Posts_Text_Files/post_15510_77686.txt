Second appointment of the Univr University of Verona program for the victims of the holocaust with the testimony of the writer Edith Steinschreiber Edith Bruck her stage name survived the deportation to the concentration camps of Auschwitz Dachau Bergen Belsen where she was deported little more than a child she spent most of her life to recount the terrible experience through writing and bringing her testimony to schools and universities In she was awarded the title of Knight Grand Cross of the Order of Merit of the Italian Republic The writer will dialogue with Michela Ponzani historian and journalist and Renato Camurri professor of Contemporary history at the Veronese university The event will open with institutional greetings from the Magnificent Rector Pierfrancesco Nocini and Olivia Guaraldo delegate for Public Engagement
