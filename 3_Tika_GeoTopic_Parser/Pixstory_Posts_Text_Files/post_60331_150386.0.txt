Ishani Dave Gujarat-based professional singer today brought the house down by her performance in National Games Opening Ceremony.
Ishani Dave started her career at the tender age of three. Her father, Gujarat's top folk singer Praful Dave, became a source of inspiration to her. Ishani has performed at numerous LIVE events across the state throughout the course of her singing career she have collaborated with a number of Bollywood music composers, including Amit Trivedi and Sachin Jigar
