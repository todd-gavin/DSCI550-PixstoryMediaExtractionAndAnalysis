A sad surprise for tennis fans comes from Buenos Aires where the Argentina Open is scheduled in a press conference Juan Martin Del Potro announced his intention to retire definitively from competitive activity Winner of a US Open Del Potro was haunted by injuries The last one fractured his kneecap suffered at the Shanghai Masters of forced him to undergo surgery which was not enough to get him back to play The former world number thus chose his home tournament to greet the public In the draw thanks to a wild card he will make his debut against his compatriot Federico Delbonis
