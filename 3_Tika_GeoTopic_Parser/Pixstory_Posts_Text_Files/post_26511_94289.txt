On the occasion of the Barcelona bridal night Viktor Rolf will present his first Mariage fashion show The show will take place on April at the Xavier Corber Research and Artistic Activities Center in Esplugues de Llobregat during the gala evening of the Barcelona bridal fashion week With Mariage the duo formed by Viktor Horsting and Rolf Snoeren will bring to the catwalk a selection of wedding dresses and haute couture creations To enrich the show the debut creations of the Viktor Rolf Mariage spring / summer collection with their floral embellishments in hand-woven silk organza twisted around the bodices The dresses that will be presented during the fashion show have all been designed using conscious materials For the occasion, the duo strengthened their ongoing collaboration with Lenzing, one of the major producers of ecological fibers
