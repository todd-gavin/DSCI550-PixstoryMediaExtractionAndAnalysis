Katrina Kaif and Ranbir Kapoor attend Kalyanaraman family Navratri Bash. 
The Kalyanaraman family hosted a Navratri Bash at their residence recently, which was attended by several stars from Hindi, Tamil and Telugu film industries. Among the guest were actors Nagarjuna, R.Madhavan, Prithviraj Sukumaran. But what draws the fans attention was exes Katrina Kaif and Ranbir Kapoor both in attendance as even they kept their distance, judging by the pics. They were seen posing with host along with other actors such as Nagarjuna.
