International chess player Mayank Chakraborty is qualified for World & Asian Chess Championships. Candidate Master Mayank Chakraborty hails from Assam and is currently ranked number 1 in India and Asia and Number 6 in the World in his age category. In 2019 he won the U-19national chess championship  title. The Assamese chess prodigy Mayank clinched the national u-17 chess title which concluded at KIIT, Bhubenswar recently. It is the first time when a chess player from Assam win the national u-17 chess championship title. Mayank scored 8.5 points in eleven rounds and finished the championship. Mayank received a prize money of Rs 90,000. He will represent India in World U-18 Chess Championship that is to be held in Romania,2023.
