The first Formula winter tests ended in Barcelona before the appointment in Bahrain in two weeks time for another three days on the track to get to know and test the new cars. The best times were those of Lewis Hamilton and George Russell, the standard bearers of Mercedes who for they mounted the softer compound tire to achieve their performances. The excellent times of Verstappen and Leclerc obtained with a two-step harder compound should be underlined. Carlos Sainz was also not bad seventh and tenths off the times of the latter. The session was interrupted more times from the red flags problems for Alpine Aston Martin Alfa Romeo and Haas In the afternoon the cars also ran on an artificially wet track to test the wet tires
