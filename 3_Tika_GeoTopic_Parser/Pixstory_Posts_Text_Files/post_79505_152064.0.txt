Rashmika Mandanna shares a photo with her real family. 
Actor Rashmika Mandanna shared a family picture on her Instagram handle on Tuesday. She introduced her real family to fans. The picture featured her parents and her younger sister. She captioned the picture, " This is my real family, and in 3 days you get to meet my reel family - The # Goodbye Family. Are you excited as I am?" The actor is currently busy promoting her film Goodbye which is all set to be released on Oct 7.
