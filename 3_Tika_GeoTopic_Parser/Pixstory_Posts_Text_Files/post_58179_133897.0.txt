Supertech ' Apex and Ceyanne towers are set to be demolished this afternoon in series of controlled blasts. People have  welcomed  this  decision and many are excited to see the demolition. 
Many tv channels and social media platforms are streaming it live . It has become a spectacle. Many have tuned in to watch it .People are uploading selfies with twin towers before their demolition. Some from delhi and other parts of noida have gone there just to see it live with their own eyes. A traffic advisory has also been issued in the newspaper. The whole process is said to take a total of 9 seconds.
