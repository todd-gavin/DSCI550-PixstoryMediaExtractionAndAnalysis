How to deal with rejection in workplace ....
1. Understand your not alone 
2. Take some time, to Understand what went wrong.
3. Think of how can you improve in the next interview.
4. Spend some time with your loved ones.
5. Focus on your strengths. Highlight that in the resume.
6. Practice mock tests and interviews.
7. Apply at places, where your skills are suitable.
8. Most importantly, keep trying 
Never give up.
