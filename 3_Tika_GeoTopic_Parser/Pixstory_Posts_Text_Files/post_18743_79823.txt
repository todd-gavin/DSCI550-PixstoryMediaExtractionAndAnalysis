Federica Brignone does not disappoint and indeed even manages to improve on the bronze medal won four years ago in PyeongChang In Beijing, the Italian skier returns to the Olympic podium in the Giant but this time she does so by wearing the silver medal around her neck, the result of a first round very aggressive and a second more experience which guaranteed her the second placement behind the usual unattainable Sara Hector Behind the Italian then bronze medal came Lara Gut Behrami who after a first heat closed in eighth place made up for it in the second heat by recording the best time in the descent
