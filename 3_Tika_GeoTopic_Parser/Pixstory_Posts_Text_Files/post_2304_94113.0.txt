Cricketer-turned-politician Harbhajan Singh has announced that he will contribute his Rajya Sabha salary for the education and welfare of the daughters of farmers.

Singh said as a Rajya Sabha member he would do everything possible for the "betterment of the country".

"As a Rajya Sabha member, I want to contribute my RS salary to the daughters of farmers for their education & welfare. I've joined to contribute to the betterment of our nation and will do everything I can. Jai Hind," he tweeted.

Indeed it is a commendable initiative.
