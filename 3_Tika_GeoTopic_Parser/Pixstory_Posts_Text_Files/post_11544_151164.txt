 A hot tea always goes well with some snacks and the snacks we eat from out could be unhealthy for us If you wish to make use of some leftover kichidi then here is a healthy yet delicious kichidi pagodas for you Ingredients Leftover Khichdi Salt Tsp Red Chilli Powder Tsp Coriander Powder Tsp Dried Mango Powder Aamchur Tsp Garam Masala Tsp Ajwain Tbsp Cornflour Or Oats Bread Crumbs Grind Slices Of Bread In A Mixer Oil Chopped Onion Chopped Green Chillies Instructions Step Add the balls to the hot oil and fry them till they turn golden brown Drain out the excess oil and remove the balls Step Take a bowl and mix all the ingredients in it Mix till these ingredients combine and make a dough like consistency If the mixture is loose add in more breadcrumbs and mix them till they become tight Step Pour in enough oil for deep frying them in the pan As the oil heats to the desired temperature make small balls and flatten them with your thumb To get the same dip in your fingers in water or oil so they become moist Step If you prefer for crispier outer crust then fry them for minutes more and then take them out and let it cool Step Serve them with your choice of chutney Sources Her Zindagi Com
