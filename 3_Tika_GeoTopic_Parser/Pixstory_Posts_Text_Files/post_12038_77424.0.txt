India started its precaution dose vaccine drive for healthcare and frontline workers and those aged 60 and above on Jan 10th. 

Since then India has administered over 1 crore doses to the eligible population in 19 days. 

ANI shared a tweet quoting Union Health Minister Dr Mansukh Mandaviya, “Over 1 crore precaution doses have been administered to the eligible population in just 19 days.”
