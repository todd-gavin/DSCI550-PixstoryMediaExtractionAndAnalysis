Barcelona have unveiled their home kit for the next season Nike has come up with a vertical stripe based design for the La Liga giants making them stand out from other teams taking to the pitch The Catalan based teams are famous for their striped jerseys with only once shifting from vertical stripes to horizontal ones in the course of their history The club has said that the kit is inspired by the Barcelona Olympics in the year that the city is celebrating the th anniversary of the transformation The new jersey harks back to the landmark event under the tagline The Flame Lives On Its design evokes the spirit of that period of regeneration and redefinition a time when Barcelona opened up to the world and the world opened up to Barcelona The jersey will also welcome new title sponsor musical and streaming giant Spotify as they begin their sponsorship of the club You can get your new Barcelona jersey from a Nike outlet near you
