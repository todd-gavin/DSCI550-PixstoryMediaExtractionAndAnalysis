The value bestowed upon the Indian market by various small-scale businesses and industries needs to be recognised! They add hugely to the overall growth of the nation, and opportunities for the unemployed.
Do you know?
In India, the earliest definition of small-scale industries in 1950 had a limit of 0.5 million rupees in fixed investment, with fewer than 50 workers in units using power or fewer than 100 workers in units not using power for manufacturing.
