Starting in Srinagar and ending in Pahalgam the Tarsar Marsar Trek leads you through some of the most beautiful scenery in the Aru Valley along the banks of the Lidder River Trek to the stunning Tarsar Marsar twin lakes as the beautiful vistas of Mount Kolahoi take your breath away Enjoy camping near Tarsar Lake during the walk as the clear waters promise to refresh your body mind and soul in preparation for the challenging hiking to Marsar and your adventure goals Location Srinagar Altitude metres Duration days Difficulty level Moderate
