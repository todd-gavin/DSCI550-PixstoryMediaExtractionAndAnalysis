 Cool leftovers as quickly as possible within hours store in the fridge and eat within days Alternatively freeze leftovers for months After this point they are still considered safe to eat but texture and flavor may be compromised Frozen leftovers should be properly defrosted before heating by transferring them to your fridge or using the defrost setting on your microwave Once defrosted refrigerate and eat within days It is safe to reheat partially defrosted leftovers using a saucepan microwave or oven However reheating will take longer if the food is not completely thawed Reheat leftovers until steaming hot throughout they should reach and maintain F C for two minutes Stir food while reheating to ensure even heating especially when using a microwave Do not reheat leftovers more than once Do not refreeze leftovers that have already been defrosted Serve reheated leftovers immediately Sources healthline com
