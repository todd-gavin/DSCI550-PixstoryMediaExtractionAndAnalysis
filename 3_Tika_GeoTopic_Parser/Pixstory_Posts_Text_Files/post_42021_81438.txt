Butter tea also known as gur gur chai in the Ladakh is a drink of the people in the Himalayan regions It is made from tea leaves yak butter water salt As per customs butter tea is drunk in separate sips that is refilled upto the brim of bowl after every Thus the bowl is never drained it is filled in constantly It is more popular in its native provinces of Ladakh adorned with cardamom and almond dashed Kahwa Ladakhis regard gur gur chai as one of the best cures to dehydration and cold Every Ladakhi kitchen has a gur gur chai cabin It is made in pans or cauldrons unlike nun chai
