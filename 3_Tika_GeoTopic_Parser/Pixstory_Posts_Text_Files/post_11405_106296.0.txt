There are some foods that can lead to toxin accumulation if eaten at night . An Ayurveda expert on what should be avoided in dinner .

1 Ayurveda recommends eating light at night considering our digestive fire or agni is the lowest . Undigested food can lead to accumulation of toxins and cause health issues . Ayurveda doctor Dr Rekha Radhamony on foods that one must avoid having for dinner .

2 Wheat is heavy (guru) and takes a long time to digest which can lead to Ama (toxicity) . This is the reason it should be avoided at dinner time .

3 Curd or yoghurt increases kapha and pitta and should be avoided at night . Buttermilk can be a replacement .

4 Refined flour : Just like wheat, refined flour too is extremely difficult to digest during night and must be avoided .

5 Desserts and chocolates : Foods with predominantly sweet taste are guru or heavy and are difficult to digest and also increases mucous .
