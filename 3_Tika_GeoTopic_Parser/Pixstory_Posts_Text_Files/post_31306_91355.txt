The edition of Milano Artweek kicks off today, the week dedicated to modern and contemporary art promoted by the Municipality of Milano Cultura which accompanies miart, the fair that hosts art galleries from all over the world. This week is very important for Milan and for the whole country above all in this delicate phase of recovery of all cultural activities affirms the Councilor for Culture Tommaso Sacchi Enthusiasts and collectors gather in the city to follow the most significant expressions of contemporary art and Milan with its public and private art institutes offers them all a of proposals of the highest quality and interest with leading artists on the international scene An important opportunity also for the Milanese who can cast their gaze on a global reality from Cattelan to mijewski from Sighicelli to Steve McQueen from Mario Air to Alfredo Jaar up to Kiki Smith a a veritable sum of contemporary creativity
