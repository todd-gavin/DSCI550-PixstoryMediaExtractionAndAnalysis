Justin Bieber has been diagnosed with Ramsay Hunt syndrome, sharing in an Instagram video that part of his face is paralyzed. 

On 10 June Justin Bieber posted a video about his health conditions where one side of his face is paralysed. 

That is visible in the video. When he is trying to move the side which is paralyzed but there is no movement at all. 

He posted the video with the caption “IMPORTANT PLEASE WATCH. I love you guys and keep me in your prayers” 

He also added that he will be back soon and taking a rest for now and don't be disappointed with his tour cancellations.
