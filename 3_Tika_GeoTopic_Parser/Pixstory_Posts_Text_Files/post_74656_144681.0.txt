After the super success of ‘Dream Girl’,Bollywood actors Ayushmann Khurrana and Ananya Panday have teamed up for the second instalment of the successful film Dream Girl. The actors were recently spotted filming for the same in Mathura and now the makers have dropped a teaser giving a sneak-peak into the film🙏The actor shared a hilarious video announcing the same✔️In the video, Ayushmann is seen planning with his friends to get back his popular character ‘Pooja’ to Mathura where the second part of the film is set. The film will also star actors like Annu Kapoor, Paresh Rawal, Vijay Raaz, Rajpal Yadav, Asrani, Manoj Joshi, Seema Pahwa, Manjot Singh and Abhishek Banerjee in key roles. He captioned the video, ‘Dream Girl phir se aa rahi hai! Meet Pooja on 29th June 2023 ki Eid par.’ Needless to say, the film is sure to be a rib-tickling ride!😍😍😍😍😍😍This is the first time Ananya and Ayushmann will be seen sharing the screen space in the film. The first instalment of the film starred Nushratt Bharuccha opposite Ayushmann.✅Reports are rife that the characters in the film will be loaded with humour just like the first part. The entire team is taking extra efforts to ensure that the script does justice to the legacy of the first part. The makers have also reportedly recreated Mathura and Agra at the studios. ✅✅Helmed by Raaj Shaandilya, the film revolves around the character of Karam (Ayushmann Khurrana), a small-town boy who is trying to make it big. Eventually he falls in love with Pari (Ananya Panday) after which there is a major turn of events where in lies the ultimate twist of the film.   ✔️✔️✔️✔️✔️
