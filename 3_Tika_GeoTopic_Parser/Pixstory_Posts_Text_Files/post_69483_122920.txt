The path from Gurez to Tulail runs through the Kishanganga River s picturesque scenery You will pass via the villages of Barnai Chakwali Kashpat and Zargai on your way to the Purani Tulail Village In the summer the virgin valley blooms and is great for picture sessions The ever alluring beauty of this valley is enhanced by the play of foggy clouds over the green mountains floral fields and unfettered horizon
