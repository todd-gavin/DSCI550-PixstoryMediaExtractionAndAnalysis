India s ace shuttler and former World Champion PV Sindhu has defeated China s Wang Zhi Yi in the final match of women s singles at the Singapore Open The two time Olympic medallist won the first game in a dominating style before she lost the second one to the Chinese The match went into the decider where Sindhu bounced back beautifully to clinch her maiden Super title of the year Wang Zhi Yi fought well till the end but Sindhu s early advantage in the final game played a crucial role in the latter s victory The Singapore Open also happens to be Sindhu s rd title in the season after winning the Syed Modi International and Swiss Open BWF Super titles This record win is certainly gonna boost her morale to defend her title in the upcoming Commonwealth Games to be held in Birmingham later this month
