Skydiving is now one of the highest rated adventure sports in India. There are quite a few skydiving spots in the country such as Aamby Valley (Maharashtra), Mysore, Pondicherry, Haryana, etc. Generally considered one of the most daring activities, there's always the element of excitement that overweighs your fear, giving you the courage to take that small yet significant jump in the infinite sky. If you're looking for a cathartic experience, then skydiving might be just the thing for you!
