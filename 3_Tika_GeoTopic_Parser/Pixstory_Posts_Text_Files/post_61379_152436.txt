Le Caf de Flore Le Caf de Flore is one of the oldest cafés in Paris It is famous for its famous clientele which once included renowned writers and philosophers It is located at the corner of Boulevard Saint Germain and the rue Saint Beno t Saint Germain des Prs in the 7th arrondissement The café still remains a popular meeting place for celebrities and its status attracts many tourists Famous celebrities who were regular customers were Georges Bataille Robert Desnos L on Paul Fargue Raymond Queneau and Pablo Picasso The café was opened in the years under the Third Republic The name is taken from a sculpture of Flora the goddess of flowers and the season of spring in Roman mythology
