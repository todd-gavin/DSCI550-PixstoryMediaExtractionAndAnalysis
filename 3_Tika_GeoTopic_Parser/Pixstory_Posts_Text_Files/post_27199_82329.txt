PEACE THE NEW WORK BY THE STREET ARTIST LAIKA ON THE CRISIS IN UKRAINE On and February in Rome the Street Artist Laika returned to stick a new work on the walls of the capital on the complicated situation in Ukraine which is a warning to peace as the title itself indicates. two twin posters affixed a few steps from the embassies of Russia and Ukraine respectively in viale Castro Pretorio and in Piazza G Verdi depicting two tanks whose cannons are folded and form the symbol of peace Above the tanks hovers a dove taken from an opera by Picasso Paloma de la Paz who shows her outrage at the tense situation in Ukraine by coating armored vehicles with guano
