The effect of rumours and fake news 
We all know that often half baked truth, distored information and rumours lead to problems. They can make someone 's life living hell . 
Same thing happened with cheng Nuo , a fresher going to college and looking forward to her experience.But after an accident in her dormitory a rumour is spread that she is unlucky and brings bad luck to people around her . Cheng Nuo becomes a victim of cyber bullying and physical bullying as well. 
Frustrated she finds an alternative virtual space , where she can interact with people and make friends. 
Will this giver the confidence to stand up for herself?
