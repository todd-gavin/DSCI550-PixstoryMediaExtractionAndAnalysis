King Karloi of Croisen hates his wife with passion. He absolutely despises her due to no fault of her own . He hates her because of her father Duke of Deluah who apparently had a hand in assassination of king's parents and is eyeing the throne .
 Fearing for his position and cornered by the duke in court , the king takes out his frustration on the queen. Their marriage has grown cold and distant . But the queen is not the person he thinks she was . The king might have a connection with queen from childhood, which is oblivious too. 
The king will have to make amends, but will it be to late by then ?
