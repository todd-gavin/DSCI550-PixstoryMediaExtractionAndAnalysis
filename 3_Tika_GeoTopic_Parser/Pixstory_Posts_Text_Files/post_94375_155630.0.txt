The Australia cricket team in England, May 1956.
Back row (left-right): Mr A James (masseur), William Dowling (manager), Ken Mackay, Jim Burke, Jack Rutherford, Alan Davidson, Peter Burge, Pat Crawford, Richie Benaud, Ron Archer, Colin McDonald, WL Rush (assistant manager), N Gorman (scorer). Seated: Ian Craig, Neil Harvey, Keith Miller, Ian Johnson (captain), Ray Lindwall, Gil Langley. In front: Len Maddocks and Jack Wilson.
