No Olympic Games for Russian and Belarusian athletes This is the final decision of the International Paralympic Committee which arrived today An important decision that echoes the many sanctions from the world of sport that are taking place in these hours against Russia and Belarus after the invasion of Ukraine Here is the official press release Following a specially convened meeting, the Board of Directors of the IPC International Paralympic Committee has decided to refuse the registrations of Russian and Belarusian athletes for the Beijing Winter Paralympic Games This means that the athletes of these respective countries will no longer be able to participate in the Games which will open next March
