Hindi Diwas is celebrated to commemorate the adoption of Hindi in the Devanagari script as one of the official languages of the nation🇮🇳.
Hindi was adopted by the National Constitution on September 14, 1949🍂and it became the official language of the country. 
India's first prime minister, Jawaharlal Nehru, decided to celebrate September 14 as Hindi Diwas🪷.
