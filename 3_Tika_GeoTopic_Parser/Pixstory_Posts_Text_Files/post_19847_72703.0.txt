Ahead of the polls in Uttarakhand, on Wednesday, General Bipin Rawat’s brother, Colonel Vijay Rawat join the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP).

He said that he joined that BJP because his father after retiring from the Army was with the BJP.

“I have now got my opportunity to join the BJP”, he added.
