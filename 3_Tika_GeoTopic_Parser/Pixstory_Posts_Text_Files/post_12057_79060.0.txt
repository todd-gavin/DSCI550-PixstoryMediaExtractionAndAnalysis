The average number of daily active cases were upto 2,04,545 during the last week. And the case positivity during the last week was at 12.98%. This was revealed by ministry of health during a press conference in Delhi 

Currently the active number of cases are 15,33,921 as of 3rd February. 

The third wave reached its peak by 21st January, reporting with a total of 3,47,254 cases per day. 

On 3rd February the cases have come down to 1,72,433 as per the data from Ministry of health and family welfare.

34 states have been witnessing a decrease in the number of cases that are being reported. But there are some states that still poses concerns with the weekly positivity rates which are still at 37.3 % in Mizoram and 47.0% in Kerala.
