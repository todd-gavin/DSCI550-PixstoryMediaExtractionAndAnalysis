The crown prince has lost his mind The sick and weak crown prince of Daqi Feng Jin Yan was taking his last breath So the worried Emperor asks the Chaplin Wu Nian to perform the opening altar practice and use of method of invoking the soul to snatch his son from the halls of hell But unfortunately a wrong soul is brought back and what happens leads to series of comedy of errors A light hearted webtoon with great storyline art well defined characters cute romance and a funny lead couple
