Tandon to lead Chandigarh cricket body for third time in a row
Former Chandigarh BJP president and party’s Himachal co-incharge Sanjay Tandon was unanimously elected as president of the UT Cricket Association (UTCA) on Monday.
Tandon, who was first elected as the UTCA president in 2016 and at that time the Chandigarh body was not affiliated to the BCCI. 
He played a major role in getting BCCI recognition. 
Yuvraj Mahajan was elected as UTCA vice-president, Devender Sharma was elected as general secretary, while Ravinder Singh was elected as joint secretary.
Alok Krishna was elected as treasurer and former Ranji trophy player Daniel Banerjee was elected as a member of UTCA apex council.
