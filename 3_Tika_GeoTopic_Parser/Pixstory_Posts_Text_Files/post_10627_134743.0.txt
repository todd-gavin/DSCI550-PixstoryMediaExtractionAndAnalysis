Kapil Dev to rejoice WC victory with his fellow players in Chandigarh

Chandigarh: Former Test cricketer Kapil Dev will rejoice the 1983 World Cup  victory once again. UT Cricket Association (UTCA) is bringing this special occasion on  Tuesday by organising a special screening of the film '83'  wherein Kapil Dev along with his city based teammates will remember this defining moment  on Tuesday. UTCA will also organize a felicitation ceremony for him on Tuesday at 3 pm at PVR Cinemas where former and current cricket players alongwith UTCA members from Chandigarh will enjoy the film.
