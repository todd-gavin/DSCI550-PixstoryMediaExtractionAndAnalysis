Prime minister Narendra Modi took to his Twitter handle to congratulate Hungarian Prime Minister Viktor Orbán on his fourth consecutive victory as Hungary's prime minister.

PM Modi's tweet :-  "Congratulations, Prime Minister Viktor Orbán, on your victory in the Parliamentary elections in Hungary. Look forward to continue working with you to further strengthen the close and friendly India-Hungary ties".
