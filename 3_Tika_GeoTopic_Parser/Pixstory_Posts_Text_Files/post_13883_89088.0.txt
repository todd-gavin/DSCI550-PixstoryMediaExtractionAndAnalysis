Jeev will be using a hybrid he last put to use on the Japan Golf Tour 20 years ago.

What prompted the move was the windy conditions expected to prevail this week on the Lodhi Course, and after pulling it out of the closet, the first thing Jeev did was to send it to the referees to check if the club met the latest specifications.

"Over the years, a lot of rules have changed and one needed to be sure that it could be brought into play," said Jeev.
