The Baisaran Valley also known as mini Switzerland due of its stunning beauty is a popular tourist destination and is only kilometres from Pahalgam in Kashmir s Anantnag district It is a beautiful green meadow bordered by snow capped mountains and dotted with dense pine forests Those seeking to relax in the company of nature should visit this well known offbeat tourist destination in Jammu Kashmir Additionally it serves as a camping area for hikers heading to Tulian Lake On the way to Baisaran you can stop at some well known tourist destinations like Kanimarg Pahalgam Old Village Kashmir Valley Point Dabyan and Deon Valley Point From here you can also take in expansive views of Pahalgam town and Lidder Valley Image source
