In a first Kashmir is set to have it first multiplex after three decades, The Dhar family from Srinagar and INOX Cinemas which is one of the big players in theatre world worked tirelessly to make this happen. Earlier in 1990’s the cinema’s didn’t have the heating arrangements making it difficult for cinema enthusiasts to watch their fav movie but now according to Mr. Vijay Dhar, owner of the multiplex has assured that the multiplex will have fully functional heating and cooling arrangements throughout the year , enabling watchers to enjoy their favourite movie in a cozy and comfortable manner.
