In the Bandipora district of Jammu and Kashmir, security personnel discovered an improvised explosive device (IED) on Saturday, according to officials.
They claimed that the IED, which weighed about 16 kg, was equipped with two gas cylinders.
The IED was found in the Astango neighbourhood of the north Kashmir district, according to a joint police and army squad.
They stated that a bomb disposal team was called to dispose of the explosive device.
