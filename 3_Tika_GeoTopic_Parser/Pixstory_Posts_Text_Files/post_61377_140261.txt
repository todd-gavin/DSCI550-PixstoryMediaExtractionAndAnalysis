Ratatouille Ratatouille is an ancient dish of French origin originating in Nice The full name of the dish is ratatouille ni oise In simple terms it is a dish of vegetables cooked in tomato sauce The recipe may differ from region to region other but typical ingredients include tomato garlic onion zucchini eggplant bell pepper and leafy herbs Walt Disney&#39;s film Ratatouille which revolved around the dish popularized the meal in American culture and around the world. ratatouille means to stir A unique thing about the dish is that the vegetables are alternately arranged during preparation. It is said to have been created in the 3rd century by poor farmers whose harvest suddenly increased and therefore they used to make stew from these vegetables
