While the world holds its breath looking at the border between Russia and Ukraine, two nations once united under the umbrella of the Soviet Union and today on the brink of a disastrous war, as often happens, the athletes of the Olympic Games are teaching peace and humanity. Freestyle Aerials the gold went to the Chinese Qi but the two other guys on the podium the Ukrainian Abramenko silver and the Russian Burov bronze celebrated laughing and joking with each other finally being portrayed together by photographers and sending everyone a message of peace as simple as it is universal
