The 36th edition of the National Games is underway in Gujarat. Kho Kho commenced on Friday, September 30 with 4 men’s and as many women’s matches.

Karnataka beat Tamil Nadu 44-42
Kerala beat Andhra Pradesh 24-22
Women’s
Maharashtra beat Gujarat 22-16
Karnataka beat Kerala 28-20
Odisha beat Punjab 28-20
Delhi beat Haryana 28-26
