Bollywood rapper Raftaar and his wife Komal Vohra are heading for a divorce. The couple has been married for 6 years now and has reportedly filed for divorce. According to reports, the two have been staying separately for quite some time now.
Both, Raftaar and Komal have unfollowed each other on social media. Moreover, they have deleted each other's pictures.

On the work front, Raftaar judged various reality shows like Hustle, Dance India Dance, and Roadies. He was the gang leader in Roadies season 3, but he had quit midway due to personal commitments. Raftaar then participated in the dance reality show Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa 8.
