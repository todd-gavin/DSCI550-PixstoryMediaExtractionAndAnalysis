India has a big members contingent for Commonwealth Games 2022, Birmingham. India would be featuring in boxing, badminton, cricket and hockey on the opening day. Friday Indian ace boxer Shiva Thapa will play in 63.5 kg weight category in pre quarter final match. Boxers Sumit Kundu (75 kg), Rohit Tomas (67 kg) and Ashish Choudhury (80 kg) will play pre quarter final match respectively. On the other hand Indian women cricket team will take on strong Australia team in group league match. In Hockey, India women team will clash with Ghana. Other events where Indian players will participate in opening day match are Lawn Bowls, Swimming, squash , Table Tennis, Track Cycling and Triathlon etc.
