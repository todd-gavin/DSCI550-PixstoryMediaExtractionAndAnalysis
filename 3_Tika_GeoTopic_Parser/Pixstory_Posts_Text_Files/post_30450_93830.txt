The world of football mourns Freddy Rincon The former Colombian midfielder died following injuries sustained in a car accident in Colombia The van he was traveling in with other people crashed into a bus at an intersection near the Pascual Guerrero stadium in Cali Rincon he had been the protagonist with the Colombian national team in three editions of the Italy, USA and France World Cup and had also played in our Serie A with the Napoli shirt in the season Once he retired he had trained for a short time while in recent years he was a sports commentator for some local media In the past, there was also the stain of an arrest in 2006 on charges of money laundering, drug trafficking and criminal association
