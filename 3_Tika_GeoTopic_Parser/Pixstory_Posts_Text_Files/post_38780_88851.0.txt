Kylian Mbappe and Erling Haaland are one of the two most sought out transfers in the market right now. They are earmarked to emulate the legendary duo of Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi. 


Erling Haaland, son of Alf Haaland, had a humble beginning. The boy from Norway, first started his career at his hometown in Bryne in 2016, before moving to Molde the following year to be coached by ex-Manchester United player/manager Ole Gunnar Solskjaer. He spent two seasons with them, winning numerous trophies before moving to Red Bull Salzburg in January 2019. In December 2019, he signed for Borussia Dortmund for a reported fee of  € 20 Million pounds. Then the manager of Manchester United, Solsjaker tried to prise him away to Old Trafford, but BVB was able to scoop him up along with Jude Bellingham. 


Haaland won the 2020 Golden Boy award. In 2020–21, he was integral in Dortmund's DFB-Pokal win, won the Bundesliga Player of the Season award, and finished as the top scorer of the Champions League.

Haaland’s main position is center forward and he has scored a total of 56 goals in his 3 seasons, having an xG of 44.51.


Kylian Mbappe began his career playing for Monaco in 2015. He won the Ligue 1 title, Player of the year, and Golden boy award in 2017. Seeing this oil-based club Paris Saint- Germain knocked on the doors of Monaco, to take him on an initial loan, which was made permanent for a huge sum of €180 million pounds. With them, Mbappé has won three Ligue 1 titles, three Coupe de France titles, was named Ligue 1 Player of the Year twice, and has finished as Ligue 1 top scorer for three consecutive seasons. He is the second-highest goalscorer in the club's history. 

Mbappe's primary position is as a center forward, but he also drifts to right-wing and left-wing. He has a total of 123 goals in his 8 seasons, having an xG of 108.16, and he is only 23 years of age.
