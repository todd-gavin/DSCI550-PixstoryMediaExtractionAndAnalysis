The Kashmir Alpine Lakes Trek will take you to the high-altitude lakes tucked away in the Himalayas. The walk offers a journey to some of the most magnificent lakes in the enigmatic country, including Srinagar's Dal Lake. 

Vishansar Lake, Krishnasar Lake, Yamsar Lake, Gadsar Lake, Satsar Lake, Nandkol and Gangabal Lake, among many more, are among the lakes that may be reached by trekking in Kashmir. A once-in-a-lifetime opportunity, trekking in Kashmir to these gorgeous lakes can help you create lifelong memories as you ascend the slopes.
