Back on the market with his footwear brand Paul Andrew The former creative director of Salvatore Ferragamo has announced the relaunch of the collection which will be in stores starting next autumn The line, photos of which will be available to view from next March, will blend elements of color with comfort and wearability as the designer revealed to Bof The collection will follow my times and rhythms he added The goals will be in tune with my ambitions and I will be able to decide to scale the business quickly or slowly at my choice The announcement of the relaunch arrives almost a year after Paul Andrew left Salvatore Ferragamo
