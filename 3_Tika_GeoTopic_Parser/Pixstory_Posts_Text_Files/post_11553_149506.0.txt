
We might want to have fruits but is not being able to put it into practice. Maybe we could try making it an habit and stop just saying it. 
So here are some simple steps to do it 
• Pick an easy behavior like to eat fruits can be your goal. 
• Then decide upon a time on when to have it, maybe in the evening when you get back you can munch on it. 
• Repeat the activity of eating for the coming days. 
• Now when you slowly start attaining your goals, reward yourself with something you really like to have.
