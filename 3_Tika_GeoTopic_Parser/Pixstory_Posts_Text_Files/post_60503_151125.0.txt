
October Variety Star Brand Reputation Rankings Announced
1. Yoo Jae Suk topped this month’s list with a brand reputation index of 3,624,461, marking a 44.82 percent increase in his score since September.
2. Kim Jong Kook rose to second place for October with a brand reputation index of 2,280,058, marking a 43.66 percent increase in his score since last month.
3. Kim Shin Young took third place with a brand reputation index of 2,105,630. 
4. Lee Sang Min came in at a close fourth with a total index of 2,092,851.
5.  Lee Seung Gi rounded out the top five for October with a brand reputation index of 1,425,560.
