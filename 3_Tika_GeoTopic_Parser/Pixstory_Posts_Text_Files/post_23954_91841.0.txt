Over 140 people, including children and relatives of VIPs and film actors have been taken into custody after a rave party was busted in the early hours of Sunday at a pub of a five-star hotel in Banjara Hills by the Hyderabad Police.

Banned drugs such as cocaine and weed were found on them, in the raid carried out at the Radisson Blu hotel, by the task force of Hyderabad police. Liquor was also flowing at the party in the Pudding & Mink pub of the hotel, police sources said.
