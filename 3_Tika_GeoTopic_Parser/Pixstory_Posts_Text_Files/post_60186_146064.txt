The annual Army Day Parade which takes place on January may soon be relocated by the Indian Army outside of New Delhi Army Day events should according to authorities take place all around the nation The parade is probably going to take place in the Southern Command region next year In Pune Maharashtra the Southern Command of the Indian Army has its headquarters Every year on the anniversary of India s first appointment as a nation with its own government Army Day is observed On January Lieutenant General KM Cariappa subsequently Field Marshal succeeded the previous British head of state At the Cariappa Parade grounds in Delhi Cantonment the Army Day Parade is held The annual fly past and display put on by the Indian Air Force IAF were also relocated this year from the Hindon Air Base near Delhi to Chandigarh
