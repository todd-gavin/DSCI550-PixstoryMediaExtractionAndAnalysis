The Massasauga Provincial Park is a provincial park in Parry Sound District Ontario Canada stretching from the town of Parry Sound south to the Moon River Massasauga means mouth of the river in Ojibwe The name also refers to the massasauga rattlesnake which is found in the area The park is home to the following notable animals Massasauga rattlesnake Ontario s only venomous snake Prairie warblers Five lined skink Ontario s only lizard Map turtles
