Former Miss World and Bollywood actress Manushi Chhillar who made her debut with Samrat Prithviraj has been signed with John Abraham for Tehran a film inspired by true events The project is proving to be a great learning experience for the actress Talking about her experience on the project so far Manushi said Tehran has been an exciting learning experience for me I love how differently I get to showcase myself as an actor She is living every moment of her journey as a growing actress adding further Since my career in cinema has just begun every day feels like my first day on set There is so much to absorb and take mental notes of She is glad that she got to dabble in very different types of films With a historical Samrat Prithviraj and now this I have got to taste two very different genres More to look forward to she said
