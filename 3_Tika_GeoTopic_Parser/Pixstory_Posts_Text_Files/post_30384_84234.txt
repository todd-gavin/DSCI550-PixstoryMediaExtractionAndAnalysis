FIFA has made a decision regarding the next commitments of the Russian national football team The team will not be able to play at home but only abroad on a neutral ground and without spectators Furthermore it will not have the flag and anthem and similarly to what was decided after the doping scandal at the Sochi Olympics will be presented with a different name from Russia In this case it will be RFU acronym which stands for Football Union of Russia The national team will be involved in the qualifying playoffs for the World Cup in Qatar
