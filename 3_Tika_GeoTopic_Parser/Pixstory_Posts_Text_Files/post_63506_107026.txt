Australia defeated Peru on penalties after a goalless minute encounter to secure qualification for their fifth consecutive World Cup tournament The Socceroos were victorious in the qualification contest much thanks to substitute goalkeeper Andrew Redmayne s dramatic penalty save that denied Peru s fifth penalty kick The year old Sidney FC goalkeeper who started on the bench in the match was brought on during the penalty shootout went on to become the hero to hand Australia a place in the Qatar World Cup scheduled to take place in November
