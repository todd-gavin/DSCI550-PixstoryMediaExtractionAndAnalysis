Big gestures, candle-lit dinners, rose petals and all that is so lovely but romance can also be found in the little things
sometimes. A balance of both is often best for a happy and healthy relationship.

* Being listened to and given undivided attention
* Being spoken to with respect and kindness
* Having your needs met without asking multiple times
* Being accepted for who you are, quirks and all
* Being given non-physical compliments
* Feeling seen and understood
* Being appreciated for the little things
