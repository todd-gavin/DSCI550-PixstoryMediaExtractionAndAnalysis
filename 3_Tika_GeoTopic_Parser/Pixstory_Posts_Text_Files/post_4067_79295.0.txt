‌Cancer is the most feared disease in the world because of the suffering it induces to the people. Certain small difference in our habits could make a big change and might help us prevent it. 

‌Avoid Tobacco - Smoking has been linked to various types of cancer — including cancer of the lung, mouth, throat, larynx, pancreas, bladder, cervix and kidney. So please understand tobacco is the closest friend of cancer and avoid it.

‌Screen your Sun - UV rays can cause skin cancer. Always use a sun screen while going out. Try to cover if you are over exposed to sun.

‌Get Vaccinated : Cancer prevention includes protection from certain viral infections. Main Vaccines includes 

‌ Against Hepatitis B- Hepatitis B can increase the risk of developing liver cancer. 

‌Against Human papillomavirus (HPV). HPV is a sexually transmitted virus that can lead to cervical and other genital cancers as well as squamous cell cancers of the head and neck. 
‌Get regular Medical Care - Always do full body check-ups regularly and never hesitate to consult a doctor.

‌Healthy diet - This is could prevent many lifestyle diseases in your way. It's always about good health.
