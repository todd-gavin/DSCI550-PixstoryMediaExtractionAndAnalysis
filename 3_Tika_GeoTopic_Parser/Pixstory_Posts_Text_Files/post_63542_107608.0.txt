According to data released by the Union Health Ministry on Thursday, the number of new coronavirus infections jumped by over 12,000 in a single day after 111 days, a 38.4 percent increase in daily cases and a 58,215 increase in active cases. In a 24-hour period, a total of 12,213 instances were reported, bringing India's total number of Covid-19 cases to 4,32,57,730 and the death toll to 5,24,803 with 11 fatalities, according to data updated at 8 a.m.
