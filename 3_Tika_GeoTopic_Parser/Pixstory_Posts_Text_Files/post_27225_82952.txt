The beauty of our Italy is so varied and never monotonous Here we are in the middle of the Sellaronda ski tour at the top of the Dolomites And the tour of the four passes Pordoi Sella Gardena and Campolongo around the rocky massif of the Sella which crosses the Ladin valleys of Fassa Trentino Gardena Alto Adige Badia Alto Adige and Arabba Veneto A carousel of medium-level or optionally advanced ski runs, all linked together by modern lifts. it can be accessed from any of the valleys Attention, however, it is particularly important to reach the last pass before 3.00 pm in order to be able to return to the starting point in time In the event of bad weather, snowstorms or strong winds, some lifts could be closed
