The competitors from India kept on their outstanding play at the Birmingham 2022 Commonwealth Games. The team had a mixed-bag performance on Tuesday, winning gold in Lawn Bowls and Table Tennis but having to settle for a silver in Badminton. The athletes continued their amazing performance on Wednesday. In weightlifting, Lovepreet and Gurdeep Singh increased India's medal total. While Tulika Maan won silver in judo, Saurav Ghosal and Tejaswin Shankar each won historic bronze medals in squash and athletics, respectively.
Lawn Bowls and Para Table Tennis were very active. The Indian men's hockey team dominated its Pool B match against Canada as well. The women's hockey team defeated Canada in a vital match.
