Indian youth boxers clinch medals at Golden Glove of Vojvodina tournament in Serbia All boxers claim medals in the women s category New Delhi Indian youth boxers bagged gold medals on the final day to conclude a successful campaign with medals in the th Golden Glove of Vojvodina Youth Boxing Tournament in Serbia on Monday Bhawna Sharma kg Devika Ghorpade kg Kunjarani Devi kg Ravina kg and Kirti kg finished on top of the podium as all participating boxers returned with a medal in the women s category Kunjarani Ravina and Kirti outpunched their opponents by unanimous decisions whereas Bhawna and Devika earned wins in the finals Muskan kg and Pranjal Yadav kg secured silver medals after losing in the finals Kashish kg Neeru kg Aarya kg Priyanka kg and Lashu kg were the other five Indian medallists in the women s section who signed off with bronze medals each Country s men boxers also had an impressive outing as all the five boxers fighting in the final emerged victorious Vishwanath kg Ashish kg and Sahil kg notched up dominating wins over their respective opponents in the final On the other hand Jadumani kg and Bharat Joon kg bagged gold medals after winning by margins Nikhil kg and Deepak kg claimed bronze medals Jadumani was adjudged the Best Fighter of the Tournament for his power packed show while Ravina won the Best Boxer award in the youth women s category The tournament saw participation of top boxing nations such as Kazakhstan Thailand and Ukraine
