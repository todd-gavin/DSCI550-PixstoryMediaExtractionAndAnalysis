On the occasion of Bihu festivities in Assam, Indian Premier League (IPL) team Rajasthan Royals has released a video where Assamese cricketer Riyan Parag is seen teaching Bihu dance steps to Dhanashree, wife of India international spinner Yuzvendra Chahal.
