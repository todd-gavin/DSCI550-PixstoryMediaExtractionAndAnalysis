Brahmastra collected Rs. 37 crores on the opening day, followed by Rs. 42 crores on the second day, and finally, Rs. 46 crores on Sunday, taking the three-day total to Rs. 125 crores. This is the biggest opening weekend total for Ranbir Kapoor, surpassing his previous record holder, Sanju. This is a historic result for a film that has been released without a single holiday.
