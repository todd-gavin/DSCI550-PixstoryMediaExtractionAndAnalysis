A number of protesting women in Panjshir demanded justice for a woman who was allegedly killed by the Taliban on Sunday Protesters Every day men and women are killed in Panjshir but no one hears the voice of the people They wanted to stop the killing of civilians in Panjshir province Women and girls in Panjshir are not safe and cannot move around The Taliban did not fulfill their promise to establish security and the members of this group act arbitrarily in Panjshir and other provinces In the statement of these women it is stated Every day our sisters and brothers are killed but no one listens to their voices The statement adds People do not raise their voices for fear of losing their lives The protesting women called the world s silence against these crimes shameful and declared that supporting the Taliban is a crime against humanity
