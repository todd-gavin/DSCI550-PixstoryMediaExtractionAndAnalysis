Tribute to the great nationalist, legendary freedom fighter, and visionary leader, Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose on his 125th birth anniversary. Here are some images of Netaji's from the Archives. 

picture 1: Netaji's Arrival in Singapore June-1945. 

picture 2: Netaji and Gandhiji at the Indian National Congress annual meeting in Haripura in 1938.
In his famous broadcast message to Mahatma Gandhi on July 6, 1944 Netaji Bose sought the Mahatma's blessing for his holy war of India's freedom. 

picture 3: CABINET OF THE PROVISIONAL GOVERNMENT OF AZAD HIND
On 21 October 1943, #NetajiSubhasChandraBose announced the formation of the Provisional Government of Azad Hind, with himself as the Head of State, Prime Minister and Minister of War. 

picture 4: In 1942, Subhas Chandra Bose meeting Hitler in East Prussia, Germany. 

picture 5: SubhashChandraBose and Capt. Lakshmi Sahgal inspect the Rani of Jhansi Regiment in 1943.
