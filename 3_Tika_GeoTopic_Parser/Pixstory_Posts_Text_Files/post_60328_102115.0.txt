Sunriser's Hyderabad fast bowler Umar Malik selected in 18 men squad for IND vs SA home series starting from June 9. 

Umran picked 22 wickets this season which makes him the highest wicket taker from the Hyderabad side . 

Umran Malik also won the 'Fastest Dilevery of the Match' award in each SRH's 14 match  this season . This 22 years old won total cash Money  of  Rs. 14 lakh . He clocked 157 kmph againt Delhi Capitals which is his fastest delivery.
