Srinagar, Mar 08, KDC: An Army soldier of JAKLI was reported missing from central Kashmir's Budgam district on Monday evening, official sources said.

They said that “One Army man of JAKLI identified as Sameer Ahmad Malla son of Mohammad Yaqoob Mall, a resident of Lokripora Khag area is missing since Monday evening from his native village”

The soldier was on leave and is posted in Jammu.

A police official confirmed the incident to Srinagar based news agency Kashmir Dot Com and said efforts are on to locate his whereabouts. (KDC)
