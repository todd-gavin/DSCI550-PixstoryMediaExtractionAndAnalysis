Hum sabhi jaante hain ki sarkar desh ke sabhi morcho par vifal rahi hai, phir chahe wo berozgari ka mudda ho, economy ka mudda ho ya rupiye ka girna. 

Mehangai, heath, bhukmari, rape jaisi bunyadi muddo par bhi asafal rahi. 

Covid 19 ke doraan hum sab ne dekha sahi dhang se ilaaz na milne par kitne log ki jaan gayi . Hunger index report ke hisab se 2021 me India 116 me se 101 place par raha, wahi 2020 me 94 th place par tha. 

Mehangai aur berojgari apni charam seema par hai. 2012 me crude oil ki keemat 112 rs per barrel thi aur petrol 64 rs ka milta tha, wahi 2022 me crude oil ki keemat 108 rs barrel hai aur petrol 100 ke par mil raha hai. 

Gas cylinder 2014 se phele 400 rs ka milta tha aur ab 1000 rs se jayada chukaane pad rahe hain. 
Sarkar ki neetiya jaise note bandi, GST, farm laws bhi galt saabit ho gayi hai. 

Aur yahi sab muddo ko chupane ki koshish me sarkar hindi-muslim ke muddo ko aage bada rahi hai. Aur yahi sab muddo ko MEDIA hava deti hai. Aur MEDIA hindu-muslim ke rishte me khattas peda karne ka kaam kar rahi hai. 

Taki in bunyadi muddo se logo ka dhyaan bhatkaya jaa sake aur sampradyakta ke naam par vote haasil kiya jaa sake. 

"Sochne wali baat hai ki aap kisse aapna asli mudda chunege hindu-muslim ka bhedbhaav ko ya wo mudde jisse desh aage bad sakega."
