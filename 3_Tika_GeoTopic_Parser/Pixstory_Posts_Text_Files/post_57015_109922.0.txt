After the supreme court decided to overturn Roe v Wade, many companies and brand names chose to help their employees with abortion.

The common affirmation was for helping employees with money who wants to travel out of state to get an abortion.
The Walt Disney Company said it would reimburse employees who want to travel out of the state.
Companies like Meta, American Express, Bank of America and Goldman Sachs said that they would cover all the travelling costs of their employees.

Patagonia posted on LinkedIn that it would provide "training and bail for those who peacefully protest for reproductive justice" and time off to vote.
Other big names like Apple, Starbucks, and Lyft announced for helping employees who want to get an abortion.
