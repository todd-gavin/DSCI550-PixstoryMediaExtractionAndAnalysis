This week two groups of Hindus from Pakistan s Sindh region arrived in Jodhpur Rajasthan alleging discrimination and persecution during the country s response to flash floods The migrants who are from Sindh s Tando Allahyar region and are members of the Bhil community expressed a desire to settle in India and stated that they didn t want to return The two batches according to Chaturaram Bhil who arrived with his wife and eight children each had about members of his village Both groups entered India via the checkpoint at Attari Wagah According to Mr Bhil the second group arrived in India on October while the first group did so on October He claimed that after arriving at Haridwar they continued on to Jodhpur The police sent teams to examine the migrants documentation in the meanwhile
