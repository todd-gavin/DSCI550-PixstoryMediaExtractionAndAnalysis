Do you know that show good doctor starring Starring Freddie Highmore as Dr Shaun Murphy is not an original show It is a reboot of the hit kdrama series of the same name The original version was released by TV network KBS in South Korea running from August to October for episodes The show starred actors Joo Won and Moon Chae Won in lead roles of Park Si On and Cha Yoon Seo The drama depicts the story of a man suffering from autism and how he overcomes his limitations to achieve his dream of becoming a surgeon while dealing with disdain from medical community who does not take him seriously His memory retention and spatial skills help him a lot The show was also remade in Japan and Turkey
