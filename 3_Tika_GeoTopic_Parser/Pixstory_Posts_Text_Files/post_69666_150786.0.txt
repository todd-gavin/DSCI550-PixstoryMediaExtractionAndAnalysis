BTS's Jimin donates 100 million KRW (~70,311.13 USD) to the Gangwon-do Office of Education
According to reports, Jimin made a private donation because he did not want his donation to become known to the public. The Gangwon Office of Education also declined to comment on Jimin's donation in a phone call with Newsen on September 28 and instead stated, "It is difficult to confirm the facts."
