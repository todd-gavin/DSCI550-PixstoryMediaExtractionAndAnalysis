Jiwoon died a tragic death. She walked in on her husband cheating on her with her best friend while she was sick from cancer and was murdered by him . Maybe the fates had pity on her , she was given another chance at life . 
This time she wows to live a happy life at all costs but not without a sweet revenge first . She will destroy her cheating husband and lying best friend . She will have vengeance while maintaining a sweet naive facade so she can destroy them slowly. As they say revenge is a dish best served cold.
