Things to do in Delhi :- 
1. Must try out street food from chandichowk, bengali lane , lajpat nagar etc. 
2. Visit some of the most heritage monuments like sunder nursery , Safdarjung tomb , india gate , lal qila etc. 
3. Try to act like a local to get some goood discounts. 
4. Do some street shopping from local markets like Sarojini, janpath , lajpat, khan market. 
5. Travel or commute from delhi metro to save your money.
