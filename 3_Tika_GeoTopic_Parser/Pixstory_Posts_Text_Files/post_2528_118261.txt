B Ga g dhar i ak July August was an Indian nationalist philosopher social reformer and the first popular leader of the Indian Independence Movement He is known as Father of the Indian unrest Reverently addressed as Lokmanya meaning Beloved of the people or Revered by the world Tilak was a scholar of Indian history Sanskrit Hinduism mathematics law and astronomy He was one of the first and strongest proponents for Swaraj complete independence in Indian consciousness and is often considered the father of Hindu nationalism His famous quote Swaraj is my birthright and I shall have it is well remembered in India even today In he established two newspapers the Marathi Kesari Lion and The Mahratta published in English to arouse political consciousness in the general population After being imprisoned for sedition from he emerged a political leader and helped to found the Home Rule League
