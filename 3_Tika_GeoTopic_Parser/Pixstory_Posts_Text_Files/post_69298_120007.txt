On Thursday a cricket competition for players with disabilities was held in Pulwama Jammu Kashmir with the goal of boosting those players self assurance that they can compete at the national and international levels The tournament s organiser told ANI that if the participants with varied abilities wish to participate in other events he is willing to organise such as well We believed it was our duty to arrange a sporting event for them I m willing to arrange for them to participate in any additional activities they choose Our intention is for kids to not see themselves as inferior to anyone They ought to feel as though they are a part of both the national and international levels he said
