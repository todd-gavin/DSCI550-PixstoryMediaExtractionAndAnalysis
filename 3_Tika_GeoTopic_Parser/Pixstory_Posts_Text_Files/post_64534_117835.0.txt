The Bible is full of precepts and truths that can provide sound guidance for our lives. Here are 10 lessons that we can build our life and faith upon.

1.Love Conquers All

2.Nothing Is Impossible If You Have Faith

3. Bless Others with Your Time, Talents, and Treasure

4. Remember That We Should Live for God, and Not for Human Approval

5. Do Not Blame God for the Troubles in Your Life

6. Caring for Others Is the Best Way to Live out Your Faith

7. Do Not Seek Positions of Power, but Instead, Serve Others

8. Take Time for What Matters When It Matters

9. Avoid Selfishness by Placing Others Ahead of Yourself

10.Forgive Others
