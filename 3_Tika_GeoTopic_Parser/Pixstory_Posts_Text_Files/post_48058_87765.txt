India is an enormous country filled with incredible people and an amazing diversity of traditions cuisines and religions From the vibrant festivals to the warm hospitality and the art of bargaining here are the things you ll love about Indian culture The spiritual side of Indian culture Indians have an ancient philosophical culture and for many their indigenous religions aren t just religions they re a way of life The art of bargaining with a smile Just one trip to the market and you ll be amazed by the art of Indian bargaining Nearly everything is negotiable in India from vegetables to clothes to taxi rides and it s always done with a smile Indian culture is all about family Indians have a fierce sense of love and protection for their family You ll see dozens of family members over generations living under a single roof from children to great grandparents Indians are the best hosts You ll also get a wonderful insight into India s legendary hospitality and generosity It can all be summed up with their motto the Sanskrit phrase Atithi Devo Bhava meaning Guest is equivalent to God The traditional Indian food Speaking of Indian food it s delicious Tasting the real Indian cuisine is one of the best parts of any trip to this wonderful country
