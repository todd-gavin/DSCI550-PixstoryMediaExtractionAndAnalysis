Manipur is heading for the hung assembly as no single party has been able to get a full majority of 31 seats 

BJP has the largest share with 23 seats out of which 12 are won and they are leading the vote share in other 11. INC has won 2 seats and might get 2 more. NPEP has won 2 seats and leading in another 5.

Now, will any party will be getting a full majority or there would be a need of an alliance, we ll find out by today's end.
