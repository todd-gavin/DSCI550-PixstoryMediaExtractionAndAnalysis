On the last day of the CinemaCon event, Disney announced the name of the second part of Avatar (as a show stopper) and also announced the dates of upcoming parts of the movies.

First things first, the second part of the movie will be called ‘Avatar-The Way of Water’ and will hit the theatres worldwide on 16th December 2022.

Disney also announced that after the second installment of the movie, three more parts of  Avatar are in development and will be released on 24th December 2024, 18th December 2026, and 22nd December 2028.

The trailer of the second part will be attached to the upcoming movie ‘Doctor Strange in the multiverse of Madness.’
