To give you a better knowledge of these beautiful animals, let's look at some remarkable dog facts. 

As per doy, for instance, did you know that the African Hunting Dog is the world's best hunter? Between 50 and 70 percent of their hunts are successful for these dogs. They even have the world record according to Guinness. 

Moreover, the Saluki holds the title of being the oldest dog breed, according to Guinness World Records. In reality, this breed has existed since 329 BC. These dogs were kept as royal pets in ancient Egypt. There are theories that this breed's origins go even further, as sculptures of a dog resembling this type have been found in southern Iraq.
