Super simple and really healthy, this protein meal is perfect to start your day. Make it a part of your daily routine and see the difference yourself!

INGREDIENTS:
Chia Seeds (approx 6 tbsp)
Flax Seeds (2 tbsp)
Blueberries (about 3 tbsp)
Plant based Protein powder (2 tbsp)
Dairy free Milk (1 big mug)
Fruits (optional)
Apple
Banana
Papava
Strawberries
Mango

METHOD:
Soak all the ingredients in milk overnight.
Next day mix well.
You can enjoy it without fruits as well.
