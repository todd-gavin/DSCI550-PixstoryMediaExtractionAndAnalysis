Bourges Cathedral Bourges Cathedral built in the French Gothic style is located on a site of religious significance that dates back to at least the 3rd century from the 13th century The cathedral dedicated to Saint Etienne serves as the residence of the Archbishop of Bourges The cathedral is renowned for its enormous size and its interior coherence the sculptural works of art on its doors and the vast collection of 13th century stained glass windows The cathedral was designated a UNESCO World Heritage Site for its iconic Gothic architecture
