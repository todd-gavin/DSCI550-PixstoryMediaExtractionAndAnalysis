They have 26 deciduous, or "baby," teeth as kittens. They have 30 permanent teeth as adults. Over short distances, a housecat may reach speeds of up to 30 mph. This means a cat can sprint faster than Usain Bolt in the 200-meter dash! House cats and tigers share 95.6 percent of their genetic composition.
