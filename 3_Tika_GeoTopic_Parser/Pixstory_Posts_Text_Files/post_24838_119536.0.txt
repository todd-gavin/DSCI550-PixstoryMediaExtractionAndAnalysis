The UEFA Europa Conference League winner, Roma signed Paula Dybala till 2025 from Juventus.  It was a free transfer as Juventus didn't extend Dybala's contract. The Argentina player will be looking to reignite his career under the special one, Jose Mourinho


Dybala joined Juventus in 2015 from Palermo FC. He was an active layer for the Italian giants and Argentina. He has played 293 games for Juventus. He has scored 115 goals in the span of 7 years. 


It will be a treat to see Dybala under Jose Mourinho. He can remove the best out of any player and Dybala will be able to get back on track. Dybala is yet to get his first minutes for AS Roma.
