1. Avoid strong food odors may help prevent nausea.
2. Don't overeat before or during the trip.
3. Try to choose a seat where you will experience the least motion.
4. Avoid excessive alcohol and foods or liquids that "do not agree with you" or make you feel unusually full.
5. Heavy, spicy, or fat-rich foods may worsen motion sickness in some people. Avoid those foods. Fruits and Fruit juices are more preferred while travelling.
6. Do not sit facing backwards from your direction of travel.
7. On a road trip, sit in the front seat of a car.
8. Do not read while traveling if you are prone to motion sickness.
9. Open a vent or source of fresh air if possible.
10. Isolate yourself from others who may be suffering from motion sickness.
