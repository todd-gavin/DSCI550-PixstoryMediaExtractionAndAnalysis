One year after the disappearance of Ambassador Luca Attanasio, victim of an attack in the Congo, the Mama Sofia Foundation is launched, chaired by Zakia Seddiki Attanasio and which includes among the founders the Secretary General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ambassador Ettore Francesco Sequi. The Mama Sofia Foundation law in a press release is inspired by the moral and spiritual legacy of Luca who, with his wife Zakia, gave life to the association of the same name in Congo. The foundation will have the task of promoting values and initiatives of peace, justice and solidarity among peoples through social interest and works of information education and training in the culture of integration of legality and non-violence
