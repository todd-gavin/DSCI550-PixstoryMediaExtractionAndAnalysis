A galaxy of teachers and Professors from Hawai and Loyola Marymount university, USA attended a seminar on the topic, " Varanasi and Ganga" organized by the Sankat Mochan Foundation (SMF) at Tulsi ghat, Varanasi today.  Prof Vishambhar Nath Mishra, a Professor of Electronics engineering in IIT, BHU and chief priest of ancient Sankat Mochan temple is the president of SMF, struggling for clean Ganga campaign. Elaborate discussions were shared by the visiting participants over the topic.
