Gorgeous art panels , great storyline and amazing plot twists. The spark in your eyes checks all the right boxes. 
It follows the story of Erkin a young pharmacist who was orphaned in the war between Northern nations and Mormeratta . Living in Mormeratta is not easy for him , but tries his best . One day he gets called to treat the master of castle that no one has seen .He agrees to order as he believes it will lead him one step closer in finding the wotch that killed his parents. But little does he realise that witch might have been closer to him than he thinks .
