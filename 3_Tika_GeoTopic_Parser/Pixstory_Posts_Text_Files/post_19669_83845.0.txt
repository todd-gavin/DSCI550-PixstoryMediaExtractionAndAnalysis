AIZAWL: The Mizoram unit BJP on Friday submitted a memorandum to state governor Dr. Hari Babu Kambhampati urging him to impose a president rule due to the alleged lawlessness in the state under the Mizo National Front (MNF) government headed by chief minister Zoramthanga.
