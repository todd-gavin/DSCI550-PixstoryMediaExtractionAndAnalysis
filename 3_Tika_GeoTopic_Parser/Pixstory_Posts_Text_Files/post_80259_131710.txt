Memorisation is a skill that is taught to students in various schools It is a technique that is used in many exams Memorisation is the process of recalling information in your head It is a way to make sure that you are able to answer questions in your exam It is not easy to memorise a lot of information but it is possible There are many techniques that can be used to help you memorise For example you can study with the method of repetition With this technique you can study a subject thoroughly and then you can memorise it The next technique is the method of association With this technique you can link two or more subjects together For example if you are studying for a geography exam you can link it to a language you are learning The final technique is the method of imagery This technique is used to help you remember something that is not related to anything you are studying
