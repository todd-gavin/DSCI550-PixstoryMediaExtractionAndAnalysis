Ajay Devgan’s Tanhaji won him the title of the Best Actor at the National Film Awards which was held at New Delhi's Vigyan Bhawan on Friday.National Film Awards is considered the most prestigious award show of the country and this was the 68th installment of it.Apart from Ajay Devgan, actor Surya also got awarded as the best actor for his film Soorarai Pottru.This is Ajay Devgan’s third win as he has won this award earlier too, for the films like Zakhm and The Legend Of Bhagat Singh.Tanhaji, a 2020 Om Raut film is all about the life of Shivaji Maharaj’s most trusted lieutenant, Tanhaji.
