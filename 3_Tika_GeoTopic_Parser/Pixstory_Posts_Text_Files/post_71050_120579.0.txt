Achintya sheuli had a great start in snatch with 137kg and made a perfect lift.
In his second attempt that was 141kg, without any shown efforts and any tensed expression on his face, he cleared his second lift.
And the last attempt for 143kg he took his time and with steady movements lifts up the barbell with ease and won the third gold medal for India.

Achinta Sheuli (born 24 November 2001) is an Indian weightlifter who competes in the 73 kg weight class. He won the silver medal at the 2021 Junior World Weightlifting Championships[2] and is a two-time Commonwealth Championships gold medalist.
