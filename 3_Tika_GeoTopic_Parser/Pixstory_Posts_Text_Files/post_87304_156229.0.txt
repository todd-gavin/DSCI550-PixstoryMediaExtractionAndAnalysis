France and Japan will be up against each other on Matchday 03 of the FIFA Under 17 Women’s World Cup 2022. While France are on the third spot in Group C of the FIFA Under 17 Women’s World Cup points table, Japan sit at the top spot in the points table.
France have played 2 games this season in FIFA U17 Women’s World Cup 2022. They have managed to draw one game and have lost one game so far. In the process, they have scored 2 goals and conceded 03 goals. France have drawn against Canada and lost to Tanzania in their first two games of the Under 17 World Cup.
Japan, on the other hand, have played 2 games this season in FIFA U17 Women’s World Cup 2022 and they have managed to win both the games. In this process, they have scored eight goals and have conceded no goals. Japan have beaten Tanzania and Canada in their opening games of the U17 World Cup.
