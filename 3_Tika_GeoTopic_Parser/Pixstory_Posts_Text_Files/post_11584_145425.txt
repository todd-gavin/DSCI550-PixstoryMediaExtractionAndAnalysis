 Oats is certainly a part of the healthy diet and you want a healthy dessert then here you go the dessert you can munch on anytime Ingredients you will need Ripe bananas Rolled oats Chocolate chips How to make these cookies So first and foremost Preheat the oven to F and place a baking sheet with parchment paper Take a bowl and mash the bananas with a fork and add to it the rolled oats chocolate chips and mix them all really well Take the mixture spoonful and by using your hands shape them to cookies They won t change their shape as they bake so make sure that you give them the right look Keep them on the baking sheet and bake them for to minutes or till they turn golden Now you can either serve them hot or wait till it all becomes cool for a crispier bite Sources Healthy Fitness meals
