The Princesa Sofia Trophy is the most important Olympic sailing trophy outside of the Five Circles races and the victory of the Italian crew made up of Ruggero Tita and Caterina Banti, reigning Olympic champions of the discipline, arrived in the Nacra class. The blue couple dominated the regattas in the waters of Palma de Mallorca securing victory a day in advance already before the Medal Race, however won on Sunday The mixed Giacomo Ferrari and Bianca Caruso also finished on the podium second behind the Spanish Xammar Brugman and the world champions juniors Jana Germania and Giorgia Bertuzzi in the erFX third behind Holland and Brazil Finally, to crown a more than positive edition for Italy in the Iberian trophy also came the silver in the iQFOiL class of Niccol Renna
