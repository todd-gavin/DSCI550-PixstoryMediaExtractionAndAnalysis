The Salernitana cluster continues to be particularly problematic In recent days, the team from Campania suffered a forfeit defeat for not showing up at the Dacia Arena for the match against Udinese last December. Today, however, the number of players positive for Covid rises to nine and therefore not available for the away match against Napoli in January According to the new rules established by the government, this is precisely the threshold for triggering the blockade of the team group and automatically postponing matches Tomorrow for Salernitana there will still be a new round of swabs to verify the definitive impossibility to take part in the match valid for the th day of Serie AA, Cagliari Fiorentina also risk positive for the hosts and Inter Venezia confirmed positive for the lagoon players
