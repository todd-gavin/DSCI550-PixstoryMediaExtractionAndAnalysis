On Wednesday, there will be 4-5 new faces as part of the reorganisation of West Bengal cabinet. Many of them are prolific writers. Mamta Banerjee stated," she doesn't intend to disband the current ministry completely and start a new". There will be a change of personnel. Sadhan Pande and Subrata Mukherjee were lost as ministers. All their tasks must be completed and Partha Chatterjee Bengal's legislative assembly member, is in prison. Banerjee said, "she cannot handle this all alone".
