-RECIPE:

•20 grams OATS
•35 grams VANILLA PROTEIN POWDER
•5 grams  BAKING POWDER
•60 grams CURD
•SOME ALMOND BUTTER
•25 grams BANANA

-PROCESS 

*Mix everything together till it’s super creamy and doughy. 
*Grease the pan and put 1 large spoon of  batter in a pan
*Keep cooking on medium Heath and flip the pancake once you start seeing small bubbles on its surface.

-FOR TOPPINGS:

•APPLE SAUCE
•ALMOND BUTTER
•ALMONDS
•FRIED APPLE (Mix some apple pieces in cinnamon and pop them in the microwave for 30-60 seconds)
