The latest addition to the series true beauty. 
It is adapted from the webtoon of the same name . The story is slightly different from the drama as the webtoon is still ongoing. 
It follows the story of highschool girl called jugyeong who after being bullied in middle school for acne hides her true face behind makeup . As she starts high school she gets famous as pretty girl . Before she knows it she has got 2 boys falling in love with her . But will all be the way it is when people discover her real face ?
