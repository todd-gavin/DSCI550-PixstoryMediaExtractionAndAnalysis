The welcome of the Afghan people from the beginning of the  new year and the celebration of Nowruz is very high these days. These days, the center of Mazar-e-Sharif city in Balkh province is witnessing an increase in the presence of people in markets and shopping centers to welcome the new solar year.

The people of Afghanistan, like all people in the region, consider the beginning of spring as the first day of the new year and perform rituals related to Nowruz.

The markets of this country these days are witnessing the presence of people to buy nuts, dried fruits and all kinds of sweets. March 20, this year, 1401 year will begin and with only one day left until Nowruz, most people have prepared themselves to hold rituals related to it.
