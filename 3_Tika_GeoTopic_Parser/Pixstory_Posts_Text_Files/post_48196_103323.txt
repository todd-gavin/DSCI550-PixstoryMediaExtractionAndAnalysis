Shubhdeep Singh Sidhu June May better known by his stage name Sidhu Moose Wala was an Indian singer rapper actor and politician associated with Punjabi music and Punjabi cinema He started his career as a songwriter for the song License by Ninja and began his singing career on a duet song titled G Wagon Following his debut he collaborated with Brown Boyz for various tracks which were released by Humble Music He gained wide attention with his track So High In fall he released his debut album PBX which peaked at th on Billboard Canadian Albums chart Following the album he started releasing his songs independently Also his single was ranked on UK Singles Chart In Sidhu was named by The Guardian among the new artists Ten of his songs have peaked on UK Asian chart two of which have topped the chart His Bambiha Bole entered in top five on Global YouTube music chart Source Wikipedia
