N. Biren Singh wins the first seat in Manipur. The cumulative numbers of votes documented by Biren till the final round (seventh round) is 24,286 and his rival P Sharatchandra secured 6,486 votes. Postal ballot is yet to be included.

Deputy CM Y Joykumar (NPP) is currently running behind BJP candidate Kh Raghumani. In the fourth round, Raghumani secured 6,524 votes and Joykumar secured 5,841 votes.


Congress leader Okram Ibobi Singh leads in Thoubal by 1225 votes.
