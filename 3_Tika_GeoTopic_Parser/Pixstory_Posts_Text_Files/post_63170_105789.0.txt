India is placed last out of 180 countries in the recently issued Environment Performance Index-2022, a worldwide rating system that analyses environmental health and sustainability. 

With a score of 18.9, India is ranked 180th, behind Pakistan, Bangladesh, Vietnam, and Myanmar, which make up the bottom five countries in terms of environmental health.

According to the EPI, India also fared poorly on rule of law, corruption control, and government performance. 

With a score of 27.6, India was rated 168th in the EPI-2020. Denmark was ranked top in EPI-2020 for environmental health and sustainability.
