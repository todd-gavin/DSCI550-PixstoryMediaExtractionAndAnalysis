Everyone must assume their responsibilities and in continuity with what has already been done in recent days, the FIGC has made timely efforts to allow Ukrainian boys and girls to play in our country, directly assuming all the costs. I am convinced that there can be no real they are also not allowed to practice sports Words from the FIGC president Gabriele Gravina who announced the concrete initiative with which the Federation wants to help young people who fled the war in Ukraine In the face of what is happening we cannot remain inert and so the FIGC will give them the possibility of being registered in the amateur and youth and school sectors until the end of this sports season. The FIGC will also bear the costs envisaged for the registration itself and for insurance coverage
