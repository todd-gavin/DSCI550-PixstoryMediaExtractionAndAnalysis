
 Goa Police won the final of the first All India Football Tournament defeating Mumbai FC and lifted Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam Trophy along with cash prize of Rs. 1,11,111/- organized by S.M.Y Group at Belgaum via tie breaker 4 - 2 margin.
The full time score was 1-1.
Nikilesh @ Dadly Naik of Goa Police was awarded the best goalkeeper of the tournament award. The Goa Police Football team was welcomed by the DGP Goa, DIGP, SP Sports, SP HQ & SP HQ - II at PHQ Panaji, who congratulated Goa Police team along with the coaches.
