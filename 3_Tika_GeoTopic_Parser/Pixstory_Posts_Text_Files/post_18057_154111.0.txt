The stars are stepping out for the big event at Children's Hospital Los Angeles.
Chris Pine and his father, Robert Pine, arrived in tuxedos to the Children's Hospital Los Angeles' 2022 CHLA Gala on Saturday (October 8) at the Barker Hangar in Santa Monica, California.
Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel also attended the event, dressed in matching black and white outfits.
Jimmy Kimmel and wife Molly McNearney, Matt LeBlanc, blink-182's Mark Hoppus and wife Skye Everly, Garcelle Beauvais and her 14-year-old twin sons Jax and Jaid Nilon, as well as Garcelle's RHOBH co-star Crystal Kung Minkoff and her husband, The Lion King co-director Rob Minkoff, were also in attendees.
