After the exciting success in the Monte Carlo Rally that opened the WRC season, Sebastien Loeb will return to the wheel of the Ford Puma in the next Rally Portugal scheduled for May. The confirmation came from the M Sport team which, together with the nine-time world champion, will field another four cars entrusted to Craig Breen Gus Greensmith Adrien Fourmaux and Pierre Louis Loubet Loeb who announced his participation in the DTM championship a few days ago has a token contract with M Sport for this season which allows him to take part in some events of the World Rally Championship. Frenchman explained that the stage in Portugal was chosen because it is an appointment that falls on a weekend in which he has no other motoring commitments, moreover it is a rally that he knows well having participated in it the last time in and which attracts him for the possibility to compete on the dirt road
