When 16 teams arrive in India for the FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup India 2022TM in October, it will be the country's first-ever FIFA women's competition.


Three European countries have joined hosts India, China PR, Japan, New Zealand, USA, Canada, Mexico, Brazil, Chile, and Colombia in the squad line-up for the FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup India 2022TM, following the completion of the UEFA Women's U-17 Championship earlier this month. Cameroon, Tanzania, Ethiopia, Nigeria, Ghana, and Morocco are among the CAF Member Associations who will compete for three more berths.


When the FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup India 2022TM Official Draw takes place in Zurich on June 24, 2022, the teams will be aware of their opponents. Following their appearance in the final of the UEFA Women's U-17 Championship, Germany and Spain had already finalized their trips to India. In the meantime, France qualified for the tournament by defeating the Netherlands in the third-place play-off.


While the Brazilian and Colombian teams expressed their delight about their upcoming trip to India on social media, French coach Cecile Locatelli remarked, "Appearing at a World Cup is excellent for the players." You simply have to look at the World Cup we won in 2012 to see how many of the players are now representing us at the full international level. It's a fantastic experience, and it's a terrific competition for the staff and coaches to understand how to assist young athletes in their development. "


"We're all happy to have qualified," Canadian player Clare Logan said of her enthusiasm about competing in the FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup India 2022TM. We'll keep working hard on and off the field to prepare for the amazing chance of competing in a FIFA World Cup. "


The FIFA U-17 Women's World Cup India 2022TM will be held from 11 to 30 October 2022 in Bhubaneswar, Goa, and Navi Mumbai.
