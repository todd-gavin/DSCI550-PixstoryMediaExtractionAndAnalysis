KGF actor Harish Rai is currently suffering from throat cancer. In an interview ,he told that he had grown his beard long for the movie so that he could cover up the swelling, he also stated that initially he did not have enough money for the surgery so he had postponed it till the movie releases so that the money he will get from the producers would help him to get the surgery done but now in the interview he stated that the condition is much worse now and he is on stage four. He has been battling cancer since three years now.
