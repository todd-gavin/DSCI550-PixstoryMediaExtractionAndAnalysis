AEK Larnaca and Rennes will be up against each other in their opening encounter of the Europa League 2022-23. This will be a Group B match which will be played at the AEK Arena.
Dynamo Kyiv, Rennes, Fenerbahce, and AEK Larnaca are the 4 clubs that have been drawn in Group B of the Europa League campaign this season.This will be the first competitive meeting between both the sides. 
Stade Rennais failed to pick up their first set of consecutive wins this season as they were held to a 1-1 draw by Troyes in Ligue 1 last time out.
