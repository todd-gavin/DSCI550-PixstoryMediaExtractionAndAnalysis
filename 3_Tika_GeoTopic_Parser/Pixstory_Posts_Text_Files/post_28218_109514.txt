There is a good piece of Italy on the night of the eagerly awaited Draft of the NBA the main and most prestigious basketball league in the world The first choice ever was in fact that of the Orlando Magic who decided to take the very young Paolo Banchero with them The name does not hide the clear Italian origins for this big boy who, dressed in a purple Dolce Gabbana suit, therefore obtained a very prestigious call, resulting in the second Italian citizen ever to be chosen first in the draft after Andrea Bargnani Born in Seattle in the Banchero he does not speak Italian but has already sent the his promise of love to the Bel Paese swearing that he wants to play with the national team shirt.
