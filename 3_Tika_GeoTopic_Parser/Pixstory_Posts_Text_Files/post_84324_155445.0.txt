Futsal is an association football based game played on a smaller hard-court. 
Some key differences from football are:
~Futsal is a mainly indoor sport. 
~Futsal is a 5-a-side team sport. 
~There are unlimited substitute allowed in Futsal. 
~There is no offsides in Futsal. 
~Futsal does not allow the ball to go above head height i.e No punts allowed. 
~A standard Futsal match is 20 minutes.
