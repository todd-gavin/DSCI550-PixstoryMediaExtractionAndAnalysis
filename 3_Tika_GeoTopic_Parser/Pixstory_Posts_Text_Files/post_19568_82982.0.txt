AGARTALA: In what can be termed as a confirmation to the reports and speculations of internal squabbling within the ruling BJP in Tripura, a group of 15 saffron party leaders in the state, including state minister Ram Prasad Paul, have demanded resignation of Tripura BJP president Manik Saha.
