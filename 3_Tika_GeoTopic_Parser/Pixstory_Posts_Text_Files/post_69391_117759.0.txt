On Thursday, the meteorological department (MeT) forecasted that Jammu and Kashmir would see a mainly gloomy day with light rain and thundershowers. 

"Cloudy conditions with light rain are predicted in the Kashmir region, while light to moderate rain/thundershowers are expected in the Jammu region. 

"As the route is difficult and the weather is unpredictable, all yatris and yatra managers are encouraged to remain wary of landslides, flash floods, shooting stones, etc "a MeT department representative said.
