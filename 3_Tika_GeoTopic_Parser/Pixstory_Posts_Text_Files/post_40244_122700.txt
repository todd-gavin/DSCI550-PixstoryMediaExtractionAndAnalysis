After the presidential election former President Donald Trump s Save America political action committee was established and it contributed thousands of dollars to former First Lady Melania Trump s stylist according to USA Today It was reported that according to documents submitted to the Federal Elections Commission the fund paid Herv Pierre Braillard who styled Melania for several years and collaborated with her to create a dress for inaugural balls for his services In total the fund disbursed four payouts Braillard received a payment from Save America PAC in April of this year followed by three additional payments of each in May and June The French American fashion designer Braillard has also created looks for Michelle Obama Hillary Clinton and Laura Bush According to the transaction description Braillard was the recipient of the payments The description of the transactions says the payments were made to Braillard for strategy consulting
