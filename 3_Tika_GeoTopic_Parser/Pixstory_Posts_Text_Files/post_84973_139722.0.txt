Malmo and Braga will be up against each other in their opening encounter of the Europa League 2022-23. This will be a Group D match which will be played at the Eleda Stadion.
Braga, Malmo, Union Berlin, and Union Saint-Gilloise are the 4 clubs that have been drawn in Group D of the Europa League campaign this season. This meeting between Malmo and Braga will be a special one as the two sides will be meeting for the first time in a competitive fixture.
Malmo do not come up with very encouraging records against Portuguese clubs in European competitions as they have lost their last 3 games against Portuguese oppositions in European competitions.
