India's 1964 Tokyo Olympics gold medal-winning hockey team skipper Charanjit Singh passed away on Thursday at his native place Una, Himachal Pradesh, after suffering a cardiac arrest that followed prolonged age-related illnesses.

He was 90 and survived by two sons and a daughter.
