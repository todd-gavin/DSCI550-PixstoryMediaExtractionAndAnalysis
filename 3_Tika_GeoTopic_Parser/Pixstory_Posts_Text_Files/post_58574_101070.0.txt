Rudraksh, another dream project of the local MP and PM, Narendra Modi, known as International Cooperation and convention Centre, constructed with a cost of Rs 186 crore, funded by Japan international cooperation Agency , is offering a glimpse of the cultural richness of the ancient city of Kashi. Rudraksh is a holy tree, beads of which in form of garland is put on around neck by devotees as a boon of Lord Shiva. And, such 108 beads, by the architect have been installed here, giving look of Rudraksh to the building.It had capacity of 1200 people to sit in conference hall. The objective of this centre is to provide opportunities for social and cultural interaction. The PM Narendra Modi had inaugurated it on July 16, 2021.
