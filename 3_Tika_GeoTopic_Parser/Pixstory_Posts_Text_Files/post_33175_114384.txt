The Tour de France takes the road to Slovenia and we are only in the sixth stage The favorite of the eve Tadej Pogacar triumphs in the longest stage of this tour km from Binche to Longwy The yellow jersey Wout Van Aert tries to do it by breaking away for good part of the stage but with km to go Pogacar also catches up with the last of the fugitives and goes on to win ahead of the Australian Matthews and the French Gaudu Now in the standings the Slovenian has a margin over the American Powless and du Vingegaard
