Officials announced on Tuesday, June 7 that a case has been filed against two YouTube channels, and they have been blocked for allegedly instigating communal rioting by streaming "misleading" news regarding the implementation of curfew and riots in Bareilly.

Senior superintendent of police Rohit Singh Sajwan claimed the two YouTube channels broadcasted "misleading" news regarding riots and the implementation of a curfew in Bareilly.

On June 6, a case was filed under the Information Technology Act against the two channels – RA Knowledge World and Bareilly Production – at the Kotwali police station, he added. 


Himanshu Nigam, the station house officer of the Kotwali police station, said both stations aired false information and an attempt was made to disrupt the city's communal environment.
