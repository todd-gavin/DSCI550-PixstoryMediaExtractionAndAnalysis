In a significant decision, Jammu and Kashmir's Chief Electoral Officer (CEO) Hirdesh Kumar announced that non-locals, including employees, students, labourers, and anyone from outside who lives ordinarily in Jammu and Kashmir, can register to vote and vote in the J&K elections. He stated that the requirement of domicile is not required for outsiders to register to vote. He also stated that armed forces personnel from other states stationed at peace stations in J&K can have their names added to the voter list.
