News agency ANI , health ministry canteen will only serve healthy food. 
So instead of the regular samosa, you will get 
Non-fried dishes including baked samosas with row banana stuffing, there will cheela or gluten free grains like ragi and millet and  “Ayurveda khichdi “. The ministry canteens' food menu has been cleared by the minister . 
The union health minister Mansukh Mandaviya was quoted by ANI saying the aim of this initiative was to adopt healthy eating habits. 
The minister who is often seen cycling is into fitness and yoga. 
A similar move had earlier this year happened at Ayush Bhavan .
