Led by Information and Broadcasting Minister Anurag Thakur as part of the official Indian delegation Rajasthani singer Mame Khan on Tuesday made history by becoming the first folk artist to open Red Carpet for India at the Cannes Film Festival Khan who has been a playback singer for several Bollywood films such as Luck By Chance No One Killed Jessica and Sonchiriya among others and has also been featured on Coke Studio along with Amit Trivedi at the red carpet could be seen dressed in a traditional Rajasthani ensemble that included a vibrant pink Kurta underneath an ethnic embroidered coat The glamorous red carpet contingent which comprised of Indian film celebrities had put the diversity and uniqueness of Indian cinema on display Read more
