A pictorial trip of Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose's ancestral house at Elgin Road also known as Lala Lajpat Rai Sarani, Kolkata. 

The house, built by Bose's father JN Bose in 1909, is owned and managed by the Netaji Research Bureau and includes a museum, archives and library. 

The bureau is run by Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose's nephew late Sisir Kumar Bose -  who helped Netaji escaping from house arrest in 1941 - son Sugata Bose. 

Bose escaped from the house arrest at Netaji Bhawan in 1941 and fled to Berlin.
