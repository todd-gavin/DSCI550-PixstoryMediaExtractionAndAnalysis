After the exclusion of Russia from the qualifying playoffs for the next soccer world cup in Qatar, Fifa has officially communicated what will happen to the teams that were supposed to play with the Russians Poland, Sweden and the Czech Republic and who had said that rather than taking the field against Russia would have lost the chance to play the World Cup The highest football body in the world has decided that Poland, which should have played in the semi-final of the playoffs against Russia, goes directly to the final match against the winner of Sweden Czech Republic Following the decision taken in February together with the UEFA Executive Committee to suspend all Russian teams from participating in FIFA and UEFA competitions until further notice the FIFA Organizing Committee Bureau has decided that Poland will receive a direct pass to the Path B final in program for March in which the winners of the match between Sweden and the Czech Republic will compete
