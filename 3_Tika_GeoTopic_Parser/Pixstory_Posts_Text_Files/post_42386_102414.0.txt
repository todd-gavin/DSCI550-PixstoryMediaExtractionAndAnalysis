After the shocking incident of shooting, that happened at an elementary school in Uvalde, Texas on Tuesday, which has left at least 14 students and one teacher dead.

After the incident, many celebrities condemn the massacre and offer prayers to the victim and their families.

The tragedy has reignited calls for politicians to urgently enact stricter gun control legislation in the United States of America.

Some of the tweets from celebrities are as follows:- 

Amanda Gorman tweeted, “It takes a monster to kill children. But to watch monsters kill children again and again and do nothing isn’t just insanity, it's humanity.”

Finneas, brother of Billie Eilish tweeted, “Anyone saying “Now isn’t the time to talk about gun control” doesn’t care that kids got fu**ing murdered today.”

Famous basketball player Lebron James also tweeted to show support for victims and his tweet is, “My thoughts and prayers go out to the families of loved one's loss and injured at Robb Elementary School in Uvalde, Texas! Like when is enough man!!! These are kids and we keep putting them in harm's way at school. Like seriously “AT SCHOOL” where it’s supposed to be the safest.”
