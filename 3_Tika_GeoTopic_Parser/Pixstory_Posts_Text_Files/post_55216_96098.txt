No Child in Trash Many wastepickers in India are children Chintan works with wastepickers communities to enable working and vulnerable children in these communities to phase out from this hazardous work enroll and stay in school and learn about their rights safety and life skills and prepare for their lives ahead Chintan Environmental Research and Action Group is an independent non profit non government organization working to mitigate environmental pollution and climate change and centering the voices of the vulnerable and most affected by it Chintan believes in a circular economy and is working actively towards strategies to move towards it
