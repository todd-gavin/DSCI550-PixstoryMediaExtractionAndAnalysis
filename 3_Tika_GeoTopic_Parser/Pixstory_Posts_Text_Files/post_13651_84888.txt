According to an EU plan to be discussed by interior ministers on Thursday asylum seekers from Ukraine can first stay in the EU for two years Afghans living in Ukraine could also benefit from the scheme Everyone who escapes Putin s bombings is welcome in Europe European Commission President Ursula Gertrud von der Leyen said in Brussels on Wednesday The plan drafted by the European Commission also gives asylum seekers the right to work for two years request health care and send their children to school According to the commission if it is impossible to return to Ukraine the protection status could be extended for another year The plan also includes immigrants from third countries such as Afghanistan who have applied for asylum or residency in Ukraine Germany Poland and other countries have promised non bureaucratic support to Ukrainian asylum seekers More than Ukrainians have fled to neighboring countries according to the UN High Commissioner for Refugees
