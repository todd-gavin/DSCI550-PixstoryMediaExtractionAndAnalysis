Thoda is one of the  outstanding form of martial art of Himachal Pradesh.The Game depicts one of the best archery skills dating  back to Mahabharata great conflict where bows and arrows were used.There are two teams in this sport named 'Pashis' and   'Saathis',who are thought to be the descendants of Pandavas and Kauravas.In this game the archers aim lower leg below the knees.This game is a mixture of Martial arts, culture and sport.
