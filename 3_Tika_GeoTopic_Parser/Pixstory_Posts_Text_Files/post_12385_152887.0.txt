
Stress is always the part of life and chronic stress can affect our health and well-being. Stress reduction strategies can help one to an extent to reduce down the physical and emotional effects of stress and it could help a person feel better to handle challenges in a better way. 
One particular stress management technique don't work for everyone. And so by getting to know which right stress management technique is needed as they have to fit into a person's lifestyle and be consistent with their values and also give them a sense of agency over their stress. 
Here are some of them to mention 
• Psychotherapy 
• Exercise 
• Mindfulness-based stress reduction 
• Breathing techniques 
• Guided imagery
