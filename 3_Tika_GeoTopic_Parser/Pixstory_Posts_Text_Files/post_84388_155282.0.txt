FIFA 23 released on 27 September, 2022, making this the final installment of the EA Sports FIFA Installment. 
EA Sports and FIFA announced their partnership of 20 years coming to an end in 2023.
What's new? 
~Women's Soccer players and clubs will be available to play 
~Also a World Cup mode will be added to coincide with the 2022 World Cup. 
~Also, FIFA 23 to support cross-platform play between consoles.
