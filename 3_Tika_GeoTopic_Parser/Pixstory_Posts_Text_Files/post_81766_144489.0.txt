Ajay Devgn and Sidharth Malhotra's Thank God gets banned in kuwait. 
Director Indra Kumar's Thank God has been facing legal issues for its portrayal of Chitragupt played by Ajay Devgn. Now the film will also be ban in kuwait. The movie was not passed by the Censor Board as they have decided to stop the release of the film in the city. As per indian mythology Chitragupt is a Hindu deity who is the bookkeeper who records the good and evil deeds of human being. While the film potrays Ajay Devgn as the modern day Chitragupt that showcase him in a funny avatar. This has not gone well with some of Indian audiences as a UP based lawyer too moved the court by filing a complaint against the makers.
