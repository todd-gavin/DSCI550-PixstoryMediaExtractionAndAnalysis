Pollen allergy is also called seasonal allergic rhinitis, seasonal allergies, or hay fever. Some of the symptoms are:
Nose – Watery nasal discharge, blocked nasal passages, sneezing, nasal itching, mouth breathing, postnasal drip, pain and pressure in the face.
●Eyes – Itchy, red eyes, feeling of grittiness in the eyes, swelling of the clear layer over the white of the eye, swelling of the skin around the eyes.
●Throat and ears – Sore throat, hoarse voice, congestion or popping of the ears, itching of the throat or ears.
●Sleep – Frequent awakening, daytime fatigue, difficulty performing work or school tasks.
