The soap opera between Pippo Inzaghi and Brescia has finally found its way The coach and the president Massimo Cellino have reached an agreement to exit the contract that bound the former striker to the Lombard club in a consensual manner. Inzaghi will thus be able to evaluate the new offers that will arrive with that of Cagliari in the lead at the moment while Brescia will restart from Pep Clotet coach already under contract after the experience of the previous year which ended with the elimination in the Serie B playoffs
