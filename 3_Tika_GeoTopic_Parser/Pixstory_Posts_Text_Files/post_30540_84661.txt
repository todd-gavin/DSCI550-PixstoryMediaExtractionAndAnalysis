Following the invasion of Ukraine by Russia ATP men WTA women and ITF international tennis federation communicated the decision to suspend Russian athletes from all team competitions So no Davis Cup of which they are defending champions and no Billie Jean King Cup Same fate for Belarusian tennis players The combined WTA ATP event scheduled in Moscow next October has also been suspended Russian and Belarusian players will be able to continue to participate in the tournaments of the season including the Grand Slams They will do so without a name and flag of their own nation
