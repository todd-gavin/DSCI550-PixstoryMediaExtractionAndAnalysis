Today ,marks the day for brahmastra release. This movie has planned trilogy and the first part has released today. Ayan Mukherji calls this film a 'dream project' it took him 10 years to turn his dream into reality. In an interview he stated that since childhood he was always intrigued by history, mythology and superheroes. The stories that he grew up watching were not connected to our culture and history. Hence, he calls brahmastra the film that has its roots and culture from our indian mythology.
He also added that the cast of this film has supported him a lot and kept him going through the project. The people will get to see every emotion in the movie and they will get to see something that they haven't seen before.
