Paul Cezanne Paul Cizanne was a French artist and a post-Impressionist painter Born in January the artist came from a wealthy family and therefore had the privilege of attending a good school He bridged the gap between 19th century Impressionism and the 20th century cubism His works are strongly influenced by realism and romanticism He defied the conventions of academic art and worked to revive conventional methods His works were widely criticized and misunderstood public at the time but later gained recognition and eventually paved the way for modern art His notable works include Mont Sainte Victoire Rideau Crunchon and Compotier Les Baigneuses His paintings consciously sought intellectual solutions to problems of representation During his life he produced a prolific number of works in oil paintings and watercolors His works influenced great painters such as Henri Matisse and Pablo Picasso
