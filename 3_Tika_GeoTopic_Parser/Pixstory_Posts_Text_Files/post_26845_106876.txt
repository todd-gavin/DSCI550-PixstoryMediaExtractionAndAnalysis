Furla Progetto Italia wins a new award After receiving the ISO certification for sustainability, the hub of the Bologna group in Tavernelle Val di Pesa built by Geza architecture won the Architizer prize at awards for the mixed use category The competition celebrates the work of the best design studios around the world, highlighting the work of architects and designers The complex was inaugurated in the spring of and covers an area of square meters in the heart of Chianti, housing offices and production plants The Geza studio interpreted and created what I had mind for Furla Progetto Italia or rather a place where man and the landscape dialogue said Giovanna Furlanetto president of the Furla Foundation
