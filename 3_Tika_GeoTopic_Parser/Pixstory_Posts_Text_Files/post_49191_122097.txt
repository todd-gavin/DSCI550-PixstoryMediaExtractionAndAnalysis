The US Posse Comitatus law prohibits using the military to enforce domestic laws Despite being a military organisation the Coast Guard reports to the Department of Homeland Security rather than the Department of Defense Due to this distinction the Coast Guard is able to use their law enforcement authority to apprehend anyone attempting to smuggle goods or people into the US across the water They serve as our maritime law enforcement agency and are also the main organisation responsible for preserving the lives of mariners who are in danger A Coast Guardsman is required to be on board US Navy ships in order to hold and formally arrest any smugglers they may catch
