The Salgaonkar family is finally back The first poster of the second instalment of Drishyam is finally out today Ajay Devgan the lead took to Instagram to share the poster with the caption aur October ko kya hua tha yaad hai na Vijay Salgaonkar is back with his family The recall teaser is all set to be released on th of September and the movie will hit the cinemas this November The recall teaser is supposed to remind and give a flashback of the previous part of the film to the fans The team is also ready to start sequel promotions from October which is also famously known as Drishyam Day
