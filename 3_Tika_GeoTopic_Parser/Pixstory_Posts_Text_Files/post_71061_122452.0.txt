All six wrestlers who competed on Day 8 of the Commonwealth Games 2022 won medals, making it a memorable day for the Indian contingent in Birmingham. Deepak Punia (men's 86kg), Bajraj Punia (men's 65kg), and Sakshi Malik (women's 62kg) won gold, while birthday girl Anshu Malik had to settle for silver in the women's 57kg category. Divya Kakran (women's 68kg) and Mohit Grewal (men's 125kg) finished the day on a high note, each winning a bronze medal in wrestling. Meanwhile, India secured at least a silver medal after the men's fours team reached the Lawn Bowls final.
