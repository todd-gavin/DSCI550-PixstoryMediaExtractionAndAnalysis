Neeraj Chopra throws a national record of 89.03 metres at the Paavo Nurmi Games, breaking his own national record of 87.58 metres set at the Tokyo Olympics.

For the first time since winning a historic Olympic gold medal at the Tokyo Olympics, Neeraj Chopra is back in action. At the Paavo Nurmi Games 2022 in Turku, Finland on June 14, he will compete against world No. 1 Johannes Vetter of Germany and reigning world champion Anderson Peters of Grenada.
