Akshay Kumar whose previous release Samrat Prithviraj failed to perform well at the box office has reportedly stated that he will return to films such as Rowdy Rathore and Housefull The film Samrat Prithviraj which had a budget of Rs crore only grossed Rs crore in its first week of release According to Navbharat Times director Dr Chandraprakash Dwivedi stated in a report that Akshay had assured him that if the film is a box office flop he will return to non controversial films like Rowdy Rathore and Housefull
