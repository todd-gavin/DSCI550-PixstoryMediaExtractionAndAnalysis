A few days after the opening of the pre-sales sold out IL CONCERTOZZO the end of bad luck party that will be held in July at the Arena Fiera di Bergamo to crown the project together for music by ELIO AND THE TESE STORIES TRIO MEDUSA and CESVI Despite the expansion of the capacity of the location after the first sold out in hours the appointment that celebrates the resumption of live music hoped for by the Medusa Trio since spring quickly returned to being sold out July thus promises to be a great party one afternoon and an evening of music and entertainment with the special concert by Elio e le Storie Tese The proceeds from the CONCERTOZZO will be donated to Cesvi to help refugees fleeing Ukraine It was the spring of when the Medusa Trio called Elio e le Storie Tese to dream together a great concert to end bad luck An indeclinable request sounded more like in reality it was blackmail for the little group which, despite having now dedicated itself to golf and modeling, put on the supergroup mask again and relaunched the proposal by adding the will to immediately help the world of music thus born together with the humanitarian organization Cesvi a solidarity auction to collect funds for all those independent and deserving bands hard hit by the pandemic while waiting to be able to celebrate together a big party to be organized in Bergamo, one of the symbolic cities of the Coronavirus emergency and also home to the Cesvi NGO Promoberg, the company that manages the Bergamo Fair, has also been transformed into the toughest months in the field hospital he puts in his own hands and decides to offer his spaces for free to organize the most beautiful party ever
