The Austrian MotoGP weekend begins under the Ducati banner on the renovated Red Bull Ring track which features a new chicane to break up the long straight after the bend intended to avoid the dangerous repetition of the accidents of the last editions of the race. Despite the broken straight, the Ducati still maintains the leadership with the Australian Jack Miller, author of the best time in FP on a damp track that gradually dried out.
