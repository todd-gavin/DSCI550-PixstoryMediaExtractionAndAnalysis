The founder of the Samajwadi Party Mulayam Singh Yadav passed away on Monday October undergoing treatment at a Gurugram hospital he was On Tuesday he will be cremated at his native village Saifai in Uttar Pradesh His cremation will take place at pm where several Chief Ministers and Lok Sabha ministers are expected to attend his funeral Yesterday his mortal remains were brought to his ancestral home and his last rites will be performed with full state honour UP Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath reached Saifai and paid tribute to Yadav yesterday Uttar Pradesh has announced a three day mourning on the death of Mulayam Singh Yadav Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath are expected to attend the funeral ceremony Several other top leaders like Union Defence Minister Rajnath Singh Lok Sabha Speaker OM Birla Telangana Chief Minister K Chandrasekhar Rao and Bihar Chief Minister Nitish Kumar will also be attending the last rites of Mulayam Singh Yadav Congress has deputed Chhattisgarh Chief Minister Bhupesh Baghel and former Madhya Pradesh CM Kamal Nath to represent the party at the funeral Large crows have also gathered to pay respect to the veteran politician
