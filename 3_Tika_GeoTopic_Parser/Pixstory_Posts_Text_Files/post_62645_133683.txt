Turmeric is a natural dye that can help you achieve a more radiant complexion Here are three face masks that will make a good space in your skincare routine Turmeric face mask Combine teaspoon of turmeric with cups of cold water in a pot and place over low heat Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about minutes Remove from heat and let cool Apply the mask to your face leaving it on for minutes before rinsing off with warm water Turmeric skin brightening face mask Mix together teaspoon of turmeric with tablespoons of apple cider vinegar in a bowl Apply the mixture to your face using gentle circular motions and leave it on for minutes Rinse off with warm water and apply moisturizer as usual Turmeric facial scrub Combine tablespoon of ground turmeric with cups of hot water in a pot Place over low heat and stir until the ingredients are fully combined Wet your hands and scrub the mask gently into your skin before rinsing off with warm water
