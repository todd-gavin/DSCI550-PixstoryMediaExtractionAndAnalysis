Massive fire yesterday in Pantelleria The designer Giorgio Armani was among the first to notice the flames, who immediately contacted the competent authorities. The couturier evacuated his home and took the guests to safety on his yacht where they spent the night. Among them also the actor Claudio Santamaria and Marco Tardelli Armani is fine and the villa has not suffered serious damage only a few palm trees on the edge of the house have been touched by the flames As reported by one of his collaborators, the creative and his guests were having an aperitif when they saw the flames Armani then he carried around people safely in his boat
