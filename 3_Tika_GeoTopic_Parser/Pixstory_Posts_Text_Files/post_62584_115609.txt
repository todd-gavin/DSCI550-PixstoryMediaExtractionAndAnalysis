Color correctors even out color irregularities on your skin to counteract discoloration and provide an even surface for your foundation The way this works is that we use a color that is opposite the pigment in our skin on the color wheel to cancel it out using the principles of color theory The color of concealer that will cover your blemish the best is determined by the color wheel On the color wheel colors that are opposite one another cancel out one another Red zits can be covered up with green concealer yellow spots can be reduced with purple concealer and bluish dark circles or scars can be covered up with orange concealer
