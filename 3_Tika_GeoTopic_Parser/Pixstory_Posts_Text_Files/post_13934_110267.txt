The new FIFA World Cup song is unlike any other having been sung by artists from three different continents the American R B singer Trinidad Cardona the Nigerian Davido and the Qatari artist Aisha Hayya Hayya which means Better Together has been sung with the purpose of bringing the world together On the song FIFA Chief Commercial Officer Kay Madati said By bringing together voices from the Americas Africa and the Middle East this song symbolises how music and football can unite the world This song just like football aims to bring the world together It has a very optimistic tone and gives inspiration on how to live life in the now and to stop worrying about the future too much
