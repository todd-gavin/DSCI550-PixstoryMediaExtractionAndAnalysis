The emergence of communist China continues The Chinese people unlike the people of India have a great interest in history and are very conscious of their long past stretching back into antiquity and legend Chinese civilization is not the oldest in the world but it is the oldest surviving one and undoubtedly the most continuous Mythical kings are believed to have invented clothing cooking and the institution of marriage and methods of government The Chinese method of writing using ideograms has changed very little over the last years and greatly unifies the Chinese people irrespective of the dialects they may speak For many centuries the Chinese believed that their country was the centre of human civilization the Central Kingdom or Zhong Guo Outside this Middle Kingdom were believed to live only barbarians lacking education culture or refinement The teaching of a common speech putong hua based on the Mandarin language is official policy as is the active nurturing and fostering of patriotism among the children and youth To be continued
