Emerald Lake is located in Yoho National Park British Columbia Canada Yoho National Park is one of the contiguous National Parks in the heart of Canada s Rocky Mountains along the boundary of British Columbia and Alberta Provinces the other Parks are Kootenay Jasper and Banff Emerald Lake is enclosed by mountains of President Range named after the President and Vice President of the coast to coast Canadian Pacific Railway built in and which runs through Yoho National Park Other peaks are Mount Burgess and Wapta Mountain The Emerald Basin opens to the west and traps rain and snowstorms causing frequent rain in summer and heavy snowfalls in winter Source Wiki
