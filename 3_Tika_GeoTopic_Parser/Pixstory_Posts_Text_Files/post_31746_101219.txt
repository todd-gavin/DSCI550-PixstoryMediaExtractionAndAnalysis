Unicef WHO report makes recommendations for concrete actions to improve access including Improving access within health education and social care systems Ensuring availability, safety, efficacy and affordability of assistive products Expand, diversify and enhance capacity of the workforce Actively engage assistive technology users and their families Raise public awareness and fight stigmatization Invest in data and evidence-based policies Invest in research, innovation and an enabling ecosystem Develop and invest in enabling environments Include technologies assistive in humanitarian responses Provide technical and economic assistance through international cooperation to support national efforts
