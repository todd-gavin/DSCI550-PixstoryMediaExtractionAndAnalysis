Charlie Puth just lit the music industry on fire by shocking his fans by announcing a collaboration with BTS' Jungkook on the new single, "Left and Right," from his upcoming album, Charlie.

In the clip, Puth pretends to call Jungkook on the phone, and gives him directions on what he wants for the song. In the caption Puth wrote that if the song gets a half-million pre-saves on Apple Music, Spotify and Deezer, he'll release the cut next Friday, June 24.

The fans have been waiting excitedly for a collab between BTS and Charlie ever since he slipped up and revealed it in an interview a few days before. 

And now, they can finally rejoice, it's not BTS per se, but it's Jungkook, so it's a win for the armys anyways
