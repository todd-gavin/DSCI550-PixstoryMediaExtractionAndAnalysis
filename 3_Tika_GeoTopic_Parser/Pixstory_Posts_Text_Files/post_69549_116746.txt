In honour of his departed daughter Aabida Ali Ali Muhammad Sheikh a Ganderbal native gave land worth Rs crore to the J K Waqf Board on Saturday When Ali Mohammad son of Mohammad Ahsan Sheikh of Wahidpora in Ganderbal announced his desire to give the land to the Waqf Board in honour of his cherished daughter he was overcome with emotion After granting his request to present the ownership of the land to the Waqf Board in memory of her daughter Dr Darakhshan Andrabi chairwoman of the J K Waqf Board consoled him and praised him for his decision Source Greater Kashmir
