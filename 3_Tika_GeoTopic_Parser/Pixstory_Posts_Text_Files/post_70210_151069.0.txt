SAG ATHLETIC GRASSROOT TALENT IDENTIFICATION PROGRAMME HELD 
The Sports Authority of Goa (SAG) conducted a talent identification cum athletic awareness programme at Peddem Sports Complex, Mapusa of Friday which witnessed the participation of over 150 students from the schools across Bardez Taluka. 
The programme aimed at encouraging the young budding talents from schools to take up Athletics and guide them. The programme emphasised on the basic techniques and skills required in running , jumping and throwing events in athletics. Among those who attend a few would be shortlisted and further invited to be enrolled at the Regular Coaching Classes (RCC) at Peddem.
