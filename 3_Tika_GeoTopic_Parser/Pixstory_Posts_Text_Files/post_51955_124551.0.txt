Father of the nation, Mahatma Gandhi had always fought and created awareness campaign against social injustice and inequality whether in India or foreign. Before being one of the pioneer one in the Indian freedom movement, he lived in South Africa for nearly ten yeras . Within 10 years, Gandhi propagated the philosophy of Satyagraha there and propelled the country towards a no class or ethnic discrimination society. It is well noted that once Bapu was pushed forcibly by ruling white people on the platform from train due to racism there.
