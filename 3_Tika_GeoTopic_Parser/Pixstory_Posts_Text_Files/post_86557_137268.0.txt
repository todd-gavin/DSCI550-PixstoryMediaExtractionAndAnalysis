Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs H E Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman Al Thani has said that the security protocol that has been placed is very strict to ensure the safety and security of everyone who is in Doha during the FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022. 
The Foreign Minister said this recently during an interview with Singapore‘s CNA, he added that: “We are very excited to host the FIFA World Cup in Doha in a few months from now. We are looking forward to bringing the world together. Post-Covid I think this is the first big event that will bring happiness to people." 
On managing the safety and security of fans during the World Cup he said, "From our perspective, we believe that any tournament has positive side and negative side, there will be some fans who misbehave, our strict security protocol, including cooperation with different countries, and the crowd management protocol, will all ensure safety and security of all visitors and will help with minimising negative impact associated with any fan experience. What we are focusing on is how to provide the best fan experience for all fans who are coming. Qatar is a peaceful country and I believe that even fans who are getting over excited will act peacefully as well when they see our environment."
