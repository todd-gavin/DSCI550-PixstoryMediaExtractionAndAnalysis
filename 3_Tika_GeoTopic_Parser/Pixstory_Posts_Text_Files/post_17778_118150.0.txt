The cast of the upcoming movie Dungeons & Dragons: Honor Among Thieves just premiered the first trailer at Comic-Con!

Chris Pine, Rege-Jean Page, and more were in attendance for the film's panel at the event on Thursday (July 21) in Hall H at the San Diego Convention Center in San Diego, Calif.

The guys were joined on-stage by co-stars Michelle Rodriguez, Sophia Lillis, and Hugh Grant, along with directors Jonathan Goldstein and John Francis Daley, as well as producer Jeremy Latcham.

Dungeons & Dragons: Honor Among Thieves will hit theaters on March 3, 2023. Here's a synopsis: "A charming thief and a band of unlikely adventurers undertake an epic heist to retrieve a lost relic, but things go dangerously awry when they run afoul of the wrong people. Dungeons & Dragons: Honor Among Thieves brings the rich world and playful spirit of the legendary roleplaying game to the big screen in a hilarious and action-packed adventure."

The movie also stars Justice Smith, Chloe Coleman, and Daisy Head.
