On Monday, April 18, at 11:30 a.m. PDT, the Hollywood Chamber of Commerce will honour Emmy-winning writer and actor Bob Odenkirk with the 2,720th star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. The star will be dedicated at 1725 Vine Street, in front of the Aster Hotel, in the category of Television.

"Bob Odenkirk has appeared in some of the most popular and history-making television shows in the entertainment industry." Fans will be overjoyed to learn that Bob's star will be displayed alongside that of his "Breaking Bad" co-star and friend Bryan Cranston!" Ana Martinez, Producer of the Hollywood Walk of Fame, stated. 

In 2022, Odenkirk's memoir Comedy Comedy Comedy Drama debuted at number two on the New York Times Bestseller list.
