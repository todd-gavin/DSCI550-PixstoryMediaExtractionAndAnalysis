A few days after the outbreak of the conflict in Ukraine, the decision by FIFA and UEFA arrived to exclude Russian national and club teams from all their competitions. The backlash from the top football bodies came after the numerous controversies that arose at a time when the participation of Russian teams in the various competitions was initially guaranteed, in fact, Poland was the first to announce that it did not want to play in the playoffs of the World Cup against the Russian national team. Therefore, FIFA and UEFA have reached the definitive sentence which replaces the sanctions imposed at first no flag and anthem neutral field and behind closed doors in the home matches the most difficult path was chosen even considering the mere Russian athletes and sportsmen who have little to do with the political decisions taken by Vladimir Putin Football however united with strength and great solidarity with the pain of Ukraine and the top bodies could not hold back any competition will host Russian teams and formations At least until further notice
