Tracking macros or calories is not necessary to lead a healthy lifestyle but it s helpful to have a general sense of what s what so you can ensure each meal you prepare is properly balanced Depending on your goals age and lifestyle your macro ratios will fluctuate but as a general rule of thumb it s recommended to get around of calories from carbs of calories from fats of calories from proteins Everyone has different needs so it s important to listen to your body and do what feels best for you but it s generally a good idea to get some of each macro in every meal
