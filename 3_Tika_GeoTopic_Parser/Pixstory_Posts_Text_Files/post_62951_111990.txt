An epidemic is a disease that spreads quickly through a community population or geographic area These diseases don t necessarily have to be contagious Yellow fever smallpox measles and polio are prime examples of epidemics An endemic is something that is specific to a nation or people Dengue malaria influenza HIV cholera ebola spread in particular regions areas or countries are some examples of endemic An epidemic that has spread across several nations or continents is referred to as a pandemic Some recent pandemics are tuberculosis Russian flu Spanish flu Asian flu cholera Hong Kong flu HIV AIDS and of course COVID Also A larger than expected spike in the number of endemic cases is known as a outbreak It might also be a solitary incident in a new region Unchecked outbreaks have the potential to develop into epidemics and slowly or not so much into pandemics
