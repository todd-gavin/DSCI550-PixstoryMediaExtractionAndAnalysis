Feeling stuck these day ?
Initially we tend to lose weight rapidly but at some time it seems that our weight won’t move ! This can be frustrating and discouraging at times .
However there is a solution to this . First you need to make sure that you are doing the following ; 
1 Not skipping meals .
2 Staying hydrated .
3 Managing stress .
4 Staying active  throughout the day .
5 Not eating mindlessly .
Even after this if you feel that your weight is stagnant and that you’ve hit a plateau . Then you must definitely go for - “ Intermittent fasting ” ! Remember it is not for everyone . And it needs proper guidance . It involves long periods of fasting , typically 16-48 hours ! 
Try these tips and thank me later !
