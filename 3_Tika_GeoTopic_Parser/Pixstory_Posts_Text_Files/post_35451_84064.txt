In recent days, Schalke had taken an important step against Russia and its wicked decisions The German club had in fact decided to break definitively with the historic shirt sponsor Gazprom, the Russian energy company The white writing that headed on the bright blue of the German shirts was therefore completely removed and replaced by a simple writing of the club which has thus renounced its main shirt sponsor An important message that demonstrates how much sport is doing to take the side of peace and non-violence During the weekend, moreover, in the Bundesliga there were many demonstrations of support for Ukraine The words Stop it Putin were also projected several times on the pitches Putin stops the war This is the very strong appeal that the clubs and players want to make to the whole world
