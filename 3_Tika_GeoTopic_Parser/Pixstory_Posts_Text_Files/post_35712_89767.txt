Keylor Navas has decided to give concrete help to the Ukrainian people by making her home available to host various refugees. They are the people brought to Spain by the Gracia voluntary association of Barcelona who went to the border in Krakow to bring food and transport distant Ukrainian citizens from those war zones Upon their return, the Costa Rican goalkeeper bought beds and set everyone up in his cinema room In the meantime, his wife put herself at their disposal to prepare food and clothes A great gesture from the Paris Saint Germain goalkeeper Photo Twitter Psg
