The Crown, the royal drama on Netflix, will premiere its fifth season on November 9.
Imelda Staunton will make her acting debut as the late Queen Elizabeth II in the upcoming season, following Olivia Colman's well-received performance. A show teaser graphic depicts a sinister fissure in a palace wall.
Jonathan Pryce as Prince Philip, Dominic West as Prince Charles, and Elizabeth Debicki as Princess Diana will all appear in the upcoming season. As Camilla Parker Bowles, Olivia Williams joins them, and Jonny Lee Miller will play Prime Minister John Major.
