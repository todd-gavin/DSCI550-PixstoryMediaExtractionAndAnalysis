The seven times Ballon d&#39;Or Leo Messi will be awarded the honorary citizenship of San Severino Marche The news sounds almost strange to the ears of the profane but after careful study and years of research carried out by a scholar of emigration from the Marches to Argentina as well as by a of football it was discovered that the maternal grandmother of the Argentine champion was originally from the municipality in the province of Macerata The words of the Councilor for Sport of the municipality of San Severino Marche leave no room for interpretation His origins derive from our territory A confirmation that will allow Leo to become an honorary citizen
