The shower of medals continues for Italy at the World Swimming Championships in Budapest Dario Verani won the grueling km by beating the Frenchman Axel Reymond in the sprint after hours of competition, the great favorite on the eve The bronze goes to the landlord Galicz while Matteo Furlan closed in seventh place Verani started remaining in the rear so much as to pass in the middle of the race before starting his comeback passing in fifth position at the kilometer and in third position at the beginning of the last meters before the powerful final sprint which gives Italy the sixth medal conquered in the waters of Lake Lupa
