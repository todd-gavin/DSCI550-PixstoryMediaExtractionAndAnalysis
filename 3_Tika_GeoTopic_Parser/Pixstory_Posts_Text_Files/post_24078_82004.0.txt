In a grand gesture, the owner of a retail chain in Kerala has given a new Mercedes Benz SUV worth Rs 45 lakh to an employee as a gift for his loyalty and hard work.

AK Shaji, who owns myG, a retailer of consumer electronics and home appliances said he has given the Mercedes as a gift to his staffer CR Anish as he has been "a pillar of support" for him for over 22 years.
