Murmure Chaat Puffed Rice Chaat is a spicy and tasty chaat recipe which is best enjoyed with hot tea or coffee It is a low fat nutritious and delicious food This chaat is a spicy food that is typically sold via street carts You can make this at home and enjoy with your family friends Here is the recipe for this chaat Ingredients Murmure cup Boiled corn cup Onion chopped big Tomato chopped big Coriander leaves tbsp Lemon juice tbsp Salt tsp Chaat masala tsp Black pepper tsp Instructions Add all the ingredients in a mixing bowl except lemon juice Mix it well At the time of serving add lemon juice Serve this tasty chaat
