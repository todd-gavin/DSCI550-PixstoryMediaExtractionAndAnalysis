The trailer of Ajay Devgan's most awaited film Drishyam 2 has been released today. Seeing the trailer, it seems that the family of the film's lead character Vijay Salgaonkar has once again been in trouble. Which they are trying to fix by betting on the heel. In the film where Tabu and Ishita Dutta were already there, now Akshaye Khanna has also entered, who is busy solving the case. Directed by Abhishek Pathak, this film will be released in theaters on November 18.
