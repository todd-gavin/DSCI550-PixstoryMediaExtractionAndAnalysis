This year, for the first time in the Bundesliga, the Fair Play prize was awarded. The winner was the Italian Vincenzo Grifo, a player from Freiburg. The winger was awarded via the web with the former captain of Bayern Munich and the German national team Philip Lahm who illustrated the prize and the winner The recognition went to the Italian footballer who counts caps for the national team for being the most loyal footballer in all of Germany last season Grifo joined Freiburg from the team where he had already played from to He always played in Germany between Hoffenheim Dynamo Dresden Frankfurt and Borussia Mgladbach
