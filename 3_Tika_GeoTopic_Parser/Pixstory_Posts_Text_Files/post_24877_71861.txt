Kabir Khan s received rave reviews when it was released in December of last year Ranveer Singh as veteran cricketer Kapil Dev Saqib Saleem Ammy Virk Pankaj Tripathi and Jatin Sarna among others acted in the film which portrayed India s historic win at the Cricket World Cup The adulation however did not transfer into box office receipts because India s third wave had already begun to sweep the country The big budget picture barely made a little more than Rs crore in theatres Kabir Khan told Bollywood Hungama that his picture had become a big victim of the third wave and that there was a feeling of disillusionment about it Two states announced night curfews the day the film was released he added
