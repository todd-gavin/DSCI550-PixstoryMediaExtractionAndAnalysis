Mumbai Indians despite putting up a brilliant fight lost to Punjab Kings. Punjab batter first and put up a total of 198. No Mumbai Indians bowlers was left out of the thrashing. Up front both the openers scored half centuries. Dhawan scored 70 of  50 balls and Mayank scored  52 out 32 balls. Then cameos by Jitesh Sharma helped the team reach this mammoth total.
 Mumbai Indians on the other hand were looking really good at stages but lost wickets at crucial stages. Rohit scored 28, Brevis 49, Varma 36 and Suryakumar Yadav 43. It was Odean Smith for Punjab who picked three crucial wickets and even made a run out to take his side to victory. Punjab won the match by 12 runs.
