It s the mango season and yes it definitely demands for some ice cream Mangoes are one of the most favourite fruits that almost everyone in the room loves Here is how the recipe goes Ingredients Ripe mangoes Milk Corn flour Semolina Sugar Vanilla essence Steps to go Mix corn flour and some Semolina and add the sugar into it and boil them As the milk boil the consistency turns into a creamy texture then put a pinch of salt and turn the stove off Let it cool Blend the mangoes and make it into a paste After this add the mango paste with the creamy texture And then add in some vanilla essence and freeze it After an hour take the semi set ice cream and blend it Then set it in the container and freeze it for to hours As you scoop them to the bowl you can top them with your favourite toppings like nuts chocolate chips or sprinkles Enjoy
