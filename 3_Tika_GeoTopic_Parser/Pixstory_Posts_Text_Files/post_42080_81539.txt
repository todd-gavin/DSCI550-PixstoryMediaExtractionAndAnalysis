COUNTING STARS SCARS The fault is not in us said Shakespeare but in our stars But in the curious case of decimating the environment the fault is in us and in our scars From time immemorial humans have detached themselves from the definition of nature It is quite counter intuitive since we are very much a part of nature as a hummingbird or a rhino In the quest of leaving a mark on the human lineage we have left only a tapestry of scars It is time we realise that touting opinions will not move anything Setting examples is the need of the hour and if it is not done the first in the race to get extinct will be us
