Sonu Nigam has been awarded the Padma Shri, India's fourth highest civilian honour. The Union Home Ministry issued a statement on the eve of Republic Day announcing the Padma awards. 

He is a well-known Hindi film singer who has also offered his voice to songs in various other languages. In addition, he has worked as a celebrity judge, music director, and actor. Nigam is regarded as one of India's most popular and influential vocalists.
