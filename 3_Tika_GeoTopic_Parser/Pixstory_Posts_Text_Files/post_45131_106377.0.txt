On Sunday, Gibraltar hosts North Macedonia at the Victoria Stadium in Group C4 of the UEFA Nations League, looking to halt an 18-game losing streak.

Gibraltar's Nations League campaign got off to a disastrous start as they were humiliated 4-0 by Georgia on Tuesday.Prior to that, they had shared the spoils in two successive friendly matches, drawing 0-0 with Grenada and 0-0 with the Faroe Islands, respectively.Gibraltar has now failed to win any of their past 18 matches in all competitions, dating back to a 1-0 success against Liechtenstein in October 2020.

North Macedonia, meanwhile, put up a tenacious performance last time out, coming back from a goal down to rescue a 1-1 draw against Bulgaria.This came after an amazing run in the World Cup qualifiers, in which they defeated European superpower Italy 1-0 to get to the playoffs final, where they were defeated 2-0 by Portugal.North Macedonia enters the weekend unbeaten in four of their past five games in all competitions, with three victories and one tie.

Gibraltar vs North Macedonia: Prediction

When pitted against the lesser lights of football on home soil, Gibraltar typically puts up a spirited fight and has recently recorded some remarkable results. The opponents on Sunday, on the other hand, are a step up in class.North Macedonia can score many goals and coast to victory at the foot of the Rock, thanks to a smattering of players with experience in Europe's best divisions.

Prediction: Gibraltar 0-2 North Macedonia
