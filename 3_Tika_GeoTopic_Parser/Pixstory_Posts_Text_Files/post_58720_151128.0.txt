Prime Minister Narendra Modi is seen getting a demonstration of 5G devices by Jio chairman Akash Ambani at the Indian Mobile Congress in Delhi’s Pragati Maidan where 5G services were launched on Saturday.Ahead of the launch, PM Modi took a first-hand experience of services related to 5G as he went around pavilions at the exhibition and got a demonstration by Reliance Jio Chairman Akash Ambani.
PM Modi was accompanied by Telecom Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw, junior Telecom Minister Devusinh Chauhan, and Reliance Industries Limited Chairman Mukesh Ambani.PM Modi tested “True 5G" devices at the Reliance Jio pavilion and was seen trying the ‘Jio Glass’ with Akash Ambani assisting him, source news 18
