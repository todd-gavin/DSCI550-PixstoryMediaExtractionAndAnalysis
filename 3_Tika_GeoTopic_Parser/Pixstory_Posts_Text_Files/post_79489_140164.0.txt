Story of Queen Elizabeth.
Queen Elizabeth was born in Mayfair, London as the first child of the Duke and Duchess of York. She was educated at home privately and she also started doing undertake public duties during the Second World War. In November 1947, she get married to Philip Mountbatten, a former prince of Greece and Denmark and their marriage lasted 73 years until he died in April 2021. They had 4 children. Charles, Prince of Wales; Anne, Princess Royal;Prince Andrew, Duke of York; and Prince Edward, Earl of Wessex. Her father died in 1952 then 25 year old Elizabeth become queen regnant of seven independent Commonwealth countries: The United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, Pakistan, and Sri Lanka as well as head of Commonwealth. Her main historic visits and meetings include state visits to China in 1986, Russia in 1994, the Republic of Ireland in 2011.
Significant events include Elizabeth's coronation in 1953 and the celebration of her Silver, Golden, Diamond and Platinum jubilees in 1977,2002,2012and 2022, respectively.
