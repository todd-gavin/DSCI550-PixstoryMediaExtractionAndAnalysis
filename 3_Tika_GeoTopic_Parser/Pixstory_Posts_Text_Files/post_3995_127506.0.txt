The video of Coldplay's frontman Chris Martin singing Vande Mataram with music maestro AR Rahman has been going around the social media on this Independence day. The performance on the video was showcased as the ending of the Global Citizen Festival India concert held in 2016. The group had performed for over an hour during the concept and ended with Vande Mataram with the music mastero himself. The pink villa USA shared this video through their Instagram handle and since then the video has been getting thousands of views.
