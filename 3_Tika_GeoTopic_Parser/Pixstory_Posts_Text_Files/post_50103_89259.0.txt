Worlds no.1 Tennis player Ashleigh Barty announced flabbergastly her retirement from tennis at just of 25.
Her incredible career includes three grand slam women's single titles - the French open in 2019,Wimbledon in 2021 and the Australian Open in 2022. 
She posted a video on Instagram with her eyes filled with tears,says "thankful for everything this  sports has given me".

She retires after staying no.1 for 121 weeks and just 2 months after winning Australian Open.
