Brahmastra Part 1: Shiva, the first instalment of a planned Astraverse trilogy, has largely received a thumbs up from both the critics and moviegoers alike. Amitabh Bachchan, Ranbir Kapoor and Alia Bhatt starrer fantasy action-adventure film’s first impression has been encouraging with fans also showing excitement around it.At the end of the film, the producers announced the title for the second film in the Astraverse universe, which will be called ‘Brahmastra Part 2: Dev’. While it is still not revealed who will play 'Dev' in the second part, there have been speculations that either Ranveer Singh or Hrithik Roshan could play the lead role in the film.
