Olympian’s Amit Panghal and Shiva Thapa will lead the men’s Indian boxing team at the Commonwealth Games 2022. On the other hand ace boxer Lovlina Borgohain and World champion Nikhat Zareen will lead the women’s team. This will be Assam’s Shiva Thapa and Lovlina Borgohain’s second Commonwealth Games appearance. 
Arjun awardee Shiva Thapa’s first Commonwealth Games was in Glasgow 2014. While Lovlina Borgohain first Commenwealth Games was in Gold Coast 2018. Shiva Thapa the former Asian champion and world bronze medalist has booked the 63.5 kg event at Bramingham 2022. The selection trials held at Netaji Subhash National Institute of Sports in patiala. 
Tokyo Olympics bronze medalist Lovlina Borgohain will play in women’s 70 kg category. People of North East Region and India are very much optimistic about Indian contingent.
