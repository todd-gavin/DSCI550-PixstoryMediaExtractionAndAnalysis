Mandy Moore made a guest appearance on ‘The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon’ on Tuesday (May 10), where she talked being on ‘The Tonight Show’ right before New York City shut down due to the pandemic, her reaction to filming the final episodes of ‘This Is Us’ and how she and Milo Ventimiglia are taking items from the set.

The actress wore a beautiful Alberta Ferretti Fall 2022 purple silk chiffon tiered maxi dress featuring gorgeous floral details and sheer skirt.

I adore this dress, but I feel that it was hugely underutilized for a TV show where she’s spending most of her time seated.
