Her name is Thenmozhi ,daughter of Mr. Sakthivel Murugan & Aruna couple in Tirunelveli district - Papanasam . she is crawling in the world of poets as a small child. Though she is still an undergraduate, she has a deep crush on tamil poetry since she was studying in 10th standard . As her love for her poetry made her successfully release her first solo tamil poetry book named “அந்த 16 நாட்கள் நானும் என் கவிதையும் ” , after her 8 months of hard work. Which she exclaimed as her proudest moment and said that this book has “my feelings as words and emotional mixture as a whole book” was her moment of speech about her first solo Tamil book .
