This installation in S o Paulo by the Brazilian artist Siron Franco entitled Renascimento or Rebirth pays tribute to the more than people who have died from covid in the country Composed of suspended mannequins the work also honours Brazil s health professionals who are facing a huge surge in cases caused by the omicron variant of SARS CoV with new daily cases on January up from a week earlier The true figures are likely to be much higher as a cyberattack on the health ministry last month has limited the collection of data from state health authorities Despite the figures and unlike previous waves omicron has not caused Brazil s health system to collapse thanks in part to of adults now being fully vaccinated
