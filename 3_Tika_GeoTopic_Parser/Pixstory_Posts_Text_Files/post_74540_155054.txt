Ranveer Singh and Kiara Advani were honoured at the Lokmat Maharashtrian of the year award event that took place in Mumbai Ranveer who won the award for the Kabir Khan film walked onto the stage in his typical style Ranveer rocked an all white look Kiara on the other hand opted for a beautiful white saree and rounded off her look with statement earrings Calling Maharashtra his karmabhoomi janambhoomi mera sab kuch the actor said whatever he is today is thanks to the state Whatever I am I am because of this state and my whole identity is related to it he said He went on to say that he will be always there to entertain people forever The Chief Minister of Maharashtra Eknath Shinde and Deputy Chief Minister Devendra Fadnavis felicitated Ranveer and Kiara Ranveer waved Indian Flag on stage Ranveer took to Instagram and shared a picture from the award ceremony The actor captioned the pic Maharashtrian Of The Year Maharashtra is my home my pride Humbled to receive this prestigious honour from stalwart leaders of India Shri Eknath Shinde ji Shri Devendra Fadnavis ji Shri Pramod Sawant ji Thank You lokmat rishidarda for this honour Kiara also shared a picture from the awards show and wrote Born and Brought up in Maharashtra my heart is filled with immense gratitude to receive the Lokmat Maharashtrian of the Year from our honourable leaders Shri Eknath Shinde ji Shri Devendra Fadnavis Ji and Shri Pramod Sawant Ji Thank you lokmat and rishidarda for this prestigious honour
