Author of midnight's children Salman Rushdie was attacked and stabbed in the neck by man while he was giving a lecture in western New york.

The man apparently confronted Rushdie on stage at Chautaqua Institution and began punching him , as he was being introduced.
The man has been arrested and Rushdie has been admitted to hospital although is condition currently is not known . 

The author has received death threats before from Iran in 1980 's due to his liberal writing . There is even a bounty on him .
