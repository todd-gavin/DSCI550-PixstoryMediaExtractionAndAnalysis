Brian Eno founder of the band Roxy Music and multifaceted artist has chosen the Trentino castles for his latest performance From August until November the light painting of Million Paintings will float on the walls of Castel Beseno in the province of Trento, overlapping in an order decided by chance paintings created by the author Making art like gardening explains the father of the environment music years you plant some seeds and then you start watching what happens to each other, how they come to life and how they interact The project is part of the initiatives proposed by Brian Eno for the Trentino region Sound installations are scheduled at Castello del Buoncosiglio in Trento For the complete program visit trentino info it art culture experiences
