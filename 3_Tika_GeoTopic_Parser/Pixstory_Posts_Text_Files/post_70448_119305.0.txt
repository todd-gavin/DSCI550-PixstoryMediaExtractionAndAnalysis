All Goa 7a side football tournament organised by team UNDERDOGS from Moira. This tournament will go on for 2 days that is 6th and 7th of August. 16 teams on each day . It is to be held on the tambdi mati ground next to shri ravalnath rashtroli temple. The tournament will be starting at 10 am on both the days and the grand finals of the tournament will be played on 7th August 2022 at 5.30 pm .
