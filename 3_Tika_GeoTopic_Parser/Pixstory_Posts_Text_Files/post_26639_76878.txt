Yesterday we talked about men and their clothes today we are dealing with women and their bodies It seems an objectifying and misogynistic speech it is absolutely not my intention to make differences in the understanding of bodies but in this fashion show it is done While as regards the choice of male models they are all thin and canonically beautiful young people the female casting is varied and interesting We see women of different ages and more natural and normal physiques than those who usually ride the catwalks So ethereal and bewildered models like angels are followed by women aware of their bodies and their way of moving in space This is only possible thanks to the work of Pierpaolo Piccioli on the anatomy of a female dress a marvelous celebration of the female strength of her beauty regardless of the form achieved and of her burning intellectual superiority over the male one Pierpaolo with a hundred pins and a few kilometers of blue silk wrote a new chapter of feminism
