In the latest development in the theft at Sonam Kapoor and Anand Ahuja’s Delhi house, the Delhi police on Wednesday arrested a nurse and her husband in connection with the incident. Notably, the FIR in this regard was filed by Sonam’s mother-in-law, Priya Ahuja. The duo is accused of stealing cash and jewellery worth Rs 2.4 crore in February.
Nurse is caregiver of Sonam’s mother-in-law
The theft took place on February 11. The house is situated at Tughlaq Road police station, said the police. The FIR was registered on February 23 after Anand Ahuja’s grandmother Sarla Ahuja found jewellery and cash missing from her cupboards.
