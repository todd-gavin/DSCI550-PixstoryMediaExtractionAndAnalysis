Crvena Zvezda and Monaco will be up against each other in their opening encounter of the Europa League 2022-23. This will be a Group H match which will be played at the Red Star Stadium.
Crvena Zvezda, Monaco, Ferencvaros, and Trabzonspor are the 4 clubs that have been drawn in Group H of the Europa League campaign this season. This will be the first time that these two sides will be meeting in a competitive match. 
Zvezda has lost just one of their last 14 matches at home in European competitions making them a fortress king. Monaco, on the other hand, have won just one of their seven away games in the UEFA Europa League.
