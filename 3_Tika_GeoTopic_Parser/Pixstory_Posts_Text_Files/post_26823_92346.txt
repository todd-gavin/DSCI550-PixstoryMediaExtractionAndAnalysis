HarperCollins Italia today announces that it has acquired the rights to the book on the philosophy of fashion written by Alessandro Michele Creative Director of Gucci and Emanuele Coccia one of the most important contemporary philosophers The book will be published in the spring of by HarperCollins in Italy in the United States Dey Street in the United Kingdom United th Estate and in Germany from Hanser Verlag Starting from the life of Alessandro Michele and the profound innovations that his work has brought to contemporary sensibility, he and Emanuele Coccia who has just given a cycle of lectures on Michele at Harvard University show how two traditionally separate forms of creativity and human knowledge philosophy and fashion are in reality inextricably linked if fashion knows how to detonate centuries of art and knowledge in a daily experience proper to every human being philosophy cannot help but become a fashion form
