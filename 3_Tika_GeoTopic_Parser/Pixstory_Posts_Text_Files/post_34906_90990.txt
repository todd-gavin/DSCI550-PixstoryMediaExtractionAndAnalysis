The dates for the recovery of the championship matches postponed in recent months due to the cases of Covid have been decided. The first to be played will be Udinese Salernitana next April on a Wednesday. Fiorentina, Udinese and Bologna Inter will also recover. Finally, Atalanta Turin will be played on Wednesday May, although this date is still subject to change based on how the team from Bergamo fares in the Europa League.
