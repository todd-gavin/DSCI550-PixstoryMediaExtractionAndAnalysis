A rocket-propelled grenade was fired at the building of the Punjab Police's Intelligence headquarters in Mohali on the outskirts of Chandigarh by suspected Khalistani terrorists on Monday evening.

The grenade hit the third floor of the building causing an explosion, that shattered the windows.

There are no reports of any casualties so far. Meanwhile, the police has cordoned off the area.
