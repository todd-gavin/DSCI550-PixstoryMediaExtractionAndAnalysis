Ayushmann Khurrana is back in theatres with Doctor G, in which he plays a gynaecologist. It also stars Shefali Shah and Rakul Preet Singh as his fellow doctors. 💯 The movie opened to a good start on Friday✅The light-hearted comedy has managed to open on a positive note at the box-office in India amidst the release of other hit movies✔️As per the report the film managed to successfully collect approximately Rs 3.50 crore on its opening day at the box-office in India.💯Trade analyst Taran Adarsh writes, "Despite low pre-release buzz, #DoctorG puts up a decent total on Day 1... National chains fare better, while mass pockets are extremely dull... Has scope for improvement on Day 2 and 3... Needs that push for a respectable weekend total... Fri ₹ 3.87 cr. #India biz."
