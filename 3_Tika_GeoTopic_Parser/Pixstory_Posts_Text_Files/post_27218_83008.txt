Thanks to the great success of the exhibition The World of Banksy The Immersive Experience set up at Milano Centrale station extended until May The first exhibition of the Bristol street artist inside an Italian station proves to be a success and the news of the extension to Milano Centrale reaches just at the dawn of the new opening scheduled for February of The World of Banksy The Immersive Experience at Turin Porta Nuova The itinerary presents over works that tell the world of the mysterious British artist famous for ironically addressing political and social denunciation issues More new works including Ozone Angel Steve Jobs Napoleon and Waiting In Vain have never been exhibited before and add to the works and murals created by young international and local artists
