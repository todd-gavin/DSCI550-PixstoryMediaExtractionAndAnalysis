Valentino Air France and Klm together for sustainability The French airline together with the Dutch one and the maison of the Mayhoola for investments group are in fact joining forces in support of the Saf Sustainable aviation fuel corporate program launched by Air France and Klm which encourages companies to carry out a active role in the future of sustainable travel But what does the Saf program do specifically? After estimating the co emissions associated with their travel, Air France and KLM corporate customers like Valentino can determine the annual contribution they wish to make to this program By investing in this project the partners undertake concrete actions to reduce co emissions and contribute to the green transition of air transport by supporting innovative solutions which aim at the creation of a sustainable fuel industry capable of making air transport increasingly eco-responsible
