Louis tomlinson , English singer and songwriter hosted a festival named Away from home festival. The concert was held on 30th August 2021 at Crystal Palace Bowl. There were 8500 fans present at the show. Louis's dedication for the show was amazing. He performed songs from his album WALLS. And some from 1D the band in which he was before. He offered free tickets to all the fans. Together their love and support were gleaming. Louis also posted a documentary which contained information about his tour and behind the stage stuff. Louis was a big fan of festivals as said by Liam Payne , one of his bandmate member of 1D. He did his best for his and never failed to express his love for them.
