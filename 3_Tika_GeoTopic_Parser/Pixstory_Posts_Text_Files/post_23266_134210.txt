Aleksandar Vucic the president of Serbia has canceled the EuroPride week of LGBTQ celebrations which was supposed to fall from to September This is a violation of minority rights Vucic acknowledged but at the moment the state is under pressure from numerous problems He cited tensions with Kosovo economic woes and concerns over anti-gay protesters The highly anticipated event would bring over 1,000 attendees from abroad to the streets of Belgrade The announcement shocked the organizers who still want to ensure the event has since been hosted by a European city They also said that they are ready to initiate a legal challenge and that a cancellation would violate their rights to freedom of assembly and expression detailed in the Serbian Constitution and protected by European human rights law
