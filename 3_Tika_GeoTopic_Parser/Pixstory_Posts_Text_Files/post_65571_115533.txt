Andreas Pereira will join Fulham on a four year deal from Manchester United for a potential million fee the club has confirmed Pereira played for United for times in the first team and was loaned to Granada Valencia Lazio and Flamengo However Pereira left the club in search of a more consistent schedule of play The club website quoted Pereira who has the option of extending his contract by an additional months as saying I want to help Fulham as much as I can to be where they belong The role of manager Marco Silva in this was crucial He and I chatted frequently and he played a significant role in my arrival
