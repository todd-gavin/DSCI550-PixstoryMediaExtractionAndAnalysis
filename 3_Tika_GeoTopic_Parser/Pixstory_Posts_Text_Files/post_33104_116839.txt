The stage of the Tour de France was won by the Belgian Jasper Philipsen in the sprint on his compatriot Wout Van Aert and on the Danish Mads Pedersen. The protagonist of the stage was the Frenchman Benjamin Thomas in a breakaway and then alone, caught up meters from the finish. The general classification does not change with the Danish Jonas Vingegaard always in the yellow jersey with an advantage over the Slovenian Tadej Pogacar and the British Geraint Thomas
