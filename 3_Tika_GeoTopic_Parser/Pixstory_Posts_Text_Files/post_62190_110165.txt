Meditation is one of the best practices that we can incorporate into our daily lives It s certainly a great practice to dive into for our mental well being But some people face a lot of obstacles during the meditation One of the most common complaints is that while meditating people feel drowsy or disoriented This is quite common and a lot of people are prone to it Drowsiness or disorientation during meditation comes from two facts one is if you haven t had a proper sleep and you re meditating rather than getting your hours in The second and the likely reason is that you re not hydrated Lack of water in our body makes us feel drowsy and when we re meditating we connect with our body and senses on a deeper level and hence we feel drowsy and disoriented So drink a cup of water before you sit down to meditate and you ll be amazed at how you feel
