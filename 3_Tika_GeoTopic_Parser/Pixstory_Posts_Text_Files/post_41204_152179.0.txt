This month's fall weather in many locations indicates that it is time for crop harvesting. The addition of pumpkins to such crops is a great delight! In addition to being tasty and nourishing in and of themselves, pumpkins also frequently contain a substantial number of tasty seeds. It's National Pumpkin Seed Day, a day to honour this wonderful and nutritious little treat! Pumpkin seeds have long been prized for their nutritional benefits, especially by prehistoric native societies like the Aztecs and Mayans. Pumpkin seeds are a highly nutrient-dense plant food that are packed with protein, vitamins, minerals, and other essential nutrients. Now that it's National Pumpkin Seed Day, it's the perfect time to savour and appreciate all things related to this small but potent cuisine!Source: DOY
