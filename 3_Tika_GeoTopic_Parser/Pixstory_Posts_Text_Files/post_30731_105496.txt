Another victim at the Tourist Trophy, the third of this edition of the prestigious but extremely dangerous motorcycling event held on the Isle of Man. His life was lost after a fall during the Supersport race held on Monday by year-old Northern Irish Davy Morgan. The expert centaur fell in the third and last lap of the race immediately suspended The first three in the classification had already crossed the finish line and the order of arrival was therefore considered valid After several hours of suspension to fix the barriers the competition resumed in the afternoon
