After learning history for long, you realize history is just a retelling of events, but in a scientific manner. Whatever events that took place, every story, every rumour is dissected and investigated to reveal the 
truth. It has been done since time immemorial, but who was the person that started it? 

Herodotus was the first ancient historian, belonging to Greek city of Halicarnassus. He wrote the Histories, describing the Greco-Persian Wars in great detail. 
He was called the Father of History by the roman orator Cicero. He was the person to perform systematic investigation of historical events.
