By Abhishek Kumar

India won a total of 61 medals in the Commonwealth Games 2022. This includes 22 Gold, 16 Silver and 23 Bronze medals. Bajrang Punia, Deepak Punia, Ravi Kumar Dahiya and Naveen are among the players who have won gold medals in wrestling.

Since the conclusion of the Commonwealth Games, a picture is viral on social media. In this photo, along with farmer leader Rakesh Tikait, wrestlers Bajrang Punia, Deepak Punia and Ravi Kumar Dahiya are seen sitting on a stage. It is shared with the claim that it was clicked during the farmers’ protest.

Rajasthan Youth Congress tweeted the picture and wrote, “This photo is of the farmers’ movement. And these three wrestlers have now returned victorious.”

This picture is viral on Facebook and Twitter with the same claim.

Fact-check
We noticed that in the viral picture there is a poster at the back that has text written in Hindi that reads ‘Players’ (‘खिलाड़ियों का’) and ‘Ceremony’ (‘समारोह’) written on it. Taking these clues, we performed a Google search using relevant keywords. This took us to a report by ETV Bharat published in September 2021. According to this report, the Sarvjati Kisan Garib Manch had organized a felicitation ceremony in Kharkhoda, Sonipat. In this programme, the winners of the Tokyo Olympics were honoured. Farmer leader Rakesh Tikait was also present at this ceremony.

We also found a video related to this event. Farmer leader Rakesh Tikait including the player present in the viral picture can be seen in this video.
