Mohammad Nader Zahir the third son of Mohammad Zahir Shah the former king of Afghanistan has died in Italy Mustafa Zahir the former head of Afghanistan s Environmental Protection Agency and the son of Prince Nader posted the news of his death on his Facebook page yesterday He wrote that his father would be buried in Rome Italy and that details of the funeral would be announced later in Kabul Nader Naeem nephew of Mohammad Nader Zahir wrote yesterday that Dear and kind uncle of Mohammad Nader Zahir the third supreme son of Mohammad Zahir Shah died today in Rome Italy Former Afghan President Hamid Karzai has expressed regret over the death of Mohammad Nader Zahir and wrote on his Twitter page that he wishes patience for his family
