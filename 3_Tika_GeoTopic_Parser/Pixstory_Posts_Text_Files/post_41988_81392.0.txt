Primarily known as litti chokha in Bihar and Daal baati in Rajasthan, this food item is famous in different regions by different names. People in Rajasthan love to eat it with daal(pulse) , bharta(mashed vegetable) and chatni. In bharta also there is various varieties comprising of bharta made of potato or tomato. In baati too there are different kind of fillings such as sattu or potato and much more. Although the dish is same but still it has different taste in different states.
