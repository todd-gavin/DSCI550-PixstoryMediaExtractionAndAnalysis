Rich legacy of Chandigarh Press Club

The Chandigarh Press Club, a hub of Journalist fraternity celebrated its 42nd Founders’ day today 15th July 2022.The club came into existence in the year July 15, 1980 and from there on this prestigious institution is serving the Journalists fraternity.

Apart from journalist fraternity, the country’s top politicians and personalities had visited the club. Giani Zail Singh the there and then president of India, Dr Manmohan Singh former Prime Minister of India, Chief Ministers of Haryana,Punjab,Himachal Pardesh are the dignitaries who have shown their presence in this prestigious instituition.The  legacy of this dignified institution still persist .
