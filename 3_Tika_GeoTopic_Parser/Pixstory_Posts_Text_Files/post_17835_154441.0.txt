Bella Hadid is celebrating her birthday in style!
The model strutted her way to the Italian restaurant Lucali for her 26th birthday dinner on Sunday night (October 9) in New York City.
For her big night, Bella rocked an oversized leather jacket over a black dress.
Joining Bella at her birthday dinner was her boyfriend Marc Kalman and her big sister Gigi Hadid.
Bella has been very busy these past several weeks, walking in over 20 shows during New York Fashion Week, London Fashion Week, Milan Fashion Week, and Paris Fashion Week.
She had one of the most talked about moments during Paris Fashion Week!
