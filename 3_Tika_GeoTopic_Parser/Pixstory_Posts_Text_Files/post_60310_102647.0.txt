With a 14-run win over the Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) at Eden Gardens in Kolkata on Wednesday, the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) secured their place in Qualifier 2 of the TATA Indian Premier League (IPL) 2022 campaign.

In a high-scoring thriller, RCB won by 14 runs thanks to Rajat Patidar's stunning maiden IPL century and Josh Hazlewood's important three-wicket haul.

Royal Challengers Bangalore will meet Rajasthan Royals in Qualifier 2 at the Narendra Modi Stadium tomorrow, May 27th, in their ninth IPL playoffs . The victor of this match, which might be a semi-final, will play Gujarat Titans in the IPL 2022 final at the same ground.
