Haewon from I am a martial arts villainess but I am the strongest . She is one of those rare few manhwa female leads who practice martial arts. Her story takes place in the world of murim ( martial arts universe) . It is a story about a normal korean girl who wakes up in the body of a Villainess from a martial arts novel .

Her outfits take inspiration from the traditional korean and Chinese clothing . They also reflect her headstrong character and personality. Her dresses change according to her mood and the atmosphere around them . As you can see she wears red when she is about to go for a fight and wears gentle colors for a evening stroll .The artist also highlights that her dresses are well suited for combat and will not hamper movement.
