Chandigarh Former Indian cricket sensation Sachin Tendulkar s son Arjun will feature in the forthcoming th All India J P Atray memorial cricket tournament starting from September The junior Tendulkar will be representing Goa Cricket Association in the tournament The inaugural match of the tournament will feature UTCA Chandigarh and Players XI Bihar According to Capt Sushil Kapoor organizing secretary teams have been drawn into four pools of four teams each Pool A consists of PCC Goa Cricket Association PSPB Players Academy XI Delhi Pool B comprising of HPCA JKCA Minerva Cricket Academy and Delhi Capitals Academy Pool C will have UTCA Chandigarh Players XI Bihar Baroda Cricket Association RBI Mumbai while Pool D has MPCA CAG Delhi SPJ Sports Delhi PCA Colts PCA Panel umpires and scorers will be officiating during the tournament The final is slated for October at I S Bindra Cricket Stadium PCA Mohali Vivek Atray convener of the tournament said that despite the crowded domestic calendar we have been able to eke out a days playing time encompassing matches in six venues with the candid help and support of PCA Management U T Sports Department and Haryana Sports Department We
