Introduction:This nazm 'Makaan' talks of the irony of labourers who help construct a building with their blood and sweat but are not allowed to enter it after the construction work getscompleted.Kaifi Azmi, a leftist in his ideology , has a historical perspective when he refers to human habitat in caves in primitive state . The protagonist in this poetry inspires the workers to unite and fight for their rights with the conviction that a "window would open up in this very wall.
