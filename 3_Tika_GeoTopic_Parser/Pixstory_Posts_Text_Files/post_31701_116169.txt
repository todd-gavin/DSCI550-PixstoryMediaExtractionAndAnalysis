LA MILANESIANA conceived and directed by Elisabetta Sgarbi arrives in July at the Rocca Sforzesca in IMOLA Piazzale Giovanni Dalle Bande Nere with the appointment WARS AND OMISSIONS The evening will feature readings by TAHAR BEN JELLOUN winner of the Goncourt Prize and LORENA CESARINI Followed by the presentation of the drape painted by TAHAR BEN JELLOUN for the city of Feltre and the piano concert and readings by AEHAM AHMAD Opening greetings from the Mayor of Imola MARCO PANIERI and the introduction by ELISABETTA SGARBI The event organized in collaboration with the EMILIA ROMAGNA REGION will be visible also in streaming on Corriere it and on the La Milanesiana channel
