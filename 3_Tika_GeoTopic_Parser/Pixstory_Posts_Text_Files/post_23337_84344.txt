Airbnb will offer free short-term accommodations to refugees fleeing Ukraine These accommodations will be funded by Airbnb Inc and donors to the Airbnb org Refugee Fund as well as the generosity of hosts through Airbnb org The announcement comes via press release from the board of the association of home renters worldwide Airbnb co-founder and CEO Brian Chesky Airbnb co-founder and Airbnb org president Joe Gebbia and Airbnb chief strategy officer and co-founder Nathan Blecharczyk sent letters to leaders across Europe Poland Germany Hungary and Romania to offer support in hosting refugees within their borders Airbnb org will be committed to facilitating short-term accommodation for refugees who are leaving Ukraine and will work closely with governments to best support specific needs of each country by also making long-term housing available
