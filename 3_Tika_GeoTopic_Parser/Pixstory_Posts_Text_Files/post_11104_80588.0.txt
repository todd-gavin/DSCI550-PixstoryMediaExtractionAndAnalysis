What are common things related to menstruation?

SIGNS OF A NORMAL PERIODS :
✔️ 3-7 days of bleeding : Generally, a period
       lasts between three to seven days. A
       menstrual period lasts longer than seven
       days is  considered as long period. 

✔️ 21-35 days of cycle : A menstrual cycle
       differs from woman to woman,but the
       average is to have periods every in 28 days. 

✔️ 2-4 tablespoons of blood loss : A common
       amount of menstrual fluid loss per period is
       in between 5ml - 80ml(approx 2-4 tbsp).
       Loosing  over 80ml of menstrual fluid per
       period is considered as heavy menstrual
       bleeding 

✔️ No frequent spotting in between :  Frequent
       spotting after periods is not considered
       alright. If you are facing this difficulty, the 
       rush to a gynecologist immediately and fix
       your lifestyle as soon as possible. 

✔️ Mild PMS symptoms : Having mild to
       moderate PMS symptoms is absolutely    
       alright before  periods. PMS(premenstrual
       syndrome) is something most women have
       at some point.You can get help if it affects
       daily life. 

Have a healthy, safe yet easy periods!
