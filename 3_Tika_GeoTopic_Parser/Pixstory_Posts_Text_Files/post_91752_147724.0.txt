Actor Alia Bhatt 💗 has responded to a question on how she gets affected by the reviews of her films 🎥🎞️.
 In a new interview, Alia revealed that she doesn't read reviews--either good or bad🤫. The actor further said that she gets feedback from 'enough people' when she meets them🌼. She also added that takes feedback for her every film on what worked and what 'could have done better'💗🤌.
