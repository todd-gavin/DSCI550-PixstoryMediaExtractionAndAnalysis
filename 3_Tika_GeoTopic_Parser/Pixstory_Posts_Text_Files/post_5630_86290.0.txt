Pushkar Singh Dhami is the current chief minister of Uttarakhand, he is the member of the BJP. Dhami is a member of the Uttarakhand Legislative Assembly from the Udham Singh Nagar Khatima constituency . Last year, Dhami was sworn-in as the Chief Minister, after the resignation of Tirath Singh Rawat  who himself succeeded Trivendra Singh Rawat in March 2021.

Dhami is a 2-term and sitting MLA from Khatima, having won this seat in both the 2012 and 2017 assembly elections and is contesting 2022 elections from the same seat. 

The exit polls are showing a close race between BJP and INC , now, whether Dhami will get elected and continue to be the CM we will get to know after today’s count.
