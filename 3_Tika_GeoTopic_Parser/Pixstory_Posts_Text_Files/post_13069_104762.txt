Reza Shahir a reporter for Rah e Farda TV says he has been beaten for the second time by Taliban in Kabul Reza Shahir posted a note on his Facebook page writing that on Friday night June while returning home he was inspected and beaten by a Taliban mobile patrol After checking my phone Taliban forces took my money and necessary documents and beat me the reporter said The Taliban also beat the reporter on Tuesday when he had traveled to western Kabul to cover several bombings at the Abdul Rahim Shahid school Reza Shahir said at the time that the Taliban had arrested him and released him after beating him The reporter says that life under the dreadful shadow of the Taliban is difficult
