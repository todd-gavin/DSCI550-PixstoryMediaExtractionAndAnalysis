The National Games of India 2022 is underway across various cities in Gujarat. The Major Dhyanchand Hockey Ground in Rajkot hosted the first round of matches on Sunday, October 2. Tamil Nadu and Karnataka men’s teams started their campaigns with victories.
Meanwhile, in the women’s competition, Haryana, Punjab, Jharkhand and Odisha registered victories in their first group stage matches. The remaining 4 teams lost their opening matches and will look to come back stronger.
36th National Games: Hockey Results - 2nd October
Men’s
Tamil Nadu beat Jharkhand 3-0
Karnataka beat Uttar Pradesh 4-2
Women’s
Odisha beat Uttar Pradesh 3-2
Haryana beat Gujarat 3-1
Punjab beat Karnataka 6-1
Jharkhand beat Madhya Pradesh 4-2
