 Ingredients pounds baby potatoes tablespoon coconut oil teaspoon black mustard seeds teaspoon turmeric teaspoon red pepper flakes tablespoon grated ginger cup canned coconut milk Salt to taste spring onions Juice of lime or lemon cup frozen green peas thawed Method Of Preparation Place potatoes in a pot large enough to cover by an inch Bring them to a boil Drain the potatoes and set aside Add frozen peas in the pot used for boiling potatoes to warm the peas Heat oil on medium high heat in a small saucepan for the dressing Add mustard seeds when hot and after the crackle and spluttering add turmeric red pepper flakes ginger stir fry for half a minute turn off the heat Next add and stir in the coconut milk Place the potatoes cut in half in a big bowl Add the spring onions green peas and turmeric coconut dressing Add the juice of one lemon Toss everything together and season with salt to taste There goes our vegan potato salad is ready
