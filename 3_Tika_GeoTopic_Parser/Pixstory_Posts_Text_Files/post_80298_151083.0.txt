What competitions will be held in this year's National Games?
Along with athletes from Services, competitors will come from 28 states, 8 union territories, and 36 different sports. This year's games also feature the native sports of kabaddi, yogasana, and mallakhambh.
In particular, the following sports are included in the list of disciplines: swimming, archery, athletics, badminton, basketball, beach sports, boxing, canoeing, cycling, fencing, football, golf, rugby 7s, shooting, netball, roller sports, rowing, soft tennis, softball, squash, table tennis, tennis, triathlon, volleyball, weightlifting, wrestling, wushu, and yogasana.
