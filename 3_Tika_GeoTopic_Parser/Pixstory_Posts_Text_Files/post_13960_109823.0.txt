In India, Nykaa.com is the market leader and go-to website for purchasing online health and cosmetic items.
Former managing director of Kotak Mahindra Capital Company Falguni Nayar started Nykaa.com in 2012. Nykaa has since gained popularity as a brand in the retail and e-commerce sectors.
Nykaa has developed steadily over the years; to get the outcomes it wants, it has always put the interests of its customers first and collaborated closely with its brands.
Nykaa grew by roughly 350 percent from last year to this year, and they expect to increase by another 300 percent this year and moving forward. Currently, they are operating at a run rate of about 600 crores, and they have highly ambitious goals that they will undoubtedly accomplish.
