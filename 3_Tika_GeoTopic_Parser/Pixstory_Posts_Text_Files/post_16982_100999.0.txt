Though Hrithik Roshan and Saba Azad made their relationship Instagram official some time ago fans are always waiting for any kind of update from the couple. And, social media followers were in for a treat on Wednesday when Hrithik Roshan's father, veteran actor-director Rakesh Roshan shared a photo from an intimate family dinner. In it, we spotted Saba Azad posing with the Roshans for a happy family photo. The family had gathered together to celebrate Hrithik's cousin Eshaan Roshan's birthday. Eshaan Roshan is the son of music composer Rajesh Roshan, Rakesh Roshan's brother.
