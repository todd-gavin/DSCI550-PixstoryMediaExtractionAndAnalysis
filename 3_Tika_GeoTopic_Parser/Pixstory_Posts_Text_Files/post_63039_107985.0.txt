India defeated South Africa by 82 runs in the fourth T201 in Rajkot to level the five-match series 2-2. The match witnessed Dinesh Karthik (55 runs) break MS Dhoni's record (52*) for the highest T201 score by a Team India batter at number six or lower. Meanwhile, India fast bowler Avesh Khan recorded bowling figures of 4-0-18-4.
