In the postponement of the Serie A day, Lazio wins at the Olimpico against Venice thanks to a penalty by Ciro Immobile who surpasses Piola and becomes the best striker in the history of Lazio with goals in the league Thanks to the three points, the Capitoline team rises to fifth place in the standings with a margin of one point over Atalanta with one game less and over Roma and points away from the Champions League Fiorentina are also eighth in points for the place in the Europa League but with one game less
