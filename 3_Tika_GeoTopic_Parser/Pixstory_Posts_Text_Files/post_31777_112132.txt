In public work, the practice of voluntary mobility between different administrations is in force, which presupposes that the administration of destination issues a tender for mobility from other administrations that an employee applies and that the administration to which he belongs gives consent to the transfer. An attempt is made to make it effective with much resistance the possibility of mandatory mobility, i.e. of a geographical transfer by unilateral determination of an administration that has vacancies to fill without the need for the consent of the administration of origin The public employee can be transferred within the same administration or by agreement between the administrations involved in other offices located in the territory of the same municipality or at a distance not exceeding km from the office to which the employee is assigned. In these cases, the administration is not required to objectively justify the transfer. In both forms of mobility, national collective agreements can integrate procedures and legal criteria
