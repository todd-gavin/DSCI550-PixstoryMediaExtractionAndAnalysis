The Scorpio N from Mahindra has been released, with prices beginning at Rs 11.99 lakh (ex-showroom). On July 30, bookings for the model will start. To learn about the new Scorpio N's price range by version 
The new Mahindra Scorpio N's 2.0-liter mStallion turbo-petrol engine cranks out 200 horsepower and 370 Newton metres of torque (380Nm for AT). Additionally, a 2.2-liter mHawk diesel engine is offered with 130 horsepower and 300 Nm of torque and 172 bhp and 370 Nm of torque (400Nm for AT). Both manual and automatic six-speed transmissions are available. Another option is a 4WD system known as 4Xplor.
