Kareena Kapoor gets together with Karisma Kapoor for 'Maharashtrian meal day.'
Kareena Kapoor was joined by sister actor Karsima Kapoor and celebrity nutritionist Rujuta Diwekar as they ate a traditional Maharashtrian meal. Karisma took her Instagram to share the photos and video of their lunch on Saturday. In one of the photos, Kareena Kapoor and Karisma Kapoor posed with Rujuta for a sun kissed picture of the trio. She shared a photo with caption "Maharashtrian Meal Day. "
