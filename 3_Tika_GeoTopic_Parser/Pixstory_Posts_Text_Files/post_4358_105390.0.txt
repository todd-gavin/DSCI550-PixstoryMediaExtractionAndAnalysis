Ingredients

-50g oats
-150ml milk
-100ml instant coffee
-2-3dates 
-half mashed banana 
-honey 
-walnuts 
-Cocoa powder
-chocochips 

Process:

Take 50g oats heated in 150ml milk. 

Add 100ml instant coffee with a couple of chopped dates, half a small mashed banana and a drizzle of honey to sweeten. 

Top it with walnuts, banana coins, a sprinkle of cacao powder and chocolate chips.
