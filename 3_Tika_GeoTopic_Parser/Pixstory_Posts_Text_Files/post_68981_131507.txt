Sonam Zomba daughter of Mr Dondup of Namazing Village of Jang Subdivision Tawang who won gold at the All India Mixed Martial Arts Federation AIMMAF competition which took place in Delhi from June to at the Pragati Maidan in New Delhi Sonam Zomba was felicitated by MMT Secy General Namgey Tsering along With VP Admin MMT Pema Chowang Kee She is the first athlete from North East India to win a medal at the AIMMAF tournament Training at BIDANG MMA gym under Bhabajeet Choudhary she has been selected to represent India at the Asian Championship taking place later this year
