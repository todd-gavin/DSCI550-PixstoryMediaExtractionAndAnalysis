Richa Chadha and Ali Fazal will get
married in September 2022. The wedding festivities will take place in Mumbai and Delhi with close friends and family in attendance. There will be a sangeet and mehendi ceremony leading up to the wedding. This wedding was going to happen in 2021 but was postponed because of the pandemic. The date later was speculated to be March 2022, but now sources confirm that the wedding will happen in September this year.😍😍🥰
