The messages in favor of peace and in support of Ukraine present at the San Siro stadium were at the basis of the decision of the Russian TV stations which hold the rights not to broadcast the Serie A match at home. Something similar happened on Saturday for Borussia Dortmund Leipzig of Bundesliga whose broadcast in Russia was interrupted after minutes due to the appearance on the billboards on the sidelines of the words Stop war and StandWithUkraine On Sunday, however, the broadcast was blocked during the execution of Imagine before the match between Juventus and Inter then the images were resumed without sound Lorenzo Casini, president of Lega Serie A, said on the matter The European leagues are discussing which line to keep for the matches to be broadcast in Russia What emerged that it is not easy to find the best choice we, like the Bundesliga and Liga, have decided to continue broadcasting as an instrumental action to promote peace
