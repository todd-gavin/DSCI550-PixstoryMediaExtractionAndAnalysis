Protagonist of numerous appeals in favor of peace and against the invasion of Ukraine by Russia Andriy Shevchenko finds himself experiencing moments of great apprehension His sister and mother are in fact blocked in Bucha city scene of heavy clashes between the two armies Speaking to Sky Sport News Shevchenko explained My mother and my sister hosted some people who moved with them because they were in terrible conditions There is no food or electricity in Bucha They are friends of my sister and for five days they had been living underground in a basement There was terrible bombing in that area Bucha and nearby Irpin and Hostomel I know these cities well and they were completely destroyed No buildings left I used to go to Bucha to train when I was a terrible young player
