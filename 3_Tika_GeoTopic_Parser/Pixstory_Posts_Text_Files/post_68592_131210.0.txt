Odisha FC beat Kerala Blasters 2-0 in the Durand Cup here on Tuesday.

Odisha shot into lead in the 51st minute through Isaac Vanmalsawma. Saul Crespo doubled the lead in the 74th.

Earlier, Odisha had thrashed NorthEast United FC 6-0 in their Group D opening match, while Kerala Blasters played out a 1-1 draw with Sudeva Delhi FC.

Now Kerala Blasters will take on North East United FC on Saturday. The top two sides from the four groups will advance to the quarterfinals.
