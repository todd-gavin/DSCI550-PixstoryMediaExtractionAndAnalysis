Why are clean skies becoming increasingly elusive The color and beauty of the sky are a direct reflection of how clean they are Heavy volumes of contaminants in the air are taking away from their majesty Vehicular pollution industrial pollution stubble burning and the like are making the air we breathe deadly and poisonous Pollution related health concerns are not trivial and cannot be ignored Contaminated air can potentially develop into life threatening respiratory conditions It is imperative that we pay heed to calls for urgent attention and acknowledge the seriousness of the situation
