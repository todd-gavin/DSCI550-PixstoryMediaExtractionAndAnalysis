Upright follows a typical odd couple combination anxious and cynical Lucky Tim Minchin and outspoken adolescent runaway Meg Milly Alcock who are thrown together after a car accident in the midst of the Australian desert They quickly become friends after deciding to work together to transport Lucky s upright piano which practically takes on a life of its own from Sydney to his mother s house in Perth on the other side of the country Having survived various misadventures and discussing a variety of themes they create an even stronger friendship during their journey across Australia As per collider the show is at times strange and absurd with a touch of melancholy but overall it s a heartwarming and entirely human show about the ups and downs of this old thing we call life complemented by beautiful Australian outback landscapes Similarly to The Gloaming a second season is in the works
