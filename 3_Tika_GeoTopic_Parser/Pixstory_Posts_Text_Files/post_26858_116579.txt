The Tiffany event was held in the wonderful setting of Palazzo Grespi, once the home of Giulia Maria Grespi, founder of the FAI. The building dates back to the 17th century and was renovated in the late 1920s by the architect Piero Portaluppi and is one of the few patrician residences in Milan still intact and inhabited Still inhabited by the heirs of the Grespi family in its magnificent gardens the jewelery party was set up Among the pieces on display also the famous Tiffany Yellow Diamond of 1 carat
