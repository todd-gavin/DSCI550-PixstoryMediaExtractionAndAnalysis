After the virtual fashion week that lasted from to March, fashion has decided to take another step forward towards the future. The Off White brand has decided to accept cryptocurrency payments including Bitcoin Btc Ethereum Eth Binance Coin Bnb Ripple Xrp Stablecoins Tether Usdt and Usd Coin Usdc inside its flagship stores in Paris, London and Milan Secure cryptocurrency transactions will be processed using the Lunu Pos terminal which, as brand spokespersons inform, finds the best cryptocurrency exchange rate at the time of the transaction and is compatible with various crypto wallets that allow scan a QR code to settle the payment
