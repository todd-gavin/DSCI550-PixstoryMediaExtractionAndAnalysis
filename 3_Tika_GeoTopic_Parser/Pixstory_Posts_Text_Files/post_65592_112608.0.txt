Rahul Narwekar, a BJP lawmaker from Mumbai, received 164 votes to win the position of Maharashtra Assembly Speaker today. The Shiv Sena lawmaker Rajan Salvi received 107 votes. On Monday, the Assembly will put the Eknath Shinde-led administration to the test.
Since Nana Patole of Congress resigned in February 2021 to become his party's state unit president, the position of Assembly Speaker has been vacant. Acting as Speaker in the meantime was Deputy Speaker Narhari Zirwal.
