According to a local source who spoke to the BBC, a strong earthquake in Afghanistan has killed at least 250 people and injured numerous others. 
Social media users posted images of the Paktika province's destroyed homes, rubble, and people on stretchers. 
More than 250 people have already died, and the number of injured people is expected to climb, a local government official told the BBC.
About 44 kilometres (27 miles) away from the city of Khost in the southeast, the earthquake occurred.
