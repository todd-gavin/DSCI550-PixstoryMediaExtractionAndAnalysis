At the Triennale Milano two exhibitions pay homage to the photographer Giovanni Gastel who died last March due to Covid The People I Like in collaboration with the MAXXI National Museum of XXI Century Arts presents over portraits a labyrinth of faces poses dreams of characters from the world of culture design of the art of fashion of music of the show of politics A collective portrait of souls met during a forty-year career including Barack Obama Vasco Rossi Roberto Bolle Bebe Vio Bianca Balti Franca Sozzani Monica Bellucci In parallel the exhibition The jewels of the imagination created in collaboration with the Museum of Contemporary Photography presents one of the first works that gave Giovanni Gastel international fame images of a larger project commissioned to the author by Daniel Swarovski Corporation in for the book of the same name and the exhibition of 20th century jewels
