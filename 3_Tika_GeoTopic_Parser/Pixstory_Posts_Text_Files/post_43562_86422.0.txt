BTS has been nominated for two awards at the 2022 Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards!
On March 10, the ceremony announced this year's nominees. The group is nominated for Favorite Music Group along with Maroon 5, Jonas Brothers, Black Eyed Peas, Florida Georgia Line, and Migos.

BTS first snagged a trophy at the Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards in 2018 when they won Favorite Global Music Star. They also won Favorite Music Group in 2020, and in 2021, they won three awards: Favorite Music Group, Favorite Song, and Favorite Global Music Star.
The 2022 Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards will be held in Los Angeles on April 9.
