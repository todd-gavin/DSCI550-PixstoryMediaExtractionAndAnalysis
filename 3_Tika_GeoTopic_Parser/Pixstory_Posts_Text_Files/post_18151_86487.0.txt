Warner Brothers have just announced that many of their movies are going to be postponed and most of these include the movies from the DC universe. "Aquaman and the Lost Kingdom"and the Flash movie have been postponed till the next year. Black Adam and the DC league of Super Pets will now release later this year.
Shazam! Fury of Gods has been preponed and will now release on 16th December 2022, it might even be clashing with the highly anticipated Avatar 2.
Aquaman and The Lost Kingdom will now release on 17th March 2023 and The Flash will release on 23rd June 2023.
Warner Brothers have also postponed Timothee Chalamet starrer, Wonka, which will be a prequel to the Willy Wonka novel. It will now release on 15th December 2023.
