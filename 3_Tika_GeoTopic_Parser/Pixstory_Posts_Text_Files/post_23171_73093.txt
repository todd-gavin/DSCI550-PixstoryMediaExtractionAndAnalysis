Of the nations taking part in the African Cup only they do not have a nickname linked to an animal A question of culture and also of symbology The most represented animal is the lion who leads the teams of Cameroon Senegal and Morocco Symbol of strength, pride and power The elephant which represents power and wisdom takes the field with the Ivory Coast and Guinea Then there are the eagles that refer to beauty, strength and prestige and are in the insignia of Mali Tunisia and Nigeria The stallions are the prerogative of Burkina Faso The long list and we will see many proud descend on the field until February, date of the last challenge of this CAN Until then, among the other wild dogs of Guinea Bissau, the desert foxes of Algeria, the crocodiles of Sudan, the scorpions of Gambia and the cela antes of the Gomore will fight to get on the podium
