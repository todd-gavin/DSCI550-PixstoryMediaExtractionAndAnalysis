Chocolate pudding-porridge🍎🫐

My go to breakfast when I don’t feel like putting efforts on meal preps! 

To prepare PORRIDGE:
-50 g millet/ pata
-125 ml water
-125 ml milk
-1 scoop chocolate protein powder
-20 g cocoa  powder

TOPPINGS:
-apple
-frozen blueberries+ raspberries 
-unsweetened cornflakes/ granola 

Instructions:

-Cook the millet/ oats in the water until it is creamy and almost all water is soaked.

-Add protein plus cocoa powder and milk and let it cook again until it is thick. 

-Then turn out the heat an let it soak again for
a few minutes. 

-Add toppings
