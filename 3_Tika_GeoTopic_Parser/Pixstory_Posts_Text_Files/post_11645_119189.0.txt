Cheeseburger Lettuce Wraps are everything you love about a burger but without the carbs. You won’t even miss the bread with how delicious these are!

Burger Preparation -

2 pounds lean ground beef

1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt

1 teaspoon black pepper

1 teaspoon dried oregano

6 slices American cheese

2 large heads iceburg or romaine lettuce rinsed then dried

2 tomatoes sliced thin

small red onion sliced thin

Additional spread (as per your choice and health concern)

1/4 cup light mayo

3 Tablespoons ketchup

1 Tablespoon Mustard Sauce

pinch of salt and pepper
