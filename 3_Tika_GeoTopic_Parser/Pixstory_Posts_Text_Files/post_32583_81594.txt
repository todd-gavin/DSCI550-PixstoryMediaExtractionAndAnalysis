The French couple Gabriella Papadakis and Guillaume Cizeron win the only gold that was missing in their incredible career with the world record ahead of the Russians Sinitsina Katsalapov and the Americans Hubbell Donohue but the protagonists were also the blues Charlene Gugnard and Marco Fabbri authors of a perfect free which allowed them to recover from seventh place in rhythm dance The Azzurri are in their third Olympics and after the place in Sochi and Pyeongchang they are now aiming for the podium for the Milan Cortina games
