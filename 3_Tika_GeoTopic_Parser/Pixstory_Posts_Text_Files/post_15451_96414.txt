Legislative elections are held in Slovenia tomorrow with Prime Minister Janez Jansa, an expression of the populist right who will seek a fourth term. He is being challenged by a fledgling environmentalist group, the Movement for Freedom, whose leader is the former head of the Gen I state energy company, Robert Golob. All polls speak of a head-to-head Golob has taken the reins of a small ecological party without parliamentary representation and transformed it into the political force of reference for the centre-left
