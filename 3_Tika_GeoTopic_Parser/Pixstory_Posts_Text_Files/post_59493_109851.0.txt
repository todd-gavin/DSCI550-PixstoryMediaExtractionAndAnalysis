Pusarla Venkata Sindhu (PV Sindhu) is an Indian professional badminton player.
She won a silver medal at the 2016 Summer Olympics, becoming the first Indian woman to do so. She is one of just two Indian badminton players—the other being Saina Nehwal—to have ever won an Olympic medal. She became the youngest recipient of the Padma Shri, India's fourth-highest civilian accolade, in March 2015. She became the youngest Indian to place on the podium in an individual event at the Olympics after winning the women's singles silver medal at the 2016 Summer Olympics. She was also the first Indian shuttler to compete in an Olympics badminton final.
