Ponniyin Selvan Is also known as PS I is an upcoming Indian Tamil language epic period drama film directed and co produced by Mani Ratnam under his production studio Madras Talkies along with Allirajah Subaskaran under the banner Lyca Productions The film boasts of an outstanding star cast that includes Vikram Karthi Trisha Krishnan Prakash Raj Jayaram Jayam Ravi and Aishwarya Lekshmi among others
