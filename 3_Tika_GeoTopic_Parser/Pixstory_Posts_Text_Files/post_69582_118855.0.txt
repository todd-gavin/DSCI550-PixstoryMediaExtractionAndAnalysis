According to the Union Home Ministry on Monday, India reported 16,866 new Covid-19 cases in the preceding day, down from the 20,279 cases reported the day before. 
41 more deaths occurred within the same time period, bringing the total number of Covid fatalities countrywide to 5,26,074. 
The number of open cases decreased to 1,50,877, or 0.34 percent of the total number of positive cases in the nation. 
The total number of patients who have recovered during the past 24 hours is 18,148, bringing the total to 4,32,28,670. India now has a recovery rate of 98.46 percent.
