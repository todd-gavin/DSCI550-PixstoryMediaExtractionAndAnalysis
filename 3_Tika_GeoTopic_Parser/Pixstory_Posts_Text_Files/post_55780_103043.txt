Tim Tams from Arnott s are possibly Australia s most popular chocolate biscuit Following his attendance at the Kentucky Derby confectionery firm owner Ross Arnott named his new brand of biscuits after the winning horse of the year Tim Tam As nomadparadise tells us the biscuit which was first introduced in Australia in consists of a thin layer of chocolaty cream sandwiched between two wafer wafers and dipped in chocolate They re delicious on their own but in the time honored tradition of the Tim Tam Slam they re often served with a hot beverage This is when you bite off the opposing corners of the Tim Tam and use it to slurp up your hot beverage with the added bonus of eating your makeshift melted straw Let me know if you like them or not
