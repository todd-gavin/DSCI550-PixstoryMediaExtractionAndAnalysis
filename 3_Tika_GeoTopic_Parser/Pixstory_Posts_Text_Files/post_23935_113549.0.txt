The Centre of Excellence on Satellite and Unmanned Remote Vehicle Initiative (CoE-SURVEI) has developed an Artificial Intelligence-based software which can automatically detect change on ground, including unauthorised constructions and encroachments in a time series using satellite imagery.

The CoE-SURVEI, inaugurated by Defence Minister Rajnath Singh in December 2021, leverages latest technologies in survey, including satellite imagery, drone imagery and geo-spatial tools, for effective land management and urban planning.
