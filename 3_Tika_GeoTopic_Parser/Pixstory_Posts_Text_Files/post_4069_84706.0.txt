Painting and sculptures that resembles a high-resolution photograph.

 Hyperrealism is considered an advancement of Photorealism by the methods used to produce the resulting paintings or sculptures. 

Hyperrealism thrives on the imperfections of an image, and as perfect as the image is “copied”, it retains all of the imperfections of the original.

Hyper-realistic art is basically a portrayal of reality, and there are often cases where the artwork cannot be distinguished from the reference.


Some of the Hyper Realists include: 

Claudio Bravo

Dennis Wojtkiewicz 

 István Sándorfi
