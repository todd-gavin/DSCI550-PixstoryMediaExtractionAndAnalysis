1. She Would Never Know
- Song-ah and Hyun-seung work at a cosmetics marketing firm, and the two slowly grow closer sharing a workplace relationship.

2. The King: Eternal Monarch
- Korean emperor Lee Gon tries to close the doors to a parallel world which was opened by demons; a detective tries to protect the people and the one she loves

3. Search WWW
- Three 21st century women, working at the top two competitive web portal companies, decide to put their careers ahead of everything else in their lives.

4. Love Revolution
- Gong Joo-young, a high school student, falls in love at first sight with Wang Ja-rim, a pretty but standoffish girl. Despite her cold attitude, Joo-young goes to great lengths to win her heart
