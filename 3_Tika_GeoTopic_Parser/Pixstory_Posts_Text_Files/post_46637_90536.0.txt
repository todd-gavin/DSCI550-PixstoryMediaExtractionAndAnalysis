Prime minister Narendra Modi took to Twitter to congratulate Pramod Sawant on his swearing as Goa's chief minister.

PM Modi's tweet :- Congratulations to DrPramodPSawant Ji and all others who took oath in Goa today. I am confident this entire team will deliver good governance to the people of Goa and build on the pro-people work done in the last decade.
