First exhibition in Italy dedicated to the creative talents of Ossie Clark and Celia Birtwell the exhibition will be held in two cities Prato Textile Museum September January Milan Sozzani Foundation January April The exhibition not only presents a series of dresses with iconic prints by Ossie and intends to tell the context and the path of the designer from the Quorum boutique in Chelsea frequented by the youth of the London scene to the performances through a series of video photos and vintage editorials memorabilia sketches and reproductions of the designs up to an exclusive video interview with Celia Birtwell herself
