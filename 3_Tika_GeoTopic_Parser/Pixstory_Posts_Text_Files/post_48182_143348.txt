The Election Commission of India launched the new digital Publication BLO e Patrika during an interactive session held with BLO s all across the States and UT s via video conferencing Vijay N Zade Chief Electoral Officer Chandigarh along with few BLO s attended the event He appreciated the initiative taken by the ECI and stated that the BLOs are the face of ECI to the general public The BLO s will also learn from the experience of their fellow colleagues of other states This BLO e Patrika will be available on the website social media platforms of ECI and also on the garudda app
