Rashid Khan Arman, the Afghan cricket star, was able to break the record of the youngest cricketer in the world and registered his name in the Guinness Book of Records in 2020.
Rashid Khan Arman Azbahar received 176 wickets in the world games before he reached the age of twenty years, his name is registered in the new book of Guinness.
Earlier, Pakistani cricketer Waqar Younis was the first person who had a record in this department
Rashid Khan, a member of the Afghanistan national cricket team, was born on the 20th of September 1998
He is also one of the top bowlers in the world.
