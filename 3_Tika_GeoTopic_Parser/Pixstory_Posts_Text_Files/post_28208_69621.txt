The story of Novak Djokovic shows no signs of closing The Serbian tennis player number in the ATP ranking had been admitted to Australia after days of uncertainty related to his vaccination status Convinced no vax, the thirty-four year old had in fact tried to enter the State of Victoria in recent days to play the Australian Open presenting a medical exemption to the anti Covid vaccine which had caused a lot of discussion For days literally imprisoned in a hotel in central Melbourne for political refugees and asylum seekers in the end Djokovic had regained his visa with a favorable ruling from a judge who would have allowed him to take the field starting from Monday January The visa for today has been revoked again this time by the immigration minister who intervened on the very delicate issue for reasons of health and good order A decision against which Djokovic&#39;s lawyers have already appealed the expulsion therefore suspended until the pronouncement of the Federal Court expected for Sunday
