Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Ahmedabad emerged as the number 1 business school in India in the NIRF rankings issued today by the ministry of education.

It was followed by IIM Bangalore grabbing the second spot and IIM Calcutta coming on 3rd.

NIRF business school rankings 2022:- 

Rank 1: IIM Ahmedabad

Rank 2: IIM Bangaluru

Rank 3: IIM Calcutta

Rank 4: IIT Delhi

Rank 5: IIM Kozhikode

Rank 6: IIM Lucknow

Rank 7: IIM Indore

Rank 8: Xavier, Jamshedpur

Rank 9: NIIE Mumbai

Rank 10 IIT Madras
