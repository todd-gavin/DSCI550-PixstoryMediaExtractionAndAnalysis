In Kerala the trend of this sweet delicacy is being seen everywhere The delicacy named as Boli and payasam Kheer It plays the most essential part in the onam sadhya The Palada payasam that s usually present in the sadhya has a companion of Boli when it comes to the sadhya in the district of Trivandrum Bolis and payasam are the main combos and people from various districts like Kollam and Kannur comes to Trivandrum to buy the authentic ones The taste of Bolis will differ based on how its kneaded Bolis are sold like hot cakes during the Onam festive The Boli is like a yellow coloured mat placed for the hot palada payasam Kheer to be poured on and there is a burst of flavours while we just place them on our mouths
