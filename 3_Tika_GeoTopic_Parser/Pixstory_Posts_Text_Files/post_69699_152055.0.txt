Red Velvet's Seulgi has dropped her music video for "28 Reasons".
In the MV, Seulgi escapes from chains and makes a run for it. "28 Reasons" is the title song of the Red Velvet's solo debut mini album of the same name, and the lyrics are about a strange and difficult attraction to someone.
