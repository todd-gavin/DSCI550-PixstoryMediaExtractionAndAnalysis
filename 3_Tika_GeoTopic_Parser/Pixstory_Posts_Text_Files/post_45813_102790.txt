Boeing s CST Starliner spacecraft landed at White Sands Space Harbor in New Mexico at p m on Wednesday May completing the uncrewed Orbital Flight Test as part of NASA s Commercial Crew Program NASA reported that following a parachute assisted landing the Starliner settled gently onto its air bags paving the way for future crewed landings A deorbit burn at p m separation of the spacecraft s service module and successful deployment of the spacecraft s three main parachutes and six airbags preceded the landing At p m on Thursday May Starliner launched from Space Launch Complex at Cape Canaveral Space Force Station in Florida on a flight test to the International Space Station on a United Launch Alliance Atlas V rocket The uncrewed spacecraft successfully docked to the space station s Harmony module at p m Friday May
