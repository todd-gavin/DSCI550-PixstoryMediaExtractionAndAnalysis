Films which were major projects that still haven’t been completed or released.

1- TAKHT🔥🔥

Takht is a big-budget, ambitious film that Karan Johar announced in 2019. It was allegedly supposed to star massive celebrities like Kareena Kapoor Khan, Ranveer Singh, Alia Bhatt, Vicky Kaushal, Bhumi Pednekar, Janhvi Kapoor and Anil Kapoor.

2-DOSTANA 2💗💗

This film was supposed to star Kartik Aaryan, but was shelved amidst rumours of a fallout between Kartik and Karan Johar. Janhvi Kapoor was also reportedly hired for the movie. But, its fate now remains uncertain. There are rumours of a recasting, but the production company hasn’t announced anything yet.

3- ‘The Immortal Ashwatthama’❤️‍🔥❤️‍🔥

After the success of Uri, Vicky Kaushal and Aditya Dhar announced their next ambitious collaboration, titled The Immortal Ashwatthama. But, during Koffee With Karan, Vicky revealed that it also had been shelved, like Takht. 

4- ‘Hera Pheri 3’😍

After very popular first and second films, this franchise was meant to return to the silver screen with Abhishek Bachchan and John Abraham. The shoot for Hera Pheri 3 started, too, and Abhishek Bachchan even tweeted about it. But, he later confirmed that the film is no longer being made but there were still promotions of hera pheri 3 but didn’t get any teasers🤔
