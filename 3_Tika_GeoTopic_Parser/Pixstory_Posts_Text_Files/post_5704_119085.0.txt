5G network is almost here. It will be 10x faster than the current 4G with ultra high speeds which can go upto 10GBps and can connect to 100x more devices than 4G at same time.

In addition to ultra high speeds, it will ensure ultra low latency which will allow downloading of high quality videos in few seconds. 

Fifth Generation or 5G would enable solutions such as e-health, connected vehicles, more immersive augmented reality and metaverse experiences, life-saving use cases, and advanced mobile cloud gaming among others.

India's 5G spectrum auction began today with Mukesh Ambani's Reliance Jio, Sunil Mittal-led Bharti Airtel, Vodafone Idea and billionaire Gautam Adani’s flagship Adani Enterprises in the race to bid on 5G airwaves worth Rs4.3 lakh crores.

The bidding which started today at 10 in morning will go on till 6 and will carry on tomorrow.
