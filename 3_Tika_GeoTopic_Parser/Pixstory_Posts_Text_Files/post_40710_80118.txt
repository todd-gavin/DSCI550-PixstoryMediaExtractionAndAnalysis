Langdi is a traditional Indian field sport played in Pandiyan Dynasty called Nondiyaattam similar to hopscotch It is described by Marathis as a sport with a Marathi ethos Langdi is considered to be useful in training for sports like kho kho volleyball and gymnastics The National Langdi Federation received national recognition in Langdi in Maharashtra is a popular childhood pastime it is described as the foundation of all sports
