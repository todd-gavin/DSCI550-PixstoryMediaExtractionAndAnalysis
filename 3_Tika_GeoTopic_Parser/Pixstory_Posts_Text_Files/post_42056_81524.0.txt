The Indian Oil & Gas industry is the third-largest energy consumer only behind USA and Chine and contributes to 5.2% of the global oil demand. Covid19 slowed down the demand of transport fuels ,(accounting for 2/3rd demand)as auto & industrial manufacturing declined and goods & passenger movement (both bulk & personal) fell. As a result 
the government increased the excise and special excise duty to make up for the revenue loss.
