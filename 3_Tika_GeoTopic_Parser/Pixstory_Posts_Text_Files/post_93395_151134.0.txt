On October 1, four pivotal matches took place in the men's and women's kho-kho events. By barest of margins Karnataka, Gujarat, and Kerala defeated Andhra Pradesh, West Bengal,and Tamil Nadu, respectively. Meanwhile, Maharashtra thrashed Delhi by a bigger margin.
However, Karnataka, Gujarat, and Haryana put up a valiant fight to win the women's events. Maharashtra, on the other hand, crushed Odisha with an 18-point victory.
Men’s
Maharashtra beat Delhi 36-22
Karnataka beat Andhra Pradesh 28-26
Gujarat beat West Bengal 34-30
Kerala beat Tamil Nadu 28-26
Women’s
Maharashtra beat Odisha 38-20
Karnataka beat Delhi 34-28
Gujarat beat Punjab 26-24
Haryana beat Kerala 18-16
