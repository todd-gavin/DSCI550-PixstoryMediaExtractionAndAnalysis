India is likely to begin vaccination drive in the age group against COVID in March once the population gets fully vaccinated a top government expert has said Chairman of the COVID working group of National Technical Advisory Group on Immunisation NTAGI Dr N K Arora said the government is likely to take a policy decision for initiating the vaccination drive for the age group in March according to news agency PTI He estimated that they are million population in the age group Buoyed by the active participation of teens in the age group he said Adolescents in this age group have been actively participating in the inoculation process and going by this pace of vaccination the rest of the beneficiaries in the age group are likely to be covered with the first dose by January end and subsequently their second dose is expected to be done by February end Provisional vaccination reports till am on Monday showed that with more than lakh doses being administered in a span of hours the cumulative number has exceeded million doses According to government data over million first doses have been given to children in the years age group so far The Indian government had begun inoculating the kids in the age group from January rd Bharat Biotech s Covaxin was the only vaccine given green light by the government to be administered among teenagers Source WION
