Man and the environment are inextricably linked Humans impact their surroundings and humans alter their environment as a result of their development dispersal activity mortality and decay among other things As a result all living organisms including man and his environment are mutually reactive impacting each other in a variety of ways and a dynamic balance between the two is attainable Every year on June th World Environment Day is commemorated to underline the importance of a green environment in our lives and to raise environmental consciousness globally
