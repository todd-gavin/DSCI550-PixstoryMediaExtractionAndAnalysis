India batter Mithali Raj has called time on her international career. She made the announcement through a statement on social media.

Mithali began playing for India as a 16-year-old in 1999 and went on to become one of the game's all-time greats. She played in 12 Tests, 232 One-Day Internationals, and 89 Twenty-20 Internationals for India, and was the captain who guided them to two World Cup finals.

In one-day internationals, she ends with a total of 7805 runs, including seven hundreds and 64 fifties, after debuting with a century in 1999.
