Pooram have taken a supreme position in the culture of Kerala It is an event that is dedicated to the goddesses Several pooram festivals happen in a year at various temples The attraction of this festival are the caparisoned elephants The elephants line up in a procession with golden nettipattams headgear peacock feathers and white turfs that holds the diety of the pooram The Thrissur pooram that happens at the Vadakkunnathan Temple is the biggest pooram in the state that happens every year After two years of break due to the pandemic the pooram has made a great come back and it is in its closing days The pooram this year falls on th and th of May with midnight fireworks and an endless crowd it s a visual bonanza that one has to experience in their lifetime
