There is growing evidence that music has therapeutic effects on both the mental and physical health of pregnant people. The main benefits of listening to music during pregnancy include:
- Reduction in stress and anxiety levels 
- Improved moods and emotions 
- Better sleep quality 
- Greater sense of well-being 
- Increased resilience against stress 
- Enhanced communication and co-operation skills between parents and children 
It's not just pregnant person who benefit from music during pregnancy; it's also beneficial for their babies. According to a study published in the journal Music Perception, infants who are exposed to musical sounds during the first few months of life are more likely to develop better cognitive functions later on. These functions include attention span, problem solving ability, memory, as well as emotional stability.
