How mental health impacts your eating patterns The practice of mindful eating is based on mindfulness You become more conscious of your feelings levels of hunger and other things when you eat thoughtfully You distinguish better between actual hunger and emotional eating with this strategy When you eat in this way it is simpler to pinpoint your food triggers A qualified therapist is best to help you process this fresh understanding of who you are According to studies conscious eating can lessen the severity and frequency of binge episodes Emotional eating and external eating are two bad eating habits that can be eliminated by eating thoughtfully Emotional eating occurs when you eat as a result of uncomfortable emotions External eating happens when you feel the need to eat because of your surroundings Remember mindful eating takes practice Have you practiced mindful eating before
