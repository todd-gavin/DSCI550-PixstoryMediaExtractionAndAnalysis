NEW DELHI: According to the Delhi High Court, a newspaper or an agency that disseminates news cannot be considered to be performing a public function. The court made the remark while dismissing a complaint alleging racial discrimination and harassment by a private international news organisation against a former employee. 

The petition was deemed "unmaintainable" by the supreme court. 

The petitioner's attorney argued that the respondent press agency plainly fulfils a public duty, and that the writ petition would certainly lie because racial discrimination accusations had been made.
