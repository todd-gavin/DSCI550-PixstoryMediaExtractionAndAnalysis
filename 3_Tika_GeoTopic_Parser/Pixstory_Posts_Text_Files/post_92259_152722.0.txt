Ahmedabad   :-  The Vande Bharat Express, running between Mumbai Central and Gandhinagar Capital, suffered minor damage when some buffaloes collided with the train on Thursday:-
The incident took place at 11:15 am when some buffaloes came infront of the moving train between Batwa and Maninagar stations.
The engine of the Vande Bharat Express was damaged which was repaired by the personnel of the Railways.
No one was injured in the incident.
The Vande Bharat Express left for its Mumbai Central from Gandhinagar Capital station on time.
The Western Railway (WR) zone further reduced the travelling time of the Vande Bharat Express train from October 5. The train will arrive 20 minutes early at Mumbai Central from Gandhinagar.
