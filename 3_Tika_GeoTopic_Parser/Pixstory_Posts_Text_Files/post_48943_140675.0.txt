
Ingredients 
2 pounds baby potatoes 
1 tablespoon coconut oil
2 teaspoon black mustard seeds
½ teaspoon turmeric
1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
1 tablespoon grated ginger
⅓ cup canned coconut milk
Salt to taste
3-4 spring onions 
Juice of 1 lime (or lemon)
1 cup frozen green peas (thawed)
Method Of Preparation : 
1. Place potatoes in a pot large enough to cover by an inch. Bring them to a boil. 
2. Drain the potatoes and set aside. Add frozen peas in the pot used for boiling potatoes to warm the peas. 
3. Heat oil on medium-high heat in a small saucepan, for the dressing. Add mustard seeds when hot, and after the crackle and spluttering, add turmeric, red pepper flakes, ginger, stir - fry for half a minute, turn off the heat. Next, add and stir in the coconut milk. 
4. Place the potatoes, cut in half, in a big bowl. Add the spring onions, green peas, and turmeric-coconut dressing. Add the juice of one lemon. Toss everything together and season with salt to taste.
There goes, our vegan potato salad is ready!
