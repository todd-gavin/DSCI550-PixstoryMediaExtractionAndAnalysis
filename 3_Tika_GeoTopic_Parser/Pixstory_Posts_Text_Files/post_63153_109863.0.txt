Shiva Marvel Nandy, CEO of Skyesports, revealed that the SkyEsports Champions Series (SCS) Phase 3: Playoffs will be held as a LAN-line event, with the site decided in Chennai, following a big success of the first Indian Valorant LAN Skyesports Souvenir Mumbai 2022. The Lan will have a small audience from May 31 to June 4. 

One of the most significant South Asian (SA) Valorant events is the Skyesports Champions Series (SCS), which will send two teams to the VCT Stage 2 APAC Challengers to compete for two spots at the VCT Stage 2 Masters.
