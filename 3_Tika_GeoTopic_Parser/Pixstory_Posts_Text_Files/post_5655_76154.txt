The central government announced Padma awards on Tuesday the eve of Republic Day Among other honours late General Bipin Rawat has been awarded the Padma Vibhushan Padma Awards one of the highest civilian Awards of the country are conferred in three categories Padma Vibhushan Padma Bhushan and Padma Shri The awards are given in various disciplines fields of activities viz art social work public affairs science and engineering trade and industry medicine literature and education sports civil service etc
