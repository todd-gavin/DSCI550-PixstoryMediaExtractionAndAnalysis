After the Valentino show at Trinit dei Monti, Rome is once again focusing on fashion with a major fashion show in Via Veneto Rome created by Stefano Dominella with the artistic direction of Guillermo Mariotto organizational coordination of Zetema Progetto Cultura promotion of Rome Capital Department of Tourism Large Events Sport and Fashion led by Alessandro Onorato with whom the fashion world will return in July to enliven Via Veneto, the symbolic street of the Dolce Vita on the Scalinata di Trinit dei Monti on an ideal walkway of meters that started from Via Gregoriana and ended in Piazza Mignanelli
