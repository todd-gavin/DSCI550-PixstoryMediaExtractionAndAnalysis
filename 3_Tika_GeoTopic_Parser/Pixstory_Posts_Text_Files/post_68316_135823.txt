Holicharan Narzary is a professional Indian footballer born on May in Kokrajhar Assam He is right footed and plays on the left wing in the midfield Holicharan also has the ability to play as a right winger cutting in on his left foot he is able to run in on defenders or beat them with pace and deliver some crucial crosses in the final third He is versatile enough to play as a centre forward which makes him different from some of the other wingers of India Holicharan is currently playing for Hyderabad Football Club and the Indian National Team
