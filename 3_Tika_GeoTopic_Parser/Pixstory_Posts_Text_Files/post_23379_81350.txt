A little drought, a little climate change The fact is that now that the almost dry flooded Alto Lindoso re-emerges a small village that had been submerged by the waters of the basin for years. It is happening in Galicia in northwestern Spain near the border with Portugal. Now Aceredo with its evocative ruins is becoming an attraction for tourists Among semi-destroyed buildings and concrete and wood debris Aceredo has returned to being visible arousing curiosity but also sadness among the inhabitants of the area
