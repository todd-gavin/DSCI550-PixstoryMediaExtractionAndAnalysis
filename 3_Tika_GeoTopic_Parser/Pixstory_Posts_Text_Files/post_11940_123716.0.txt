Research has shown that Vitamin B6 may play a role in improving the production of fertile cervical mucous

Let's hear it for B6!
Fertile cervical mucous is needed to help nourish and
transport sperm to the released egg. Without it, sperm die.

Vitamin B6 rich foods may help to improve the production of this fertile cervical mucous.
Try adding some more of these B6 rich foods into your diet to enhance your chances of conceiving.
