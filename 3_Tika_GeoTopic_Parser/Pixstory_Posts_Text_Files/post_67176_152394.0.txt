Apple's iPhone exports from India crossed $1 billion in five months since April. 

The outbound shipments of India-made iPhones, mainly to Europe and Middle East, are set to reach $2.5 billion in twelve months through March 2023, almost double when compared to the March 2022.

Apple, which started making iPhones in India from 2017, started manufacturing iPhone 13 here earlier this year, and announced its plans for the production of iPhone 14.
