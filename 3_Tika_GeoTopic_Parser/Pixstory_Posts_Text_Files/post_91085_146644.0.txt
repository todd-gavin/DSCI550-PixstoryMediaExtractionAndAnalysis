Actor Ayushman Khurana took to Instagram to launch the trailer of his upcoming Junglee Production film “Doctor G”. The movie is all set to release on October 14 this year and is also starring actors like Shefali Shah and Rakul Preet Singh.Doctor G is a story about a gynaec student who is struggling hard to treat females without feeling uncomfortable and wants to switch to studying orthopaedics. From the makers of Badhai Ho and Bareilly Ki Barfi, this film too seems to be a typical light hearted Ayushman Khurana film that is too good to miss out on.
