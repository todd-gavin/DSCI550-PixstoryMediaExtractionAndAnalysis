Champions League draws Milan and Juventus do well Napol and Inter in iron groups The draws for the Champions League groups were held yesterday in Nyon The fixture list will be announced on Saturday but in the meantime the opponents of the four Italian teams are already known Best of all went to Milan who drew the very strong Chelsea but also the affordable Salzburg and Dinamo Zagreb Juventus cannot complain either, whose real rock apart from Paris Saint Germain will be Benfica with Maccabi Haifa as the fourth team in the group. Naples which, in addition to the formidable Liverpool, has already faced several times in the recent past, will also face off against Ajax and Glasgow Rangers, two noble and perky teams. The palm of bad luck is won by Inter who draws Bayern Munich and Barcelona in addition to the Czechs of Viktoria Plzen
