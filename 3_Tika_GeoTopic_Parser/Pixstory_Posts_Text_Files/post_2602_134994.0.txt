Today is birthday of Shubha Khote, a popular face of hindi movies. She was born in a family of theatre actors which included her father Nandu Khote and her aunt, Durga Khote. 
Shubha started acting in 1941 at the age of 4 and is still at it. Films, TV serials and theatre in hindi and marathi. 
A relatively unknown fact was that she was National Champion in Cycling though only Seema enabled her to show her prowess.
Her brother Viju Khote was the famous ‘ab tera kya hoga Kaalia’ from Sholay
