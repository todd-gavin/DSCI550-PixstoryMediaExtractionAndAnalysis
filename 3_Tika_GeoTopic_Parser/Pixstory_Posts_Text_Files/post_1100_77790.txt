Iranian women were allowed to share a landmark moment as Iran secured qualification on Thursday for November s World Cup in Qatar According to the national team s website female supporters entered through a special entrance via a car park controlled by policewomen wearing black chador robes and red badges on their arms They were separated from men in the stadium Since October Covid restrictions in the country have prohibited all fans from entering stadiums to watch the national team that was until Thursday According to the country s news agency ISNA out of tickets sold for the the World Cup qualifying match between Iran and Iraq at the Azadi Stadium in Tehran were given to women
