To celebrate National Cinema Day, around 40,000 theatres across India are already selling movie tickets at Rs 75 but now several theatres have brought down the price of the snack items as well!While, InOX has decided to offer Food and Beverage combos at just Rs 75 , Cinnepolis is offering combos starting at a very minimal price range. The movie snacks which are usually so infamous for being overpriced, were today priced at just Rs 100 too in many theatres.To all movie buffs out there- now is your chance, go grab your popcorn and that movie ticket because this day is going to be marked forever in the history of Indian Cinema.
