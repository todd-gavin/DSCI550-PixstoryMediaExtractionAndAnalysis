Delhi Public School, Lava Nagpur, Nagpur, commemorated #EarthDay2022 with a Morning Assembly by grades IX and X. The prayer service was followed by a pledge, presentation and skit on 'Gender-inclusive climate justice'. EarthDay.org My Future My Voice Youth Ambassador Climate Communicator Ms. Bindu Bhandari from Nepal conducted a webinar on Climate Action Interactive Simulations.
