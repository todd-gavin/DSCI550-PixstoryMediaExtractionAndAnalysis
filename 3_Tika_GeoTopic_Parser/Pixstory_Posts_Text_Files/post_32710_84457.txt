In these hours the measures of the various sports federations are multiplying to punish Russia after the start of the brutal invasion war against Ukraine The national football team has been suspended from the World Cup qualifiers and therefore from the World Cup which will be held in Qatar as well as all the Russian teams will not be able to continue playing in the European cups not only in football but also in basketball The Euroleague has in fact suspended from the CSKA Moscow championship UNICS Kazan and Zenit St. Petersburg and from the Eurocup Lokomotiv Kuban Krasnodar The International Olympic Committee in addition invites all federations to follow the example of those who have already taken steps such as the FIS which canceled all the next races in Russia and the Formula which canceled the GP in Sochi and to not allow any Russian or Belarusian athlete to participate in the events
