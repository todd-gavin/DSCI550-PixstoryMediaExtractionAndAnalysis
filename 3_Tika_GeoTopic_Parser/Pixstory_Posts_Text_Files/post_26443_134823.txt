We want to enhance five Turkish brands to allow them to start a globalization process and become influential in the world fashion system. The project will kick off when we celebrate the centenary of the foundation of our Republic With these words Mustafa Pasahan vice chairman of Ihkib Istanbul apparel exporters association MFF announced to MFF during the Ifco fair in Istanbul in August the willingness of the Turkish government and institutions to support the development in Europe, the US and other markets of five brands already present locally and in areas such as Russia. The names of the fashion houses they will be unveiled in the coming months after a selection that will include menswear, womenswear and denim in both the high and medium range Thanks to the Turquality program, the selected brands will receive state incentives to open single-brand stores around the world but also for marketing and communication with Ihkib itself which will support them in their development across borders
