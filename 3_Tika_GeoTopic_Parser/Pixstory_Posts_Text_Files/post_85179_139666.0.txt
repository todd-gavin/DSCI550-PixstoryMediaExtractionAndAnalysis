HJK and Real Betis will be up against each other in their opening encounter of the Europa League 2022-23. This will be a Group C match which will be played at the Sonera Stadium.
Roma, Ludogorets, HJK, and Betis are the 4 clubs that have been drawn in Group C of the Europa League campaign this season. Both these sides will be meeting for the first time in a competitive fixture. 
HJK have faced only one Spanish opposition in European competitions when they lost to Athletic Bilbao in the 2012-13 season. Real Betis, on the other hand, will be facing a Finnish opposition for the first time in their European history.

HJK vs Real Betis Probable Winners
HJK do not boast a lot of experience on the European stage and hence, Betis might go on to be a superior opposition on field. On paper, Betis’ squad depth and players are far ahead of HJK and hence, Betis might cruise in this game past HJK.
