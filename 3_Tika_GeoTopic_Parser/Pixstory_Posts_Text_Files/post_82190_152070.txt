Have you ever seen a Dhunuchi Dance It is an integral part of Durga Puja Dhunuchi is an earthenware pot with a stem Dried coconut husk and camphor is used to lit a flame Also Dhuno is added in the mixture It is the dried resin of Sal tree which gives the Dhunuchi smoke the distinct fragrance It is used while worshipping Dhunuchi dance is performed by holding burning dhunuchi in hands or even in the mouth and dancing to the beats of Dhak the bengali drum During Durga Puja especially on Navami almost all Puja Pandals hold Dhunuchi Dance competitions The entire evening is spent dancing and rejoicing to the beats of Dhak
