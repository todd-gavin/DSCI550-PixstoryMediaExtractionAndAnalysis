 AJAY DEVGN The Legend Of Bhagat Singh directed by Rajkumar Santoshi starred Ajay Devgn as the revolutionary battle martyr Bhagat Singh It depicted the narrative of a year old rebel who plotted a strategic bombing at the British parliament in retaliation for Lala Lajpat Rai s death Ajay nailed the role of the young hero and it s one of those performances when you forget about the actor and just enjoy the performance
