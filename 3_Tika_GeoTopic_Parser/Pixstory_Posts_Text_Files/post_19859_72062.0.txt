Taking on the initiative to showcase the beautiful and resourceful Northeast India, the NEW Expedition (North East on Wheels) was launched in New Delhi on Tuesday.

The event was graced by Meenakshi Lekhi, Minister of State for Culture & External Affairs in presence of other dignitaries including Ashish Kumar Chauhan, MD & CEO, Bombay Stock Exchange.
