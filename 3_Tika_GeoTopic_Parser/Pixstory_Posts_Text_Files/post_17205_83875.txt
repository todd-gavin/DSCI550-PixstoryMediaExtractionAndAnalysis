Bhaag Milkha Bhaag is one of those rare films which are hard to forget long after you are done with them This movie is based on the life of an Indian Athlete Milkha Singh who is the twice champion of the Asian Games a one time winner of Commonwealth Games and an Olympian The Role of Milkha Singh is Played by Farhan Akhtar in this movie which was superb This story is really inspirational as it highlights the struggles of Milkha Singh from a child who lost his parents birthplace and love but still without losing hope he works hard on his dream to represent India
