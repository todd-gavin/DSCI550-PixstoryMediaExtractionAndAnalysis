Anchar Lake is one of Srinagar s most tranquil locations since it is connected to the lovely Dal Lake by a stream known as Amir Khan Nallah During floods Dal Lake s floodwaters are directed here For those who love the outdoors and wish to go birdwatching and take pictures Anchar Lake is a fantastic destination Winter is a particularly popular time for birdwatching since it allows for easy viewing of birds like Teal Pochard Gadwall Snipe and Mallard Visitors can also take Shikara rides at the Anchar Lake Image source
