Kathmandu: Prime Minister Narendra Modi will visit Lumbini, the birthplace of Lord Buddha, in Nepal on Buddha Jayanti.

The visit of the Prime Minister to Nepal comes less than a month after his Nepali counterpart Sher Bahadur Deuba visited India.

"Prime Minister Modi will be coming to Nepal on May 16 on Buddha Jayanti but this time Lumbini only. Preparations are already underway," a source told ANI.
