The Cannes Film Festival, often known as the Festival de Cannes, is an annual film festival held in Cannes, France and well-known for its great fashion and showbiz. Deepika Padukone, a well-known and glamorous actress who served on the Cannes jury this year, had numerous opportunities to show off her incredible and mind-blowing fashion fusion apparel and looks produced by the world's top most fashion designers and makeup experts.

So, at the Cannes Film Festival's closing ceremony on Saturday, Deepika looked stunning in a ruffled white saree by Abu Jani-Sandeep Khosla, which she accessorised with a large pearl necklace, rich copper eye makeup, nude lips, and a matte facial look. The photos of her outfit are going viral on social media like wildfire, with incredible fan love and adoration.
