The students of Chinmaya Vidyalaya Vaduthala, Kochi, Kerala does regular environmental Morning Assemblies. The school came together to celebrate World Environment Day keeping the theme 'ONLY ONE EARTH' in mind. Mr. Manu Sathyan, District Forest Officer, flying squad was the Chief Guest. The programme commenced with environmental pledge written by Ignatia Arin Arooja, a student of Std 9, followed by an enlightening skit depicting the various causes and consequences of global warming. The Chief Guest, Mr Manu Sathyan, and Principal Ms Prathiba V addressed the gathering and proceeded to plant a few saplings in the school premises.
