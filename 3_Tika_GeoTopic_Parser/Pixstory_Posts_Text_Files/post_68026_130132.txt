Army Green defeated NorthEast United FC NEUFC in their Durand Cup Group D match at Indira Gandhi Athletic Stadium Sarusajai here today This is second straight loss to the Highlanders in the tournament Greens skipper PC Lallawmkima got a brace while Somesh Kothari struck the other in a game completely controlled by the Army team Dipu Mirdha got the lone goal for NEUFC in stoppage time With this victory Army Green team earned full three points
