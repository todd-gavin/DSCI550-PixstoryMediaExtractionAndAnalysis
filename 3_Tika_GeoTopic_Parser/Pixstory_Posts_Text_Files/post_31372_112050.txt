Due to the drought of these days, the Tiber river is far below its levels and waste of all kinds is surfacing. For this reason, we have activated voluntary associations with the regional civil protection which will take care of mapping and monitoring the waste emerged from Castel Giubileo up to the Magliana on both banks of the river This was declared by the president of the Lazio Region Nicola Zingaretti From Monday on the basis of the reports the governor explains we will then start the immediate removal of the identified waste Our commitment to make the Tiber a river of the capital continues more livable and cleaner In recent years, the Lazio Region concludes Zingaretti has always been at the forefront to promote and protect the environment and to make the river of Rome livable and not a pile of waste
