Martina Trevisan has reached the quarterfinals of the WTA tournament in Budapest. After the disappointing interlude with grass, the semifinalist of the last Roland Garros returned to her level on her beloved clay by overcoming the Ukrainian Kozlova and earning the match on Friday against the landlady, the Hungarian Bondar Elisabetta Cocciaretto who is also playing in Hungary will challenge the Czech Siniakova tomorrow for another place in the quarterfinals
