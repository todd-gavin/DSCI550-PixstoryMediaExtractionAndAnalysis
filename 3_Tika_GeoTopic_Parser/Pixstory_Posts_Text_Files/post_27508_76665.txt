Now official Marc Marquez recovered from diplopia and was given the go-ahead by his doctors to return to training on the saddle of a motorcycle. The Catalan centaur has therefore resolved the discomfort of double vision which occurred following the head trauma that had prevented him from participating in the GPs of Portimao and Valencia A problem that is finally behind us Marc will be regularly riding his Honda HRC already at the first tests of the season in Sepang starting next February
