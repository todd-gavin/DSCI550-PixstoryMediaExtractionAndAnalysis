One of the most well liked genres of music worldwide is country which has its roots in the United States The annual celebration of International Country Music Day takes place on September th and dates back to the s At different locations all around the world country music festivals are hosted on this unique day Country music fans congregate to enjoy live performances while drinking and dancing On International Country Music Day line dancing is popular and contests to find the greatest dancers are frequently held The purpose of International Country Music Day is to honour some of the greatest country musicians in the world Whether you love the genre a lot or not you are encouraged to listen to some country music on this date You could even break out the fiddle or banjo and get involved yourself Source DOY
