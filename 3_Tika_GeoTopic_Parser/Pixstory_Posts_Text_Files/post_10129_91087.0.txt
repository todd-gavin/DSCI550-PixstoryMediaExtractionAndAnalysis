In a society where education is not valued, I have always wished to be a good teacher. However, I was unable to continue my education after the 12th grade. I married for the approval of my family.
Amina Hazrati is a 29-year-old refugee. She has spent the last five years in India. My two daughters are both girls. I came to India to educate my daughters and give them a better future. And one day, they will be able to demonstrate to the world that girls can achieve their goals and stand on their own. She stated.
