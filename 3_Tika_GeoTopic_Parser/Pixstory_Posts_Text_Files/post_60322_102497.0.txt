Justin Bieber is planning another trip to India. On October 18, he will be performing in the nation's capital.
 In 2017, Indian Beliebers had the opportunity to see him play live in the country at Mumbai's DY Patil Stadium.

 The singer of " Sorry " will visit India as part of his globe tour to promote his new album " Justice ". He'll be in New Delhi this time and will perform at the Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium.

 Beginning June 4, tickets for the show will be available on BookMyShow. However, ticket registration has already begun, and pre-registered users will be able to purchase permits beginning June 2.

The ticket range between Rs 4000 to Rs 37,000.
