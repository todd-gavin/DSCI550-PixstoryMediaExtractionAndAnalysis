In a major setback for Uddhav Thackeray in his fight against Eknath Shinde the Supreme Court today refused to prevent the Election Commission from determining who constitutes the true Shiv Sena Uddhav Thackeray s faction had asked the Supreme Court to prevent the Election Commission from ruling on Eknath Shinde s claim to the real Shiv Sena and its symbol The petition was heard by the Supreme Court s Constitution bench
