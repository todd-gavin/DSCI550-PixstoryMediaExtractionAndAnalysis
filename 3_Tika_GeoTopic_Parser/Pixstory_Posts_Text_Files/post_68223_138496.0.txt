Karbi Anglong FCC beat Morigaon by 2-0 in the final Match of C-Zone Qualifying Round.

#DIPHU, Sept' 5 | Karbi Anglong Qualified from C- Zone of the 70th Senior Inter-District football Championship for Khirud Baruah Memorial Trophy. The Match was played at Karbi Anglong Sports Association (KASA) ground on Monday, 5th September 2022.
