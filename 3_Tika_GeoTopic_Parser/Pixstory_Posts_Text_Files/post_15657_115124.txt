Food insecurity has reached dramatic levels across the Horn of Africa Millions of people could face high levels of acute food insecurity by September due to an exceptionally persistent drought In an already very critical situation brought about by armed conflicts between clans health consequences economic effects of the pandemic and the effects of climate change, the impact of the war in Ukraine is also included, which aggravates the estimated forecasts We are facing a huge humanitarian crisis, a real disaster Here people die of hunger and thirst explains Isabella Garino Head of Mission Cesvi in the Horn of Africa
