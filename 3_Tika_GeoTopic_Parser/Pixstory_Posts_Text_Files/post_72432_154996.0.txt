A popular Film production banner on Wednesday announced the sequel to its hit movie "Yaariyan", with Divya Khosla Kumar, Meezaan Jafri and Yash Das Gupta in lead. The production company took to Twitter to share the details about the upcoming movie, which will arrive in theatres next year in May. "Cousins by blood, friends by choice! A family glued by the bond of true friendship brings back to you, #Yaariyan2 In cinemas 12th May 2023," the post read.
