What’s better than breakfast with a caffeine kick!

Here’s how I make my all time favourite Tiramisu Waffles 

Waffle ingredients:

*225ml plant milk

*2 eggs

*50ml vegetable/coconut oil

*1/2 tsp vanilla extract

*110 g flour

*7tsp baking powder

*pinch salt

*1 tsp coffee powder

For coffee cream:

*3 tbsp yogurt 

*1 tsp strong instant coffee powder

*syrup to taste

And enjoyyy!
