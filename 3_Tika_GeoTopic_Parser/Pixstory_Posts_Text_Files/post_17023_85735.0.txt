Last night, with much fanfare and glamour, the 21st Indian Television Academy Awards 2022 took place just before International Women's Day. What stole the show was Rakhi Sawant's banter with Ranveer Singh.
Ranveer Singh walked the red carpet showing off his uber-cool side in a velvet suit With him, Rakhi could be seen enjoying a banter and also asking him to replicate the now famous 'Pushpa' signature move.
