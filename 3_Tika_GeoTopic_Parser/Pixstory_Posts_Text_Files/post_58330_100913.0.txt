A romance novel matches the taste of various readers.

This is one of them , a book full of drama written with humourous touch .

As you can understand from the title , the main character is a girl, not a girl but a lady in her thirtees  - Henrietta Tate with 2 kids which are now in boarding school , is happily married living in country side is persuaded by her best friend to work as she has no work to do.

But when she joins the work, her life took a turn , her marriage comes at verge of breaking. Also her lover also enters her life.

Now her life is just a confusion , what should she do, what will happen next?

These questions will be answered after reading!

Thank you for reading!
