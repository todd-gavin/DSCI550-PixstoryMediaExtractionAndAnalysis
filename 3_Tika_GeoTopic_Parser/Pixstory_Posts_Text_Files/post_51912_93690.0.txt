B. R. Ambedkar :- 
 Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar  was born in 14th April 1891. He was also known as Babasaheb..
He was an Indian jurist, economist, politician, social reformar, writer and dalit leader.
In 1990, he was awarded with Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award...
Since 1948, Ambedkar suffered from diabetes. His health worsened during 1955.
      He died in his sleep on 6 December 1956 at his home in delhi.
