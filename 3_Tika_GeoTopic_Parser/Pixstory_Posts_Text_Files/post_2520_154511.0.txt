Amitabh was given the name Inquilaab Srivastava on his birth but Was changed later.
After Anand he faced constant failure and then Zanjeer happened. He never looked back. He had a major accident while shooting for ‘Coolie’ and his survival was a miracle. But his return to movies was not very successful. After a brief stint in movies he became a producer and his venture was a commercial failure and huge financial dues piled on him. But Luck and ‘Kaun Banega A Crorepati’ gave him a third innings.
