After being stranded due to an engine problem in his Aston Martin Mercedes during the first free practice of the Australian GP, Sebastian Vettel was the protagonist of a curious little scene after the end of the session, the German borrowed a scooter from one of the course marshals choosing to return to the pits not as usually happens through the service lanes but directly from the track waving to the ecstatic fans A stunt that cost him a fine from the FIA Seb had not in fact been authorized to enter the circuit
