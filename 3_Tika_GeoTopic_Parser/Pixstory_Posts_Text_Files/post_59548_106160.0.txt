Live Case Updates from Covid India: According to data published on Friday by the Union Health Ministry, India's COVID-19 case count grew by 7,584 in a day to 4,32,05,106, with 36,267 active cases. According to figures updated at 8 a.m., the death toll has risen to 5,24,747, with 24 more deaths. The number of active cases has risen to 3,769, accounting for 0.08 percent of all infections, according to the health ministry, while the national COVID-19 recovery rate has reached 98.70 percent. The daily positivity rate was found to be 2.26 percent, whereas the weekly positivity rate was 1.50 percent.
