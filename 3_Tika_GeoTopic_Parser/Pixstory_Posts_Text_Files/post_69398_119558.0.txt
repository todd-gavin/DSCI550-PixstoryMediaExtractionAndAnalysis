Pooja Devi, the first female bus and truck driver from Kathua in Jammu and Kashmir, rose to fame after Union Minister Dr. Jitendra Sing posted a photo of her on Twitter on December 25, 2020, which quickly went viral. "Proud to have from district Kathua, Jammu And Kashmir, the first women bus driver Pooja Devi," stated the post's caption. 

A former heavy vehicle driver who advocated for women's emancipation established herself as a role model in Jammu and Kashmir's Kathua area. 

Pooja Devi has worked as a bus and truck driver in the past, and most recently, she started a store in the Kathua district.
