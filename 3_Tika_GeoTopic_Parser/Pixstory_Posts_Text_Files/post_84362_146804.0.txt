The fourth charity match between the Sidemen FC (SDMN) and YouTube All-Stars (YTAS) will be taking place on Saturday, 24th September, 2022.
The previous charity match took place in 2018, with the Sidemen winning 7-1, leading the series 2-1.
Can the YouTube All-Stars catch up? Or will the Sidemen FC pull ahead? 
Only time will tell.
