The edition of MONFERRATO ON STAGE is coming, the traveling review that through culture and entertainment wants to enhance and make the Monferrato area increasingly known. July will then be the turn of FABRIZIO MORO and the young singer-songwriter MATTEO ROMANO who will take turns on the stage in Piazza Cattedrale in ASTI The first two appointments of the new edition of the review to which other events organized with the support of the participating municipalities will be added mark the beginning the collaboration with Astimusica which has included the concerts of Gianluca Grignani Fabrizio Moro and Matteo Romano in its program
