From the artist of what's wrong with secretary Kim comes another office Romance series  An hour Of Romance.

Dojin is a clumsy absent minded klutz who by sheer luck , managed to get a job at a big company. His boss Jooahn is serious, business Lady who doesn't tolerate nonsense. But when these two very different individuals swap boodies due to an unexpected temple visit , chaos breaks through. 

What follows are a series of embarrassments and messups . As they forced to swap boodies for an hour a day they come closer and cooperate . Will both of them understand each other and perhaps trail down on the path of love ?
