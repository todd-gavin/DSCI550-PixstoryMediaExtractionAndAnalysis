36th National Games: Football Results - 9th October
Kerala and West Bengal registered victories in their semi-final matches against Karnataka and West Bengal, respectively to make it to the summit clash of the Men’s Football event. The two sides will lock horns on Tuesday, October 11 in pursuit of gold. Meanwhile, the semi-finalists will battle for the bronze medal. 
Men’s
Kerala beat Karnataka 2-0
West Bengal beat Services Sports Control Board 1-0
