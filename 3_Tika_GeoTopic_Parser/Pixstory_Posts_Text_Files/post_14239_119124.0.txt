Juve are about to leave California, but only momentarily, for a two-day stopover in Texas, where tomorrow evening (on Wednesday morning in Italy) they will face Barcelona in the second friendly on US soil.

The team worked early in the morning to be ready to leave promptly in the afternoon. Today's session included pre-game tactical work with a focus on situations that could arise from set pieces.


PAUL POGBA UPDATE

Following complaining of pain in his right knee, Paul Pogba underwent radiological examinations that revealed a lesion of the lateral meniscus. In the next few hours he will undergo a specialist orthopedic consultation. Therefore, in order to continue treatment, he is not taking part in the trip to Dallas.
