Famous local folklore singer, vocalist, and harmonium player Ustad Salaam Logari has died at the age of 86 after being bedridden for years. He sang and performed original local Logari songs for four decades. He was very popular and was once awarded the title of Ustadi by the government.

According to his relatives, Ustad Logari has been bedridden in recent years.

He was laid to rest on Saturday in Mohammad Agha district of Logar province.

Salam Logari was 86 years old, and he was awarded the title of Ustadi by the Ministry of Information and Culture.

Ustad Salam Logari was famous for performing original Logari songs in Afghanistan and abroad, and most people are familiar with his name and voice.
