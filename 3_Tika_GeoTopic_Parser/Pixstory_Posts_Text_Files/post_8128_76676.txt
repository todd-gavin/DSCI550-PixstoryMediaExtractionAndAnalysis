The sixth transfer of humanitarian aid in US dollars from the United Nations has arrived in Afghanistan Da Afghanistan Bank said on Monday On Sunday the aid totaling million was immediately handed over to the Afghanistan International Bank AIB Da Afghanistan Bank the central bank has thanked the UN for its assistance in helping to overcome the current economic crisis in the country Last month the United Nations announced it would provide million a week in cash as humanitarian aid to Afghanistan by the end of March this year to save the banking system from running out of money The Central Bank of Afghanistan announced four days ago that it had also received the fifth package of million in cash from the United Nations After the withdrawal of US troops from Afghanistan the United States froze nearly billion in foreign reserves belonging to Afghanistan
