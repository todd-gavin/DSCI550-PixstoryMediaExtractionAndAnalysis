Loewe ready to make its return to the Milan Furniture Fair for the seventh time with a project dedicated to repairing through the art of craftsmanship Entitled Weave Restore Renew the project revolves around the idea of giving a new life to everything that would otherwise be forgotten or thrown Thus all the mastery of Spanish craftsmanship is recovered and reused in a new and unexpected way As part of the exhibition the Repaired in Spain collection will be launched consisting of baskets repaired by craftsmen which will be available for purchase from Bergdorf Goodman
