Professor Roop Rekha Verma (79) is seen in a viral video on the streets of Lucknow, the birthplace of the 1857 revolution, keeping the spirit of the revolution alive. She was previously the vice-chancellor of Lucknow University. Verma's age was given as 77 in a post that praised her efforts. Breaking stereotypes about women wanting to hide their age. Prof. Roop Rekha Verma was seen distributing pamphlets to people on the streets that shed light on the freedom struggle known as the Lucknow Seige. The pamphlets are distributed solely to keep the freedom revolution alive and to raise awareness about Lucknow's glorious history. The Lucknow Seige was a battle between Indian rebel sepoys and the British.
