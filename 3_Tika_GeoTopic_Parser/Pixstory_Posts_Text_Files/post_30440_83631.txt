The echoes of the war between Ukraine and Russia are also felt in the world of F The American team Haas communicated on Thursday evening the decision to remove the colors of the Russian flag and the logo of the sponsor Uralkali from the livery of its single-seater. Haas will therefore take to the track on the last day of winter testing essentially with a white livery. The team led by the South Tyrolean Gunther Steiner is in a difficult situation, with American ownership, but a good portion of the Russian budget, as well as the Russian driver Nikita Mazepin who, with his presence, guarantees also economic revenues such as that of the sponsor Uralkali The fate of the Russian GP scheduled for the end of September in Sochi is also uncertain Sebastian Vettel has already said that he will not participate but the race could be removed from the calendar shortly
