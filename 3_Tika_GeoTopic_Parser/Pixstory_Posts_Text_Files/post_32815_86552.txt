The first of three days of Formula winter testing in Bahrain took place on the Sakhir track After the three days in Barcelona two weeks ago, the Formula circus met again to prepare for the new season. The fastest at the end of the first day was the Frenchman Pierre Gasly of AlphaTauri with a time set on the soft tire and the Ferraris of Carlos Sainz and Charles Leclerc who stopped respectively a and tenths from Gasly but using a slower medium compound The Red Bull big names were hidden laps for Perez today and Mercedes which presented a car with revolutionary solutions which, however, also aroused some protests because they were not in line with the spirit of the regulation
