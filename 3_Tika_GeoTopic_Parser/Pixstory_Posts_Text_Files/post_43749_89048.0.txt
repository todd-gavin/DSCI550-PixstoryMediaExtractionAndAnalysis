The video of the young goalkeeper scoring from within his own half has been viewed over a million times on the internet.

A video of Charlie Clarke, an Under-12s goalkeeper, striking a long-range winner in the final minute of a cup quarter-final has gone viral.

At the weekend, Gedling Southbank U12 Panthers goalkeeper Clarke came out of his net to hit a first-time shot into the opposition net from inside his own half, eliciting ecstatic cheers from the onlookers.

Charlie's incredible goal was filmed by his father Gary, and the video has been seen over one million times online.
