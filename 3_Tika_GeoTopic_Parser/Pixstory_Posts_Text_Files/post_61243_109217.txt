Simple yet delicious this omelette enough for breakfast Serve with lemony greens to make the meal complete Ingredients tbsp olive oil yellow onion finely chopped large eggs salt tbsp unsalted butter oz Parmesan finely grated tbsp fresh lemon juice oz baby spinach Directions to make omelette Heat tablespoon oil in large nonstick skillet on medium Add onion and saut until tender about minutes Transfer to a small bowl In a large bowl whisk together eggs tablespoon water and teaspoon salt Return skillet to medium and add butter Add eggs and cook stirring constantly with rubber spatula until eggs are partially set Turn heat to low and cover pan tightly cooking until eggs are just set to minutes Top with Parmesan and cooked onion fold in half In a medium bowl whisk together lemon juice and remaining tablespoons olive oil Toss spinach with vinaigrette and serve with omelet And then you re done Do try this recipe at your home and enjoy your breakfast
