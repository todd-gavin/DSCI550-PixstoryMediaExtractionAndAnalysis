India bears world s highest health economic burden due to lead poisoning Centre A report by the United Nations Children s Fund Unicef and non profit Pure Earth had found India to be home to a major chunk of the million children poisoned by lead globally Now a central report has found that India bears the world s highest health and economic burden due to lead poisoning The report prepared jointly by government think tank Niti Aayog and the Council of Scientific amp Industrial Research CSIR was published in July this year A meeting was organised by Pure Earth and The Energy and Resource Institute in the national capital October to discuss the findings of the report
