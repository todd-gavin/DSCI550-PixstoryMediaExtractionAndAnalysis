Prime Minister Liz Truss who was appointed by the Queen on Tuesday said the monarch was the rock on which modern Britain was built who had provided us with the stability and strength that we needed Speaking about the new King she said We offer him our loyalty and devotion just as his mother devoted so much to so many for so long And with the passing of the second Elizabethan age we usher in a new era in the magnificent history of our great country exactly as Her Majesty would have wished by saying the words God save the King The Archbishop of Canterbury Justin Welby spiritual leader to the Church of England of which the monarch is supreme governor expressed his profound sadness He said his prayers are with the King and the Royal Family
