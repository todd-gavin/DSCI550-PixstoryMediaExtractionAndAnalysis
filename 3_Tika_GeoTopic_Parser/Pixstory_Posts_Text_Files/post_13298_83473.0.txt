The Saudi women's national team celebrated its first appearance on the football field with a 2-0 victory over Seychelles. The game will be played in a small tournament in the Maldives and Saudi Arabia will face the Maldives in the next game. The Saudi women's national football team is still awaiting official FIFA approval.
