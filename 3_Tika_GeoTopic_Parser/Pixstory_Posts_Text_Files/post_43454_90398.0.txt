Zendaya’s Oscars 2022 outfit shines as bright as she does.

The actress, 25, hit Sunday’s red carpet looking every inch the sophisticated style star.

The Dune star sported a Valentino Haute Couture look consisting of a white silk button-up crop top with a silver sequined maxi skirt, and added the bling with piles of silver and diamond bracelets and silver chrome nails.

Dune, a sci-fi film has been nominated for 10 Oscars which also includes Best Picture. So far, Dune bags 6 awards.
