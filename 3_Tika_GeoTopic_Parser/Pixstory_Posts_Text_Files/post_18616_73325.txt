From 1st July, at the behest of Fifa, there will be a new regulation regarding the loan of players. All with the aim of developing young players, promoting competitive balance and preventing hoarding. Among the new rules, for example, there is the obligation of a written agreement. defining the terms of the loan in particular its duration and financial conditions a ban on sub-borrowing a professional player who is already on loan and again a limitation on the number of loans per season between the same clubs The new regulation will be presented to the FIFA Council for approval at its next meeting in view of their entry into force These new rules are part of a larger project started in 2010 on the player transfer system Covid has only slowed down the process of change
