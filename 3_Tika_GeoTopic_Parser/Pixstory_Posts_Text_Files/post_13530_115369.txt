A number of citizens of Jaghori district of Ghazni Province organized a book reading competition on the days of Eid al Adha in order to spread the knowledge and culture of reading in this district A group of male and female students participated in this competition This book reading competition was organized with the support of an institution called Javad Amiri Charitable Foundation and the local people welcomed it In the video that the organizers of this book reading competition have published on YouTube three people are going to be chosen as the winners of the competition and will receive prizes from the organizers Jaghori is one of the largest districts of Ghazni province This district has seen many book reading competitions before Male and female students participated in this reading competition It has been more than days that the Taliban have prevented the reopening of girls secondary schools in Afghanistan and have ignored domestic and international protests in this regard
