UNICEF donates refrigerated vans to Ukraine to transport vaccines to all regions of the country The first vehicles were delivered by UNICEF Director-General Catherine M Russell during her official visit Vaccines in Ukraine are transported and stored in strict compliance with manufacturer-specified requirements despite challenges to the healthcare system caused by the large-scale invasion of Russia the involvement in this process of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention of the Ministry of Health of Ukraine to use immunoprophylaxis more effectively This way we will be able to ensure uninterrupted access to vaccines in every single health facility The first pilot delivery of vaccines to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention will start shortly in the Lviv region said the Minister of Health of Ukraine Viktor Liashko The supplied refrigerated vans are equipped with special cooling equipment with a transport capacity of cubic meters of vaccines
