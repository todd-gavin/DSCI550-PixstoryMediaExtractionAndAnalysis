Quiche Fran aise A quiche is a French pie that has a pastry shell that is filled with salted cream and pieces of cheese pork fish or vegetables Quiche Lorraine a well-known variation contains lardons or Bacon You can serve the quiche ti cold or hot The main ingredients include a pie crust filled with eggs and cheese meat vegetables or seafood Its recipe was first tested at the end years There are many variations of the dish The shelf life of a quiche is days if refrigerated It is part of French cuisine but is also popular in other countries especially as a festive food La classic French quiche originated in Germany in the medieval kingdom of Lothringen which was under German rule but the dish was renamed Lorraine when it came under French control
