Domestic natural gas prices will be more than doubled to USD per million British thermal unit mmBtu effective from April from the current USD per mmBtu Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell PPAC of the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas announced on Thursday The new price will be effective for the first six months of the financial year In accordance with Para of the New Domestic Natural Gas Pricing Guidelines issued vide Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas Govt of India F No O ONG D V dated the price of domestic natural gas for the period st April to th September is MMBtu on the Gross Caloric Value GCV basis Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell said in a release Read more
