Imran Khan is the first Pakistan Prime Minister to get ousted through a non confidence motion in parliament. 

His 21-year cricketing career  overshadowed his 26-year political journey. He never missed a cha to treat almost all Opposition leaders with disdain. 

Then, they all came together at the behest of the real power centre in Pakistan and showed him the door.

He first bowled a no ball. Then said would play till the last ball. That he did.
