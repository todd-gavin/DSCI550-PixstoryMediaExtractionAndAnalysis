The Afghan men s and women s national snowboard teams won two gold and one bronze medal in the Pakistan tournament The Afghan men s and women s national snowboard teams won two gold and one bronze medal in the Pakistan tournament The Afghan men s national team won the gold medal in the individual event according to the Afghan Shafaqna news agency On the other hand the women s national team also won a bronze medal in the individual section of the competition The tournament was held from February to in Pakistan controlled Kashmir
