China has developed the ability to collect and analyze nearly millions of tubes of molecular test samples per day according to the Chinese news agency, reporting the words of representatives of the National Health Commission. Currently, these tests for Covid are carried out by technicians in institutions Commission official Guo Yanhong says Chinese residents of major cities can find a collection site just minutes from home thanks to significant advances in the past two years, added Guo Local authorities have been encouraged to employ more than just doctors and testing nurses but also people with relevant technical qualifications, Guo added
