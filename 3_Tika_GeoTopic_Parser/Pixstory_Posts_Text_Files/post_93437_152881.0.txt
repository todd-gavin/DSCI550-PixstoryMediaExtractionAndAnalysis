The struggle:
From being the most popular to being the most hated person in her locality, Pallavi had to go through an emotional trauma fighting the guilt, embarrassment, hate and sorrow as soon as her identity got revealed to people. She didn't only have to fight society but also her family. 
The message: 
This is a very impactful portrayal of how Pallavi came in terms with her own sexuality and how her family accepted her as who she was getting over the denial phase. The matter of the fact is whether a lesbian or a gay or bisexual, or transgender person, or queer, one must not change themselves to fit in.
