The Central government has decided to examine the issue of fake reviews of products and services being used by e commerce companies to mislead consumers into buying goods on their platforms The government is keen to ensure that the reviews being posted are by consumers who had actually used the product or service that was reviewed To gauge the magnitude of fake reviews on E Commerce platforms which mislead consumers into buying online products or services and to prepare a roadmap ahead the Department of Consumer Affairs DoCA will be holding a virtual meeting on Friday th May along with various stakeholders according to an official statement issued on Thursday Read more
