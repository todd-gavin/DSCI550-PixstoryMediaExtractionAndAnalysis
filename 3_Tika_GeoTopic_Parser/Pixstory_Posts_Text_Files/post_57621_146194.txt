We all have heard people talking about a person having distinct personalities often trivialising it by mocking or making fun of the whole concept But split personality is not as trivial as it seems it s a disorder that might have severe repercussions if not taken care of on time Most people are unaware of the scientific term which defines it split personality disorder is called a dissociative identity disorder sometimes also known as multiple personality disorder It is a mental health condition where people have two or more separate personalities These identities control a person s behaviour at different times As a result DID can cause gaps in memory and other problems Various types of psychotherapy can help people manage the symptoms of DID These different personalities affect the person s identity and take control of it A person having DID maintains their primary or host identity which is their original personality and will answer to their given name Their primary identity is generally more passive and they may be unaware of the other personalities
