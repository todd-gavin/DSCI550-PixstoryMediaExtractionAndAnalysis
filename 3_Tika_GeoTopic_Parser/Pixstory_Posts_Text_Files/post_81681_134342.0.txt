Shenaaz Gill to star with John Abraham, Ritesh Deshmukh and Nora Fatehi. 
Housefull director is back with a comedy film starring Ek villian return actor John Abraham, Ritesh Deshmukh and Nora Fatehi and popular reality show contestant who had entertained everyone Shenaaz Gill. The film will be produced by Bhushan Kumar and Amar Butala. The film will be titled as '100%.'
The sajid khan film is set against the backdrop of big fat indian wedding and the crazy world of spies. It promise to be full ride of joy, comedy, action and chaos. 100% is expected to go on floor in the first half of 2023 and makers are planning to release it on Diwali 2023. 
Nora Fatehi is currently seen as judge in Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa. Ritesh deshmukh was last seen as the public prosecutor in Amazon mini-tv show, Shenaaz Gill will also be seen in Kisi ka bhai kisi ki jaan with salman khan.
