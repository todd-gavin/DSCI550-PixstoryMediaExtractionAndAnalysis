St. Alberts College, Ernakulam in association with Albertian Institute of Management Conducted Inter-departmental competition on Indian economic summit on 19th of August. Each department was allotted respective selected states and they needed to present PowerPoint Presentation based on the culture, customs, art forms and attractions of the state and organize cusines to taste the flavours of ethnic food and dishes of the respective state and finally the contestants needed to perform traditional dance to glitter the souls of the Judges and the audience. The summit was really overwhelming and stupendous to acknowledge and interpret the economic, social and political dimensions of different states of india.
