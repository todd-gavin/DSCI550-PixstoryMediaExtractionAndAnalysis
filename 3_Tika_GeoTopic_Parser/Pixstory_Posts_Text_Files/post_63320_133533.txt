Love a rose flavored drink There is nothing better than a summer afternoon and a glass of Pink Lemonade You can drink rose anytime Pink Lemonade or Rose Lemonade it s a refreshing drink with just a few ingredients to beat the heat Ingredients tbsp rose syrup tbsp lemon juice tsp sumac powder cups chilled water or soda Pour the rose syrup and lemon juice into a serving pitcher Add cups of cold water and taste Add more water if needed Refrigerate to minutes serve with lots of ice cubes and fresh mint leaves sprinkle some sumac on top and you re done
