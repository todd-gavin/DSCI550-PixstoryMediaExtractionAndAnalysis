Salvador Ramos 18-yr-old killed 19 children and 2 adults at Robb Elementary School in Texas on Tuesday. 
Ramos was a student at Uvalde High school . He hinted about the attack on social media by posting picture of semi automatic guns and suggested kids to watch out. 

He also shot his grandmother before driving to Robb Elementary just before midday on Tuesday. She is in critical condition right now.

Police said that he was wearing a body armour . He started shooting children, teachers whoever's in his way as soon as he made his entry into the school however he was later killed by the police .

Joe Biden reacting on this incident told the nation it was time to "turn this pain into action" and change gun laws following this massacre.
