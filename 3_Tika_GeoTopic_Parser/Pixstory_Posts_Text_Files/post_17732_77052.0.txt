Jake Gyllenhaal is set to star in a speedboat Heist movie named " Cut & Run"
John Glenn has written the speculative script of the movie, Brian Oliver and Bradley Fisher's New Republic is the production behind the movie. Apart from starring in the movie, Jake Gyllenhaal will also be producing in the movie.
New Republic picked up the spec script in a competitive bidding race. 
The logline of the movie is, “A group of thieves using high-powered speedboats to rob superyachts steal the wrong thing from the wrong group of people.”
