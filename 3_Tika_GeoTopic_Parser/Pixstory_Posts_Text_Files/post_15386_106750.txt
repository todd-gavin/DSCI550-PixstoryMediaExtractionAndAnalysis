The last meeting of the authorities of the Po basin certified the extent of the problem of drought in the river, which has never been so dry in recent years. To deal with the emergency, withdrawals have begun from Lake Garda towards the Po. Moreover, the snow in the Alps is totally exhausted in Piedmont e Lombardia and the lakes starting from Maggiore are at historic lows for the period except for Garda The crops are suffering and the irrigation plan had to be postponed by days Utilitalia representing the integrated water service multi-utilities confirms that the focus above all on resource supplies for drinking water, a hundred Municipalities in Piedmont and Lombardy were asked to the mayors to prepare eventual nocturnal suspensions to replenish the levels of the tanks with ordinances aimed at an extremely sparing use of water
