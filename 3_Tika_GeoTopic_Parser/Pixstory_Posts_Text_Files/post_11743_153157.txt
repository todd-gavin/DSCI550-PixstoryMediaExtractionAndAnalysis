 Do you want a nice and a healthy meal after you get back home from your busy schedule Then here s a recipe of granola which might be suitable for your craving Ingredients cups old fashioned rolled oats cup raw nuts and or seeds teaspoon fine grain sea salt teaspoon ground cinnamon cup melted coconut oil or olive oil cup maple syrup or honey teaspoon vanilla extract cup dried fruit chopped if large Optional add in cup chocolate chips or coconut flakes Directions Preheat the oven to degrees Fahrenheit and place a large sheet with parchment paper Take a large bowl and combine the oats nuts and or seeds salt and cinnamon Stir them to blend Pour in oil maple syrup or honey and vanilla Mix them till every oat and nut is lightly coated Transfer it to an pan and spread it out Bake them till they turn golden brown for like about to minutes and just mix them in between with a spatula The granola will become more crisp as it cools down Let the granola cool down completely Top it with some dried fruits break the granola with your hand or stir it with a spoon Sources cookie Kate
