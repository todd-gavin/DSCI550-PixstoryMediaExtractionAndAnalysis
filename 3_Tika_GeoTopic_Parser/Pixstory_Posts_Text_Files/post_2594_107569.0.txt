Today is the birth anniversary of Hemant Kumar. 
He started singing in 1933 at age of 13, but his voice and singing were nurtured under the Great Faiyaz Khan. Hemant learnt music from Khan Saheb till 1950 till death of Ustad. 
Though he was popular in Bangla movies, it was success of Anand Math in 1952 which brought him recognition. His greatest success was Nagin.
As a musician his association with SD Burman and Dev Anand brought him fame. 
Kalyanji and Ravi were both his assistants.
Khamoshi, Anupama, Bees Saal Baad are evidence of virtuosity. 
Like many before he faced bankruptcy when he produced a movie. 
Mousimi Chatterjee is his daughter in law
