Zarif Baba, an Afghan youth who claimed to be a religious leader, has been killed in Maharashtra.
According to the police, this 35-year-old man was shot in the forehead by four unknown persons on Tuesday and died instantly.

Zarif Baba considered himself from the Cheshtiyya dynasty, which has many followers in Afghanistan and the Indian subcontinent.

According to the Indian police, the motive behind Zarif Baba's murdered is not known and the attackers have managed to escape.
Investigations have been started to identify and arrest the attackers.
