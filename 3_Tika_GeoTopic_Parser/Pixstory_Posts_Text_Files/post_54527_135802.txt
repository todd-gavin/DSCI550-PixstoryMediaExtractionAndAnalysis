appreciated Waste pickers often work without protective gear unassisted and without access to primary care or first aid and employment regulations They operate on the margins of or outside the formal process of managing solid waste but play vital roles especially in reuse recycling and cost recovery They work in unsheltered environments and are unprotected from severe heat sun rain and cold weather These conditions have been linked with cardiovascular disorders Likewise exposure to dust micro organisms and microbial toxins can result in chronic respiratory diseases skin problems and
