Only a few people have saved as many lives as Nils Bohlin a Volvo engineer who invented the contemporary three point seat belt in Despite the fact that the concept was patented the business opted to leave the patent open allowing every vehicle manufacturer to use it for free This fairly unusual choice was made in the interest of public safety so that everyone may be safer in traffic regardless of whether they drove a Volvo or not This decision turned out to be extremely helpful to the entire world In today s world three point seat belts are a legal necessity in all cars trucks and buses As a result every vehicle on the road has a small but vital piece of Volvo technology The use of a seat belt can be the difference between life and death in an accident According to reports if the driver and passengers strap up their chances of surviving a catastrophic road collision are doubled
