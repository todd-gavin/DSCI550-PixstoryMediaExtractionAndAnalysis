Assam Chief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma has denied allegations made by two activists that he Sadhguru Jaggi Vasudev and Tourism Minister Jayanta Malla Baruah violated wildlife protection laws by going on a night safari in Kaziranga National Park Two activists in Assam have filed a police complaint against Sadhguru Mr Sarma and Mr Baruah alleging that they stayed much longer than the scheduled visit time on Saturday in the national park which is home to the Indian one horned rhino The activists claimed that the Wildlife Protection Act of prohibits safari tours within the national park after a certain time in order to protect the animals and preserve their nature
