Defence Minister Rajnath Singh said today that the government has taken a "serious note" of the recent incident relating to inadvertent release of a missile into Pakistani territory during inspection.

The incident took place on March 9 when, during routine maintenance and inspection, a missile was accidentally released at around 7 PM.
