Oda, the author of One Piece finally confirmed that this is the start of the final arc of One Piece. He took a four week break for the first time after the Marineford arc which was 10 years ago. Just before the release of this newest chapter of the manga, he had a message for the fans. He said that he's finally going to reveal every little secret of the One Piece world. The chapter 1054 released today and it's taking everyone by storm, fan favourite and rarely appearing Shanks is seen and he's finally going after the One Piece. This final arc is going to be one for the ages and probably has the hype and story to become the greatest arc in the history of Manga.
