Besides Lee Jong Suk and Yoona, the third main character of "Big Mouth', antagonist Choi Do Ha, played by Kim Joo Hun is also leaving a great impression on viewers.
MBC's Friday-Saturday drama "Big Mouth' recorded a viewership rating of 8.7% in the metropolitan area and 8.6% nationwide (Nielsen Korea paid households). "Big Mouth", which premiered on July 29th and started with a nationwide rating of 6.2%, briefly slowed down to 6.1% in episode 2, but later rose to 7.6% and 8.5%.

After breaking its own highest ratings successively, it took first place in terms of TV topicality among Friday-Saturday dramas for 2 weeks in a row. (Good Data Corporation)
