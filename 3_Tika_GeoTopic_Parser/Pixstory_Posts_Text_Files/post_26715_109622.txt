When designed by Glenn Martens, the clothes come to life and fly or cling to the human body like perturbing yellow boas. Tank tops and very thin silk dresses are held up on the bust thanks to invisible shoulder straps that magically disappear while the belts twist and move like the fingers of the masters dancers of the art of Kathakali The work on the trompe oeil effect begun with the collection in collaboration with Jean Paul Gaultier continues, as does the study of denim and its experimentation. element of feeble comfort in front of the surprising and all-encompassing talent of the Belgian creative
