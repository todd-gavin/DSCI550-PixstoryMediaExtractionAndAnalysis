Teachers Day is celebrated in India on the birth anniversary of India s second President Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan A teacher s job is not just to educate students but also to teach them important life lessons While all of us have had such influential Gurus in real life there are portrayals in Bollywood that have driven home this point Let s have a look at some of the best mentors on screen Ram Shankar Nikumbh in Taare Zameen Par Aamir Khan essayed the role of Ram Shankar Nikumbh a fun loving and caring teacher in Taare Zameen Par who helps a dyslexic child Ishaan played by Darsheel Safary find innovative ways of learning in the film which was also directed by the Laal Singh Chaddha star Kabir Khan in Chak De India Shah Rukh Khan as coach of the Indian women s hockey team Kabir Khan inspires the players to put aside their differences and play for the country as one unit The Shimit Amin directorial Chak De India is one of the best sporting films in the history of Indian cinema Debraj Sahai in Black This Sanjay Leela Bhansali directorial showed how a mentor can help you lead a meaningful life Amitabh Bachchan played an eccentric Debraj Sahai who supports his deaf and blind student named Michelle McNally played by Rani Mukerji at every step in this film Anand Kumar in Super Hrithik Roshan portrayed the real life mathematician Anand Kumar who starts an initiative named Super to teach poor students for JEE Advanced examination in the Vikas Bahl directorial Mrunal Thakur Pankaj Tripathi and Vijay Varma play pivotal supporting roles Dr Jehangir Khan in Dear Zindagi Shah Rukh Khan played Alia Bhatt s character Kaira s mentor Dr Jehangir Jug Khan and helps her find answers to her life s problems in this coming of age drama film directed by Gauri Shinde Kunal Kapoor Angad Bedi and Ali Zafar are also seen in small yet important roles
