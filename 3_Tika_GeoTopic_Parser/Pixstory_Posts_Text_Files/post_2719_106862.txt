Today is birth anniversary of Prem Dhawan He was a lyricist music director and choreographer in the Hindi film industry Born in Ambala he learnt classical music from Pandit Ravi Shankar whom he met in Mumbai while he was with the Indian People s Theatre Association As a lyricist he debuted with Dharti Ke Lal and went on to write song lyrics till the s Some of the notable films he worked for as lyricist are Aaram Tarana Aasman Taangewali Shola Aur Shabnam Kabuliwala Ek Phool Do Mali and Purab Aur Pachhim With songs like Aye Mere Pyaare Watan Kabuliwala and Chodo Kal Ki Baatein Hum Hindustani Prem Dhawan came to be associated with patriotic songs It was around the mid s that his association with Manoj Kumar started With Shaheed both started a new phase of their careers Manoj Kumar as the patriotic hero and Prem Dhawan as the music director lyricist The fim Shaheed and its songs specially Ae Watan Ae Watan and Mera Rang De Basanti Chola were very successful and paved the way for many more collaborations between the two songs to remember him Mesmerising number of Lata Haye jiya roye set beautifully in Raag Darbari by Hansraj Behl A Salil Chaudhary composition Dhirang Dhitang dole both hindi and bangla versions sung Lata and Hemant kumar Le chala jidhar ye dil Ajit as the hero Sab kuch luta ke hosh mein aaye by Lata And of course Mera rang de basanti chola
