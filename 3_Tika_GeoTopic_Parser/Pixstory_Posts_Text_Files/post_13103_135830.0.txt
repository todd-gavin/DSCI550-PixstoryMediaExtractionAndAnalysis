As a national identity and one of the most obvious ethnic manifestations in Afghanistan, local costumes play an important role in maintaining the social identity of this country's society and are closely related to the cultural, moral and spiritual values ​​and norms of its various ethnic groups.
The use of indigenous and traditional clothing decreased in the 1980s and 1990s, but now it has increased significantly. Men's and women's clothes with variety in sewing and selection of different types, shapes and designs with different traditional styles can be found in Afghan shops.
This photo is from 2019. From a Traditional dance group of Afghan refugees on World Refugees Day. They had a very beautiful performance. Each nationality represented a Traditional dance, a Traditional dress, customs and traditions of the country came to the stage, among which the Afghan  Refugees won the first place.
