Dhanush and Aishwaryaa Rajinikanth had shocked everyone when they announced their divorce in January this year. The two had also put out a statement confirming that they were ending their 18-year-long marriage. But it seems like the ex couple are now planning to call off their divorce after 9 months of separation. According to several reports, Dhanush and Aishwaryaa will be trying to make things work between them and save their marriage. Both families have been trying to convince the two to get back together.
