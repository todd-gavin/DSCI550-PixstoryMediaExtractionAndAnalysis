For Sampdoria came the third consecutive defeat in the league After the last against Salernitana in the standings, they brought the blucerchiati closer to the relegation zone, now only points away And the protest from the fans arrived on time, which began when at the end of the game the players approached the stands to greet the public The dispute also continued outside the stadium The bus with the players and coach Gianpaolo was stopped by the cars of the fans parked in the street before arriving at the hotel for the training camp There was a confrontation with the older members of the group who promised fans ready for redemption starting from the next match In the evening it seems that the management also spoke about the future with the coach who for the moment would not be risking the bench
