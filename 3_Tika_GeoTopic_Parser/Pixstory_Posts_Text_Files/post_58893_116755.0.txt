India once more makes history! Congratulations to everyone in India on reaching the historic milestone of 200 crore vaccination doses. Proud of those who helped India's immunisation campaign achieve unprecedented levels of scale and speed, said PM Modi.

On Sunday, the nation was congratulated by the prime minister and the union minister of health for completing 200 crore Covid-19 vaccinations. Data from the health ministry show that 98% of adults have taken at least one dose, and 90% have received all recommended doses.

The data revealed that 82% of teenagers between the ages of 15 and 18 have received the first dosage of the vaccine since the vaccination programme for this age group began on January 3, while 68% have received both the first and second doses.
