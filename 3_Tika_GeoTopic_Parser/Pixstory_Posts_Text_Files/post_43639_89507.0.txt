(G)I-DLE has achieved a perfect all-kill with their latest release "TOMBOY"!

On March 24 at 2:30 p.m. KST, Instiz's iChart announced that (G)I-DLE's "TOMBOY" had scored a perfect all-kill, making them the first group to achieve this feat in 2022.

A song is awarded a certified all-kill when it is No. 1 on the daily and 24Hits charts of Melon, the daily and realtime charts of Genie and Bugs, VIBE's daily chart, and the realtime charts of FLO and iChart. A perfect all-kill means the song has also topped iChart's weekly chart.
