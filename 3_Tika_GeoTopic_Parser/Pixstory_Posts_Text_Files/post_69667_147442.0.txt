BTS to release 2022 FIFA World Cup Song, reinterpretation of 'Yet To Come' on 23rd Sept.
Hyundai Motor Company announced that they'll release "Yet To Come (Hyundai Ver.)" on Sep. 23 at 6PM KST through streaming platforms such as Spotify and Apple Music, along with a full MV on Hyundai's official YouTube Channel. "Yet To Come (Hyundai Ver.)" is a British rock remix version of BTS' "Yet To Come" which emphasizes the climax to deliver the cheering message for 'Goal of the Century' and anticipation toward the World Cup!
