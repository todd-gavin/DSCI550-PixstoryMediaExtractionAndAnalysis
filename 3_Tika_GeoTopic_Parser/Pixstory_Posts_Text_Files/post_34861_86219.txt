According to ESPN there would already be three offers for the Chelsea club put up for sale by the Russian Abramovich after the outbreak of war in Ukraine One of the entrepreneurs interested in the Blues Champions League and Club World Cup holders would be Robert Johnson owner of the New York Jets professional team of american football of the NFL Johnson, again according to the US television broadcaster, has already spoken to the Raine Group, the company that is handling the sale of Chelsea, even if apparently it has not yet made any offers One of the questions is that of the value of the club and the request for Abramovich The latter would like a figure of around billion euros but the value would not exceed one and a half billion Robert Johnson was very close to London in the past having been ambassador of the United States
