Hard work and grit has paid off for talented 19-year-old Neeraj Chauhan, the son of a cook, as he has been selected for India’s archery team and will represent the country for the World Cup and the Asian Games.

Neeraj and his brother, a talented boxer, were at one point reduced to selling vegetables by the roadside to make a living as his father had lost his job as a cook at the mess in Meerut’s Kailash Prakash Stadium due to the Covid pandemic.
