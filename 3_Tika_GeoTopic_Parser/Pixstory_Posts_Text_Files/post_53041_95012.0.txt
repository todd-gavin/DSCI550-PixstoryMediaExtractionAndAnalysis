Vibha Dhawan is Director General of TERI - The Energy and Resources Institute based in New Delhi, which specializes in the fields of energy, environment, and sustainable development.

She has written extensively on biotechnology, biofuels, and biocommodities, and was a bioresources and biotechnology advisor to Tarun Gogoi, the late former Chief Minister of Assam.
