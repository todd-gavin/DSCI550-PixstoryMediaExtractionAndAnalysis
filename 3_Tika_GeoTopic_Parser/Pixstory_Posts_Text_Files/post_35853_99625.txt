Spezia Calcio announces that the diagnostic tests to which the player Simone Bastoni was subjected to premature exit from the match against Atalanta showed a dislocation of the acromion clavier of his left shoulder The player will undergo surgery which will be performed tomorrow afternoon at the hospital San Raffaele di Milano from Prof Vincenzo Salini This is the official press release issued in these hours by the Spezia company regarding the conditions of its defender Bastoni The footballer will in fact have to undergo an operation on his left shoulder for the dislocation remedied in the match against Atalanta
