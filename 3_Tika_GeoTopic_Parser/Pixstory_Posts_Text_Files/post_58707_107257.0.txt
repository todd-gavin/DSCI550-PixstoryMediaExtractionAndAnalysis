The Convocation function of the St . Mother Teresa University , established for Digital Education Excellence and sustainability development and World Peace , was celebrated at the Constitution Club in New Delhi. The function was followed by an international Conference on the Digital Educational Excellence and Appreciation Awards Ceremony. Former Union Cabinet Minister and Chairman of the University, Dr Krishna Kumar Das and Pro VC, Dr Vijaya Saraswathy graced the occasion and gave awards to the passouts of university and participants of conference
