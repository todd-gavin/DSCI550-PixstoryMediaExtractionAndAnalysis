Hadrian&#39;s Wall one of the world&#39;s finest in antiquity a massive stone fortification that separated the Roman Empire from the Pictish territories In practice it defined the northern border of the Roman Empire in Britain Now a stop on the way to Scotland&#39;s capital Edinburgh or to Glasgow is the largest city in the country.
