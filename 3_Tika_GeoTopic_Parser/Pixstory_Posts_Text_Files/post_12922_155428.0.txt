Neeraj Chopra on October 13 was felicitated by the Indian Army for his historic victory at the Diamond League Final 2022.
The Indian Army subedar was accorded a grand reception by the his regiment – the Rajputana Rifles – at the RAJ RIF Regimental Centre in the national capital. He was also felicitated by Lieutanant General CP Cariappa, Colonel of the Rajputana Rifles Regiment.
It was Chopra's another historic victory after winning gold in Olympics as he's the first Indian to win the prestigious Diamond League Finals title in Zurich, Switzerland last month.
