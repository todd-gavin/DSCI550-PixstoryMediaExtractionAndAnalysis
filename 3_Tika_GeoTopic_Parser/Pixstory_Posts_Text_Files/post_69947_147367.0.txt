Dealing with anxiety!😬
The 5,4,3,2,1 Method
When feeling anxious or panicked think of:
5  things you can see
4  things you can hear
3  things you can touch 
2 things you can smell
1  thing you can taste
This method helps you for instant distraction from your anxiety!
The 4,7,8 Method
To calm yourself:
Breath in 4 seconds
Hold your breath for 7 seconds
Breath out for 8 seconds 
This method personally really helpful to feel serenity, just try it when feel panicked or uncomfortable and you immediately can feel the coolness of the mind 🌼🍂
