After the Visarjan of Maa Durga the bengalis celebrate Bijoya It literally means victory We celebrate the victory of good over evil We touch elder s feet taking their blessings People of same age do Kolakuli a hugging gesture Sweets are distributed Special sweets called naru are small balls made out of jaggery and coconut Gaja fried flaky dough dipped in sugar syrup and nimki a savoury snack are made For a month long this celebration continues as people wish each other Shubho Bijoya and feed sweets
