SLSuri DAV Public Sr. Sec. School, Janakpuri New Delhi organised a special assembly. Highlights of the programme were the havan, pledge on not to use single plastic, Nukkad Natak, speeches, plays on role of tribal society in the conservation of the environment, and drawing to show gratitude to mother Earth.
