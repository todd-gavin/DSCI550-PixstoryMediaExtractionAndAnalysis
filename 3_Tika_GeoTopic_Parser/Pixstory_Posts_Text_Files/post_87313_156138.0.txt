Nigeria and Chile will be up against each other on Matchday 03 of the FIFA Under 17 Women’s World Cup 2022. While Nigeria are on the second spot in Group B of the FIFA Under 17 Women’s World Cup points table, Chile sit at the third spot in the points table.
Nigeria have played 2 games this season in FIFA U17 Women’s World Cup 2022. They have managed to win one game and have lost a game so far, In the process, they have scored 5 goals and conceded 02 goals. Nigeria have beaten New Zealand and lost to Germany in their first two games of the Under 17 World Cup.
Chile, on the other hand, have played 2 games this season in FIFA U17 Women’s World Cup 2022 and they have managed to win one game and have lost a game so far. In this process, they have scored 3 goals and have conceded 7 goals. Chile, on the other hand, have beaten New Zealand and have lost to Germany in their opening games of the U17 World Cup.
