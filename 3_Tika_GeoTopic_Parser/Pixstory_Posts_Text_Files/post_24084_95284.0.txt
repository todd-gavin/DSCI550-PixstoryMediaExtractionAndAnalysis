Actor Ezra Miller, known for his role as a superhero in “The Flash,” was arrested early Tuesday in Hawaii after allegedly throwing a chair at a woman and leaving her with a cut on her forehead, according to local newspaper Hawaii News Now.

Hawaii Island police said Miller was taken into custody about 1:30 a.m. Tuesday for second-degree assault. Miller allegedly “became irate” when asked to leave an acquaintance’s home and threw a chair at a woman.
