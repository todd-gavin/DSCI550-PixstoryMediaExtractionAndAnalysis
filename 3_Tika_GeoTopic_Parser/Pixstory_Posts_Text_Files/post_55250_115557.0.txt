Earth Day Network-India offers you a short course free of charge on Energy Literacy compiled by our advisor, Prof. Chetan S Solanki a world-renowned renewable energy expert and his Energy Swaraj Foundation - ESF, partners to Earth Day Network. 
Those taking the course will be helped to understand energy-related issues such as its generation, consumption, wastage. Also, ways to minimise (and even avoid) energy usage and thus your carbon footprint and the impacts of fossil-fuel use on the environment, along with alternative energy solutions. In addition, misconceptions about solar energy and methods to adopt solar solutions will be highlighted.
The course covers 12 modules of 10-15 minutes each that you can complete at your own pace. On successful completion, you get a certificate.
