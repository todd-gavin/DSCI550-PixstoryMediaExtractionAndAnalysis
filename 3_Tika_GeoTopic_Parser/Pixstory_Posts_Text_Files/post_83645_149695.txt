Ever wondered why were razors were inexpensive compared to the extra blades you bought Well worry not today I ll tell you about this amazing marketing technique developed by Gillette The company were looking for a way to generate more numbers in sales and profits without increasing the cost of the main product that being Razor so the company came up with the idea to sell razors at a much discounted rates than the blades The goal was to shift the cost of razors on the extra blades to be bought by the customers The concept can be seen in areas like gaming where companies sold Xbox and other accessories at a discounted rates to cover the cost on the games sold
