US Rapper Drake has offered condolences at the death of Punjabi singer Sidhu Moose Wala. Sidhu, who was also a Congress leader, was shot dead on Sunday by unidentified assailants in Punjab's Mansa district, a day after the Punjab government curtailed his security cover. On Monday, Drake took to Instagram Stories to share a picture of Sidhu and his mother. He wrote, RIP Moose and added a bird emoji
