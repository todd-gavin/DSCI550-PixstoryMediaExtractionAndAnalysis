Bollywood Comedian and politicion , Raju Shrivastava has been fighting for life on ventilator in the cardiac unit of ICU(AIIMS) here. He was admitted to the AIIMS yesterday following a cardiac arrest during workout in the gym of a hotel in the capital.  During the medical, examination, some artilleries of his heart were found blocked after angioplasty. His condition is stated to be critical as brain is not responding . His fans are praying to God for his recovery. He had come to Delhi to meet some high politicians of the ruling BJP . He is also the chairperson of the UP Film Development Council
