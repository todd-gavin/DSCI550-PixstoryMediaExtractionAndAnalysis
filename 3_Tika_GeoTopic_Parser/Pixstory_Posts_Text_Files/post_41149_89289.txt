Lewis Hamilton has reacted to critics who claim that he should focus on racing rather than politics Hamilton has long been a vocal supporter of the Black Lives Matter movement and has long utilised his celebrity to advocate for change on a range of causes This season Express Co reported that Formula One has made it optional for drivers to take a knee before races a concept that has become a common sight in sport since George Floyd s tragic death in May At race weekends the British driver has worn t shirts supporting the campaign including one that reads Let s show compassion in an apparent message to society Last season Hamilton wore a rainbow colored helmet at Grand Prix races in Qatar Saudi Arabia and Abu Dhabi to show his support for the LGBTQ community during the F circuit s controversial three race swing through the Middle East All three countries have been chastised for their anti LGBTQ legislation
