Directions 

First let's go for the batter 

Combine together all the wet ingredients in one bowl and mix all the dry ingredients in other bowl. And now mix both the wet and dry ingredients together and combine them. Don't overmix them, if that happens the pancakes could be dense. 

Let's cook them up 

First heat the non stick pan over medium low heat and pour 1/3 cup of batter for each pancake. Cook each side for 1 to 2 minutes and flip to the other side. The pancakes are ready to go from the pans when they get a golden brown colour, that's puffed and cooked through.
