One of Kashmir s most uncommon location Sinthan Top is best connected by the recently completed Anantnag Kokernag Sinthan Kishtwar route This area will enchant and fully engross your imagination with its densely covered pine trees lakes and mountains The landscape is at its most beautiful here and the mountain peak offers views of two distinct valleys Due to its unique location it is a sure hit with everybody who has the opportunity to visit this captivating location It is quite accessible because to the good upkeep of the roads and it is a must see location for its breathtaking panoramic views of the snow capped mountains Accessibility kilometres from Srinagar How to get there Take a cab or a private minibus from Srinagar on the way The best seasons to visit are spring and summer April to June when the weather is pleasant and winter December to March when one can experience snowfall
