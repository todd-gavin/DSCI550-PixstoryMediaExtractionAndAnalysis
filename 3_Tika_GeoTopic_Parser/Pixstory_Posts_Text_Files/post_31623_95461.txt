Providing an authentic travel experience to discover new places is precisely the objective of the Rovigo Convention Visitors Bureau which also gives the organization responsible for implementing the Strategic Plan of the Land Area Brand between the Adige and the Po, a public-private aggregation that brings together entities for more effective tourist promotion The unique and distinctive trait of the lands between the Adige and the Po underlines Cristina Regazzo, Rovigo CVB contact person and local promotion reference point, precisely the heritage of historical and cultural beauty that becomes one with an environmental context that is sometimes intact and not attacked by staggering crowds but capable instead of touching the deepest chords of the soul or surprising even the scholars
