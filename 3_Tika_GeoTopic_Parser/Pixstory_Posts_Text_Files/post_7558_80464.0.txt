Gangster movies come and go but seldom or never will a movie reach the status of The Godfather. Francis Ford Coppola made the gangster movie genre his own. The movie based on Mario Puzo's novel of the same name. The legendary Marlon Brando plays the role of The Godfather, Don Vito Corleone. Al Pacino plays the role of his youngest son, Michael Corleone just as in the movie it was like it was a passing of the baton. Al Pacino become the new force to reckon with. Apart from these two the cast featured James Caan, Diana Keaton, Talia Shire, John Cazale, Robert Duvall and many other legends.
One amazing performance after another. If you're still to watch this all time great movie, you need to do it now. This year the movie celebrates it 50th year. The movie is set to re-release all over the world on 25th February 2022. It's going to be in 4K, its going to be a nostalgic trip for all cinema lovers, this movie shaped everyone in the cinema field, it's legacy is unparalleled.
