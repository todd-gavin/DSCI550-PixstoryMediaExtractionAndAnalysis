Amul is an Indian dairy state government cooperative society based at Anand in Gujarat Formed in it is a cooperative brand managed by the Gujarat Cooperative Milk Marketing Federation Ltd GCMMF which today is jointly controlled by lakh milk producers in Gujarat and the apex body of district milk unions spread across villages of Gujarat Amul spurred India s White Revolution which made the country the world s largest producer of milk and milk products word AMUL stands for Anand Milk Union Limited
