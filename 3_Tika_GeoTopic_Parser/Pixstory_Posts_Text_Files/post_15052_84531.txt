The first humanitarian aid from the Italian Red Cross for the Ukrainian population left from the Emergency Operations Center of the Red Cross in Avezzano this morning. It is a convoy with four articulated lorries and two light machines with ten volunteers and operators on board. blankets and first aid kit This is the first mission of the Italian Red Cross explains Francesco Rocca President of the CRI IFRC in support of the population and the activities of the Ukrainian Sister There will be others structured according to the needs that will gradually arise As always we will be active until there it will be needed At the same time, as an operational structure of the Civil Protection we are supporting the mission to send direct humanitarian aid to Poland, which also started this morning from Avezzano Meanwhile, the Red Cross is continuing to raise funds launched precisely in support of the Ukrainian Emergency to which it is possible to contribute through the CRI website
