The All India Senior Selection Committee on Monday announced the member India squad for the upcoming Asia Cup which will begin on August and conclude on September Virat Kohli and KL Rahul have made a return to the side while Sanju Samson and Ishan Kishan miss out Pace spearhead Jasprit Bumrah and Harshal Patel were not available for selection owing to injuries They are currently undergoing rehab at the NCA in Bengaluru Rahul who did not feature in the T series against West Indies due to COVID has recuperated from sports hernia surgery and is back as vice captain The th edition of the tournament will be played in the UAE between six teams Defending champions India are also the most successful team having won the trophy on seven occasions While the last edition of the tournament was held in an ODI format this edition will feature the T format The six teams are divided into two groups with India Pakistan and a qualifying team in Group A and Sri Lanka Bangladesh and Afghanistan forming Group B
