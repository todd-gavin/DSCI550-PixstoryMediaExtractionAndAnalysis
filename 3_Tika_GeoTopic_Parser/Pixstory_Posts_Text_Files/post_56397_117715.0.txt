How can you distinguish between disagreement and Gas lighting? Often people have doubts in this case as they are under constant pressure. 

‌Disagreeing: 
‌Genuine disagreement over an event or opinion.

Can include varying opinions and perceptions about what happened.

Can include questioning your knowledge to learn more or to express your own experience.

The goal is to understand someone else's side.

Can be frustrating, but remains respectful.

Gaslighting 

Manipulating you by making you question your reality.

Using blatantly untrue statements to question your sanity.

Questioning your experience is used as a way to make you feel inadequate or less credible than you are.

The goal is to erode your confidence.

Abusive, your views are ignored or ridiculed.
