Modi Govt Announces Diwali Bonus for Railway Employees New Delhi In a major incentive for Railway employees the Modi Govt on Wednesday announced Diwali Bonus The decision was announced by Union minister Anurag Thakur following the cabinet meeting Earlier the government had announced a performance linked incentive for the non gazetted railway employees Productivity linked bonus of Rs crores will be given to lakh employees of railways It will be a bonus of days and Rs will be its maximum limit Anurag Thakur said Thakur further informed that the Union Cabinet has approved the Multi State Cooperative Societies Amendment Bill which seeks to amend the Multi State Cooperative Societies Act
