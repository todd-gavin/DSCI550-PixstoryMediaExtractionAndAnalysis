Kashmiri television actress and social media influencer Amreen Bhat was shot dead by terrorists in Jammu and Kashmir's Budgam district on Wednesday.

A 10-year-old, the woman's nephew, was also injured in the attack. The attack was carried out by Lashkar-e-Taiba (LeT) terrorists, police said.

"At around 7.55 pm, terrorists fired upon one lady Amreen Bhat at her home. She was shifted to hospital in injured condition where doctors declared her dead," Kashmir Zone Police said.
