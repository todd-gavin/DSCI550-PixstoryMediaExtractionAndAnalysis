See how many different outfits you can put together with the clothes you already have.
Start by grabbing a shirt. Now take a look at all of the bottoms and see how many you can wear with the shirt. Now add a sweater, or a denim jacket, or a blazer…WOW..we’ve just come up with 4 different outfit ideas!
While you are putting together outfits, don’t forget to take a look at shoes and accessories. Accessories are the quickest and easiest way to change up an outfit.
