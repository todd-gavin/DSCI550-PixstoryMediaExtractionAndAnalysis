Assam Swimming Association will organize 75th Senior National Aquatic Championship in the city from September 6. The tournament will be held at the Dr. Zakir Hussain Swimming pool at the Sarusajai Sports complex.
General Secretary of the Assam Swimming Association Bhaskar Das informed that around 750 swimmers from 27th States and 3 boars will participate in the competition. Apart from swimming two other events-Water Polo and Diving- will also be the part of this championship.
