Kapil Sharma pays tribute to late Mohammad Rafi. 
Comedian, actor Kapil sharma recently pays a tribute to Mohammad Rafi during his show in Melbourne. He shared a video on his Instagram account in which he is seen singing late playback singer Mohammad Rafi's song 'Parda hai Parda' from the movie, Amar Akbar Anthony. In the video Kapil is seen requesting the audience to give a huge round of applause for Mohammad Rafi while mentioning that the late payback singer belonged to Amritsar. Captioning the video the comedians use one word "just" Followed by a folding hand emoji. 
Kapil sharma is all set to return on the TV screen with return of his popular comedy show. It will go on air from September 10
