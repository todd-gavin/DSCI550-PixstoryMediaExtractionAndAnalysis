Bollywood Wives actress celebrate Karwa chauth. 
Popular series The Fabulous Lives of Bollywood Wives actress Neelam Kothari, Bhavna Pandey, Maheep Kapoor celebrated Karwa chauth . Maheep Kapoor was spotted at Anil Kapoor house with Bhavna Pandey and Neelam Kothari to celebrate the festival with sister-in-law Sunita Kapoor as has been a long tradition. Maheep was seen in a green outfit with mehandi on her hands and carrying a puja thali. Bhavana wore a pink suit and Neelam was in a multip- coloured one.
