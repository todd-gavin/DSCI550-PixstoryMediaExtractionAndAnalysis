Devotees including women and children, marching to Vishvanath Dham , carrying Ganga Jal to offer before Lord Shiva . Devotees started from Dashwaswamedh ghat after taking holi dip in Ganges. They took holy Gangajal in their earthen and metal utensils and marched in procession to the ancient Kashi Vishwanath temple to perform Rudrabhishek of Baba Vishwanath
