On Monday Arvind Kejriwal the chief minister of Delhi will present at a public rally in Veraval and offer prayers at a temple in Rajkot in Gujarat s Saurashtra area This will be Mr Kejriwal s fourth trip to the State in a month and his second in Rajkot in the past week A key announcement concerning the next Assembly election will be made during the national convener of the Aam Aadmi Party speech in the Saurashtra area At pm in the afternoon in Veraval the chief of the Aam Aadmi Party will address a crowd at KCC Ground Railway Colony After the rally Mr Kejriwal will fly to Rajkot where he will attend the Maha Aarti at a temple constructed on the grounds of Sanjay Rajguru College before flying back to Delhi
