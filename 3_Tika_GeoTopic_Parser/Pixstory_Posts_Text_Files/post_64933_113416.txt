In view of bad weather conditions Amarnath Yatra has been temporarily suspended as no yatri has been allowed to move ahead from Nuwan base camp in Pahalgam area of Anantnag district officials said here on Tuesday An official told that Yatra has been stopped for time being in view of the bad weather conditions He said that no yatri has been allowed to move towards holy cave due to inclement weather conditions enroute to the holy cave shrine The official added that more than Yatris are staying at Nunwan base camp right now
