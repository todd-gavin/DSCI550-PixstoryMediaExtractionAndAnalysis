A BTS based webtoon! 
The BTS members based characters are in 7 fates : Chakho .
Their fictional counterparts are living in the city of Sin - in . The city is terrorized by supernatural tigers called beom who have a bounty on them .The series is based on mythology of "Chakhogapsa" (toger hunters) from josen dynasty of Korea . The fictional BTS members team up to form a beom hunting group called chakho .
