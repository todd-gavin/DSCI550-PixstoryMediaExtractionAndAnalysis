The National Consumer Union has decided to present a complaint to the Antitrust against the increases in petrol and diesel prices asking that the Guardia di Finanza ascertain the speculation along the supply chain It is clear that there is shameful speculation underway on fuel prices In fact, the increases are not certain justified by the war in Ukraine not so much and not only because at the moment there is no blockade on gas and oil imports from Russia but above all because the surge in prices on international markets cannot already have reached the pump given that crude oil before becoming petrol must be distilled and refined says Massimiliano Dona, president of the National Consumer Union
