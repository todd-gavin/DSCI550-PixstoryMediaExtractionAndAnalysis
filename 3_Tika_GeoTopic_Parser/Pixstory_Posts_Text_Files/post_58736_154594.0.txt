Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath welcomed Home Minister Amit Shah at the airport.

       Union  Home Minister,  Amit Shah landed at Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport in Varanasi today. He  was received by Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath on his arrival at the Airport here. Home Minister and Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath after having a brief meeting with local BJP leaders  left for Bihar from the airport itself.
          On this occasion, BJP State President Bhupesh Chaudhary, Co-in-Charge Sunil Ojha, MP Machali Shahar ,  BP Saroj, President District Panchayat Poonam Maurya, Mayor Mridula Jaiswal, MLC Laxman Acharya, Former Minister and MLA Dr. Neelkanth Tiwari, MLA Dr. Avadhesh Singh, MLA Saurabh Srivastava  , MLA Sunil Patel, Metropolitan President Vidyasagar Rai, besides Commissioner Kaushal Raj Sharma welcomed the Home Minister .
