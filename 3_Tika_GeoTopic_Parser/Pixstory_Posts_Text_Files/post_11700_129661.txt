Ingredients quart whole milk preferably organic cup full fat plain yogurt preferably organic Mixed berries such as raspberries blueberries blackberries and red currants cup honey more as needed Preparation Without scraping the bottom of the pan transfer the yogurt to the strainer and let it drain refrigerated for to hours Discard or reserve the whey for another use Store the yogurt in the refrigerator in a large resealable container for up to days Whisk before serving To assemble the dessert set out shallow bowls and dollop cup of the yogurt into each Divide the berries among the bowls and top with a drizzle of honey
