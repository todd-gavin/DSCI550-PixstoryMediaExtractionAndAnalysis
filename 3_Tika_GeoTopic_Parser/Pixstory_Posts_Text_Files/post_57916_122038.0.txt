It rains on precious days 
Kyoko at the age of 24 , decides to move to tokyo to pursue her dreams . After her previous relationship ended  miserably she lost faith in love , but fate has other plans . Kyoko meets Yoichi her supervisor at work , his calm nature and warmth draw her towards him and despite him being 17 years older than her , she falls in love with him . But will society accept it ? Will she be able to make it work?
