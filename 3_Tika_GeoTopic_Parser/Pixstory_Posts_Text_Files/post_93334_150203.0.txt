Following the huge success of Spanish series Elite, streaming giant Netflix is set to release its official Indian adaptation with the name 'Class'.
The news was announced on Saturday in Netflix's Tudum Event, where Class got eyeballs for its similarities with Elite.
Like Elite, the teaser trailer gives us a glimpse of two high profile young teenagers in Upscale Hampton International, an exclusive private school New Delhi school.
It might be too early to discern how the storyline of Class will differ from its source material, but it will definitely portray the lives of LGBTQ+ characters in an Indian setting.
Class does not have any release date as of now, but season 6 of Elite is likely to be released in 2023.
