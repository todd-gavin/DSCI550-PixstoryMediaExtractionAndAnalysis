The song pehli baar hai from dhadak is Hindi version of the marathi song Yada Lagla from the film Sairat upon which the  hindi remake is based . 
The song decribes the impatience of two young people who are in love and are eager to meet each other  . 
Sung by Ajay Gogavale written and composed by Ajay -Atul
