NAVRASA THE EMOTIONS OF LIFE The word NAVRASA has its origin in sanskrit and is a combination of two words NAV meaning nine and RASA meaning emotions Put together it translates to nine emotions that define the whole essence of our lives Isn t it beautiful The ancient Sanskrit scholars defined the emotions as Shringara love beauty Hasya laughter Karuna sorrow Raudra anger Veera courage Bhayanak fear Bibhatsa disgust Adbutha surprise and Shantha peace
