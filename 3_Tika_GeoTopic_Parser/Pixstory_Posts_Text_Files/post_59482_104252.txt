Ishita Rathi received a special note of thanks from the Delhi police department for her outstanding achievement in the Union Public Service Commission UPSC exams Ms Rathi came in eighth place in the civil service exams which were released earlier this week Ms Rathi whose parents work for the Delhi Police was honoured on Thursday by Commissioner of Police Rakesh Asthana Mr Asthana can be seen chatting with Ms Rathi and her parents who work as the ASI Assistant Sub Inspector and Head Constable in the police department in a video published by the department
