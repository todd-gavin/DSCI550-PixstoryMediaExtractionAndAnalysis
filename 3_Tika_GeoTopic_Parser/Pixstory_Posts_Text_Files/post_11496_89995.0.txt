DASH as the abbreviation says Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension. This diet is highly recommended to those who wants to prevent and treat hypertension. By following this diet it helps to reduces high blood pressure and the possibilities of heat disease. 

The DASH diet includes foods like fruits, vegetables, whole grains and lean meats. 

Researchers observed that while following this diet the high blood pressure became less among people who sticked to plant based diet. 

DASH diet usually emphasizes on fruits and vegetables. The diet also consists lean protein sources like chicken, fish and beans. The diet cuts down red meat, salt, fats and added sugars. 

Since the diet reduces salt intake, scientists suggests that people with high blood pressure to follow this diet. 

Not more than 1 teaspoon (2,300 mg) of sodium per day is involved in the diet and the lower salt diet recommends not to use more than 3/4 teaspoon (1,500 mg) of sodium per day.
