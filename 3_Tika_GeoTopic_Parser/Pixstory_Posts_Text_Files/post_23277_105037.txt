With an immense crowd in the streets of the kingdom and a surprise appearance on the balcony of Buckingham Palace, the celebrations for the years of the crown came to an end. Queen Elizabeth, more determined than ever to lead her people, addressed a message of thanks to her subjects for the warmth shown in these festive days And moved the supporters when she surprisingly appeared on the balcony of Buckingham Palace to greet everyone at the conclusion of the Jubilee
