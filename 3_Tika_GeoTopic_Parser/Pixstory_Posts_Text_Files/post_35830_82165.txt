As had been in the air for several weeks Jannik Sinner has made the decision to separate from his coach Riccardo Piatti The difficult decision was made today by the Italian tennis player and took place with great civility on both sides An idea had already matured in Australia during the last year Australian Open The South Tyrolean wanted to personally thank all the staff and his coach for what they have given him over the years with a note published on his social networks. We remind you that Sinner will soon be busy in Dubai with the tournament starting next February
