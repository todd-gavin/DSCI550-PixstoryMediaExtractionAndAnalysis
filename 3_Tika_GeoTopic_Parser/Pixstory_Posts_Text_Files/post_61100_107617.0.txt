You may wonder if a simple glass of water with your meal could have negative effects — or if that’s just another myth.
 
We have heard both sides of it , some people believe we shouldn’t drink water along with meals as it affects our digestion. Some people believe that we should wait for sometime after having meal for drinking water.

So what is the entire truth?
Here are some reasons why you should not drink water along with meals:
1. Reduces saliva, send weaker signals to your stomach which end up impacting the release of gastric juices and enzymes 
2. Slow down absorption of nutrients 
3. It can cause acidity
4. It may cause weight gain
