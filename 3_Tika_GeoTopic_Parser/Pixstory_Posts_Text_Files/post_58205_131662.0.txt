Did you love Archie comics? Enjoyed reading about Betty , Varonika , Archie ,Jug head and all the other characters? Then you will love this webtoon too ! 
Return to Riverdale with Big Ethel Energy, a spinoff of the Archie comics . It follows the story of Big ethel . 
Ethel happened to be an outcast , but after highschool she is changed and working in the big city .When the Mayor of town calls her back to write its history she encounters all people and memory she had left behind.
