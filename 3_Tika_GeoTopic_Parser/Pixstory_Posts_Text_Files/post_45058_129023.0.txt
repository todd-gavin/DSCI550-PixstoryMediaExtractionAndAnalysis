In the third gameweek of the English Premier League, “The Traditional Top Six” team is all set to face the newly promoted club. AFC Bournemouth are all set to host Arsenal at Dean Court after getting contrasting results in their respective previous games. 

AFC Bournemouth registered the win in the first game of the campaign but they were unable to stop Manchester City. Manchester City thrashed AFC Bournemouth 4-0 at Etihad Stadium. 

On the other hand, Arsenal registered their second win in as many games in the English Premier League. In the second game, they faced Leicester City at Emirates Stadium. The Foxes posed a challenge but Gabriel Jesus’ made his home debut special by scoring a brace. Arsenal registered a 4-2 win by spoiling any comeback plans of the Foxes.
