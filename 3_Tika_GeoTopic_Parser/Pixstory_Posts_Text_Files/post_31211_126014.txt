The Lombardy Region adheres to the Territorial Pact for the integrated development of the mountain areas and the ski and hiking areas of Piani di Bobbio Valtorta and Piani di Artavaggio In this way, the Regional Council makes available millions of euros for the expansion of two sports and tourist poles in the province of Lecco A contribution explains the Undersecretary for Sport, Olympics and Major Events and head of the Lecco Territorial Table, which will make it possible to increase the appeal of the two districts towards winter and summer sports enthusiasts and many families who love mountain holidays
