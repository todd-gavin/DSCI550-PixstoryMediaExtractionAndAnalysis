"Hellbound" season 2 is on the way 
On September 24 KST, Netflix officially announced the second season of its 2021 series"hellbound".
Hellbound series was based on a Popular webtoon of the same name season 1. "Hellbound" was a series where humans faced a frightening supernatural phenomenon: emissaries from hell who appeared on Earth without warning and condemned people to hell. The first season of the drama starred with the cast Yoo Ah In, Kim Hyun Joo, Park Jung Min, Won Jin Ah, Yang Ik Joon, and more.
