Bollywood, the nation's Hindi-language film industry, is one of the most prolific in the world, producing more than 1,000 films annually .

The two or more hours you'll need to set aside in your schedule to watch these movies will be well worth it. No matter if you enjoy romance, tragedy, or humor, this vibrant world has a movie for you.
