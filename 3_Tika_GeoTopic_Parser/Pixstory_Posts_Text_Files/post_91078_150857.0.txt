Ever since people got to know that the two mega stars- Saif Ali Khan and Hrithik Roshan are coming together for the film, Vikram Vedha, there has been a constant buzz about it.The excitement level peaked when the movie finally got released this Friday morning. People had a lot of expectations from the film and whether the movie will be able to meet it or not depends on its total collectionBy now the movie had a fair start and according to early estimates, the film has already collected around 11 crores approximately. The collection shall certainly rise this weekend and the coming week being a festive one is expected to bring a lot more!While a lot were apprehensive that the film being a remake might not be as good as the original movie, people confirmed that the movie is not only good, but even better than the 2017 Tamil movie.
