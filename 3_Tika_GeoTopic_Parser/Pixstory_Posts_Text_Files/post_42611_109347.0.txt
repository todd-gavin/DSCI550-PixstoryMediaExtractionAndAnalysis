La Liga just announced the schedule for the 2022-2023 season, and match day one of La Liga will start on 14th August 2022 and the last match day of La Liga will be played on 4th June 2023.

La Liga will stop on 9th November because of the FIFA World Cup Schedule.

The current La Liga Champion, Real Madrid will play their first match against UD Almeria on 14th August 2022.
