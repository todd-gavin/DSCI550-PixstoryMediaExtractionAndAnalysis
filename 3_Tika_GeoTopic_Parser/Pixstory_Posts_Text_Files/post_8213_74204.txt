Local officials of the Islamic Emirate of Afghanistan in the eastern Kunar province confirmed that a local commander including six others was killed in gunfire on Wednesday January Intelligence officials of the IEA said that the commander his son and five others were shot killed in the Narang district of the province The officials said that the people were killed due to personal enmity The incident is not new in Afghanistan since the Taliban takeover people across Afghanistan have often been killed due to the enmity In the meantime shooting civilians by the Taliban affiliates in check posts is another issue that concerns people across Afghanistan
