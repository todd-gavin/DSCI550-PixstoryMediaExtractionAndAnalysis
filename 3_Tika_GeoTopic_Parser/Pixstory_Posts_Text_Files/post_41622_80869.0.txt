Mallakhamb is a historic Indian subcontinent sport in which a gymnast uses a vertical stationary or dangling wooden pole, cane, or rope to perform aerial yoga or gymnastic poses and wrestling holds. Mallakhamb is also the name of the pole used in the sport.Sheesham (Indian rosewood) is commonly used for the pole, which is polished with castor oil. Mallakhamb is performed in three different ways: with a sheesham pole, a cane, or a rope.
