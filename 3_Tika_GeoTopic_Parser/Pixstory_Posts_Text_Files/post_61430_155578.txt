Paris metro In the French metropolis of Paris there is a rapid transit system called the Paris metro. It is considered a symbol of the city and is distinguished by the compactness of the geographical borders of the city. uniformity of architecture and distinctive entrances with Art Nouveau influences It includes stations including line transfers It is more than times busier than the London Underground and ranks tenth in the world It is the second busiest in Europe after the Moscow metro In November the construction starts In July the Porte Maillot Porte de Vincennes the first line is inaugurated within the framework of the Universal Exhibition de Paris Hector Guimard is in charge of creating the entrances to the station There are still some of his entrances in use
