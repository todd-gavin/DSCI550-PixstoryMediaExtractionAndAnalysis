Local sources in Kunduz confirm that Dr Hoshang Rostaghi was abducted three months ago and released from the kidnappers Dr Rostaghi has been released from the abductors as a result of efforts by Taliban security forces Mohammad Naeem Mangal director of Kunduz Hospital told the media However Mr Mangal did not provide further details to the media and said Dr Hoshang who was abducted three months ago was released last night and has returned home In addition Dr Rostaghi s family members confirm that he was released from the kidnappers but they also did not give more details In the meantime some sources said that the doctor had been released from the kidnappers in exchange for money Meanwhile about two weeks ago Taliban security forces released a doctor from the kidnappers in Kabul
