Lazio and Feyenoord will be up against each other in their opening encounter of the Europa League 2022-23. This will be a Group F match which will be played at the Stadio Olimpico.
Lazio, Feyenoord, Midtjylland, and SK Sturm Graz are the 4 clubs that have been drawn in Group F of the Europa League campaign this season. This will just be the 2nd meeting between both these clubs in a European competition.
Feyenoord won the game against Lazio in the 1999-2000 season which they played away from home in the UEFA Champions League. Lazio have drawn each of their last 3 matches in European competitions at home.
