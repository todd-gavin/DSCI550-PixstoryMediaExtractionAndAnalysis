In the second leg of their Champions League quarter final Paris Saint Germain s women team will host the German side at the capital s legendary ground Les Parisiennes will face Bayern Munich in the quarter finals of the UEFA Women s Champions League The first leg will take place in Munich on Tuesday nd March at CET while the second leg will be held at the Parc des Princes on Wednesday th March at CET It will be the second match at the Parc des Princes for the club s women s team this season Last November Les Rouge et Bleue beat Real Madrid in front of spectators a record attendance for a Women s Champions League group game Didier Oll Nicolle s players made it through to the quarter finals by finishing top of their group with the best attack goals scored and the best defence no goals conceded in the competition
