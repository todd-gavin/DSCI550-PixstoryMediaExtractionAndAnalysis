He represented India when the UN Convention on Trans-national Organised Crime was being deliberated in Vienna. The Government of India he recognized his work by awarding him the Police Medal for Meritorious Service in 1993 and the President’s Police Medal for Distinguished Service in 1999. 

Neeraj Kumar’s strengths and competencies lie in his well-developed human resource management skills, his diagnostic and forensic acumen across a wide variety of issues relating to crime and internal and external security, and his ability to apply a highly educated and sensitive mind to the challenges of maintaining law and order in an increasingly fast paced and stressed society.
