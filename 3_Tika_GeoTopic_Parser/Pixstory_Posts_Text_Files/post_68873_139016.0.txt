Assam’s international swimmer Shivangi Sharma won two medals in the very first day of the 75 th senior national aquatic championship at Dr. Zakir Hussain Aquatic complex,Sarusajai Guwahati today.

Shivangi begged one bronze medal in women’s individual 400 mtr freestyle and one silver medal in 400 mtr mix relay.
The prestigious aquatic championship is going to be concluded on 10 th September 2022.
