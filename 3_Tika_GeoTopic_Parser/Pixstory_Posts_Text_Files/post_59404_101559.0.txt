Tata Motors is all set to launch a new SUV Blackbird that will be positioned in between Tata Nexon and Harrier . Blackbird will compete with Hyundai Creta ,Skoda Kushaq, Kia Seltos and VW Taigun . As expected, it has a 5 star safety rating, powerful engine, six airbags, ventilated seats with CVT transmission . It is equipped with fully  loaded audio system with apple car play and Android auto.  This SUV is definitely a strong contender in mid segment .Tata Blackbird is all geared up to take on the cut throat competition in its segment.
