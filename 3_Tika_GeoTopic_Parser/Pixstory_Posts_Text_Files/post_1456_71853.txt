Six years to the day that year old PhD student Rohith Vemula took his life after relentless institutional harassment on account of his marginalised status as a Dalit on campus His story has become emblematic of the ceaseless struggle of the marginalised castes in the Brahminical world of Indian academia In his letter he expresses the feeling that birth was his fatal accident He wished to travel to the stars and to the other worlds in which a man was not reduced to his political identity his caste status I hope he found the peace he was seeking Jai Bhim
