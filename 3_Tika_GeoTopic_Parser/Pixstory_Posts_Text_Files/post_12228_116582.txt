Techotsav or the Tech Festival was organized by GDSC Google Developing Students Club of DMCE This events was organized in the month of April In this event participants had to prepare a project as a solution to a problem statement All the problem statement were related to software domain The event was divided into three rounds where first two rounds were conducted online to analyze various problem statements that participant had chosen Top professionals were part of the jury Experts reviewed every project minutely and cross questioned their doubts One had to present their demo in the first two rounds Round was an offline event conducted in seminar hall of the College The finalist had to give a live presentation and submit live project in front of all the proffesors juries and the audience Winner s were based on whose solution is more effective Top winners were awarded certificate and cash prizes Techotsav gave an opportunity to think and find solutions on various issues of the society
