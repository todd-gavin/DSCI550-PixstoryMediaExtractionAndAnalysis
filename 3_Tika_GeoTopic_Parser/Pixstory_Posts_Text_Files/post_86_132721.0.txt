Allahabad High Court maintains a permanent circuit bench at Lucknow, the administrative capital of the state. The maximum number of serving judges is 160, the highest in India.
Location: Principal Seat: Allahabad; Permanent Bench: Lucknow
Established: 17 March 1866 (in Agra); 1869 (in Allahabad)
