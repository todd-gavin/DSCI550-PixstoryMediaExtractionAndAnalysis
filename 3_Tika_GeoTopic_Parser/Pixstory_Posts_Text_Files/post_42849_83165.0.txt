Great news for Haikyuu fans has arrived this month. To commemorate 10th Anniversary of Haikyuu! A new one-shot manga of haikyuu has been announced. 

The one-shot manga of Haikyuu will be focusing on what Hinata and other are upto in 2022. The manga will be more 'slice-of-life'.

It's quite disappointing because fans were expecting Season 5 of Haikyuu. But don't lose hope yet!
