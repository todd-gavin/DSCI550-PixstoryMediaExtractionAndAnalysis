Kajol shares Ajay devgan's pic from National Film Awards. 
Ajay Devgn received the National Film Award in the best actor category for his performance in the 2020 film Tanhaji. The actor took his twitter to express his joy and thank his fans. Now Kajol gave him a shoutout on social media as she shared a photo of Ajay from the prestigious awards events. She posted the story of receiving the award from the President Droupadi Murmu at the ceremony. She wrote along with the photo, "Bringing home two National Awards and looking good is difficult! "
