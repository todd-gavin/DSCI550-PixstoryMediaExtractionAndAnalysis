The sheer majesty of Aharbal which is situated on the River Veshav and is one of the most well liked tourist destinations in Kulgam is a sight to behold and worthy of its name Niagara falls of Kashmir After the meter drop the roar of powerful white water flowing through blends with the serene surroundings into a pool of still azure blue water This one promises to enthral not only nature enthusiasts but also ardent photographers and with nearby fishing holes and hiking trails it has something for everyone
