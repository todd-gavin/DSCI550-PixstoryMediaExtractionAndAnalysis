The Harry Styles starring romantic drama My Policeman from Amazon Studios is set for a world premiere at the Toronto Film Festival The film is about three people caught up in the shifting tides of history liberty and forgiveness Harry styles plays Tom Burgess a policeman in s Brighton who marries school teacher Marion Emma Corrin while engaging in a gay relationship with museum curator Patrick played by David Dawson Flashing forward to the s Tom Linus Roache Marion Gina McKee and Patrick Rupert Everett are still reeling with longing and regret but now they have one last chance to repair the damage of the past Source The Hollywood Reporter
