More than 10 Afghan publishers participated in the 33rd Tehran International Book Fair. Saeed, Zaryab, Amiri, Vaja, Khorasan, Sobh Omid, Negar and Koodakaneh are some of the publishers who have presented their works in this exhibition.

Tehran Book exhibition will be open to visitors until Saturday, May 21st.

1700 publishers from Iran and 170 publishers from other countries have participated in this book exhibition.

Afghan publishers have said their work at the exhibition has been well received.

The Afghan Publishers Union is present in the International Publishing Hall of the Tehran International Book exhibition.

Many Persian book publishers in Afghanistan have been forced to shut down due to the economic crisis in Afghanistan and the slump in the book market following the Taliban takeover, media reported.
