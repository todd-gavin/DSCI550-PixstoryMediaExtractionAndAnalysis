Students of Royale School Taleigao on Friday flagged off a tiranga cycle rally to create awareness about the Har Ghar Tiranga programme to mark India s years of Independence The rally was flagged off by dance choreographer Akshay Vishwakarma in the presence of the Headmistress Maria Da C Afonso as well as other teaching and non teaching staff The vision of Har Ghar Tiranga campaign is to unify the nation and remember the country s freedom struggle by instilling patriotism in the hearts of citizens Primary and High School students participated enthusiastically in the km cycle rally
