The teaser and poster of the film “Phone Bhoot” got released and audience can’t keep calm. The film has been the talk of town since a very long time and this Diwali, finally it is going to be released. Phone Boot is big for Katrina as after her marriage this is going to be the first time she will be seen on screen.
