I swear it is the  most creamy milkshake oats ever- very easy to make & so flavorful.

Topped it with banana🍌, strawberries 🍓blueberries 🫐 & peanut butter 

Ingredients:

300ml oat milk
80g strawberries
1/2 banana
50g oats


1. Put the oat milk, strawberries and agave syrup ir the
banana in a blender and blend.

2. Put the milkshake in a saucepan with the oat flakes and simmer for 8-10 minutes.

3. Chop up a few strawberries and mix them in - this makes it more fruity🤩
