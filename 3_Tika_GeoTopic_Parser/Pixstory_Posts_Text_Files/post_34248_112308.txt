Starting next season, Maria Sole Ferrieri Caputi could become the first woman to referee in Serie A. In fact, the whistler from Livorno will be included in the staff of the CAN, the referee commission to which the match officials of Serie A and Serie BA belong, on the sidelines of the Ast seminar Ussi and Aia in Florence on Arbitration at the time of the Var the designator Gianluca Rocchi said that the women who are part of the AIA are technically and athletically prepared like men If one of them were to arrive in Serie A it will only be because she deserved it
