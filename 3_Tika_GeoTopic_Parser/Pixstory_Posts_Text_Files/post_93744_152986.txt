 French author Annie Ernaux has won the Nobel Prize in literature organizers announced in Stockholm on Thursday The Swedish academy applauded her work for the courage and clinical acuity with which she uncovers the roots estrangements and collective restraints of personal memory Most of her work focuses on personal history and her memoir is the great archetype According to the Washington Post report Happening discusses an illegal abortion that she had in the s A translation of her memoir The Years was shortlisted for the Booker Prize In addition a translation of Ernaux s Getting Lost a diary of her affair with a younger married man was published this year Anders Olsson of the Swedish Academy said this while announcing the author s accolade Her work is uncompromising and written in plain language scraped clean And when she with great courage and clinical acuity reveals the agony of the experience of class describing shame humiliation jealousy or inability to see who you are she has achieved something admirable and enduring
