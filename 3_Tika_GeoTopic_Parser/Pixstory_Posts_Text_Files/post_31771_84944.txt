From Wednesday March, Le intermittenze della vita, the new instrumental album by the Italian-Swiss harpist RAOUL MORETTI, will be available on streaming and digital download platforms. he emotional psychological evolution experienced during the lockdown period Raoul Moretti through unconventional sounds and techniques declines the use of the electric harp as a direct emanation to express himself as an artist and a human being. The album is structured in four parts each consisting of three songs. In the first part the point of view of the people and external images are represented in music such as the silence of deserted cities and solitary runners in the second part the gaze opens to the world and to China represented in the sounds by the stringed instruments of the Chinese tradition The third part instead describes in music the psychological aspects of moments of crisis due to the persistence of the situation finally the last part brings with it the birth of an inner strength of man with the hope that everything will end
