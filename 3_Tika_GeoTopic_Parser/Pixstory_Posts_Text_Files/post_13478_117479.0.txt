A local journalist in Uruzgan, who was arrested by the Taliban a few days ago, was released again.
The Journalist Association of Afghanistan has announced that Mr. Shams was released yesterday, Tuesday.

Not long ago, a local journalist was arrested by the Taliban in Uruzgan and was imprisoned by this group for two months. After two months of imprisonment, the Taliban released this journalist and said that he had not committed any crime.
According to media support organizations, three journalists named Khaled Qadri, Mirza Hosni and Abdul Hanan Mohammadi are currently in Taliban prison in Herat and Kapisa provinces.
