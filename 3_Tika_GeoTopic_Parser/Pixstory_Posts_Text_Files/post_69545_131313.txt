One of the most well liked tourist destinations in between Kishtwar and the Breng Valley in South Kashmir is the mountain pass known as Sinthan Top Sinthan Top which is metres high and offers stunning degree views of both the Jammu and Kashmir regions is a well known tourist attraction This must see location in Kishtwar draws snow lovers as it is coated with snow the entire year The neighbouring Sinthan Maidan km is likewise becoming into a significant hub for skiers climbers and trekkers In addition Sinthan Maidan is a wonderful family vacation spot offering sports like horseback riding and paragliding The ideal time to visit Sinthan Top in Kishtwar is between April and September Due to the significant snowfall throughout the winter Sinthan Top becomes inaccessible Image source
