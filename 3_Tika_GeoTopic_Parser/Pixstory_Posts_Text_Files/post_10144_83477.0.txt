Indian chess genius Ramesh Babu Pragannanha has been hailed after Magnus Carlsen, the world number one chess player, stunningly lost at the Airthings Masters.

 The 16-year-old Prannanands defeated Magnus Carlsen in 39 moves in this online tournament and became the youngest chess player to defeat this great chess master since 2013.

 In 2016, at the age of 10, Ramesh Babu Pragannanha became the youngest chess master in the world.

 Carlsen, who is from Norway, has won five world chess championships.

 Although the world champions tried to regain control of the game, Prague did not give him a chance and eventually won the World Chess Tournament.

 "I'm very happy," Prague said after the victory.
