
Mount Carmel School, Arambol emerged as Pernem taluka champions in the U-14 football tournament organised by Directorate of Sports and Youth Affairs, at Mandrem Panchayat ground on Friday.
They defeated St Joseph High School, Pernem 1-0.
The all-important goal for Mount Carmel came in the 45th minute through the boots of Polwin Rodrigues which ultimately turned out to be the match winner.
Mount Carmel who had dominated the play in both the halves, could have won by bigger margin but forwards were not quick in grasping the opportunities before them.
