The sixth stage of the Giro d Italia almost all flat kilometers between the provinces of Reggio Calabria and Cosenza was designed especially for sprinters and so it was. The Frenchman Arnaud Demare won at the photo finish and thus obtained the second consecutive success Beaten by a handful of centimeters the Australian Caleb Ewan third place for Mark Cavendish The general classification unchanged with the pink jersey that remains on the shoulders of Juan Pedro Lopez Tomorrow a much more eventful stage is expected with arrival in Potenza after km and over m in altitude Possible the action of a breakaway but even some skirmishes between the ranking men
