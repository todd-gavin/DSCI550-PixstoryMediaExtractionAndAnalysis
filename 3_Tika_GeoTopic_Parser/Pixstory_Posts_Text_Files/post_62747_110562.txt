 There is a cult of ignorance in the United States and there has always been The strain of anti intellectualism has been a constant thread winding its way through our political and cultural life nurtured by the false notion that democracy means that my ignorance is just as good as your knowledge One of the most prolific writers in history was Isaac Asimov Nearly the course of his life he published over books essays and around letters The best thing about Asimov in my opinion was his ability to present issues and arguments in wholly unanticipated contexts allowing readers to view them from a fresh perspective Getting the other person to view the issue from a different perspective is one of the most challenging things to do in a philosophical debate and Asimov was excellent at it
