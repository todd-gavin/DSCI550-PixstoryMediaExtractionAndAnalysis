Jonathan Anderson reflects on memories of summers spent in Ibiza giving life to a Loewe collection Paula s Ibiza The creative youngster used to spend his summers in Ibiza as a child Paula s was a boutique founded in by Armin Heinemann and Stuart Rudnick right on the island The clothes that Paula s sold were made with colorful and cheerful prints so peculiar that they became characteristics of the boutique which over the years became a cult location parties were organized and among the customers there were always great celebrities The boutique sadly closed in but a few years later in Jonathan Anderson he contacted the founders and proposed a collaboration to them The creative director of Loewe began to mix and merge the characteristic prints of the boutique with those of the maison, giving life to the collaboration that is renewed every summer
