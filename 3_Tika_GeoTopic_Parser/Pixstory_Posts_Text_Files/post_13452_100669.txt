The National Resistance Front has released images of the presence of former Afghan police and army commandos in Panjshir According to Sibghatullah Ahmadi spokesman for the Resistance Front the two commandos were previously active in the Afghan Ministries of Defense Interior and National Security and have now moved to Panjshir to fight the Taliban Mr Ahmadi has called these forces special heroes Our special forces have dealt heavy blows to the Taliban occupying forces with rapid counterattacks he wrote on Twitter In recent weeks Resistance Front forces have launched coordinated attacks on Taliban insurgents in Panjshir province forcing the Taliban to send large numbers of reinforcements from other provinces to Panjshir At the same time the Afghan Freedom Front attacked Taliban positions in Andrab Baghlan
