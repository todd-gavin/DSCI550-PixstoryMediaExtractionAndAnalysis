Dr.Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan ( teachers day special ) ✨✨✨✨
Dr. Sarvepalli radhakrishnan was a great scholar , philosopher and a famous teacher . He was born on 5th September 1888 at a village near thiruttani ( Madras ) in India. Dr.Sarvepalli radhakrishnan belonged to  a poor family and completed  his most of education with scholarship. his father's name was Sarvepalli Veeraswami and his mother's name was sitamma. He was married to sivakamu.Dr. Sarvepalli radhakrishnan completed his B.A and M.A degrees in philosophy . Dr. Sarvepalli radhakrishnan was first vice president of India from 1952 -1962 and second president of India from 1962-1967. He also become a professor at Oxford University because of his good academic career , he became a chancellor at Delhi University and vice chancellor at Benares hindu University.
He wrote many books to popularize Indian tradition.some of his famous book are the philosophy of upnishads ,East and West some reflection eastern religions and western religion thought and so many. Dr. Sarvepalli radhakrishnan was awarded with the Bharat Ratna in 1954. His birthday anniversary is celebrated every year 5th of September to honor and tribute to his great working forever .
Philosophical thoughts by radhakrishnan :
radhakrishnan attempted to bring eastern and western  ideas together. Radhakrishnan was one of Neo-most vedanta's influential spokesman.
Death of Sarvepalli radhakrishnan :
In 1967 radhakrishnan stepped down from public life. He spent the last 8 years of his life in  Mylapore madras in the house he designed on April 17,1975 radhakrishnan passed away.
Awards and honours of Sarvepalli radhakrishnan :
Radhakrishnan was awarded Bharat Ratna ,the highest civilian award of India 1954. He was honoured with the recipient of the pol le merite for sciences and art in 1954 by Germany . In1975,he received  the Templeton prize  for promotion nonviolence.
