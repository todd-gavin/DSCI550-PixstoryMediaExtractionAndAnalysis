Local Guwahati team clinched 1st Assam Youth Olympics title with 76 gold medals. 6 days long tournament concludes at Srimanta Sankardev Kalakshetra today. Although 4500 players from 35 districts participated in the prestigious event. Chief Minister Dr Himanta Biswa Sharma and Assam Olympic Association chief, union minister Sarbananda Sonowal gave away the awards to champion guwahati team. Also arjuna Awardee Monalisa Baruah Mehta, Kamlesh Mehta, International Shooter Joydip Das, International footballer Alen Deory, International boxer Ankushita Boro present during the closing ceremony.
