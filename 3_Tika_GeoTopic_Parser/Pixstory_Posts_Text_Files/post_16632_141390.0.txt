After a grand opening on Day 1, Brahmastra Part One: Shiva staring Ranbir Kapoor, Alia Bhatt, Amitabh Bachchan, Mouni Roy and Nagarjuna has earned ₹37 crore in Hindi language on Day 2 of the screening.As per the Pinkvilla report , ₹37 crore was collected in Hindi language while 5 crore in regional languages which makes Day 2 collection ₹42 crore nett.According to production banners Star Studios and Dharma Productions, the second day figures of "Brahmastra Part One: Shiva" stood at ₹85, bringing up the total collection to ₹160 crores.
