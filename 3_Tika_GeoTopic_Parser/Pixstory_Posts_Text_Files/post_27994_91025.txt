The Football League has communicated the scheduled dates for the recovery of the two matches that had been postponed in recent months due to the numerous defections due to Covid In April the salvation clash between Udinese and Salernitana will be played while in April we will have Fiorentina Udinese Salernitana Venice and Bologna Inter On the other hand, the recovery of Atalanta Turin is expected in May, a date that could also undergo a further postponement in the event that the Goddess advances in the Europa League. It should be emphasized however that for Udinese Salernitana Bologna Inter and Atalanta Turin the Guarantee College has yet to pronounce del Coni to decide on the table victory of the teams that had duly presented themselves on the pitch.
