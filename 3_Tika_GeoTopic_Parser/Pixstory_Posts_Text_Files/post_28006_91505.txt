Sonny Colbrelli can go home After the great fear following the cardiac arrest that saw him protagonist after the sprint at the end of the first stage of the Tour of Catalonia in which he finished in second place, the thirty-one year old had been hospitalized in Girona in Spain before being transferred to the university cardiology clinic in Padua, where the strong Lombard cyclist, winner of the last edition of the Paris Roubaix, as well as being the current Italian and European champion, was implanted with a subcutaneous defibrillator. A fully successful operation that will solve the arrhythmia problems suffered by the athlete. After the necessary rehabilitation it will be discovered whether Sonny will be able to return to pedaling at a competitive level given, for example, the ban in Italy on participating in sports competitions with a subcutaneous defibrillator Instagram photo sonnycolbrelli
