The telenovela between Dybala and Inter has reached its final stages The Argentinian player should join the Nerazzurri at the beginning of next week after the decisive meeting between Jorge Antun and Fifa agent Giacomo Petralito at the Inter headquarters today The final details will be finalized, starting from a four-year contractual basis with an engagement that Inter offers millions plus bonuses Dybala asks for fixed ones The parties will meet and in the end the solution should satisfy both Dybala would have been convinced not only by the four-year contract but also by the possibility to play both with Lautaro Martinez and with Lukaku In fact, after closing with his signing, Inter will also give the final assault to the Belgian striker who should return on loan to form an incredible trio
