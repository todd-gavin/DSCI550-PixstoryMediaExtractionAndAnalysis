The MotoGP is changing to try to stem the diaspora of its public by adopting one of the strategies also followed by the F. Next season there will be a substantial change to the race weekend with the introduction of the Sprint Race, a race with half the duration of the will assign half the points The starting grid of both races will in any case be decided in qualifying as opposed to in F where the Sprint arrival order will decree the starting grid of the Sunday GP The solution will be adopted in all championship weekends with the consequence that there will be at least races per season between real GPs and Sprint Races.
