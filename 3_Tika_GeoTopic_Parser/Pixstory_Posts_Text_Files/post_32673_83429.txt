Sometimes they come back and if they are great MotoGP managers Livio Suppo is a familiar face in the MotoGP paddock where he landed years ago with the Benetton Honda team Since then he has spent years with Ducati as Project Leader the years of the triumphs of Casey Stoner and seven in Honda HRC where he was the Team Principal for seven years obtaining five world titles with Marc Marquez Since he left MotoGP Suppo has created a successful e-bike company but now he is back in the paddock and his task will be mainly that of making Davide Brivio forget who moved to Formula with the Alpine team at the end of the resounding success of Joan Mir and the Ecstar team in that championship
