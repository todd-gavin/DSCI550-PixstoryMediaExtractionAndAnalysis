The recovery from the operation on his right hand takes longer than expected and so Matteo Berrettini announced his withdrawal from the tournaments on red clay in Monte Carlo, Madrid and Rome. On his social profiles, the Italian tennis player wrote Do not participate in these events in particular in Rome was an extremely difficult decision which, however, I believe is the right one to be able to return to the highest levels It remains to be seen whether Berrettini will be able to participate in the Roland Garros tournament where he reached the quarter-finals last year and under what conditions he will be able to play the Grand Slam on red clay
