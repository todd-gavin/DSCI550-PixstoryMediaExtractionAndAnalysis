With the three medals won inside the Capital Stadium in Beijing, the gold in the meters, the silver in the meters and in the medley relay, all in the short track discipline, Arianna Fontana was without a shadow of a doubt one of the most representative personalities of this edition of the Winter Olympic Games. a true legend of Italian sport as the most medal-winning Italian athlete ever at the Olympics will be celebrated at the closing ceremony on Sunday February.
