Assam Cricket Association yesterday signed a MoU with Assam Police for developing cricket infrastructure at Adarsha Gaon Barhampur Nagaon The MoU was signed in the presence of a host of dignitaries including Bhaskar Jyoti Mahanta DGP Assam Jitu Goswami MLA Barhampur Nitul Gogoi IGP Admin along with president and secretary of the Assam Cricket Association Romen Dutta and Devajit Saikia respectively On the occasion the foundation stone of the ACA AP Cricket Complex was also laid down by Bhaskar Jyoti Mahanta
