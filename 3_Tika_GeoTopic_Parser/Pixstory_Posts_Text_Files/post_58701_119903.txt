This historical site of Sarnath situated km north east of holy city of Varanasi is protected by the Archeological Survey of India ASI The site reflects year old civilization here It is adjacent to the Mulgandha Kuti Vihara It is famous because Lord Buddha preached his first sermon here on Dharma The monuments digged out through excavation near the Dhamek Stupa It is also home to the legendary lion capital memorial pillar built by King Ashoka the current Indian national emblem
