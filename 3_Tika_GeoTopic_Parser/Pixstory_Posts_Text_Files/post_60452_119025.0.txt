Netflix's new series 'Daily dose of Sunshine' confirms its production and casting lineup of Park Bo-young, Yeon Woo-jin, Jang Dong-yoon, Lee Jung-eun

Director Lee jae-kyu of series 'All of Us Are Dead' will join hands with Netflix once again to introduce a healing drama called 'Daily dose of Sunshine". "Daily dose of Sunshine' tells stories of the world and heartbroken people Da-eun, who has just become a nurse in the Department of Mental Health, meets in a psychiatric ward.
