To celebrate upcoming Holy Navratras, Sargam Society of Indian Music & Arts (Regd), Mohali has organized a three day Devotional Musical Program from 2nd to 4th April to be held in Tagore Mini Hall, 6.00 PM onwards. In this event, 20 singers have been shortlisted who will dole out devotional offering and will also pay a tribute to legendary Lata Mangeshkar ji, said President of the Society & Chairman, Aryans Group of Colleges, Rajpura, Near Chandigarh, Dr. Anshu Kataria
