On Tuesday a Sikh organisation in Jammu and Kashmir stated that it will give Rs lakh to restore a gurdwara that was damaged in a terror attack in Kabul over the weekend Baldev Singh Raina chairman of the United Kashmiri Sikhs Progressive Forum said they were pained by the terror attack on Karte Parwan Gurudwara Kabul which left two people dead We opposed this attack because it was a painful affair This attack is against humanity and the peaceful Sikh community in Afghanistan he stated in a written statement
