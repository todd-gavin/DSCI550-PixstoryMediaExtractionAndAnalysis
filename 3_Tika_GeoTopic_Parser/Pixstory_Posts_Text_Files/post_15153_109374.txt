In Paris Natalia Bruno, researcher of the National Institute of Optics of the National Research Council Cnr Ino was awarded as one of the most promising scientists International Rising Talents in the context of the initiative L Or al UNESCO For Women in Science International Awards Natalia Bruno is awarded for her studies in quantum optics that contribute to the development of new generations of quantum computers high-performance networks and sensors technologies useful in sectors where considerable computing power is required such as medicine and climate research and to allow greater security for the exchange of sensitive information
