Thursday May at the National Science and Technology Museum presents the documentary Giuseppe Penone Organic Rebirth made by D Produzioni with the contribution of the Giulio and Giovanna Sacchetti Foundation The documentary reconstructs the genesis of the project for the installation entitled Indistinct borders created in the Bosco della Memoria in Bergamo in memory of the victims of Covid through the work and poetics of one of the most important exponents of Italian Arte Povera The event is open to the public with free admission subject to availability of seats by booking directly from the Museum website www museoscienza org
