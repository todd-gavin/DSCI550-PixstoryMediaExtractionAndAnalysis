Joaquin Phoenix has performed tremendously in many movies such as The Master, Gladiator, Her, Walk the Line and people have thought that he should've won an Oscar for some of them. He finally won the Academy Award for his titular role as Joker, in the movie directed by Todd Phillips. 
He prepared extensively for the role, he lost 52 kgs for the role.He studied and observed thr people who had Pseudobulbar effect to know how they express their emotions. 
Joaquin Phoenix was heavily inspired from Heath Ledger's portrayal of the character. Phoenix was a good friend of the actor and playing this role is a tribute to him. 
Joaquin pulled out a nuanced performance and with the DC universe expanding, the journey of his character might have just started. It will be very exciting to see if a sequel of the movie comes out or the character appears in some other movie.
