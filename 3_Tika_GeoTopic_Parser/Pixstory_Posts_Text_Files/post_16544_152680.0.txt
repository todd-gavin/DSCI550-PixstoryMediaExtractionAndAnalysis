Ponniyin Selvan had another big day at the Indian box office, collecting Rs. 27 crores on Wednesday, taking its running total to Rs. 192 crores approx. The collections remained similar to Tuesday as some places had a drop while others jumped depending on how the holiday played in that region. The film will be crossing Rs. 200 crores in India today, for a first-week number that looks like will be in the range of Rs. 205-210 crores.
