The project with which the Dolce Gabbana brand makes its debut in the gaming world takes its name from Good game, the global greeting between gamers before each game. The project is developed through a series of initiatives including a partnership with Mkers to create one of the first twinnings between Gaming House never documented SKNUPS for the creation of ad hoc digital content and collections and a collection designed for gamers To complete the initiative, Domenico Dolce and Stefano Gabbana have created a limited edition no gender collection consisting of hats, t shirts, trousers and sweatshirts customized with the crossed DG logo hyper digitized writings and a mix of glitch effects and nuanced colors in ANSA tie dye
