Valeria Bruni Tedeschi presented her new directorial film Les Amandiers in competition at Cannes. The French-Italian actress and director is not new to sitting behind the camera, in fact she has already shot and written well films and a documentary but for the first time she does not appear as an interpreter in one of her stories The film also stars Louis Garrel, former love of Bruni Tedeschi and father of her eldest daughter Oumy On the occasion of the red carpet, the unforgettable co-star of La Pazza Gioia wore a golden evening dress made especially for she from the brand Ami Paris The stylist in question Alexandre Mattiussi has designed a dress with a deep neckline and thin straps that underlines the sensual physique of the artist sister of Carla Bruni Sarkozy
