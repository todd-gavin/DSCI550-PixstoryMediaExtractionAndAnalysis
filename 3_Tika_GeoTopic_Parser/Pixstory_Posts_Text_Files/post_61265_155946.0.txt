Actress Vaishali Thakkar was found hanging at her home in Indore on Sunday morning. A suicide note has been discovered as well.While the reason is still unknown, the news was shocking to her family and friends. Her last post on Instagram was on October 1 and has been MIA from social media since then.The actress was well known for her role in Sasural Simar ka as Anjali Bharadwaj. The case has been reported in Tetaji Nagar police station and further investigation has started.
