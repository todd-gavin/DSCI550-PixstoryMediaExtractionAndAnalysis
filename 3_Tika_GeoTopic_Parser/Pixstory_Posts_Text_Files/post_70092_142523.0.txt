Rosary HSS girls win U-17 Subroto tourney 
South Goa winners Rosary HSS, Navelim hammered North Goa winners Our Lady of
Mount Carmel HS, Arambol by 13 goals to emerge state champions of the
U-17 Subroto Mukherjee girls football tournament. 
Rosary HSS will now represent Goa in the U-17 All India Subroto Mukherjee football tournament to be held in New Delhi.
