Eighteen-year-old Gyamar Nikum from Arunachal Pradesh helped Rajasthan United FC (RUFC) produce the biggest upset of the 131st Durand Cup so far, beating local favourites ATK Mohun Bagan (ATKMB) 3-2 in a group B fixture at the Vivekananda Yuba Bharati Krirangan (VYBK) in Kolkata on August 20.It was a grand debut for RUFC in the tournament, as Gyamar Nikum got the all-important winner in the fifth minute of stipulated time.
