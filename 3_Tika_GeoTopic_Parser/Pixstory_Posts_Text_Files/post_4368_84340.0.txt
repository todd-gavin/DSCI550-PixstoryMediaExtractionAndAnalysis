The purpose of commemorating National Science Day is to send a message to the kids about the value of science and to show them how science is employed in our daily life. It is also observed to display scientific activities, efforts, and achievements, as well as to discuss scientific development. 

Integrated Approach in Science and Technology for a Sustainable Future is the theme for NSD 2022. This subject was launched by Union Minister Dr. Jitendra Singh, who emphasised the importance of an integrated approach to science and technology for a sustainable future.
