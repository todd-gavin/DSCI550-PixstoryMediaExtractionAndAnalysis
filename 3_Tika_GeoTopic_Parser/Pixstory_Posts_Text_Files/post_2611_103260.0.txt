Today is her Birth Anniversary. Second daughter of Ustad Abdul Karim Khan. Very accomplished Singer and Also a Movie star in Marathi Movies. Her Singing was recognised when she was chosen to Sing ‘Vande Mataram’ on 15th August 1947 at Red Fort.
Vande Mataram was unofficial National Anthem till 24th January 1950, when ‘Jana Gana Mana’ was adopted as the National Anthem.
