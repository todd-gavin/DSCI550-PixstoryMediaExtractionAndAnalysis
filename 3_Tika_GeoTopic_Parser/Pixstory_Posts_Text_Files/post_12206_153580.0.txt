We've got our second fastest Indian, in 110m hurdles ....
 Tejas Shirse of Maharashtra became the second fastest Indian in the men’s 110m hurdles. He won gold in Gandhinagar with a time of 13.84s, just 0.01 seconds off Siddhanth Thingalaya’s meet record of 13.83. The previous one a holds the National Record of 13.48s.
