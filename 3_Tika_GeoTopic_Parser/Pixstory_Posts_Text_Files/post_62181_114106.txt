Medication and therapy are good ways to manage your ADHD symptoms But they re not your only options Research now shows that mindfulness meditation where you actively observe your moment to moment thoughts and feelings may also be a good way to calm your mind and improve your focus Mindfulness meditation strengthens your ability to control your attention It teaches you how to observe yourself and to focus on something And it trains you to bring your wandering mind back into the moment when you get distracted It can also make you more aware of your emotions so you re less likely to act impulsively Meditation is thought to help with ADHD because it thickens your prefrontal cortex a part of your brain that s involved in focus planning and impulse control It also raises your brain s level of dopamine which is in short supply in ADHD brains
