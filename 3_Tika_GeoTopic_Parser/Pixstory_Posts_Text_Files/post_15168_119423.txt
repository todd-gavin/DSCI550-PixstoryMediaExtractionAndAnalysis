STM Live and Fondazione Teatro Coccia are pleased to announce the first Italian production of tick tick BOOM the musical by Jonathan Larson author and composer of RENT which will debut at the Coccia Theater in Novara on September and will continue its tour in the main Italian theaters Already on the calendar Milanese dates of the Teatro Franco Parenti from November tick tick BOOM conquered the general public with the recent film version interpreted by Andrew Garfield nominated for an Oscar and awarded at the Golden Globe Awards for best leading actor The Italian edition of the musical is directed by Massimiliano Perticari and Marco Iacomelli and in the role of the protagonist Jon the very young Italian talent Nicol Bertonelli fresh from the television success of Bracciotti Rossi
