Don Bosco Oratory Fatorda defeated Raia Sporting Club 2-1 in the Raia SC’s Festival football match played at Raia Village Panchayat ground on Friday. 
Serineo Fernandes and Richie Vaz scored for Don Bosco Oratory while Lloyd Cardozo reduced the margin for Raia SC.
Individual prizes: First scorer of the final- Serineo Fernandes (DBO Fatorda), best striker – Lloyd
Cardozo (Raia SC), best midfielder – Maxcel Fernandes (DBO Fatorda), best defender – Roque Borges (DBO Fatorda)
