Today is also birth annniversary of Shailendra also known as Shail was a noted Hindi film lyricist during the s and s who is best remembered for Vijay Anand s magnum opus Guide Besides that he also worked in a number of successful projects like Bimal Roy s Madhumati Raj Kapoor s Shri and Kishore Sahu s Dil Apna Preet Parayi Shailendra penned a vast number of patriotic verses at the time His poem Jalta hai Punjab was very popular amongst the masses and during one of his recitals filmmaker Raj Kapoor met him and offered him his first role as a lyricist in the Hindi film industry Thus he started work with Kapoor s super hit classic Barsaat Over the next few years Shailendra was already working with veteran music artists such as Shankar Jaikishan Salil Chowdhary S N Tripathi and S D Burman Shailendra also produced the film Teesri Kasam but the film did not fare well at the box office and it led to financial problems and he died soon after He wrote great songs my favourites are from Bandini Guide Sangam and the great Ajeeb Dastaan and Awaara ae Mere Dil great songs from Shree Dil ka Haal and Ramaiya Vastavayia
