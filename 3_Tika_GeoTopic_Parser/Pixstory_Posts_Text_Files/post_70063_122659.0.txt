The Directorate of Sports & Youth Affairs (DSYA) will organize Road Races on the occasion of Independence Day on August 15, 2022 from Old Secretariat, Panaji to Miramar and back immediately after the official State function to commemorate the 75th Anniversary of Indian Independence Day “Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav”.

The road races will be organized in 3 age groups  - between 14 to 16 years, 17 to 19 years and 20 years and above for men & women as part of the celebration of Independence Day.

Those individuals desirous of participating in these Road Races may submit their entries with date of Birth at office of the Directorate of Sports & Youth Affairs, Campal, Panaji latest by August 12, 2022 upto 5.00 p.m. in person or by special messenger.

All the participants shall report at the Old Secretariat in proper Sports Kit on August 15, 2022 at 7.30 a.m.  For further information, Ram D. Kamat, Assistant Physical Education Officer may be contacted on Mobile No. 7030450091.
