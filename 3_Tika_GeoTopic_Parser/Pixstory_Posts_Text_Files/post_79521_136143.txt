Brahmastra grand event at Ramoji Film City cancelled after Jr NTR fan storm sweeps the venue Brahmastra is all set to hit the big screen on September This is one of the most awaited films of the year The makers had planned a great event in Hyderabad which was supposed to take place at Ramoji Film City Along with the media the fans of the actors were supposed to be there But the event at Ramoji film city has been cancelled Jr NTR is the special guest at the event and the actor fans came in a huge number at the venue nbsp So to avoid any type of commotion the makers decided to change the venue and cancel the grand event Now a normal press conference will take place at plush hotel in Hyderabad nbsp The movie is directed by Ayan Mukerji and has the special cameos of Shah Rukh Khan and Deepika Padukone
