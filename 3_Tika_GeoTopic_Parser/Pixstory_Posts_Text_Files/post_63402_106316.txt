It s not just vaccines Covid has also divided opinions on WFH A McKinsey survey has found that most employees will look for a new job if forced to come to office every day Deferring to this sentiment tech firms are increasingly offering a work from anywhere option But in the other camp are hardliners like Elon Musk who has made it clear that employees must spend a minimum of hours in office per week or resign Then there are some companies like Citi which are taking a middle path by offering a three day of office per week option to employees It may seem like the ideal solution but there is a pitfall proximity bias You performance may be exceptional while working from home but a colleague in office who chats up the boss after the meeting will always have the edge There are no easy choices here What do you prefer
