Ingredients l of alcohol the peel of medium lemons gr water gr sugar Preparation We wash the lemons well with warm water and dry them. Peel them with a potato peeler to avoid removing even the white part of the peel which would make the limoncello bitter Put the peels in a container hermetically sealed glass with a liter of alcohol The first job done now we have to let the skins macerate for a month in a cool, dark place After the month of maceration, prepare the syrup Boil the water, add the sugar and mix until it has dissolved let everything cool down The time has come to open the container with the peels in infusion We filter the peels and insert the syrup close the container and let it rest again in the dark and in the cool for about days After this time we filter and bottle our limoncello
