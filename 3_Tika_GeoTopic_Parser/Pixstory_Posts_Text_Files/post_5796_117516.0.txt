According to the latest rankings released by Henley Passport Index, Indian passport is ranked at 87 out of 193 with an easy access to 60 countries.

Japan, Singapore & South Korea are the world's strongest passports. A Japanese passport provides visa free entry to 193 countries and remains the most powerful for the fifth year in a row. Singapore and South Korea came at number 2 and 3 slots respectively providing access to 192 countries.

United Kingdom is ranked at 6th with a hassle free access to 187 countries and USA got the seventh spot with 186 countries in it's kitty.

Afghanistan ranked at the lowest by granting it's holder access to only 27 countries.

Visa free travel and tourism not only provides a hassle free travel but is also inexpensive compared to acquiring a visa prior.
