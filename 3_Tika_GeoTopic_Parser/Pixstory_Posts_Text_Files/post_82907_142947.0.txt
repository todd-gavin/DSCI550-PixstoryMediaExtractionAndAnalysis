14th September
Hindi Diwas
These day is celebrated all over India to mark the adoption of Hindi language. Hindi is our national language. It is celebrated in many colleges schools government institutions  in  places of different parts of India. This day is celebrated with great joy. Hindi is also spoken in other countries of the world. It is one of the most spoken language in the world. It is a medium of communication in India. Hindi is taught in schools and colleges. Hindi helped in binding the country together. Different types of competitions are organised on this day like reading, speaking, and writing hindi. This day is a great day for all the Indians.
