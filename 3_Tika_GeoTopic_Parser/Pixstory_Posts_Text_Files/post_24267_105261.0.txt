Maruti Suzuki India installs Asia's largest 20 MWp solar power plant at its production facility in Manesar, Haryana
The solar plant will contribute 28,000 MWH of power per annum, covering 11.5% of the total energy requirement of the facility
This energy will be sufficient to manufacture over 67,000 cars annually at the production facility
The move will lead to the avoidance of 20,000 tons of CO2 emissions per year
