The Court of Inquiry determined that the officers' deviation from SOP resulted in the accidental firing of a Brahmos missile, which landed in Pakistan. Three officers have been held primarily responsible for the BrahMos missile misfiring incident on March 9, 2022. The Central Government has terminated their services with immediate effect. The officers were served with termination orders today, August 23rd, according to the Indian Air Force.

A Group Captain, a Wing Commander, and a Squadron Leader are among the officials who have been let go. The officers deviated from standard operating procedures, resulting in the missile being accidentally fired.
