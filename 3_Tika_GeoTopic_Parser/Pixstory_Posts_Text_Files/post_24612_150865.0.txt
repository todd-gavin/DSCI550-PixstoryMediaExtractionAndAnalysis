The Men’s & Women’s teams were in action during today’s tennis matches. Services Sports Control Board and Maharashtra are through to the Finals, having defeated Delhi and Karnataka, respectively in their semi-final encounters. The winners will lock horns tomorrow in the Men’s
Team event final.
In the women’s team event, Gujarat will take on Maharashtra in the summit clash. They defeated Karnataka and Tamil Nadu with ease and will look to clinch the gold medal on Saturday, October 1.
