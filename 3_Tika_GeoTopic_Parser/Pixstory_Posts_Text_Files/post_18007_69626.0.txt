Zach Galifianakis and  Elizabeth Banksare set to star in 'The Beanie Bubble' movie that will be an exclusive for Apple TV+. Sarah Snook and Geraldine Viswanathan have also been cast in the movie. The will be co-directed by Kristin Gore and Damian Kulash. Kristin wrote the movie and will direct it with  her husband Damian. The movie is based on The Great Beanie Baby Bubble: Mass Delusion and the Dark Side of Cute written by Zac Bissonnette.
