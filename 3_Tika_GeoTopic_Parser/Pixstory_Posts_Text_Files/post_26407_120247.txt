Convivio celebrates years and celebrates them at the steam factory in Milan from to November The foundation returns with the most important market exhibition in Italy to raise funds to finance research and fight HIV The event stems from an idea of the stylist Gianni Versace in and from first promoting committee composed of Giorgio Armani Gianfranco Ferr Valentino Garavani and Versace himself Over the years the tradition has been carried forward thanks to Franca Sozzani supported by institutions and companies A legacy that has also been enhanced by the attention that many personalities from the world of fashion, culture and show dedicated to it as well as a large audience participation
