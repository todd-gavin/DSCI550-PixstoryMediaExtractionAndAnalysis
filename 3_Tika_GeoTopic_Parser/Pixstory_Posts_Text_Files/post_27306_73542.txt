The exhibition stems from a collaboration between the Italian-Israeli artist Sabina Schkolnik Saad, eleven artist members of the Permanente, the director Alberto Nacci and two hundred and twenty students of the Artistic high school of Brera and the Scientific Primo Levi high school of San Donato. A selection of forty works by Sabina Schkolnik Saad on display the theme of the Star of David and its symbology as well as the works of the students gained starting from the readings The perfume of my father by Emanuele Fiano and Remembering the future For a non-purged story of the Holocaust by Nicola Reale And a documentary film directed by Alberto Nacci who presents the interviews of eleven artist members of the Permanente who talk about their creative activity from the training period to the present Museo della Permanente Free admission until February
