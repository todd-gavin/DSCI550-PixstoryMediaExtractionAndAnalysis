The Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) has barred 90 pilots of low-cost airline SpiceJet from flying operating Boeing 737 Max aircraft as they have not undergone proper simulator training to operate the aircraft.

The 90 pilots will have to go through proper training on the Boeing Max simulator before they can fly these planes again.

These pilots will have to undergo training again to the satisfaction of DGCA," DGCA Director General Arun Kumar said.
