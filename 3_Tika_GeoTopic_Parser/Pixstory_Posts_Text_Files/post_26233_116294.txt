The new advertising campaign by JW Anderson is an irreverent ode to the search for lightness and divertissment in the contemporary world Shot by Kito Munoz, a young Spanish photographer who combines homoeroticism with irony, undressing the ephebic models without making the photographs sexual. Protagonists of the campaign Joakim Gjemmestad and Felix Archer young fashion promises The artistic direction entrusted to Doncaster creative director and founder of The Forgotten Book magazine The campaign is therefore the result of a collaboration between determined and talented young people who grasp the sense of contemporaneity
