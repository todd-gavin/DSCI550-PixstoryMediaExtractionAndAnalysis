Man United and Real Sociedad will be up against each other in their opening encounter of the Europa League 2022-23. This will be a Group E match which will be played at Old Trafford.
Man United, Real Sociedad, Sheriff, and Omonia are the 4 clubs that have been drawn in Group E of the Europa League campaign this season. Man United have an excellent record against Sociedad as they have never conceded against them in any fixture as yet.
Real Sociedad, on the other hand, have a very poor record against English opponents as they have not won a single match so far against English teams. Man United are unbeaten in their last 18 UEFA Europa League home games..
