Olivier Rousteing will sign the next couture by Jean Paul Gaultier The artistic director of Balmain will be the third guest designer of the Parisian haute couture house which will be presented next July The presence of Jean Paul Gaultier himself at the Balmain show last March confirmed the news After Chitose Abe of Sacai and Glenn Martens founder of Y Project it will be Rousteing to propose his version of the couture of the enfant terrible of French fashion The show will debut in July on the same day as Balenciaga but a small preview has already appeared and it seems that Rousteing has created shoes inspired by the tin box in which the iconic Gaultier perfume is sold
