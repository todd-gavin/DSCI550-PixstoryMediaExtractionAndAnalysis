	• For Windows
place awning or shutters on the outside , use insulated window film and spray a sheet with cold water to cover the window’s opening. 
	• For Fans
	put them in a corner, low to the ground , with a bowl of ice in front of it. In the summer, ceiling fan blades should rotate counter-clockwise 
	• For Lamps
	use fluorescent ones instead of your incandescent lights
	• For Chores
	that create heat, try to do them at night
	• For exterior Walls
	the placement of planting vines can cool them down
