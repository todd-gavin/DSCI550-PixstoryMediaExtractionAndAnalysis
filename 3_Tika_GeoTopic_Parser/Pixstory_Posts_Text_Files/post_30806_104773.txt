The MotoGP qualifying held today on the Barcelona circuit and valid for the Catalunya GP saw the Italian bikes still in great evidence with well between Aprilia and Ducati in the top nine positions. Pole position went to Aleix Espargaro Aprilia who preceded Francesco by just a thousandths Bagnaia Ducati third time for the championship leader Fabio Quartarato Yamaha The second row all Ducati thanks to Johan Zarco Fabio Di Giannantonio and Jorge Martin while in the third row we find Alex Rins Suzuki Maverick Vinales Aprilia and Luca Marini Ducati An excellent scenario in view of the race tomorrow scheduled at 2.00 pm with Espargaro and Bagnaia who will try to reduce the gap that divides them in the general standings from reigning world champion Quartararo
