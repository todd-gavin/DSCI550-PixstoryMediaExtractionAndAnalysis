The World Environment Day is observed every year on 5th June. World Environment Day is celebrated across the world to raise an awareness and describe the importance of Mother nature.

As per TOI, World Environment Day was established in 1972 by the UN General Assembly at the time of Stockholm Conference. This was the world's first conference to make an environment protection, a major issue. World Environment Day was first celebrated in the year 1974.

This day has created a platform to raise an awareness that the world is facing the problem such as air pollution, plastic pollution, global warming and sea level increasing day by day.
