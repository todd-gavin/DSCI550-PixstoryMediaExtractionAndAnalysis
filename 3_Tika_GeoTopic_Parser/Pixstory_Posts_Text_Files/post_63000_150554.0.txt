
The end of all flight operations was declared by Orlando International Airport.
Likewise, Southwest Florida International Airport reported that as of Thursday, the airport is still closed.
“We are working to assess damage to RSW’s facilities and property. All flights are cancelled today,” it said.
Additionally, Jacksonville International Airport's terminal is still closed and all flights are cancelled for today.
The airport issued a statement saying, "The airport will reopen as soon as the airfield and terminal are deemed safe for flight ops."
Airlines cancelled around 2,000 flights for Thursday.
