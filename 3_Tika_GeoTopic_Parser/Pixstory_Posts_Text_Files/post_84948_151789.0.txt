Sevilla and Borussia Dortmund will be up against each other on Matchday 03 of the UEFA Champions League. Sevilla are on the absolute bottom spot in the points table in Group G of the UEFA Champions League 2022-23 while Borussia Dortmund sit at the 2nd spot in the points table.
Sevilla are on a poor run in the UEFA Champions League this year and haven’t done well in La Liga too. In the 2 games that they have played in this season so far, they have drawn a game and lost a game against Man City and Copenhagen.
Borussia Dortmund, on the other hand, have done decently in the Champions League this year. In the last couple of games that they have played this year in the UEFA Champions League, they have won and lost a game each.
