Cleverman is an Australian drama set in the future and its main premise is based on the mythology of Indigenous Australian Peoples Creatures with strength and speed among other powers arrive and try to live amongst the human race but instead of living in harmony humanity seeks to destroy them There s one person referred to as the Cleverman who can bridge the divide in this intense drama These hairy people endowed with incredible cunningness speed and longer life spans must fight to survive among humans who want to silence exploit and raze their kind Let s see who wins
