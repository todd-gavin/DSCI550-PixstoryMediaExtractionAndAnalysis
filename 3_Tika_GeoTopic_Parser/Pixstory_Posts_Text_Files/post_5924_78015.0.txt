Rafael Nadal staged a five-set comeback to get the better off Daniil Medvedev in the Australian Open on Sunday. With today's win, he earned his 21st grand slam title -- most in the men's singles category. 

As Nadal makes history, let’s take a look at which men’s tennis players have the most major singles titles:

Roger Federer, Novak Djokovic— 20

Pete Sampras — 14

Roy Emerson — 12

Rod Laver, Björn Borg — 11

Bill Tilden — 10
