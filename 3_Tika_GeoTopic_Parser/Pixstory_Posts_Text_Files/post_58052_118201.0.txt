Penelope my baby ❣️, her wardrobe is totally iconic. I love all her dresses they are stylish and elegant  . Her outfits have unique designs  and color combinations. If I could I would definitely have them in real life .

Villains are Destined to Die is a story about a normal korean University freshman student Cha Siyeon who somehow found her herself inside the mobile game she had been playing.That too as the Villainess of the otome game Penelope who dies at every route . Now to go back to her own world Siyeon must win the hearts of  the capture targets while avoiding the death flags . But as she plays along she realises that Penelope and her have so much in common. Can she really return to her world !
