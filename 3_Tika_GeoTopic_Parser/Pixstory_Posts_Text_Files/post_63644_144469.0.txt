Uncharted: Legacy of Thieves Collection, which bundles Uncharted 4: A Thief’s End and Uncharted: Lost Legacy, will be released on PC on October 19, as announced by Sony Interactive Entertainment and developers Naughty Dog and Iron Galaxy making this the first of the acclaimed treasure hunting series to be released on PC.
The PC version will include a number of specific enhancements for PC optimized performance such as  4K resolution, Ultra-Wide Monitor support and a host of enhanced graphical adjustment features such as adjustable Texture and Model Quality, Anisotropic Filtering, Shadows, Reflections and Ambient Occlusion.
The game will also feature  haptic feedback and dynamic trigger effects of a Duel Sense controller provided you plug it, enhanced graphics card memory management, reimagined UI, scale sliders, variable load speed support, support for Razer Chroma RGB dynamics, supercharged frame-rates, next-level temporal upscaling technology from AMD Fidelity FX Super Resolution etc.
Uncharted: Legacy of Thieves on PC will cost $49.99, and is available for pre-order on both Steam and Epic Games Store. Pre-ordering on Epic’s store will let players nab an exclusive Uncharted-themed glider in Fortnite.
