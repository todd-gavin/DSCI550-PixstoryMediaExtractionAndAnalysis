Children s Day in Korea is an important holiday On this day South Korea celebrates its most loved inhabitants its children This public holiday red days is celebrated on May th every year in Korea That means that salaried workers get a day off work and children get a day off school The origins of Children s Day in South Korea come from the s During that time students wanted to draw attention to their situation to improve their status One of the main supporters of the movement was children s writer Dr Bang Jeong Hwan who started using the word eorini to mean children Children s Day was originally on May st but as this coincided with Labor Day it was moved to May th The day became an official public holiday in the s Koreans have been enjoying a day off work on May th ever since
