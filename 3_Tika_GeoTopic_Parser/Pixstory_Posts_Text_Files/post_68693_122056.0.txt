Assam ‘s Tokyo Olympics medalist Lovlina Borgohain lost to Wales’s Rosie Eccles via split decision to  2-3 in the women's 70kg category at the Commonwealth Games. Lovlina Borgohain won the opening round 4-1 but she lost steam in the last two rounds as Eccles came up with an excellent game. Lovlina who won bronze at the Olympic Games a year ago, failed to go past the quarter-final in Birmingham on Wednesday. This was her consecutive second Commonwealth Games. 
She has already qualified for 19th Asian Games 2023, which is going to held in Hongzhou, China.
