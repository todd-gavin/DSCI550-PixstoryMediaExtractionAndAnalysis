Rajasthan Royals has shown their brillant form this season and finished as the second best team before the play-offs. 
Yuzvendra Chahal has been ruling this  tournament with 26 wickets  with one hat-trick and one fifer. He is also the current purple cap holder.

Jos Buttler has been in the form this season he has 629 runs in 14 matches with three half century and three century . He is the leading  run scorer and most likely to win the orange cap this season.

In 2018 Rajasthan Royals was eliminated by Sunriser's Hyderabad  by 9 wickets which put them in the 4th position that season .

Rajasthan Royals will face Gujrat Titans on 24th May in Eden Gardens.
