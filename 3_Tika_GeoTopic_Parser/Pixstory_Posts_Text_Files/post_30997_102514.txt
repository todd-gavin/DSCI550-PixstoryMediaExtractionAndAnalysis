After the scudetto won with Milan Zlatan Ibrahimovic underwent surgery on his left knee in an extreme attempt to further prolong his career The almost year-old Swede underwent surgery in Lyon by Dr. Bertrand Sonnery Cottet the intervention had been planned for some time specifying the objective of the operation Definitively resolve the instability of the joint through the reconstruction of the anterior cruciate ligament with lateral reinforcement and meniscal repair The arthroscopy was perfectly successful and recovery times are estimated at months
