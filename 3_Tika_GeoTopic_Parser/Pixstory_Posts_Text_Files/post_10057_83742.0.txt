Ukrainian authorities said dozens of people have been injured and killed as Russia continues to launch a wide-ranging attack on the country, hitting cities and military bases with airstrikes and shelling.⁣
⁣
As of Thursday evening CET, at least 68 people had been killed across the country, according to a tally by news agency AFP. Ukraine official sources say the count includes both soldiers and civilians. ⁣
⁣
Ukraine said that Russian forces had managed to reach the region around Kyiv and a battle was under way for an airbase near the city.⁣
⁣
In the deadliest single strike reported by authorities, 18 people were killed at a military base near Ukraine's Black Sea port city of Odessa, according to AFP. ⁣
