With the deployment of the indigenous aircraft carrier INS Vikrant, India has strengthened its expeditionary force capability in the Indo-Pacific and is projecting maritime dominance from both its eastern and western seaboards. This is the first of many steps that India must take to counter China's rising maritime threat and ensure freedom of navigation in the Indo-Pacific.
The Integrated Defence Staff Headquarters must see synergy among the fighting forces on the ground, not on paper. India cannot afford to have its armed forces operating in silos, each defending its own fiefdoms and imperial legacy. Following Prime Minister Narendra Modi's unveiling of the new Naval Ensign, the Indian military must abandon its British military past, Raj traditions, and ceremonies in order to create a new ensign.
