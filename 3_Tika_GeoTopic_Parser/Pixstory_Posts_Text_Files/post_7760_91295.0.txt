Apple TV+ after becoming the very first streaming service to win the Academy Award for Best Picture has accuquired another big movie. Apple TV+ has landed Project Artemis, the movie will star Scarlett Johansson and Chris Evans. The movie is being directed by Jason Bateman who has won the Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Directing for a Drama Series in 2019, for his work on Ozark. 
The screenplay gas been written by Rose Gilroy and will be around the space race. Scarlett Johansson, Jonathan Lia and Keenan Flynn are producing the movie under These Pictures Banner. Bateman soll produce through his company Aggregate Films.
