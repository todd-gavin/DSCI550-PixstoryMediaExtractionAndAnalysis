Here is the recipe I followed Melt the butter in a saut pan over medium heat Add the mushrooms and garlic cooking until the mushrooms lose their moisture and become soft about minutes Bring a large pot of water to a boil then add the salt and spaghetti Cook the pasta according to the instructions on the package until the spaghetti is al dente When finished save cup of water that you cooked the pasta in and strain out the rest Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium high heat When hot add the onions and garlic and saut until the onion turns translucent and begins to brown about minutes Sprinkle over the flour and stir to coat the onions let cook for about seconds Whisk in all of the remaining ingredients Cook the sauce for about minutes until thickened whisking as needed Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss well to combine Serve hot Garnish with parsley and parmegan if desired
