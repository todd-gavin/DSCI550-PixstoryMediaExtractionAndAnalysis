A teen from Arunachal Pradesh who went missing along the Line of Actual Control with China has been handed over to the Indian Army Union Minister Kiren Rijiju tweeted today The Chinese PLA has handed over the young boy from Arunachal Pradesh Shri Miram Taron to Indian Army Due procedures are being followed including the medical examination Rijiju tweeted China s People Liberation Army or PLA had earlier confirmed that Mr Taron would be repatriated to the Indian side There was a delay earlier in repatriating him due to bad weather conditions in the mountainous area
