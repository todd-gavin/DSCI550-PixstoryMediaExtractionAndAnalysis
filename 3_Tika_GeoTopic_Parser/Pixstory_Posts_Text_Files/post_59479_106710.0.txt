Mumbai police officials announced on Saturday (June 11, 2022) that Nupur Sharma, the suspended spokesperson for the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), has been asked to appear at Mumbai's Pydhonie police station on June 25. The summons was issued by Mumbai police in connection with the investigation and recording of Sharma's statement, according to officials. On the basis of a complaint filed by Raza Academy, police had previously initiated a case against her. The news comes as the controversy over suspended BJP spokeswoman Nupur Sharma's comments on Prophet Muhammad has erupted, causing widespread uproar around the world.
