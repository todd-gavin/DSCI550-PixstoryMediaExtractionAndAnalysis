Britney Spears is finally getting out of tight grip of her father, Jamie Spears. E! News reported that a judge in Los Angeles has ordered that Jamie must depose and produce documents related to the alleged surveillance of Britney as part of an ongoing investigation into his role as conservator of her estate. The conservatorship was terminated in November 2021 after 13 years. The judge ruled on July 13.
