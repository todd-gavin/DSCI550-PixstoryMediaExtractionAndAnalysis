Bharatiya Janata Party's Kirodi Lal Meena today will introduce The Uniform Civil Code in India Bill, 2020 in the Rajya Sabha as a Private Member Bill. 

The Bill procures for the constitution of the National Inspection and Investigation Committee for the preparation of the Uniform Civil Code and its implementation throughout the territory of India and matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.

In the Rajya Sabha, the opposition has upped its ante on Pegasus and farmers' issues.
