Amidst the rising mental health issues, first Suicide Prevention Helpline named 'Zindagi' has been launched in Kashmir.

The Helpline (18002701008) is the initiative of Institute of Mental Health and Neurosciences, GMC Srinagar and SRO, Kashmir.
