Did you know that the Australian Alps get more snow than the Swiss Alps? Well, now you do!

While many visitors come to Australia for its beautiful beaches and year-round sunlight, skiers flock to the Australian Alps, which span New South Wales and Victoria. Snow frequently occurs between June and September, making Australia an ideal destination for a northern hemisphere summer ski vacation. 

As per travel nation, Thredbo and Perisher are two significant resorts near Canberra, with the latter being the southern hemisphere's largest ski resort. Blue Cow Mountain is one of the most popular destinations, offering a diverse  selection of lodging and runs, as well as breathtaking views of the Alps.

With June heading up, this sets your vacation plans! Head over to Australia to have the time of your life!
