Short note on a ⚽️ Striker!a player in a game such as football whose main purpose is to try to score goals rather than to prevent the opposing team from scoring.  A striker's role is to stay close to the opponent's goal during a football match and be ready to take goal shots. Since they play on offence, they are an attacking player, which means they need to find ways to drive the ball into the opponent’s goal.# 6 traits of a Football Striker:1.
