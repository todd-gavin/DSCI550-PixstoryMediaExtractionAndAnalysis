Excellent performance for Italian universities worldwide, above all the Statale of Milan stands out par excellence, which ranks first in Italy for the Arwu Academic Ranking World University ranking. The ranking is published every August 15th and is indicative of the progress made in the field of research and university education This year La Statale is placed in the so-called range between the th and th place worldwide, an exceptional placement if one considers that the universities surveyed are all of which are at least in the first band of excellence Among the parameters evaluated by the jury there are the Nobel Prizes the Fields medals the number of cited researchers and the indexed scientific publications Congratulations to the Rector Elio Franzini to the professors, the researchers and all those who have contributed to achieving this important result, declared the president of the Lombardy Region
