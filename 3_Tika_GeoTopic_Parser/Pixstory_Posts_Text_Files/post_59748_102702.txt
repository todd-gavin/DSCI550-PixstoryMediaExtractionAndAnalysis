Communication skill is the most crucial skill required in today s Era To master the same we should ensure these C s during Communication CLARITY It means conveying of message in clear manner so that it transfers accurately from the sender to the receiver For this unnecessary information or complicated words should be avoided CORRECTNESS We should ensure that the message being communicated must be correct in terms of grammar punctuation spelling and facts CONCISENESS This means that the message should be brief and complete It should not include irrelevant matter or repetition or wordy expressions COURTESY Through this we should ensure that we communicate politely We should show respect and avoid discriminatory language CONCRETENESS The communication must be specific and clear It shouldn t be vague To be concrete one should use active voice and specific facts and figures to support their argument CONSIDERATION While communicating we must think keeping receiver in mind We should see the message from receiver s point if view COMPLETENESS The matter should be complete and should consist of all the necessary information One must remember W s What When where why who and how while ensuring this One can be regarded as a GOOD COMMUNICATOR if he follows these C s while communicating
