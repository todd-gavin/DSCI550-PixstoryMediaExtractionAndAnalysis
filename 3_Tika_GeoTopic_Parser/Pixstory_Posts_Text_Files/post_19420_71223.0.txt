Assam’s own pianist and composer Ron Cha’s single LOST was released today in the city. LOST is an Assamese folk melody passed down over multiple generations, featuring legendary folk singer Kalpana Patowary from Assam, drummer Ferenc Nemeth from Hungary, Alexander Tóth on Bass from USA and Mylai Karthikeyan on Nadhaswaram from Chennai.
