Tens of thousands of ancient manuscripts preserved in Timbuct city in northern Mali are finally available in digital form A virtual gallery has been launched on the internet with the aim of preserving the historical and cultural heritage of a region known for being one of the most important crossroads of the African continent The project called Mali magic involved Malian and foreign researchers, interpreters, librarians and computer scientists.
