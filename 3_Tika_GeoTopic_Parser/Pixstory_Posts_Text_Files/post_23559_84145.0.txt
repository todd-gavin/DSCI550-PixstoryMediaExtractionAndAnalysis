South Korean boy group, Stray Kids,  have released the main trailer for their upcoming 6th mini-album "ODDINARY" on February 27th, on their agency, JYP Entertainment's YouTube channel. 

Stray Kids released the teaser for "ODDINARY" on February 14th, which showed the members in a tale of temptation. The members were trying to  entice Felix in what appears to be the "ODDINARY" world and it seems that they have finally succeeded in their mission in the main trailer.

Stray Kids' 6th mini-album will be released on March 18, 1PM KST.
