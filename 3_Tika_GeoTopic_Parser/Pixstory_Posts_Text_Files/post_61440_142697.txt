YOGA Yoga is a Sanskrit word meaning yoke or union It can be characterized as a set of physical, mental and spiritual disciplines that have their roots in ancient India They are meant to control or calm the mind while recognizing a detached consciousness which is unaffected by the chitta mind and ordinary dukha suffering Hinduism Buddhism and Jainism all have different schools of yoga with different practices and purposes Ancient and contemporary yoga are practiced all over the world The Rigveda is where yoga is first discussed and other Upanishads also refer to it Traditional yoga is aimed at meditation and letting go of worldly attachments Yoga is practiced with a variety of methods by all Indian religions In Hinduism practices include jnana yoga bhakti yoga karma yoga kundalini yoga and hatha yoga From large scale meetings under the Eiffel Tower to classes in mansions festivals and workshops yoga is becoming increasingly popular In France
