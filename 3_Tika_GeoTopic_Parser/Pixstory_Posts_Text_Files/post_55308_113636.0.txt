Birla Public School, Bander Sindri, Kishangarh, Ajmer, Rajasthan, performed a skit and took an oath to protect our earth in a special assembly for #EarthDay2022. Earth Day Network-India requests schools across India to do a special Morning Assembly on the theme #InvestInOurPlanet. Toolkit and logos are available on https://bit.ly/EarthDayMorningAssemblyContest2022 
The last date for schools to submit photos and details online on the link is extended to July 31.
