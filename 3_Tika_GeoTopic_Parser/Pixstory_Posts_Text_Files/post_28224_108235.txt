In the end Ferrari decided After the position penalty ordered by the FIA to Charles Leclerc for the replacement of the electronic control unit, the Maranello engineers decided to change the whole power unit again in the morning before free practice for the Canadian GP. A choice this time not dictated by mechanical breakdowns but by a strategy that will allow the Monegasque to have many practically new components to manage in the next races, avoiding the imminent danger of further penalties on the grid. The price to pay will be for the obligation to start from the back of the grid, thus making rossa will sacrifice the Montreal race with Leclerc but will practically have two fresh engines to rotate in the next races Instagram photo scuderiaferrari
