Mount Carmel Schools, Dwarka, Delhi conducted environmental morning assembly events between April-June such as skits about saving wildlife and saying no to single-use plastic, poster making about saving the planet, clicking photographs of the school's flora, and making murals using broken tiles and bottle caps.
