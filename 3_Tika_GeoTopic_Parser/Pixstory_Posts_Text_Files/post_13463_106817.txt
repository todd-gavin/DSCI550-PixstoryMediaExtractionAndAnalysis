Afghanistan was already one of the most dangerous places in the world to be a child Over the past year the situation has become even more desperate as crippling poverty and hunger violence drought and COVID have collided to create an humanitarian emergency Rising malnutrition an unprecedented food crisis drought disruptions to vital health and nutrition centres and poor access to water and sanitation services have left more than half of the population in need of assistance Millions of children girls especially risk being permanently shut out of education
