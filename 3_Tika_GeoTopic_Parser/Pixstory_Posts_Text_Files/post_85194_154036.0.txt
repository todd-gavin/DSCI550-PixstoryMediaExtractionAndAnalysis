Celtic and RB Leipzig will be up against each other on Matchday 04 of the UEFA Champions League. Celtic are on the bottom spot in the points table in Group F of the UEFA Champions League 2022-23 while RB Leipzig sit at the third spot in the points table.
Celtic have underperformed this season in the Champions League. They have drawn and lost a couple of  games so far that they played in the Champions League. Celtic have lost to Real Madrid and RB Leipzig and have drawn against Shakhtar Donetsk in the UCL so far.
RB Leipzig, too, haven’t been able to put up inspirational performances in this year’s UEFA Champions League campaign so far.. They have played three games this season so far and have won a game and lost a couple in the coveted European league.
