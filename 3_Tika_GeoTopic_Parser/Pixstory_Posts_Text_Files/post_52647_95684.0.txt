Browsing the internet can be tedious with so many websites and its hard to catch up with the site you need in hand . These are four websites that are generally good for students and go un noticed.
1) Shortlyai - is an excellent website to easily and instantly write essays. The program has AI which completes your paragraphs 
2) Z-library- provides you with comprehensive library of open - source books that are unique and helpful. This resource allows you to download free ebooks.
3)Podcastle - can convert any text from an academic textbook ,article or novel into your own podcast. 
4)EDX - online learning platform offering 2000 courses from 160 universities worldwide. Helps students expand their skills and knowledge.
