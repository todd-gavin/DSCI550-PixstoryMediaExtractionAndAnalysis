The news has finally arrived that we all wanted to hear from Roberto De Zerbi and his staff who have left the hotel in Kiev where they had been entrenched for days after the outbreak of war The Shakhtar Donetsk coach and eight other compatriots who are staff members of the Ukrainian team are now in journey to a safe place This is what we learn from Ansa which reports that a decisive role for this operation was played by UEFA which constantly kept in contact with the president of the Football Federation Gabriele Gravina Gravina in turn was engaged in acting as an intermediary with De Zerbi and his collaborators who were barricaded inside a hotel in the Ukrainian capital and had decided to leave the country now under siege by the Russian invasion De Zerbi had repeatedly refused to leave unless his players had escaped first
