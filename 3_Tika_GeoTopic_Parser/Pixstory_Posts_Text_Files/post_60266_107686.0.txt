According to the latest research by the brand value consultant "Brand Finance," Indian Hotels Company's "Taj" has been regarded as the world's strongest hotel brand.

The Taj scored an overall Brand Strength Index of 88.9 out of 100, with a AAA grade for consumer familiarity, employee happiness, corporate reputation, and world-class customer service.
Brand Finance's annual report honours the world's most valuable and powerful hotel brands.

Taj was named first in the top ten strongest hotel brands by Brand Finance, ahead of Premier Inn, Hilton Hotels and Resorts, Hampton by Hilton, Embassy Suites Hotels, JW Marriott and Shangri-La Hotels & Resorts, Residence Inn by Marriot, Waldorf Astoria Hotels and Resorts, and W Hotels Worldwide.

"With a Brand Strength Index (BSI) score of 88.9 out of 100 and a corresponding AAA brand grade, Taj Hotels (brand value up 6% to $314 million) is the strongest brand in the ranking," according to the report.

Taj, like other hotels throughout the world, was devastated by the pandemic and accompanying national lockdowns, but it was able to successfully alter its methods to remain relevant to the needs of travellers.

"With agility and strategic initiatives like delivering support to the healthcare sector, Taj was at the forefront of this,".
