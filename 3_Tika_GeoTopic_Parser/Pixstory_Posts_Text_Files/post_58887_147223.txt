Following midnight raids against the PFI in at least locations across states nearly people were arrested including all top leaders of the Popular Front of India The searches were carried out in concert by the National Investigation Agency the Enforcement Directorate and state police Among the states searched were Bihar Kerala Andhra Pradesh Delhi Uttar Pradesh Karnataka and Telangana The entire crackdown was being monitored by the Union Home Ministry Searches were conducted in the residential and official premises of people involved in terror funding organising training camps and radicalising people to join proscribed organisations according to the NIA Specifics about the cases detentions and locations will be disclosed
