Hoardings showcasing Congress MLA Sachin Pilot have appeared at several spots in Jodhpur even as the ongoing political chaos in Rajasthan over the choice of the state s next chief minister drags on The posters are inscribed with the slogan Satyamev Jayate Naye Yug ki Taiyari Last night MLAs who support Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot threatened to resign if Mr Pilot his arch rival in the Rajasthan Congress were given the opportunity to succeed Mr Gehlot as chief minister This heightened the competition for the position in the state which is governed by the Congress The MLAs who support Mr Gehlot were unable to attend a meeting with the central leadership of the Congress to resolve the issues between the two opposing groups
