It is a life saving noble act Uncountable lives have been saved by those uncountable people who have donated their blood for a cause and a good deed A person who has donated their blood must know how they changed the course of one s or many people s lives in a positive manner In order to raise awareness and give recognition to those performing this act of love and kindness Every year on June World Blood Donor Day is observed all over the world The day shows support for blood transfusion services blood donor groups and other non governmental organizations involved in blood donation campaigns
