Arsenal officially announces their partnership with Pixstory in an attempt to eliminate hate speech and abuse in online fan comments and debates This tie up means that Pixstory is the go to social media application for the team and fans to connect From memes to footballs discussions and more Pixstory is a safe space for any conversation As a part of this partnership Arsenal will also be partaking in campaigns with Pixstory with the purpose of creating a safe space on the Internet free of toxicity and negativity This is a big step for both Pixstory and Arsenal towards creating a positive environment for football fans all over the world
