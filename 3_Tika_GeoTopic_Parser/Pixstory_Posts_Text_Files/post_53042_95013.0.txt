Daniel Aldana Cohen is Assistant Professor of Sociology at the University of California, Berkeley, where he is Director of the Socio-Spatial Climate Collaborative.

His work focuses on the intersections of the #ClimateEmergency, housing, political economy, social movements, and inequalities of race and class in the United States and Brazil.

He is Founding Co-Director of the Climate and Community Project and co-author of critically-acclaimed A Planet to Win: Why We Need a Green Deal (Verso 2019)
