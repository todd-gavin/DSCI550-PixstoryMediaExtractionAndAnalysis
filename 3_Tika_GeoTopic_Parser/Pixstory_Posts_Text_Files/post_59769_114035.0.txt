On Thursday, Punjab Chief Minister Bhagwant Mann will marry Gurpreet Kaur, a doctor by trade, in a private ceremony at his official residence in front of their close friends and family. 

Arvind Kejriwal, the chief minister of Delhi and national convenor of the Aam Aadmi Party, will attend the wedding along with other notable figures. For the event, Bhagwant Mann's mother travelled to the CM residence from their home hamlet of Satauj in Sangrur, Punjab.
