BSF Jammu Neutralized three Pak Smugglers on Samba border and recovered 36 Packets of Narcotics.

In the early hours of 6 Feb, the alert troops of BSF Jammu neutralized 03 Pak smugglers who were trying to smuggle Narcotics through Samba International border and recovered 36 Packets (Approx 36 Kgs) of Narcotics , likely to be Heroin and thwarted a big smuggling attempt.
The search of the area is in progress.
