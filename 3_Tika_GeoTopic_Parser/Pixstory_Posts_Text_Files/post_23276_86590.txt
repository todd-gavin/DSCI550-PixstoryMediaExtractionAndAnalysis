Negotiations failed and the meeting on Thursday March between the foreign ministers of Russia and Ukraine which was held in Turkey on Thursday March was resolved with nothing Two weeks have passed since the Russian army invaded the Ukrainian territories and while the bombing continues which also affect civilian targets, the damage count begins which is already estimated to amount to over billions of dollars The Russians have surrounded numerous Ukrainian cities and are destroying much of their infrastructure making it increasingly difficult if not impossible for civilians to evacuate More than millions have left their homes including the thousand refugees of the last two days
