Live updates on India's Missile Test: On June 6, 2022, at approximately 1930 hours, a successful training launch of an Intermediate Range Ballistic Missile, Agni-4, was carried out from APJ Abdul Kalam Island in Odisha. 

The successful test was carried out as part of the Strategic Forces Command's routine user training launches. 
The launch confirmed all operational parameters as well as the system's dependability. 
The test's success reinforces India's goal of maintaining a "Credible Minimum Deterrence" capability.
