South Korea announced Thursday that Seoul plans to provide $ 1 million in humanitarian aid to earthquake victims in Afghanistan.

Taliban spokesman Zabihullah Mujahid, said the world was receiving aid from some countries in the region and the world.

Mujahid has confirmed that two planes have received Iranian humanitarian aid and that Qatar has sent one humanitarian aid plane.

According to him, Pakistan has sent eight trucks full of food and essential items to Paktika province.

Meanwhile, Japan has announced its readiness to provide assistance to earthquake victims, and the United States, Germany and Turkey have said they will provide assistance to earthquake victims in Afghanistan.
