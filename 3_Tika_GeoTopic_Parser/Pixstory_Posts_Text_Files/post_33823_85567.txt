After being purchased for just pounds sterling through the lottery promoted by the eBay Dream Transfer project Roberto Carlos is back on the pitch The initiative created for charity provided for the possibility of having a football legend just like Roberto Carlos available for just one match The Brazilian full-back made his debut today with the Bull in the Barne United amateur team of the Shrewsbury District Sunday Football League the equivalent of the Italian third tier The former Real Madrid full-back scored a goal from a penalty but was unable to prevent his team from defeat in the friendly loss against Harlescott Rangers
