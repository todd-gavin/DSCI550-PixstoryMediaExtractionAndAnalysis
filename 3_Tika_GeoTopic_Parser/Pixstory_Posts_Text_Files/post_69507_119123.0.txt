A student from Delhi Public School Budgam has received a state honour in the sports category. 
A statement from the school read, "FarheenFayaz, a student in Delhi Public School Budgam's 12th Humanities class, has been bestowed with the State Award for the Outstanding Sports Person on July 15, 2022, for which she was nominated on January 26, 2019." 
"She received this honour in recognition of her contributions to the sports industry. A PencakSilat player who has competed in a number of State, National, and International tournaments during her athletic career, the girl has exceptional sporting skill, it added. 
The statement read, "She earned numerous gold, silver, and bronze medals and made not only the Valley but her Country proud.
