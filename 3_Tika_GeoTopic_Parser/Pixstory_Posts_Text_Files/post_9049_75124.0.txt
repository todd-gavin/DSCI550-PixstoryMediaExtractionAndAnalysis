🙏Heartfelt tributes to Shri Balasaheb Thackeray Ji on his birth anniversary. My ancestors and the whole Kashmiri pandit community owe a lot to his unswerving support while we lived in exile.

 
Do you know?
Balasaheb Thackeray went out of his way to extend support to the Kashmiri Pandits during the exodus. He helped the community settle in Maharashtra, and got seats reserved for the students in state colleges, along with offering heavy concession in fees.
