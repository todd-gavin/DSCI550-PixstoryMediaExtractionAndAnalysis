Bollywood actor Ranbir Kapoor turns 40 today and to celebrate that he had thrown a big Bollywood birthday bash last night in his Vastu residence in Bandra, Mumbai. It was a midnight affair and was attended by several celebrities and stars like Karan Johar, Akash Ambani and many other.Today morning, the birthday boy celebrated his day along with his fans and wife Alia Bhatt, in front of his residence. He cut the cakes that his fans had brought for him while Alia Bhatt sat beside him looking prettier than ever in an yellow suit. Alia Bhatt also took to Instagram to wish her darling husband a very happy birthday with a Polaroid of last night’s party and captioned it “Happy 40 Baby 💛”
