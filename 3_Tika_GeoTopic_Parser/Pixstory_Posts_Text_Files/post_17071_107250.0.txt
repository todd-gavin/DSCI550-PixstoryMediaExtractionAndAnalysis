Sara Ali Khan has shared a sweet memory as she remembered her first co-star, late actor Sushant Sing Rajput on his death anniversary. Sharing a picture from the shoot of their film, Kedarnath, Sara wrote that he gave her many firsts of her life. She also mentioned how she would be connecting with him on Tuesday

Sharing the picture with Sushant on Instagram, Sara wrote, “From facing the camera for the first time to seeing Jupiter and the Moon through your telescope - so many firsts have happened because of you. Thank you for giving me all those moments and memories.”She further added, "Today on Full Moon night when I look up at the sky I know you’ll be up there amongst your favourite stars and constellations, shining bright. Now and forever. #JaiBholenath."

Sara made her acting debut with Kedarnath, with Sushant as her co-star. Directed by Abhishek Kapoor, the film revolved around the devastating floods in Uttarakhand in 2013
