What would an Australian kid s birthday party be without the ever present cheap and cheerful fairy bread This simple snack consists of a slice of white bread whole grain rye and sourdough not welcome spread with margarine and topped with rainbow colored sprinkles known as hundreds and thousands Beloved by Aussies young and old there is even a Fairy Bread Day on the th of November each year to celebrate this dish As per CT the first description of this sugary treat can be traced back to from a Perth based confectionery company though the name fairy bread was not associated with it until much later The fact that there is even Fairy Bread merchandise available speaks to the special place this nostalgic treat holds in Australian hearts and stomachs
