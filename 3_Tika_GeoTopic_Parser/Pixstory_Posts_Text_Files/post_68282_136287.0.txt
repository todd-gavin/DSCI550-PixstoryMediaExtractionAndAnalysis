Commonwealth Games Gold medallist Nayanmoni Saikia was formally appointed as a Deputy Superintendent of Police (DSP) by the Assam Government on Saturday.

Assam Chief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma handed over the letter of appointment to a 29-year-old Lawn Bowl player at Janata Bhawan in Dispur.

The DSP post was a reward for her fine showing at the Commonwealth Games. 

However, she was also awarded by giving a cheque of Rs. 50 lakhs for her excellent performance.
