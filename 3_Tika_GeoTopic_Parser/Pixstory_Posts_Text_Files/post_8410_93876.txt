Russian warship Moskva that was damaged by an explosion on Wednesday has sunk while being towed to port Russia s defence ministry has confirmed late on Thursday Previously The Ukrainian southern military command claimed late on Thursday to have struck the Moskva with Neptune anti ship missiles causing it to start sinking and forcing the crew to abandon ship Russians however denied the ship had been struck and said a fire had broken out on board that resulted in an explosion of ammunitions In their statement on the sinking Russian officials said that the ship sunk in stormy conditions due to hull damage sustained during the fire from detonating ammunition They also repeated that all crew members had been evacuated The apparent attack and sinking of the flagship days after Putin launched his invasion of Ukraine represents a symbolic blow to the Kremlin The crew vessel Moskva was the pride of Russia s Black Sea naval fleet and the most prestigious vessel involved in the war against Ukraine Sources
