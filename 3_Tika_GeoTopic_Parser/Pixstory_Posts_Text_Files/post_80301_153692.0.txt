Srihari Nataraj successfully completes his campaign.
In the men's 100m Freestyle final at the Sardar Patel Aquatics Complex today, Olympian Srihari Nataraj (Karnataka) finished his swimming participation in the National Games in a blaze of glory, winning his sixth gold medal. He won in a new National Games record time of 50.41 seconds.
Sajan Prakash, a senior from Kerala, won five gold, two silver, and one bronze medal throughout the course of the week. He swept to a 100m Freestyle victory today, with Sajan Prakash coming in eighth. Srihari succeeded in completing his task, winning two gold medals in the freestyle sprint event to go along with his two backstroke victories and leading the Karnataka relay teams to two more golds.
