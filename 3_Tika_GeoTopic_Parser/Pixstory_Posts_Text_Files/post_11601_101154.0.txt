Be it anytime of the day you can go for a smoothie especially when you want to make a meal replacement. So even if it is the breakfast, lunch, dinner, dessert, snack, post workout, late night cravings you have got the smoothie. 

Ingredients to follow 

• 1 cup kale (or spinach) 

• 1 cup almond milk (unsweetened) 

• 1 cup blueberries (frozen) 

• 1 banana 

• 1 tablespoon almond butter (or raw almonds) 

• 2 tablespoon rolled oats 

• 1 serving Protein Smoothie Boost (optional) 

Instructions 

• Mix kale and almond milk in a high speed blender until it becomes smooth. 

• Eventually add in the remaining ingredients and blend them until it becomes smooth. 

Tips to use 

• Take some frozen fruit to make smoothie cold 

• You can switch from almond milk to any non dairy milk that you would like.
