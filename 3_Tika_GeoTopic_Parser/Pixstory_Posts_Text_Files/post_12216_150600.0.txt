The official logo and motto of the games was unveiled on 22 July 2022.
The official motto of the games is "Celebrating unity through sports".
The games mascot is Savaj the asiatic lion. The logo depicts an Asiatic lion head make up of sporting pictograms and the Statue of Unity. This game wants to celebrate the unity of the Indians across the state.
The Games due to various unavoidable reasons are happening after a gap of five years. And the budget estimated for the same is 2000 crore.
