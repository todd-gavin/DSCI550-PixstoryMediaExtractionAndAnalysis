Iss saal hone wale hai president ke liye elections. Jisme NDA ki DRAUPADI MURMU aur UPA ke YASHWANT SINHA ke beech kaate ki takkar nazar aa rahi hai. 

President RAM NATH KOVIND ka karyekaal 24 july, 2022 ko khatam ho raha hai. Aur president chunaav 18 july, 2022 ko hai. 

President election me loksabha, Rajyasabha aur Vidhansabha ke members vote daalte hain. 245 members wale Rajyasabha, 543 members wale Loksabha aur 4033 MLA vote daalte hain. 

Har ek vote ki value alag hoti hai aur total value 10,86,431 votes ki hai. Aur jeetne ke liye 5,43,216 votes ki zarurat hoti hai. 

Maana jaa raha tha ki agar sabhi opposition party ek sath apna collective president face utare to iss baar opposition ka president ban sakta tha. 

Lekin opposition puri tarah se bikhra huya nazar aa raha hai. Aur NDA ki DRAUPATI MURMU ke sath 6.67 lakhs value vote hai aur opposition ke YASHWANT SINHA ke sath 3.89 lakhs value vote hai aur jeetne ke liye 5,43,216 value votes ki zarurat hai.
