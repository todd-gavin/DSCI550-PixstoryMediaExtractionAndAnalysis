Ingredients: 

1. 6cups of seedless watermelon( cube shape)
2. 3cup of cucumber( cube shape)
3. 225 crushed cheese- feta
4. 1/4 cup of virgin olive oil 
5. 1 tablespoon of lemon juice.
6. 1/2 teaspoon lime zest
7. 1 handful mint leaves
8. Salt and black pepper as per requirement. 

Preparation:
1. Add lemon juice, lemon zest, olive oil, salt, mint leaves, pepper into a bowl and mix them well.
2. Add the main ingredient watermelon and cucumber into another bowl.
3. Pour the already mixed solution over watermelon and cucumber.
4. Add crushed feta cheese and toss it well. 

Here is our freshening watermelon feta salad.
