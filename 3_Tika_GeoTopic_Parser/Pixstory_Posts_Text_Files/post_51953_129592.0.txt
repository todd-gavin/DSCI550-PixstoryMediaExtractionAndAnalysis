To mark the World Photography Day, here is the brief story of an energetic, passionate  solo travel photographer , Dr Kaynat Kazi, born in Firozabad and did Ph.D in Hindi from Agra university. She is also an author and blogger . Facing a lot of challenges and difficulties, yet She has traveled many countries and twenty states of India to capture real life of India. Due to sicknecc caused by high altitude in Laddkh during travelling, she was admitted to Army hospital for two days and also fell ill in Gulmarg of Kashmir. She kept travelling due to her passion for photography. She tells, I got the inspiration for traveling in childhood when I used to listen and read about travelling of Chinese travellors to India , thousands year back
