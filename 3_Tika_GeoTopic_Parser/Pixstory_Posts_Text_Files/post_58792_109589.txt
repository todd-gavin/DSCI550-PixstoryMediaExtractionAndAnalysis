Arvind Kejriwal the Chief Minister of Delhi stated on Friday that the future Delhi Sports University will offer degrees to athletes to assist them in finding employment if they so want The lack of facilities a lack of financial backing and political interference in selection procedures he claimed were no longer issues for athletes in Delhi Players will receive degrees from the Delhi Sports University in their chosen sports including the BA in Kabbadi BA in Wrestling and BA in Cricket among others At a ceremony to give financial aid to athletes here Kejriwal stated that this would help them find employment should they choose to do so Sportspeople receive financial support through the Mission Excellence programme There is no political meddling in the process of choosing players for the programmes here and we have better sports facilities Under the Mission Excellence programme players received financial assistance totaling Rs crore on Friday
