The newspaper La Gazzetta dello Sport has done the calculations to understand in which brackets the Italian teams qualified for the next Champions League will be placed. Serie A is currently the only European championship in which the qualifiers for the next edition of the top continental competition are already known, i.e. Milan Inter Naples and Juventus The Italian champion team will come out of the head-to-head between the two Milanese and this will be placed in the first tier together with the winners of the other championships and therefore Real Madrid Bayern Munich PSG one between Manchester City and Liverpool one Portuguese between Porto and Sporting Lisbon plus this year&#39;s Champions League and Europa League winners. Milan would be placed in the bottom pot, aiming for third pot also for Napoli, while Juventus are currently fourth in the standings three days from the end of the championship and would probably be placed in the second pot in the company of Chelsea and Barcelona
