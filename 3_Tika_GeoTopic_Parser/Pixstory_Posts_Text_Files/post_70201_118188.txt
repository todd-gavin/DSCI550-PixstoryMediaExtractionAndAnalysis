Unnati Hooda and Bharat Raghav won girls and boys singles title while Divyam Arora Mayank Rana won boys doubles title at the Yonex Sunrise All India Junior U Ranking Badminton Tournament at Manohar Parrikar indoor stadium here in South Goa on Thursday Sathwik Reddy K and Vaishnavi Khadkekar won the mixed doubles title while Isharani Baruah and Devika Sihag were crowned girls doubles champions The final day started with the girls singles summit clash between Unnati Hooda and Devika Sihag both from Haryana The contest progressed on an even keel with both girls displaying top notch skills Unnati took the first game but she was error prone in the second game As a result Devika leveled the match taking the game The deciding game was testimony to the prodigious talent of Unnati who is the youngest Indian to win a BWF Super competition as she fought back from being five points down at to win the final game and with that the match in one hour seven minutes In the boys final second seed Bharat Raghav of Haryana surprised Pranav Rao Gandham with a triumph The minute duel proved to be a largely one sided affair contrary to the expectation of a high voltage thriller Pranav Rao who had displayed a ferocious game in his previous outings looked off colour while Bharat Raghav looked composed and in total control Devika Sihag also from Haryana was amongst the winners in the girls doubles event having begun her day with runner up trophy in singles The year old Infosys Foundation shuttler teaming up with Isharani Baruah of Assam registered a fantastic comeback win against TN duo of Gnanadha Karthikeyan and Sania Sikkandar in an exciting tussle In the mixed doubles final Sathwik Reddy K and Vaishnavi Khadkekar got the better of Vighnesh Thathineni Sri Sai Sravya Lakkamraju in an all Telangana affair In the boys doubles Divyam Arora and Mayank Rana defeated Darshan Pujari and Abhinav Thakur seeded in minutes
