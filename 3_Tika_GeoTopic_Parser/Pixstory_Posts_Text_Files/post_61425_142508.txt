Christmas in France Christmas is celebrated every year with great zeal in France The holiday is observed in the country on December People gather in cathedrals and churches across the country to pray and sing Christmas carols Many French families go to the circus People change presents like wrapped chocolates or candied fruits with some notes inside They are known as Les Papillotes They put up a Christmas tree and the decorated with ribbons and balls attend special religious services and savor hot Christmas meals such as caviar oysters lobsters and roast turkey stuffed with chestnuts After the meal they eat the thirteen traditional desserts they represent Jesus and the apostles and are composed of dried fruits such as dates and figs and a traditional cake called oil pump
