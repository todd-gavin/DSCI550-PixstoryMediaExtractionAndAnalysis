A little more than a year after the withdrawal of the United Nations and the control of the Taliban the threat of famine threatens Afghanistan in the coming winter Millions of families in Afghanistan have almost no way to cope with another harsh winter ahead The World Food Program announced on Monday in a report that the organization needs billion dollars to be able to provide monthly aid to the million Afghans who are severely food insecure in the next six months The report states that in the past year Afghanistan has witnessed the collapse of its fragile economy loss of jobs and destruction of homes and livelihoods due to climate shocks
