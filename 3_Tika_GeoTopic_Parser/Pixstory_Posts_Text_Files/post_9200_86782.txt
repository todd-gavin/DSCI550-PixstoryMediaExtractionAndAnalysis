Paying my humble tributes to the Satyagrahis who started the Dandi March on this day in led by Mahatma Gandhi His simple act of protest shook the roots of colonialism became a pivotal moment in the struggle for Indian independence Do you know The Salt March was a collective beginning of a mass resistance movement against British tyranny The British had prohibited Indians from collecting or selling salt Indians were also forced to buy it from the British who not only exercised a monopoly over its manufacture and sale but also levied a heavy salt tax
