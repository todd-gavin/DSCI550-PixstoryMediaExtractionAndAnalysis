We're excited to announce that Mr. Atsushi Nakamura has joined the Sudeva family as Head Coach of the Sudeva I-League Team. 🎉
Mr. Nakamura's credentials include a pro license from the Japanese Football Association, as well as extensive experience as a football coach & trainer at highly reputable organizations such as Bhutan's National Team, as well as some top-tier Japanese football teams like Togichi City FC and Sagan Tosu FC.
We can't wait to see what he has in store for SDFC 🙌🏽
