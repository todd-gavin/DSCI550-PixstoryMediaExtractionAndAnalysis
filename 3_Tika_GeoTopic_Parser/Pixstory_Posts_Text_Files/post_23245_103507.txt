Building on the success achieved in Rome, the Torlonia marbles exhibition Collecting masterpieces arrived in Milan where it will remain under construction at the Intesa Sanpaolo Galleries of Italy until September A unique opportunity to admire the precious marbles Originally at the end of the 19th century, many sculptures were of the museum founded by Alessandro Torlonia in via della Lungara in Rome Ninety-six works including busts statues and a sarcophagus have been brought back to their former glory thanks to a complex restoration work carried out in the Torlonia Laboratories thanks to the commitment of the Bulgari maison and with the contribution of Gallerie d Italy And after Milan they are ready to leave for a world tour
