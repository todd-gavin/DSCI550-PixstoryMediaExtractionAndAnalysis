Union Minister G Kishan Reddy on Friday held a meeting to discuss various developmental projects and North East Economic Corridor and said that the main aim includes economic development of the region and improvement of its trade with South East Asia.

In his address, Reddy said that the ministry will support the implementation of the ambitious projects of the Northeast economic corridor.

"I congratulate Asian Development Bank (ADB) for the excellent study on the Northeast economic corridor. DoNER (Development of North Eastern Region) Ministry will fully support the implementation of the ambitious projects of the Northeast economic corridor.
