Chief Minister Yogi Aditya Nath took stock of flood situation of Ganges in Varanasi. He took stock of situation by riding on motor boat of the NDRF . Ganges has started decreasing but still flowing above the warning level. He boarded the boat with administrative officials and watched to the affected people and localities along the Ganges. He directed officials to speed up relief operation for the affected citizens on a warfoot. chief Minister also went to Ghazipur district and took aerial view of the flood and met the affected people in relief camps there . He distributed necessary essential commodities to the displaced people, taking shelter in government relief camps there
