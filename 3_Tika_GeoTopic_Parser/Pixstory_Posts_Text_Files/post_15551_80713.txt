The story of the famous Lady Diana arrives at the theater with the show Lady D written by Clelia Ciammarelli and directed by Pino Ammendola on stage from to February at the Teatro Lo Spazio Immediately after the road accident under the Pont De L Alma tunnel in Paris from the smoking wreckage the voice of Lady D hovers from the car the most loved princess in the world In a sort of near-death delirium she begins to tell her story leading the spectators into her most intimate and secret world Annalisa Favetti restores the sweetness, grit and agony through the story of Lady D as a woman as a human being who suffers mistakes but always finds a way to recover The scepter of life
