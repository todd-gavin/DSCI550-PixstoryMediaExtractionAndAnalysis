By Shailesh Khanduri New Delhi Aam Aadmi Party supremo and Delhi chief minister Arvind Kejriwal Monday said that CBI has arrested his deputy Manish Sisodia Kejriwal said this during his public rally in Gujarat Addressing the public gathering Kejriwal said that Sisodia was to come to Gujarat but he was arrested today They will keep him in jail for a month till the elections in Gujarat are over and then he will be released Kejriwal added
