Smoothies are an increasing popular
wellness trend and its frequently marketed as a “health
food"

Here are a few pros and cons of this popular drink:
Pros:
• If you're someone whose fruity and vegetables is
inadequate, smoothies can be a delicious way to pack in
2-3 more servings.
• Increases fiber intake, some of the most common fiber
rich foods are also common smoothie ingredients,
including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, nuts and seeds.

Cons:
• May contain large quantities of added sugar
• Can be High in calories
