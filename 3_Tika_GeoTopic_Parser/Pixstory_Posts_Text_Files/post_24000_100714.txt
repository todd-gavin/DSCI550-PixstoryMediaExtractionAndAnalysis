Fishing can be exhilarating and also surprising at times like in Cambodia when fishers landed up with an enormous stingray From the depths of the Mekong River they brought out this stingray which had eaten up a fish snagged on the line said an article in livescience com It was a giant freshwater stingray whose scientific name is Urogymnus polylepis and it had been netted by local residents of northeastern Cambodia s Stung Treng province This news was conveyed to the Wonders of the Mekong project a conservation group run by University of Nevada Reno UNR in tandem with local fishing authorities Read more
