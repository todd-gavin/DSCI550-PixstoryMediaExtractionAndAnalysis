An idyllic South Pacific Islands beach coconut palms bristling above a bone white sliver of sand and luminous aqua water lapping softly on shore This is the delicious stretch of sand on Direction Island in the Cocos Keeling islands This remote Australian Territory of two atolls and coral islands lies about a hour flight from Perth In with permission from the locals beach expert and author Brad Farmer christened this sublime slice of coast Cossies after Australia s th Governor General Sir Peter Cosgrove Bordered by lush coconut palms this beguiling beach curves seductively out into the tropical turquoise sea and you can snorkel among coral gardens and diverse marine life in the crystal clear waters
