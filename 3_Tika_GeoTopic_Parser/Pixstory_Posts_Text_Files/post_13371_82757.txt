One of the most important human needs is to achieve peace of mind and many of the actions and deeds he does are to meet this need The question that has always been a concern for man is what or what is the way to achieve true peace of mind The question that is clearly answered in our religious texts is that true peace is possible only with the remembrance of God and the highest degree of remembrance of God is achieved through prayer The question that this article seeks to answer is how from the point of view of the Holy Quran one can find peace through prayer And in what way will this be achieved
