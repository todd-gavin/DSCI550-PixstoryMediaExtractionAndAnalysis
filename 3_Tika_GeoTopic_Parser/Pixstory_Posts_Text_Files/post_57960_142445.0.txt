Nari was looking forward to her school life ! Like every other student she was excited to make friends, hang out and study together like all normal people. Somehow she managed to become friends with the 3 of school 's prettiest girls who are always in the limelight. 
They get attention every where they go and Nari is starting to feel left out. 
She starts feeling inferior and this impacts her life .will Nari let go of her friends due to her complex or will she able to face her fears ? 
A webtoon which teaches you the value of true friends and importance of inner beauty. Because  there is  more to life than  just being good looking and pretty.It has been adapted into a Kdrama titled  World of my 17
