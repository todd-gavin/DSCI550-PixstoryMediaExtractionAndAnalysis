Baltal Valley is situated at almost 2,743 meters and located 15 kilometers north of Sonamarg in Jammu & Kashmir. The valley is incredibly beautiful, with snow-capped mountain peaks, waterfalls, glaciers, and a merriment Sindh River. This is the ideal location to visit if you enjoy both nature and photography. You can also enjoy breathtaking views of the Zoji La Pass and the mountains that surround it from this location. A well-known base camp location for pilgrims travelling to the Amarnath Cave is the Baltal Valley.
