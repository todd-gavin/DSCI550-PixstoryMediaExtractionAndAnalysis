The wait for YOUNITE is finally nearing its end!
On March 18 at midnight KST, YOUNITE shared a teaser for their debut EP "YOUNI BIRTH." The EP will be released on April 20 at 6 p.m. KST. YOUNITE is Brand New Music's newest boy group which includes former X1 member Lee Eun Sang as well as former JYP Entertainment trainee and "LOUD" contestant Lim Kyeong Mun. In February, YOUNITE confirmed their group name and began dropping teasers and prologue films to introduce their members.
