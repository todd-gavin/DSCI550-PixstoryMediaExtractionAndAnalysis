Tara Sutaria, Tiger Shroff and Nawazuddin Siddiqui recently attended a promotional event for their upcoming actioner 'Heronpanti 2' directed by Ahmed Khan. Tara Sutaria had donned a gorgeous black and white outfit and surprised fans with her fringe.
The 26-year-old styled her day's look with pointed-toe pumps and gold accessories such as multiple rings and textured hoop earrings.
