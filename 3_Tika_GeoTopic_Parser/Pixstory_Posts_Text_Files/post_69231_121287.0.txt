Kashmir is renowned for its illustrious history, vibrant culture, and, above all, its exquisite cuisine. When we talk about food, the Kashmiri Wazwan is the first dish that comes to mind. This lavish feast is cherished by non-vegetarians all over the world. But vegetarians should not give up hope! Every foodie can find something to enjoy in the Valley of Rich Cuisines. The dried foods, or Hokh Syun in Kashmiri, are equally as delicious and flavorful. As they say, inventions are born out of necessity. The winters in the Kashmir Valley are notoriously cold and harsh, and when it snows, the valley frequently becomes cut off from the outside world. In order to persevere through the challenges.
