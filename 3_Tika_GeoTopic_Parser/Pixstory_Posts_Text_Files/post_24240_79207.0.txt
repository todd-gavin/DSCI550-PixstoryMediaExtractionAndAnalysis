State Disaster Management Authority and Uttarakhand Police on Friday ramped up rescue and relief operations for people after heavy rainfall and snowfall battered parts of Uttarakhand.

SDRF, Uttarakhand Police teams rescued people stuck at places due to extreme weather conditions. The official information received from SDRF today informed that as many as six people, who were trapped due to snowfall in the Diwalikhal area of Chamoli district were rescued by a team of SDRF.
