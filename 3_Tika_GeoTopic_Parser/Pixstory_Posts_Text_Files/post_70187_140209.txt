FC Tuem President Caitano Fernandes has revealed that he is ready to contest for the post of GFA President and has a vision for Goan football He wants to bring about a change in the game over the next four years There is a lot that Goa Football Association can do for the upliftment of the sport The small clubs in villages are closed due to financial reasons and i am willing to spend money from my pocket The maintenance of the Duler turf which is in a bad shape will be my top priority he pointed out to mediapersons on Thursday I will share my vision plan for the game with the football clubs across the state in the coming days I want to make it clear from the start that I will be contesting for the post of GFA President not for greed or make money from their coffers but instead fill it up he added
