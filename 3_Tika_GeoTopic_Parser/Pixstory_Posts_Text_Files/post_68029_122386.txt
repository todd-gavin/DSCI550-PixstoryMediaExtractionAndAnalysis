The Board of Control for Cricket in India BCCI announced the schedule for the upcoming Australia and South Africa s tour of India India s international home season will commence with a three match T series against Australia in September and will be followed by a three match T and ODI series against South Africa The BCCI press release on Wednesday confirmed Barsapara stadium in Guwahati as one of the hosts for the much awaited T series which will be played just ahead of the T World Cup in Australia The home series against South Africa will begin in Thiruvananthapuram The second T will be played on October nd at Guwahati followed by the last T at Indore The action will then shift to Lucknow on th October where the ODI series will get underway Ranchi and Delhi will host the second and third ODI respectively Earlier in January India was scheduled to play at Barsapara stadium against Sri Lanka in a T match which was abandoned due to rain and poor playing conditions
