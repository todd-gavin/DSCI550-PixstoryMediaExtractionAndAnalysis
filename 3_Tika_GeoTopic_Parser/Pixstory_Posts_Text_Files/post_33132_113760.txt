After a long pursuit of Turkey that lasted for all the days of competitions, Italy concluded the XIX edition of the Mediterranean Games hosted by the Algerian city of Oran in a triumphant manner. For the Italian Olympic team, this is the consecutive affirmation in the final medal table of a well-known event also as the Mediterranean Olympics in which all the countries bordering the Mare Nostrum participate. In the end, the overtaking of the Turks materialized with the shower of gold medals in recent days, mainly from swimming and fencing. Italy finished with gold, silver and bronze in front of a surprising Turkey and France booed by the Algerian public The hosts of Algeria conquered gold medals finishing in fourth place overall ahead of Spain or Egypt and Serbia both with It is the absolute victory of the Belpaese at the Games on overall editions and the next edition will will be held in Taranto
