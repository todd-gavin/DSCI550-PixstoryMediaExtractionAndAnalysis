Mother nature has always stood by our side and it is time we repay her The following measures can be taken to restore her health Smoke from industry should be appropriately disposed of The cleanliness of the surroundings should be given special consideration There should be an increase in the number of trees and plants planted Tree cutting should be prohibited indiscriminately Vehicles should only be used in dire circumstances For the disposal of tainted and toxic chemicals strict legislation should be imposed People should raise awareness of the environment s importance
