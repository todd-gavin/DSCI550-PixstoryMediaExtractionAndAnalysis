Nimona is coming to Netflix after years of development and production halts The streaming service will release a feature animation adaptation of ND Stevenson s best selling comic novel in with production beginning earlier this year The film directed by Nick Bruno and Troy Quane Spies in Disguise stars Chloe Grace Moretz and Riz Ahmed and is produced by Netflix and Annapurna Pictures A synopsis for the animation reads A Knight is framed for a crime he didn t commit and the only person who can help him prove his innocence is Nimona a shape shifting teen who might also be a monster he s sworn to kill Set in a techno medieval world unlike anything animation has tackled before this is a story about the labels we assign to people and the shapeshifter who refuses to be defined by anyone
