Chandigarh: After the intervention and assurance by Punjab Revenue, Rehabilitation and Disaster Management Minister Mr. Bram Shanker Jimpa, the staff of Revenue Department, on Wednesday, called off strike and assured the minister to immediately return to work to provide hassle free services to public with full zeal and in transparent manner.
During a meeting with Punjab Revenue Officers Association here at Punjab Bhawan, the Revenue Minister assured representatives of the association that all their demands will be considered sympathetically, while asking them to immediately join their offices in larger public interest.
