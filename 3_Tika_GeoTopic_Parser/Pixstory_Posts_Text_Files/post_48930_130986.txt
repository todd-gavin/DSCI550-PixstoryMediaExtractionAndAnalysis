Ingredients cup vegan butter or vegan margarine cups sugar cup cornstarch teaspoon baking powder teaspoon salt cups all purpose flour teaspoons vanilla extract Instructions Preheat oven to degrees F degrees C Grease and flour one x inch baking pan In a medium bowl cream vegan butter and sugar until light and fluffy Add cornstarch baking powder and salt mix well Gradually add in flour mixing until just blended This mixture will be thick Pour batter into the prepared pan spread evenly Bake for minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean Cool before frosting or serving as desired
