In the history of Afghanistan the Hazara people have suffered various collective sufferings in different periods According to history and the testimony of undeniable documents massacres ethnic cleansing enslavement of women and children forced migration from ancestral lands usurpation of land and property systematic racial discrimination social exclusion systematic exclusion from economic and political relations and other types of sufferings and hardships Jami is a summary of the hardships that this ethnic group has experienced in the history of Afghanistan Therefore the systematic military political and social pressure on millennials and the rejection of this ethnic group is not only a new phenomenon that has been applied to this ethnic group with more intensity than in the current era
