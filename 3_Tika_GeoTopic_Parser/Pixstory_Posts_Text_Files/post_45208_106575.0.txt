Iceland will face Israel in the UEFA Nations League on Monday at Laugardalsvöllur.


Iceland is coming off a 1-0 victory over Fabrizio Costantini's San Marino in their most recent match. Arnar Vidarsson's Iceland won thanks to a goal from OB midfielder Aron Elis Prandarson in the first half.Iceland, on the other hand, has only won two home games in the last 20 months, with Liechtenstein being their lone victory on home soil since the start of 2021.


Israel, on the other side, recently defeated Edoardo Reja's Albania 2-1. Alon Hazan's Israel won thanks to a second-half brace from Shakhtar Donetsk striker Manor Solomon. Albania's goal, scored by Chelsea striker Armando Broja, was only a consolation.Due to Russia's expulsion from the tournament as a result of its military invasion of Ukraine, Israel has emerged as the favorite to win the three-nation group.
