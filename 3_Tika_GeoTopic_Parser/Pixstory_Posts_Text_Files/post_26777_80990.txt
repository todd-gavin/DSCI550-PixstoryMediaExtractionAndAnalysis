The tailoring of the duo behind the brand is a constant reference to the artist Meret Oppenheim and her idea of sculpture For the inauguration of New York Fashion Week McCollough and Hernandez worked on historical silhouettes such as peplums and bustiers contextualizing them in a contemporary Drape and parts of peplums adorn the female figures on the catwalk by alternating bold colors such as blue and yellow with a purely naturalistic palette made of beige and gray without ever diverting attention from the most important point of the body, the waist
