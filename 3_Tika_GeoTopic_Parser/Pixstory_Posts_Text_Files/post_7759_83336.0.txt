The Weeknd will premiere a fully immersive cinematic music special of his nsw album, Dawn FM, the name is "The Dawn FM Experience". It will premiere exclusively on Prime Video on Saturday, February 26.
The Weeknd in a statement said,  “I’m ecstatic to partner with Amazon to premiere the most elaborate live TV special I’ve ever done...Welcome to the next phase of Dawn FM—a purgatory otherworld where live performance, theater, and performance art collide for a night out at the club.”
