Tarte Tatin Tarte Tatin is a pastry of French origin It is prepared from caramelized fruits, generally apples The apples are caramelized in butter and sugar then cooked on a golden puff pastry The name of the dish was borrowed from the Tatin sisters who invented it and served it as a signature dish in their hotel over the years This dish can also be made in turnovers when the dough is not only cooked upside down but also inverted. best wine to pair with tarte tatin could be Sparkling White Wine of California Sweet White C rons Sweet White Sauternes etc
