Felipe Caicedo is no longer a Genoa player The striker saw the appeal presented to the Arbitration Board to which he had turned to obtain a renewal for another two seasons with the rossoblu rejected. Why this Because the contract signed with Genoa was annual but included an option for two successive seasons that he himself should have asserted. Genoa, which entrusted the lawyer Mattia Grassani of the Court of Bologna, demonstrated that Caicedo had not correctly exercised the option right and consequently his contract had not been renewed. The Arbitration Panel agreed with the club by rejecting the two requests presented by the attacker
