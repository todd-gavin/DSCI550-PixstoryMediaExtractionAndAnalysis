Five meerkat puppies were born in the Cornelle park Meerkats suricatta to the delight of all the little fans of Timon from The Lion King These friendly mongooses typical of the desert areas of Southern Africa are easily identifiable thanks to their innate curiosity which drives them to stand upright on their hind legs The group made up of the five latest arrivals and eight other adult specimens The increase in the number of members of the small family of meerkats is an excellent sign that the protection program that the Park has implemented for the conservation of the species has proved to be effective and efficient. Four other puppies from recent years, now adults, have been transferred to the zoological facility in Fasano
