This morning the Spanish National Police arrested three men identified as the perpetrators of the theft that took place in Ibiza in the villa rented by Marco Verratti together with his wife Jessica Aidi. They are members of a gang that the police have been following for some time. were arrested at the port of Denia, a town where ferries to the Balearic Islands depart and arrive Part of the loot was found in the operation The theft had taken place on Sunday evening and was simplified by a window left open Euros in cash and various valuables had disappeared such as diamonds and watches whose value at the time of the complaint was estimated by Verratti in millions of euros
