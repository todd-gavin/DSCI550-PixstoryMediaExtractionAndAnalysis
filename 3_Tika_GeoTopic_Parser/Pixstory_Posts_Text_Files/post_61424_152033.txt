Camille Pissarro Camille Pissarro was an impressionist and nei impressionist painter of French Danish origin He was born in St Thomas now in the American Virgin Islands but then in the Danish West Indies Great predecessors like Gustave Courbet and Jean Baptiste Camille Corot inspired Pissarro He helped found a society of fifteen budding artists by playing the pivotal role in maintaining the organization and motivating the other participants Not only was Pissarro a member of the group but also by virtue of his intelligence and character. balanced kind and warm according to art historian John Rewald Pissarro was the dean of the impressionist painters Pissarro filled his canvases with depictions of the daily life of French peasants unlike the impressionists known for their depictions of city streets and country houses. rural recreation
