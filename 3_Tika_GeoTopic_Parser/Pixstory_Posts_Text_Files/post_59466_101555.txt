Heartstopper is a sweet heartwarming adaptation of Alice Oseman s graphic novel series of the same name it follows Charlie and Nick two British boys who fall madly in love over the course of a school year Charlie is his school s only gay kid had experienced bullying in the past for the same and is recovering from his own past experiences On the other hand Nick Nelson is a straight very popular rugby team player who is now questioning his sexuality and feelings for Charlie Spring The series has done amazing work with these sensitive topics I completed the whole series episodes each of mins approx in one sitting with a big smile on my face This is such a sweet joyous wonderful series that everyone should watch The ending felt like a solid warm hug It is one of the most adorable adaptations ever and I can not recommend it enough If you have already watched it let me know your thoughts too
