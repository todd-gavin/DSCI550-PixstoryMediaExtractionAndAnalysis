I know that everyone is looking for the one answer to help improve their digestion, but oftentimes I find it comes down to a handful of small behaviors throughout the day that can make a world of difference!
These tips may seem simple but I challenge you to give them a try and feel the difference for yourself!

*stay hydrated!
*reduce stress
*eat slowly and chew!
*incorporate bitter foods
*eat foods rich in soluble fiber
*look at your food (seriously!)
*avoid drinking around meals
*tune into your hunger and fullness cues
*incorporate foods with natural digestive properties
*focus on what foods feel good and which one's don't
