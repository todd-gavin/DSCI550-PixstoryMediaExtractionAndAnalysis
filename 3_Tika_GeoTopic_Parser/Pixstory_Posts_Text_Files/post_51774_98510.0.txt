BTS have got fans all over the world excited ever since they announced their comeback on June 10, 2022. A logo trailer for their new album was released on May 4, which revealed the title as well as what to expect from it. The new album will be called ‘Proof’, and will be a mix of new and old songs. It will be an anthology album.

BigHit music, in a statement said, “After ceaselessly sprinting forward since 2013, BTS is celebrating their 9th anniversary this coming June. It is all thanks to ARMY who have sent us your unwavering love and support."
