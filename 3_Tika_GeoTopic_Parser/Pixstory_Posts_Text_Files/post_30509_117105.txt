The Richard Mille branded watch stolen from Charles Leclerc last spring in Tuscany has been traced to Spain Investigations by the Neapolitan Police Headquarters made it possible to identify the perpetrators of the theft, three snatchers from Naples already known to the police who tried to place the loot hoping to obtain an amount close to one million euros. The presence of a serigraphy with the pilot&#39;s signature on the model dedicated to him by the Swiss company made the watch too recognizable, which paradoxically lost its market value. The object was then purchased by an entrepreneur Spanish for 1,000 euros and tracked down by the Anti-Robbery Section Mobile Squad
