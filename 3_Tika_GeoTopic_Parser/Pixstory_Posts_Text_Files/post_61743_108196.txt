Shiva is the omnipotent God in the hindu mythology Shiva is considered as one of the big gods of the hinduism He is known for his calm demeanor and his dispassionate love for the good and bad people alike Shiva is a complicated being While he is the embodiment of sacrifice he is also the embodiment of anger The dichotomy of Shiva is what makes him so interesting He is the God who hasn t shied away from helping the demons but he is the same God who also gets angered at the slightest disturbance in his meditation Shiva is also known as Kailashpathy meaning the one who resides at Mount Kailash Shiva is the destroyer of evil and is said to be one of the most powerful beings on the planet even without his godlike powers
