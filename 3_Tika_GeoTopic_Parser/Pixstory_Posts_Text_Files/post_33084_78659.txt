FINA International Swimming Federation has officially announced the postponement of the World Aquatics Championships scheduled for May in Fukuoka, Japan and for the Short Course Swimming Championships scheduled for in Doha, Qatar Both events postponed by a year due to the Pandemic and political situation in the Rising Sun and so Paltrinieri Quadarella co will have to wait an extra year to fight for the world crown The new dates for the Fukuoka World Water Sports Championships are from to July while the Doha championships will be held in January a few months from the Paris Olympics Due to this change in the calendar, the key event of the will become the European water sports championships which will be held in Italy in Rome from to August
