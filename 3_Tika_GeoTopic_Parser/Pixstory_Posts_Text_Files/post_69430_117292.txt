The crunchy deep fried lotus stem fritters known as Nadir Monje are Kashmir s all time favourite street dish The other name for the lotus stem is nadur in kashmiri The lotus stems are split lengthwise dipped in a batter made of rice flour and deep fried in a bowl of oil Due to its rough nature you usually need to boil the stem before frying At these Nadir Monj carts one can find these deep fried lotus stems along with potatoes peas and fishes similarly dipped in rice flour batter followed by being deep fried It pairs perfectly with the chutney provided along It s a must to try this mouth watering street food if you are in Kashmir anytime
