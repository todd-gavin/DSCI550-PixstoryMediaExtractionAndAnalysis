In the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir s Anantnag district close to Pahalgam is the alpine lake known as Tulian Lake It is metres above sea level and kilometres miles southwest of Pahalgam and kilometres miles from Baisaran The lake frequently has ice floes floating on it Mountains that reach heights of nearly metres feet and are typically covered with snow encircle it on three sides It is situated in a pine forest dotted meadow The massive Himalayan range includes the lake The colour of the lake which contrasts with the snow capped mountains in the distance makes it one of Kashmir s most picturesque landscapes A freshwater lake can be found there
