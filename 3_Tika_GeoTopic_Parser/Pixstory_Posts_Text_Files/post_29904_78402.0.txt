Lewis Hamilton was born on 7 January 1985 in Stevenage, England. Won the British Formula Renault title in 2001 and the Formula 3 Euro Series two years later. Won his first Formula One World Championship in 2008 at the age of 23 years, nine months and 26 days. After six successful seasons with McLaren, Lewis joined the works Mercedes team in 2013. He secured his first podium in a Mercedes Formula One car in only his second race.
