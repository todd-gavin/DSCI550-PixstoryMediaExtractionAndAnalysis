The Hyundai Venue is the first small SUV to emerge from hiding this month. Due to the fact that it accounts for 42% of all SUV sales, this car is quite significant for the Korean manufacturer. According to Hyundai, one of the reasons the compact SUV segment has been growing at the expense of hatchbacks is because 38% of first-time customers choose the Venue. We test drive the 2022 facelift avatar to see if it is more than simply a cosmetic makeover.
