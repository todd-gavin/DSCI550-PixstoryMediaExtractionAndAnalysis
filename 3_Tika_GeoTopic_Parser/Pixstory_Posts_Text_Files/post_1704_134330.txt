Music maestro AR Rahman s already sparkling achievement list just added another gemstone to it He now has a street named after him in the City of Markham in Canada The year old globally popular singer composer shared the news on social media on Monday He also expressed his heartfelt gratitude to Markham s mayor Frank Scarpitti and the Canadian citizens for this incredible honour Talking about his adopted name the Jai Ho singer also said that AR Rahman means merciful and wished that the name brings peace prosperity happiness and health to all the people living in Canada
