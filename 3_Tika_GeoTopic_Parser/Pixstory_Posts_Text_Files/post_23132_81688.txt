The regional government of Balearic Spain has approved a moratorium which freezes the granting of new tourist licenses for establishments such as hotels and lodgings for four years. This is a measure aimed at moving from a tourism model based on quantity to one that favors quality according to regional president Francina Armengol Cos for the next four years the tourist licenses granted cannot be higher than the current ones explained the government The measure designed to try to promote sustainable development of the tourism sector from a social, economic, environmental and territorial point of view
