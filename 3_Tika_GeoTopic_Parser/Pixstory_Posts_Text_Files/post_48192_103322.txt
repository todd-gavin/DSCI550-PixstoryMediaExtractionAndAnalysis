Punjabi singer and Congress leader Sidhu Moosewala was shot dead in a firing incident at Jawaharke in Mansa district on Sunday According to available reports two others were also injured in the incident in which more than rounds were fired Moosewala was rushed to the civil hospital in Mansa in a critical condition where he was declared dead The incident happened a day after the Punjab Police ordered the withdrawal of security of his security along with more than people including former MLAs Jathedars of two Takhts heads of Deras and police officers The year old singer had joined the Congress in December last year and unsuccessfully contested Punjab elections on a Congress ticket from Mansa district Source Hindustan Times
