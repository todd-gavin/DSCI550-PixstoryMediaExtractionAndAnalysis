After receiving overwhelming reviews from critics and fans, Alia Bhatt's Gangubai Kathiawadi seems to be setting the box office on fire. Directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali, the film is racing towards Rs 100 crore in India. According to reports, Gangubai Kathiawadi saw a big jump in collections on Sunday (March 6). Early estimates suggest that the film could collect over Rs 10 crore.
