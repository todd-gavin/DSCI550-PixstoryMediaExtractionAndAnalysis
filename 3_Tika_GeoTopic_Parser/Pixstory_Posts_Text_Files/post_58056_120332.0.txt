Empress Laya from  The witch and the Bull. 
Meet the charming yet dangerous empress of the witch empire . She is cunning, manipulate and ruthless . She will trick you with her sweet words and have you walk into the trap yourself. Ruling the empire for about 300 years she still manages to retain her beauty using magic . She wins the war without fighting herself . An evil empress indeed.
