If freehand drawing your cat eye just isn’t gonna happen for you, grab a spoon🥄 and use it as a stencil🖇️.
•Hold the stem of a spoon🥄 against the outer corner of your eye👁️💕.
•Use a liquid eyeliner  to draw a straight line for the cat eye look😸👁️.
•Then, flip the spoon so it’s hugging your eyelid, and use the rounded outer edge to create a perfectly curved winged effect💕.
And you are done with this perfect winged eyeliner within 2 minutes with a simple makeup hack ladiess👠!!!
