Litti Batsi and Chokha
Litti, along with chokha, is a complete meal originated from the Indian state of Bihar and parts of Eastern Uttar Pradesh. It is a dough ball made up of whole wheat flour and stuffed with gram flour, pulses and mixed with herbs and spices and then baked over coal or wood then it is tossed with much pure desi ghee.It is popular traditional dish of East UP, Bihar, MP, Jharkhand, Chhatisgarh and Rajasthan. Hardworking people of low income group buy and taste this fresh cooked at affordable price of Rs 20 to 30 for one plate to satisfy their appetite. 
With passage of time, amid era of fast food, it is becoming more popular. Even in marriages of rich people in above mentioned states, one separate stall of Litti, Bati, Daal and Chokha is being served. A unique restuarent of Litti Bati has been opened on Varanasi- Allahabad highway at Dafi near Ganges in Kashi. It is being looked after and managed only by laides. Men are not allowed alone, they can come with families. It may be first of its kind in Northern Indian States.
