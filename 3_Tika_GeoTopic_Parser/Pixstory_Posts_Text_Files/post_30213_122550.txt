The friendly scheduled in Tel Aviv between Juventus and Atletico Madrid is skipped, the last test before the start of the championship which should have been played on Sunday evening The tensions currently being experienced in Israel with a series of air strikes carried out in the Gaza Strip which were followed by the launch of numerous rockets towards Israeli territory The Islamic organization Ziad Nahaleh specified that Tel Aviv and all other Israeli cities are being targeted, which is why it preferred to avoid a potentially risky trip Both teams, who now find themselves having to organize a last-minute test before the start of the season, have confirmed the cancellation of the match via social media. Due to the latest developments, the friendly between Atletico Madrid and Juventus scheduled in Israel has been canceled. wrote the Turin company
