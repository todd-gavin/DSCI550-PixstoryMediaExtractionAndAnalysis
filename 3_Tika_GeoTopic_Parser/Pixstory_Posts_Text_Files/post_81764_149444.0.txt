Shilpa Shetty performs Navaratri Pooja at home. 
Actress Shilpa Shetty has kickstarted the festivities in her home by performing pooja to mark the beginning of Navaratri. She took her Instagram to shared a video of her pooja at home. She captioned the video " Shardiya Navratri ki dheron shubhkamnaayein! (Many Many happy returns of Shardiya Navratri). Happy Navratri to all of you. "
