Misadventure on vacation for Marco Verratti The midfielder of the national team and Paris Saint Germain was in Ibiza staying in the villa owned by the former Brazilian footballer Ronaldo According to reports from Il Diario de Ibiza, thieves allegedly broke into the property stealing jewels and cash belonging to the player and his companions for a value of millions of euros The Guardia Civil confirmed the theft but not the value of the stolen goods At the time of the break-in, no one was present in the house After the filing of the complaint this morning, the investigations are ongoing
