Every year on June 21st, since 2015, the International Day of Yoga has been observed around the world. 

Prime Minister Narendra Modi declared on June 21 that yoga provides harmony to our universe as he chaired the major event of the International Day of Yoga in front of the magnificent Mysuru Palace. 

The Prime Minister was joined by tens of thousands of people in a mass Yoga demonstration. 

"Everything in the universe begins with our own body and soul. Mr. Modi stated, "The universe begins with us." "Yoga also makes us aware of all that is going on inside of us and develops a sense of awareness."
