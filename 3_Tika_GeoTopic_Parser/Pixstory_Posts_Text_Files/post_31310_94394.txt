Feminist and genderqueer Xiran Jay Zhao won over critics and audiences with the US release of her first novel Iron Widow The first volume of a duology that will captivate those looking for powerful feminist narratives wrote the Publishers Weekly Twenty-year-old raised in Vancouver with Chinese immigrant parents Xiran Jay Zhao studied biochemistry loves popular culture and Chinese history fantasy and science fiction At the center of this narrative debut of hers is the eighteen-year-old Zetian who fights against the aliens who have invaded the Earth against the unjust rules against a still powerfully male-dominated world
