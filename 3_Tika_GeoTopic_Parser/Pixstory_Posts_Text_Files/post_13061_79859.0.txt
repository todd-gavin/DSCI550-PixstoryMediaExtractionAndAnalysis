Cristiano Ronaldo, the famous star of Manchester United Football Club, became the first person to reach the record of 400 million followers on Instagram, one day after his 37th birthday. She is followed by Kim Kardashian's sister Kylie Jenner with 308 million and Lionel Messi with 306 million followers.

The famous football player has more than 3,200 Instagram posts, each of which has received an average of 10 million "likes". Ronaldo, who celebrated his birthday a few days ago, thanked all his fans on this social network.

"Life goes on and on," he wrote. Hard work, high speed, immediate goals, hard expectations ... but in the end it all comes down to family, love, honesty, friendship and values ​​that are dear to us. "Thanks for all the messages."
