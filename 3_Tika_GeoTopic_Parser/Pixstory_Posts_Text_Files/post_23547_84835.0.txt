On March 1st, the International Federation of the Phonographic Industry (IFPI) revealed its rankings of last year's best selling albums. 

SEVENTEEN landed in the Top 10 with two of their albums- their ninth mini album, "Attacca", ranked at no.3 while their eight mini album ranked at no.8. 

Meanwhile BTS landed in the Top 10 with their Japanese compilation album, "BTS, THE BEST", at no.4. 

Source: Soompi.com
