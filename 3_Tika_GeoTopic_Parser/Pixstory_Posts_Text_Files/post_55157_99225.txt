Australia has been ranked among the most open minded countries on the planet in study after study and it was put to the test last year with a postal survey on marriage equality Despite months of divisive public debate percent of Australians voted in favour of legalising same sex marriage a resounding and long overdue win for a society that prides itself on its progressive principles No wonder it is called The Lucky Country According to the United Nations Human Development Report and the Economist Intelligence Unit s quality of life index Australia is one of the world s most prosperous countries Since the moniker was coined half a century ago The Lucky Country has become a popular nickname for Australia It s an accurate moniker for a nation that enjoys such an enviable climate stable political system and wealth of natural resources
