Veteran Star Asha Parekh is going to be felicitated with the highest recognition in the field of Indian cinema,  Dada Phalke Award 2020. Anurag Thakur, Union Minister for Information and Broadcasting  declared the news on Tuesday. The veteran actress is well known for her outstanding performance in films such as "Dil Deke Dekho", "Kati Patang", "Teesri Manzil". Till date, the star has worked in more than 95 films and had even served the  Central Board of Film Certification from 1998-200 as the Chairperson .
