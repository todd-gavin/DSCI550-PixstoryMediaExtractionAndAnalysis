Monopoly which is the best selling privately patented board game in history gained popularity in the United States during the Great Depression when Charles B Darrow an unemployed heating engineer sold the concept to Parker Brothers in Before then homemade versions of a similar game had circulated in many parts of the United States Most were based on the Landlord s Game a board game designed and patented by Lizzie G Magie in She revised and renewed the patent on her game in Notably the version Magie originated did not involve the concept of a monopoly for her the point of the game was to illustrate the potential exploitation of tenants by greedy landlords Magie used the Landlord s Game to promote a remedy for such exploitation namely the single tax on property owners a leading social issue among those who criticized land speculation as a cause of economic injustice
