Andy Warhol born Andrew Warhola Jr August February was an American artist film director and producer who was a leading figure in the visual art movement known as pop art His works explore the relationship between artistic expression advertising and celebrity culture that flourished by the s and span a variety of media including painting silkscreening photography film and sculpture Some of his best known works include the silkscreen paintings Campbell s Soup Cans and Marilyn Diptych the experimental films Empire and Chelsea Girls and the multimedia events known as the Exploding Plastic Inevitable
