Madhya Pradesh's Advait Page won the 800m freestyle gold. Sajan Prakash (Kerala) and Aryan Nehra (Gujarat) win silver and bronze, respectively.
Women's 100m backstroke gold medalist Maana Patel of Gujarat sets a new National Games record with a time of 1:04.35. The gold medalist in the men's 100m backstroke is Srihari Nataraj of Karnataka.
The men's 50m butterfly is the final event of the day, and Sajan Prakash of Kerala wins the gold medal by taking the lead. Tamil Nadu's Rohit Benediction accepts the silver medal, while Harsh Saroha of Haryana defeats Srihari Nataraj to claim the bronze.
