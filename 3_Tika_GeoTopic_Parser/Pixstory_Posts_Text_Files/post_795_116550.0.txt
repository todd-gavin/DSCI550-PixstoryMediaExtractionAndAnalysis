I am not a great fan of Virat Kohli. But I respect him for what he is. That he is loud is not an issue for me because he is among the boldest sportspersons in India.

He can speak his mind. He backs his men to the hilt and he is not afraid of getting this wrong. And his ability to work harder each passing day is amazing. 

See how he sees things. 
Amid the constant discussions over the 33-year-old's batting struggles, Kohli took to his official Twitter profile to share a motivational quote with a cryptic one word caption - ‘Perspective’.

The quote read, “What if I fall? Oh, but darling, what if you fly?”
