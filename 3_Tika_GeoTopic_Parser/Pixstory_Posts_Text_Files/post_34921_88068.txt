The Russian Football Federation saw its appeal on the suspension of all clubs and the national team from Fifa competitions rejected The sentence by the CAS which officially rejected the request presented The main objective was to be able to participate in the playoffs for the next World Cup in which however Russia will not be present The CAS press release states that as a result the contested decision remains in force and all Russian teams and clubs continue to be suspended from participating in FIFA competitions The CAS arbitration proceedings continue the establishment of a panel arbitration and the parties are exchanging written briefs. No hearing has yet been set
