Shooting reasults of national games 2022, Gujarat
The winner of the women's competition was Neeru from Madhya Pradesh. The silver medal awarded to Sabeera Haris of UP, and Seema Tomar of Services won the bronze.
The winner of the men's trap competition was Vivaan Kapoor of Rajasthan, who scored 31. Silver was won by Zoravar Sandhu of Punjab (age 26), and bronze was awarded to Balabhadra Tarashia of Services (age 20).
