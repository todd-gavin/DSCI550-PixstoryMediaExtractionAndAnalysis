The artistic director and presenter of the Italian Song Festival was appointed Ambassador of Milano Cortina The investiture took place on the stage of the Ariston Theater during the second evening of the Sanremo Festival and is the natural recognition for a great sports fan who has supported with enthusiasm the Road to The Games towards the Winter Olympics and Paralympics of Amadeus joins a dream team which already includes Deborah Compagnoni Federica Pellegrini Alberto Tomba Francesco Totti and Bebe Vio
