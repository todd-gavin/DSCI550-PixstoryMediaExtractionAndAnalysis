Indian hockey star and one of the state icons of Mizoram Lalremsiami returned home after winning a bronze medal in the recently concluded Birmingham Commonwealth Games She was received by a warm reception at the Lengpui airport Lalremsiami Hmar was one of the key players in the Indian women s hockey team in the recently concluded Commonwealth Games She is also the first woman from the state to win a medal at the Commonwealth Games A special state reception was arranged for her when she arrived at Aizawl s Lengpui Airport She was formally welcomed by Dr Lalrozama Secretary of the Sporting Youth Services Department Vice Chairman of the Sports Youth Affairs Board Pu TC Pachhunga and other government officials as well as various sports associations and well wishers
