government Nepal s domestic demand has reduced and India has reduced buying more electricity the Nepal Electricity Authority NEA saw massive power spillage during the last few weeks As Indian electricity went to waste in a week during Dashain said Kul Man Ghising managing director of the NEA If we calculate the cost of spillage based on Rs per unit we lost as much as Rs million in potential earnings in a week The Nepal authority said spillage reached as high as MW at times with domestic demand falling as low as MW against the peak demand of around MW The total installed capacity of Nepal s power projects exceeds MW and all of them were operating at their total capacity aided by continued rainfall for days during the festival season All the extra electricity has
