Women’s
Maharashtra narrowly defeated Odisha to win the gold medal by just 2 points. Meanwhile, Delhi defeated Karnataka to win the bronze medal.
Final - Maharashtra beat Odisha 18-16
Third Place - Delhi beat Karnataka 1-0
Men’s
In the Men’s Event, Maharashtra registered a convincing victory over Kerala by 4 points to win the gold medal. West Bengal defeated Karnataka in the third place match to win the bronze medal.
Final - Maharashtra beat Kerala 30-26
Third Place - West Bengal Beat Karnataka 1-0
