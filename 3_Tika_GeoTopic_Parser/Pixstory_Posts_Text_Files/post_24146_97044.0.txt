At least 11 persons have died due to during the chariot procession at Appar Swami temple of Tamil Nadu's Thanjavur today.

"A total of 11 people have died. Three people died on the spot and seven died in the hospital. Another 15 people are injured and have been taken to Thanjavur Medical College for treatment. FIR has been filed and an investigation into the matter has started," said V. Balakrishnan Inspector General of Police, Tiruchi Range.
