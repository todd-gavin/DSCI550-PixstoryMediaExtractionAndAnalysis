To give healthy and nutritious foods to kids it s necessary to present them in a great and interesting way Also having a touch of nutrients in this dessert like snack which is also simple to make and delicious to eat Ingredients and materials you will need Bananas Vanilla Greek Yogurt Skewers Sprinkles optional but fun INSTRUCTIONS Peel off and cut down the Bananas Pour the vanilla Greek Yogurt in a small bowl Dip in each banana piece into the yogurt and keep each piece on the parchment paper According to your choice you can add sprinkles or leave them plain Place them in the freezer for one hour it s better to freeze them overnight Sources Healthy Family Project
