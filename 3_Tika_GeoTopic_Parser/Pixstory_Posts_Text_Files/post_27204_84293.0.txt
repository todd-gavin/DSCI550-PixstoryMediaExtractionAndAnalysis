Will Smith chose to wear Dolce&Gabbana in occasion of the 28th Annual Screen Actors Guild Awards at Barker Hangar in Santa Monica, California.
 The actor and SAG winner of the “Outstanding Performance by a Male Actor in a Leading Role” award is wearing a custom midnight blue three-piece suit featuring a double-breasted waistcoat and satin peak lapels. He finished his look with a blue shirt and a black textured silk tie.
