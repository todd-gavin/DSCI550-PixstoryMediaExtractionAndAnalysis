Toot is one of the fruits that Afghans eat fresh and dried.
The most famous Toots in Afghanistan are white Toot, seedless Toot, blackToot, Ebrahim Khani Toot, sixty Toot and berry milk.
Toota is very popular in Kabul style and many mulberry trees can be seen in this area.Panjshir province has different types of Toot due to its suitable and refreshing climate.

Toot is one of the indigenous foods of the people there. Gardeners have long used to invite their close friends and relatives when the Toots are cooked, or their friends used to go to the berry-eating holidays, a custom that is still alive.Toot is often eaten in the morning or evening, when the stomach is empty of food, so that the body can digest food quickly and absorb it.
