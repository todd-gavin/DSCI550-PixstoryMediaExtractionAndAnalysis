Deepika Padukone is currently enjoying the love and success that she received after her last release 'Gehraiyaan.' The actress seems to be in a celebratory mood and hosted a success party for the cast at Bastian Worli restaurant in Mumbai. Director Shakun Batra was seen outside the restaurant as he made to the success party of his film also starring Siddhant, Ananya and Dhairya Karwa.The lead actor of the film Siddhant Chaturvedi made a stylish entry at the success party. He was seen wearing a red shirt, black ripped jeans and black hat.
