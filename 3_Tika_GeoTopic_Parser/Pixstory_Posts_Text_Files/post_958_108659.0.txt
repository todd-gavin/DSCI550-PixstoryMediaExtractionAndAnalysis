This is a heritage jackfruit tree in Panruti, Tamil Nadu. The tree which is more than 200 years old, yields 150-200 fruits a year, weighing 3 kg to 12 kg.

The State fruit of Tamil Nadu has an age-old connection with Panruti.  Panruti is also supposedly the only place in India where jackfruit is grown as a monocrop (a single crop throughout the year). Each tree yields around 150-250 fruits per season. Panruti has an annual production capacity of 45,000 metric tonnes to 50,000 metric tonnes.
