Today is superstar Katrina kaif's 39th birthday.
On this wonderful day, lets have a look at our beloved actress's finest work.

1. Zindagi Na Milegi Dobaara

Three friends decide to take a bachelor's trip to Spain that they had planned during their school days when one of them gets engaged. This film received wide popularity among the crowd and was one of the biggest blockbusters of the year. Kat plays the character of Laila in it.

2. Jab Tak Hai Jaan

Released in 2012, Jab Tak Hai Jaan is a romantic drama directed by Yash Chopra. The film stars Katrina with superstar Shahrukh Khan alongside Anushka Sharma. One of the best romantic movies of our times.

3. Ek Tha Tiger

A spy thriller starring Katrina with her Mega Movie star Salman Khan, the film released in 2012. The plot of the film revolves around the life of two spies Zoya & Tiger played by Katrina and Salman respectively who fights against all odds for their love.

4. Race

This film released in 2008 and starred Saif Ali Khan, Bipasha Basu, Akshaye Khanna, and Katrina Kaif. The film plays with the idea of 'Everything is fair in love and war' when two brothers become enemies. One of the best thrillers to come out of bollywood containing all the elements like betrayal, love, actions and music.

5. Bang Bang

Based on Tom Cruise's Knight and Day, this is the Bollywood remake of the film. The film starred Hrithik Roshan alongside Katrina Kaif.

A complete entertainment package full of romance, hyper action and fabulous music.
