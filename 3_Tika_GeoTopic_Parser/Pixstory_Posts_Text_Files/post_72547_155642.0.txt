
We all are Students in one way or another. We gather information from different arenas of life in our personal and professional fields.
But we must understand one simple but significant thing that the source of knowledge is not just books ; mean while the biggest encyclopedia which we always misses out is People around us.
A P J Abdul Kalam once said: one good friend is better than one good library.
It's time to read people, their actions, attitudes and thoughts.
Let's discover new spectrum of ideas and implications from our fellow mates and all kinds of people around us.
Let's be a voracious reader of people than books....
