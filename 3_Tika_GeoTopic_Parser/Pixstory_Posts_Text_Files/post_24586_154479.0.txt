Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh have qualified for the summit clash of the Men’s Hockey event. The winner of the final will take home the gold medal, while the loser gets the silver medal. Meanwhile, the semi-finalists Haryana and Maharashtra will lock horns in a bid to win the bronze medal.
In the Women’s event, Punjab will play Haryana in the final, while Madhya Pradesh will lock horns with Jharkhand in the third-place match. All 4 matches across both men’s & women’s events will take place on Tuesday, October 11.
