On Tuesday, the Islamic University of Science and Technology (IUST) in Awantipora commenced a two-day Kick Boxing Championship. 

There are 270 athletes competing, according to the statement. The district Pulwama Kickboxing Association and 42RR, Victor Force are working together to organise the event. 

Prof. Naseer Iqbal (Registrar, IUST) inaugurated the competition,SSO Awantipora Muhammad Yousuf, Commanding Officer 44 RR Colonel Rajkumar Singh, and others were present.
