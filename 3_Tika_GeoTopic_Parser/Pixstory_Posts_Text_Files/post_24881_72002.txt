According to IIT Mandi researchers from the Indian Institute of Technology IIT Mandi and the International Centre for Genetic Engineering and Biotechnology ICGEB in New Delhi discovered phytochemicals in the petals of a Himalayan plant that could be utilised to treat COVID infections The findings of the study team were recently published in the journal Biomolecular Structure and Dynamics according to the statement Among the different types of therapeutic agents being studied phytochemicals chemicals derived from plants are considered particularly promising because of their synergistic activity and natural source with fewer toxicity issues said Dr Shyam Kumar Masakapalli Associate Professor BioX Centre School of Basic Science IIT Mandi We re on the lookout for interesting compounds in Himalayan flora using multi disciplinary approaches
