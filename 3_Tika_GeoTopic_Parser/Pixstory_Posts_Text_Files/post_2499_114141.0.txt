Brussel's "Floral Carpet" - Show Stopper :

Every 2 years there is a flower carpet made out of 750 000 flowers , in the capital of Belgium, Brussels.

 It`s a tradition that started in 1952, but it wasn`t until 1971 that the first large flower carpet was made on the "Grande-Place" in Brussels.

Every two years there is a different theme and this year the ‘Belgian Presidency of the Council of the European Union’ is the theme.

The Flower Carpet is 70 m long & 24 m wide with mostly 1,680 m2 of begonias, dahlias, grass and bark. To complete the display , hundred volunteers assemble the carpet in less than eight hours.
