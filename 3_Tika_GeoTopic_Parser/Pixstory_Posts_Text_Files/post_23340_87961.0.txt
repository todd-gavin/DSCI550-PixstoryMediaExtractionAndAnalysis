“This little girl is the future and, in this war, she reminds us what Ukrainians are fighting for 💪🏼🇺🇦” JR Artist

Unveiled to the world today, this work depicts 5-year-old Valeriia from Kryvyi Rih, Ukraine. A drone was used to capture the cover image, a 45 meter photo of Valeriia held up by more than 100 people outside the National Opera in Lviv on March 14 2022.
