The Hyderabad edition of Modern Love is set to release on 8 July 2022 on Amazon Prime. 

Modern Love Hyderabad by Nagesh Kukunoor, Venkatesh Maha, Uday Gurrola and Devika Bahudhanam will showcase a bouquet of 6 heart-warming stories that explore the various facets, shades and emotions of love across myriad human relationships.

Hyderabad has vouched for timely modernisation in the last decade. We all enjoyed the series in English(Modern Love New York )and Hindi (Modern Love Mumbai) now it's a bit to watch the modern love in our Language (Telugu) and our culture.
