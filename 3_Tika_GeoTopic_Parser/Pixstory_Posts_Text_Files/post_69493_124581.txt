Elite J K cricketers will compete against one another in a first of its kind day competition on August at the SK Cricket Stadium Sonwar The multi day J K Cricket Association competition which included league games being played simultaneously at several sites in Jammu and Kashmir is now over Five teams made up of the Under and Senior Cricketers from each province competed against one another in two day matches Top cricketers from J K including Umran Malik Abdul Samad and Parvez Rasool took part in the matches Two teams have been formed that will compete in the final game after the league stage is over elite athletes from each squad were selected based on their league performances According to JKCA authorities this will be the first time a domestic three day match has ever been played in J K The final will be a three day affair Source Greater Kashmir
