By BestMediaInfo Bureau The Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) has extended the deadline for sending comments on the 'Draft Telecommunication (Broadcasting and Cable) Services Interconnection (Addressable Systems) (Fourth Amendment) Regulations, 2022' to November 4, 2022. Counter comments, if any, may also be submitted by November 18, 2022.The stakeholders have sought an extension of time for sending their comments. Although, TRAI said that no further requests for extension would be considered after this. TRAI issued the 'Draft Telecommunication (Broadcasting and Cable) Services Interconnection (Addressable Systems) (Fourth Amendment) Regulations 2022' on September 9, 2022, for comments/counter-comments of stakeholders. The last date 'for receiving written comments from the stakeholders was fixed as October 7, 2022, and counter comments, if any, by October 21, 2022.
