 S S Dempo College Goa College of Engineering champions at Inter Collegiate Badminton The Goa University Inter Collegiate Badminton championship for men and women was held at S A G Sports Complex Paddem from th to th August S S Dempo College of Commerce and Economics Cujira won the women title and Goa College of Engineering Farmagudi won the men title In the women finals S S Dempo College defeated St Xavier s College Yashmin Sayed defeated Sunidhi Wader in the first singles Sachi Dhavlikar defeated Sinikka Sapeco in the second singles In double Yashmin Sayed amp Samiksha Pednekar defeated Sachi Dhavlikar amp Sunidhi Wader Conceded In the men finals Goa College of Engineering Farmagudi defeated St Xavier s College Mapusa In the first singles Mrunal Bhatkande defeated Menino D souza Soham Mandrekar defeated Shreyas Tendulkar in the second singles In first double Soham Mandrekar amp Menino D souza defeated Satej Khandeparkar amp Mrunal Bhatkande In third single Soham Shet defeated Andrion D souza And in decider second doubles Shreyas Tendulkar amp Soham Shet defeated Andrion D souza amp Kaushik Sakhalkar Shri Roy Athide Badminton Coach Sports Authority of Goa was the Chief Guest for the presentation ceremony He presented medals and trophies to the winners and runners ups S S Dempo College women team Yasmin Sayed Samiksha Pednekar Sinikka Sapeco and Nikita Yadav St Xavier s College women team Sachi Dhavalikar Sunidhi Wader Karen Miranda and Analisa D souza Goa College of Engineering Men team Satej Khandeparkar Shreyas Tendulkar Soham Shet Mrunal Bhatkande Dashrath Mangelkar and Zishan Khan St Xavier s College Men team Minino D souza Soham Mandrekar Adrian D souza Jason Fernandes Kaushik Sakhalkar and Ethan D costa
