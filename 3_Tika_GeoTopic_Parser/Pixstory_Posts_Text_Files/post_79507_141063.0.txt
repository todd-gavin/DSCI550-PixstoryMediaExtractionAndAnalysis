Brahmastra 2 day box office collection. 
Brahmastra made a record breaking start at the Box office. There is a jump of around 17 to 20 percent in the three national chains - PVR, INOX and Cinepolis. Brahmastra has collected on Saturday in the range of Rs 36.75 to Rs 37.75, taking the two day total to Rs 68.50 collection. Brahmastra is steady in its South dubbed version too. The second day trend indicate the Telugu version has collected in the range of Rs 3 crore . Brahmastra is now headed to record an opening weekend to the North of Rs 122 crores. The hindi version of Brahmastra will too hit a century over the weekend. The final figure can be much higher too as the night show on Saturday will be much higher.
