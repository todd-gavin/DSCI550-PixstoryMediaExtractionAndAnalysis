Chetan Bhagat is the author of five bestselling novels- Five Point Someone (2004), The Three Mistakes of my Life (2008), 2 States (2009), Revolution 2020 (2011) and Half Girlfriend (2014) --- which have sold over 10 million copies and have been translated into over a dozen languages worldwide. 
       TIME magazine named him as one of the 100 most influential people in the world and Fast Company, listed him as one of the 100 most creative people in business globally.
