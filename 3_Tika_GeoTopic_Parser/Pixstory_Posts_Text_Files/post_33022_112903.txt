The third stage of the Grande Boucle was won by the Dutch Dylan Groenewegen who in the last of three stages in Denmark outsprinted the Belgians Van Aert and Philipsen on the finish line in Sonderborg Wim Van Aert second for the second consecutive day collects more bonuses and strengthens his leadership in the general classification he is followed by his compatriot Lampaert and by the Slovenian Pogacar
