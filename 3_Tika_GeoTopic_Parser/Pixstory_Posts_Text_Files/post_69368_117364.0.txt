Tuesday's weather forecast for Jammu and Kashmir called for dry conditions over the next 24 hours and rain and thunderstorms from July 20 to 24.

“Dry weather is expected for most part of the day. However, a brief spell of rain/Thunderstorm may occur towards late afternoon/evening, although chances are less,” a meteorological department official was quoted by the news agency GNS as saying.

He predicted that rain/thunderstorms would likely occur between the dates of 20 and 24 in various locations in Jammu and dispersed locations in Kashmir (mostly in the morning).

Source: Greater Kashmir
