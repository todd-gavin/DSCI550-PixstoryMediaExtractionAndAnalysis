Doctor G's song 'Dil Dhak Dhak Karta Hai' gets released. 
On Thursday makers of Doctor G unveiled a new song 'Dil Dhak Dhak Karta Hai' from their film. It showcase a sizzling Chemistry of Ayushmaan Khurana and Rakul Preet. It is themed around the subject of the film, a medical Campus comedy - darma. It also witness cool dance steps by the choreographer Bosco. The song is composed by Amjad Nadeem Aamir and sung by Raj Braman and Sakshi Holkar.
