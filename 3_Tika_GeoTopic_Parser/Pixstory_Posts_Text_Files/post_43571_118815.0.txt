On July 19th, the Busan city government and the 2030 Busan World Expo Bid Committee announced that they had held an ambassador appointment ceremony for BTS at the HYBE Conference Hall. All members of BTS, co-chairman of the 2020 Busan World Expo - Prime Minister Han Deok-su, Korea Chamber of Commerce and Industry chairman Choi Tae-won, Busan Mayor Park Hyung-joon, and

HYBE's CEO Park Ji-won attended the event.
