Durand Cup is the oldest existing football tournament in Asia and the third oldest tournament in the World. It is a part of India’s rich footballing history. The domestic football league was first played in the year 1888 in Shimla. Durand Cup 2022 is the 131st edition of this historic tournament and it is being hosted by Kolkata, Guwahati and Imphal. 

We all know that India’s footballing history can not be written without mentioning the names of two of India’s oldest clubs - ATK Mohun Bagan and East Bengal. The rivalry between these two clubs is indeed one of the oldest rivalries in the world. Whenever they face each other, their fans create immense hype about the match and there has been a similar vibe ahead of their upcoming match in the 2022 Durand Cup as well. 

ATK Mohun Bagan and East Bengal will face each other on 28th August in the 25th match of the Durand Cup 2022 at the Vivekananda Yuba Bharati Krirangan, famously called the Salt Lake Stadium. 

How to Buy tickets for East Bengal vs ATK Mohun Bagan match?

The tickets for East Bengal vs ATK Mohun Bagan can be bought from Book My Show app. You will have to select Yuba Bharati Krirangan first and then click on the match on 28th August, i.e, Emami East Bengal vs ATK Mohun Bagan. There are two different categories of tickets for the match - 100 rupees and 50 rupees. 

Where to Watch East Bengal vs ATK Mohun Bagan match? 

Viacom18 has bought the broadcasting rights for Durand Cup 2022. You can watch the match on Sports18 1 SD/HD and Sports 18 Khel on television and you can also stream the match live on Voot.
