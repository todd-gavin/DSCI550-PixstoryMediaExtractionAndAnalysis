The Formula Haas team has decided to terminate its contract with the Russian driver Nikita Mazepin and subsequently with the sponsor Uralkali following the invasion of Ukraine by Russia with immediate effect. This is the drastic measure communicated by the US team a few days before the start of the Formula World Championship An important decision that echoes other sanctions arrived in these days from the world of sport Here is the official press release from Haas The Haas F team has decided to terminate the partnership for the Uralkali title and the driver contract with immediate effect by Nikita Mazepin Like the rest of the Formula community the team is shocked and saddened by the invasion of Ukraine and wants a quick and peaceful end to the conflict
