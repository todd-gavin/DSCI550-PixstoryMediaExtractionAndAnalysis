Olympic medallist PV Sindhu clinched the Singapore Open 2022 title after defeating China's Wang Zhiyi in the final of the women's singles category on Sunday. "It was like a rollercoaster of emotions, but managed to pulled through and win the championship! Thank you for all your support, it means so much to me," said Sindhu in a tweet after the game.
