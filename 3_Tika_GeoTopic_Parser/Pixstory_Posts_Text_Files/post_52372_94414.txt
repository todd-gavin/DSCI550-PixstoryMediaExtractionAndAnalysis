Fox had been the world s best whitewater paddler for years but an Olympic gold medal had proven her to be a unicorn A silver in London a bronze in Rio there was a lot of expectation and pressure in Tokyo but Fox powered to Olympic gold in the C with her father Richard on commentary She not only won the C event but she blew it out by more than three seconds She also won bronze in the K making her the only athlete to finish on the podium in both canoeing and kayaking in Tokyo
