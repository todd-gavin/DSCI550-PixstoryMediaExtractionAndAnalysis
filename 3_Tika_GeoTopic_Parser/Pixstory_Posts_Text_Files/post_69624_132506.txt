The Suru Valley is known for being the most fertile and beautiful valley in all of Ladakh The valley which is filled with picturesque villages is traversed by the Suru River For its picturesque terrain chances for sightseeing photography and breathtaking views it is a destination that travellers must visit On the Kargil Road the Suru Valley is directly across the Pensi La It stretches for around kilometres from Kargil in the south to Zanskar In Suru Valley there are numerous options for sightseeing You can go to Sankoo which is renowned for being one of Ladakh s greenest grasslands and is filled with plantations of wild roses Located in the historic Buddhist town of Kartse Khar which dates to the th century is a foot statue of Gautam Buddha Image source
