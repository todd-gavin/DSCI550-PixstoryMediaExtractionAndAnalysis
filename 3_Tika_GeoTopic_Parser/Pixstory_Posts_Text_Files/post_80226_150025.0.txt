About 2022 National Games of India, its participating team and it's Logo:
The 36th iteration of the #NationalGames of India, commonly known as Gujarat 2022, will occur between September 29 and October 12, 2022, in Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Surat, Vadodara, Rajkot, and Bhavnagar in the state of Gujarat.
Host city: #Gujarat, India
Motto: Celebrating unity through sports
Teams participating: 37 (expected)
Athletes participating: 7000 (expected)
Events: 36
Opening ceremony: September 29, 2022
Closing ceremony: October 10, 2022
Main venue: Narendra Modi Stadium, Ahmedabad
i. Participating teams:
Teams from each of India's 28 states, eight union territories, and one from the Indian Armed Forces are anticipated. Ladakh, Dadra and Nagar Haveli, and Daman and Diu, three recent union territories, are anticipated to participate in their first National Games in these events. Likely, a team representing Bodoland may also compete in these games since the Bodoland Territorial Region was granted the opportunity to compete in national sporting events under the provisions of a contract signed in January 2020.

iii. Logo:
The game's official slogan and logo were released on July 22, 2022. The logo shows the Statue of Unity and an Asian lion head made up of sports symbols. "Celebrating togetherness through sports" is the official slogan of the competitions. Savaj, the Asian lion, serves as the logo for the game.
