INGREDIENTS g kacha aam green mangoes g mustard oil pc dried red chilli tsp mustard seeds g salt tsp turmeric g sugar g water METHOD Peel mangoes and cut them in cm chunks In small mangoes remove the pit Soak the mangoes in water for minutes to reduce the astringent taste Strain and set aside Heat g mustard oil in a pan Temper it with dried red chilli and mustard seeds Add mangoes and fry them on medium high heat for minutes Add salt and cook for another minute before adding g water Add turmeric and sugar Since tartness of mangoes can vary widely you may need a little more or a little less sugar than we ve mentioned Bubble on medium heat until the mangoes are cooked This should take about minutes Be careful not to overcook or the mangoes will disintegrate and turn mushy Remove from heat Once cool you can store this in the fridge for about weeks Serve chilled
