In their fourth phone call since the Russian invasion of Ukraine on February Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Russian President Vladimir Putin spoke on Friday Modi underlined India s stand on dialogue and diplomacy during their discussion on the situation in Ukraine When Indian nationals especially students were still stranded in Ukraine on February March and March they previously chatted over the phone The two leaders discussed the implementation of the decisions made during President Putin s visit to India in December according to a statement from the Ministry of External Affairs They discussed ways to promote bilateral commerce in agricultural products fertilisers and pharmaceuticals in particular Despite the fact that Russia has grown to be India s top energy supplier Modi participated in the G summit India has not criticised Russia but it has denounced the murders in Bucha and called for an international investigation which is seen as a move toward the US led west
