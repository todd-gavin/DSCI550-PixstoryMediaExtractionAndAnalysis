The education ministry stated that due to an extraordinary fuel crisis Sri Lanka will be closing its schools for an additional week beginning July Due to a lack of fuel public and state approved private schools will be closed for a week This will prevent parents and teachers from transporting students to their classes According to the Sri Lankan government the curriculum would be covered in the following summer session Due to fuel shortages schools were shuttered for a day last month statewide and remained closed for the past two weeks in urban areas In response to lengthy power outages the Sri Lankan ministry of education declared that all government and government approved private schools in Colombo city boundaries as well as schools in other key cities in the other provinces would be closed for the next week
