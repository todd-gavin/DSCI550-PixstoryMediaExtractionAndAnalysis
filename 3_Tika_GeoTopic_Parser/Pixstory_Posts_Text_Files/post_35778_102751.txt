Sampdoria redeems its playmaker Sabiri also for next season The attacking midfielder will therefore remain in the blucerchiato for at least another year after arriving on loan from Ascoli last January In fact, the player has a buy-out obligation following his salvation by the team of Giampaolo The blucerchiati will spend around one million euros for him A paltry amount compared to what Sabiri has demonstrated on the pitch in recent months
