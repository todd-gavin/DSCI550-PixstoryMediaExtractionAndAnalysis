Green Tea Bags

Green tea extract is rich in bioactive compounds that possess anti-inflammatory properties. Using green tea bags may soothe your eyes and reduce the swelling.

Take two used green tea bags and Refrigerate them for a while and place them on your eyes for 15-20 minutes.
Remove them and wash your eyes.

Turmeric

Curcumin is the major bioactive compound in turmeric. Its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties can help alleviate the symptoms associated with eye infections .

Boil a cup of water and add a teaspoon of turmeric to it and allow it to cool for a while.
Soak a sterile washcloth in this solution.
Use this as a warm compress and wash your eyes after this process.

Lemon Juice

Due to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, lemon juice can help fight eye infections and their symptoms.

Extract the juice from the lemon.
Add this to a glass of warm water and mix well and drink this up.
