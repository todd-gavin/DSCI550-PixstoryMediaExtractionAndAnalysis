Alia Bhatt made her Hollywood debut on Tuesday morning with the Netflix spy thriller film Heart Of Stone. Several Bollywood celebrities praised her after seeing the tweet. Arjun Kapoor, an actor, compared her to Meryl Streep, a senior actress. Gal Gadot and Jamie Dornan will also star in Heart Of Stone. Heart of Stone will be directed by Tom Harper from a script written by Greg Rucka and Allison Schroeder.
