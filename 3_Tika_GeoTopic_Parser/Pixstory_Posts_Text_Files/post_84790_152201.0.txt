Roma and Real Betis will be up against each other on Matchday 03 of the UEFA Europa League. Roma are on the third spot in the points table in Group C of the UEFA Europa League 2022-23 while Real Betis sit at the top spot in the points table.
Roma have failed to meet the expectations of their fans in the Europa League this season. In the 2 games that they have played so far this year in the Europa League, they have won a game and lost a game so far.
Real Betis, on the other hand, have shown great dominance in the UEFA Europa League 2022/23 season. In the 2 games that they have played this season, Real Betis have won both the games so far.
