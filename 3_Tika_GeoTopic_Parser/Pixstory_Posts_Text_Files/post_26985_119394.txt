These are the Italian films competing for the Golden Lion at the Venice Film Festival in August and September, the selection of which was announced by the artistic director Alberto Barbera with the president of the Biennale, Roberto Cicutto. These are THE SIGNOR OF THE ANTS by Gianni AMELIO with Luigi Lo Cascio Elio Germano Sara Serraiocco BONES AND ALL by Luca GUADAGNINO with Timoth and Chalamet CHIARA by Susanna Nicchiarelli with Margherita Mazzucco MONICA by Andrea PALLAORO IMMENSITY by Emanuele CRALESE with Penelope Cruz A creative and Lgbtq friendly quintet given that the men in the competition define themselves as queer Venice celebrates diversity and new perspectives on the world
