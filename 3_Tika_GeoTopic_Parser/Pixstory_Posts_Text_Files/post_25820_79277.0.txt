India has planned to finally execute the CHANDRAYAN 3 mission, minister Jitendra Singh informed the Lok Sabha on Wednesday. ISRO is also set to execute the plan in August.
Chandrayan 3 is a follow-up mission of Chandrayan 2 of 2019 which was designed to be land a rover on the lunar South Pole. However, due to inevitable circumstances and technical glitches, the mission could not become a success.
