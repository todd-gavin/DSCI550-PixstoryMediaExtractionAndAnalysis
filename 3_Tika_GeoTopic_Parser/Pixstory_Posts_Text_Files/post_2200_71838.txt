It s official now Lok Sabha member from Sangrur Bhagwant Mann is the Aadmi Party AAP Chief Ministerial candidate for the upcoming asembly elections in Punjab AAP national convenor and Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal on Tuesday announced Mann s name at a press conference in Chandigarh The comedian turned politician is arguable one of the most popular political leaders in Punjab especially among the youth Mann is a two term Lok Sabha member from Sangrur Kejriwal said they had received about lakh people had sent their suggestions and responses As many as of the respondents favoured year old Mann he added Kejriwal said some had even suggested his name but those votes were nullified
