One of the most auspicious Hindu festival celebrating the nine forms of Durga Navratri The word Navratri signifies Nav as in nine and Ratri as in nights Thus the festival is celebrated for nine nights each day in honour of one of the nine forms of Goddess Durga Devotees of the Goddess believe that this is the time when Maa Durga ascends to Earth along with her children Lakshmi Ganesh Kartikeya and Saraswati At the heart of it the festival celebrates the win of good over evil as the festival signifies Goddess Durga s killing of the demon Mahishasura This year the festival of Navratri began on September and it ended on October
