Roger Federer made a long awaited return to Centre Court to celebrate its th anniversary on Sunday receiving a standing ovation from the crowd in what was already a legend studded affair Emceed by Sue Barker and John McEnroe Centre Court s Centennial featured a parade of former champions from Rafael Nadal and Rod Laver to Venus Williams and Bjorn Borg Six time winners Billie Jean King and Novak Djokovic emerged as a pair before Federer took his unexpected stroll into the stadium The former world No was last on Centre Court when he reached the quarterfinals of the Championships and endured a three set defeat to Hubert Hurkacz We can all agree that it was absolutely amazing to see Federer back on court
