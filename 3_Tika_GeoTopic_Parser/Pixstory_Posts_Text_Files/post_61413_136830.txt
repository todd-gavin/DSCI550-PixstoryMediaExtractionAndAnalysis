National Flag of France The French national flag is a tricolor composed of three vertical bands colored blue white and red France had many flags before the adoption of the tricolor The flag was adopted after the French Revolution where the Parisian militia which stormed the Bastille wore a cockade in the traditional colors of the country The cockade was also worn by the national guard which succeeded the militia The tricolor flag is based on the design and colors of the cockade and is came into use in It was disbanded during the restoration of Bourban but was brought back after the July Revolution and has been in use ever since
