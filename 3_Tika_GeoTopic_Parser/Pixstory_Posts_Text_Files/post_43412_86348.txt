Oats Chills Recipe Stepwise Add cup of oats to a grinder jar Grind to a slightly coarse powder If the flour is too coarse the oats chilla can break Add it to a mixing bowl along with cup besan to cumin seeds jeera or carom seeds ajwain teaspoon salt Add to tbsps fine chopped onions to green chilies fine chopped Make a batter adding water as needed I added another cup this may depend on the kind of oatmeal used If using rolled oats you will need about cup water The batter should be of spreading consistency similar to that of dosa batter Adjust salt as needed Heat your pan well When it is hot enough drizzle few drops of oil and smear with a tissue or cloth If using a non stick pan you don t need to grease it Regulate the heat to medium flame Mix the batter well Pour ladle full of batter It will be about cup Spread it with a light hand Do not spread it too much If the batter is too thick you won t be able to spread it as it will stick to the ladle as you spread In this case add little more water If the batter is too runny the oats chilla will be too soft and limpy In this case you may need to add a tbsp of besan Add half to teaspoon oil or ghee around the edges Cook until the base is firm and set well Flip it Cook on the other side as well pressing down gently with a spatula Cook well on both the sides To make it lightly crisp cook on a medium heat for few minutes Repeat making the rest With this batter you will be able to make chilla Stir the batter well before making the next oats chilla If required sprinkle little water You may need to tbsp more water as the batter thickens upon resting
