Farida is an Afghan refugee who currently resides in India. She fled Afghanistan with her four children in 2018 to reach India. It took her 5 months to settle in India, and then she began knitting to make money. “Now that my children are in school and have learned English and Hindi, I am happy that they can communicate with others,” she said.

When She was 15 years old, She began knitting. And now She knits sweaters, socks, and cardigans to display in bosco programmes. Bosco is a place where refugees go there for study.
