Dortmund and Sevilla will be up against each other on Matchday 04 of the UEFA Champions League. Dortmund are on the second spot in the points table in Group G of the UEFA Champions League 2022-23 while Sevilla sit at the third spot in the points table.
Borussia Dortmund have done decently in the Champions League this year. In the three games  that they have played this year in the UEFA Champions League, they have won two matches and lost a game each.
Sevilla are on a poor run in the UEFA Champions League this year and haven’t done well in La Liga too. In the 3 games that they have played in this season so far, they have drawn a game and lost 2 games against Man City and Copenhagen.
