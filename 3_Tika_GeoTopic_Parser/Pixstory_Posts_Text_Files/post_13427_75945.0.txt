In the second one-day match between the national cricket teams of Afghanistan and the Netherlands, which was held in Qatar, the Afghan players managed to take the lead by 48 points.
In this match, the Afghan team first played a ball that lost six players in the fifty overs and was able to set 237 runs for the opponent. When it came to the Afghan team, the Dutch players could not reach the set goal. The team lost 189 games in 48 overs and managed to make 189 runs.
Thus, Afghanistan was recognized as the winner of the one-day tournament with the Dutch national cricket team. The Afghan national team had defeated the country by 36 runs in the previous one-day match.
The current tournament is part of the World Cup Cricket Super Cup, which is held to introduce the top teams to participate in the 2023 Cricket World Cup.
