Open bite could occur in the front or in the back of the mouth although a frontal open bite is much more common There are two underlying causes for an open bite skeletal or dental sometimes the problem is caused by both Open bite is primarily caused by four factors Thumb or pacifier sucking When someone sucks on their thumb or a pacifier or another foreign object like a pencil they strain the alignment of their teeth This can cause an open bite Tongue thrusting An open bite can occur when a person speaks or swallows and pushes their tongue between their upper and lower front teeth This can also create gaps between teeth Temporomandibular joint disorder TMD or TMJ TMJ disorders cause chronic jaw pain Sometimes people use their tongue to push their teeth apart and comfortably reposition their jaw which can cause an open bite Skeletal problem This occurs when your jaws grow apart as opposed to growing parallel to each other and are often influenced by genetics Sources healthline com
