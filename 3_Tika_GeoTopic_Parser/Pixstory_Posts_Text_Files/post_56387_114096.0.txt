Anxiety is a very tricky disorder. It numbs and confuses you making you distrust yourself. Anxious people find even daily tasks difficult for them. They suffer through anxiousness. 

Anxious people may find it difficult to speak up in classes on group chats.

They find it difficult to go without their phone. 

Cleaning stresses them leaving an even bigger mess.

They find it difficult to visit a doctor or dentist. 

They cannot even spend money on things they even find are worth it. 

They choose not to correct the restaurants or put in their needs. 

They find it difficult to stay throughout the event and even feel anxious to open up to others. 

Anxiety is something that your tackle very slowly. Give yourself some time and trust the process.
