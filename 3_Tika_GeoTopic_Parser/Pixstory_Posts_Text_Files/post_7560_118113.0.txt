The Winners of 68th National Awards have been announced and it is for the movies that were released in 2020 as the ceremony hadn't been conducted that year. The Best Actor, Best Actress and Best Film, all went to Soorarai Pottru the Tamil film. Saravanan Sivakumar won the Best Actor and Aparrna Balamurali won Best Actress for the movies. The Best Actor award was shared and the other winner was Ajay Devgan for his role in Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior. The movie also won Best Popular Film Providing Wholesome Entertainment.
