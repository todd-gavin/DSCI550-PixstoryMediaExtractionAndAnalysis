Your go to foods like Pizza,Chips are surely delectable. However, starchy foods are rich in refined carbs that gets stuck on your teeth more easily and stays longer than any other food item. This releases acids which attacks the teeth for a prolonged period . The hard tooth enamel is subjected to repeated attacks which increases the chances of tooth decay. If regularly consumed, it causes inflammation in blood vessels that may lead to diabetes, cardiac arrests and obesity.So next time,think multiple times before you grab a packet of chips.
