Have you ever experienced an oscillating mood that often irritates you Of course yes and every one of us has experienced such mood changes where we are confused about what we want Although it happens to many and is expected only a few comprehend the degree to which it can be avoided It can be a disorder too A mood disorder is a mental health condition that primarily affects your emotional state It s a disorder in which you experience long periods of extreme happiness sadness or both In addition certain mood disorders involve other persistent emotions such as anger and irritability It s normal for your mood to change depending on the situation However symptoms must be present for several weeks or more for a mood disorder diagnosis This is because mood disorders can cause changes in your behaviour and can affect your ability to perform routine activities such as work or school
