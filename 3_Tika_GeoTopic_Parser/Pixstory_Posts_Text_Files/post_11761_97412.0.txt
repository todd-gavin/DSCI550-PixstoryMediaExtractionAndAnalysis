Yes you heard it right, we can make healthy Lollies from mixed veggies that's nutritious and tasty which is ideal for evening delights or as party appetisers. Serve the snack with some spicy green chutney and enjoy your snack. 

Let's make the healthy lollies 

Ingredients you will need 

• 4 Potatoes (Aloo) , boiled 

• 1 Carrot (Gajjar) , grated 

• 1 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) , finely chopped 

• 1 Onion , finely chopped 

• 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder 

• 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi) 

• 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania) 

• 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder 

• Salt , to taste 

• Oil , for cooking 

How to make Spicy Mixed Vegetable Lollipop Recipe 

• To kick start the recipie first you need to add in some oil onto the pre heated pan and after the oil is heated enough add the onions. Saute the onions till they become translucent and once that's done add the carrot and cabbage. 

• Saute the vegetables for 2 to three minutes till they get cooked. Saute the veggies for 2 to 3 minutes till they get cooked. 

• When the vegetables are cooked well add in all the spices and season it with some salt and pepper. Cover it with the lid and cook for 2 more minutes. And turn off the stove. 

• Add in the veggies that are cooked to the boiled potatoes and mash them all together and combine all the ingredients well.

• Now make the mixtures into small balls. Heat the pan and add the oil and put the balls into the heated oil. 

• Cook each side till it turns brown and serve them with some green chutney.
