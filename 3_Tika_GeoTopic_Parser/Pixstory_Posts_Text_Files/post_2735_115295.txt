Also known as Khutka Gatka is a spiritual practice along with martial arts This art brings together the physical flamboyance with sacred devotion Gatka is a Punjabi word which means a wooden stick that was used instead of swords Gatka uses the sword as the main weapon amongst others Nihangs or armed Sikh warriors are the ones who practice it From the th th century Gatka was used by Sikh warriors to defend themselves from Mughals and their atrocities Gatka has been divided into the traditional Rasmi and sports Khel style Some other Shastras used in Gatka are Khanda Double edged Sword Lathi Quarterstaff Gargaj Mace Tir Kaman Bow Arrow Chakkar Wagon wheel with weights at the spoke ends
