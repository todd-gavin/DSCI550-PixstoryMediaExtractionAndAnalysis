Manipuri weightlifter Bindyarani Devi clinched silver medal in Commonwealth Games 2022. The 23-year-old from Manipur lifted a total of 202 kgs, narrowly missing out on Nigerian Olarinoye’s gold by a sole kilogram. Fraer Morrow won bronze with a new personal best of 198kgs at Commonwealth Games 2022.
Silver at the 2022 Commonwealth Games adds to Bindyarani’s growing medal collection. She won gold at the 2019 Commonwealth Weightlifting Championships with a total of 198 kgs.
