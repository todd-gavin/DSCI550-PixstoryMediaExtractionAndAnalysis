The Black Mountain poets also called projective poets were a group of mid th century American avant garde or postmodern poets centred on Black Mountain College in North Carolina Black Mountain college launched an extraordinary number of artists who spearheaded the avant garde in the America of the s It showed off an extraordinary curriculum in the visual literary and performing arts Among the literature teachers and students were Robert Creeley Fielding Dawson Ed Dorn Robert Duncan Paul Goodman Charles Olson M C Richards Arthur Penn John Wieners William Carlos Williams and more In Charles Olson published his important essay Projective Verse In this he called for a poetry of open field composition to replace traditional tight poetic forms with an improvised form that should reflect precisely the content of the poem This form was to be based on the line and each line was to be a unit of breath and utterance
