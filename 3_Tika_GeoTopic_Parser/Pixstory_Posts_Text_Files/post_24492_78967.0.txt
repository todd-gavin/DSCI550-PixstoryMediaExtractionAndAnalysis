India advanced to their eighth ICC Under 19 Men's Cricket World Cup final with a comprehensive 96-run defeat of Australia at the Sir Vivian Richards Cricket Stadium in Antigua, earlier today.

Shaik Rasheed and Yash Dhull put on the second biggest partnership of the tournament, adding 204 runs for the third wicket after a shaky start for the Men in Blue.
