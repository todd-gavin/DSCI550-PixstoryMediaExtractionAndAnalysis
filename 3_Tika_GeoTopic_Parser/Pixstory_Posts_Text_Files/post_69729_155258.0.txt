Stary Kids made history. Album"MAXIDENT" surpassed 1 million sales on 1st day of release. 
Stray Kids' "MAXIDENT" has become their best-selling album as it surpassed 1.3 million sales in just 1 day. They have also joined Blackpink, BTS, and Seventeen as the only artists in Hanteo history who have surpassed 1 million sales on the 1st day of an album release
