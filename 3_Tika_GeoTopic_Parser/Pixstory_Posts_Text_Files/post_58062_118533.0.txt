After korea Japanese and Chinese dramas  have been popular , they are gaining fans from all around the world. Here are  some cute light hearted romantic comedies that will give you a warm feeling.

Put your head on my shoulder
A beautiful love story about two University student who are miles apart from each other , but learn to understand and rely on each other . 

My little Happiness
A cute , heartwarming drama without any major conflicts. It will make you laugh and appreciate little things in life . Perfect if you want to watch sweet , soft romance.

Unforgettable Love 
Highly recommended, not for the male or female lead but the adorable child actor who plays the role of xiao bao . It follows the story of a child psychologist who is employed by a CEO to take of of his son ,who suffers from selective mutism .
