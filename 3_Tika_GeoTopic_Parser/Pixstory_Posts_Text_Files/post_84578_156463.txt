Indian paramilitary troopers stand guard along a street in Srinagar on October In a suspected militant attack in South Kashmir s Shopian district two non local labourers from Uttar Pradesh were killed in the middle of the night Police sources told that the attack on non local labourers was carried out in the intervening night of Monday and Tuesday at around am in Hermain hamlet of Shopian They said militants barged into rented accommodation and lobbed a grenade on the labourers who were asleep Two labourers sustained grievous injuries and were taken to District Hospital Shopian where doctors declared them dead They have been identified as Ram Sagar and Manish Kumar of UP This is the second militant attack in the Shopian district A couple of days ago militants shot dead a Kashmiri Pandit Pooran Krishna in Chodrigund village
