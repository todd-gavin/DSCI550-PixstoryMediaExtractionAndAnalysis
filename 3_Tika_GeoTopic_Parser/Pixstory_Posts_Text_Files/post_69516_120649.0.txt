Dastgeer Sahib is a 200-year-old shrine situated in Khanyar.Sheikh Syed Abdul Qadir Jelani never stayed there. It has the old Quran written by Ali Ibn Abi Talib A. S, and the relic which is locally called Mouia Pak, a hair strand of Abdul Qadir Gilani. It was constructed in 1806 and expanded in 1877 by Khwaja Sanaullah Shawl. 
It is the center of the activity hub of the Khanyar and is surrounded by a large market. On the Urs or birth date of the Abdul Qadir Gilani (R. A), thousands of people from all over Kashmir go there to pray to Allah and watch the ziyarat.
