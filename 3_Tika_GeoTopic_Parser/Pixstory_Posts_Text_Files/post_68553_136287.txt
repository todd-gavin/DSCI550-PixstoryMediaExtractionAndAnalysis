Commonwealth Games Gold medallist Nayanmoni Saikia was formally appointed as a Deputy Superintendent of Police DSP by the Assam Government on Saturday Assam Chief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma handed over the letter of appointment to a year old Lawn Bowl player at Janata Bhawan in Dispur The DSP post was a reward for her fine showing at the Commonwealth Games However she was also awarded by giving a cheque of Rs lakhs for her excellent performance Nayanmoni is one of the team members of the Indian Women s team who created history by winning the gold medal in Lawn Bowl at the Commonwealth Games in Birmingham
