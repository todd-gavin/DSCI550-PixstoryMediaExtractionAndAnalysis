One more Mars sample has been collected by NASA's Perseverance rover, bringing the total to 12. 

As per ommcomm News, this week, the car-sized Perseverance extracted and sealed its 12th rock sample, mission team members tweeted. 

"Did you hear about the dusty dozen? I currently have 12 rock core samples aboard, which I obtained while #SamplingMars at these various locations near Jezero Crater, the team reported. 

In less than a month, there have been four similar operations.
