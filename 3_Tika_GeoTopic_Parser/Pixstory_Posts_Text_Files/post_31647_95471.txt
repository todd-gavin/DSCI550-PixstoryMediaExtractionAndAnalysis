The very prestigious Francesco Venezze Conservatory of Music is an important meeting point for students from Italy and beyond who, starting from the basic courses, conclude their training course by choosing between two post-graduate Masters in Music applied to images and in Piano. The Conservatory for those who live in Rovigo not only a place of education but of lively creativity Every year the Francesco Venezze Music Conservatory organizes various cultural events and participates in various projects Not to be missed from all May the world premiere represented at the Social Theater of Flatlandia a multimedia show where live scenes alternate with video images and sounds reproduced as part of the Venezze Musica Festival in July set the Venezze Jazz Festival at the beginning of September the Cello City Festival which for twelve years has been attracting world-famous cellists to Rovigo such as Sollima Brunello and many others
