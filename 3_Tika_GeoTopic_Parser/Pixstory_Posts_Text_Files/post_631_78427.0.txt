While there is a lot of focus in mental health across the world , India has also take a step in that direction. 
While presenting the union budget Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman, said a national tele-mental health program will be launched for mental health counselling. IIT Bangalore will provide tech support for the programme.

She also announced  an open platform for the National Digital Health Ecosystem. 

"It will consist of digital registries of health providers and health facilities, unique health identity and universal access to health facilities," she said.

After years of low budget allocations on health, experts have called for strengthening of the healthcare system post covid 19 pandemic. Like in many other countries the weaknesses in healthcare infrastructure were highlighted like never before during the pandemic . 

There has also been a renewed interest in mental health as a good majority of people reported with mental health issues during the pandemic
