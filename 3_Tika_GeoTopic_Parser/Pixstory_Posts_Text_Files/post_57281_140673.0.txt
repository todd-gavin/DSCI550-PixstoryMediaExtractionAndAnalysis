The Queen's Coffin will be presented for procession in the most royal manner.
According to BBC, the coffin will be draped in royal standard and the imperial State crown.
The royal standard is a flag representing the Sovereign and the UK.
The flag features three gold lions for England, a red lion for Scotland and a harp for Ireland.
Along with this, the Imperial State crown is of utmost importance.
After taking the coffin to the Palace of West minister, the Sovereign's orb and a sceptre will also be laid on it.
After making it completely ready for the procession, the coffin is placed in the hall for a short service.
