Bollywood’s First Family – the Kapoors – are back in the headlines as popular star Ranbir Kapoor, son of Rishi Kapoor -- who passed away last year -- is all set to tie the knot with glamorous actress Alia Bhatt today.

The customary mehendi ceremony which was held yesterday was attended by close family members and friends.
