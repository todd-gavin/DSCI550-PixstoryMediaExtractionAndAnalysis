The Baton Relay and Official Video Launching Program for 1st Edition of the Assam Youth Olympics 2022 organised by Assam Olympic Association in association with the Government of Assam at Nehru Stadium, Guwahati yesterday. Cultural Minister Bimal Borah was present as the Chief Guest on the occasion. Taranga Gogoi, MLA, Nahrkatia, Partha Sarathi Mahanta, Joint Commissioner of Police, Dr. Bishnu Ram Nunisa, VP, AOA, Lakhya Konwar, General Secretary, AOA,Hemanta Kalita, Secretary General, Boxing Federation of India, Tapan Das, Treasurer,AOA and Sports lovers were also present in the occasion. The Assam Youth Olympics 2022 will be held from 22 nd July to 27th July.
