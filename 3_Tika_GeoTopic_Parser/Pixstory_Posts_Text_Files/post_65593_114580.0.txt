As they worked on Imtiaz Ali's Love Aaj Kal, their lone film together, there had been rumours of a connection between Kartik Aaryan and Sara Ali Khan for a while. According to reports, the couple broke up just before the movie's debut. The actors, however, made no remarks about it. However, director Karan Johar has finally revealed the truth and stated that the two actors were in reality "dating."
