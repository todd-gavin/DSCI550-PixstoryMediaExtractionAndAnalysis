The Astral Clash, a competition featuring female-identifying talent, will be hosted by Gen.G together with Galorants, Tampax, and Always. Some of the top NA female-identifying VALORANT players will compete in the competition to compete for charity funds. 

The largest VALORANT community of female-identifying and nonbinary gamers, Galorants, and Tampax and Always are collaborating to abolish gendered stigmas and launch the Astral Clash tournament series featuring some of the top female-identifying VALORANT players in North America, according to a news release.
