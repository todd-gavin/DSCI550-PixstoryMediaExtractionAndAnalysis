As Services' Sarvesh Anil Kushare flies in the men's high jump with 2.27m on second try, Tejaswin Shankar's national record of 2.29m is in trouble!
Sarvesh takes home the gold, but he misses on all three of his efforts to clear the 2.3-meter barrier.
Sheen of Kerala won the gold in the women's triple jump with 13.37m.
With 7121 points, Uttar Pradesh's Usaid Khan wins the men's decathlon gold medal. Rajasthan's Yamandeep Sharm and Umesh Lamba won silver and bronze.
