Prime Minister Narendra Modi has decided to send four Union Ministers to countries such as Poland, Romania and Hungary that border Ukraine in order to help expedite the evacuation of Indian students stranded there after the Russian military operation in Ukraine.

Union Minister for Housing and Urban Affairs, and Petroleum and Natural Gas Hardeep Puri, Minister for Law and Justice Kiren Rijiju, Civil Aviation Minister Jyotiradtiya Scindia, and and Minister of State for Road Transport and Highways and Civil Aviation General V K Singh will fly out as Special Envoys of the Indian government, sources said.
