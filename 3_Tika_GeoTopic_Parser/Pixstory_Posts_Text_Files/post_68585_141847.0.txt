Robert Lewandowski’s hat-trick inspired me, admits midfielder Anju Tamang.The day after scoring a record three four goals against the Maldives, Indian women’s football team midfielder Anju Tamang admitted that she took inspiration from Robert Lewandowski’s hat-trick in the UEFA Champions League.“Prior to any game I generally go through football videos of players whom I adore. I need to admit that prior to going to the game against the Maldives, I was watching Robert Lewandowski’s hat-trick in the UEFA Champions League. It inspired me a lot. The moment I scored my first goal, I wanted to go on, and score more,” she said.
