Ankit Sharma achieves success in boxing after struggling in two other sports.
Ankit Sharma of Haryana was airdropped into boxing after struggling in two other sports, and he has quickly advanced to the Men's Flyweight semifinals of the 36th National Games, which are now taking place here.
The National Championship silver medallist took his hand at taekwondo after falling during a competition while trying out skating and breaking some of his teeth. He tried his hardest to lose the excess weight, but he was unable to do so, making this another non-event.
