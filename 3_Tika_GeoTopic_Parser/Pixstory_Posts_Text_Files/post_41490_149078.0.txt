On Sunday, Croatia and the Netherlands successfully completed their World Cup warm-up by winning their final Nations League matches to secure their spots in the Final Four event the following year. US News reported that the unbeaten Dutch defeated visiting Belgium 1-0 and won Group A4 with 16 points, six more than second-place Belgium, while Croatia won Group A1 by defeating Austria 3-1 to send the hosts to the second division of the Nations League. Despite suffering a 2-0 setback in Denmark, defending Nations League and World Cup winner France avoided relegation due to Austria's loss. The World Cup qualifiers played their final game before the tournament kicked off in Qatar on November 20. The World Cup group that the Netherlands is in includes Senegal, Ecuador, and Qatar.The French and the Danes will meet again in Qatar with Tunisia and Australia also in their group.
