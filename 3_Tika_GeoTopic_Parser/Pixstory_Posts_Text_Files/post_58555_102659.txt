Manikarnika ghat is one of the most famous and significant out of ghats on bank of Ganges in Varanasi Mortal remains are brought here for cremation on wooden pyre It is religious belief of Hindus that dying and getting cremated here on bank of Ganges paves the way for the departed soul to attain Moksha Salvation Many aged persons at the age of onwards with weak health are brought and stayed on rented houses for couple of days and weeks to breath their last here There are two dying centers in Varanasi one by Birla Charitable trust and another by Dalmia Charitable trust Such aged people are admitted here on nomonal payment Surprisingly a few of them at the age of recover in health and go back to native town
