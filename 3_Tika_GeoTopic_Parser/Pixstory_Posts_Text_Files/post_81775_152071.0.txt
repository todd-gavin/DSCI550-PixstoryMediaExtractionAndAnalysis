Ranveer Singh Calls Deepika Padukone 'my queen' amid the separation rumours. 
From past few days there were rumours of seperation of Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone but now Ranveer singh has indirectly rubbish all the rumours. He praised her for becoming the brand ambassador of an international jewellery brand . After Cartier welcomed Deepika Padukone as its 'newest member' along with a stunning black and white picture of her sporting a diamond necklace from the brand, Ranveer tweeted to the post, "My Queen. Doing us proud! "
