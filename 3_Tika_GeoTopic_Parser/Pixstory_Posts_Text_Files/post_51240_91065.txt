The Reunion is a reunion special of the American television sitcom Friends The special is hosted by James Corden and executive produced by the show s co creators Marta Kauffman and David Crane Kevin S Bright the show s main cast and Ben Winston who also directed the special The special sees the main cast revisit the sets of the original show such as the Friends apartments the Central Perk coffee shop and the signature water fountain meet with guests who appeared on the show as well as celebrity guests do table reads and re enactments of Friends episodes and share behind the scenes footage
