JAMMU & KASHMIR : Built on the bank of river Chenab, Akhnoor Fort stands tall since 1802 and is one of the tourist attractions of Jammu. The construction of this beautiful fort was starred by Raja Tegh Singh in 1762 and completed by his successor Raja Alam Singh IN 1802.
