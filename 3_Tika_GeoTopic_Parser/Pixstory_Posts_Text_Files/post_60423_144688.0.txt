Kdramas 'Goblin' and 'W:Two Worlds' will have Chinese remakes
The popular romantic fantasy Korean dramas "Goblin," starring Gong Yoo and Kim Go Eun, and 'W Two Worlds' starring Han Hyo Joo and Lee Jong Suk will be having a Chinese remake. Zhao Yaoke and Chang Bin are officially main leads of W Two Worlds.
