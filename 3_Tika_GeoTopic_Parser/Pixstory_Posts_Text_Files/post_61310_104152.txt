Looking for a refreshing drink in this oppressive weather Look no further than a glass if chilled rose lassi Recipe Take cups of fresh chilled curd in a mixer or blender jar Add cup chilled water to dried rose petals or to fresh rose petals tsp rose water and tbsp rose syrup I did not add ice cubes hence I added cup chilled water if adding ice cubes then add cup water The dried rose petals are from my terrace garden I dry them in shade and then add them to kheers or the sweets that I prepare Blend everything till smooth and till the lassi becomes frothy you can make the lassi thick or thin by adjusting the amount of water Instead of water you can also add milk or half half of water and milk Pour the lassi in glasses and Serve rose lassi with some sliced or chopped dry fruits like almonds pistachios or cashews
