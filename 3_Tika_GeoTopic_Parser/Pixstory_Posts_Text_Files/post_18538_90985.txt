After the double withdrawal due to abdominal pain, Matteo Berrettini was also forced to withdraw from the Miami tournament, this time due to a problem with his right hand. After consulting with the doctors, he and his team decided to proceed with a small operation to ensure rapid and complete recovery in view of the clay court tournaments Berrettini himself made it known with a post on social networks in which he showed himself with his right hand bandaged After his withdrawal from the Miami tournament due to an injury to his right hand, the my team we spoke to doctors and specialists having carried out all the necessary checks and investigations they advised us to proceed with a small operation The doctors and my team are already discussing the program to return to the field and as soon as we have a clearer picture you will provide more updates As always, thank you very much for your support wrote Berrettini communicating that the recovery time and return to the field are not yet certain
