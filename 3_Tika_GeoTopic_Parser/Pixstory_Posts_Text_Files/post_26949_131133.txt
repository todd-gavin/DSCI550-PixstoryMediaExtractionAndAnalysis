After years of absence from the cosmetics market, the British stylist and entrepreneur is trying again with a skincare brand created in collaboration with Lvmh. and cruelty free in line with the sustainable and minimal philosophy of the English brand For her debut Stella McCartney has chosen to launch only three products on the market which include a cleanser, a serum and a moisturizer The goal is to create a skin routine that contains fewer products with the fewest ingredients possible By marrying a less is more approach, the designer thus reaffirms her commitment to the environment not only in terms of quality but also in terms of quantity
