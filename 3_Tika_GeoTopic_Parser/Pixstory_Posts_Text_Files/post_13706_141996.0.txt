preliminary competitions under 20 years of Asia; Afghanistan national youth football team went to Oman
The Afghanistan Football Federation announced that the national youth football team has traveled to Muscat, the capital of Oman, to participate in the Asian Under-20 Championship qualifiers.
The preliminary competitions of the Asian Championship in the under-20 age group will be held from 23 to 27 Sambla at the Sultan Qaboos Stadium in Muscat.
Afghanistan will compete with the football teams of Thailand, Philippines and Oman in the "G" group of this competition.
On Wednesday, Afghanistan is going to face Thailand in the first match.
