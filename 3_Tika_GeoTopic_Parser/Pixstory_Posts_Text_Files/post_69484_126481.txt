Vishansar Lake is a high altitude lake with amazing natural beauty and pure surroundings that is close to Sonmarg On one side it is encircled by towering angular mountain peaks and on the other by lush green meadows Apart from its beauty though what draws visitors there are the summertime prospects for trout and mahseer fishing The lake is always frozen in the winter Visit Vishansar Lake to rejuvenate your spirit and calm your inner mind
