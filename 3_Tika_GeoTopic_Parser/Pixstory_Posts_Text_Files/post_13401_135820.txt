 In the past week more than migrants have been returned from Iran to Afghanistan A number of Afghan immigrants have been expelled from the Silk Bridge in Nimroz province by Iranian military forces The process of forced deportation of Afghan citizens from Iran is going on every day from the Silk Bridge in Nimroz and the Islam Qala crossing in Herat This is while the Afghan ambassador to Iran said in an interview with one of the country s media two days ago that after the Taliban took over about one million Afghan citizens immigrated to Iran According to him the living conditions of Afghan immigrants in Iran are satisfactory
