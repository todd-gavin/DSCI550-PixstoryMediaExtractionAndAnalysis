On the occasion of the Autumn Winter collection, Dior presented the unexpected collaboration with the Birkenstock footwear brand. A clog or clog made in various soft shades such as gray and milky coffee and decorated with floral embroidery that also adorns the sweaters. The result of the collab is the version updated of the classic Birkenstock model called Boston Clog The fact that the new clog was present on almost all the looks already suggests that the brand wants to invest heavily in what will probably become the it shoe of the year
