India is set to get it's 15th President today as the vote count begins. The competition is between NDA candidate Droupadi Murmu and opposition candidate Yashwant Sinha.

On July 18, the vote took place, all elected MLAs & MPs across the country voted for the next president of the country.

Of the 736 electors comprising 727 MPs and nine MLAs who were permitted by the Election Commission to vote at Parliament House, 728 electors cast their votes. The total turnout for the vote was 98.91 percent.
