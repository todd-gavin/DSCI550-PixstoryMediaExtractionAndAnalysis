Kapil Dev to rejoice WC victory with his fellow players in Chandigarh Chandigarh Former Test cricketer Kapil Dev will rejoice the World Cup victory once again UT Cricket Association UTCA is bringing this special occasion on Tuesday by organising a special screening of the film wherein Kapil Dev along with his city based teammates will remember this defining moment on Tuesday UTCA will also organize a felicitation ceremony for him on Tuesday at pm at PVR Cinemas where former and current cricket players alongwith UTCA members from Chandigarh will enjoy the film
