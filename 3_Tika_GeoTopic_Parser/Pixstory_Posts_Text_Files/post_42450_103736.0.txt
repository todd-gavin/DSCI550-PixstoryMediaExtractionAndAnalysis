Dharma Productions and director, Ayan Mukherji announced the date of the launch of their upcoming movie, Brahmastra.

The trailer of Brahmastra will release on 15th June 2022 and the release date of the movie is 9th September 2022.

The Star cast of Brahmastra is Amitabh Bachchan, Nagarjuna, Alia Bhatt, Ranbir Kapoor and Mouni Roy.
