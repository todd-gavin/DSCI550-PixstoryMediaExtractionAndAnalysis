As many as 1,63,670 Indians renounced their citizenship and shifted their base to 103 other countries last year, the Union Government informed the Lok Sabha.

The United States is the most sought after country for emigration by Indians. In 2021, 78,284 Indians renounced their Citizenship for the US, followed by Australia where numbers go as high as 23,533. 

As many as 21,597 people moved to Canada whereas the United Kingdom received 14637 new citizens from our country. 

Italy, Germany and New Zealand issued 5986, 2381 and 2643 citizenships to Indians respectively, and 2187 Indians became citizens of Netherlands.
