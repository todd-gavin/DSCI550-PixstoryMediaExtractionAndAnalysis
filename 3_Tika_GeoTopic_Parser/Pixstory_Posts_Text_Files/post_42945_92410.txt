India continues to perform poorly in various international health index reports by recording most malnutrition cases and deaths here is the health report of the country On the occasion of World Health Day th April we aim to draw your attention to the essential needs in children s early years for overall development In Mobile Creches a general physician conducts check ups twice a month for the children and monitors their growth and development Medical cards are made for children A special diet is received by malnourished children that include eggs All children receive suji halwa for breakfast and khichdi with vegetables for lunch in their regular diet To avail essential health care services childcare centres and government health facilities play a vital role They prevent child malnutrition stunting and sub optimal children s cognitive development
