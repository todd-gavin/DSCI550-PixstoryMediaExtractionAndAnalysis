 The United Nations Children s Fund or UNICEF says that it is going to provide education to more than thousand children in Nimroz province The officials of the United Nations Children s Fund said on Saturday that this number of children will benefit from the benefits of education by hiring male and female teachers in the remote areas of the districts of Nimroz province According to UNICEF officials this program will cover children deprived of education in Nimroz for an indefinite period of time Nimroz is one of the provinces where many children are deprived of education due to lack of education
