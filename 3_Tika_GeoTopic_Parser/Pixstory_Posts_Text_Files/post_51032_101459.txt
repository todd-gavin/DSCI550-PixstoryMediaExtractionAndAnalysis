Ramen is a type of Japanese or Korean noodles Ramen noodles are wheat based but what makes them unique is the use of kansui an alkaline mineral water They are served with egg meat or veggies on the top And with side salads known as kimchi Kimchi is a traditional Korean side dish of salted and fermented vegetables such as napa cabbage and Korean radish A wide selection of seasonings are used including spring onions garlic ginger sweet potato etc
