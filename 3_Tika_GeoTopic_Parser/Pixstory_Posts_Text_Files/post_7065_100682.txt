By RB Sinha In its first Performance Audit Report on the functioning of the Noida Authority the Comptroller and Auditor General CAG has pointed out that there have been flaws in the tendering process and financially inadequate and ambiguous criteria were followed in the allotment of large plots of land to acres in prime sectors in Noida for developing world class sports infrastructure facilities This has led to the selection of incompetent consortiums with weak financial capabilities that have failed to enforce the terms and conditions of the schemes The lack of a monitoring mechanism related to the projects has resulted in non existent sports infrastructure in the region for over a decade now acres of the prime land grabbed in the name of development of Sports Infrastructure With a vision to hold international sporting events like the Commonwealth Games and Asian games in Noida City the Noida Authority allotted Sports City plots admeasuring sqm acres of land in prime sectors of Noida from May July at the lease premium of Rs crores to a consortium of Members led by a Software Company Logix Soft Tel Pvt Ltd of the Logix group in May a consortium of members led by Xanadu Pvt Ltd of C group in May a consortium of members led by the Lotus Green Developers Pvt Ltd of C Group in September and a consortium of members led by ATS Home Pvt Ltd in July The allotment was made based on competitive bidding against a reserve price RP fixed by the Authority The Sport City schemes envisaged that the allottees would construct world class integrated sports infrastructure and recreational facilities in of the plot and the remaining plot area would be used for residential and commercial projects Read the full story here
