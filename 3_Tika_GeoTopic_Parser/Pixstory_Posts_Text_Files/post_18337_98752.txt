After directing the first training session with the team since his resignation from the hospital, Mihajlovic will already be on the bench on Sunday to manage his Bologna away match in Venice. The coach will not start with the team tomorrow but will arrive in the Veneto capital on Sunday to comply with the possible the precautions and indications dictated by the treatments sustained following the relapse of leukemia From what has also been learned Mihajlovic will present himself in the press room for the pre-match conference After the match against Venice, Bologna will have to play the remaining matches against Sassuolo and Genoa this last still in the race for salvation
