 Pramod Sawant is the current Chief Minister of Goa Sawant represents Bharatiya Janata Party BJP in the Sanquelim constituency in Goa Legislative Assembly He works as an Ayurvedic medical practitioner He was the speaker of the Goa legislature before being sworn in as Chief Minister after the death of Manohar Parrikar He also served as the spokesperson of the Goa unit of the Bharatiya Janata Party for a brief period of time In the Assembly elections Sawant defeated Congress candidate Dharmesh Saglani by over votes He is again contesting from the Sanquelim constituency
