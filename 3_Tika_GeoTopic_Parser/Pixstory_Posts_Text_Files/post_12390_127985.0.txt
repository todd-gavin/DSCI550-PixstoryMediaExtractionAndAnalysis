Signs that show you’re Struggling With Perfectionism.

Do you feel like you're never done your "best", even if you've tried super hard?

If you're reading this and nodding - you may struggle with perfectionism.

Here are some examples that may help you recognize when perfectionism is starting to motivate your actions. If you recognize any tendencies towards perfectionism, you can decide how to move forward. The first step is noticing it’s  signs.

1. You never feel that you know enough
2. You doubt yourself and your abilities
3. You see mistakes as proof that you're inadequate
4. You never celebrate your success
5. You hold high standards of others
6. You negatively compare yourself to others
