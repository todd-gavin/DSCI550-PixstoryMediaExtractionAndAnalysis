Much like another ton of things India gave us a magnificent form of artform Yoga It is considered to be one of the oldest forms of exercise It is based on the practice of meditation and self awareness and has become a popular way to improve health and wellbeing around the world Nowadays there are many types of yoga that are practiced all over the world However the traditional style of yoga that originated in India is still considered to be the most effective This style focuses on stretching and breathing exercises that help improve flexibility balance and coordination It also helps reduce stress and tension in the body making it an ideal form of exercise for people of all ages and backgrounds Yoga has been proven to be an effective way to maintain physical health and increase mental wellbeing It has also been linked with improved moods better sleep patterns decreased anxiety levels stronger immune systems and increased happiness levels In short yoga is truly an amazing tool that can help us improve our overall wellbeing both physically and mentally
