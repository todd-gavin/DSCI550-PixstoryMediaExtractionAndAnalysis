The United Kingdom sanctions the Russian owner of Chelsea Roman Abramovich blocks the sale of the club announced in recent days but not only The British government, blocking all the assets of the owner of the Blues, confirmed that it also suspended any profit making activity for the club, ticket sales and including marketing activities Only season-ticket holders will be able to attend matches and shops inside the stadium will also remain closed Restrictions on asset freezes also apply to all entities owned or controlled by Roman Abramovich explains a government note This means that Chelsea Football Club and now also subject to an asset freeze under UK financial sanctions
