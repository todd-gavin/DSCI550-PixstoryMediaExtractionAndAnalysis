The International Trans Day of Visibility is an annual event held on March to honour transgender persons and raise awareness of persecution they suffer around the world The day is dedicated to honouring transgender people raising awareness about the discrimination they suffer around the world and recognising their contributions to society Rachel Crandall of Michigan a transgender activist based in the United States launched the day in In response to the lack of LGBT recognition of transgender people citing frustration that the only well known transgender centered day was the Transgender Day of Remembrance which mourned transgender people s murders but did not acknowledge and celebrate living members of the transgender community On March the first International Transgender Day of Visibility was held Trans Student Educational Resources a student advocacy organisation established in the United States has led the charge since then
