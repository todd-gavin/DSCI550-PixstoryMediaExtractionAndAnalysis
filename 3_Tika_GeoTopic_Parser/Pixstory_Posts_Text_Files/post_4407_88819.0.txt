I absolutely had to share this pancake recipe.  I've been craving pancakes for weeks, but I've
always been too lazy to make them🤌
Finally motivated myself to make really tasty and very simple recipe that you will surely love 💁🏻‍♀️

Recipe:

-60 g whole grain flour
-20 g vegan chocolate protein powder 
-1 tsp of baking powder
-60 g apple puree
-20 g vegan yogurt 
-1 tbsp honey 
-100-125 ml milk 
-10 g choco chips 

Topping:

-Berries
-Bananas
-Almond Butter 
-1 tbsp honey
