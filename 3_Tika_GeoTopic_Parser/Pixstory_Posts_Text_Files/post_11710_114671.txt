We know that in any festive while we celebrate them food plays a main role in embracing the festive Similarly while we think about Eid most of us would think about the main course which is of course biryani If you are looking for a balance of both healthy and rich foods you can opt put few of the option mentioned below Oats Cutlets Chickpea Yoghurt Chaat Pomegranate smoothie Dates roll Palak Chicken Tikka Shredded Chicken Salad How to make these We got your back Follow the ingredients and steps to make the first one in the list Oats Cutlets Ingredients Dry Roasted Oats Cup Grated Carrot Tbsp Paneer Cup Finely Chopped green chillies counts Grated ginger Tsp Garam Masala powder Tsp Red Chilly powder Tsp Onion Chopped Cup Coriander leaves chopped Tbsp Egg Full Egg can also be replaced with boiled and mashed potato cup Olive Oil Tsp Salt as per taste Method Take a large bowl and add oats grated ginger chopped green chillies carrots chilli powder garam masala powder onion egg mashed potatoes coriander leaves and salt Crumble the panner and add it with the mixture prepared Combine them both well and make it into a dough like consistency Now take small portions from the dough and make them into balls to shape the cutlets On a medium flame place a saucepan and grease it well with oil Keep the cutlets on the pan and tawa fry for sometime and do the same for the two sides Wait till it turns golden brown and prepare all the cutlets Serve them hot and enjoy a healthy Eid Sources M Fine
