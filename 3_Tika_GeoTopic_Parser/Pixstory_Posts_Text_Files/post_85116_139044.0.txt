Club Brugge and Leverkusen will be up against each other in their opening encounter of the UEFA Champions League 2022-23. This will be a Group B match which will be played at the Jan Breydel Stadium.
Porto, Atletico Madrid, Leverkusen, and Club Brugge are the 4 clubs that have been drawn in Group B of the UCL campaign this season. This will be the second meeting between both the sides in European competitions and their last tie ended in a draw. 
German sides have historically been better against Belgian clubs in the UEFA Champions league as they have managed to win 18 of the last 24 games that were played against Belgian clubs in the competition.
