Sh Amit Shah, Hon'ble Union Home Minister, Minister of Cooperation chaired a detailed review meeting on various aspects related to Arunachal Pradesh in Namsai today. Sh Sanjay Arora, DG ITBP alongwith senior officials of the Force attended the meeting. A 'Bada Khana' was organized in which ITBP jawans and personnel of sister organisations participated. The Hon'ble Home Minister interacted with jawans and boosted their morale.
