Charanjit Singh Channi was made Punjab chief minister after ouster of Captain Amarinder Singh as CM on the direction of Congress high command in September He is Punjab s first Dalit Sikh Chief Minister It is said that Congress choice of Channi as Singh s successor was motivated by Punjab s significant Dalit community and other parties attempts to appeal to them In this election he is running from two seats in the state legislature AICC confirmed his candidacy from the Bhadaur seat in addition to his current Chamkaur Sahib seat which he currently represents in the outgoing assembly If reports are to be believed Channi might be able to retain his Chamkaur Sahib seat but may find it difficult to win the second seat of Bhadaur If he manages to bring Congress back to power against all odds his stature will see an exponential rise in the party and outside it
