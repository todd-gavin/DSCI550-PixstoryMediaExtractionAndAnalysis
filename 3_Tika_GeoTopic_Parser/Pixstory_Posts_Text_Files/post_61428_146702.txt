Apple turnovers Apple turnovers are a type of puff pastry It is essentially the French version of apple turnovers The dough is made with flour dry butter water and salt It has a filling of apples which is made from peeled and cored apples with vanilla butter and sugar It was first made in Saint Calais There is an interesting story behind its creation At that time the town was suffering from a epidemic so the lady of the town gave apples and flour to the inhabitants and they created this dish with The filling is placed in the center of the circle of dough then it is folded and using beaten egg to stick the folds The dough is baked until golden and puffy
