Here's how to make this super yummy snack with
only 4 ingredients

-2 Bread 
-butter
-Coconut sugar, or brown sugar
-Ground cinnamon

Steps to prepare 

1. Butter the bread on one side, and sprinkle sugar
and cinnamon evenly.

2. In a frying pan, cook the bread on LOW heat
until golden brown, about 3 minutes per side. (Be
careful not to let the caramel burn )

3. Let it cool completely, so that the caramel
hardens and gets crispy 

AND ENJOY!!
