The Norwegian Nobel Committee on October Friday at am Norway Local Time awarded the Nobel Peace Prize to Ales Bialiatski from Belarus Russian human rights organisation Memorial and Ukrainian human rights organisation Center for Civil Liberties The award is presented to those who have conferred the greatest benefit to humankind and in light of the ongoing Ukraine Russia war the awardees have made valuable contributions to prevent human rights violations in conflict ridden areas Cofounders of the fact checking website Pratik Sinha and Mohommad Zubair from India were also nominated by the committee for the prestigious award for relentlessly battling misinformation in India
