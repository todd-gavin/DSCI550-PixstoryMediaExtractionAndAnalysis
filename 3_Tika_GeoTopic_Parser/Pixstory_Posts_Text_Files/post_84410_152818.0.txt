Over the course of five days, 3 national records were broken in the 2022 National Games:
Rosy Meena Paulraj cleared a height of 4.20m to break the 8-year old record of 4.15m
Siva Subramani, eclipsed his own record of 5.30m with 5.31m in the men's pole vault
Ram Baboo clocked 2:36.34 to beat the previous record by nearly 4 minutes in the men's 35km racewalk.
