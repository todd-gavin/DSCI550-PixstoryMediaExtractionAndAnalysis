Deepika and Ranvir started dating in the year 2018 and have now completed 10 years of togetherness. While the couple is well known for setting major relationship goals, a tweet indicating their separation has gone viral in the internet.The tweet was posted by an unverified account of an over seas director and it read “Everything is not well between Ranveer and Deepika”. While this tweet has no proof, the couple has no clue that rumours about their separation is spreading like wild fire. Many Deepvir fans have even called out the twitter user for spreading fake separation rumours about Bollywood’s favourite couple
