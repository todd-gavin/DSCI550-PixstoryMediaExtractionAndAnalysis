While the preparations for Iftar will be going on if you want to add some sweetness for today s iftar feast try on this Dates Barfi recipe It s healthy and it also satisfies your sweet tooth It s absolutely quick easy and delicious snack to indulge in Ingredients gms dates deseeded gms poppy seeds gms almonds chopped gms cashewnut chopped gms dry grapes chopped gms coconut grated tsp cardamom powder gms ghee Steps to go Heat up the pan on low flame and roast the poppy seeds and keep them aside Blend the dates Roast the dry fruits and nuts in ghee at low flame When it turns into golden brown add the grated coconut and cardamom powder and mix them up well and at last add the dates Cook them well for about to minutes Shift the mixture into a plate when it s hot and spread it out evenly and cut them into slices drizzle over the barfi with some poppy seeds After slicing them into squares give it some time to cool down and cover the container After it s completely cool take a bite and enjoy your iftar Sources
