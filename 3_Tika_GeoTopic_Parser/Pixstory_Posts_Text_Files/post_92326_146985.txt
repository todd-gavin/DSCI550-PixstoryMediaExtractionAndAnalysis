Savarkar At Reception For Rahul Gandhi s Bharat Jodo Yatra Savarkar the Hindutva ideologue charged as conspirator in Mahatma Gandhi assassination case appeared among tall leaders on Congress billboard at the reception for Rahul Gandhi s Bharat Jodo Yatra The incident happened in Chengamanad where the Congress Mandalam Committee on the national highway at near Nedumbassery Kochi erected a mammoth arch with picture of the Sangh ideologue suspected of planning Gandhi assasination VD Savarkar to welcome Rahul s yatra As resentment grew a photo of Mahatma Gandhi was hung to cover Savarkar s picture Incidentally this controversial banner was installed close to the residence of Congress MLA Anwar Sadhat in his constituency Aluva
