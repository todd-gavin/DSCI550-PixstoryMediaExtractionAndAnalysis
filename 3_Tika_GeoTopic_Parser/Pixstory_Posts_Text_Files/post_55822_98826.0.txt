Moving to Victoria at the age of eight, Harper has since made her name in the internationally competitive field of thriller fiction, with crime novels sold in 40 territories worldwide. 

Following an early career in journalism, in 2014 she submitted a short story to the Big Issue’s annual fiction edition, and after it’s selection for publication, Harper decided to pursue creative writing full time. 

Source: hunterandbligh

Her 2018 release, The Lost Man, received a plethora of awards, including Best Paperback at the International Thriller Writers Awards. Dive into Harper’s work for gripping tales and haunting voices.
