The Princeton offense was created by Hall of Fame coach Pete Carril during his incredible year win tenure as the coach of Princeton Tigers The most important thing you need for this offense to be successful is a post player who has a high basketball IQ passes exceptionally well and who can knock down a shot consistently from distance This offense is primarily run through a big man who patrols around the high post The team relies on this player to read the defense and make smart decisions Surrounding this versatile player the other players must be able to shoot from distance and make smart cuts and passes Teams like Sacramento and New Orleans run this offense
