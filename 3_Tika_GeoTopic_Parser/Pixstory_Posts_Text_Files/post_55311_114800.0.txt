Our Earth Day Network-India Advisor Prof. Chetan S Solanki, founder of Energy Swaraj Foundation - ESF will be in West Bengal, July 12 to 22, 2022 on his solar yatra. Prof. Solanki is on a 10 year-long journey to build a movement to save the planet by transitioning to clean energy. He will be in Kolkata 14-18 July meeting with various stakeholders as well as school children.
