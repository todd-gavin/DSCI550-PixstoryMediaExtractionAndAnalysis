news this morning the disappearance of Maurizio Zamparini years former President of the sports clubs of Venice and Palermo Known by all as the President of the layoffs due to his particularly volcanic temperament which led him to have very little patience with the coaches that he himself chose to lead his teams In fact, he became the protagonist in the years of good exemptions, a real record Zamparini left us due to some medical complications that arose after a peritonitis operation that had forced him into intensive care in the last few days in the Cotignola hospital in province of Ravenna With his teams he managed to gain the satisfaction of bringing both Venezia and Palermo to Serie A.
