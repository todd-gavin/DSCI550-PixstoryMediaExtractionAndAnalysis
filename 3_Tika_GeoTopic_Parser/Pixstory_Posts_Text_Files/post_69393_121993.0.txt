In a men's football tournament between departments, the Media Education and Research Department (MERC) of Kashmir University defeated the Physical Education Department on Thursday. 

Basit of Physical Education opened the scoring just before halftime from the edge of the box, and Yameen Dar of MERC levelled the score at 1-1 with a tap-in from within the box. 

However, the game remained tied until the very end and then went to penalties, where MERC triumphed over Physical Education 4-3. 

The Directorate of Physical Education and Sports of Kashmir University hosted the competition that included the knockout round.
