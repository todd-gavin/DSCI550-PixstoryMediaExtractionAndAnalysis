Chanel under the creative direction of Virginie Viard makes critics turn up their noses but drives the customers crazy The turnover does not grow year after year and the ladies of high society if we talk about couture adore its simplicity and the attentions of a woman who knows how women want to feel Gone are the eras of experimentation of continuous renewal of the stylistic codes of the Chanel house when the Kaiser of fashion Karl Lagerfeld reigned in the maison The tweed suit the blouses the long dresses and even the evening dresses take on softer and more pragmatic shapes After all, the average customer of the maison is an oversized European woman who is not really interested in leather leggings and skimpy jackets
