Russian swimmer Evgeny Rylov suspended for nine months by FINA for publicly supporting his nation&#39;s president Vladimir Putin Rylov not a common athlete but double Olympic champion in Tokto in the backstroke The International Swimming Federation has taken the decision to suspend the swimmer for nine months following his participation and behavior at an event at the Luzhniki stadium in Moscow last March On that occasion with his presence and his words he evidently legitimized the political positions of the Russian president Choices that did not please FINA which triggered thus nine months of disqualification which began last Wednesday April when this decision was made official
