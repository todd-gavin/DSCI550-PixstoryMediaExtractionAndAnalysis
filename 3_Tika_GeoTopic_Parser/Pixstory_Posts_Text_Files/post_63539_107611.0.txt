The two-day visit of Prime Minister Narendra Modi is slated on June 16 in Dharamshala, and on Wednesday, Himachal Pradesh Chief Minister Jai Ram Thakur inspected the preparations. The Chief Minister presided over the review meeting and gave the relevant officials instructions to make sure that the right arrangements were made according to protocol. Jai Ram also discusses the roadshow's preparations. He stated that numerous ethnic folk groups from different cultures would be present at the roadshow's location wearing their traditional garb and carrying musical instruments.
