Alia Bhatt❤️opened up about the character of Amrita in Brahmastra Part Two🔥: Shiva and placed her bets on Deepika Padukone💟. While there is a lot of speculations about who should play the role of Amrita🧐🧐, she said, "Deepika for sure for Amrita 🤌!” adding “She is gorgeous and stunning⚡. She has that amazing stature💕."
Brahmastra is the story of Shiva🔥, essayed by Ranbir Kapoor🌈, and is directed by Ayan Mukerji. The film also features Amitabh Bachchan, Nagarjuna and Shah Rukh Khan in extended cameos🎥.
