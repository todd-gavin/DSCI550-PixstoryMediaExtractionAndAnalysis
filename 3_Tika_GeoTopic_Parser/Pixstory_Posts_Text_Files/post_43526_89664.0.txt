On March 24, it was announced that Youngjae will transform into the DJ of "GOT7's Youngjae's Close Friend," which will premiere on March 28 (Monday) at 8 p.m. KST. For two hours every night, fans will be able to enjoy Youngjae's sweet voice and excellent hosting skills. In 2020, Youngjae received much love as a DJ for "Idol Radio," and he was recently a special DJ for "Dreaming Radio." "Close Friend" ended in 2013, but it is returning after nine years with Youngjae as the host.
