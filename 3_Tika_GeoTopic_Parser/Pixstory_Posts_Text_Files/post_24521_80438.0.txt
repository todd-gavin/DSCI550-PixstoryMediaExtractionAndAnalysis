EXO's D.O. and Won Jin Ah completed filming for the upcoming Romance Fantasy Film " SECRET".

SECRET is a korean remake of the 2008 Taiwanese film of the same name.

Plot-
Secret' is a time travel romance movie in which a piano prodigy transfers into a new school and meets a girl playing a mysterious melody in an old practice room. They both soon fall in love, but a secret threatens to create disharmony in this relationship
