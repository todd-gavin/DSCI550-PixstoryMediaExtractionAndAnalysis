On Monday, Slovakia will face Kazakhstan in Group C3 of the UEFA Nations League at the
Anton Malatinsky Stadium.

Slovakia began their Nations League campaign with a 1-0 victory over Belarus on Friday.

Following a 2-0 win over Finland in a friendly match in March 2022, this was the second time the
two teams met.Slovakia has won three times and drawn twice in their past six games, being
undefeated in all but one of them.

Kazakhstan beat Azerbaijan 2-0 in their Nations League opening last time out, just like the
hosts.Since their humiliating 8-0 loss to France in November 2021, they have gone undefeated
in each of their previous four games across all competitions, winning three and drawing one.
Kazakhstan has only won one of their nine road games, losing five and drawing three.
