Luxmi Narayan Mandir Charitable Trust organises blood donation camps frequently and they hold up 10-12 camps per year. They held their first blood donation camp in January, 2011. On this World Blood Donor Day which is being celebrated today, they held 3 blood donation camps. This was their 5th Blood donation camp of this year. The blood donation  camps were started with the noble initiative to help save lives and the donors have always come out in great numbers. They hold many campaigns to urge people to donate blood. Apart from blood donations,  eyes & organs are also donated in these camps.
