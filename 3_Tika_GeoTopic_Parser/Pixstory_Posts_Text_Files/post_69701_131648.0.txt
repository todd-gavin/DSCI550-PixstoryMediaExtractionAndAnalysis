BLACKPINK has won their first music show trophy for “Pink Venom“!

On the August 24 episode of “Show Champion,” the candidates for first place were BLACKPINK’s “Pink Venom,” THE BOYZ‘s “WHISPER,” Choi Ye Na’s “SMARTPHONE,” NewJeans’ “Attention,” and Girls’ Generation‘s “FOREVER 1.”

The trophy ultimately went to BLACKPINK!
