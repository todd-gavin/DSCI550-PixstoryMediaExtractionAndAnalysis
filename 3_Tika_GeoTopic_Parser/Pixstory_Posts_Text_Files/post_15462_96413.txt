The cases of hepatitis in children who have also been registered in Italy in recent days arouse attention, not yet alarm The Ministry of Health requests the reporting of any case of acute hepatitis that meets the definition of case currently adopted by the WHO It is recommended at this stage wanting to favor sensitivity over specificity, to evaluate and possibly report even cases that partially meet the definition of case It is also recommended to provide for the conservation of biological samples in any case to allow any other possible verification deemed necessary And what is stated in the circular of the Ministry of Salute signed by the director of prevention Gianni Rezza issued today
