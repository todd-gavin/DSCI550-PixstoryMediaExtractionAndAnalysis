A memorial meeting for Ahmad Shah Massoud the national hero of Afghanistan was held on Wednesday in the British Parliament In this meeting which was attended by supporters of the National Resistance Front and a number of members of the British Parliament a short film of the battle of the National Resistance Front with the Taliban was shown This meeting was held on the eve of the st anniversary of the assassination of Ahmad Shah Massoud Ahmad Shah Massoud was assassinated on September by two Arab suicide bombers who introduced themselves as journalists Ahmad Shah Massoud is considered one of the prominent commanders of guerilla wars in the th century He was able to defend his hometown Panjshir Valley against the attacks of the Soviet Red Army and later the Taliban During the presidency of Hamid Karzai the Afghan government gave Ahmad Shah Massoud the title of National Hero
