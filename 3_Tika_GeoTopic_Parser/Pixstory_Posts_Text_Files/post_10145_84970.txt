The alchemist uses the Philosopher s stone and turns lead into gold He gives this gold to Santiago and returns to the oasis saying that Santiago will have to continue the journey alone After reaching the Pyramids he starts digging but finds no treasure Once again he is beaten and robbed by the thieves He then tells them about his dream On hearing him one of the thieves says that he also has a similar dream He tells him that according to his dream the treasure is buried under a sycamore tree in an abandoned church in Spain Santiago now knows where his treasure was He returns back to Andalusia and digs under the tree Thus he finds his treasure
