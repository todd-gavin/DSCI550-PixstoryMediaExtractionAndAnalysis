The fans of the Indian web series should really get excited because one of the most loved Indian web series is all set to return with a third season. College Romance Season 3 is officially happening and actor Gagan Arora, who plays Bagga in the series, has revealed it through an Instagram post.

Gagan Arora uploaded a picture with the script of College Romance Season 3’s First Episode and captioned it “Look who’s back”. So, it is finally official that College Romance Season 3 will be released and the shooting for the web series has already begun.
